# nails - treatment

**GPC Brick Code**: 10000361
**Class**: [nail cosmetic/care product](Nail Cosmetic—Care Product.mdx)
**Family**: [cosmetics/fragrance](../Cosmetics—Fragrance.mdx)
**Segment**: [beauty/personal care/hygiene](../../Beauty—Personal Care—Hygiene.mdx)

Includes any products that can be described/observed as a cosmetic treatment designed to care for the nail, such as to soften and help remove overgrown cuticles and dead skin. They also include products that are used to provide a health and/or nutritional benefit to finger/toe nails. Products may claim to have formulations such as moisturizing or infection prevention. Excludes products such as Manicure Devices or Accessories, Nail Polish Removers and Nail Polish.
