# fragrance

**GPC Brick Code**: 10000365
**Class**: [fragrance](Fragrance.mdx)
**Family**: [cosmetics/fragrance](../Cosmetics—Fragrance.mdx)
**Segment**: [beauty/personal care/hygiene](../../Beauty—Personal Care—Hygiene.mdx)

Includes any products that can be described/observed as a fragrance containing fragrant natural oils extracted from flowers, wood or similar synthetic or infused oils. Usually sprayed or dabbed on the body in order to create a pleasant aroma or refresh the body and can have various levels of perfumed essences concentration. Includes products such as Eau de Toilette, Parfum and Eau de Cologne. Specifically includes Liquid Aftershave. Excludes products such as Fragranced Essences and Room Fragrances.
