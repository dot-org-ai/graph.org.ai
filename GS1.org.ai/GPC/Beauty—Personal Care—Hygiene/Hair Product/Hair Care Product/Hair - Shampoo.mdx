# hair - shampoo

**GPC Brick Code**: 10000368
**Class**: [hair care product](Hair Care Product.mdx)
**Family**: [hair product](../Hair Product.mdx)
**Segment**: [beauty/personal care/hygiene](../../Beauty—Personal Care—Hygiene.mdx)

Includes any products that can be described/observed as a preparation which is specifically used to clean the hair. These products may be applied to the hair either with or without the addition of water and may be liquid, powder or applied from an aerosol. Products may also contain a conditioner to allow the user to wash and condition the hair at the same time. Includes products that may also have formulation claims such as anti-dandruff, for oily hair, sensitive or sun protection and conditioning. Specifically includes products such as 2 in 1 Shampoo and Conditioning products. Excludes products such as Hair Conditioners, Shampoos containing Colourants, Medicated Shampoo and Animal Shampoo.
