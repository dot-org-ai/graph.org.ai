# hair - styling (non powered)

**GPC Brick Code**: 10000381
**Class**: [hair care product](Hair Care Product.mdx)
**Family**: [hair product](../Hair Product.mdx)
**Segment**: [beauty/personal care/hygiene](../../Beauty—Personal Care—Hygiene.mdx)

Includes any products that can be described/observed as a non-powered preparation applied to the hair to style and hold. Products provide temporary styling and may be labelled according to the degree of hold they provide, such as 'regular' or 'extra hold'. Includes products such as preparations that may have additional formulation claims such as volumising, conditioning, and UV protection. Specifically excludes all powered hair styling products such as hairdryers and heated rollers. Excludes products such as Powered Hair Styling Products, Hair Perm products and Conditioners and non-powered styling aids such as brushes, curlers and rollers.
