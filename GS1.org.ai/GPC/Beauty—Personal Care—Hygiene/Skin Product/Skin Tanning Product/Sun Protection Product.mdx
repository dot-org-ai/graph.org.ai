# sun protection product

**GPC Brick Code**: 10000373
**Class**: [skin tanning product](Skin Tanning Product.mdx)
**Family**: [skin product](../Skin Product.mdx)
**Segment**: [beauty/personal care/hygiene](../../Beauty—Personal Care—Hygiene.mdx)

Includes any products that can be described/observed as a preparation specifically designed for sun protection or use in conjunction with the sun and typically intended to be applied/sprayed directly onto to the skin prior to sun exposure. Products may claim formulations with moisturising or other additional properties. Excludes products such as Aftersun Products, Oral Tan Accelerators and Sunless Tanning Products.
