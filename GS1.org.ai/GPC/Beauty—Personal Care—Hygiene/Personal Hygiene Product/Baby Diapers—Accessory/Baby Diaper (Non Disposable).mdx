Includes any products that can be described/observed as a non-disposable paper-based or fabric garment specifically designed to collect and/or absorb bodily waste, and typically intended to be worn in place of underpants by a baby or infant who is not yet toilet trained. Includes non-disposable paper-based products that are enclosed within a waterproof backing, and non-disposable fabric products that may be worn with or without outer waterproof pants. Specifically excludes all disposable baby diapers. Excludes products such as Disposable Baby Diapers, Baby Inserts/Pads and Adult Incontinence Products.
