# camping water purification treatment

**GPC Brick Code**: 10005198
**Class**: [camping cooking/drinking/eating equipment](Camping Cooking—Drinking—Eating Equipment.mdx)
**Family**: [camping](../Camping.mdx)
**Segment**: [camping](../../Camping.mdx)

Includes any products that can be described/observed as a chemical treatment used by campers and travellers to ensure that drinking water is purified, safe and palatable to drink. Includes products such as chlorine tablets and iodine droplets. Excludes products such as camping water filter equipment and bleach tablets classified in Homecare, which are used for cleansing and disinfection of a toilet.
