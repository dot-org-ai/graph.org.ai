# computer docking ports/cradle

**GPC Brick Code**: 10001109
**Class**: [computer/video game accessory](Computer—Video Game Accessory.mdx)
**Family**: [computers/video game](../Computers—Video Game.mdx)
**Segment**: [computing](../../Computing.mdx)

Includes any products that can be described/observed as a desktop device into which portable products such as laptops and personal digital assistants can be fitted. Fixed connectors at the rear of the portable product and in the docking port/cradle provide a quick and convenient means of connection to peripheral devices such as printers, keyboards, mice and monitors, which are connected directly to the docking port/cradle. Excludes products such as non-connecting devices such as computer stands or supports.
