# audio cassettes - recordable

Includes any products that can be described/observed as a blank recordable audio cassette consisting of a plastic case containing magnetic tape. They are specifically designed to record and replay recorded sound and are typically intended for use in conjunction with an audio cassette player. Includes microcassettes mostly used for recording voice in dictation machines and answering machines. Specifically excludes pre-recorded audio cassettes and audio cassette players. Excludes products such as recordable CDs, DVDs, MDs and recordable Video Cassettes.
