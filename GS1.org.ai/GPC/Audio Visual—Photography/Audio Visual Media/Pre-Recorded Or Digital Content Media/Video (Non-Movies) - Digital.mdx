Includes any products that can be described / observed as digital video content which is downloaded or streamed to a video playing device. They are typically intended for use in conjunction with a television, MP3 player, smart phone, or computer. Includes videos that may be instructional, inspirational, or those demonstrating exercise or DIY techniques. Specifically excludes audio books, music videos, movies, movie trailers, pre-recorded CDs, DVDs and television shows.
