# photographic studio flash gun

**GPC Brick Code**: 10005753
**Class**: [photography](Photography.mdx)
**Family**: [photography/optic](../Photography—Optic.mdx)
**Segment**: [audio visual/photography](../../Audio Visual—Photography.mdx)

Includes any products that can be described/observed as a dry-cell powered photographic apparatus, not incorporated in a camera, that holds and electrically triggers a flashbulb simultaneously with the opening of a camera shutter. The effect is to provide momentary light while taking a photograph in a photographic studio. Excludes products such as cameras with incorporated flashes, photography lights and light reflectors and diffusers.
