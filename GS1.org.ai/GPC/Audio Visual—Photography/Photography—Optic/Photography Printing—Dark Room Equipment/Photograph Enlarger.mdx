# photograph enlarger

**GPC Brick Code**: 10001508
**Class**: [photography printing/dark room equipment](Photography Printing—Dark Room Equipment.mdx)
**Family**: [photography/optic](../Photography—Optic.mdx)
**Segment**: [audio visual/photography](../../Audio Visual—Photography.mdx)

Includes any products that can be described/observed as a device consisting of a light source, lens and a photographic film negative holder. When the light source is passed through the negative and lens an enlarged image of the photograph is projected onto photographic paper. Excludes products such as Tanks, Trays, Reels and Safelights.
