# photography dark room safelight

**GPC Brick Code**: 10001512
**Class**: [photography printing/dark room equipment](Photography Printing—Dark Room Equipment.mdx)
**Family**: [photography/optic](../Photography—Optic.mdx)
**Segment**: [audio visual/photography](../../Audio Visual—Photography.mdx)

Includes any products that can be described/observed as a lamp used in an enclosed darkroom where photographic film is developed. The lamp is fitted with a filter to screen out light rays to which film and paper are sensitive. Excludes products such as Enlargers or Reflectors/Diffusers/Backgrounds.
