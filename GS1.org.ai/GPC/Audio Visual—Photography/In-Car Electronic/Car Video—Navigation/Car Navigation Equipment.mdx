# car navigation equipment

**GPC Brick Code**: 10001517
**Class**: [car video/navigation](Car Video—Navigation.mdx)
**Family**: [in-car electronic](../In-Car Electronic.mdx)
**Segment**: [audio visual/photography](../../Audio Visual—Photography.mdx)

Includes any products that can be described/observed as a car audio visual system that uses a combination of information received from several Global Positioning System (GPS) satellites, an internal gyroscope and connections to the Vehicle Speed Sensor (VSS) to navigate the car. The system is capable of planning the optimum route and guiding the driver with audible instructions, including warnings of traffic congestion and accident black spots. Includes integrated and portable systems. Excludes products such as Car Video Monitors or Car Video Receiving Equipment and mobile GPS tracking equipment classified in Communications.
