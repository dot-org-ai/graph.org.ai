# car radar detector

**GPC Brick Code**: 10005728
**Class**: [car video/navigation](Car Video—Navigation.mdx)
**Family**: [in-car electronic](../In-Car Electronic.mdx)
**Segment**: [audio visual/photography](../../Audio Visual—Photography.mdx)

Includes any products that can be described/observed as an electronic device used by motorists to determine if their speed is being monitored by a radar unit. The detector may give verbal as well as visual warnings. Includes products that are fixed within the instrument panel of the vehicle and portable products that can be plugged into the cigarette lighter socket. Excludes products such as car navigation equipment and mobile GPS tracking equipment classified in Communications.
