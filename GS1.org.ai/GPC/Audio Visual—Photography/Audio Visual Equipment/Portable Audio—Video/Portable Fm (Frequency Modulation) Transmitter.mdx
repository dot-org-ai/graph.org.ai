# portable fm (frequency modulation) transmitter

**GPC Brick Code**: 10005765
**Class**: [portable audio/video](Portable Audio—Video.mdx)
**Family**: [audio visual equipment](../Audio Visual Equipment.mdx)
**Segment**: [audio visual/photography](../../Audio Visual—Photography.mdx)

Includes any products that can be described/observed as a portable low powered FM (Frequency Modulation) transmitter specifically designed for interfacing with personal audio devices. The transmitter generally plugs into an audio output and converts the signal into an FM radio signal, which can then be transmitted to a car radio, a portable radio, a media player such as a CD player, or a satellite radio system. Excludes products such as fixed home audio receivers, tuners and portable radios.
