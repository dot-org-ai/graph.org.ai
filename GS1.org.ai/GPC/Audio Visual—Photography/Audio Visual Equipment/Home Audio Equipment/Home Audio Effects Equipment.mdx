Includes any products that can be described/observed as a home audio device that can be used to process the signals entering a secondary audio device in order to create the required sound effect. The product may also be able to directly record sound from an external source and play this back. This effects equipment is used in conjunction with guitars, microphones, synthesisers and keyboards as well as other musical instruments and equipment. Includes Digital Injection Units as well as Sampling Units. Excludes products such as Audio Mixers, CD Players, Vinyl Turntables and Audio Equipment Accessories.
