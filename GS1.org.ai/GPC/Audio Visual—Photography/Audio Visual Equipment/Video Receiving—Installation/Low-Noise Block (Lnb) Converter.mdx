# low-noise block (lnb) converter

**GPC Brick Code**: 10005736
**Class**: [video receiving/installation](Video Receiving—Installation.mdx)
**Family**: [audio visual equipment](../Audio Visual Equipment.mdx)
**Segment**: [audio visual/photography](../../Audio Visual—Photography.mdx)

Includes any products that can be described/observed as an electronic converter that is usually fixed on or in a satellite dish. The LNB uses special electronic engineering techniques of amplification and mixing before cable attenuation. The block is free of additional electronics like a power supply or a digital receiver and this results in a signal which has less noise and unwanted signals. Excludes products such as aerials and set-top boxes.
