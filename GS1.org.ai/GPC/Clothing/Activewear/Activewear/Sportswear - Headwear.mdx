# sportswear - headwear

**GPC Brick Code**: 10003708

Includes any products that may be described/observed as headwear to be worn while playing sport. Includes products such as helmets and Swimming Caps. Specifically excludes Sporting Equipment such as Swimming Goggles and Skiing Glasses. Excludes products such as everyday, general purpose Clothing Accessories and a Protective Hard Hat.
