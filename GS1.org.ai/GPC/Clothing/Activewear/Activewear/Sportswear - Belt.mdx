# sportswear - belt

**GPC Brick Code**: 10004114
**Class**: [activewear](Activewear.mdx)
**Family**: [activewear](../Activewear.mdx)
**Segment**: [clothing](../../Clothing.mdx)

Includes any products that may be described/observed as a band to place around the waist to fasten clothing. These products are often decorative and help to accessorise sports clothing and identify the particular sport. Includes products such as belts worn to identify different Martial Arts. Specifically excludes Sports Protective Support Belts. Excludes products such as Scarves and Ties and Protective Belts.
