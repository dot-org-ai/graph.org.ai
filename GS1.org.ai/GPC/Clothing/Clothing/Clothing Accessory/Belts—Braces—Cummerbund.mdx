# belts/braces/cummerbund

**GPC Brick Code**: 10001326
**Class**: [clothing accessory](Clothing Accessory.mdx)
**Family**: [clothing](../Clothing.mdx)
**Segment**: [clothing](../../Clothing.mdx)

Includes any products that may be described/observed as either a band to place around the waist to fasten clothing, or in the case of braces, two straps which stretch from the front of trousers, over each shoulder to the back and help to hold up the trousers. These products are often decorative and help to accessorise clothing. Excludes products such as Scarves and Ties, Protective Belts and specific Sporting Belts.
