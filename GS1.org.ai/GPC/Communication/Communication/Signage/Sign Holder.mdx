# sign holder

**GPC Brick Code**: 10006226
**Class**: [signage](Signage.mdx)
**Family**: [communication](../Communication.mdx)
**Segment**: [communication](../../Communication.mdx)

Includes any products that can be described/observed as a device designed to the hold a sign. The holder keeps the sign protected, but visible to users. It may be mounted on a wall set upon a surface or stand on its own. Excludes products such as Preprinted Signs, Unprinted Signs and Combination Signs.
