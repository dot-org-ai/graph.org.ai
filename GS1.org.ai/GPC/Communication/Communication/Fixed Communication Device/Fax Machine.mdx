# fax machine

**GPC Brick Code**: 10001186
**Class**: [fixed communication device](Fixed Communication Device.mdx)
**Family**: [communication](../Communication.mdx)
**Segment**: [communication](../../Communication.mdx)

Includes any products that may be described/observed as a fixed electronic device that can transmit or receive copies of pictures, text and photographs through a telephone line. Excludes products such as multifunctional devices that include a fax machine and which are classified with office machinery, as well as telephones or answer machines sold singly.
