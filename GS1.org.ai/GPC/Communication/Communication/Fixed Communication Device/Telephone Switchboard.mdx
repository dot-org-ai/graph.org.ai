# telephone switchboard

**GPC Brick Code**: 10001190
**Class**: [fixed communication device](Fixed Communication Device.mdx)
**Family**: [communication](../Communication.mdx)
**Segment**: [communication](../../Communication.mdx)

Includes any products that may be described/observed as an apparatus containing switches by means of which a connection may be made from an incoming telephone line to any one of a series of outgoing lines. The switches can be either mechanical or electronic with the switching action being automatic, controlled by signals in the incoming call or manually controlled by a an operator. Excludes products such as Telephones.
