# two-way radio

**Family**: [communication](../Communication.mdx)

Includes any products that may be described/observed as a portable bi-directional device for communication by electromagnetic waves, without a connecting wire. These products can only send and receive verbal messages across a limited range of radio frequencies. Includes products such as Walkie-Talkies. Specifically excludes Amateur (Ham) Radio Sets. Excludes products such as One-Way Communication Radios and Mobile Telephones.
