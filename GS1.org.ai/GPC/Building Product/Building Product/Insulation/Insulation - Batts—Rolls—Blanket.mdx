# insulation - batts/rolls/blanket

**GPC Brick Code**: 10002454
**Class**: [insulation](Insulation.mdx)
**Family**: [building product](../Building Product.mdx)
**Segment**: [building product](../../Building Product.mdx)

Includes any products that may be described/observed as a thick blanket that consists of flexible fibres, most commonly fibreglass, mineral wool, plastic fibres, and natural fibres, such as cotton and sheep's wool.  The primary purpose of this product is to provide thermal insulation but it can be used for other insulation except acoustic insulation. Excludes products such as rigid fibreglass, spray foam.
