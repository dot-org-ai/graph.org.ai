# acoustic insulation - radiant barriers/heat shield

**GPC Brick Code**: 10007963
**Class**: [insulation](Insulation.mdx)
**Family**: [building product](../Building Product.mdx)
**Segment**: [building product](../../Building Product.mdx)

Includes any products that may be described/observed as a layer of metal such as aluminium, placed in an airspace to block radiant heat transfer between a heat-radiating surface, for example, a hot roof and a heat-absorbing surface, for example, conventional attic insulation. Excludes products such as boards.
