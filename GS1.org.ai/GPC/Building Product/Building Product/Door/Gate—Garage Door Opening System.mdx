# gate/garage door opening system

**GPC Brick Code**: 10005673
**Class**: [door](Door.mdx)
**Family**: [building product](../Building Product.mdx)
**Segment**: [building product](../../Building Product.mdx)

Includes any products that may be described/observed as a device that is designed to open and close, either a gate or garage door via a remote control. These systems are bought complete and can be attached universally to most gates and garage doors. These products are available in various designs and mechanical systems dependent on the user requirements. Includes products such as Chain Driven Garage Door Openers and Belt Driven Gate Openers. Excludes products such as Door Hardware and Complete Doors as well as Manual Door Openers.
