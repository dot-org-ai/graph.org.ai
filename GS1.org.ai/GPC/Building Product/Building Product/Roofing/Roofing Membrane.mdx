# roofing membrane

**GPC Brick Code**: 10002687
**Class**: [roofing](Roofing.mdx)
**Family**: [building product](../Building Product.mdx)
**Segment**: [building product](../../Building Product.mdx)

Includes any products that may be described/observed as a thin pliable sheet of material applied to roofs, typically for weatherproofing, as well as increasing the insulating properties of the complete roof structure. These products may be made from rubber, asphalt, PVC or similar polymeric material. Includes products such as PVC Roof Membranes. Excludes products Roll Roofing, Roofing Tiles, Roofing Panels, Roofing Fabrics/Felts and Roof Trusses.
