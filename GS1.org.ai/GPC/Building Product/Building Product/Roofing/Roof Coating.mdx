# roof coating

Includes any products that may be described/observed as an additional layer designed to be applied over existing roofing material for purposes of waterproofing and heat reflection. These products are designed to increase the efficiency of existing roofing materials. Includes products such as White Latex Coating and Asphalt-based Coating. Excludes products such as Preservers, Paints, Wood Coatings, Fillers and Sealants.
