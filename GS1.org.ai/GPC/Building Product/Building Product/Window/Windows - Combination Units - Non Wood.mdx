# windows - combination units - non wood

**GPC Brick Code**: 10005286
**Class**: [window](Window.mdx)

Includes any products that may be described/observed as a glass multipanel windowpane contained in a plastic or metal frame, pre-assembled and ready to be built into the wall or roof of a building in order to admit light and air. Excludes products such as single units, wood window panes, window frames and solid wood and wood clad combination units.
