# windows - single units - wood

**GPC Brick Code**: 10003946
**Class**: [window](Window.mdx)
**Family**: [building product](../Building Product.mdx)
**Segment**: [building product](../../Building Product.mdx)

Includes any products that may be described/observed as a glass single windowpane contained in a solid wood frame, pre-assembled and ready to be built into the wall or roof of a building in order to admit light and air. Excludes products such as combination units, wood window panes, window frames and non wood and wood clad single units.
