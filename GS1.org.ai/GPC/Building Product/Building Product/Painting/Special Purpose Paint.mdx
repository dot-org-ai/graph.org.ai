# special purpose paint

**GPC Brick Code**: 10002462
**Class**: [painting](Painting.mdx)
**Family**: [building product](../Building Product.mdx)
**Segment**: [building product](../../Building Product.mdx)

Includes any products that may be described/observed as a substance used as a coating to protect or decorate a surface. These products may either be emulsion or solvent based and have been specifically manufactured to be applied to a particular surface. Includes products such as Textured Paints, Bitumous Paints, Metal Care Paints, Anti-slip Floor Paints, Blackboard Paint and Tile Paint, Furniture Paint as well as Marine Paints. Excludes products such as Trim Paints, Artists' Paint and General Wall and Floor Paints as well as Paint Additives such as Anti-Slip Additive.
