# flooring - cork/bamboo

**GPC Brick Code**: 10002449

**Family**: [building product](../Building Product.mdx)

Includes any products that may be described/observed as a floor covering formed of the bark of the cork oak tree or the hard stems of bamboo grass. These products are used in used in numbers to form patterns across a designated area. Excludes products such as cork wall covering as well as wood flooring.
