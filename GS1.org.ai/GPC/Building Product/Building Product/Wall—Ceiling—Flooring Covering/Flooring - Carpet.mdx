# flooring - carpet

**GPC Brick Code**: 10002446
**Class**: [wall/ceiling/flooring covering](Wall—Ceiling—Flooring Covering.mdx)
**Family**: [building product](../Building Product.mdx)
**Segment**: [building product](../../Building Product.mdx)

Includes any products that can be described/observed as a floor covering formed of textile fibres installed around the home. These products can be internal or external to the home and are available in many different styles. Includes products such as wool carpets. Specifically excludes rugs and mats as well as artificial turf. Excludes products such as textile wall hangings and coverings.
