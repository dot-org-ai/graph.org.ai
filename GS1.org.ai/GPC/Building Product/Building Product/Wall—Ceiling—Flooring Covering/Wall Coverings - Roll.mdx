# wall coverings - roll

**GPC Brick Code**: 10002429
**Class**: [wall/ceiling/flooring covering](Wall—Ceiling—Flooring Covering.mdx)
**Family**: [building product](../Building Product.mdx)
**Segment**: [building product](../../Building Product.mdx)

Includes any products that may be described/observed as a non-rigid decorative covering for walls. These products are presented in Rolls before being cut to the required length and applied as a wall covering. These products are available in a variety of different materials dependent required effect. Includes products such as Patterned Wallpaper Rolls and Plain Fabric Wall Covering Rolls. Excludes products such as Wall Tiles, Vinyl or Metal Wall Coverings as well as Artists Paper.
