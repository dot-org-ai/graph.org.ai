# concrete mix

**GPC Brick Code**: 10002522
**Class**: [asphalt/concrete/masonry](Asphalt—Concrete—Masonry.mdx)

Includes any product that may be described/observed as a blend of portland cement, sand and gravel or stone, premixed and ready to be added with water to form concrete. Includes products such as normal, heavyweight, mass concrete mix. Excludes products such as cement and mortar mix, leveling mixes as well as aggregate.
