# plaster of pari (agent)

**GPC Brick Code**: 10003896
**Class**: [asphalt/concrete/masonry](Asphalt—Concrete—Masonry.mdx)
**Family**: [building product](../Building Product.mdx)
**Segment**: [building product](../../Building Product.mdx)

Includes any products that may be described/observed as powdered and heat-treated gypsum, which can be mixed with water (about 2 parts plaster to each part of water), hardening to a smooth solid which does not shrink or lose volume because it hardens before all the water can evaporate. Excludes plaster (mix) and cement as well as stucco and concrete.
