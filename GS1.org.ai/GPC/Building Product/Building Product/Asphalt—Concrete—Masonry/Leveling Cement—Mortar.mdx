# leveling cement/mortar

**GPC Brick Code**: 10007760
**Class**: [asphalt/concrete/masonry](Asphalt—Concrete—Masonry.mdx)
**Family**: [building product](../Building Product.mdx)
**Segment**: [building product](../../Building Product.mdx)

Includes any products that may be described/observed as a cement that has high flow characteristics and, in contrast to traditional concrete, does not require the addition of excessive amounts of water for placement. Self-leveling concrete is typically used to create a flat and smooth surface with a compressive strength similar to or higher than that of traditional concrete prior to installing interior floor coverings. Excludes any other type of mortar mixes, grouts, cement/mortar mixes, sealants, fillers or caulkers.
