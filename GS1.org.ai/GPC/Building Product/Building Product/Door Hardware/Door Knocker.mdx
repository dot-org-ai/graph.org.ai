# door knocker

**GPC Brick Code**: 10002581
**Class**: [door hardware](Door Hardware.mdx)
**Family**: [building product](../Building Product.mdx)
**Segment**: [building product](../../Building Product.mdx)

Includes any product that may be described/observed as a hinged device attached to a door, intended to signal a desire for admittance. These products consist of a movable section which is rapped against a plate or back. These products are typically metal and often decorative as well as functional. Excludes products such as doorbells.
