# hinges - door

**GPC Brick Code**: 10003191
**Class**: [door hardware](Door Hardware.mdx)
**Family**: [building product](../Building Product.mdx)
**Segment**: [building product](../../Building Product.mdx)

Includes any products that may be described/observed as two jointed plates hinged together and attached to a door, or window etc and its frame. Serves to support the door and allows the door to swing or move. Includes products such as Adjustable Hinges, Hinges with non-removable Pins and Ball-bearing Hinges. Excludes products such as Clips and Fasteners.
