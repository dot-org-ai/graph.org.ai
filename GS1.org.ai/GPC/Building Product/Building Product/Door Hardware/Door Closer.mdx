# door closer

**GPC Brick Code**: 10002574
**Class**: [door hardware](Door Hardware.mdx)
**Family**: [building product](../Building Product.mdx)
**Segment**: [building product](../../Building Product.mdx)

Includes any product that may be described/observed as a device which is fixed to the top of the door and the door frame, and which pulls the door closed after it has been opened. Includes products such as parallel arm, push side, pull side in left hand, right hand and non-handed models. Excludes products such as door handles.
