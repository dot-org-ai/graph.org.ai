# emery cloths/sheet

**GPC Brick Code**: 10003159
**Class**: [abrasive](Abrasive.mdx)
**Family**: [building product](../Building Product.mdx)
**Segment**: [building product](../../Building Product.mdx)

Includes any products that may be described/observed as a cloth covered in fragments of the hard grey-black mineral emery, which consists of corundum and either haematite or magnetite. These products are used for removing burrs and cleaning metalwork. Excludes products such as Sandpaper and Steel Wool.
