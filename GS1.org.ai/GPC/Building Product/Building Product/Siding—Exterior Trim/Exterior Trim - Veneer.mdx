Includes any products that may be described/observed as a fine layer of wood or stone adhered to the face of a substrate. These very thin sheets of wood, stone or brick literally are sliced from their parent material and used to cover the exterior of a home wall, to create the illusion of a more "solid" structure. Includes products such as Brick and Stone Veneer as well as Wood Veneer. Excludes products such as Exterior Trim - Siding, Wood Panel, Stone Pavers, Exterior Trim - Corbels/Pediments and Roofing - Shingles/Shakes.
