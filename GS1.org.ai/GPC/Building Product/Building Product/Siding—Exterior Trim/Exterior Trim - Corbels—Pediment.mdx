Includes any products that may be described/observed as a projecting block of stone built into a wall or roof during construction; step-wise construction, as in an arch. Corbels are either used to support other projections above them (such as cornices, window hoods) or for purely ornamental reasons. Pediments are peaked, rounded or other decorative panels above doorways, usually triangular in shape but can be scrolled or segmental. Includes products such as Gables, Decorative Corbels or Scrolled Pediments.
