# iron on edging strip/banding

**GPC Brick Code**: 10006793
**Class**: [mouldings/millwork/stair part](Mouldings—Millwork—Stair Part.mdx)
**Family**: [building product](../Building Product.mdx)

Includes any products that may be described/observed as a  banding to create a smooth, finished edge on woodworking projects especially those that use a laminate product such as plywood. A hot clothes iron activates the adhesive on the banding which causes it to stick to the wood.
