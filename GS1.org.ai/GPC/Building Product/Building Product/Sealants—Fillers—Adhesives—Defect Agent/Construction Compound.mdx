# construction compound

Includes any products that may be described/observed as a  bonding compound to directly bond plaster boards to flat and secure walls, such as masonry and bricks, by applying the compound in evenly spaced dots and dabs across the surface of the wall or plasterboard. Excludes products such as quick-setting lightweight plaster, cement plaster, lime plaster, earthen plaster, cement, stucco, mortar mixes, and plaster of Paris (gypsum plaster).
