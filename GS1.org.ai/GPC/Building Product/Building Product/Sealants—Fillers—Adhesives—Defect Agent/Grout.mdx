# grout

**GPC Brick Code**: 10003203
**Class**: [sealants/fillers/adhesives/defect agent](Sealants—Fillers—Adhesives—Defect Agent.mdx)
**Family**: [building product](../Building Product.mdx)

Includes any products that may be described/observed as a thin mortar used in masonry work to fill joints between bricks, blocks and tiles. These products are a mixture of cement, sand and water and are sometimes used under base plates to obtain uniform bearing surfaces. Includes products such as Tiling Grout and Resin Grout. Excludes products such as Filler, Sealant and Caulking.
