Includes any products that may be described/observed as a mastic or asphalt plastic cement used to fill gaps and joints to prevent leaks. Caulking is a flexible material that can be used to seal a gap between two surfaces such as siding panels or the corners in bath tub walls. Includes products such as Exterior Caulking and Interior Caulking. Excludes products such as Filler and Grout and Sealant as well as Cement.
