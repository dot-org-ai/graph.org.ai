Includes any products that may be described/observed as a metal block with an internal screw thread to be fitted onto a bolt. Nuts are usually hexagonal to allow easy tightening with a wrench but may also be winged, knurled or another shape. Along with a bolt nuts are designed to capture and fasten objects together. Includes products such as Hexagonal Nuts, Flanged Nuts and Wing Nuts. Excludes products such as Bolts, Screws, Rivets, Anchors and Washers.
