# dish care/protection

Includes any products that can be described/observed as a preparation, specially designed for use with a dishwasher to protect glass and plastic ware from corrosion. Products include plastic dispenser intended to be hung in a dishwasher during the wash cycle so as to release the zinc additive during the washing process and granular salts in polythene bags or soluble tablets in boxes. Excludes products such as Rinse Aids, Dish Salts and all Auto Dish Detergents and Water Softeners.
