Includes any products that can be described/observed as a preparation that is added during the finishing of the laundering process, to enhance soil resistance, facilitate soil removal and ease the ironing process. Fabric starch products are more effective on cotton or cotton blend-fabrics, whereas finishers are specifically targeted to be used on synthetic fabrics or blended fabrics with a high fabric content. Includes products of various forms such as liquid, spray, powder and tablets. Excludes products such as laundry/fabric detergents that are used to clean and wash fabrics such as clothes/upholstery/carpet but may also incorporate a fabric finisher/starch.
