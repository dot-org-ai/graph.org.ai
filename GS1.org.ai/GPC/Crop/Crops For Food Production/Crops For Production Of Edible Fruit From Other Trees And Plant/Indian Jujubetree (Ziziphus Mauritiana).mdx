# indian jujubetree (ziziphus mauritiana)

Includes any product that can be described/observed as the cultivated unharvested form of Indian jujubetrees cultivated as crops for production of edible fruit from other treetypes or crops of Indian jujube and varieties (cultivars), grown from Ziziphus mauritiana . Specifically excludes Indian jujubetrees cultivated for other purposes than as crops for production of edible fruit from other treetypes, harvested products from Indian jujubetrees and by-products such as Fresh, Prepared/Processed or Unprepared/Unprocessed and Shelf Stable or Frozen Indian jujube.
