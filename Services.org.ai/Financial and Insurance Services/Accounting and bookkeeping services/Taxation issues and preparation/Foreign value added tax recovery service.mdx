# Foreign value added tax recovery service

Considering the, 6th, 8th and 13th EU Directives, applicable international conventions and/or national laws, service provider has to support and manage the VAT reclaim procedure relating to the buyer's expenses. Reclaim procedure starts from the identification of eligible invoices and finish with transfer of refunded VAT to Buyer.
