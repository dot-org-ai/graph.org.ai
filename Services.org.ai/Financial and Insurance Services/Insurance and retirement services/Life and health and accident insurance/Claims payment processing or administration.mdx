# Claims payment processing or administration

**UNSPSC Code**: 84131613
**Class**: [Life and health and accident insurance](Life and health and accident insurance.mdx)
**Family**: [Insurance and retirement services](../Insurance and retirement services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Financial and Insurance Services](../../Financial and Insurance Services.mdx)

Services offered by suppliers (Third-Party Payers or Third-Party Administrators) who collect insurance premiums from various group clients, handle payment to service providers (health, dental, drug) and remit premiums collected back to insurance companies. In some cases, may act to pay on behalf of insurance company, and directly contact and maintain information with the group clients.
