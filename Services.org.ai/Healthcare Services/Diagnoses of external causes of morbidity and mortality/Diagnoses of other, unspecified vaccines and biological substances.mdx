In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of other and unspecified vaccines and biological substances. Other and unspecified vaccines and biological substances is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Y59.
