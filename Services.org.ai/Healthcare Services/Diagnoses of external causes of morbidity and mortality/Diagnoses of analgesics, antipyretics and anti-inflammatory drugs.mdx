# Diagnoses of analgesics, antipyretics and anti-inflammatory drugs

**UNSPSC Code**: 85445000
**Family**: [Diagnoses of external causes of morbidity and mortality](Diagnoses of external causes of morbidity and mortality.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of analgesics, antipyretics and anti-inflammatory drugs. Analgesics, antipyretics and anti-inflammatory drugs is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Y45. ICD-10 also has The following Analgesics, antipyretics and anti-inflammatory drugs children in its four-character category: Y45.0 (Opioids and related analgesics); Y45.1 (Salicylates); Y45.2 (Propionic acid derivatives); Y45.3 (Other nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs [NSAID]); Y45.4 (Antirheumatics); Y45.5 (4-Aminophenol derivatives); Y45.8 (Other analgesics and antipyretics); Y45.9 (Analgesic, antipyretic and anti-inflammatory drug, unspecified)
