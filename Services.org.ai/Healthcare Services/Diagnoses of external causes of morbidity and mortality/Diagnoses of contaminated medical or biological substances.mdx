# Diagnoses of contaminated medical or biological substances

**UNSPSC Code**: 85446900
**Family**: [Diagnoses of external causes of morbidity and mortality](Diagnoses of external causes of morbidity and mortality.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of contaminated medical or biological substances. Contaminated medical or biological substances is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Y64. ICD-10 also has The following Contaminated medical or biological substances children in its four-character category: Y64.0 (Contaminated medical or biological substance, transfused or infused); Y64.1 (Contaminated medical or biological substance, injected or used for immunization); Y64.8 (Contaminated medical or biological substance administered by other means); Y64.9 (Contaminated medical or biological substance administered by unspecified means)
