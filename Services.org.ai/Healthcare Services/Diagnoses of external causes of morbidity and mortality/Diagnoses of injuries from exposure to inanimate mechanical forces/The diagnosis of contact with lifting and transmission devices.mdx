# The diagnosis of contact with lifting and transmission devices

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of contact with lifting and transmission devices, not elsewhere classified. Contact with lifting and transmission devices, not elsewhere classified is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code W24.
