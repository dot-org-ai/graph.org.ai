# Diagnoses of systemic anti-infectives and antiparasitics

**UNSPSC Code**: 85444600
**Family**: [Diagnoses of external causes of morbidity and mortality](Diagnoses of external causes of morbidity and mortality.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of other systemic anti-infectives and antiparasitics. Other systemic anti-infectives and antiparasitics is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Y41. ICD-10 also has The following Other systemic anti-infectives and antiparasitics children in its four-character category: Y41.0 (Sulfonamides); Y41.1 (Antimycobacterial drugs); Y41.2 (Antimalarials and drugs acting on other blood protozoa); Y41.3 (Other antiprotozoal drugs); Y41.4 (Anthelminthics); Y41.5 (Antiviral drugs); Y41.8 (Other specified systemic anti-infectives and antiparasitics); Y41.9 (Systemic anti-infective and antiparasitic, unspecified)
