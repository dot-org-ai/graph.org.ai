# Diagnoses of bacterial vaccines

**UNSPSC Code**: 85446300
**Family**: [Diagnoses of external causes of morbidity and mortality](Diagnoses of external causes of morbidity and mortality.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of bacterial vaccines. Bacterial vaccines is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Y58. ICD-10 also has The following Bacterial vaccines children in its four-character category: Y58.0 (BCG vaccine); Y58.1 (Typhoid and paratyphoid vaccine); Y58.2 (Cholera vaccine); Y58.3 (Plague vaccine); Y58.4 (Tetanus vaccine); Y58.5 (Diphtheria vaccine); Y58.6 (Pertussis vaccine, including combinations with a pertussis component); Y58.8 (Mixed bacterial vaccines, except combinations with a pertussis component); Y58.9 (Other and unspecified bacterial vaccines)
