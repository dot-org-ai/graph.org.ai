# Diagnoses of agents primarily affecting the cardiovascular system

**Family**: [Diagnoses of external causes of morbidity and mortality](Diagnoses of external causes of morbidity and mortality.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of agents primarily affecting the cardiovascular system. Agents primarily affecting the cardiovascular system is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Y52.
