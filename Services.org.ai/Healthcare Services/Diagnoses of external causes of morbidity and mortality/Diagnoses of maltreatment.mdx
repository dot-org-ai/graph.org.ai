# Diagnoses of maltreatment

**UNSPSC Code**: 85444200
**Family**: [Diagnoses of external causes of morbidity and mortality](Diagnoses of external causes of morbidity and mortality.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of other maltreatment. Other maltreatment is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Y07. ICD-10 also has The following Other maltreatment children in its four-character category: Y07.0 (By spouse or partner); Y07.1 (By parent); Y07.2 (By acquaintance or friend); Y07.3 (By official authorities); Y07.8 (By other specified persons); Y07.9 (By unspecified person)
