# Diagnoses of foreign object accidentally left in body during surgical and medical care

**UNSPSC Code**: 85446600
**Family**: [Diagnoses of external causes of morbidity and mortality](Diagnoses of external causes of morbidity and mortality.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of foreign object accidentally left in body during surgical and medical care. Foreign object accidentally left in body during surgical and medical care is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Y61. ICD-10 also has The following Foreign object accidentally left in body during surgical and medical care children in its four-character category: Y61.0 (During surgical operation); Y61.1 (During infusion or transfusion); Y61.2 (During kidney dialysis or other perfusion); Y61.3 (During injection or immunization); Y61.4 (During endoscopic examination); Y61.5 (During heart catheterization); Y61.6 (During aspiration, puncture and other catheterization); Y61.7 (During removal of catheter or packing); Y61.8 (During other surgical and medical care); Y61.9 (During unspecified surgical and medical care)
