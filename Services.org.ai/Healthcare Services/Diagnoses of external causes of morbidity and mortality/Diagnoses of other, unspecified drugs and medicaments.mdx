# Diagnoses of other, unspecified drugs and medicaments

**UNSPSC Code**: 85446200
**Family**: [Diagnoses of external causes of morbidity and mortality](Diagnoses of external causes of morbidity and mortality.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of other and unspecified drugs and medicaments. Other and unspecified drugs and medicaments is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Y57. ICD-10 also has The following Other and unspecified drugs and medicaments children in its four-character category: Y57.0 (Appetite depressants [anorectics]); Y57.1 (Lipotropic drugs); Y57.2 (Antidotes and chelating agents, not elsewhere classified); Y57.3 (Alcohol deterrents); Y57.4 (Pharmaceutical excipients); Y57.5 (X-ray contrast media); Y57.6 (Other diagnostic agents); Y57.7 (Vitamins, not elsewhere classified); Y57.8 (Other drugs and medicaments); Y57.9 (Drug or medicament, unspecified)
