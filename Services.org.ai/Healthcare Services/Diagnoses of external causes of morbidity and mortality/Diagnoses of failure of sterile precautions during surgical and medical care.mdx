In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of failure of sterile precautions during surgical and medical care. Failure of sterile precautions during surgical and medical care is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Y62.
