# Diagnoses of neglect and abandonment

**UNSPSC Code**: 85444100
**Family**: [Diagnoses of external causes of morbidity and mortality](Diagnoses of external causes of morbidity and mortality.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of neglect and abandonment. Neglect and abandonment is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Y06. ICD-10 also has The following Neglect and abandonment children in its four-character category: Y06.0 (By spouse or partner); Y06.1 (By parent); Y06.2 (By acquaintance or friend); Y06.8 (By other specified persons); Y06.9 (By unspecified person)
