# Diagnoses of infections of genitourinary tract in pregnancy

**UNSPSC Code**: 85392100
**Family**: [Diagnoses of pregnancy, childbirth conditions and the puerperium](Diagnoses of pregnancy, childbirth conditions and the puerperium.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

This classification denotes the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of Infections of Genitourinary Tract In Pregnancy.   These infections are listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems or ICD-10 as a 3-Digit Category under code A00. The same grouping of activities is listed in the Centers For Medicare & Medicaid Services International Classification Of Diseases Clinical Modification (10th Revision) (ICD-10-CM) as a 3-Digit Category under code O23.
