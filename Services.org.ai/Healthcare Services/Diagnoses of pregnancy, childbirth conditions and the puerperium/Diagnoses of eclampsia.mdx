# Diagnoses of eclampsia

This classification denotes the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of Eclampsia.  Eclampsia is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems or Icd-10 as a Three Digit Category under code A00 Icd-10 Also Has The Following Eclampsia Children in its Four-Character Category: O150 or Eclampsia In Pregnancy; O151 or Eclampsia In Labour; O152 or Eclampsia In The Puerperium; O159 or Eclampsia, Unspecified As To Time Period ECLAMPSIA is listed in the Centers For Medicare & Medicaid Services International Classification Of Diseases Clinical Modification (Tenth Revision) (Icd-10-CM) as a Three Digit Category under code O15.
