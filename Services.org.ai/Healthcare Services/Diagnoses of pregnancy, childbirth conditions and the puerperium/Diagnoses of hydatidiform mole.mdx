# Diagnoses of hydatidiform mole

**UNSPSC Code**: 85392500
**Family**: [Diagnoses of pregnancy, childbirth conditions and the puerperium](Diagnoses of pregnancy, childbirth conditions and the puerperium.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

This classification denotes the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of Hydatidiform Mole.  Hydatidiform Mole is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems or Icd-10 as a Three Digit Category under code A00 Icd-10 Also Has The Following Hydatidiform Mole Children in its Four-Character Category: O010 or Classical Hydatidiform Mole; O011 or Incomplete and Partial Hydatidiform Mole; O019 or Hydatidiform Mole, Unspecified HYDATIDIFORM MOLE is listed in the Centers For Medicare & Medicaid Services International Classification Of Diseases Clinical Modification (Tenth Revision) (Icd-10-CM) as a Three Digit Category under code O01. ICD-10-CM also has the following HYDATIDIFORM MOLE CHILDREN in its Four-Character or Five-Character Categories: (CLASSICAL HYDATIDIFORM MOLE O01.0), (INCOMPLETE and PARTIAL HYDATIDIFORM MOLE O01.1), (HYDATIDIFORM MOLE, UNSPECIFIED O01.9).
