# Diagnoses of toxoplasmosis

**UNSPSC Code**: 85223300
**Family**: [Diagnoses of infectious and parasitic diseases-part b](Diagnoses of infectious and parasitic diseases-part b.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

This classification denotes the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of Toxoplasmosis  or Schistosomiasis or bilharziasis TOXOPLASMOSIS is listed in the Centers For Medicare & Medicaid Services International Classification Of Diseases Clinical Modification (Tenth Revision) (Icd-10-CM) as a Three Digit Category under code B58.
