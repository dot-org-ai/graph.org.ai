# Diagnoses of blastomycosis

**UNSPSC Code**: 85222000
**Family**: [Diagnoses of infectious and parasitic diseases-part b](Diagnoses of infectious and parasitic diseases-part b.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

This classification denotes the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of Blastomycosis. Blastomycosis is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems or Icd-10 as a Three Digit Category under code A00. Icd-10 Also Has The Following Blastomycosis Children in its Four-Character Category: B40.0 or Acute Pulmonary Blastomycosis; B40.1 or Chronic Pulmonary Blastomycosis; B40.2 or Pulmonary Blastomycosis, Unspecified; B40.3 or Cutaneous Blastomycosis; B40.7 or Disseminated Blastomycosis; B40.8 or Other Forms of Blastomycosis; B40.9 or Blastomycosis, Unspecified. BLASTOMYCOSIS is listed in the Centers For Medicare & Medicaid Services International Classification Of Diseases Clinical Modification (Tenth Revision) (Icd-10-CM) as a Three Digit Category under code B40. ICD-10-CM also has the following BLASTOMYCOSIS CHILDREN in its Four-Character or Five-Character Categories: (ACUTE PULMONARY BLASTOMYCOSIS B40.0), (CHRONIC PULMONARY BLASTOMYCOSIS B40.1), (PULMONARY BLASTOMYCOSIS, UNSPECIFIED B40.2), (CUTANEOUS BLASTOMYCOSIS B40.3), (DISSEMINATED BLASTOMYCOSIS B40.7), (OTHER FORMS of BLASTOMYCOSIS B40.8), (BLASTOMYCOTIC MENINGOENCEPHALITIS B40.81), (OTHER FORMS of BLASTOMYCOSIS B40.89), (BLASTOMYCOSIS, UNSPECIFIED B40.9).
