# Diagnoses of congenital iodine-deficiency syndrome

This classification denotes the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of Congenital Iodine-Deficiency Syndrome.  Congenital Iodine-Deficiency Syndrome is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems or Icd-10 as a Three Digit Category under code A00 Icd-10 Also Has The Following Congenital Iodine-Deficiency Syndrome Children in its Four-Character Category: E000 or Congenital Iodine-Deficiency Syndrome, Neurological Type; E001 or Congenital Iodine-Deficiency Syndrome, Myxoedematous Type; E002 or Congenital Iodine-Deficiency Syndrome, Mixed Type; E009 or Congenital Iodine-Deficiency Syndrome, Unspecified CONGENITAL IODINE-DEFICIENCY SYNDROME is listed in the Centers For Medicare & Medicaid Services International Classification Of Diseases Clinical Modification (Tenth Revision) (Icd-10-CM) as a Three Digit Category under code E00.
