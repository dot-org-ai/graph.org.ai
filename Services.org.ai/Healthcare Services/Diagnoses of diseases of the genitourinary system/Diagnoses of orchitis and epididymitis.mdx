# Diagnoses of orchitis and epididymitis

**Family**: [Diagnoses of diseases of the genitourinary system](Diagnoses of diseases of the genitourinary system.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

This classification denotes the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of Orchitis and Epididymitis.  Orchitis and Epididymitis is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems or Icd-10 as a Three Digit Category under code A00 Icd-10 Also Has The Following Orchitis and Epididymitis Children in its Four-Character Category: N450 or Orchitis, Epididymitis and Epididymo-Orchitis With Abscess; N459 or Orchitis, Epididymitis and Epididymo-Orchitis Without Abscess ORCHITIS and EPIDIDYMITIS is listed in the Centers For Medicare & Medicaid Services International Classification Of Diseases Clinical Modification (Tenth Revision) (Icd-10-CM) as a Three Digit Category under code N45.
