This classification denotes the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of Absent, Scanty and Rare Menstruation.  Absent, Scanty and Rare Menstruation is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems or Icd-10 as a Three Digit Category under code A00 Icd-10 Also Has The Following Absent, Scanty and Rare Menstruation Children in its Four-Character Category: N910 or Primary Amenorrhoea; N911 or Secondary Amenorrhoea; N912 or Amenorrhoea, Unspecified; N913 or Primary Oligomenorrhoea; N914 or Secondary Oligomenorrhoea; N915 or Oligomenorrhoea, Unspecified ABSENT, SCANTY and RARE MENSTRUATION is listed in the Centers For Medicare & Medicaid Services International Classification Of Diseases Clinical Modification (Tenth Revision) (Icd-10-CM) as a Three Digit Category under code N91.
