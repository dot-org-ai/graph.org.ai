# Diagnoses of toxic effect of inorganic substances

**UNSPSC Code**: 85434700
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2.](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2..mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of toxic effect of other inorganic substances. Toxic effect of other inorganic substances is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code T57. ICD-10 also has The following Toxic effect of other inorganic substances children in its four-character category: T57.0 (Toxic effect: Arsenic and its compounds ); T57.1 (Toxic effect: Phosphorus and its compounds ); T57.2 (Toxic effect: Manganese and its compounds ); T57.3 (Toxic effect: Hydrogen cyanide ); T57.8 (Toxic effect: Other specified inorganic substances ); T57.9 (Toxic effect: Inorganic substance, unspecified )
