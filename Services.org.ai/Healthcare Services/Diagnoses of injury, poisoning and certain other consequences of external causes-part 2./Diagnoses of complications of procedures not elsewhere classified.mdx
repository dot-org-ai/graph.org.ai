# Diagnoses of complications of procedures not elsewhere classified

**UNSPSC Code**: 85436100
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2.](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2..mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of complications of procedures, not elsewhere classified. Complications of procedures, not elsewhere classified is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code T81. ICD-10 also has The following Complications of procedures, not elsewhere classified children in its four-character category: T81.0 (Haemorrhage and haematoma complicating a procedure, not elsewhere classified ); T81.1 (Shock during or resulting from a procedure, not elsewhere classified ); T81.2 (Accidental puncture and laceration during a procedure, not elsewhere classified ); T81.3 (Disruption of operation wound, not elsewhere classified ); T81.4 (Infection following a procedure, not elsewhere classified ); T81.5 (Foreign body accidentally left in body cavity or operation wound following a procedure ); T81.6 (Acute reaction to foreign substance accidentally left during a procedure ); T81.7 (Vascular complications following a procedure, not elsewhere classified ); T81.8 (Other complications of procedures, not elsewhere classified ); T81.9 (Unspecified complication of procedure )
