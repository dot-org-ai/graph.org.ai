# Diagnoses of open wounds involving multiple body regions

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of open wounds involving multiple body regions. Open wounds involving multiple body regions is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code T01. ICD-10 also has The following Open wounds involving multiple body regions children in its four-character category: T01.0 (Open wounds involving head with neck ); T01.1 (Open wounds involving thorax with abdomen, lower back and pelvis ); T01.2 (Open wounds involving multiple regions of upper limb(s) ); T01.3 (Open wounds involving multiple regions of lower limb(s) ); T01.6 (Open wounds involving multiple regions of upper limb(s) with lower limb(s) ); T01.8 (Open wounds involving other combinations of body regions ); T01.9 (Multiple open wounds, unspecified )
