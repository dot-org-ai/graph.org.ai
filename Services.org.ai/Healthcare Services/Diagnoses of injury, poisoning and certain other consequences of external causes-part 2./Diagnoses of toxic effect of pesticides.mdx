# Diagnoses of toxic effect of pesticides

**UNSPSC Code**: 85434900
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2.](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2..mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of toxic effect of pesticides. Toxic effect of pesticides is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code T60. ICD-10 also has The following Toxic effect of pesticides children in its four-character category: T60.0 (Toxic effect: Organophosphate and carbamate insecticides ); T60.1 (Toxic effect: Halogenated insecticides ); T60.2 (Toxic effect: Other insecticides ); T60.3 (Toxic effect: Herbicides and fungicides ); T60.4 (Toxic effect: Rodenticides ); T60.8 (Toxic effect: Other pesticides ); T60.9 (Toxic effect: Pesticide, unspecified )
