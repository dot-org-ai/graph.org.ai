# Diagnoses of toxic effect of noxious substances eaten as seafood

**UNSPSC Code**: 85435000
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2.](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2..mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of toxic effect of noxious substances eaten as seafood. Toxic effect of noxious substances eaten as seafood is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code T61. ICD-10 also has The following Toxic effect of noxious substances eaten as seafood children in its four-character category: T61.0 (Toxic effect: Ciguatera fish poisoning ); T61.1 (Toxic effect: Scombroid fish poisoning ); T61.2 (Toxic effect: Other fish and shellfish poisoning ); T61.8 (Toxic effect: Toxic effect of other seafoods ); T61.9 (Toxic effect: Toxic effect of unspecified seafood )
