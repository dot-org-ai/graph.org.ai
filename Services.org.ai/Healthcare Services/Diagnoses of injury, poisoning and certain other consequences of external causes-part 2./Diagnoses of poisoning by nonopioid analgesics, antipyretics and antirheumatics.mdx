# Diagnoses of poisoning by nonopioid analgesics, antipyretics and antirheumatics

**UNSPSC Code**: 85433100
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2.](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2..mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of poisoning by nonopioid analgesics, antipyretics and antirheumatics. Poisoning by nonopioid analgesics, antipyretics and antirheumatics is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code T39. ICD-10 also has The following Poisoning by nonopioid analgesics, antipyretics and antirheumatics children in its four-character category: T39.0 (Poisoning: Salicylates ); T39.1 (Poisoning: 4-Aminophenol derivatives ); T39.2 (Poisoning: Pyrazolone derivatives ); T39.3 (Poisoning: Other nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs [NSAID] ); T39.4 (Poisoning: Antirheumatics, not elsewhere classified ); T39.8 (Poisoning: Other nonopioid analgesics and antipyretics, not elsewhere classified ); T39.9 (Poisoning: Nonopioid analgesic, antipyretic and antirheumatic, unspecified )
