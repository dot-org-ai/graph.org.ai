# Diagnoses of poisoning by antiepileptic, sedative-hypnotic and antiparkinsonism drugs

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of poisoning by antiepileptic, sedative-hypnotic and antiparkinsonism drugs. Poisoning by antiepileptic, sedative-hypnotic and antiparkinsonism drugs is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code T42.
