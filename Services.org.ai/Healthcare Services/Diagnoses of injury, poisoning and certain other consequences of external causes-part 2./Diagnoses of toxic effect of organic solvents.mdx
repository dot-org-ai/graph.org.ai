# Diagnoses of toxic effect of organic solvents

**UNSPSC Code**: 85434300
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2.](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2..mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of toxic effect of organic solvents. Toxic effect of organic solvents is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code T52. ICD-10 also has The following Toxic effect of organic solvents children in its four-character category: T52.0 (Toxic effect: Petroleum products ); T52.1 (Toxic effect: Benzene ); T52.2 (Toxic effect: Homologues of benzene ); T52.3 (Toxic effect: Glycols ); T52.4 (Toxic effect: Ketones ); T52.8 (Toxic effect: Other organic solvents ); T52.9 (Toxic effect: Organic solvent, unspecified )
