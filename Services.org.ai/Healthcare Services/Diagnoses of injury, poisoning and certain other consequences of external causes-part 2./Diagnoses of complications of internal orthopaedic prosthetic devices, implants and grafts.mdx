# Diagnoses of complications of internal orthopaedic prosthetic devices, implants and grafts

**UNSPSC Code**: 85436400
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2.](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2..mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of complications of internal orthopaedic prosthetic devices, implants and grafts. Complications of internal orthopaedic prosthetic devices, implants and grafts is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code T84. ICD-10 also has The following Complications of internal orthopaedic prosthetic devices, implants and grafts children in its four-character category: T84.0 (Mechanical complication of internal joint prosthesis ); T84.1 (Mechanical complication of internal fixation device of bones of limb ); T84.2 (Mechanical complication of internal fixation device of other bones ); T84.3 (Mechanical complication of other bone devices, implants and grafts ); T84.4 (Mechanical complication of other internal orthopaedic devices, implants and grafts ); T84.5 (Infection and inflammatory reaction due to internal joint prosthesis ); T84.6 (Infection and inflammatory reaction due to internal fixation device [any site] ); T84.7 (Infection and inflammatory reaction due to other internal orthopaedic prosthetic devices, implants and grafts ); T84.8 (Other complications of internal orthopaedic prosthetic devices, implants and grafts ); T84.9 (Unspecified complication of internal orthopaedic prosthetic device, implant and graft )
