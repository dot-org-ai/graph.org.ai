# Diagnoses of effects of deprivation

**UNSPSC Code**: 85435700
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2.](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2..mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of effects of other deprivation. Effects of other deprivation is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code T73. ICD-10 also has The following Effects of other deprivation children in its four-character category: T73.0 (Effects of hunger ); T73.1 (Effects of thirst ); T73.2 (Exhaustion due to exposure ); T73.3 (Exhaustion due to excessive exertion ); T73.8 (Other effects of deprivation ); T73.9 (Effect of deprivation, unspecified )
