# Diagnoses of injury of unspecified body region

**UNSPSC Code**: 85432000
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2.](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2..mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of injury of unspecified body region. Injury of unspecified body region is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code T14. ICD-10 also has The following Injury of unspecified body region children in its four-character category: T14.0 (Superficial injury of unspecified body region ); T14.1 (Open wound of unspecified body region ); T14.2 (Fracture of unspecified body region ); T14.3 (Dislocation, sprain and strain of unspecified body region ); T14.4 (Injury of nerve(s) of unspecified body region ); T14.5 (Injury of blood vessel(s) of unspecified body region ); T14.6 (Injury of muscles and tendons of unspecified body region ); T14.7 (Crushing injury and traumatic amputation of unspecified body region ); T14.8 (Other injuries of unspecified body region ); T14.9 (Injury, unspecified ); T15.0 (Foreign body in cornea ); T15.1 (Foreign body in conjunctival sac ); T15.8 (Foreign body in other and multiple parts of external eye ); T15.9 (Foreign body on external eye, part unspecified )
