# Diagnoses of toxic effect of other and unspecified substances

**UNSPSC Code**: 85435300
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2.](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2..mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of toxic effect of other and unspecified substances. Toxic effect of other and unspecified substances is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code T65. ICD-10 also has The following Toxic effect of other and unspecified substances children in its four-character category: T65.0 (Toxic effect: Cyanides ); T65.1 (Toxic effect: Strychnine and its salts ); T65.2 (Toxic effect: Tobacco and nicotine ); T65.3 (Toxic effect: Nitroderivatives and aminoderivatives of benzene and its homologues ); T65.4 (Toxic effect: Carbon disulfide ); T65.5 (Toxic effect: Nitroglycerin and other nitric acids and esters ); T65.6 (Toxic effect: Paints and dyes, not elsewhere classified ); T65.8 (Toxic effect: Toxic effect of other specified substances ); T65.9 (Toxic effect: Toxic effect of unspecified substance )
