# Diagnoses of effects of other external causes

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of effects of other external causes. Effects of other external causes is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code T75. ICD-10 also has The following Effects of other external causes children in its four-character category: T75.0 (Effects of lightning ); T75.1 (Drowning and nonfatal submersion ); T75.2 (Effects of vibration ); T75.3 (Motion sickness ); T75.4 (Effects of electric current ); T75.8 (Other specified effects of external causes )
