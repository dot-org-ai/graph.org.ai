# Diagnoses of injuries involving multiple body regions

**UNSPSC Code**: 85431600
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2.](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2..mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of other injuries involving multiple body regions, not elsewhere classified. Other injuries involving multiple body regions, not elsewhere classified is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code T06. ICD-10 also has The following Other injuries involving multiple body regions, not elsewhere classified children in its four-character category: T06.0 (Injuries of brain and cranial nerves with injuries of nerves and spinal cord at neck level ); T06.1 (Injuries of nerves and spinal cord involving other multiple body regions ); T06.2 (Injuries of nerves involving multiple body regions ); T06.3 (Injuries of blood vessels involving multiple body regions ); T06.4 (Injuries of muscles and tendons involving multiple body regions ); T06.5 (Injuries of intrathoracic organs with intra-abdominal and pelvic organs ); T06.8 (Other specified injuries involving multiple body regions )
