# Diagnoses of toxic effect of noxious substances eaten as food

**UNSPSC Code**: 85435100
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2.](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2..mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of toxic effect of other noxious substances eaten as food. Toxic effect of other noxious substances eaten as food is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code T62. ICD-10 also has The following Toxic effect of other noxious substances eaten as food children in its four-character category: T62.0 (Toxic effect: Ingested mushrooms ); T62.1 (Toxic effect: Ingested berries ); T62.2 (Toxic effect: Other ingested (parts of) plant(s) ); T62.8 (Toxic effect: Other specified noxious substances eaten as food ); T62.9 (Toxic effect: Noxious substance eaten as food, unspecified )
