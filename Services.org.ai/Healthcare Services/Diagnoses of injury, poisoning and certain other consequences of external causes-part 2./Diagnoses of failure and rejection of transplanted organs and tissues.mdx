# Diagnoses of failure and rejection of transplanted organs and tissues

**UNSPSC Code**: 85436600
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2.](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2..mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of failure and rejection of transplanted organs and tissues. Failure and rejection of transplanted organs and tissues is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code T86. ICD-10 also has The following Failure and rejection of transplanted organs and tissues children in its four-character category: T86.0 (Bone-marrow transplant rejection ); T86.1 (Kidney transplant failure and rejection ); T86.2 (Heart transplant failure and rejection ); T86.3 (Heart-lung transplant failure and rejection ); T86.4 (Liver transplant failure and rejection ); T86.8 (Failure and rejection of other transplanted organs and tissues ); T86.9 (Failure and rejection of unspecified transplanted organ and tissue )
