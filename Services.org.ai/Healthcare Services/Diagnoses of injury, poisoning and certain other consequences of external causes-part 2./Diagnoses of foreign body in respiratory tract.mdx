# Diagnoses of foreign body in respiratory tract

**UNSPSC Code**: 85432100
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2.](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2..mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of foreign body in respiratory tract. Foreign body in respiratory tract is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code T17. ICD-10 also has The following Foreign body in respiratory tract children in its four-character category: T17.0 (Foreign body in nasal sinus ); T17.1 (Foreign body in nostril ); T17.2 (Foreign body in pharynx ); T17.3 (Foreign body in larynx ); T17.4 (Foreign body in trachea ); T17.5 (Foreign body in bronchus ); T17.8 (Foreign body in other and multiple parts of respiratory tract ); T17.9 (Foreign body in respiratory tract, part unspecified )
