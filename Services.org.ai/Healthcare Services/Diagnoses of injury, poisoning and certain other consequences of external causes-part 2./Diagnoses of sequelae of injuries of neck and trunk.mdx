# Diagnoses of sequelae of injuries of neck and trunk

**UNSPSC Code**: 85437000
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2.](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2..mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of sequelae of injuries of neck and trunk. Sequelae of injuries of neck and trunk is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code T91. ICD-10 also has The following Sequelae of injuries of neck and trunk children in its four-character category: T91.0 (Sequelae of superficial injury and open wound of neck and trunk ); T91.1 (Sequelae of fracture of spine ); T91.2 (Sequelae of other fracture of thorax and pelvis ); T91.3 (Sequelae of injury of spinal cord ); T91.4 (Sequelae of injury of intrathoracic organs ); T91.5 (Sequelae of injury of intra-abdominal and pelvic organs ); T91.8 (Sequelae of other specified injuries of neck and trunk ); T91.9 (Sequelae of unspecified injury of neck and trunk )
