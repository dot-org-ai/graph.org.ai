# Diagnoses of adverse effects

**UNSPSC Code**: 85436000
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2.](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2..mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of adverse effects, not elsewhere classified. Adverse effects, not elsewhere classified is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code T78.
