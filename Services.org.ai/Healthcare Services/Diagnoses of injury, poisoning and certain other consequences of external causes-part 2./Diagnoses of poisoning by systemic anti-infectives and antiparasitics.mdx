# Diagnoses of poisoning by systemic anti-infectives and antiparasitics

**UNSPSC Code**: 85432900
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2.](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2..mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of poisoning by other systemic anti-infectives and antiparasitics. Poisoning by other systemic anti-infectives and antiparasitics is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code T37. ICD-10 also has The following Poisoning by other systemic anti-infectives and antiparasitics children in its four-character category: T37.0 (Poisoning: Sulfonamides ); T37.1 (Poisoning: Antimycobacterial drugs ); T37.2 (Poisoning: Antimalarials and drugs acting on other blood protozoa ); T37.3 (Poisoning: Other antiprotozoal drugs ); T37.4 (Poisoning: Anthelminthics ); T37.5 (Poisoning: Antiviral drugs ); T37.8 (Poisoning: Other specified systemic anti-infectives and antiparasitics ); T37.9 (Poisoning: Systemic anti-infective and antiparasitic, unspecified )
