# Diagnoses of foreign body in alimentary tract

**UNSPSC Code**: 85432200
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2.](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2..mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of foreign body in alimentary tract. Foreign body in alimentary tract is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code T18. ICD-10 also has The following Foreign body in alimentary tract children in its four-character category: T18.0 (Foreign body in mouth ); T18.1 (Foreign body in oesophagus ); T18.2 (Foreign body in stomach ); T18.3 (Foreign body in small intestine ); T18.4 (Foreign body in colon ); T18.5 (Foreign body in anus and rectum ); T18.8 (Foreign body in other and multiple parts of alimentary tract ); T18.9 (Foreign body in alimentary tract, part unspecified )
