# Diagnoses of toxic effect of metals

**UNSPSC Code**: 85434600
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2.](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 2..mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of toxic effect of metals. Toxic effect of metals is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code T56. ICD-10 also has The following Toxic effect of metals children in its four-character category: T56.0 (Toxic effect: Lead and its compounds ); T56.1 (Toxic effect: Mercury and its compounds ); T56.2 (Toxic effect: Chromium and its compounds ); T56.3 (Toxic effect: Cadmium and its compounds ); T56.4 (Toxic effect: Copper and its compounds ); T56.5 (Toxic effect: Zinc and its compounds ); T56.6 (Toxic effect: Tin and its compounds ); T56.7 (Toxic effect: Beryllium and its compounds ); T56.8 (Toxic effect: Other metals ); T56.9 (Toxic effect: Metal, unspecified )
