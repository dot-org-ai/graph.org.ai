This classification denotes the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of Glanders and Melioidosis. Glanders and Melioidosis is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems or Icd-10 as a Three Digit Category under code A00. Icd-10 Also Has The Following Glanders and Melioidosis Children in its Four-Character Category: A24.0 or Glanders; A24.1 or Acute and Fulminating Melioidosis; A24.2 or Subacute and Chronic Melioidosis; A24.3 or Other Melioidosis; A24.4 or Melioidosis, Unspecified.
