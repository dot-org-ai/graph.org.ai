# Diagnoses of gonococcal infection

**UNSPSC Code**: 85214800
**Family**: [Diagnoses of infectious and parasitic diseases-part a](Diagnoses of infectious and parasitic diseases-part a.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

This classification denotes the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of Gonococcal Infection. Gonococcal Infection is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems or Icd-10 as a Three Digit Category under code A00.
