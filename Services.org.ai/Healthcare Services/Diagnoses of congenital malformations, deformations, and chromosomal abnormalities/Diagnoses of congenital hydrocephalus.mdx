# Diagnoses of congenital hydrocephalus

**UNSPSC Code**: 85401700
**Family**: [Diagnoses of congenital malformations, deformations, and chromosomal abnormalities](Diagnoses of congenital malformations, deformations, and chromosomal abnormalities.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

This classification denotes the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of Congenital Hydrocephalus.  Congenital Hydrocephalus is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems or Icd-10 as a Three Digit Category under code A00 Icd-10 Also Has The Following Congenital Hydrocephalus Children in its Four-Character Category: Q030 or Malformations of Aqueduct of Sylvius; Q031 or Atresia of Foramina of Magendie and Luschka; Q038 or Other Congenital Hydrocephalus; Q039 or Congenital Hydrocephalus, Unspecified CONGENITAL HYDROCEPHALUS is listed in the Centers For Medicare & Medicaid Services International Classification Of Diseases Clinical Modification (Tenth Revision) (Icd-10-CM) as a Three Digit Category under code Q03. ICD-10-CM also has the following CONGENITAL HYDROCEPHALUS CHILDREN in its Four-Character or Five-Character Categories: (MALFORMATIONS of AQUEDUCT of SYLVIUS Q03.0), (ATRESIA of FORAMINA of MAGENDIE and LUSCHKA Q03.1), (OTHER CONGENITAL HYDROCEPHALUS Q03.8), (CONGENITAL HYDROCEPHALUS, UNSPECIFIED Q03.9).
