# Diagnoses of down syndrome

**Family**: [Diagnoses of congenital malformations, deformations, and chromosomal abnormalities](Diagnoses of congenital malformations, deformations, and chromosomal abnormalities.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

This classification denotes the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of Down Syndrome.  Down Syndrome is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems or Icd-10 as a Three Digit Category under code A00 Icd-10 Also Has The Following Down Syndrome Children in its Four-Character Category: Q900 or Trisomy 21, Meiotic Nondisjunction; Q901 or Trisomy 21, Mosaicism or Mitotic Nondisjunction; Q902 or Trisomy 21, Translocation; Q909 or Down Syndrome, Unspecified DOWN SYNDROME is listed in the Centers For Medicare & Medicaid Services International Classification Of Diseases Clinical Modification (Tenth Revision) (Icd-10-CM) as a Three Digit Category under code Q90. ICD-10-CM also has the following DOWN SYNDROME CHILDREN in its Four-Character or Five-Character Categories: (TRISOMY 21, NONMOSAICISM (MEIOTIC NONDISJUNCTION) Q90.0), (TRISOMY 21, MOSAICISM (MITOTIC NONDISJUNCTION) Q90.1), (TRISOMY 21, TRANSLOCATION Q90.2), (DOWN SYNDROME, UNSPECIFIED Q90.9).
