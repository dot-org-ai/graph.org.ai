# Diagnoses of carcinoma in situ of oral cavity, oesophagus and stomach

**UNSPSC Code**: 85252900
**Family**: [Diagnoses of neoplasms](Diagnoses of neoplasms.mdx)

This classification denotes the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of Carcinoma In Situ of Oral Cavity, Oesophagus and Stomach. Carcinoma In Situ of Oral Cavity, Oesophagus and Stomach is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems or Icd-10 as a Three Digit Category under code A00. Icd-10 Also Has The Following Carcinoma In Situ of Oral Cavity, Oesophagus and Stomach Children in its Four-Character Category: D00.0 or Carcinoma In Situ: Lip, Oral Cavity and Pharynx; D00.1 or Carcinoma In Situ: Oesophagus; D00.2 or Carcinoma In Situ: Stomach.
