# Diagnoses of carcinoma in situ of breast

**UNSPSC Code**: 85253400
**Family**: [Diagnoses of neoplasms](Diagnoses of neoplasms.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

This classification denotes the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of Carcinoma In Situ of Breast. Carcinoma In Situ of Breast is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems or Icd-10 as a Three Digit Category under code A00. Icd-10 Also Has The Following Carcinoma In Situ of Breast Children in its Four-Character Category: D05.0 or Lobular Carcinoma In Situ; D05.1 or Intraductal Carcinoma In Situ; D05.7 or Other Carcinoma In Situ of Breast; D05.9 or Carcinoma In Situ of Breast, Unspecified. CARCINOMA in SITU of BREAST is listed in the Centers For Medicare & Medicaid Services International Classification Of Diseases Clinical Modification (Tenth Revision) (Icd-10-CM) as a Three Digit Category under code D05.
