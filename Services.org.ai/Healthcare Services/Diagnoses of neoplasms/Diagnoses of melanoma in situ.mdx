# Diagnoses of melanoma in situ

**UNSPSC Code**: 85253200

This classification denotes the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of Melanoma In Situ. Melanoma In Situ is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems or Icd-10 as a Three Digit Category under code A00.
