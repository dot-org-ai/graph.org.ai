This classification denotes the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of Haemangioma and Lymphangioma, Any Site. Haemangioma and Lymphangioma, any site is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems or Icd-10 as a Three Digit Category under code A00. Icd-10 Also Has The Following Haemangioma and Lymphangioma, Any Site Children in its Four-Character Category: D18.0 or Haemangioma, Any Site; D18.1 or Lymphangioma, Any Site.
