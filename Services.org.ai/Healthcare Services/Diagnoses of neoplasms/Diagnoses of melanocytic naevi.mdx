# Diagnoses of melanocytic naevi

**UNSPSC Code**: 85254800
**Family**: [Diagnoses of neoplasms](Diagnoses of neoplasms.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

This classification denotes the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of Melanocytic Naevi. Melanocytic Naevi is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems or Icd-10 as a Three Digit Category under code A00. Icd-10 Also Has The Following Melanocytic Naevi Children in its Four-Character Category: D22.0 or Melanocytic Naevi of Lip; D22.1 or Melanocytic Naevi of Eyelid, Including Canthus; D22.2 or Melanocytic Naevi of Ear and External Auricular Canal; D22.3 or Melanocytic Naevi of Other and Unspecified Parts of Face; D22.4 or Melanocytic Naevi of Scalp and Neck; D22.5 or Melanocytic Naevi of Trunk; D22.6 or Melanocytic Naevi of Upper Limb, Including Shoulder; D22.7 or Melanocytic Naevi of Lower Limb, Including Hip; D22.9 or Melanocytic Naevi, Unspecified.
