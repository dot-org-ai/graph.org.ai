# Diagnoses of mental and behavioral disorders

**UNSPSC Code**: 85270000
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](Healthcare Services.mdx)

This classification denotes the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of Mental and behavioral Disorders. Mental and behavioral Disorders is listed in The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems or ICD-10 Under Chapter V.
