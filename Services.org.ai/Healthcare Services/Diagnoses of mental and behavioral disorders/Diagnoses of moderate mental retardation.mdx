# Diagnoses of moderate mental retardation

**UNSPSC Code**: 85275400
**Family**: [Diagnoses of mental and behavioral disorders](Diagnoses of mental and behavioral disorders.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

This classification denotes the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of Moderate Mental Retardation . Moderate Mental Retardation  is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems or Icd-10 as a Three Digit Category under code A00 Icd-10 Also Has The Following Moderate Mental Retardation Children in its Four-Character Category: F710 or Moderate Mental Retardation With The Statement of No, Or Minimal, Impairment of Behaviour; F711 or Moderate Mental Retardation : Significant Impairment of Behaviour Requiring Attention Or Treatment; F718 or Moderate Mental Retardation : Other Impairments of Behaviour; F719 or Moderate Mental Retardation Without Mention of Impairment of Behaviour
