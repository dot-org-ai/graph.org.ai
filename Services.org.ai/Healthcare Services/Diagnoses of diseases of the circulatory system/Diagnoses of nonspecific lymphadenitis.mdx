# Diagnoses of nonspecific lymphadenitis

This classification denotes the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of Nonspecific lymphadenitis or noninfective disorders of lymphatic vessels and lymph nodes NONSPECIFIC LYMPHADENITIS is listed in the Centers For Medicare & Medicaid Services International Classification Of Diseases Clinical Modification (Tenth Revision) (Icd-10-CM) as a Three Digit Category under code I88. ICD-10-CM also has the following NONSPECIFIC LYMPHADENITIS CHILDREN in its Four-Character or Five-Character Categories: (NONSPECIFIC MESENTERIC LYMPHADENITIS I88.0), (CHRONIC LYMPHADENITIS, EXCEPT MESENTERIC I88.1), (OTHER NONSPECIFIC LYMPHADENITIS I88.8), (NONSPECIFIC LYMPHADENITIS, UNSPECIFIED I88.9).
