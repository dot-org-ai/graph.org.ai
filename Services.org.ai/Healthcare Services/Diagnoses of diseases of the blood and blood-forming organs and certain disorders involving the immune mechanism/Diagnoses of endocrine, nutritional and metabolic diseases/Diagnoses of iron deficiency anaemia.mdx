# Diagnoses of iron deficiency anaemia

**UNSPSC Code**: 85321501
**Class**: [Diagnoses of endocrine, nutritional and metabolic diseases](Diagnoses of endocrine, nutritional and metabolic diseases.mdx)
**Family**: [Diagnoses of diseases of the blood and blood-forming organs and certain disorders involving the immune mechanism](../Diagnoses of diseases of the blood and blood-forming organs and certain disorders involving the immune mechanism.mdx)

This classification denotes the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of Iron Deficiency Anaemia. Iron Deficiency Anaemia is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems or Icd-10 as a Three Digit Category under code A00. Icd-10 Also Has The Following Iron Deficiency Anaemia Children in its Four-Character Category: D50.0 or Iron Deficiency Anaemia Secondary To Blood Loss or Chronic; D50.1 or Sideropenic Dysphagia; D50.8 or Other Iron Deficiency Anaemias; D50.9 or Iron Deficiency Anaemia, Unspecified.
