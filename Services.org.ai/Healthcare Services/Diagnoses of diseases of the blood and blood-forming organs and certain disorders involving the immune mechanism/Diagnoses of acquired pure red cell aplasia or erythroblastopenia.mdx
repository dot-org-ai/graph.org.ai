# Diagnoses of acquired pure red cell aplasia or erythroblastopenia

**UNSPSC Code**: 85321900
**Family**: [Diagnoses of diseases of the blood and blood-forming organs and certain disorders involving the immune mechanism](Diagnoses of diseases of the blood and blood-forming organs and certain disorders involving the immune mechanism.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

This classification denotes the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of Acquired Pure Red Cell Aplasia or Erythroblastopenia. Acquired Pure Red Cell Aplasia or Erythroblastopenia is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems or Icd-10 as a Three Digit Category under code A00. Icd-10 Also Has The Following Acquired Pure Red Cell Aplasia or Erythroblastopenia Children in its Four-Character Category: D60.0 or Chronic Acquired Pure Red Cell Aplasia; D60.1 or Transient Acquired Pure Red Cell Aplasia; D60.8 or Other Acquired Pure Red Cell Aplasias; D60.9 or Acquired Pure Red Cell Aplasia, Unspecified.
