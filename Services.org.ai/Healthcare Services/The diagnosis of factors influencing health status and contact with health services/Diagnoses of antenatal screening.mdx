# Diagnoses of antenatal screening

**UNSPSC Code**: 85453300
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of antenatal screening. Antenatal screening is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z36. ICD-10 also has The following Antenatal screening children in its four-character category: Z36.0 (Antenatal screening for chromosomal anomalies); Z36.1 (Antenatal screening for raised alphafetoprotein level); Z36.2 (Other antenatal screening based on amniocentesis); Z36.3 (Antenatal screening for malformations using ultrasound and other physical methods); Z36.4 (Antenatal screening for fetal growth retardation using ultrasound and other physical methods); Z36.5 (Antenatal screening for isoimmunization); Z36.8 (Other antenatal screening); Z36.9 (Antenatal screening, unspecified)
