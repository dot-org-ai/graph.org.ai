# Diagnoses of need for immunization against certain single viral diseases

**UNSPSC Code**: 85452200
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of need for immunization against certain single viral diseases. Need for immunization against certain single viral diseases is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z24. ICD-10 also has The following Need for immunization against certain single viral diseases children in its four-character category: Z24.0 (Need for immunization against poliomyelitis); Z24.1 (Need for immunization against arthropod-borne viral encephalitis); Z24.2 (Need for immunization against rabies); Z24.3 (Need for immunization against yellow fever); Z24.4 (Need for immunization against measles alone); Z24.5 (Need for immunization against rubella alone); Z24.6 (Need for immunization against viral hepatitis)
