# Diagnoses of problems related to lifestyle

**UNSPSC Code**: 85456000
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of problems related to lifestyle  . Problems related to lifestyle   is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z72. ICD-10 also has The following Problems related to lifestyle   children in its four-character category: Z72.0 (Tobacco use  ); Z72.1 (Alcohol use  ); Z72.2 (Drug use  ); Z72.3 (Lack of physical exercise  ); Z72.4 (Inappropriate diet and eating habits  ); Z72.5 (High-risk sexual behaviour  ); Z72.6 (Gambling and betting  ); Z72.8 (Other problems related to lifestyle  ); Z72.9 (Problem related to lifestyle, unspecified  )
