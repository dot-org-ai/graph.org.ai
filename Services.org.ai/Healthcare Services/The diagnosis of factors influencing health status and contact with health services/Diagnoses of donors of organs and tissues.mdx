# Diagnoses of donors of organs and tissues

**UNSPSC Code**: 85454400
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of donors of organs and tissues. Donors of organs and tissues is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z52. ICD-10 also has The following Donors of organs and tissues children in its four-character category: Z52.0 (Blood donor); Z52.1 (Skin donor); Z52.2 (Bone donor); Z52.3 (Bone marrow donor); Z52.4 (Kidney donor); Z52.5 (Cornea donor); Z52.6 (Liver donor); Z52.7 (Heart donor); Z52.8 (Donor of other organs and tissues); Z52.9 (Donor of unspecified organ or tissue)
