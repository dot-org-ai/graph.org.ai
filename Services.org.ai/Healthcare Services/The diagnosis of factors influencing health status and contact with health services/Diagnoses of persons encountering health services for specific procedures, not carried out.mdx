# Diagnoses of persons encountering health services for specific procedures, not carried out

**UNSPSC Code**: 85454500
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of persons encountering health services for specific procedures, not carried out. Persons encountering health services for specific procedures, not carried out is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z53. ICD-10 also has The following Persons encountering health services for specific procedures, not carried out children in its four-character category: Z53.0 (Procedure not carried out because of contraindication); Z53.1 (Procedure not carried out because of patient's decision for reasons of belief and group pressure); Z53.2 (Procedure not carried out because of patient's decision for other and unspecified reasons); Z53.8 (Procedure not carried out for other reasons); Z53.9 (Procedure not carried out, unspecified reason)
