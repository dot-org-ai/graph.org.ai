# The diagnosis of occupational therapy and vocational rehabilitation

**UNSPSC Code**: 85454208
**Class**: [Diagnoses of care involving use of rehabilitation procedures](Diagnoses of care involving use of rehabilitation procedures.mdx)
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](../The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of occupational therapy and vocational rehabilitation, not elsewhere classified. Occupational therapy and vocational rehabilitation, not elsewhere classified is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z50.7.
