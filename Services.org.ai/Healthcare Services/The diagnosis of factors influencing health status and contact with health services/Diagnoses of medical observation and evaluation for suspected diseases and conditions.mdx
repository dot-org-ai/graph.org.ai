# Diagnoses of medical observation and evaluation for suspected diseases and conditions

**UNSPSC Code**: 85451300
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of medical observation and evaluation for suspected diseases and conditions. Medical observation and evaluation for suspected diseases and conditions is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z03. ICD-10 also has The following Medical observation and evaluation for suspected diseases and conditions children in its four-character category: Z03.0 (Observation for suspected tuberculosis); Z03.1 (Observation for suspected malignant neoplasm); Z03.2 (Observation for suspected mental and behavioural disorders); Z03.3 (Observation for suspected nervous system disorder); Z03.4 (Observation for suspected myocardial infarction); Z03.5 (Observation for other suspected cardiovascular diseases); Z03.6 (Observation for suspected toxic effect from ingested substance); Z03.8 (Observation for other suspected diseases and conditions); Z03.9 (Observation for suspected disease or condition, unspecified)
