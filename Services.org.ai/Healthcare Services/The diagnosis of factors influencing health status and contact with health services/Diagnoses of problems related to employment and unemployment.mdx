# Diagnoses of problems related to employment and unemployment

**UNSPSC Code**: 85454800
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of problems related to employment and unemployment. Problems related to employment and unemployment is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z56. ICD-10 also has The following Problems related to employment and unemployment children in its four-character category: Z56.0 (Unemployment, unspecified); Z56.1 (Change of job); Z56.2 (Threat of job loss); Z56.3 (Stressful work schedule); Z56.4 (Discord with boss and workmates); Z56.5 (Uncongenial work); Z56.6 (Other physical and mental strain related to work); Z56.7 (Other and unspecified problems related to employment)
