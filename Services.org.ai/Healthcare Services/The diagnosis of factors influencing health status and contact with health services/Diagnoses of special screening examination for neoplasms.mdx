# Diagnoses of special screening examination for neoplasms

**UNSPSC Code**: 85451700
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of special screening examination for neoplasms. Special screening examination for neoplasms is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z12. ICD-10 also has The following Special screening examination for neoplasms children in its four-character category: Z12.4 (Special screening examination for neoplasm of cervix); Z12.6 (Special screening examination for neoplasm of bladder); Z12.8 (Special screening examination for neoplasms of other sites); Z12.9 (Special screening examination for neoplasm, unspecified)
