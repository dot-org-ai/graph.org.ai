In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of persons encountering health services in other circumstances  . Persons encountering health services in other circumstances   is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z76.
