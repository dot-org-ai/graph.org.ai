# Diagnoses of problems related to education and literacy

**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of problems related to education and literacy. Problems related to education and literacy is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z55. ICD-10 also has The following Problems related to education and literacy children in its four-character category: Z55.0 (Illiteracy and low-level literacy); Z55.1 (Schooling unavailable and unattainable); Z55.2 (Failed examinations); Z55.3 (Underachievement in school); Z55.4 (Educational maladjustment and discord with teachers and classmates); Z55.8 (Other problems related to education and literacy); Z55.9 (Problem related to education and literacy, unspecified)
