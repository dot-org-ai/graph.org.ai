# Diagnoses of problems related to social environment

**UNSPSC Code**: 85455200
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of problems related to social environment. Problems related to social environment is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z60. ICD-10 also has The following Problems related to social environment children in its four-character category: Z60.0 (Problems of adjustment to life-cycle transitions); Z60.1 (Atypical parenting situation); Z60.2 (Living alone); Z60.3 (Acculturation difficulty); Z60.4 (Social exclusion and rejection); Z60.5 (Target of perceived adverse discrimination and persecution); Z60.8 (Other problems related to social environment); Z60.9 (Problem related to social environment, unspecified); Z61 (Problems related to negative life events in childhood)
