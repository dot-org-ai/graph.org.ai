# Diagnoses of need for immunization against other single viral diseases

**UNSPSC Code**: 85452300

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of need for immunization against other single viral diseases. Need for immunization against other single viral diseases is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z25. ICD-10 also has The following Need for immunization against other single viral diseases children in its four-character category: Z25.0 (Need for immunization against mumps alone); Z25.1 (Need for immunization against influenza); Z25.8 (Need for immunization against other specified single viral diseases)
