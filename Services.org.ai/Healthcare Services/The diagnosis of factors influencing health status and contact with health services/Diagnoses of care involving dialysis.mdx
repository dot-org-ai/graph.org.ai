**UNSPSC Code**: 85454100
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of care involving dialysis. Care involving dialysis is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z49. ICD-10 also has The following Care involving dialysis children in its four-character category: Z49.0 (Preparatory care for dialysis); Z49.1 (Extracorporeal dialysis); Z49.2 (Other dialysis)
