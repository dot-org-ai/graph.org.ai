# Diagnoses of need for immunization against single bacterial diseases

**UNSPSC Code**: 85452100
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of need for immunization against single bacterial diseases. Need for immunization against single bacterial diseases is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z23. ICD-10 also has The following Need for immunization against single bacterial diseases children in its four-character category: Z23.0 (Need for immunization against cholera alone); Z23.1 (Need for immunization against typhoid-paratyphoid alone [TAB]); Z23.2 (Need for immunization against tuberculosis [BCG]); Z23.3 (Need for immunization against plague); Z23.4 (Need for immunization against tularaemia); Z23.5 (Need for immunization against tetanus alone); Z23.6 (Need for immunization against diphtheria alone); Z23.7 (Need for immunization against pertussis alone); Z23.8 (Need for immunization against other single bacterial diseases)
