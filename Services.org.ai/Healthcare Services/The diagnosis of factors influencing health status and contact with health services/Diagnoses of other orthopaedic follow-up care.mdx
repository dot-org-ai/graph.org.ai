# Diagnoses of other orthopaedic follow-up care

**UNSPSC Code**: 85453900
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of other orthopaedic follow-up care. Other orthopaedic follow-up care is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z47. ICD-10 also has The following Other orthopaedic follow-up care children in its four-character category: Z47.0 (Follow-up care involving removal of fracture plate and other internal fixation device); Z47.8 (Other specified orthopaedic follow-up care); Z47.9 (Orthopaedic follow-up care, unspecified)
