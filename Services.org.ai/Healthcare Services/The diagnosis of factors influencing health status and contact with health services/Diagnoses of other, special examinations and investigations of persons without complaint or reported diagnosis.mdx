# Diagnoses of other, special examinations and investigations of persons without complaint or reported diagnosis

**UNSPSC Code**: 85451100
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of other special examinations and investigations of persons without complaint or reported diagnosis. Other special examinations and investigations of persons without complaint or reported diagnosis is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z01. ICD-10 also has The following Other special examinations and investigations of persons without complaint or reported diagnosis children in its four-character category: Z01.0 (Examination of eyes and vision); Z01.1 (Examination of ears and hearing); Z01.2 (Dental examination); Z01.3 (Examination of blood pressure); Z01.4 (Gynaecological examination (general)(routine)); Z01.5 (Diagnostic skin and sensitization tests); Z01.6 (Radiological examination, not elsewhere classified); Z01.7 (Laboratory examination); Z01.8 (Other specified special examinations); Z01.9 (Special examination, unspecified)
