# Diagnoses of persons encountering health services for counselling and medical advice

**UNSPSC Code**: 85455900
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of persons encountering health services for other counselling and medical advice, not elsewhere classified  . Persons encountering health services for other counselling and medical advice, not elsewhere classified   is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z71. ICD-10 also has The following Persons encountering health services for other counselling and medical advice, not elsewhere classified   children in its four-character category: Z71.0 (Person consulting on behalf of another person  ); Z71.1 (Person with feared complaint in whom no diagnosis is made  ); Z71.2 (Person consulting for explanation of investigation findings  ); Z71.3 (Dietary counselling and surveillance  ); Z71.4 (Alcohol abuse counselling and surveillance  ); Z71.5 (Drug abuse counselling and surveillance  ); Z71.6 (Tobacco abuse counselling  ); Z71.7 (Human immunodeficiency virus [HIV] counselling  ); Z71.8 (Other specified counselling  ); Z71.9 (Counselling, unspecified  )
