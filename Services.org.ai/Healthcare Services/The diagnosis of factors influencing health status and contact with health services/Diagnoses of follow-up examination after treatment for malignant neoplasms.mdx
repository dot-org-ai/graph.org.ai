# Diagnoses of follow-up examination after treatment for malignant neoplasms

**UNSPSC Code**: 85451500
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of follow-up examination after treatment for malignant neoplasms. Follow-up examination after treatment for malignant neoplasms is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z08. ICD-10 also has The following Follow-up examination after treatment for malignant neoplasms children in its four-character category: Z08.0 (Follow-up examination after surgery for malignant neoplasm); Z08.1 (Follow-up examination after radiotherapy for malignant neoplasm); Z08.2 (Follow-up examination after chemotherapy for malignant neoplasm); Z08.7 (Follow-up examination after combined treatment for malignant neoplasm); Z08.8 (Follow-up examination after other treatment for malignant neoplasm); Z08.9 (Follow-up examination after unspecified treatment for malignant neoplasm)
