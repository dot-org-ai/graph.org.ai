# Diagnoses of problems related to medical facilities and health care

**UNSPSC Code**: 85456300
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of problems related to medical facilities and other health care  . Problems related to medical facilities and other health care   is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z75. ICD-10 also has The following Problems related to medical facilities and other health care   children in its four-character category: Z75.0 (Medical services not available in home  ); Z75.1 (Person awaiting admission to adequate facility elsewhere  ); Z75.2 (Other waiting period for investigation and treatment  ); Z75.3 (Unavailability and inaccessibility of health-care facilities  ); Z75.4 (Unavailability and inaccessibility of other helping agencies  ); Z75.5 (Holiday relief care  ); Z75.8 (Other problems related to medical facilities and other health care  ); Z75.9 (Unspecified problem related to medical facilities and other health care  )
