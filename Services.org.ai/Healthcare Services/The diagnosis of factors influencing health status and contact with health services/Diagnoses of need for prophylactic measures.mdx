# Diagnoses of need for prophylactic measures

**UNSPSC Code**: 85452700
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of need for other prophylactic measures. Need for other prophylactic measures is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z29. ICD-10 also has The following Need for other prophylactic measures children in its four-character category: Z29.0 (Isolation); Z29.1 (Prophylactic immunotherapy); Z29.2 (Other prophylactic chemotherapy); Z29.8 (Other specified prophylactic measures); Z29.9 (Prophylactic measure, unspecified)
