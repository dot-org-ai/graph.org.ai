# Diagnoses of fitting and adjustment of devices

**UNSPSC Code**: 85453800
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of fitting and adjustment of other devices. Fitting and adjustment of other devices is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z46. ICD-10 also has The following Fitting and adjustment of other devices children in its four-character category: Z46.0 (Fitting and adjustment of spectacles and contact lenses); Z46.1 (Fitting and adjustment of hearing aid); Z46.2 (Fitting and adjustment of other devices related to nervous system and special senses); Z46.3 (Fitting and adjustment of dental prosthetic device); Z46.4 (Fitting and adjustment of orthodontic device); Z46.5 (Fitting and adjustment of ileostomy and other intestinal appliances); Z46.6 (Fitting and adjustment of urinary device); Z46.7 (Fitting and adjustment of orthopaedic device); Z46.8 (Fitting and adjustment of other specified devices); Z46.9 (Fitting and adjustment of unspecified device)
