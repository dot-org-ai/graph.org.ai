# Diagnoses of supervision of normal pregnancy

**UNSPSC Code**: 85453100
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of supervision of normal pregnancy. Supervision of normal pregnancy is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z34. ICD-10 also has The following Supervision of normal pregnancy children in its four-character category: Z34.0 (Supervision of normal first pregnancy); Z34.8 (Supervision of other normal pregnancy); Z34.9 (Supervision of normal pregnancy, unspecified)
