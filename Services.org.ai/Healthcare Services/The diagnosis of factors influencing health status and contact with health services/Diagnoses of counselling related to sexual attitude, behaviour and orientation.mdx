# Diagnoses of counselling related to sexual attitude, behaviour and orientation

**UNSPSC Code**: 85455800
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of counselling related to sexual attitude, behaviour and orientation  . Counselling related to sexual attitude, behaviour and orientation   is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z70. ICD-10 also has The following Counselling related to sexual attitude, behaviour and orientation   children in its four-character category: Z70.0 (Counselling related to sexual attitude  ); Z70.1 (Counselling related to patient's sexual behaviour and orientation  ); Z70.2 (Counselling related to sexual behaviour and orientation of third party  ); Z70.3 (Counselling related to combined concerns regarding sexual attitude, behaviour and orientation  ); Z70.8 (Other sex counselling  ); Z70.9 (Sex counselling, unspecified  )
