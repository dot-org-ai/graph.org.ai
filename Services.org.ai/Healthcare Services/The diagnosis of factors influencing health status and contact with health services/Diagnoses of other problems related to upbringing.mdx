# Diagnoses of other problems related to upbringing

**UNSPSC Code**: 85455400
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of other problems related to upbringing. Other problems related to upbringing is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z62. ICD-10 also has The following Other problems related to upbringing children in its four-character category: Z62.0 (Inadequate parental supervision and control); Z62.1 (Parental overprotection); Z62.2 (Institutional upbringing); Z62.3 (Hostility towards and scapegoating of child); Z62.4 (Emotional neglect of child); Z62.5 (Other problems related to neglect in upbringing); Z62.6 (Inappropriate parental pressure and other abnormal qualities of upbringing); Z62.8 (Other specified problems related to upbringing); Z62.9 (Problem related to upbringing, unspecified); Z63 (Other problems related to primary support group, including family circumstances)
