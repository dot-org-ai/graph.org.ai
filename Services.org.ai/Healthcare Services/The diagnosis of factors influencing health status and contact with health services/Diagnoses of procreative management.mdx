# Diagnoses of procreative management

**UNSPSC Code**: 85452900
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of procreative management. Procreative management is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z31. ICD-10 also has The following Procreative management children in its four-character category: Z31.0 (Tuboplasty or vasoplasty after previous sterilization); Z31.1 (Artificial insemination); Z31.2 (In vitro fertilization); Z31.3 (Other assisted fertilization methods); Z31.4 (Procreative investigation and testing); Z31.5 (Genetic counselling); Z31.6 (General counselling and advice on procreation); Z31.8 (Other procreative management); Z31.9 (Procreative management, unspecified)
