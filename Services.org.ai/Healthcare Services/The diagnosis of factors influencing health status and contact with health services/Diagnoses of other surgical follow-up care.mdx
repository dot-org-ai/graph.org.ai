# Diagnoses of other surgical follow-up care

**UNSPSC Code**: 85454000
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of other surgical follow-up care. Other surgical follow-up care is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z48. ICD-10 also has The following Other surgical follow-up care children in its four-character category: Z48.0 (Attention to surgical dressings and sutures); Z48.8 (Other specified surgical follow-up care); Z48.9 (Surgical follow-up care, unspecified)
