# Diagnoses of carrier of infectious disease

**UNSPSC Code**: 85452000
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of carrier of infectious disease. Carrier of infectious disease is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z22. ICD-10 also has The following Carrier of infectious disease children in its four-character category: Z22.0 (Carrier of typhoid); Z22.1 (Carrier of other intestinal infectious diseases); Z22.2 (Carrier of diphtheria); Z22.3 (Carrier of other specified bacterial diseases); Z22.4 (Carrier of infections with a predominantly sexual mode of transmission); Z22.6 (Carrier of human T-lymphotropic virus type- 1 [HTLV-1] infection); Z22.8 (Carrier of other infectious diseases); Z22.9 (Carrier of infectious disease, unspecified)
