# Diagnoses of occupational exposure to risk-factors

**UNSPSC Code**: 85454900
**Family**: [The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services](The diagnosis of factors influencing health status and contact with health services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of occupational exposure to risk-factors. Occupational exposure to risk-factors is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code Z57. ICD-10 also has The following Occupational exposure to risk-factors children in its four-character category: Z57.0 (Occupational exposure to noise); Z57.1 (Occupational exposure to radiation); Z57.2 (Occupational exposure to dust); Z57.3 (Occupational exposure to other air contaminants); Z57.4 (Occupational exposure to toxic agents in agriculture); Z57.5 (Occupational exposure to toxic agents in other industries); Z57.6 (Occupational exposure to extreme temperature); Z57.7 (Occupational exposure to vibration); Z57.8 (Occupational exposure to other risk-factors); Z57.9 (Occupational exposure to unspecified risk-factor)
