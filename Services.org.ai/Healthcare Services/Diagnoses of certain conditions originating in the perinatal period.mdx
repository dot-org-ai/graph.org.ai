# Diagnoses of certain conditions originating in the perinatal period

**UNSPSC Code**: 85380000
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](Healthcare Services.mdx)

This classification denotes the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of Certain Conditions Originating in the Perinatal Period. Certain Conditions Originating in the Perinatal Period is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems or ICD-10 Under Chapter XVI.
