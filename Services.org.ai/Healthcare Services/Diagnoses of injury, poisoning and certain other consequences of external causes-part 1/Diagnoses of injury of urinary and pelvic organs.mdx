# Diagnoses of injury of urinary and pelvic organs

**UNSPSC Code**: 85424300
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 1](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 1.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of injury of urinary and pelvic organs. Injury of urinary and pelvic organs is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code S37. ICD-10 also has The following Injury of urinary and pelvic organs children in its four-character category: S37.0 (Injury of kidney ); S37.1 (Injury of ureter ); S37.2 (Injury of bladder ); S37.3 (Injury of urethra ); S37.4 (Injury of ovary ); S37.5 (Injury of fallopian tube ); S37.6 (Injury of uterus ); S37.7 (Injury of multiple pelvic organs ); S37.8 (Injury of other pelvic organs ); S37.9 (Injury of unspecified pelvic organ )
