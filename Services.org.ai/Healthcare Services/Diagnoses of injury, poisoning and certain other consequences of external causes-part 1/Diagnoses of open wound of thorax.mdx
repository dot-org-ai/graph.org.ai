# Diagnoses of open wound of thorax

**UNSPSC Code**: 85422800
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 1](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 1.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of open wound of thorax. Open wound of thorax is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code S21. ICD-10 also has The following Open wound of thorax children in its four-character category: S21.0 (Open wound of breast ); S21.1 (Open wound of front wall of thorax ); S21.2 (Open wound of back wall of thorax ); S21.7 (Multiple open wounds of thoracic wall ); S21.8 (Open wound of other parts of thorax ); S21.9 (Open wound of thorax, part unspecified )
