# Diagnoses of other injuries of neck

**UNSPSC Code**: 85422600
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 1](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 1.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of other and unspecified injuries of neck. Other and unspecified injuries of neck is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code S19. ICD-10 also has The following Other and unspecified injuries of neck children in its four-character category: S19.7 (Multiple injuries of neck ); S19.8 (Other specified injuries of neck ); S19.9 (Unspecified injury of neck ); S20.0 (Contusion of breast ); S20.1 (Other and unspecified superficial injuries of breast ); S20.2 (Contusion of thorax ); S20.3 (Other superficial injuries of front wall of thorax ); S20.4 (Other superficial injuries of back wall of thorax ); S20.7 (Multiple superficial injuries of thorax ); S20.8 (Superficial injury of other and unspecified parts of thorax )
