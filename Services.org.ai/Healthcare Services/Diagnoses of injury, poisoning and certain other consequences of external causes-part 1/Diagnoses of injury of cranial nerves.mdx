# Diagnoses of injury of cranial nerves

**UNSPSC Code**: 85421400
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 1](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 1.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of injury of cranial nerves. Injury of cranial nerves is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code S04. ICD-10 also has The following Injury of cranial nerves children in its four-character category: S04.0 (Injury of optic nerve and pathways ); S04.1 (Injury of oculomotor nerve ); S04.2 (Injury of trochlear nerve ); S04.3 (Injury of trigeminal nerve ); S04.4 (Injury of abducent nerve ); S04.5 (Injury of facial nerve ); S04.6 (Injury of acoustic nerve ); S04.7 (Injury of accessory nerve ); S04.8 (Injury of other cranial nerves ); S04.9 (Injury of unspecified cranial nerve )
