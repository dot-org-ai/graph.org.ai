# Diagnoses of open wound of neck

**UNSPSC Code**: 85422000
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 1](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 1.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of open wound of neck. Open wound of neck is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code S11. ICD-10 also has The following Open wound of neck children in its four-character category: S11.0 (Open wound involving larynx and trachea ); S11.1 (Open wound involving thyroid gland ); S11.2 (Open wound involving pharynx and cervical oesophagus ); S11.7 (Multiple open wounds of neck ); S11.8 (Open wound of other parts of neck ); S11.9 (Open wound of neck, part unspecified )
