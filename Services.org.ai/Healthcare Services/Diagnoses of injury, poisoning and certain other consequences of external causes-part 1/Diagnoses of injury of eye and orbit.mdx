# Diagnoses of injury of eye and orbit

**UNSPSC Code**: 85421500
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 1](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 1.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of injury of eye and orbit. Injury of eye and orbit is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code S05. ICD-10 also has The following Injury of eye and orbit children in its four-character category: S05.0 (Injury of conjunctiva and corneal abrasion without mention of foreign body ); S05.1 (Contusion of eyeball and orbital tissues ); S05.2 (Ocular laceration and rupture with prolapse or loss of intraocular tissue ); S05.3 (Ocular laceration without prolapse or loss of intraocular tissue ); S05.4 (Penetrating wound of orbit with or without foreign body ); S05.5 (Penetrating wound of eyeball with foreign body ); S05.6 (Penetrating wound of eyeball without foreign body ); S05.7 (Avulsion of eye ); S05.8 (Other injuries of eye and orbit ); S05.9 (Injury of eye and orbit, unspecified )
