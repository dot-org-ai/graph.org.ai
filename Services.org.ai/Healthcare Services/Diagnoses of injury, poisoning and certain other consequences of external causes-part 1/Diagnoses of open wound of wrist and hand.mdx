# Diagnoses of open wound of wrist and hand

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of open wound of wrist and hand. Open wound of wrist and hand is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code S61. ICD-10 also has The following Open wound of wrist and hand children in its four-character category: S61.0 (Open wound of finger(s) without damage to nail ); S61.1 (Open wound of finger(s) with damage to nail ); S61.7 (Multiple open wounds of wrist and hand ); S61.8 (Open wound of other parts of wrist and hand ); S61.9 (Open wound of wrist and hand, part unspecified )
