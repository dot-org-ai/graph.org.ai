In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of injury of heart. Injury of heart is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code S26. ICD-10 also has The following Injury of heart children in its four-character category: S26.0 (Injury of heart with haemopericardium ); S26.8 (Other injuries of heart ); S26.9 (Injury of heart, unspecified )
