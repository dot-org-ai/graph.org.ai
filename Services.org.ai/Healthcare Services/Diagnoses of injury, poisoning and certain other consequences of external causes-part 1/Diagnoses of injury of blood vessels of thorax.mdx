In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of injury of blood vessels of thorax. Injury of blood vessels of thorax is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code S25.
