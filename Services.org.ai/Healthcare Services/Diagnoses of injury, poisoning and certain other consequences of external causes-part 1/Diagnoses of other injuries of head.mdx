# Diagnoses of other injuries of head

**UNSPSC Code**: 85421900
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 1](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 1.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of other and unspecified injuries of head. Other and unspecified injuries of head is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code S09. ICD-10 also has The following Other and unspecified injuries of head children in its four-character category: S09.0 (Injury of blood vessels of head, not elsewhere classified ); S09.1 (Injury of muscle and tendon of head ); S09.2 (Traumatic rupture of ear drum ); S09.7 (Multiple injuries of head ); S09.8 (Other specified injuries of head ); S09.9 (Unspecified injury of head ); S10.0 (Contusion of throat ); S10.1 (Other and unspecified superficial injuries of throat ); S10.7 (Multiple superficial injuries of neck ); S10.8 (Superficial injury of other parts of neck ); S10.9 (Superficial injury of neck, part unspecified )
