# Diagnoses of intracranial injury

**UNSPSC Code**: 85421600
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 1](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 1.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of intracranial injury. Intracranial injury is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code S06. ICD-10 also has The following Intracranial injury children in its four-character category: S06.0 (Concussion ); S06.1 (Traumatic cerebral oedema ); S06.2 (Diffuse brain injury ); S06.3 (Focal brain injury ); S06.4 (Epidural haemorrhage ); S06.5 (Traumatic subdural haemorrhage ); S06.6 (Traumatic subarachnoid haemorrhage ); S06.7 (Intracranial injury with prolonged coma ); S06.8 (Other intracranial injuries ); S06.9 (Intracranial injury, unspecified )
