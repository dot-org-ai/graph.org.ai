# Diagnoses of open wound of head

**UNSPSC Code**: 85421100
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 1](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 1.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of open wound of head. Open wound of head is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code S01. ICD-10 also has The following Open wound of head children in its four-character category: S01.0 (Open wound of scalp ); S01.1 (Open wound of eyelid and periocular area ); S01.2 (Open wound of nose ); S01.3 (Open wound of ear ); S01.4 (Open wound of cheek and temporomandibular area ); S01.5 (Open wound of lip and oral cavity ); S01.7 (Multiple open wounds of head ); S01.8 (Open wound of other parts of head ); S01.9 (Open wound of head, part unspecified )
