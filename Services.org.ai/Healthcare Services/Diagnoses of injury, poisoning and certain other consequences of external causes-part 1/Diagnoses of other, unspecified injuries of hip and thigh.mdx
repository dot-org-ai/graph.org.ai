# Diagnoses of other, unspecified injuries of hip and thigh

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of other and unspecified injuries of hip and thigh. Other and unspecified injuries of hip and thigh is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code S79.
