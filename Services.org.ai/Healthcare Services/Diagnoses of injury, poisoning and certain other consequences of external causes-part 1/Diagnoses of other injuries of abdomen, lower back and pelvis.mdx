# Diagnoses of other injuries of abdomen, lower back and pelvis

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of other and unspecified injuries of abdomen, lower back and pelvis. Other and unspecified injuries of abdomen, lower back and pelvis is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code S39. ICD-10 also has The following Other and unspecified injuries of abdomen, lower back and pelvis children in its four-character category: S39.0 (Injury of muscle and tendon of abdomen, lower back and pelvis ); S39.6 (Injury of intra-abdominal organ(s) with pelvic organ(s) ); S39.7 (Other multiple injuries of abdomen, lower back and pelvis ); S39.8 (Other specified injuries of abdomen, lower back and pelvis ); S39.9 (Unspecified injury of abdomen, lower back and pelvis ); S40.0 (Contusion of shoulder and upper arm ); S40.7 (Multiple superficial injuries of shoulder and upper arm ); S40.8 (Other superficial injuries of shoulder and upper arm ); S40.9 (Superficial injury of shoulder and upper arm, unspecified )
