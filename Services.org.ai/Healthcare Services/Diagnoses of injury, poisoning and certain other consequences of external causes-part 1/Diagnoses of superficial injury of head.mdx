# Diagnoses of superficial injury of head

**UNSPSC Code**: 85421000
**Family**: [Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 1](Diagnoses of injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes-part 1.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of superficial injury of head. Superficial injury of head is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code S00. ICD-10 also has The following Superficial injury of head children in its four-character category: S00.0 (Superficial injury of scalp ); S00.1 (Contusion of eyelid and periocular area ); S00.2 (Other superficial injuries of eyelid and periocular area ); S00.3 (Superficial injury of nose ); S00.4 (Superficial injury of ear ); S00.5 (Superficial injury of lip and oral cavity ); S00.7 (Multiple superficial injuries of head ); S00.8 (Superficial injury of other parts of head ); S00.9 (Superficial injury of head, part unspecified )
