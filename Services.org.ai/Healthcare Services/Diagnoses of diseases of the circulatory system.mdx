# Diagnoses of diseases of the circulatory system

**UNSPSC Code**: 85310000
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](Healthcare Services.mdx)

This classification denotes the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of Diseases of the Circulatory System. Diseases of the Circulatory System is listed in The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems or ICD-10 Under Chapter IX.
