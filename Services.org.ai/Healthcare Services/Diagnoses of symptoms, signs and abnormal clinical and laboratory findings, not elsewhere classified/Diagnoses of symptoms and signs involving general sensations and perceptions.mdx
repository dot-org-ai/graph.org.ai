# Diagnoses of symptoms and signs involving general sensations and perceptions

**UNSPSC Code**: 85413500
**Family**: [Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified](Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of other symptoms and signs involving general sensations and perceptions. Other symptoms and signs involving general sensations and perceptions is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code R44. ICD-10 also has The following Other symptoms and signs involving general sensations and perceptions children in its four-character category: R44.0 (Auditory hallucinations); R44.1 (Visual hallucinations); R44.2 (Other hallucinations); R44.3 (Hallucinations, unspecified); R44.8 (Other and unspecified symptoms and signs involving general sensations and perceptions)
