# Diagnoses of conditions causing skin changes

**UNSPSC Code**: 85412600
**Family**: [Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified](Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of other skin changes. Other skin changes is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code R23. ICD-10 also has The following Other skin changes children in its four-character category: R23.0 (Cyanosis); R23.1 (Pallor); R23.2 (Flushing); R23.3 (Spontaneous ecchymoses); R23.4 (Changes in skin texture); R23.8 (Other and unspecified skin changes); R25.0 (Abnormal head movements); R25.1 (Tremor, unspecified); R25.2 (Cramp and spasm); R25.3 (Fasciculation); R25.8 (Other and unspecified abnormal involuntary movements)
