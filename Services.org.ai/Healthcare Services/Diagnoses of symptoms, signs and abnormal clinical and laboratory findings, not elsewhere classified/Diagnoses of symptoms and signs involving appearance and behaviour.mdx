In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of symptoms and signs involving appearance and behaviour. Symptoms and signs involving appearance and behaviour is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code R46.
