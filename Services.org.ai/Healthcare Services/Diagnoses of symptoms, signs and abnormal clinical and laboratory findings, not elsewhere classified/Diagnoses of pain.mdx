# Diagnoses of pain

**UNSPSC Code**: 85414000
**Family**: [Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified](Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of pain, not elsewhere classified. Pain, not elsewhere classified is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code R52. ICD-10 also has The following Pain, not elsewhere classified children in its four-character category: R52.0 (Acute pain); R52.1 (Chronic intractable pain); R52.2 (Other chronic pain); R52.9 (Pain, unspecified)
