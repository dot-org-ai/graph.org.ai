# Diagnoses of disturbances of smell and taste

**UNSPSC Code**: 85413400
**Family**: [Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified](Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of disturbances of smell and taste. Disturbances of smell and taste is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code R43. ICD-10 also has The following Disturbances of smell and taste children in its four-character category: R43.0 (Anosmia); R43.1 (Parosmia); R43.2 (Parageusia); R43.8 (Other and unspecified disturbances of smell and taste)
