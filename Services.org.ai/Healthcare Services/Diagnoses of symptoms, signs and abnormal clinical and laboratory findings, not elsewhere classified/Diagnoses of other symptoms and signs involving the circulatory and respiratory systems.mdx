# Diagnoses of other symptoms and signs involving the circulatory and respiratory systems

**UNSPSC Code**: 85412100
**Family**: [Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified](Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of other symptoms and signs involving the circulatory and respiratory systems. Other symptoms and signs involving the circulatory and respiratory systems is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code R09. ICD-10 also has The following Other symptoms and signs involving the circulatory and respiratory systems children in its four-character category: R09.0 (Asphyxia); R09.1 (Pleurisy); R09.2 (Respiratory arrest); R09.3 (Abnormal sputum); R09.8 (Other specified symptoms and signs involving the circulatory and respiratory systems); R10.0 (Acute abdomen); R10.1 (Pain localized to upper abdomen); R10.2 (Pelvic and perineal pain); R10.3 (Pain localized to other parts of lower abdomen); R10.4 (Other and unspecified abdominal pain)
