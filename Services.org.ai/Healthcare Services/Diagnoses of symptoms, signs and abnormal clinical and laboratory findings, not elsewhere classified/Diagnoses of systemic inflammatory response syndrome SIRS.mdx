# Diagnoses of systemic inflammatory response syndrome SIRS

**UNSPSC Code**: 85414800
**Family**: [Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified](Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of systemic Inflammatory Response Syndrome [SIRS]. Systemic Inflammatory Response Syndrome [SIRS] is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code R65. ICD-10 also has The following Systemic Inflammatory Response Syndrome [SIRS] children in its four-character category: R65.0 (Systemic Inflammatory Response Syndrome of infectious origin without organ failure); R65.1 (Systemic Inflammatory Response Syndrome of infectious origin with organ failure); R65.2 (Systemic Inflammatory Response Syndrome of non-infectious origin without organ failure); R65.3 (Systemic Inflammatory Response Syndrome of non-infectious origin with organ failure); R65.9 (Systemic Inflammatory Response Syndrome, unspecified)
