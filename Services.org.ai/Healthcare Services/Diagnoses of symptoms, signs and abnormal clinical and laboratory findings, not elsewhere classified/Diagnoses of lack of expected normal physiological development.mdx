# Diagnoses of lack of expected normal physiological development

**UNSPSC Code**: 85414600
**Family**: [Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified](Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of lack of expected normal physiological development. Lack of expected normal physiological development is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code R62. ICD-10 also has The following Lack of expected normal physiological development children in its four-character category: R62.0 (Delayed milestone); R62.8 (Other lack of expected normal physiological development); R62.9 (Lack of expected normal physiological development, unspecified)
