# Diagnoses of sudden death

**UNSPSC Code**: 85416600
**Family**: [Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified](Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of other sudden death, cause unknown. Other sudden death, cause unknown is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code R96. ICD-10 also has The Following Other sudden death, cause unknown children in its four-character category: R96.0 (Instantaneous death); R96.1 (Death occurring less than 24 hours from onset of symptoms, not otherwise explained)
