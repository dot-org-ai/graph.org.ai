# Diagnoses of abnormal findings of blood chemistry

**UNSPSC Code**: 85415600
**Family**: [Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified](Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of other abnormal findings of blood chemistry. Other abnormal findings of blood chemistry is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code R79. ICD-10 also has The following Other abnormal findings of blood chemistry children in its four-character category: R79.0 (Abnormal level of blood mineral); R79.8 (Other specified abnormal findings of blood chemistry); R79.9 (Abnormal finding of blood chemistry, unspecified)
