# Diagnoses of hyperhidrosis

**UNSPSC Code**: 85414500
**Family**: [Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified](Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of hyperhidrosis. Hyperhidrosis is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code R61. ICD-10 also has The following Hyperhidrosis children in its four-character category: R61.0 (Localized hyperhidrosis); R61.1 (Generalized hyperhidrosis); R61.9 (Hyperhidrosis, unspecified)
