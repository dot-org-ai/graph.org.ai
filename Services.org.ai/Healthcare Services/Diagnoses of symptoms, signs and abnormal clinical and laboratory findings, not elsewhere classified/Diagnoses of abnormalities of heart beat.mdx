# Diagnoses of abnormalities of heart beat

**UNSPSC Code**: 85411500
**Family**: [Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified](Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of abnormalities of heart beat. Abnormalities of heart beat is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code R00. ICD-10 also has The following Abnormalities of heart beat children in its four-character category: R00.0 (Tachycardia, unspecified); R00.1 (Bradycardia, unspecified); R00.2 (Palpitations); R00.8 (Other and unspecified abnormalities of heart beat)
