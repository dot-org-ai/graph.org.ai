# Diagnoses of symptoms and signs involving the urinary system

**UNSPSC Code**: 85413100
**Family**: [Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified](Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of other symptoms and signs involving the urinary system. Other symptoms and signs involving the urinary system is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code R39. ICD-10 also has The following Other symptoms and signs involving the urinary system children in its four-character category: R39.0 (Extravasation of urine); R39.1 (Other difficulties with micturition); R39.2 (Extrarenal uraemia); R39.8 (Other and unspecified symptoms and signs involving the urinary system); R40.0 (Somnolence); R40.1 (Stupor); R40.2 (Coma, unspecified)
