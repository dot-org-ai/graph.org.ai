# Diagnoses of symptoms and signs involving the nervous and musculoskeletal systems

**UNSPSC Code**: 85413000
**Family**: [Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified](Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of other symptoms and signs involving the nervous and musculoskeletal systems. Other symptoms and signs involving the nervous and musculoskeletal systems is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code R29. ICD-10 also has The following Other symptoms and signs involving the nervous and musculoskeletal systems children in its four-character category: R29.0 (Tetany); R29.1 (Meningismus); R29.2 (Abnormal reflex); R29.3 (Abnormal posture); R29.4 (Clicking hip); R29.6 (Tendency to fall, not elsewhere classified); R29.8 (Other and unspecified symptoms and signs involving the nervous and musculoskeletal systems); R30.0 (Dysuria); R30.1 (Vesical tenesmus); R30.9 (Painful micturition, unspecified)
