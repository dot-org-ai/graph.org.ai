# Diagnoses of abnormalities of plasma proteins

**Family**: [Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified](Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of other abnormalities of plasma proteins. Other abnormalities of plasma proteins is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code R77. ICD-10 also has The following Other abnormalities of plasma proteins children in its four-character category: R77.0 (Abnormality of albumin); R77.1 (Abnormality of globulin); R77.2 (Abnormality of alphafetoprotein); R77.8 (Other specified abnormalities of plasma proteins); R77.9 (Abnormality of plasma protein, unspecified)
