# Diagnoses of symptoms and signs concerning food and fluid intake

**UNSPSC Code**: 85414700
**Family**: [Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified](Diagnoses of symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified.mdx)
**Segment**: [Healthcare Services](../Healthcare Services.mdx)

In this classification, this covers the group of activities that yields a diagnosis of symptoms and signs concerning food and fluid intake. Symptoms and signs concerning food and fluid intake is listed In The World Health Organization's International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD-10) as a four-character category under code R63. ICD-10 also has The following Symptoms and signs concerning food and fluid intake children in its four-character category: R63.0 (Anorexia); R63.1 (Polydipsia); R63.2 (Polyphagia); R63.3 (Feeding difficulties and mismanagement); R63.4 (Abnormal weight loss); R63.5 (Abnormal weight gain); R63.6 (Insufficient intake of food and water due to self neglect); R63.8 (Other symptoms and signs concerning food and fluid intake)
