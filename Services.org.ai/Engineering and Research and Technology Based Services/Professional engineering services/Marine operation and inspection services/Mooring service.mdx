# Mooring service

**UNSPSC Code**: 81103004
**Class**: [Marine operation and inspection services](Marine operation and inspection services.mdx)
**Family**: [Professional engineering services](../Professional engineering services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Engineering and Research and Technology Based Services](../../Engineering and Research and Technology Based Services.mdx)

A service involving the securing of a vessel to any permanent structure such as quays, wharfs, jetties, piers, anchor buoys, and mooring buoys. A ship is secured to a mooring to forestall free movement of the ship on the water..
