# Geomatics engineering

**UNSPSC Code**: 81103300
**Family**: [Professional engineering services](Professional engineering services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Engineering and Research and Technology Based Services](../Engineering and Research and Technology Based Services.mdx)

The ISO/TC 211 series of standards defines geomatics as the "discipline of collecting, storing, analyzing, processing, and presenting geographical data". In other words, it is a set of products, services and tools for collecting, integrating, and managing geographic (geospatial) data. Also known as geomatic(s) engineering (geodesy and geoinformatics engineering or geospatial engineering), it is also known as geomatic(s) engineering. Geomatic(s) engineering was previously known as surveying engineering. Geomatics engineers collect, develop, and analyze spatial information about land, oceans, natural resources, and manmade infrastructure.
