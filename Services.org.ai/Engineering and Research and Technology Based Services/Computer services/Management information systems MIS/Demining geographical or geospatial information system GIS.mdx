# Demining geographical or geospatial information system GIS

**UNSPSC Code**: 81111709
**Class**: [Management information systems MIS](Management information systems MIS.mdx)
**Family**: [Computer services](../Computer services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Engineering and Research and Technology Based Services](../../Engineering and Research and Technology Based Services.mdx)

An organized collection of computer hardware, software, geographic data, and personnel designed to efficiently capture, store, update, manipulate, analyze, and display all forms of geographically referenced information related to a demining operation. Note: GIS allows a user to graphically view multiple layers of data based on their geographic distribution and association. GIS incorporates powerful tools to analyze the relationships between various layers of information.
