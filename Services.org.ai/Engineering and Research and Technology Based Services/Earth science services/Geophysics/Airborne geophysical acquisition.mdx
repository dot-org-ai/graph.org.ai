# Airborne geophysical acquisition

**UNSPSC Code**: 81151910
**Class**: [Geophysics](Geophysics.mdx)
**Family**: [Earth science services](../Earth science services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Engineering and Research and Technology Based Services](../../Engineering and Research and Technology Based Services.mdx)

Airborne geophysical acquisition is a process of measuring the variation of several key physical or geochemical parameters of the earth. The most important parameters measured are conductivity, magnetic susceptibility, rock density, radioactive element concentration, and reflectance spectra.
