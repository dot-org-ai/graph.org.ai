# Extended stay services

**UNSPSC Code**: 90111903
**Class**: [Specialized accommodation services](Specialized accommodation services.mdx)
**Family**: [Hotels and lodging and meeting facilities](../Hotels and lodging and meeting facilities.mdx)
**Segment**: [Travel and Food and Lodging and Entertainment Services](../../Travel and Food and Lodging and Entertainment Services.mdx)

Accommodation, apartment, and extended stay service mean the provision of accommodation, typically on a daily or weekly basis, principally for a short stay of tourists provided by different types of establishments, including but not limited to hotels, motels, pensions, bed and breakfast services, hostels, commercially marketed private accommodations, etc. This definition excludes the provision of homes and furnished or unfurnished flats or apartments for more permanent use, typically on a monthly or annual basis, unless offered for tourism purposes.
