# Animal carcass disposal site restoration service

**UNSPSC Code**: 77111604
**Class**: [Environmental rehabilitation](Environmental rehabilitation.mdx)
**Family**: [Environmental protection](../Environmental protection.mdx)
**Segment**: [Environmental Services](../../Environmental Services.mdx)

A service to dig up buried areas where animals infected with livestock infectious diseases such as foot-and-mouth disease and avian influenza, or the bodies of animals killed prophylactically, are recovered after a certain period of time and restored to the environment before they were buried.
