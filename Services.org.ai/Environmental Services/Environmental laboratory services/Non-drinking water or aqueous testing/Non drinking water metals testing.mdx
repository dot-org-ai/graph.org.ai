# Non drinking water metals testing

**UNSPSC Code**: 77141602
**Class**: [Non-drinking water or aqueous testing](Non-drinking water or aqueous testing.mdx)
**Family**: [Environmental laboratory services](../Environmental laboratory services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Environmental Services](../../Environmental Services.mdx)

Non-potable water metals testing by ICP to include, but not limited to As, Ag, Al, Ba, Ca, Cd, Cr, Cu, Pb, Mo, Hg, Se,Mg, K, Na, Zn, V, Ti, Tl, Sr, etc. Testing is performed by a NELAC / VELAP certified laboratory by EPA approved methodology.
