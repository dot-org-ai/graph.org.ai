# Telecom shelter

**UNSPSC Code**: 95131607
**Class**: [Portable commercial and industrial buildings and structures](Portable commercial and industrial buildings and structures.mdx)
**Family**: [Portable buildings and structures](../Portable buildings and structures.mdx)
**Segment**: [Land and Buildings and Structures and Thoroughfares](../../Land and Buildings and Structures and Thoroughfares.mdx)

A telecom shelter protects ground-based telecommunications equipment, the structure is built from precast concrete or lightweight steel for longevity, roof top and green field shelters or cabinets for mobile base stations and telecommunication applications. Protects against water, high-speed winds generated by storms, fire damage, and unauthorized access.
