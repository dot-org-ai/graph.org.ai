# Public changing room and shower facility

**UNSPSC Code**: 95121714
**Class**: [Public buildings and structures](Public buildings and structures.mdx)
**Family**: [Permanent buildings and structures](../Permanent buildings and structures.mdx)
**Segment**: [Land and Buildings and Structures and Thoroughfares](../../Land and Buildings and Structures and Thoroughfares.mdx)

These are local government or publicly owned and maintained facilities for public use. They are most frequently near beaches and lakes. They have showers and toilets within the same facility and enable people to change from casual clothes into swim wear, swim, shower and change back into casual clothes.
