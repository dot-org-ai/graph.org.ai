# Cuttings re-injection service

**UNSPSC Code**: 71161112

**Family**: [Oil and gas well project management services](../Oil and gas well project management services.mdx)

Cuttings Re-Injection (CRI) involves injection of waste from drilling operations (slop and drill cuttings) into a suitable deep geologic formation. Injection will typically require pressures that result in hydraulic fracturing in the formation.
