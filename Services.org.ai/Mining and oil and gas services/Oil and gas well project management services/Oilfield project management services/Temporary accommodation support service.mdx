# Temporary accommodation support service

**UNSPSC Code**: 71161309

Temporary accommodation support means short-term, temporary or transient accommodation such as a hotel, motel, bed and breakfast or boarding house. Also provides an intensive support service to households placed or facilitated into any form of Temporary Accommodation or Private Rented accommodation and is delivered to support those clients to enable them to sustain their accommodation.
