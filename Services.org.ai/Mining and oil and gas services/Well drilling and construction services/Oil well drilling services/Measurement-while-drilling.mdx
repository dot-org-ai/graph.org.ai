# Measurement-while-drilling

**UNSPSC Code**: 71121656
**Class**: [Oil well drilling services](Oil well drilling services.mdx)
**Family**: [Well drilling and construction services](../Well drilling and construction services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Mining and oil and gas services](../../Mining and oil and gas services.mdx)

Measurement-While-Drilling (or MWD) is a type of well logging that incorporates the measurement tools into the drill string and provides real-time information to help with steering the drill. Service provides downhole evaluation of formation gamma ray, resistivity, and porosity while drilling is in progress. Mechanical parameters such as the following are also measured and recorded for well surveillance Well deviation and azimuth, Rate of penetration (ROP) ,Weight and torque on bit.
