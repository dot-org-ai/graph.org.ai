Frac pumps are diesel-engine-powered fracturing equipment used in hydraulic fracturing of shale rock formations to extract the trapped oil and gas. Frac pumps that can pump fracking fluid down the wellbore (water, silica sand, and fracking chemicals) are in high demand.
