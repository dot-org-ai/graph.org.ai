# Hydrocarbon allocation management and consultancy services

**UNSPSC Code**: 71151500
**Family**: [Oil and gas data management and processing services](Oil and gas data management and processing services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Mining and oil and gas services](../Mining and oil and gas services.mdx)

Management and consultancy services for hydrocarbon allocation, which is the process of measuring and determining the ownership of units of hydrocarbons, from the point of extraction all the way through to the processing refinery. This relates to the value of payments allocated to each operator whose wells have contributed to the hydrocarbons being processed at the refinery.
