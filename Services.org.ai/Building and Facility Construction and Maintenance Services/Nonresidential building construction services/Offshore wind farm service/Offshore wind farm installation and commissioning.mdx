# Offshore wind farm installation and commissioning

**UNSPSC Code**: 72121604
**Class**: [Offshore wind farm service](Offshore wind farm service.mdx)
**Family**: [Nonresidential building construction services](../Nonresidential building construction services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Building and Facility Construction and Maintenance Services](../../Building and Facility Construction and Maintenance Services.mdx)

All installation and commissioning of balance of plant and turbines, including land- and sea-based activity. For offshore activities, the process starts by transporting components from the nearest port to manufacture to either the construction port or straight to site. Activities are complete at the wind farm construction works completion date, where assets are handed over to operational teams.
