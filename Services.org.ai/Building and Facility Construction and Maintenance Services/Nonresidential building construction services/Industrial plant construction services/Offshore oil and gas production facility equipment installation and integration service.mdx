# Offshore oil and gas production facility equipment installation and integration service

**UNSPSC Code**: 72121512
**Class**: [Industrial plant construction services](Industrial plant construction services.mdx)
**Family**: [Nonresidential building construction services](../Nonresidential building construction services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Building and Facility Construction and Maintenance Services](../../Building and Facility Construction and Maintenance Services.mdx)

Service to mechanically install offshore oil and gas production facilities including piles, anchors, gravity based structures, mooring systems, jackets, towers, hulls, production or storage vessels, topsides, well head platforms, living quarter modules, bridges, and flare booms. Includes quayside integration of top sides to a hull.
