# Oil and gas plant Insulation and heat tracing

**UNSPSC Code**: 72154071
**Class**: [Specialty building and trades services](Specialty building and trades services.mdx)
**Family**: [Specialized trade construction and maintenance services](../Specialized trade construction and maintenance services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Building and Facility Construction and Maintenance Services](../../Building and Facility Construction and Maintenance Services.mdx)

Heat tracing Insulation is also called Trace Heating. Insulation and heat tracing are essential techniques used in oil and gas plants to maintain process temperatures, conserve energy, and reduce the risk of equipment failure caused by thermal stress or freezing. A service which gives a solution of controlling pipe temperature. In this system electric heating element run along the pipe length, mostly coupled with insulation, to facilitate even heating.
