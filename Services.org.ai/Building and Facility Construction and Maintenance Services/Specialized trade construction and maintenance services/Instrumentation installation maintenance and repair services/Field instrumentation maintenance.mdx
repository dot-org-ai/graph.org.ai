# Field instrumentation maintenance

**UNSPSC Code**: 72154202
**Class**: [Instrumentation installation maintenance and repair services](Instrumentation installation maintenance and repair services.mdx)
**Family**: [Specialized trade construction and maintenance services](../Specialized trade construction and maintenance services.mdx)
**Segment**: [Building and Facility Construction and Maintenance Services](../../Building and Facility Construction and Maintenance Services.mdx)

A service for calibration, predictive maintenance (vibration, infrared, motor test) and computerized uniformity surveys. The prompt maintenance of redundant components assures reliability, minimizing downtime. Testing, troubleshooting, repairing, or replacing a component or subsystem in parallel with redundant components or subsystems is known as concurrent maintenance.
