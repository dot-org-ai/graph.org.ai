# Digital event service

**UNSPSC Code**: 80141904
**Class**: [Trade shows and exhibits](Trade shows and exhibits.mdx)
**Family**: [Marketing and distribution](../Marketing and distribution.mdx)
**Segment**: [Management and Business Professionals and Administrative Services](../../Management and Business Professionals and Administrative Services.mdx)

A Digital Event is an occurrence of people gathering together where some or all of the attendees are not physically in the same location but are connected in a common environment. The common environment might be one of many types but is usually enabled through the use of computers and the Internet. Digital Event Services are used specifically throughout a live physical, virtual and or when engaging with an attendee using Webcasting, Webinars, Virtual & Live Streaming, Mobile Event apps, Registration, Check-in, on site Lead Retrieval, Technology to Track Attendance, etc  These digital channels and technology are specific to the events space.
