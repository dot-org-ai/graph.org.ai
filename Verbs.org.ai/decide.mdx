# Decide

Making a choice or determination between alternatives, courses of action, or options based on analysis, judgment, authority, or prescribed criteria.

## O*NET Context

Work activity involving the evaluation of options and selection of courses of action. Common in management, leadership, professional judgment roles, and any position requiring decision-making authority.

## Semantic Forms

## Usage Examples

**Management & Leadership:**
- Manager decides on hiring between candidates
- Executive decides on company acquisition or divestment
- Director decides on resource allocation

**Professional Services:**
- Judge decides on legal case outcome
- Doctor decides on treatment plan
- Engineer decides on design specifications

**Business Rules:**
- Decisions require appropriate authority level
- Criteria must be documented before deciding
- Major decisions may require consultation or approval
- Decision reversibility affects decision-making process

## Related Verbs
