# Contract

Establishing formal binding agreements between an organization and external parties (suppliers, vendors, service providers) that define terms, conditions, and obligations.

## APQC Process Context

Procurement and vendor management activity where organizations negotiate, document, and execute legal agreements that specify scope, pricing, terms, and performance expectations.

## APQC Process Category

Category 2: Source/Procure
- Subcategory: Develop supplier relationships
- Process: Negotiate supplier contracts

## Business Rules

- Contract must be approved by legal and management
- All material terms must be documented
- Contract must comply with organizational policies
- Contract changes require formal change orders
- Contract performance must be monitored
