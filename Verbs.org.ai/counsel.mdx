# Counsel

Providing guidance, support, advice, and recommendations to individuals regarding personal development, career choices, emotional issues, or behavioral concerns through discussion and professional expertise.

## O*NET Context

Work activity involving one-on-one or group guidance sessions to help individuals understand problems, develop coping strategies, and make informed decisions. Common in counseling, social work, human resources, and educational roles.

## Usage Examples

**Mental Health & Wellness:**
- Therapist counsels patient on anxiety management
- Career counselor guides job seeker through transition
- School counselor advises student on college selection

**Employee Relations:**
- HR specialist counsels employee on performance improvement
- Manager counsels team member on workplace conduct
- EAP counselor provides confidential support

**Business Rules:**
- Counseling requires privacy and confidentiality protections
- Licensed professionals must maintain required credentials
- Session notes must comply with regulatory requirements
- Client consent required for all guidance activities
