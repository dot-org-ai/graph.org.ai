# Oversee

Supervising, monitoring, and taking responsibility for the work of others or operations to ensure quality and compliance.

## O*NET Context

Work activity focused on supervising and monitoring the work of other employees or the operations of a department, project, or function to ensure quality standards, deadlines, and objectives are met.

## Semantic Forms

```typescript

```
