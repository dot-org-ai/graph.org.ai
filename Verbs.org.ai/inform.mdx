# Inform

Communicating knowledge, facts, or information to others in order to enhance understanding or facilitate decision-making.

## O*NET Context

Work activity focused on conveying information, updates, or knowledge to colleagues, clients, or stakeholders to ensure shared understanding and informed decision-making.

## Semantic Forms

### Action (Base Form)
```typescript
await $.Team.inform({ recipients: 'all-stakeholders', content: message, priority: 'high' })
```

### Predicate (Relationship)
```typescript
$.Manager.informs.Team
$.Communication.informs.Decision
```

### Event (Past Tense)
```typescript
on($.Stakeholder.informed, async (event) => {
  await $.AuditLog.record({ action: 'informed', timestamp: event.timestamp })
  await $.Notification.acknowledge({ status: 'received' })
})
```

### Activity (Ongoing Process)
```typescript
const activeInforming = $.Manager.informing.DirectReports
console.log(`Currently informing: ${activeInforming.scope}`)
```

### Role (Actor)
```typescript

```

### Concept (Abstraction)

## Usage Examples

**Internal Communication:**
- Informing team members of policy changes
- Informing management of project status
- Informing stakeholders of risks or issues
- Providing regular briefings or updates

**External Communication:**
- Informing customers of service changes
- Informing clients of project progress
- Informing regulatory bodies of compliance status
- Sending notifications to partners

**Knowledge Transfer:**
- Informing staff of new procedures
- Briefing team on industry developments
- Sharing best practices and lessons learned
- Educating others about system changes

## Related Verbs

- explain - Making something clear or understandable
- educate - Teaching knowledge or skills
