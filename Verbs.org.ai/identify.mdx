# Identify

Recognizing, naming, or determining the nature, character, or essence of something or someone.

## O*NET Context

Critical analytical work activity across numerous occupations including scientists, diagnosticians, inspectors, and investigators. Involves observation, comparison, and classification to recognize and name entities, problems, or solutions.

## Semantic Forms

### Action (Base Form)
```typescript
await $.Diagnostic.identify({ specimen: sample, method: 'analysis' })
```

### Predicate (Relationship)
```typescript
$.Inspector.identifies.Defects

```

### Event (Past Tense)
```typescript
on($.Issue.identified, async (event) => {
  await $.Alert.send({ stakeholders: event.impactedParties })
  await $.Remediation.plan({ problem: event.issue })
```

## Usage Examples

**Scientific Research:**
- Biologists identify species from samples
- Chemists identify compounds through testing
- Geologists identify rock and mineral types

**Quality & Safety:**
- Inspectors identify defects in products
- Safety engineers identify hazards
- Auditors identify compliance issues

**Diagnosis & Investigation:**
- Doctors identify diseases from symptoms
- Mechanics identify car problems
- Investigators identify suspects or causes

**Business Rules:**
- Identification methods documented with confidence levels
- Chain of custody maintained for evidence
- Multiple confirmations for critical identifications
- Record audit trail for identification decisions
