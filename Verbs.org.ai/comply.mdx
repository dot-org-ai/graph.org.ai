# Comply

Acting in accordance with applicable laws, regulations, standards, and organizational policies to ensure legal and regulatory conformity.

## APQC Process Context

Governance and risk management activity where organizations ensure that all operations, processes, and decisions conform to legal, regulatory, and internal policy requirements.

## APQC Process Category

Category 1: Plan & Organize
- Subcategory: Develop and manage enterprise compliance
- Process: Manage regulatory compliance

## Business Rules

- Compliance requirements must be identified and documented
- Compliance must be continuously monitored and verified
- Non-compliance triggers corrective action
- All employees must understand applicable requirements
- Compliance documentation must be maintained
