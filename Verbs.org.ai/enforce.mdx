# Enforce

Compelling or requiring adherence to rules, standards, regulations, or requirements through authority or action.

## O*NET Context

Work activity involving ensuring compliance with policies, procedures, regulations, or safety standards. Common in supervisory, security, legal, and compliance roles where monitoring and corrective actions are required.

## Semantic Forms

### Action (Base Form)
```typescript
await $.Manager.enforce({ policy: 'attendance-policy', consequence: 'written-warning' })
await $.Security.enforce($.SiteRules, { violation: 'trespassing' })
```

## Usage Examples

**Workplace Compliance:**
- Manager enforces attendance policies
- Safety officer enforces workplace safety regulations
- HR enforces code of conduct policies
- Supervisor enforces production standards

**Legal and Regulatory:**
- Government agency enforces environmental regulations
- Court enforces contractual obligations
- Compliance officer enforces regulatory requirements

**Business Rules:**
- Enforcement requires documented violations
- Enforcement actions must follow due process
- Enforcement history should be tracked and audited
- Enforcement requires proper authority and authorization
