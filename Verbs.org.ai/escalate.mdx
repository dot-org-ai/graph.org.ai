# Escalate

Raising issues, problems, or concerns to higher authority or management levels when normal resolution processes are insufficient or decisions require senior input.

## APQC Process Context

Issue and conflict management activity where organizations route unresolved matters through hierarchy to achieve timely resolution and appropriate decision-making.

## APQC Process Category

Category 4: Enable
- Subcategory: Manage customer service and support
- Process: Resolve customer issues and complaints

## Business Rules

- Escalation requires documented justification
- Clear escalation path must be defined
- Timeline for escalation must be specified
- Senior level must be involved within SLA
- Resolution status must be communicated to originator
