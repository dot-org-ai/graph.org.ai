# Customize

Modifying or tailoring products, services, or configurations to meet specific customer requirements, preferences, or unique needs.

## APQC Process Context

Product and service delivery activity where organizations adapt standard offerings to match customer specifications while maintaining quality and efficiency.

## APQC Process Category

Category 3: Make/Deliver
- Subcategory: Manage customer service and support
- Process: Develop product specifications based on customer needs

## Business Rules

- Customization must be within product capabilities
- Specifications must be clearly documented
- Customization must not compromise quality standards
- Customization may affect pricing and delivery
- Customer approval required before execution
