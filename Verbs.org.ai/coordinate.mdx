# Coordinate

Organizing and harmonizing the activities, efforts, resources, or timing of multiple people, teams, or processes to work together effectively toward shared goals.

## O*NET Context

Work activity involving the integration and synchronization of multiple work streams, departments, or stakeholders. Common in project management, event planning, logistics, and team leadership roles.

## Semantic Forms

### Action (Base Form)
```typescript
await $.Event.coordinate({ teams: ['logistics', 'marketing', 'operations'], timeline: '6 months' })
```

## Usage Examples

**Project & Operations Management:**
- Project manager coordinates cross-functional teams
- Event coordinator synchronizes vendor activities
- Logistics coordinator aligns transportation and warehousing

**Business Rules:**
- Coordination requires clear communication channels
- All teams must acknowledge and confirm schedules
- Changes require re-synchronization with stakeholders
