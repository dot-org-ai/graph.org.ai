# Market

Promoting and advertising products or services to target customers through various channels and strategies to generate demand and brand awareness.

## APQC Process Context

Customer development and demand management activity where organizations communicate product value, build brand awareness, and drive customer acquisition through marketing programs.

Category 2: Source/Procure

## Business Rules

- Marketing must align with product strategy
- Target audience must be clearly identified
- Budget must be approved before execution
- Campaign effectiveness must be measured
- Results must inform future marketing decisions
