# Replenish

Restocking or refilling inventory levels to maintain adequate supplies for operations, sales, or customer service based on demand forecasts.

## APQC Process Context

Inventory management activity where organizations order and receive goods to maintain stock levels that support operational and customer service needs.

## APQC Process Category

Category 3: Make/Deliver
- Subcategory: Manage inventory and fulfillment
- Process: Manage inventory

## Business Rules

- Replenishment must be triggered at defined levels
- Quantity must align with demand forecasts
- Supplier must be reliable and timely
- Stock-out situations must be prevented
- Inventory records must be accurate
