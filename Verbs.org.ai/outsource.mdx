# Outsource

Transferring operations, functions, or services to external service providers or vendors to reduce costs, access specialized expertise, or focus on core competencies.

## APQC Process Context

Strategic sourcing and vendor management activity where organizations delegate specific business functions to external parties through contractual arrangements.

## Business Rules

- Outsourcing decision must be strategically justified
- Service level agreements must be clearly defined
- Transition plan must minimize disruption
- Performance monitoring must be ongoing
- Contract terms must align with business needs
