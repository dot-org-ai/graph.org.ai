# Prepare

Making ready or getting something into a suitable condition for use, action, or consumption.

## O*NET Context

Work activity focused on readying materials, documents, equipment, or people for specific purposes or operations.

## Semantic Forms

### Action (Base Form)
```typescript
await $.Document.prepare({ format: 'pdf', audience: 'stakeholders' })
```

### Predicate (Relationship)
```typescript

```

```typescript

```
