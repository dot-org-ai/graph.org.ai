# Organize

Arranging, structuring, or coordinating elements into a systematic order or coherent whole for effective management or operation.

## O*NET Context

Work activity focused on arranging, planning, and coordinating work, materials, people, or information in an orderly and efficient manner to accomplish goals and facilitate operations.

## Semantic Forms

### Action (Base Form)
```typescript
await $.Workspace.organize({ system: 'alphabetical', frequency: 'weekly' })
```

### Predicate (Relationship)
```typescript

```

### Event (Past Tense)
