# Acquire

Obtaining or purchasing goods, services, or resources through procurement and sourcing processes.

## APQC Process Context

Strategic sourcing and procurement activity where organizations identify, select, and obtain goods or services needed for operations, capital assets, or resale.

## Semantic Forms

### Action (Base Form)
```typescript
await $.Procurement.acquire({ supplier, item, quantity, terms })
```

### Predicate (Relationship)


## APQC Process Category

Category 1: Plan & Organize
- Subcategory: Develop Sourcing Strategy
- Process: Identify and select suppliers

## Business Rules

- Acquisition requires approved purchase requisition
- Must comply with procurement policies and spend limits
- Triggers accounts payable and inventory processes
- Price and terms must align with contracts

## Related Verbs
- procure - Formal purchasing process
- purchase - Commercial transaction
- contract - Establishing legal terms for acquisition
- receive - Taking delivery of acquired goods
