# Motivate

Stimulating or inspiring people to take action or achieve goals through encouragement, incentives, and recognition of effort.

## O*NET Context

Work activity focused on influencing human behavior and commitment toward organizational or personal objectives. Includes providing encouragement, recognition, incentives, and removing barriers to help individuals and teams achieve their best performance.

## Semantic Forms

### Action (Base Form)
```typescript
await $.Team.motivate({ approach: 'recognition-program', incentives: [...], goals: [...], timeframe: 'quarterly' })
```

### Predicate (Relationship)
```typescript
$.Leader.motivates.Team
$.Manager.motivates.Employee
```

### Event (Past Tense)
```typescript
on($.Person.motivated, async (event) => {
  await $.Goal.accelerate({ person: event.personId, engagement: 'high' })
  await $.Achievement.celebrate({ milestone: event.trigger, recognizedPerson: event.personId })
})
```

### Activity (Ongoing Process)
```typescript
const activeInspiration = $.Coach.motivating.Athlete
console.log(`Currently motivating: ${activeInspiration.person} - Morale: ${activeInspiration.moraleLevel}`)
```

### Role (Actor)
```typescript
const motivator: $.Motivator = await $.findAgent({ expertise: 'leadership', trait: 'inspirational' })
motivator.inspireTeam(objectives)
```

### Concept (Abstraction)
```typescript
const motivation: $.Motivation = {
  motivateeId: person.id,
  motivatorId: leader.id,
  motivationStrategy: 'intrinsic-reward',
  goalsSetTogether: [...],
  progressTracked: true,
  engagementLevel: 'high',
  resultingPerformance: 'exceeds-expectations'
}
```

### Inverse (Passive)
```typescript
const influencers = await db.related($.Person, $.motivatedBy, $.Leader)
```

## Usage Examples

**Team Leadership:**
- Manager motivates team through clear goals and recognition programs
- Team lead motivates developers through mentoring and growth opportunities
- Executive motivates organization with inspiring vision and mission
- Supervisor motivates staff through positive feedback and rewards

**Personal Development:**
- Coach motivates athlete through personalized training and encouragement
- Mentor motivates mentee to overcome challenges and achieve goals
- Teacher motivates students through engaging lessons and constructive feedback
- Therapist motivates client toward positive behavioral change

**Sales and Business:**
- Sales manager motivates sales team through incentive programs and targets
- Business owner motivates employees through profit-sharing and advancement
- Marketing director motivates campaign team with stretch goals
- Customer service manager motivates representatives through recognition

**Community and Social:**
- Community organizer motivates residents to participate in initiatives
- Non-profit director motivates volunteers through meaningful work
- Social worker motivates clients toward recovery and independence
- Volunteer coordinator motivates helpers through appreciation and impact stories
