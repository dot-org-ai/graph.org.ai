# Subcontract

Assigning portions of contracted work to secondary vendors or subcontractors while maintaining responsibility for overall delivery and quality.

## APQC Process Context

Vendor management and procurement activity where organizations delegate portions of contracted work to qualified subcontractors based on specialization or capacity needs.

## APQC Process Category

Category 2: Source/Procure
- Subcategory: Develop supplier relationships

## Business Rules

- Subcontracting must be authorized in prime contract
- Subvendor must be qualified and approved
- Primary contractor remains accountable for performance
- Service levels and terms must be defined
- Payment must be contingent on performance
