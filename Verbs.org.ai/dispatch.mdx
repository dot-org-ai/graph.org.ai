# Dispatch

Sending out or releasing orders, shipments, personnel, or vehicles to designated locations to fulfill operations or delivery requirements.

## APQC Process Context

Supply chain and logistics activity where organizations send goods, people, or resources from one location to another to meet customer or operational needs.

## APQC Process Category

Category 3: Make/Deliver
- Subcategory: Deliver products and services
- Process: Ship products and services

## Business Rules

- Dispatch requires completed order verification
- Shipment must match customer specifications
- Dispatch timing must meet customer requirements
- Tracking information must be provided
- Dispatch records must be maintained

## Related Verbs
- ship - Moving goods to destination
- deliver - Providing goods to customer
- release - Making available or sending out
- transport - Moving from one location to another
- schedule - Planning timing of dispatch
