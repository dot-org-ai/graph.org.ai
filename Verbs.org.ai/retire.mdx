# Retire

Removing assets, systems, equipment, or products from active service at the end of their useful lifecycle through structured retirement processes.

## APQC Process Context

Asset management and operational activity where organizations systematically remove, dispose of, or archive resources that have reached end-of-life or become obsolete.

## Semantic Forms

## APQC Process Category

Category 4: Enable
- Subcategory: Manage organizational resources
- Process: Manage fixed assets

## Business Rules

- Retirement must follow asset lifecycle policies
- Disposal method must comply with regulations
- Records must be updated and archived
- Any salvage or recovery value must be recorded
- Environmental and safety requirements must be met

- archive - Preserving records
- discontinue - Ending availability
