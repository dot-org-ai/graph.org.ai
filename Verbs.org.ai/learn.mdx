# Learn

Acquiring knowledge, skills, or understanding through study, experience, or instruction.

## O*NET Context

Work activity focused on obtaining new information, competencies, or expertise through formal education, training, self-study, or experience-based development. Critical for professional development and adaptation to new systems or methodologies.

## Semantic Forms

### Action (Base Form)
```typescript
await $.Employee.learn({ subject: 'new-software', duration: '40-hours', format: 'instructor-led' })
```

### Predicate (Relationship)
```typescript
$.Team.learns.NewTechnology
$.Organization.learns.BestPractices
```

### Event (Past Tense)
```typescript
on($.Professional.learned, async (event) => {
  await $.Certification.issue({ recipient: event.person, skill: event.skill })
  await $.Dashboard.update({ accomplishment: event.learningOutcome })
})
```

### Activity (Ongoing Process)
```typescript
const activeTraining = $.Developer.learning.AdvancedArchitecture
console.log(`Currently learning: ${activeTraining.module}`)
```

### Role (Actor)
```typescript
const learner: $.Learner = await $.findAgent({ status: 'in-training' })
learner.completeModule(trainingCourse)
```

### Concept (Abstraction)

## Usage Examples

**Training and Development:**
- Employee learns new software during onboarding
- Team learns industry best practices through workshops
- Manager learns leadership techniques in professional development program
- Technician learns equipment operation through certification course

**Education and Skill Building:**
- Student learns course material through lectures and assignments
- Professional learns emerging technologies through self-directed learning
- Team member learns compliance requirements during mandatory training
- Worker learns specialized skills through apprenticeship

**Organizational Learning:**
- Organization learns from customer feedback and market research
- Team learns from past projects to improve processes
- Department learns new regulatory requirements through training
- Company learns emerging trends through industry participation
