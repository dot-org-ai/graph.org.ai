# Administer

Managing the execution and control of operational processes, policies, and systems to ensure compliance and effectiveness.

## APQC Process Context

Administrative control and management activities that oversee operational processes, enforce policies, and maintain organizational standards and procedures.

## Business Rules

- Administration requires documented authority
- Must follow governance framework
- Requires audit trail and compliance documentation
- Changes require approval and communication
