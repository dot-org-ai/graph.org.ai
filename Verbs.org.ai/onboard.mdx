# Onboard

Integrating new employees, customers, suppliers, or partners into organizational systems, processes, and operations through structured orientation and setup activities.

## APQC Process Context

Human resource and relationship management activity where organizations prepare new team members or partners with necessary information, access, and training to become productive contributors.

## APQC Process Category

Category 4: Enable
- Subcategory: Manage human resources
- Process: Develop and manage workforce

## Business Rules

- Onboarding requires documented process
- All required systems access must be provisioned
- Orientation must cover key policies and procedures
- Manager must be engaged in integration
- Progress must be tracked until productivity
