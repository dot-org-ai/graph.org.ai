# Drive

To operate or control a vehicle, or to propel and cause movement; alternatively, to motivate, compel, or push forward action toward a goal or objective. This verb encompasses activities such as operating vehicles and equipment, moving cargo, motivating teams, pursuing strategic initiatives, and propelling organizational progress.
