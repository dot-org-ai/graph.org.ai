# Serve

Providing services, products, or support to customers, clients, or patrons to meet their needs, expectations, or requirements.

## O*NET Context

Work activity focused on delivering goods, services, or assistance directly to end-users or customers in a professional or commercial context.

## Semantic Forms

### Action (Base Form)
```typescript
await $.Staff.serve({ customer: client, service: 'consultation', duration: '1-hour' })
```

### Predicate (Relationship)
```typescript
$.Restaurant.serves.Food
$.Employee.serves.Customer
$.Organization.serves.Community
```

### Event (Past Tense)
```typescript
on($.Customer.served, async (event) => {
  await $.Feedback.request({ customerId: event.customer.id })
  await $.Transaction.record({ serviceType: event.service, timestamp: event.timestamp })
})
```

### Activity (Ongoing Process)
```typescript
const activeService = $.Waitstaff.serving.Table
console.log(`Currently serving ${activeService.partySize} guests`)
```

### Role (Actor)
```typescript
const server: $.Server = await $.findAgent({ role: 'customer-service-representative' })
server.attendToCustomerNeeds(client)
```

### Concept (Abstraction)
```typescript
const service: $.Service = {
  provider: organization.id,
  customer: client.id,
  type: 'consultation',
  deliveryMethod: 'in-person',
  quality: 'excellent',
  timestamp: now()
}
```

### Inverse (Passive)
```typescript
const servingOrganizations = await db.related($.Customer, $.servedBy, $.Business)
```

## Usage Examples

**Customer Service:**
- Serving customers at retail counter or checkout
- Providing telephone or email support to clients
- Delivering goods or services to patrons
- Attending to customer inquiries and requests

**Hospitality:**
- Serving food and beverages in restaurants
- Providing accommodation services in hotels
- Delivering room service and amenities
- Managing guest needs and requests

**Professional Services:**
- Serving clients as a consultant or advisor
- Providing legal or medical services
- Offering financial planning assistance
- Delivering training or educational services
