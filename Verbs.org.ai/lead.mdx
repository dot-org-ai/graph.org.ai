# Lead

Guiding, directing, or influencing individuals or groups toward achieving goals, providing vision and direction while supporting and enabling their efforts.

## O*NET Context

Work activity focused on taking charge of a group or initiative, establishing direction, motivating others, making decisions, and accepting responsibility for outcomes.

## Semantic Forms

```typescript
await $.Manager.lead({ team: group, direction: vision, duration: '12-months' })
```

- Supervising direct reports

- Leading business growth efforts
- Providing leadership in new markets

**Operational Leadership:**
- Leading daily operations
- Leading meetings and discussions
- Leading process improvements
- Guiding team decision-making
