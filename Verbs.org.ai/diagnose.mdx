# Diagnose

To identify the nature, cause, or underlying factors of a problem, disease, malfunction, or condition through systematic examination, analysis, and investigation. This verb encompasses activities such as medical diagnosis, troubleshooting technical systems, analyzing business problems, and determining root causes of issues.
