# Draft

To prepare a preliminary, initial, or rough version of a document, plan, design, or proposal. This verb encompasses activities such as writing initial versions of reports, preparing design sketches, creating working documents, outlining plans, and developing preliminary versions intended for review, refinement, and finalization.
