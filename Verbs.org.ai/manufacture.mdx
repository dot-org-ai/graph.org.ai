# Manufacture

Producing goods through the transformation of raw materials, components, or parts into finished products using labor, machinery, and processes.

## APQC Process Context

Production process where organizations convert inputs through defined manufacturing processes to create products meeting quality and specification standards.

## APQC Process Category

Category 3: Make/Deliver
- Subcategory: Produce and deliver products
- Process: Produce/manufacture products

## Business Rules

- Manufacturing must follow approved specifications
- Quality standards must be met during production
- Production must be scheduled and planned
- Raw materials must be available and verified
- Finished goods must be inspected before release
