# Audit

Examining and verifying organizational processes, records, and systems for compliance with standards, regulations, and internal policies.

## APQC Process Context

Independent evaluation and assessment of processes, controls, and compliance to provide assurance and identify improvement opportunities.

## APQC Process Category

Category 1: Plan & Organize
- Subcategory: Develop and manage enterprise risk management
- Process: Conduct compliance and risk assessments

## Business Rules

- Audit requires documented standards and criteria
- Auditor must be independent from area being audited
- Findings require formal documentation and reporting
- Management must respond to recommendations

## Related Verbs
- assess - Evaluating performance or capability
- inspect - Examining physical items or processes
- verify - Confirming accuracy or compliance
- review - Examining for quality or correctness
- control - Enforcing standards and procedures
