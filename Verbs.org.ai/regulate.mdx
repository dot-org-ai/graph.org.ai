# Regulate

Controlling, governing, or maintaining something within specified parameters through the application of rules, standards, or enforcement mechanisms.

## O*NET Context

Work activity focused on ensuring compliance with established rules, standards, or parameters by monitoring, controlling, or adjusting systems, processes, or behaviors to maintain desired conditions and prevent deviations.

## Semantic Forms

### Action (Base Form)
```typescript
await $.EnvironmentalAgency.regulate({
  sector: 'manufacturing',
  standard: 'emissions-limits',
  enforcementLevel: 'strict'
})
```

### Predicate (Relationship)

### Event (Past Tense)
```typescript
on($.Business.regulated, async (event) => {
  await $.Compliance.verify({ businessId: event.id })

```

## Usage Examples

**Environmental Regulation:**
- Regulating emissions from industrial facilities
- Controlling water quality and pollution standards
- Enforcing environmental protection standards
- Managing hazardous waste handling requirements

**Financial Regulation:**
- Regulating banking and lending practices
- Enforcing consumer protection standards
- Controlling financial market operations
- Managing insurance industry compliance

**Professional Regulation:**
- Regulating medical and healthcare practitioners
- Enforcing professional licensing standards
- Controlling professional conduct and ethics
- Managing disciplinary procedures
