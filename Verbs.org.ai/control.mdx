# Control

Managing, regulating, or directing the operation or behavior of systems, equipment, processes, or personnel to maintain desired standards or outcomes.

## O*NET Context

Work activity involving the operation and management of systems, machinery, or processes to ensure they function according to specifications. Common in manufacturing, utilities, transportation, and operational management roles.

## Semantic Forms

### Action (Base Form)
```typescript
await $.ProductionLine.control({ settings: 'standard', monitoring: true })
```

## Usage Examples

**Manufacturing & Operations:**
- Operator controls manufacturing equipment settings
- Technician controls airflow in HVAC system
- Manager controls project timeline and resources

**Business Rules:**
- Control requires proper certification or training
- Parameters must stay within tolerance ranges
- Control changes must be documented and logged

## Related Verbs

- manage - Directing or overseeing activities
- monitor - Observing and checking systems
- regulate - Maintaining conformity to standards
