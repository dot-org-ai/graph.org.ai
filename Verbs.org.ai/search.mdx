# Search

Looking for or attempting to locate specific information, items, resources, or records through investigation, inquiry, or examination.

## O*NET Context

Work activity focused on seeking, investigating, or exploring to find needed information, items, or solutions to support work activities and problem-solving.

## Semantic Forms

### Action (Base Form)
```typescript
await $.Detective.search({ location: 'scene', objective: 'evidence' })
```

### Predicate (Relationship)
```typescript
$.Researcher.searches.Database
$.Team.searches.Solution
```

### Event (Past Tense)
```typescript
on($.Query.searched, async (event) => {
  await $.Result.compile({ findings: event.matches })

```

### Activity (Ongoing Process)

## Usage Examples

**Information Retrieval:**
- Searching databases for specific records or documents
- Looking for information in libraries or archives
- Researching online resources and references
- Hunting for answers in documentation or manuals

**Investigative Work:**
- Searching for evidence at a location
- Investigating records for discrepancies
- Looking for patterns in data
- Locating missing persons or items

**Business Operations:**
- Searching for vendor or supplier information
- Looking for best practices and benchmarks
- Investigating cost-saving opportunities
- Seeking solutions to operational problems
