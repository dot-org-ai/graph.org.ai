# Liquidate

Selling off remaining inventory, excess stock, or assets to convert them into cash, often due to discontinuation, overstock, or asset disposal needs.

## APQC Process Context

Inventory and asset management activity where organizations dispose of surplus or obsolete items through sales at reduced prices to minimize holding costs.

## Business Rules

- Liquidation requires approval based on value
- Pricing must balance cash recovery with speed
- All applicable disposal rules must be followed
- Proceeds must be recorded and reconciled
- Inventory records must be updated

## Related Verbs

- dispose - Removing from operations
- discontinue - Ending availability
- auction - Offering to highest bidder
- clearance - Quick sale at reduced price
