# Manage

Directing, controlling, or overseeing activities, resources, or personnel to achieve organizational objectives and ensure effective operations.

## O*NET Context

Work activity focused on coordinating people, resources, processes, and information to accomplish organizational goals. Includes planning, decision-making, delegation, monitoring, and adjustment of operations to maintain effectiveness and efficiency.

## Semantic Forms

### Action (Base Form)
```typescript
await $.Project.manage({ scope: 'product-launch', timeline: '6-months', budget: 500000, stakeholders: [...] })
```

### Predicate (Relationship)
```typescript
$.Manager.manages.Team
$.Department.manages.Budget
```

### Event (Past Tense)
```typescript
on($.Initiative.managed, async (event) => {
  await $.Report.generate({ initiative: event.initiativeId, performance: event.metrics })
  await $.Stakeholder.notify({ status: 'management-complete', outcome: event.result })
})
```

### Activity (Ongoing Process)

## Usage Examples

**People Management:**
- Manager manages team of developers with clear goals and accountability
- Supervisor manages workforce scheduling and resource allocation
- Director manages department budget and strategic initiatives
- Coach manages athlete performance and development

**Project Management:**
- Project manager manages complex product development from conception to launch
- Program director manages portfolio of related initiatives
- Product manager manages feature development and roadmap
- Operations manager manages daily business processes

**Resource Management:**
- Finance manager manages organizational budget and expenditures
- Inventory manager manages warehouse stock levels and ordering
- HR manager manages talent acquisition, development, and retention
- IT manager manages technology infrastructure and systems

**Performance Management:**
- Manager manages team performance through evaluation and feedback
- Executive manages organizational strategy and long-term direction
- Coordinator manages event logistics and vendor relationships
- Administrator manages office operations and administrative functions
