# Merge

Combining or consolidating separate entities, departments, systems, data, or operations into a single unified structure or organization.

## APQC Process Context

Strategic and operational activity where organizations consolidate duplicate, overlapping, or aligned operations to improve efficiency, reduce costs, or strengthen market position.

## APQC Process Category

Category 1: Plan & Organize
- Subcategory: Manage organizational resources
- Process: Design and reorganize organizational structure

## Business Rules

- Merge requires strategic justification and approval
- Integration plan must be documented
- System and process alignment must be planned
- Duplicate resources must be consolidated
- Stakeholder communication is critical
