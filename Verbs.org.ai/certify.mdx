# Certify

Formally attesting, validating, or confirming that products, processes, people, or systems meet established standards or requirements.

## APQC Process Context

Quality assurance and validation activity where qualified parties formally verify and document that something meets specified criteria, standards, or requirements.

## APQC Process Category

Category 1: Plan & Organize
- Subcategory: Develop and manage enterprise compliance
- Process: Manage compliance and certifications

## Business Rules

- Certification requires independent assessment
- Certifier must have proper credentials and authority
- Certification has defined validity period
- Non-compliance requires corrective action
- Certification status must be maintained and monitored
