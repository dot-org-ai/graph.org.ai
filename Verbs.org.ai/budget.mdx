# Budget

Planning and allocating financial resources across departments and activities for future operating periods based on strategic objectives.

## APQC Process Context

Financial planning process where organizations establish spending plans, allocate resources across business units, and set financial targets for the upcoming period.

## APQC Process Category

Category 1: Plan & Organize
- Subcategory: Develop and manage enterprise financial resources
- Process: Develop financial plan and budget

## Business Rules

- Budget must align with strategic plan
- All departments must submit budget requests
- Budget requires approval by finance leadership
- Actual spending must be monitored against budget
- Budget changes require formal approval

## Related Verbs
- plan - Establishing strategies and approaches
- allocate - Distributing resources
- forecast - Projecting future financial performance
- control - Managing spending against budget
- measure - Tracking actual versus budget
