# Promote

Advancing someone's career position or publicizing, encouraging the adoption, support, or awareness of something.

## O*NET Context

Work activity focused on marketing products or services, advocating for ideas, or advancing employees to higher positions within an organization.

## Semantic Forms

### Action (Base Form)
```typescript
await $.Marketing.promote({ product: 'service', audience: 'target-market', channels: ['digital', 'traditional'] })
```

### Predicate (Relationship)
```typescript
$.Manager.promotes.Employee
$.Company.promotes.Brand
```

### Event (Past Tense)
```typescript
on($.Employee.promoted, async (event) => {
  await $.Announcement.send({ audience: 'organization', newTitle: event.newTitle })
  await $.Compensation.update({ newSalary: event.newSalary })
})
```

### Activity (Ongoing Process)
```typescript
const activePromotion = $.Team.promoting.Campaign
console.log(`Currently promoting: ${activePromotion.reachEstimate} potential customers`)
```

### Role (Actor)
```typescript
const promoter: $.Promoter = await $.findAgent({ expertise: 'marketing-communications' })
await promoter.launchCampaign(strategy)
```

### Concept (Abstraction)
```typescript
const promotion: $.Promotion = {
  subjectId: product.id,
  type: 'marketing',
  targets: [],
  channels: [],
  budget: 0,
  timeline: { start: date, end: date },
  objectives: []
}
```

### Inverse (Passive)
```typescript
const campaigns = await db.related($.Brand, $.promotedBy, $.Marketing)
```

## Usage Examples

**Career Advancement:**
- Promoting employees to management positions
- Promoting managers to leadership roles
- Promoting team members for increased responsibilities
- Promoting internal candidates to open positions

**Product Marketing:**
- Promoting new products to market
- Promoting brand awareness
- Promoting product features and benefits
- Promoting sales and special offers

**Communications and Advocacy:**
- Promoting organizational initiatives
- Promoting health and safety awareness
- Promoting compliance and policies
- Promoting company culture and values

**Sales Promotion:**
- Promoting special pricing or bundles
- Promoting loyalty programs
- Promoting customer referral programs
- Promoting seasonal campaigns

## Related Verbs
- market - Promoting and selling products
- advance - Moving forward or improving
- publicize - Making widely known
- advocate - Supporting or recommending
