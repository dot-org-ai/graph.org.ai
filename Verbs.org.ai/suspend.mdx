# Suspend

Temporarily halting or pausing operations, services, access, or activities pending resolution of issues or changes in business conditions.

## APQC Process Context

Operations and access control activity where organizations temporarily stop activities while maintaining the ability to resume when conditions change or issues are resolved.

## APQC Process Category

Category 1: Plan & Organize
- Subcategory: Manage organizational resources
- Process: Manage operational controls

## Business Rules

- Suspension must be documented with justification
- Affected parties must be notified
- Expected resumption timeline must be set
- Impact must be assessed and communicated
- Conditions for resumption must be defined
