# Formulate

Creating or developing a specific formula, plan, theory, or approach by combining elements in a structured way.

## O*NET Context

Common work activity in scientific, technical, and professional roles including chemists, engineers, strategists, and policy makers. Involves systematic thinking to create solutions, strategies, or compositions that address specific objectives.

## Usage Examples

**Scientific Development:**
- Chemists formulate new drug compounds
- Researchers formulate theories about natural phenomena
- Engineers formulate design specifications

**Strategic Planning:**
- Executives formulate corporate strategy
- Policy makers formulate regulations
- Teams formulate project approaches

**Business Rules:**
- Formulations must document all components and ratios
- Peer review required before finalization
- Change control process for existing formulations
- Approval workflows based on complexity level
