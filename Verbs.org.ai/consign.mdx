# Consign

Handing over goods to a carrier or third party with responsibility for delivery, or delivering goods to a sales agent for sale on consignment basis.

## GS1 Context

Business step marking the transfer of custody and responsibility for goods to a shipping carrier, logistics provider, or sales agent. The consigner retains ownership while the consignee assumes delivery or sales responsibility.

## Usage Examples

**Freight & Logistics:**
- Shipper consigns goods to freight carrier
- Warehouse consigns full container to logistics company
- Port authority consigns imports to customs broker
- Exporter consigns goods to international freight forwarder

**Retail & Sales:**
- Manufacturer consigns inventory to retail partner
- Distributor consigns products to retail outlet
- Wholesaler consigns goods for consignment sales

**Business Rules:**
- Consignment transfers delivery responsibility
- Consignment documents must be prepared
- Consignment initiates tracking workflow
- Consignment terms determine payment and returns
