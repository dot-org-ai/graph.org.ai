# Fulfill

Completing customer orders by picking, packing, and shipping products or delivering services within agreed timeframes and quality standards.

## APQC Process Context

Order-to-delivery process where organizations ensure customer requests are met completely and on time through coordinated warehouse, logistics, and service delivery activities.

## APQC Process Category

Category 3: Make/Deliver
- Subcategory: Deliver products and services
- Process: Fulfill customer orders

## Business Rules

- Fulfillment requires validated customer order
- Items must be available or ordered
- Quality must meet customer specifications
- Delivery must occur within promised timeframe
- Fulfillment completion must be confirmed

## Related Verbs

- pick - Selecting items from inventory
- pack - Preparing shipment
- dispatch - Sending shipment
- deliver - Providing to customer
