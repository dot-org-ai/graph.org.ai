# Integrate

Combining or coordinating separate components, systems, teams, or processes into a unified, cohesive, and functioning whole.

## O*NET Context

Work activity focused on bringing together diverse elements, ensuring compatibility, and creating seamless connections between parts to achieve unified functionality.

**System Integration:**
- Integrating software systems
- Integrating hardware and software

**Team Integration:**
- Integrating new employees into teams
- Merging departmental efforts
- Coordinating cross-functional projects
- Combining team skills and perspectives

**Process Integration:**
- Integrating business processes
- Harmonizing workflow procedures
- Combining workflow steps
- Unifying operational standards
