# Educate

Teaching others through instruction, training, or learning development activities.

## O*NET Context

Work activity involving instructing employees or customers on how to accomplish job-related tasks. This is a core competency for trainers, educators, managers, and customer service professionals.

## Usage Examples

**Corporate Training:**
- HR manager educates new employees on company policies
- Product manager educates sales team on new features
- Technical trainer educates support team on troubleshooting procedures

**Customer-Facing:**
- Sales associate educates customer on product benefits
- Service technician educates user on equipment operation
- Consultant educates client on best practices

**Business Rules:**
- Education should be tracked and documented
- Educators must have subject matter expertise
- Education completion should trigger certification processes
