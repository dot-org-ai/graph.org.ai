# Establish

Creating, founding, or setting up something new such as procedures, relationships, organizations, or standards.

## O*NET Context

Work activity involving creating new systems, procedures, policies, or organizations. Common in management, entrepreneurship, strategic planning, and organizational development roles where the goal is to create and implement new structures.
