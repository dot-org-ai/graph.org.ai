# Standardize

Establishing uniform methods, formats, practices, and procedures across the organization to ensure consistency and improve efficiency.

## APQC Process Context

Process improvement and governance activity where organizations define and implement standard approaches to reduce variation and improve quality and efficiency.

## Business Rules

- Standardization requires stakeholder input
- Standard must be documented clearly
- Implementation timeline must be communicated
- Training must be provided
- Compliance must be monitored
