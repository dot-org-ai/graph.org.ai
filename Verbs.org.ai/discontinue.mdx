# Discontinue

Ending or ceasing production, sale, or support of products or services that no longer meet business objectives or market demand.

## APQC Process Context

Product lifecycle management activity where organizations formally end the availability and support of products or services through structured processes.

## APQC Process Category

Category 3: Make/Deliver
- Subcategory: Manage product quality
- Process: Manage product/service discontinuation

## Business Rules

- Discontinuation requires formal decision and approval
- Customers must be notified with sufficient notice
- Supply chain must be managed for existing orders
- Warranty and support obligations must be honored
- Product documentation must be archived
