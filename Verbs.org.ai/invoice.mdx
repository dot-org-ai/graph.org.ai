# Invoice

Creating and issuing billing documents that request payment for products delivered or services rendered to customers.

## APQC Process Context

Billing and revenue process where organizations document goods delivered or services provided and request payment from customers within defined terms.

## APQC Process Category

Category 4: Enable
- Subcategory: Manage customer billing
- Process: Manage customer billing and collections

## Business Rules

- Invoice must match delivery and customer agreement
- Invoice amounts must be accurate and complete
- Invoice must be issued within contractual timeframe
- Invoice terms must comply with contract
- Invoice must be tracked for payment collection
