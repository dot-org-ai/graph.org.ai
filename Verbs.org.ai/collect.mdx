# Collect

Gathering or accumulating objects, items, or goods together from multiple sources or locations.

## GS1 Context

Business step involving the consolidation of items, often used in fulfillment operations where products are gathered from a warehouse and combined for shipment. Also applies to data collection and metrics aggregation.

## Semantic Forms

### Action (Base Form)
```typescript
await $.Order.collect({
  items: lineItems,
  location: pickingAreaId,
  timestamp: now()
})
```

## Usage Examples

**Fulfillment Operations:**
- Worker collects items from warehouse shelves for an order
- System collects multiple smaller shipments into consolidated container
- Collector gathers returns from customers
- Distribution center collects items from multiple suppliers

**Business Rules:**
- Collection initiates picking workflow
- Collection must match order requirements
- Collection triggers verification and quality check
- Collection updates inventory allocation
