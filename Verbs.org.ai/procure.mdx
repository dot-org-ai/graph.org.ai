# Procure

Obtaining needed goods or services through formal purchasing processes that include requisitioning, supplier selection, negotiation, and order management.

## APQC Process Context

Supply chain sourcing activity where organizations identify requirements, select suppliers, negotiate terms, and execute purchase orders for goods and services.

## APQC Process Category

Category 2: Source/Procure
- Subcategory: Procure materials and services
- Process: Procure goods and services

## Business Rules

- Procurement requires documented requirement
- Supplier must be qualified and approved
- Cost must be within budget authority
- Terms must comply with contracts and policies
- Purchase order must be issued before delivery
