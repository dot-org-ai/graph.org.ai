# Streamline

Simplifying and improving the efficiency of processes and operations by removing unnecessary steps, reducing complexity, and optimizing workflows.

## APQC Process Context

Continuous improvement activity where organizations eliminate waste, reduce cycle time, and enhance operational efficiency through process redesign.
