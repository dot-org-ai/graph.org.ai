# Handle

Managing, dealing with, or taking care of something or someone, whether physical objects, situations, or responsibilities.

## O*NET Context

Broad work activity across many occupations including customer service representatives, logistics workers, nurses, and administrative professionals. Encompasses both physical handling of materials and figurative handling of situations or responsibilities.

## Usage Examples

**Customer Service:**
- Support teams handle customer inquiries
- Resolution specialists handle complaints
- Representatives handle service requests

**Logistics & Operations:**
- Warehouses handle inventory movement
- Couriers handle package delivery
- Staff handle equipment and materials

**Healthcare & Professional:**
- Nurses handle patient care
- Managers handle personnel issues
- Administrators handle paperwork and scheduling

**Business Rules:**
- Handling procedures documented by item type
- Fragile items require special handling protocols
- Chain of custody maintained for critical items
- Handling tracked with timestamps and responsible party
