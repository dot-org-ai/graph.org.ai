# Reconcile

Verifying that accounts, records, or systems match expected values and resolving any discrepancies through investigation and adjustment.

## APQC Process Context

Financial and operational control activity where organizations ensure internal records align with external documentation and identify and correct variances.

## APQC Process Category

Category 4: Enable
- Subcategory: Manage financial resources
- Process: Manage accounting and financial closing

## Business Rules

- Reconciliation must occur at defined intervals
- Internal and external records must be obtained
- Differences must be investigated and explained
- Adjustments must be documented and approved
- Reconciliation records must be retained

## Related Verbs
- verify - Confirming accuracy
- match - Comparing for alignment
- analyze - Examining differences
- adjust - Correcting discrepancies
- close - Finalizing periods
