# Service Unit Operators, Oil and Gas

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-5013.00

Operate equipment to increase oil flow from producing wells or to remove stuck pipe, casing, tools, or other obstructions from drilling wells. Includes fishing-tool technicians.

## Tasks

- [Maintain and perform safety inspections on equipment and tools.](Tasks/20451.mdx)
- [Operate controls that raise derricks or level rigs.](Tasks/7080.mdx)
- [Listen to engines, rotary chains, or other equipment to detect faulty operations or unusual well conditions.](Tasks/20452.mdx)
- [Prepare reports of services rendered, tools used, or time required, for billing purposes.](Tasks/7083.mdx)
- [Install pressure-control devices onto wellheads.](Tasks/7076.mdx)
- [Confer with others to gather information regarding pipe or tool sizes or borehole conditions in wells.](Tasks/7074.mdx)
- [Operate pumps that circulate water, oil, or other fluids through wells to remove sand or other materials obstructing the free flow of oil.](Tasks/7078.mdx)
- [Drive truck-mounted units to well sites.](Tasks/7075.mdx)
- [Interpret instrument readings to ascertain the depth of obstruction.](Tasks/7088.mdx)
- [Thread cables through derrick pulleys, using hand tools.](Tasks/7077.mdx)
