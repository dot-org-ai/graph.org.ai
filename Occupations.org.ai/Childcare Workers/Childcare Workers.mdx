# Childcare Workers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 39-9011.00

Attend to children at schools, businesses, private households, and childcare institutions. Perform a variety of tasks, such as dressing, feeding, bathing, and overseeing play.

## Tasks

- [Maintain a safe play environment.](Tasks/18756.mdx)
- [Observe and monitor children's play activities.](Tasks/2336.mdx)
- [Communicate with children's parents or guardians about daily activities, behaviors, and related issues.](Tasks/18757.mdx)
- [Support children's emotional and social development, encouraging understanding of others and positive self-concepts.](Tasks/2331.mdx)
- [Care for children in institutional setting, such as group homes, nursery schools, private businesses, or schools for people with disabilities.](Tasks/2332.mdx)
- [Sanitize toys and play equipment.](Tasks/2333.mdx)
- [Dress children and change diapers.](Tasks/2346.mdx)
- [Keep records on individual children, including daily observations and information about activities, meals served, and medications administered.](Tasks/2337.mdx)
- [Identify signs of emotional or developmental problems in children and bring them to parents' or guardians' attention.](Tasks/2335.mdx)
- [Instruct children in health and personal habits, such as eating, resting, and toilet habits.](Tasks/2338.mdx)
