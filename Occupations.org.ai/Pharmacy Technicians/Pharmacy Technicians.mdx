# Pharmacy Technicians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 29-2052.00

Prepare medications under the direction of a pharmacist. May measure, mix, count out, label, and record amounts and dosages of medications according to prescription orders.

## Tasks

- [Receive written prescription or refill requests and verify that information is complete and accurate.](Tasks/1939.mdx)
- [Enter prescription information into computer databases.](Tasks/23924.mdx)
- [Establish or maintain patient profiles, including lists of medications taken by individual patients.](Tasks/1946.mdx)
- [Maintain proper storage and security conditions for drugs.](Tasks/1940.mdx)
- [Receive and store incoming supplies, verify quantities against invoices, check for outdated medications in current inventory, and inform supervisors of stock needs and shortages.](Tasks/18545.mdx)
- [Answer telephones, responding to questions or requests.](Tasks/1941.mdx)
- [Assist customers by answering simple questions, locating items, or referring them to the pharmacist for medication information.](Tasks/1943.mdx)
- [Operate cash registers to accept payment from customers.](Tasks/1954.mdx)
- [Price and file prescriptions that have been filled.](Tasks/1944.mdx)
- [Mix pharmaceutical preparations, according to written prescriptions.](Tasks/1953.mdx)
