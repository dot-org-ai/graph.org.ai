# Packers and Packagers, Hand

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 53-7064.00

Pack or package by hand a wide variety of products and materials.

## Tasks

- [Examine and inspect containers, materials, or products to ensure that product quality and packing specifications are met.](Tasks/21216.mdx)
- [Measure, weigh, and count products and materials.](Tasks/3212.mdx)
- [Record product, packaging, and order information on specified forms and records.](Tasks/3214.mdx)
- [Seal containers or materials, using glues, fasteners, nails, and hand tools.](Tasks/3216.mdx)
- [Assemble, line, and pad cartons, crates, and containers, using hand tools.](Tasks/3218.mdx)
- [Obtain, move, and sort products, materials, containers, and orders, using hand tools.](Tasks/3222.mdx)
- [Mark and label containers, container tags, or products, using marking tools.](Tasks/3211.mdx)
- [Clean containers, materials, supplies, or work areas, using cleaning solutions and hand tools.](Tasks/3219.mdx)
- [Remove completed or defective products or materials, placing them on moving equipment, such as conveyors, or in specified areas, such as loading docks.](Tasks/3215.mdx)
- [Place or pour products or materials into containers, using hand tools and equipment, or fill containers from spouts or chutes.](Tasks/3221.mdx)
