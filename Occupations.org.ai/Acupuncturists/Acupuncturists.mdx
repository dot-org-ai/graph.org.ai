# Acupuncturists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 29-1291.00

Diagnose, treat, and prevent disorders by stimulating specific acupuncture points within the body using acupuncture needles. May also use cups, nutritional supplements, therapeutic massage, acupressure, and other alternative health therapies.

## Tasks

- [Maintain and follow standard quality, safety, environmental, and infection control policies and procedures.](Tasks/17357.mdx)
- [Treat patients using tools, such as needles, cups, ear balls, seeds, pellets, or nutritional supplements.](Tasks/17362.mdx)
- [Adhere to local, state, and federal laws, regulations, and statutes.](Tasks/17361.mdx)
- [Identify correct anatomical and proportional point locations based on patients' anatomy and positions, contraindications, and precautions related to treatments, such as intradermal needles, moxibustion, electricity, guasha, or bleeding.](Tasks/17373.mdx)
- [Develop individual treatment plans and strategies.](Tasks/17370.mdx)
- [Insert needles to provide acupuncture treatment.](Tasks/17372.mdx)
- [Evaluate treatment outcomes and recommend new or altered treatments as necessary to further promote, restore, or maintain health.](Tasks/17364.mdx)
- [Collect medical histories and general health and lifestyle information from patients.](Tasks/17366.mdx)
- [Maintain detailed and complete records of health care plans and prognoses.](Tasks/17358.mdx)
- [Educate patients on topics, such as meditation, ergonomics, stretching, exercise, nutrition, the healing process, breathing, or relaxation techniques.](Tasks/17363.mdx)
