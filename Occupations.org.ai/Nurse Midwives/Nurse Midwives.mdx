# Nurse Midwives

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 29-1161.00

Diagnose and coordinate all aspects of the birthing process, either independently or as part of a healthcare team. May provide well-woman gynecological care. Must have specialized, graduate nursing education.

## Tasks

- [Provide prenatal, intrapartum, postpartum, or newborn care to patients.](Tasks/18442.mdx)
- [Monitor fetal development by listening to fetal heartbeat, taking external uterine measurements, identifying fetal position, or estimating fetal size and weight.](Tasks/18444.mdx)
- [Document patients' health histories, symptoms, physical conditions, or other diagnostic information.](Tasks/18443.mdx)
- [Provide patients with direct family planning services, such as inserting intrauterine devices, dispensing oral contraceptives, and fitting cervical barriers, including cervical caps or diaphragms.](Tasks/18455.mdx)
- [Prescribe medications as permitted by state regulations.](Tasks/18454.mdx)
