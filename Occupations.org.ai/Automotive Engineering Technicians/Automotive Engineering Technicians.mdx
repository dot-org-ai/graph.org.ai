# Automotive Engineering Technicians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 17-3027.01

Assist engineers in determining the practicality of proposed product design changes and plan and carry out tests on experimental test devices or equipment for performance, durability, or efficiency.

## Tasks

- [Document test results, using cameras, spreadsheets, documents, or other tools.](Tasks/16606.mdx)
- [Set up mechanical, hydraulic, or electric test equipment in accordance with engineering specifications, standards, or test procedures.](Tasks/16599.mdx)
- [Read and interpret blueprints, schematics, work specifications, drawings, or charts.](Tasks/16602.mdx)
- [Inspect or test parts to determine nature or cause of defects or malfunctions.](Tasks/16605.mdx)
- [Monitor computer-controlled test equipment, according to written or verbal instructions.](Tasks/16601.mdx)
- [Analyze test data for automotive systems, subsystems, or component parts.](Tasks/16608.mdx)
- [Install equipment, such as instrumentation, test equipment, engines, or aftermarket products, to ensure proper interfaces.](Tasks/16604.mdx)
- [Perform or execute manual or automated tests of automotive system or component performance, efficiency, or durability.](Tasks/16611.mdx)
- [Maintain test equipment in operational condition by performing routine maintenance or making minor repairs or adjustments as needed.](Tasks/16603.mdx)
- [Analyze performance of vehicles or components that have been redesigned to increase fuel efficiency, such as camless or dual-clutch engines or alternative types of air-conditioning systems.](Tasks/19696.mdx)
