# Biofuels/Biodiesel Technology and Product Development Managers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 11-9041.01

Define, plan, or execute biofuels/biodiesel research programs that evaluate alternative feedstock and process technologies with near-term commercial potential.

## Tasks

- [Design or conduct applied biodiesel or biofuels research projects on topics, such as transport, thermodynamics, mixing, filtration, distillation, fermentation, extraction, and separation.](Tasks/15574.mdx)
- [Analyze data from biofuels studies, such as fluid dynamics, water treatments, or solvent extraction and recovery processes.](Tasks/15578.mdx)
- [Prepare, or oversee the preparation of, experimental plans for biofuels research or development.](Tasks/15567.mdx)
- [Provide technical or scientific guidance to technical staff in the conduct of biofuels research or development.](Tasks/15566.mdx)
- [Propose new biofuels products, processes, technologies or applications based on findings from applied biofuels or biomass research projects.](Tasks/15580.mdx)
- [Conduct experiments on biomass or pretreatment technologies.](Tasks/15576.mdx)
- [Prepare biofuels research and development reports for senior management or technical professionals.](Tasks/15568.mdx)
- [Develop lab scale models of industrial scale processes, such as fermentation.](Tasks/15563.mdx)
- [Oversee biodiesel/biofuels prototyping or development projects.](Tasks/15579.mdx)
- [Conduct experiments to test new or alternate feedstock fermentation processes.](Tasks/15577.mdx)
