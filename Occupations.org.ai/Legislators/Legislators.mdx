# Legislators

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 11-1031.00

Develop, introduce, or enact laws and statutes at the local, tribal, state, or federal level. Includes only workers in elected positions.

## Tasks

- [Analyze and understand the local and national implications of proposed legislation.](Tasks/15267.mdx)
- [Appoint nominees to leadership posts, or approve such appointments.](Tasks/15268.mdx)
- [Confer with colleagues to formulate positions and strategies pertaining to pending issues.](Tasks/15269.mdx)
- [Debate the merits of proposals and bill amendments during floor sessions, following the appropriate rules of procedure.](Tasks/15270.mdx)
- [Develop expertise in subject matters related to committee assignments.](Tasks/15271.mdx)
- [Hear testimony from constituents, representatives of interest groups, board and commission members, and others with an interest in bills or issues under consideration.](Tasks/15272.mdx)

- [Maintain knowledge of relevant national and international current events.](Tasks/15274.mdx)
