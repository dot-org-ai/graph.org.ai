# Chief Sustainability Officers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 11-1011.03

Communicate and coordinate with management, shareholders, customers, and employees to address sustainability issues. Enact or oversee a corporate sustainability strategy.

## Tasks

- [Monitor and evaluate effectiveness of sustainability programs.](Tasks/15382.mdx)
- [Develop or execute strategies to address issues such as energy use, resource conservation, recycling, pollution reduction, waste elimination, transportation, education, and building design.](Tasks/15384.mdx)
- [Develop, or oversee the development of, sustainability evaluation or monitoring systems.](Tasks/15379.mdx)
- [Supervise employees or volunteers working on sustainability projects.](Tasks/15372.mdx)
- [Develop sustainability reports, presentations, or proposals for supplier, employee, academia, media, government, public interest, or other groups.](Tasks/15378.mdx)
- [Develop, or oversee the development of, marketing or outreach media for sustainability projects or events.](Tasks/15380.mdx)
- [Identify and evaluate pilot projects or programs to enhance the sustainability research agenda.](Tasks/15368.mdx)
- [Create and maintain sustainability program documents, such as schedules and budgets.](Tasks/15370.mdx)
- [Formulate or implement sustainability campaign or marketing strategies.](Tasks/15375.mdx)
- [Research environmental sustainability issues, concerns, or stakeholder interests.](Tasks/15376.mdx)
