# Helpers--Carpenters

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-3012.00

Help carpenters by performing duties requiring less skill. Duties include using, supplying, or holding materials or tools, and cleaning work area and equipment.

## Tasks

- [Clean work areas, machines, or equipment, to maintain a clean and safe job site.](Tasks/8271.mdx)
- [Fasten timbers or lumber with glue, screws, pegs, or nails and install hardware.](Tasks/8270.mdx)
- [Perform tie spacing layout and measure, mark, drill or cut.](Tasks/8276.mdx)
- [Select tools, equipment, or materials from storage and transport items to work site.](Tasks/8269.mdx)
- [Drill holes in timbers or lumber.](Tasks/20443.mdx)
- [Cut timbers, lumber, or paneling to specified dimensions.](Tasks/20444.mdx)
- [Position and hold timbers, lumber, or paneling in place for fastening or cutting.](Tasks/8267.mdx)
- [Align, straighten, plumb, or square forms for installation.](Tasks/8273.mdx)
- [Hold plumb bobs, sighting rods, or other equipment to aid in establishing reference points and lines.](Tasks/8272.mdx)
- [Erect scaffolding, shoring, or braces.](Tasks/8268.mdx)
