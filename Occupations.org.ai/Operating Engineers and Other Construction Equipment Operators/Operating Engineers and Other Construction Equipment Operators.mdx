# Operating Engineers and Other Construction Equipment Operators

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-2073.00

Operate one or several types of power construction equipment, such as motor graders, bulldozers, scrapers, compressors, pumps, derricks, shovels, tractors, or front-end loaders to excavate, move, and grade earth, erect structures, or pour concrete or other hard surface pavement. May repair and maintain equipment in addition to other duties.

## Tasks

- [Learn and follow safety regulations.](Tasks/9849.mdx)
- [Take actions to avoid potential hazards or obstructions, such as utility lines, other equipment, other workers, or falling objects.](Tasks/9850.mdx)
- [Start engines, move throttles, switches, or levers, or depress pedals to operate machines, such as bulldozers, trench excavators, road graders, or backhoes.](Tasks/9852.mdx)
- [Coordinate machine actions with other activities, positioning or moving loads in response to hand or audio signals from crew members.](Tasks/9858.mdx)
- [Align machines, cutterheads, or depth gauge makers with reference stakes and guidelines or ground or position equipment, following hand signals of other workers.](Tasks/9855.mdx)
- [Locate underground services, such as pipes or wires, prior to beginning work.](Tasks/9853.mdx)
- [Signal operators to guide movement of tractor-drawn machines.](Tasks/9868.mdx)
- [Repair and maintain equipment, making emergency adjustments or assisting with major repairs as necessary.](Tasks/9860.mdx)
- [Load and move dirt, rocks, equipment, or other materials, using trucks, crawler tractors, power cranes, shovels, graders, or related equipment.](Tasks/9856.mdx)
- [Drive and maneuver equipment equipped with blades in successive passes over working areas to remove topsoil, vegetation, or rocks or to distribute and level earth or terrain.](Tasks/9857.mdx)
