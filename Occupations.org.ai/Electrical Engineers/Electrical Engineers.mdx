# Electrical Engineers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 17-2071.00

Research, design, develop, test, or supervise the manufacturing and installation of electrical equipment, components, or systems for commercial, industrial, military, or scientific use.

## Tasks

- [Design, implement, maintain, or improve electrical instruments, equipment, facilities, components, products, or systems for commercial, industrial, or domestic purposes.](Tasks/1348.mdx)
- [Oversee project production efforts to assure projects are completed on time and within budget.](Tasks/1357.mdx)
- [Direct or coordinate manufacturing, construction, installation, maintenance, support, documentation, or testing activities to ensure compliance with specifications, codes, or customer requirements.](Tasks/1350.mdx)
- [Perform detailed calculations to compute and establish manufacturing, construction, or installation standards or specifications.](Tasks/1351.mdx)
- [Operate computer-assisted engineering or design software or equipment to perform engineering tasks.](Tasks/1349.mdx)
- [Confer with engineers, customers, or others to discuss existing or potential engineering projects or products.](Tasks/1347.mdx)
- [Investigate or test vendors' or competitors' products.](Tasks/1356.mdx)
- [Inspect completed installations and observe operations to ensure conformance to design and equipment specifications and compliance with operational, safety, or environmental standards.](Tasks/20731.mdx)
- [Investigate customer or public complaints to determine the nature and extent of problems.](Tasks/20730.mdx)
- [Prepare technical drawings, specifications of electrical systems, or topographical maps to ensure that installation and operations conform to standards and customer requirements.](Tasks/1358.mdx)
