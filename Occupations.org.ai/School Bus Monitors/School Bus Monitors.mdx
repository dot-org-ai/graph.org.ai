# School Bus Monitors

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 33-9094.00

Maintain order among students on a school bus. Duties include helping students safely board and exit and communicating behavioral problems. May perform pretrip and posttrip inspections and prepare for and assist in emergency evacuations.

## Tasks

- [Announce routes or stops.](Tasks/23081.mdx)
- [Assist children with disabilities or children with psychological, emotional, or behavioral issues with boarding and exiting the school bus.](Tasks/23082.mdx)
- [Buckle seatbelts or fasten wheelchair tie-down straps to secure passengers for transportation.](Tasks/23083.mdx)
- [Clean school bus interiors by picking up waste, wiping down windows, or vacuuming.](Tasks/23084.mdx)
- [Direct students boarding and exiting the school bus.](Tasks/23085.mdx)
- [Direct students evacuating the bus during safety drills.](Tasks/23086.mdx)
- [Escort young children across roads or highways.](Tasks/23087.mdx)
- [Evacuate students from the school bus in emergency situations.](Tasks/23088.mdx)
- [Guide the driver when the bus is moving in reverse gear.](Tasks/23089.mdx)
- [Monitor for trains at railroad crossings and signal the bus driver when it is safe to proceed.](Tasks/23090.mdx)
