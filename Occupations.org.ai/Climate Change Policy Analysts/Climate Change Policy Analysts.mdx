# Climate Change Policy Analysts

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 19-2041.01

Research and analyze policy developments related to climate change. Make climate-related recommendations for actions such as legislation, awareness campaigns, or fundraising approaches.

## Tasks

- [Provide analytical support for policy briefs related to renewable energy, energy efficiency, or climate change.](Tasks/16849.mdx)
- [Propose new or modified policies involving use of traditional and alternative fuels, transportation of goods, and other factors relating to climate and climate change.](Tasks/16853.mdx)
- [Prepare study reports, memoranda, briefs, testimonies, or other written materials to inform government or environmental groups on environmental issues, such as climate change.](Tasks/16850.mdx)
- [Analyze and distill climate-related research findings to inform legislators, regulatory agencies, or other stakeholders.](Tasks/16854.mdx)
- [Make legislative recommendations related to climate change or environmental management, based on climate change policies, principles, programs, practices, and processes.](Tasks/16851.mdx)
- [Present climate-related information at public interest, governmental, or other meetings.](Tasks/16843.mdx)
- [Gather and review climate-related studies from government agencies, research laboratories, and other organizations.](Tasks/16846.mdx)
- [Review existing policies or legislation to identify environmental impacts.](Tasks/16848.mdx)
- [Promote initiatives to mitigate climate change with government or environmental groups.](Tasks/16842.mdx)
- [Research policies, practices, or procedures for climate or environmental management.](Tasks/16852.mdx)
