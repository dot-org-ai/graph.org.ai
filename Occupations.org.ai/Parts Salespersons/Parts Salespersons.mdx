# Parts Salespersons

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 41-2022.00

Sell spare and replacement parts and equipment in repair shop or parts store.

## Tasks

- [Receive payment or obtain credit authorization.](Tasks/2430.mdx)
- [Assist customers, such as responding to customer complaints and updating them about back-ordered parts.](Tasks/20776.mdx)
- [Fill customer orders from stock, and place orders when requested items are out of stock.](Tasks/20775.mdx)
- [Receive and fill telephone orders for parts.](Tasks/2427.mdx)
- [Locate and label parts, and maintain inventory of stock.](Tasks/20777.mdx)
- [Prepare sales slips or sales contracts.](Tasks/2429.mdx)
- [Read catalogs, microfiche viewers, or computer displays to determine replacement part stock numbers and prices.](Tasks/2425.mdx)
- [Determine replacement parts required, according to inspections of old parts, customer requests, or customers' descriptions of malfunctions.](Tasks/2426.mdx)
- [Examine returned parts for defects, and exchange defective parts or refund money.](Tasks/2433.mdx)
- [Manage shipments by researching shipping methods or costs and tracking packages.](Tasks/20780.mdx)
