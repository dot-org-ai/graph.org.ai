# Art, Drama, and Music Teachers, Postsecondary

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 25-1121.00

Teach courses in drama, music, and the arts including fine and applied art, such as painting and sculpture, or design and crafts. Includes both teachers primarily engaged in teaching and those who do a combination of teaching and research.

## Tasks

- [Explain and demonstrate artistic techniques.](Tasks/6262.mdx)
- [Evaluate and grade students' class work, performances, projects, assignments, and papers.](Tasks/6261.mdx)
- [Prepare students for performances, exams, or assessments.](Tasks/6263.mdx)
- [Initiate, facilitate, and moderate classroom discussions.](Tasks/6267.mdx)
- [Prepare and deliver lectures to undergraduate or graduate students on topics such as acting techniques, fundamentals of music, and art history.](Tasks/6264.mdx)
