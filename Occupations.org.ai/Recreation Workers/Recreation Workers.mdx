# Recreation Workers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 39-9032.00

Conduct recreation activities with groups in public, private, or volunteer agencies or recreation facilities. Organize and promote activities, such as arts and crafts, sports, games, music, dramatics, social recreation, camping, and hobbies, taking into account the needs and interests of individual members.

## Tasks

- [Enforce rules and regulations of recreational facilities to maintain discipline and ensure safety.](Tasks/2380.mdx)
- [Organize, lead, and promote interest in recreational activities, such as arts, crafts, sports, games, camping, and hobbies.](Tasks/2381.mdx)
- [Assess the needs and interests of individuals and groups and plan activities accordingly, given the available equipment or facilities.](Tasks/21010.mdx)
- [Manage the daily operations of recreational facilities.](Tasks/2382.mdx)
- [Administer first aid according to prescribed procedures and notify emergency medical personnel when necessary.](Tasks/2383.mdx)
- [Complete and maintain time and attendance forms and inventory lists.](Tasks/2388.mdx)
- [Explain principles, techniques, and safety procedures to participants in recreational activities and demonstrate use of materials and equipment.](Tasks/2386.mdx)
- [Direct special activities or events, such as aquatics, gymnastics, or performing arts.](Tasks/2393.mdx)
- [Supervise and coordinate the work activities of personnel, such as training staff members and assigning work duties.](Tasks/2390.mdx)
- [Evaluate recreation areas, facilities, and services to determine if they are producing desired results.](Tasks/2387.mdx)
