# Robotics Technicians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 17-3024.01

Build, install, test, or maintain robotic equipment or related automated production systems.

## Tasks

- [Make repairs to robots or peripheral equipment, such as replacement of defective circuit boards, sensors, controllers, encoders, or servomotors.](Tasks/21059.mdx)
- [Troubleshoot robotic systems, using knowledge of microprocessors, programmable controllers, electronics, circuit analysis, mechanics, sensor or feedback systems, hydraulics, or pneumatics.](Tasks/16589.mdx)
- [Install, program, or repair programmable controllers, robot controllers, end-of-arm tools, or conveyors.](Tasks/16593.mdx)
- [Maintain service records of robotic equipment or automated production systems.](Tasks/16579.mdx)
- [Modify computer-controlled robot movements.](Tasks/16584.mdx)
- [Perform preventive or corrective maintenance on robotic systems or components.](Tasks/16592.mdx)
- [Align, fit, or assemble components, using hand tools, power tools, fixtures, templates, or microscopes.](Tasks/16588.mdx)
- [Attach wires between controllers.](Tasks/16586.mdx)
- [Evaluate the efficiency and reliability of industrial robotic systems, reprogramming or calibrating to achieve maximum quantity and quality.](Tasks/19684.mdx)
- [Test performance of robotic assemblies, using instruments such as oscilloscopes, electronic voltmeters, or bridges.](Tasks/16591.mdx)
