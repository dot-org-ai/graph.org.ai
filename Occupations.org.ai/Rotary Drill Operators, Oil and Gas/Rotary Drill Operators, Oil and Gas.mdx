# Rotary Drill Operators, Oil and Gas

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-5012.00

Set up or operate a variety of drills to remove underground oil and gas, or remove core samples for testing during oil and gas exploration.

## Tasks

- [Train crews, and introduce procedures to make drill work more safe and effective.](Tasks/8296.mdx)
- [Observe pressure gauge and move throttles and levers to control the speed of rotary tables, and to regulate pressure of tools at bottoms of boreholes.](Tasks/8297.mdx)
- [Count sections of drill rod to determine depths of boreholes.](Tasks/8298.mdx)
- [Push levers and brake pedals to control gasoline, diesel, electric, or steam draw works that lower and raise drill pipes and casings in and out of wells.](Tasks/8299.mdx)
- [Connect sections of drill pipe, using hand tools and powered wrenches and tongs.](Tasks/8300.mdx)
- [Maintain records of footage drilled, location and nature of strata penetrated, materials and tools used, services rendered, and time required.](Tasks/8301.mdx)
- [Maintain and adjust machinery to ensure proper performance.](Tasks/8302.mdx)
- [Start and examine operation of slush pumps to ensure circulation and consistency of drilling fluid or mud in well.](Tasks/8303.mdx)
- [Locate and recover lost or broken bits, casings, and drill pipes from wells, using special tools.](Tasks/8304.mdx)
- [Weigh clay, and mix with water and chemicals to make drilling mud.](Tasks/8305.mdx)
