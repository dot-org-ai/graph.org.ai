# Correspondence Clerks

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 43-4021.00

Compose letters or electronic correspondence in reply to requests for merchandise, damage claims, credit and other information, delinquent accounts, incorrect billings, or unsatisfactory services. Duties may include gathering data to formulate reply and preparing correspondence.

## Tasks

- [Maintain files and control records to show correspondence activities.](Tasks/11276.mdx)
- [Read incoming correspondence to ascertain nature of writers' concerns and to determine disposition of correspondence.](Tasks/11273.mdx)
- [Gather records pertinent to specific problems, review them for completeness and accuracy, and attach records to correspondence as necessary.](Tasks/11277.mdx)
- [Prepare documents and correspondence, such as damage claims, credit and billing inquiries, invoices, and service complaints.](Tasks/11270.mdx)
- [Compile data from records to prepare periodic reports.](Tasks/11271.mdx)
- [Compose letters in reply to correspondence concerning such items as requests for merchandise, damage claims, credit information requests, delinquent accounts, incorrect billing, or unsatisfactory service.](Tasks/11280.mdx)
- [Route correspondence to other departments for reply.](Tasks/11279.mdx)
- [Ensure that money collected is properly recorded and secured.](Tasks/11281.mdx)
- [Process orders for goods requested in correspondence.](Tasks/11289.mdx)
- [Present clear and concise explanations of governing rules and regulations.](Tasks/11272.mdx)
