# Purchasing Agents, Except Wholesale, Retail, and Farm Products

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 13-1023.00

Purchase machinery, equipment, tools, parts, supplies, or services necessary for the operation of an establishment. Purchase raw or semifinished materials for manufacturing. May negotiate contracts.

## Tasks

- [Monitor and follow applicable laws and regulations.](Tasks/1146.mdx)
- [Purchase the highest quality merchandise at the lowest possible price and in correct amounts.](Tasks/1142.mdx)
- [Formulate policies and procedures for bid proposals and procurement of goods and services.](Tasks/1159.mdx)
- [Prepare purchase orders, solicit bid proposals, and review requisitions for goods and services.](Tasks/1143.mdx)
- [Write and review product specifications, maintaining a working technical knowledge of the goods or services to be purchased.](Tasks/1157.mdx)
- [Analyze price proposals, financial reports, and other data and information to determine reasonable prices.](Tasks/1145.mdx)
- [Hire, train, or supervise purchasing clerks, buyers, and expediters.](Tasks/1156.mdx)
