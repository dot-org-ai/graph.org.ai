# Biomass Power Plant Managers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 11-3051.04

Manage operations at biomass power generation facilities. Direct work activities at plant, including supervision of operations and maintenance staff.

## Tasks

- [Manage safety programs at power generation facilities.](Tasks/21162.mdx)
- [Review biomass operations performance specifications to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements.](Tasks/15471.mdx)
- [Review logs, datasheets, or reports to ensure adequate production levels and safe production environments or to identify abnormalities with power production equipment or processes.](Tasks/15470.mdx)
- [Supervise operations or maintenance employees in the production of power from biomass, such as wood, coal, paper sludge, or other waste or refuse.](Tasks/15468.mdx)
- [Supervise biomass plant or substation operations, maintenance, repair, or testing activities.](Tasks/15478.mdx)
- [Conduct field inspections of biomass plants, stations, or substations to ensure normal and safe operating conditions.](Tasks/15480.mdx)
- [Plan and schedule plant activities, such as wood, waste, or refuse fuel deliveries, ash removal, and regular maintenance.](Tasks/15474.mdx)
- [Prepare and manage biomass plant budgets.](Tasks/15473.mdx)
- [Evaluate power production or demand trends to identify opportunities for improved operations.](Tasks/15477.mdx)
- [Inspect biomass gasification processes, equipment, and facilities for ways to maximize capacity and minimize operating costs.](Tasks/15476.mdx)
