# Electrical and Electronics Repairers, Powerhouse, Substation, and Relay

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 49-2095.00

Inspect, test, repair, or maintain electrical equipment in generating stations, substations, and in-service relays.

## Tasks

- [Inspect and test equipment and circuits to identify malfunctions or defects, using wiring diagrams and testing devices such as ohmmeters, voltmeters, or ammeters.](Tasks/11743.mdx)
- [Prepare and maintain records detailing tests, repairs, and maintenance.](Tasks/11747.mdx)
- [Consult manuals, schematics, wiring diagrams, and engineering personnel to troubleshoot and solve equipment problems and to determine optimum equipment functioning.](Tasks/11744.mdx)
- [Analyze test data to diagnose malfunctions, to determine performance characteristics of systems, or to evaluate effects of system modifications.](Tasks/11748.mdx)
- [Open and close switches to isolate defective relays, performing adjustments or repairs.](Tasks/11746.mdx)
- [Notify facility personnel of equipment shutdowns.](Tasks/11745.mdx)
- [Repair, replace, and clean equipment and components such as circuit breakers, brushes, and commutators.](Tasks/11750.mdx)
- [Run signal quality and connectivity tests for individual cables, and record results.](Tasks/11753.mdx)
- [Maintain inventories of spare parts for all equipment, requisitioning parts as necessary.](Tasks/11756.mdx)
- [Construct, test, maintain, and repair substation relay and control systems.](Tasks/11742.mdx)
