# Art Therapists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 29-1129.01

Plan or conduct art therapy sessions or programs to improve clients' physical, cognitive, or emotional well-being.

## Tasks

- [Observe and document client reactions, progress, or other outcomes related to art therapy.](Tasks/19151.mdx)
- [Design art therapy sessions or programs to meet client's goals or objectives.](Tasks/19146.mdx)
- [Conduct art therapy sessions, providing guided self-expression experiences to help clients recover from, or cope with, cognitive, emotional, or physical impairments.](Tasks/19143.mdx)
- [Confer with other professionals on client's treatment team to develop, coordinate, or integrate treatment plans.](Tasks/19144.mdx)
- [Assess client needs or disorders, using drawing, painting, sculpting, or other artistic processes.](Tasks/19141.mdx)
- [Talk with clients during art or other therapy sessions to build rapport, acknowledge their progress, or reflect upon their reactions to the artistic process.](Tasks/19153.mdx)
- [Develop individualized treatment plans that incorporate studio art therapy, counseling, or psychotherapy techniques.](Tasks/19147.mdx)
- [Write treatment plans, case summaries, or progress or other reports related to individual clients or client groups.](Tasks/19154.mdx)
- [Select or prepare artistic media or related equipment or devices to accomplish therapy session objectives.](Tasks/19161.mdx)
- [Analyze or synthesize client data to draw conclusions or make recommendations for art therapy.](Tasks/19140.mdx)
