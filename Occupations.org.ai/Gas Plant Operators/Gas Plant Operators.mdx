# Gas Plant Operators

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-8092.00

Distribute or process gas for utility companies and others by controlling compressors to maintain specified pressures on main pipelines.

## Tasks

- [Monitor equipment functioning, observe temperature, level, and flow gauges, and perform regular unit checks to ensure that all equipment is operating as it should.](Tasks/14287.mdx)
- [Distribute or process gas for utility companies or industrial plants, using panel boards, control boards, and semi-automatic equipment.](Tasks/14299.mdx)
- [Control operation of compressors, scrubbers, evaporators, and refrigeration equipment to liquefy, compress, or regasify natural gas.](Tasks/14288.mdx)
- [Control equipment to regulate flow and pressure of gas to feedlines of boilers, furnaces, and related steam-generating or heating equipment.](Tasks/14300.mdx)
- [Record, review, and compile operations records, test results, and gauge readings such as temperatures, pressures, concentrations, and flows.](Tasks/14290.mdx)
- [Determine causes of abnormal pressure variances, and make corrective recommendations, such as installation of pipes to relieve overloading.](Tasks/14294.mdx)
- [Adjust temperature, pressure, vacuum, level, flow rate, or transfer of gas to maintain processes at required levels or to correct problems.](Tasks/14291.mdx)
- [Collaborate with other operators to solve unit problems.](Tasks/14293.mdx)
- [Monitor transportation and storage of flammable and other potentially dangerous products to ensure that safety guidelines are followed.](Tasks/14286.mdx)
- [Start and shut down plant equipment.](Tasks/14289.mdx)
