# Facilities Managers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 11-3013.00

Plan, direct, or coordinate operations and functionalities of facilities and buildings. May include surrounding grounds or multiple facilities of an organization's campus.

## Tasks

- [Monitor the facility to ensure that it remains safe, secure, and well-maintained.](Tasks/21276.mdx)
- [Oversee the maintenance and repair of machinery, equipment, and electrical and mechanical systems.](Tasks/21278.mdx)
- [Oversee construction and renovation projects to improve efficiency and to ensure that facilities meet environmental, health, and security standards, and comply with government regulations.](Tasks/21277.mdx)
- [Plan, administer, and control budgets for contracts, equipment, and supplies.](Tasks/21280.mdx)
- [Participate in architectural and engineering planning and design, including space and installation management.](Tasks/21279.mdx)
- [Set goals and deadlines for the department.](Tasks/21282.mdx)
- [Conduct classes to teach procedures to staff.](Tasks/21273.mdx)
- [Prepare and review operational reports and schedules to ensure accuracy and efficiency.](Tasks/21281.mdx)
- [Acquire, distribute and store supplies.](Tasks/21272.mdx)
- [Dispose of, or oversee the disposal of, surplus or unclaimed property.](Tasks/21274.mdx)
