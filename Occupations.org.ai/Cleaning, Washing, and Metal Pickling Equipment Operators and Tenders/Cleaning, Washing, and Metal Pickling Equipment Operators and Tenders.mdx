# Cleaning, Washing, and Metal Pickling Equipment Operators and Tenders

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-9192.00

Operate or tend machines to wash or clean products, such as barrels or kegs, glass items, tin plate, food, pulp, coal, plastic, or rubber, to remove impurities.

## Tasks

- [Add specified amounts of chemicals to equipment at required times to maintain solution levels and concentrations.](Tasks/14368.mdx)
- [Observe machine operations, gauges, or thermometers, and adjust controls to maintain specified conditions.](Tasks/14372.mdx)
- [Set controls to regulate temperature and length of cycles, and start conveyors, pumps, agitators, and machines.](Tasks/14373.mdx)
- [Drain, clean, and refill machines or tanks at designated intervals, using cleaning solutions or water.](Tasks/14369.mdx)
- [Operate or tend machines to wash and remove impurities from items such as barrels or kegs, glass products, tin plate surfaces, dried fruit, pulp, animal stock, coal, manufactured articles, plastic, or rubber.](Tasks/14367.mdx)
- [Record gauge readings, materials used, processing times, or test results in production logs.](Tasks/14375.mdx)

- [Draw samples for laboratory analysis, or test solutions for conformance to specifications, such as acidity or specific gravity.](Tasks/14371.mdx)
