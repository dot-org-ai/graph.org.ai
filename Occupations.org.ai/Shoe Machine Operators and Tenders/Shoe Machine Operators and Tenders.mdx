# Shoe Machine Operators and Tenders

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-6042.00

Operate or tend a variety of machines to join, decorate, reinforce, or finish shoes and shoe parts.

## Tasks

- [Inspect finished products to ensure that shoes have been completed according to specifications.](Tasks/24000.mdx)
- [Align parts to be stitched, following seams, edges, or markings, before positioning them under needles.](Tasks/14094.mdx)
- [Operate or tend machines to join, decorate, reinforce, or finish shoes and shoe parts.](Tasks/14100.mdx)
- [Remove and examine shoes, shoe parts, and designs to verify conformance to specifications such as proper embedding of stitches in channels.](Tasks/14090.mdx)
- [Switch on machines, lower pressure feet or rollers to secure parts, and start machine stitching, using hand, foot, or knee controls.](Tasks/14096.mdx)
- [Draw thread through machine guide slots, needles, and presser feet in preparation for stitching, or load rolls of wire through machine axles.](Tasks/14101.mdx)
- [Study work orders or shoe part tags to obtain information about workloads, specifications, and the types of materials to be used.](Tasks/14089.mdx)
- [Perform routine equipment maintenance such as cleaning and lubricating machines or replacing broken needles.](Tasks/14091.mdx)
- [Test machinery to ensure proper functioning before beginning production.](Tasks/14099.mdx)
- [Select and place spools of thread or pre-wound bobbins into shuttles, or onto spindles or loupers of stitching machines.](Tasks/14105.mdx)
