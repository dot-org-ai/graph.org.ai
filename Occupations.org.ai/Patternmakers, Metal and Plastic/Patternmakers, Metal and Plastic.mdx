# Patternmakers, Metal and Plastic

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-4062.00

Lay out, machine, fit, and assemble castings and parts to metal or plastic foundry patterns, core boxes, or match plates.

## Tasks

- [Verify conformance of patterns or template dimensions to specifications, using measuring instruments such as calipers, scales, and micrometers.](Tasks/12038.mdx)
- [Set up and operate machine tools, such as milling machines, lathes, drill presses, and grinders, to machine castings or patterns.](Tasks/12036.mdx)
- [Repair and rework templates and patterns.](Tasks/12042.mdx)
- [Assemble pattern sections, using hand tools, bolts, screws, rivets, glue, or welding equipment.](Tasks/12041.mdx)
- [Read and interpret blueprints or drawings of parts to be cast or patterns to be made, compute dimensions, and plan operational sequences.](Tasks/12037.mdx)
- [Construct platforms, fixtures, and jigs for holding and placing patterns.](Tasks/12045.mdx)
- [Clean and finish patterns or templates, using emery cloths, files, scrapers, and power grinders.](Tasks/12044.mdx)
- [Mark identification numbers or symbols onto patterns or templates.](Tasks/12046.mdx)
- [Program computerized numerical control machine tools.](Tasks/12039.mdx)
- [Create computer models of patterns or parts, using modeling software.](Tasks/21025.mdx)
