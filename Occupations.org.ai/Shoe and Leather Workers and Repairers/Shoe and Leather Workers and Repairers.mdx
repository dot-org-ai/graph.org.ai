# Shoe and Leather Workers and Repairers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-6041.00

Construct, decorate, or repair leather and leather-like products, such as luggage, shoes, and saddles. May use hand tools.

## Tasks

- [Dye, soak, polish, paint, stamp, stitch, stain, buff, or engrave leather or other materials to obtain desired effects, decorations, or shapes.](Tasks/14065.mdx)
- [Cut out parts, following patterns or outlines, using knives, shears, scissors, or machine presses.](Tasks/14062.mdx)
- [Construct, decorate, or repair leather products according to specifications, using sewing machines, needles and thread, leather lacing, glue, clamps, hand tools, or rivets.](Tasks/14063.mdx)
- [Repair and recondition leather products such as trunks, luggage, shoes, saddles, belts, purses, and baseball gloves.](Tasks/14074.mdx)
- [Align and stitch or glue materials such as fabric, fleece, leather, or wood, to join parts.](Tasks/14064.mdx)
- [Inspect articles for defects, and remove damaged or worn parts, using hand tools.](Tasks/14079.mdx)
- [Drill or punch holes and insert or attach metal rings, handles, and fastening hardware, such as buckles.](Tasks/14080.mdx)
- [Prepare inserts, heel pads, and lifts from casts of customers' feet.](Tasks/14087.mdx)
- [Dress and otherwise finish boots or shoes, as by trimming the edges of new soles and heels to the shoe shape.](Tasks/14067.mdx)
- [Attach insoles to shoe lasts, affix shoe uppers, and apply heels and outsoles.](Tasks/14069.mdx)
