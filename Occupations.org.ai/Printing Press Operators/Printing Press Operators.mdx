# Printing Press Operators

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-5112.00

Set up and operate digital, letterpress, lithographic, flexographic, gravure, or other printing machines. Includes short-run offset printing presses.

## Tasks

- [Start presses and pull proofs to check for ink coverage and density, alignment, and registration.](Tasks/19430.mdx)
- [Examine job orders to determine quantities to be printed, stock specifications, colors, or special printing instructions.](Tasks/19423.mdx)
- [Adjust ink fountain flow rates.](Tasks/19417.mdx)
- [Verify that paper and ink meet the specifications for a given job.](Tasks/19431.mdx)
- [Collect and inspect random samples during print runs to identify any necessary adjustments.](Tasks/19421.mdx)
- [Feed paper through press cylinders and adjust feed and tension controls.](Tasks/19424.mdx)
- [Monitor automated press operation systems and respond to fault, error, or alert messages.](Tasks/19427.mdx)
- [Load presses with paper and make necessary adjustments, according to paper size.](Tasks/19426.mdx)
- [Secure printing plates to printing units and adjust tolerances.](Tasks/19429.mdx)
- [Clean ink fountains, plates, or printing unit cylinders when press runs are completed.](Tasks/19419.mdx)
