# Farm Equipment Mechanics and Service Technicians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 49-3041.00

Diagnose, adjust, repair, or overhaul farm machinery and vehicles, such as tractors, harvesters, dairy equipment, and irrigation systems.

## Tasks

- [Reassemble machines and equipment following repair, testing operation and making adjustments, as necessary.](Tasks/13749.mdx)
- [Maintain, repair, and overhaul farm machinery and vehicles, such as tractors, harvesters, and irrigation systems.](Tasks/13750.mdx)
- [Examine and listen to equipment, read inspection reports, and confer with customers to locate and diagnose malfunctions.](Tasks/13751.mdx)
- [Record details of repairs made and parts used.](Tasks/13748.mdx)
- [Dismantle defective machines for repair, using hand tools.](Tasks/13752.mdx)
- [Clean and lubricate parts.](Tasks/13755.mdx)
- [Repair or replace defective parts, using hand tools, milling and woodworking machines, lathes, welding equipment, grinders, or saws.](Tasks/13754.mdx)
- [Test and replace electrical components and wiring, using test meters, soldering equipment, and hand tools.](Tasks/13753.mdx)
- [Tune or overhaul engines.](Tasks/13756.mdx)
- [Drive trucks to haul tools and equipment for on-site repair of large machinery.](Tasks/13757.mdx)
