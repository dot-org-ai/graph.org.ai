# Food Batchmakers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-3092.00

Set up and operate equipment that mixes or blends ingredients used in the manufacturing of food products. Includes candy makers and cheese makers.

## Tasks

- [Record production and test data for each food product batch, such as the ingredients used, temperature, test results, and time cycle.](Tasks/4919.mdx)
- [Clean and sterilize vats and factory processing areas.](Tasks/4921.mdx)
- [Set up, operate, and tend equipment that cooks, mixes, blends, or processes ingredients in the manufacturing of food products, according to formulas or recipes.](Tasks/4924.mdx)
- [Mix or blend ingredients, according to recipes, using a paddle or an agitator, or by controlling vats that heat and mix ingredients.](Tasks/4925.mdx)
- [Follow recipes to produce food products of specified flavor, texture, clarity, bouquet, or color.](Tasks/4926.mdx)
- [Give directions to other workers who are assisting in the batchmaking process.](Tasks/4931.mdx)
- [Select and measure or weigh ingredients, using English or metric measures and balance scales.](Tasks/4927.mdx)
- [Press switches and turn knobs to start, adjust, and regulate equipment, such as beaters, extruders, discharge pipes, and salt pumps.](Tasks/4922.mdx)
- [Determine mixing sequences, based on knowledge of temperature effects and of the solubility of specific ingredients.](Tasks/4929.mdx)
- [Observe and listen to equipment to detect possible malfunctions, such as leaks or plugging, and report malfunctions or undesirable tastes to supervisors.](Tasks/4923.mdx)
