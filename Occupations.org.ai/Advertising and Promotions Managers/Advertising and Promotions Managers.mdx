# Advertising and Promotions Managers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 11-2011.00

Plan, direct, or coordinate advertising policies and programs or produce collateral materials, such as posters, contests, coupons, or giveaways, to create extra interest in the purchase of a product or service for a department, an entire organization, or on an account basis.

## Tasks

- [Plan and prepare advertising and promotional material to increase sales of products or services, working with customers, company officials, sales departments, and advertising agencies.](Tasks/3224.mdx)
- [Inspect layouts and advertising copy, and edit scripts, audio, video, and other promotional material for adherence to specifications.](Tasks/3226.mdx)
- [Confer with department heads or staff to discuss topics such as contracts, selection of advertising media, or product to be advertised.](Tasks/3231.mdx)
- [Coordinate with the media to disseminate advertising.](Tasks/3242.mdx)
- [Coordinate activities of departments, such as sales, graphic arts, media, finance, and research.](Tasks/3227.mdx)
- [Plan and execute advertising policies and strategies for organizations.](Tasks/3238.mdx)
- [Direct, motivate, and monitor the mobilization of a campaign team to advance campaign goals.](Tasks/3237.mdx)
- [Prepare budgets and submit estimates for program costs as part of campaign plan development.](Tasks/3223.mdx)
- [Contact organizations to explain services and facilities offered.](Tasks/3243.mdx)
- [Monitor and analyze sales promotion results to determine cost effectiveness of promotion campaigns.](Tasks/3233.mdx)
