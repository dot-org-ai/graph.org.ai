# Parking Attendants

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 53-6021.00

Park vehicles or issue tickets for customers in a parking lot or garage. May park or tend vehicles in environments such as a car dealership or rental car facility. May collect fee.

## Tasks

- [Take numbered tags from customers, locate vehicles, and deliver vehicles, or provide customers with instructions for locating vehicles.](Tasks/3187.mdx)
- [Inspect vehicles to detect any damage.](Tasks/3196.mdx)
- [Greet customers and open their car doors.](Tasks/3192.mdx)
- [Issue ticket stubs or place numbered tags on windshields, log tags or attach tag to customers' keys, and give customers matching tags for locating parked vehicles.](Tasks/20835.mdx)
- [Perform cash handling tasks, such as making change, balancing and recording cash drawer, or distributing tips.](Tasks/20836.mdx)
- [Explain and calculate parking charges, collect fees from customers, and respond to customer complaints.](Tasks/20837.mdx)
- [Park and retrieve automobiles for customers in parking lots, storage garages, or new car lots.](Tasks/3191.mdx)
- [Provide customer assistance and information, such as giving directions or handling wheelchairs.](Tasks/20838.mdx)
- [Keep parking areas clean and orderly to ensure that space usage is maximized.](Tasks/3188.mdx)
- [Call emergency responders or the proper authorities and provide motorist assistance, such as giving directions or helping jump start a stalled vehicle.](Tasks/20839.mdx)
