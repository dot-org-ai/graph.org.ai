# Public Relations Managers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 11-2032.00

Plan, direct, or coordinate activities designed to create or maintain a favorable public image or raise issue awareness for their organization or client.

## Tasks

- [Assign, supervise, and review the activities of public relations staff.](Tasks/21226.mdx)
- [Confer with labor relations managers to develop internal communications that keep employees informed of company activities.](Tasks/21227.mdx)
- [Design and edit promotional publications, such as brochures.](Tasks/21228.mdx)
- [Develop and maintain the company's corporate image and identity, which includes the use of logos and signage.](Tasks/21229.mdx)
- [Develop, implement, or maintain crisis communication plans.](Tasks/21230.mdx)
- [Direct activities of external agencies, establishments, or departments that develop and implement communication strategies and information programs.](Tasks/21231.mdx)
- [Draft speeches for company executives and arrange interviews and other forms of contact for them.](Tasks/21232.mdx)
- [Establish and maintain effective working relationships with clients, government officials, and media representatives and use these relationships to develop new business opportunities.](Tasks/21233.mdx)
- [Evaluate advertising and promotion programs for compatibility with public relations efforts.](Tasks/21234.mdx)
- [Facilitate consumer relations or the relationship between parts of the company, such as the managers and employees, or different branch offices.](Tasks/21235.mdx)
