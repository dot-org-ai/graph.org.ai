# Interpreters and Translators

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 27-3091.00

Interpret oral or sign language, or translate written text from one language into another.

## Tasks

- [Follow ethical codes that protect the confidentiality of information.](Tasks/9326.mdx)
- [Translate messages simultaneously or consecutively into specified languages, orally or by using hand signs, maintaining message content, context, and style as much as possible.](Tasks/9328.mdx)
- [Listen to speakers' statements to determine meanings and to prepare translations, using electronic listening systems as necessary.](Tasks/9335.mdx)
- [Compile terminology and information to be used in translations, including technical terms such as those for legal or medical material.](Tasks/9333.mdx)
- [Refer to reference materials, such as dictionaries, lexicons, encyclopedias, and computerized terminology banks, as needed to ensure translation accuracy.](Tasks/9332.mdx)
- [Check translations of technical terms and terminology to ensure that they are accurate and remain consistent throughout translation revisions.](Tasks/9330.mdx)
- [Identify and resolve conflicts related to the meanings of words, concepts, practices, or behaviors.](Tasks/9327.mdx)
- [Compile information on content and context of information to be translated and on intended audience.](Tasks/9337.mdx)
- [Adapt translations to students' cognitive and grade levels, collaborating with educational team members as necessary.](Tasks/9334.mdx)
- [Check original texts or confer with authors to ensure that translations retain the content, meaning, and feeling of the original material.](Tasks/9336.mdx)
