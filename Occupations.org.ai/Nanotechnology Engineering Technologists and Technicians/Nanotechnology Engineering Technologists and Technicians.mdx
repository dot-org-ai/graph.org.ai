# Nanotechnology Engineering Technologists and Technicians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 17-3026.01

Implement production processes and operate commercial-scale production equipment to produce, test, or modify materials, devices, or systems of unique molecular or macromolecular composition. Operate advanced microscopy equipment to manipulate nanoscale objects. Work under the supervision of nanoengineering staff.

## Tasks

- [Produce images or measurements, using tools or techniques such as atomic force microscopy, scanning electron microscopy, optical microscopy, particle size analysis, or zeta potential analysis.](Tasks/22047.mdx)
- [Maintain accurate record or batch-record documentation of nanoproduction.](Tasks/22063.mdx)
- [Calibrate nanotechnology equipment, such as weighing, testing, or production equipment.](Tasks/22053.mdx)
- [Maintain work area according to cleanroom or other processing standards.](Tasks/22046.mdx)
- [Repair nanotechnology processing or testing equipment or submit work orders for equipment repair.](Tasks/22049.mdx)
- [Assist nanoscientists or engineers in processing or characterizing materials according to physical or chemical properties.](Tasks/22068.mdx)
- [Collaborate with scientists or engineers to design or conduct experiments for the development of nanotechnology materials, components, devices, or systems.](Tasks/22048.mdx)
- [Operate nanotechnology compounding, testing, processing, or production equipment in accordance with appropriate standard operating procedures, good manufacturing practices, hazardous material restrictions, or health and safety requirements.](Tasks/22045.mdx)
- [Monitor hazardous waste cleanup procedures to ensure proper application of nanocomposites or accomplishment of objectives.](Tasks/22054.mdx)
- [Monitor equipment during operation to ensure adherence to specifications for characteristics such as pressure, temperature, or flow.](Tasks/22051.mdx)
