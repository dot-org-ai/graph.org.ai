# Helpers--Electricians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-3013.00

Help electricians by performing duties requiring less skill. Duties include using, supplying, or holding materials or tools, and cleaning work area and equipment.

## Tasks

- [Strip insulation from wire ends, using wire stripping pliers, and attach wires to terminals for subsequent soldering.](Tasks/4840.mdx)

- [Measure, cut, and bend wire and conduit, using measuring instruments and hand tools.](Tasks/4833.mdx)

- [Maintain tools, vehicles, and equipment and keep parts and supplies in order.](Tasks/4834.mdx)
