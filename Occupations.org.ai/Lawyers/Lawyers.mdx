# Lawyers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 23-1011.00

Represent clients in criminal and civil litigation and other legal proceedings, draw up legal documents, or manage or advise clients on legal transactions. May specialize in a single area or may practice broadly in many areas of law.

## Tasks

- [Interpret laws, rulings and regulations for individuals and businesses.](Tasks/3774.mdx)
- [Analyze the probable outcomes of cases, using knowledge of legal precedents.](Tasks/3775.mdx)
- [Gather evidence to formulate defense or to initiate legal actions by such means as interviewing clients and witnesses to ascertain the facts of a case.](Tasks/3778.mdx)
- [Represent clients in court or before government agencies.](Tasks/3779.mdx)
- [Evaluate findings and develop strategies and arguments in preparation for presentation of cases.](Tasks/3777.mdx)
- [Advise clients concerning business transactions, claim liability, advisability of prosecuting or defending lawsuits, or legal rights and obligations.](Tasks/3773.mdx)
- [Examine legal data to determine advisability of defending or prosecuting lawsuit.](Tasks/3780.mdx)
- [Prepare, draft, and review legal documents, such as wills, deeds, patent applications, mortgages, leases, and contracts.](Tasks/20877.mdx)
- [Study Constitution, statutes, decisions, regulations, and ordinances of quasi-judicial bodies to determine ramifications for cases.](Tasks/3783.mdx)
- [Negotiate settlements of civil disputes.](Tasks/3786.mdx)
