# Digital Forensics Analysts

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 15-1299.06

Conduct investigations on computer-based crimes establishing documentary or physical evidence, such as digital media and logs associated with cyber intrusion incidents. Analyze digital evidence and investigate computer security incidents to derive information in support of system and network vulnerability mitigation. Preserve and present computer-related evidence in support of criminal, fraud, counterintelligence, or law enforcement investigations.

## Tasks

- [Adhere to legal policies and procedures related to handling digital media.](Tasks/21786.mdx)
- [Analyze log files or other digital information to identify the perpetrators of network intrusions.](Tasks/21787.mdx)
- [Conduct predictive or reactive analyses on security measures to support cyber security initiatives.](Tasks/21788.mdx)
- [Create system images or capture network settings from information technology environments to preserve as evidence.](Tasks/21789.mdx)
- [Develop plans for investigating alleged computer crimes, violations, or suspicious activity.](Tasks/21790.mdx)
- [Develop policies or requirements for data collection, processing, or reporting.](Tasks/21791.mdx)
- [Duplicate digital evidence to use for data recovery and analysis procedures.](Tasks/21792.mdx)
- [Identify or develop reverse-engineering tools to improve system capabilities or detect vulnerabilities.](Tasks/21793.mdx)
- [Maintain cyber defense software or hardware to support responses to cyber incidents.](Tasks/21794.mdx)
- [Maintain knowledge of laws, regulations, policies or other issuances pertaining to digital forensics or information privacy.](Tasks/21795.mdx)
