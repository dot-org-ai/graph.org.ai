# Foreign Language and Literature Teachers, Postsecondary

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 25-1124.00

Teach languages and literature courses in languages other than English. Includes teachers of American Sign Language (ASL). Includes both teachers primarily engaged in teaching and those who do a combination of teaching and research.

## Tasks

- [Prepare course materials, such as syllabi, homework assignments, and handouts.](Tasks/6335.mdx)
- [Maintain student attendance records, grades, and other required records.](Tasks/6337.mdx)
- [Evaluate and grade students' class work, assignments, and papers.](Tasks/6334.mdx)
- [Initiate, facilitate, and moderate classroom discussions.](Tasks/6336.mdx)
- [Prepare and deliver lectures to undergraduate or graduate students on topics such as how to speak and write a foreign language and the cultural aspects of areas where a particular language is used.](Tasks/6340.mdx)
- [Conduct research in a particular field of knowledge and publish findings in scholarly journals, books, or electronic media.](Tasks/6345.mdx)
