# Remote Sensing Scientists and Technologists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 19-2099.01

Apply remote sensing principles and methods to analyze data and solve problems in areas such as natural resource management, urban planning, or homeland security. May develop new sensor systems, analytical techniques, or new applications for existing systems.

## Tasks

- [Manage or analyze data obtained from remote sensing systems to obtain meaningful results.](Tasks/18270.mdx)
- [Analyze data acquired from aircraft, satellites, or ground-based platforms, using statistical analysis software, image analysis software, or Geographic Information Systems (GIS).](Tasks/18256.mdx)
- [Integrate other geospatial data sources into projects.](Tasks/18258.mdx)
- [Organize and maintain geospatial data and associated documentation.](Tasks/18260.mdx)
- [Compile and format image data to increase its usefulness.](Tasks/18265.mdx)
- [Prepare or deliver reports or presentations of geospatial project information.](Tasks/18259.mdx)
- [Discuss project goals, equipment requirements, or methodologies with colleagues or team members.](Tasks/18267.mdx)
- [Process aerial or satellite imagery to create products such as land cover maps.](Tasks/18261.mdx)
- [Design or implement strategies for collection, analysis, or display of geographic data.](Tasks/18262.mdx)
- [Develop or build databases for remote sensing or related geospatial project information.](Tasks/18257.mdx)
