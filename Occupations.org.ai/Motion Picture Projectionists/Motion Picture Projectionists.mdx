# Motion Picture Projectionists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 39-3021.00

Set up and operate motion picture projection and related sound reproduction equipment.

## Tasks

- [Monitor operations to ensure that standards for sound and image projection quality are met.](Tasks/8030.mdx)
- [Start projectors and open shutters to project images onto screens.](Tasks/8029.mdx)
- [Open and close facilities according to rules and schedules.](Tasks/8040.mdx)
- [Operate equipment to show films in a number of theaters simultaneously.](Tasks/8031.mdx)
- [Perform regular maintenance tasks, such as rotating or replacing xenon bulbs, cleaning projectors and lenses, lubricating machinery, and keeping electrical contacts clean and tight.](Tasks/21201.mdx)
- [Set up and adjust picture projectors and screens to achieve proper size, illumination, and focus of images, and proper volume and tone of sound.](Tasks/8034.mdx)
- [Inspect projection equipment prior to operation to ensure proper working order.](Tasks/8035.mdx)
- [Perform minor repairs, such as replacing worn sprockets, or notify maintenance personnel of the need for major repairs.](Tasks/8039.mdx)
- [Set up and inspect curtain and screen controls.](Tasks/8042.mdx)
- [Coordinate equipment operation with presentation of supplemental material, such as music, oral commentaries, or sound effects.](Tasks/8046.mdx)
