# Conveyor Operators and Tenders

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 53-7011.00

Control or tend conveyors or conveyor systems that move materials or products to and from stockpiles, processing stations, departments, or vehicles. May control speed and routing of materials or products.

## Tasks

- [Inform supervisors of equipment malfunctions that need to be addressed.](Tasks/10748.mdx)
- [Observe conveyor operations and monitor lights, dials, and gauges to maintain specified operating levels and to detect equipment malfunctions.](Tasks/10750.mdx)
- [Record production data such as weights, types, quantities, and storage locations of materials, as well as equipment performance problems and downtime.](Tasks/10747.mdx)
- [Load, unload, or adjust materials or products on conveyors by hand, by using lifts, hoists, and scoops, or by opening gates, chutes, or hoppers.](Tasks/10757.mdx)
- [Stop equipment or machinery and clear jams, using poles, bars, and hand tools, or remove damaged materials from conveyors.](Tasks/10755.mdx)
- [Distribute materials, supplies, and equipment to work stations, using lifts and trucks.](Tasks/10760.mdx)
- [Observe packages moving along conveyors to identify packages, detect defective packaging, and perform quality control.](Tasks/21030.mdx)
- [Collect samples of materials or products, checking them to ensure conformance to specifications or sending them to laboratories for analysis.](Tasks/10756.mdx)
- [Position deflector bars, gates, chutes, or spouts to divert flow of materials from one conveyor onto another conveyor.](Tasks/10744.mdx)
- [Repair or replace equipment components or parts such as blades, rolls, and pumps.](Tasks/10753.mdx)
