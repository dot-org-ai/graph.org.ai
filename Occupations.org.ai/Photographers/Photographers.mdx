# Photographers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 27-4021.00

Photograph people, landscapes, merchandise, or other subjects. May use lighting equipment to enhance a subject's appearance. May use editing software to produce finished images and prints. Includes commercial and industrial photographers, scientific photographers, and photojournalists.

## Tasks

- [Adjust apertures, shutter speeds, and camera focus according to a combination of factors, such as lighting, field depth, subject motion, film type, and film speed.](Tasks/9344.mdx)
- [Create artificial light, using flashes and reflectors.](Tasks/9346.mdx)
- [Determine desired images and picture composition, selecting and adjusting subjects, equipment, and lighting to achieve desired effects.](Tasks/9347.mdx)
- [Transfer photographs to computers for editing, archiving, and electronic transmission.](Tasks/20558.mdx)
- [Use traditional or digital cameras, along with a variety of equipment, such as tripods, filters, and flash attachments.](Tasks/9345.mdx)
- [Manipulate and enhance scanned or digital images to create desired effects, using computers and specialized software.](Tasks/9352.mdx)
- [Take pictures of individuals, families, and small groups, either in studio or on location.](Tasks/9343.mdx)
- [Enhance, retouch, and resize photographs and negatives, using airbrushing and other techniques.](Tasks/9357.mdx)
- [Test equipment prior to use to ensure that it is in good working order.](Tasks/9349.mdx)
- [Estimate or measure light levels, distances, and numbers of exposures needed, using measuring devices and formulas.](Tasks/9351.mdx)
