# Real Estate Brokers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 41-9021.00

Operate real estate office, or work for commercial real estate firm, overseeing real estate transactions. Other duties usually include selling real estate or renting properties and arranging loans.

## Tasks

- [Sell, for a fee, real estate owned by others.](Tasks/4599.mdx)

- [Act as an intermediary in negotiations between buyers and sellers over property prices and settlement details and during the closing of sales.](Tasks/4603.mdx)
- [Generate lists of properties for sale, their locations, descriptions, and available financing options, using computers.](Tasks/4604.mdx)
- [Manage or operate real estate offices, handling associated business details.](Tasks/4610.mdx)
