# Sound Engineering Technicians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 27-4014.00

Assemble and operate equipment to record, synchronize, mix, edit, or reproduce sound, including music, voices, or sound effects, for theater, video, film, television, podcasts, sporting events, and other productions.

## Tasks

- [Confer with producers, performers, and others to determine and achieve the desired sound for a production, such as a musical recording or a film.](Tasks/7720.mdx)
- [Regulate volume level and sound quality during recording sessions, using control consoles.](Tasks/7722.mdx)
- [Record speech, music, and other sounds on recording media, using recording equipment.](Tasks/7727.mdx)
- [Separate instruments, vocals, and other sounds, and combine sounds during the mixing or postproduction stage.](Tasks/7729.mdx)
- [Set up, test, and adjust recording equipment for recording sessions and live performances.](Tasks/18662.mdx)
- [Report equipment problems and ensure that required repairs are made.](Tasks/7724.mdx)
- [Prepare for recording sessions by performing such activities as selecting and setting up microphones.](Tasks/7723.mdx)
- [Mix and edit voices, music, and taped sound effects for live performances and for prerecorded events, using sound mixing boards.](Tasks/7725.mdx)
- [Keep logs of recordings.](Tasks/7730.mdx)
- [Tear down equipment after event completion.](Tasks/18660.mdx)
