# Model Makers, Metal and Plastic

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-4061.00

Set up and operate machines, such as lathes, milling and engraving machines, and jig borers to make working models of metal or plastic objects. Includes template makers.

## Tasks

- [Study blueprints, drawings, and sketches to determine material dimensions, required equipment, and operations sequences.](Tasks/12022.mdx)
- [Set up and operate machines, such as lathes, drill presses, punch presses, or bandsaws, to fabricate prototypes or models.](Tasks/12023.mdx)
- [Program computer numerical control (CNC) machines to fabricate model parts.](Tasks/20924.mdx)
- [Inspect and test products to verify conformance to specifications, using precision measuring instruments or circuit testers.](Tasks/12024.mdx)
- [Cut, shape, and form metal parts, using lathes, power saws, snips, power brakes and shears, files, and mallets.](Tasks/12025.mdx)
- [Rework or alter component model or parts as required to ensure that products meet standards.](Tasks/12030.mdx)
- [Drill, countersink, and ream holes in parts and assemblies for bolts, screws, and other fasteners, using power tools.](Tasks/12027.mdx)
- [Grind, file, and sand parts to finished dimensions.](Tasks/12028.mdx)
- [Devise and construct tools, dies, molds, jigs, and fixtures, or modify existing tools and equipment.](Tasks/12034.mdx)
- [Record specifications, production operations, and final dimensions of models for use in establishing operating standards and procedures.](Tasks/12029.mdx)
