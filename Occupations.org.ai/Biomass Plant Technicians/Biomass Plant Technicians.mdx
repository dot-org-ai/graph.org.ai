# Biomass Plant Technicians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-8013.03

Control and monitor biomass plant activities and perform maintenance as needed.

## Tasks

- [Operate biomass fuel-burning boiler or biomass fuel gasification system equipment in accordance with specifications or instructions.](Tasks/17861.mdx)
- [Perform tests of water chemistry in boilers.](Tasks/21208.mdx)
- [Operate high-pressure steam boiler or water chiller equipment for electrical cogeneration operations.](Tasks/17864.mdx)
- [Operate equipment to heat biomass, using knowledge of controls, combustion, and firing mechanisms.](Tasks/17862.mdx)
- [Operate equipment to start, stop, or regulate biomass-fueled generators, generator units, boilers, engines, or auxiliary systems.](Tasks/17863.mdx)
- [Inspect biomass power plant or processing equipment, recording or reporting damage and mechanical problems.](Tasks/17860.mdx)
- [Record or report operational data, such as readings on meters, instruments, and gauges.](Tasks/17866.mdx)
- [Operate valves, pumps, engines, or generators to control and adjust production of biofuels or biomass-fueled power.](Tasks/17855.mdx)
- [Calculate, measure, load, or mix biomass feedstock for power generation.](Tasks/17858.mdx)
- [Clean work areas to ensure compliance with safety regulations.](Tasks/17867.mdx)
