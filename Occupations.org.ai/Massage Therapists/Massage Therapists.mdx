# Massage Therapists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 31-9011.00

Perform therapeutic massages of soft tissues and joints. May assist in the assessment of range of motion and muscle strength, or propose client therapy plans.

## Tasks

- [Confer with clients about their medical histories and problems with stress or pain to determine how massage will be most helpful.](Tasks/7878.mdx)
- [Massage and knead muscles and soft tissues of the body to provide treatment for medical conditions, injuries, or wellness maintenance.](Tasks/7880.mdx)
- [Maintain massage areas by restocking supplies or sanitizing equipment.](Tasks/23928.mdx)
- [Apply finger and hand pressure to specific points of the body.](Tasks/7879.mdx)
- [Develop and propose client treatment plans that specify which types of massage are to be used.](Tasks/7884.mdx)
- [Maintain treatment records.](Tasks/7881.mdx)
- [Assess clients' soft tissue condition, joint quality and function, muscle strength, and range of motion.](Tasks/7883.mdx)
- [Provide clients with guidance and information about techniques for postural improvement and stretching, strengthening, relaxation, and rehabilitative exercises.](Tasks/7882.mdx)
- [Treat clients in professional settings or travel to clients' offices and homes.](Tasks/7887.mdx)
- [Refer clients to other types of therapists when necessary.](Tasks/7885.mdx)
