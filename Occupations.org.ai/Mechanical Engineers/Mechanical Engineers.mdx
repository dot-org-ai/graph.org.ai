# Mechanical Engineers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 17-2141.00

Perform engineering duties in planning and designing tools, engines, machines, and other mechanically functioning equipment. Oversee installation, operation, maintenance, and repair of equipment such as centralized heat, gas, water, and steam systems.

## Tasks

- [Read and interpret blueprints, technical drawings, schematics, or computer-generated reports.](Tasks/1411.mdx)
- [Research, design, evaluate, install, operate, or maintain mechanical products, equipment, systems or processes to meet requirements.](Tasks/20733.mdx)
- [Specify system components or direct modification of products to ensure conformance with engineering design, performance specifications, or environmental regulations.](Tasks/20736.mdx)
- [Confer with engineers or other personnel to implement operating procedures, resolve system malfunctions, or provide technical information.](Tasks/1412.mdx)
- [Investigate equipment failures or difficulties to diagnose faulty operation and recommend remedial actions.](Tasks/20734.mdx)
- [Recommend design modifications to eliminate machine or system malfunctions.](Tasks/1421.mdx)
- [Research and analyze customer design proposals, specifications, manuals, or other data to evaluate the feasibility, cost, or maintenance requirements of designs or applications.](Tasks/1413.mdx)
- [Provide technical customer service.](Tasks/20741.mdx)
- [Oversee installation, operation, maintenance, or repair to ensure that machines or equipment are installed and functioning according to specifications.](Tasks/1419.mdx)
- [Assist drafters in developing the structural design of products, using drafting tools or computer-assisted drafting equipment or software.](Tasks/20737.mdx)
