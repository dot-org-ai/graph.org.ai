# Exercise Physiologists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 29-1128.00

Assess, plan, or implement fitness programs that include exercise or physical activities such as those designed to improve cardiorespiratory function, body composition, muscular strength, muscular endurance, or flexibility.

## Tasks

- [Develop exercise programs to improve participant strength, flexibility, endurance, or circulatory functioning, in accordance with exercise science standards, regulatory requirements, and credentialing requirements.](Tasks/19193.mdx)
- [Provide emergency or other appropriate medical care to participants with symptoms or signs of physical distress.](Tasks/19212.mdx)
- [Demonstrate correct use of exercise equipment or performance of exercise routines.](Tasks/19192.mdx)
- [Recommend methods to increase lifestyle physical activity.](Tasks/19201.mdx)
- [Interpret exercise program participant data to evaluate progress or identify needed program changes.](Tasks/19195.mdx)
- [Prescribe individualized exercise programs, specifying equipment, such as treadmill, exercise bicycle, ergometers, or perceptual goggles.](Tasks/19199.mdx)
- [Provide clinical oversight of exercise for participants at all risk levels.](Tasks/19200.mdx)
- [Explain exercise program or physiological testing procedures to participants.](Tasks/19194.mdx)
- [Interview participants to obtain medical history or assess participant goals.](Tasks/19207.mdx)
- [Assess physical performance requirements to aid in the development of individualized recovery or rehabilitation exercise programs.](Tasks/19203.mdx)
