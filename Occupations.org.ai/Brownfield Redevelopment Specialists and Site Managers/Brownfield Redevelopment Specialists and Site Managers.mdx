# Brownfield Redevelopment Specialists and Site Managers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 11-9199.11

Plan and direct cleanup and redevelopment of contaminated properties for reuse. Does not include properties sufficiently contaminated to qualify as Superfund sites.

## Tasks

- [Identify environmental contamination sources.](Tasks/15849.mdx)
- [Coordinate on-site activities for environmental cleanup or remediation projects to ensure compliance with environmental laws, standards, regulations, or other requirements.](Tasks/15857.mdx)
- [Identify and apply for project funding.](Tasks/23901.mdx)
- [Plan or implement brownfield redevelopment projects to ensure safety, quality, and compliance with applicable standards or requirements.](Tasks/15848.mdx)
- [Estimate costs for environmental cleanup and remediation of land redevelopment projects.](Tasks/15850.mdx)
- [Conduct quantitative risk assessments for human health, environmental, or other risks.](Tasks/15858.mdx)
- [Design or implement plans for surface or ground water remediation.](Tasks/15852.mdx)
- [Design or implement measures to improve the water, air, and soil quality of military test sites, abandoned mine land, or other contaminated sites.](Tasks/15854.mdx)
- [Review or evaluate environmental remediation project proposals.](Tasks/15839.mdx)
