# Archivists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 25-4011.00

Appraise, edit, and direct safekeeping of permanent records and historically valuable documents. Participate in research activities based on archival materials.

## Tasks

- [Organize archival records and develop classification systems to facilitate access to archival materials.](Tasks/7631.mdx)
- [Provide reference services and assistance for users needing archival materials.](Tasks/7633.mdx)
- [Prepare archival records, such as document descriptions, to allow easy access to information.](Tasks/7635.mdx)
- [Create and maintain accessible, retrievable computer archives and databases, incorporating current advances in electronic information storage technology.](Tasks/7630.mdx)
- [Establish and administer policy guidelines concerning public access and use of materials.](Tasks/7637.mdx)
- [Direct activities of workers who assist in arranging, cataloguing, exhibiting, and maintaining collections of valuable materials.](Tasks/7634.mdx)
- [Preserve records, documents, and objects, copying records to film, videotape, audiotape, disk, or computer formats as necessary.](Tasks/7636.mdx)
- [Research and record the origins and historical significance of archival materials.](Tasks/7639.mdx)
- [Locate new materials and direct their acquisition and display.](Tasks/7638.mdx)
- [Authenticate and appraise historical documents and archival materials.](Tasks/7632.mdx)
