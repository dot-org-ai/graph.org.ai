# Mixing and Blending Machine Setters, Operators, and Tenders

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-9023.00

Set up, operate, or tend machines to mix or blend materials, such as chemicals, tobacco, liquids, color pigments, or explosive ingredients.

## Tasks

- [Weigh or measure materials, ingredients, or products to ensure conformance to requirements.](Tasks/12397.mdx)
- [Read work orders to determine production specifications or information.](Tasks/12410.mdx)
- [Observe production or monitor equipment to ensure safe and efficient operation.](Tasks/12402.mdx)
- [Mix or blend ingredients by starting machines and mixing for specified times.](Tasks/20682.mdx)
- [Stop mixing or blending machines when specified product qualities are obtained and open valves and start pumps to transfer mixtures.](Tasks/12403.mdx)
- [Compound or process ingredients or dyes, according to formulas.](Tasks/12411.mdx)
- [Examine materials, ingredients, or products visually or with hands to ensure conformance to established standards.](Tasks/12406.mdx)
- [Operate or tend machines to mix or blend any of a wide variety of materials, such as spices, dough batter, tobacco, fruit juices, chemicals, livestock feed, food products, color pigments, or explosive ingredients.](Tasks/12400.mdx)
- [Dump or pour specified amounts of materials into machinery or equipment.](Tasks/12401.mdx)
- [Record operational or production data on specified forms.](Tasks/12407.mdx)
