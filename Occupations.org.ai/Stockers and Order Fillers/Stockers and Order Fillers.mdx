# Stockers and Order Fillers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 53-7065.00

Receive, store, and issue merchandise, materials, equipment, and other items from stockroom, warehouse, or storage yard to fill shelves, racks, tables, or customers' orders. May operate power equipment to fill orders. May mark prices on merchandise and set up sales displays.

## Tasks

- [Complete order receipts.](Tasks/23871.mdx)
- [Answer customers' questions about merchandise and advise customers on merchandise selection.](Tasks/23877.mdx)
- [Issue or distribute materials, products, parts, and supplies to customers or coworkers, based on information from incoming requisitions.](Tasks/23886.mdx)
- [Keep records of out-going orders.](Tasks/23895.mdx)
- [Stock shelves, racks, cases, bins, and tables with new or transferred merchandise.](Tasks/23880.mdx)
- [Operate equipment such as forklifts.](Tasks/23899.mdx)
- [Stamp, attach, or change price tags on merchandise, referring to price list.](Tasks/23879.mdx)
- [Obtain merchandise from bins or shelves.](Tasks/23872.mdx)
- [Receive and count stock items, and record data manually or on computer.](Tasks/23878.mdx)
- [Read orders to ascertain catalog numbers, sizes, colors, and quantities of merchandise.](Tasks/23873.mdx)
