# Sales Representatives, Wholesale and Manufacturing, Except Technical and Scientific Products

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 41-4012.00

Sell goods for wholesalers or manufacturers to businesses or groups of individuals. Work requires substantial knowledge of items sold.

## Tasks

- [Answer customers' questions about products, prices, availability, product uses, and credit terms.](Tasks/8113.mdx)
- [Recommend products to customers, based on customers' needs and interests.](Tasks/8114.mdx)
- [Estimate or quote prices, credit or contract terms, warranties, and delivery dates.](Tasks/8116.mdx)
- [Consult with clients after sales or contract signings to resolve problems and to provide ongoing support.](Tasks/8117.mdx)
- [Prepare sales contracts and order forms.](Tasks/20267.mdx)
- [Provide customers with product samples and catalogs.](Tasks/8119.mdx)
- [Monitor market conditions, product innovations, and competitors' products, prices, and sales.](Tasks/8122.mdx)
- [Perform administrative duties, such as preparing sales budgets and reports, keeping sales records, and filing expense account reports.](Tasks/8124.mdx)
- [Contact regular and prospective customers to demonstrate products, explain product features, and solicit orders.](Tasks/8115.mdx)
- [Identify prospective customers by using business directories, following leads from existing clients, participating in organizations and clubs, and attending trade shows and conferences.](Tasks/8120.mdx)
