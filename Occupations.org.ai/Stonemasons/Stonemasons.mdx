# Stonemasons

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-2022.00

Build stone structures, such as piers, walls, and abutments. Lay walks, curbstones, or special types of masonry for vats, tanks, and floors.

## Tasks

- [Set vertical and horizontal alignment of structures, using plumb bob, gauge line, and level.](Tasks/8768.mdx)
- [Lay out wall patterns or foundations, using straight edge, rule, or staked lines.](Tasks/8766.mdx)
- [Set stone or marble in place, according to layout or pattern.](Tasks/8771.mdx)
- [Remove wedges, fill joints between stones, finish joints between stones, using a trowel, and smooth the mortar to an attractive finish, using a tuck pointer.](Tasks/8770.mdx)
- [Clean excess mortar or grout from surface of marble, stone, or monument, using sponge, brush, water, or acid.](Tasks/8772.mdx)
- [Shape, trim, face and cut marble or stone preparatory to setting, using power saws, cutting equipment, and hand tools.](Tasks/8767.mdx)
- [Mix mortar or grout and pour or spread mortar or grout on marble slabs, stone, or foundation.](Tasks/8769.mdx)
- [Construct and install prefabricated masonry units.](Tasks/8779.mdx)
- [Smooth, polish, and bevel surfaces, using hand tools and power tools.](Tasks/8775.mdx)
- [Lay brick to build shells of chimneys and smokestacks or to line or reline industrial furnaces, kilns, boilers and similar installations.](Tasks/8773.mdx)
