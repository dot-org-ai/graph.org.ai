# Animal Scientists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 19-1011.00

Conduct research in the genetics, nutrition, reproduction, growth, and development of domestic farm animals.

## Tasks

- [Study nutritional requirements of animals and nutritive values of animal feed materials.](Tasks/5415.mdx)
- [Write up or orally communicate research findings to the scientific community, producers, and the public.](Tasks/20865.mdx)
- [Develop improved practices in feeding, housing, sanitation, or parasite and disease control of animals.](Tasks/5417.mdx)
- [Advise producers about improved products and techniques that could enhance their animal production efforts.](Tasks/5414.mdx)
- [Conduct research concerning animal nutrition, breeding, or management to improve products or processes.](Tasks/5413.mdx)
- [Study effects of management practices, processing methods, feed, or environmental conditions on quality and quantity of animal products, such as eggs and milk.](Tasks/5416.mdx)
- [Research and control animal selection and breeding practices to increase production efficiency and improve animal quality.](Tasks/5418.mdx)
- [Determine genetic composition of animal populations and heritability of traits, using principles of genetics.](Tasks/5419.mdx)
- [Crossbreed animals with existing strains or cross strains to obtain new combinations of desirable characteristics.](Tasks/5420.mdx)
