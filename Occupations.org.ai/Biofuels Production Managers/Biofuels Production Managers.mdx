# Biofuels Production Managers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 11-3051.03

Manage biofuels production and plant operations. Collect and process information on plant production and performance, diagnose problems, and design corrective procedures.

## Tasks

- [Supervise production employees in the manufacturing of biofuels, such as biodiesel or ethanol.](Tasks/15454.mdx)
- [Manage operations at biofuels power generation facilities, including production, shipping, maintenance, or quality assurance activities.](Tasks/15462.mdx)
- [Provide direction to employees to ensure compliance with biofuels plant safety, environmental, or operational standards and regulations.](Tasks/15450.mdx)
- [Confer with technical and supervisory personnel to report or resolve conditions affecting biofuels plant safety, operational efficiency, and product quality.](Tasks/15453.mdx)
- [Review logs, datasheets, or reports to ensure adequate production levels or to identify abnormalities with biofuels production equipment or processes.](Tasks/15456.mdx)
- [Monitor meters, flow gauges, or other real-time data to ensure proper operation of biofuels production equipment, implementing corrective measures as needed.](Tasks/15458.mdx)
- [Adjust temperature, pressure, vacuum, level, flow rate, or transfer of biofuels to maintain processes at required levels.](Tasks/15461.mdx)
- [Provide training to subordinate or new employees to improve biofuels plant safety or increase the production of biofuels.](Tasks/15449.mdx)
- [Shut down and restart biofuels plant or equipment in emergency situations or for equipment maintenance, repairs, or replacements.](Tasks/15455.mdx)
- [Monitor transportation and storage of flammable or other potentially dangerous feedstocks or products to ensure adherence to safety guidelines.](Tasks/15451.mdx)
