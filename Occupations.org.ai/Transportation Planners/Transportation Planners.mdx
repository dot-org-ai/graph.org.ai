# Transportation Planners

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 19-3099.01

Prepare studies for proposed transportation projects. Gather, compile, and analyze data. Study the use and operation of transportation systems. Develop transportation models or simulations.

## Tasks

- [Define regional or local transportation planning problems or priorities.](Tasks/16953.mdx)
- [Participate in public meetings or hearings to explain planning proposals, to gather feedback from those affected by projects, or to achieve consensus on project designs.](Tasks/16945.mdx)
- [Prepare reports or recommendations on transportation planning.](Tasks/16943.mdx)
- [Collaborate with engineers to research, analyze, or resolve complex transportation design issues.](Tasks/16951.mdx)
- [Recommend transportation system improvements or projects, based on economic, population, land-use, or traffic projections.](Tasks/16952.mdx)
- [Develop computer models to address transportation planning issues.](Tasks/16948.mdx)
- [Analyze information related to transportation, such as land use policies, environmental impact of projects, or long-range planning needs.](Tasks/16954.mdx)
- [Interpret data from traffic modeling software, geographic information systems, or associated databases.](Tasks/20976.mdx)
- [Design transportation surveys to identify areas of public concern.](Tasks/16949.mdx)
- [Collaborate with other professionals to develop sustainable transportation strategies at the local, regional, or national level.](Tasks/19779.mdx)
