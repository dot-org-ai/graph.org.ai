# Customs and Border Protection Officers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 33-3051.04

Investigate and inspect persons, common carriers, goods, and merchandise, arriving in or departing from the United States or between states to detect violations of immigration and customs laws and regulations.

## Tasks

- [Examine immigration applications, visas, and passports and interview persons to determine eligibility for admission, residence, and travel in the U.S.](Tasks/9450.mdx)
- [Detain persons found to be in violation of customs or immigration laws and arrange for legal action, such as deportation.](Tasks/9451.mdx)
- [Inspect cargo, baggage, and personal articles entering or leaving U.S. for compliance with revenue laws and U.S. customs regulations.](Tasks/9454.mdx)
- [Locate and seize contraband, undeclared merchandise, and vehicles, aircraft, or boats that contain such merchandise.](Tasks/9452.mdx)
- [Interpret and explain laws and regulations to travelers, prospective immigrants, shippers, and manufacturers.](Tasks/9453.mdx)
- [Institute civil and criminal prosecutions and cooperate with other law enforcement agencies in the investigation and prosecution of those in violation of immigration or customs laws.](Tasks/9456.mdx)
- [Testify regarding decisions at immigration appeals or in federal court.](Tasks/9457.mdx)
- [Record and report job-related activities, findings, transactions, violations, discrepancies, and decisions.](Tasks/9455.mdx)
- [Determine duty and taxes to be paid on goods.](Tasks/9458.mdx)
- [Collect samples of merchandise for examination, appraisal, or testing.](Tasks/9459.mdx)
