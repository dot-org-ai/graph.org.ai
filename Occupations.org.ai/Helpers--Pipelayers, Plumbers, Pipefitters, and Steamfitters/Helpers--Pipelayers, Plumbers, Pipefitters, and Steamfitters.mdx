# Helpers--Pipelayers, Plumbers, Pipefitters, and Steamfitters

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-3015.00

Help plumbers, pipefitters, steamfitters, or pipelayers by performing duties requiring less skill. Duties include using, supplying, or holding materials or tools, and cleaning work area and equipment.

## Tasks

- [Measure, cut, thread and assemble new pipe, placing the assembled pipe in hangers or other supports.](Tasks/4858.mdx)
- [Cut or drill holes in walls or floors to accommodate the passage of pipes.](Tasks/4857.mdx)
- [Perform rough-ins, repair and replace fixtures and water heaters, and locate, repair, or remove leaking or broken pipes.](Tasks/20144.mdx)
- [Assist pipe fitters in the layout, assembly, and installation of piping for air, ammonia, gas, and water systems.](Tasks/4862.mdx)
- [Cut pipe and lift up to fitters.](Tasks/4864.mdx)
- [Fit or assist in fitting valves, couplings, or assemblies to tanks, pumps, or systems, using hand tools.](Tasks/4861.mdx)
- [Requisition tools and equipment, select type and size of pipe, and collect and transport materials and equipment to work site.](Tasks/4860.mdx)
- [Mount brackets and hangers on walls and ceilings to hold pipes, and set sleeves or inserts to provide support for pipes.](Tasks/4859.mdx)
- [Excavate and grade ditches, and lay and join pipe for water and sewer service.](Tasks/4863.mdx)
- [Disassemble and remove damaged or worn pipe.](Tasks/4865.mdx)
