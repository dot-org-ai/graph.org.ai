# Electronic Equipment Installers and Repairers, Motor Vehicles

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 49-2096.00

Install, diagnose, or repair communications, sound, security, or navigation equipment in motor vehicles.

## Tasks

- [Install equipment and accessories, such as stereos, navigation equipment, communication equipment, and security systems.](Tasks/13728.mdx)
- [Inspect and test electrical or electronic systems to locate and diagnose malfunctions, using visual inspections and testing instruments, such as oscilloscopes and voltmeters.](Tasks/13727.mdx)
- [Cut openings and drill holes for fixtures and equipment, using electric drills and routers.](Tasks/13732.mdx)
- [Splice wires with knives or cutting pliers, and solder connections to fixtures and equipment.](Tasks/13725.mdx)
- [Diagnose or repair problems with electronic equipment, such as sound, navigation, communication, and security equipment, in motor vehicles.](Tasks/13726.mdx)
- [Run new speaker and electrical cables.](Tasks/13731.mdx)
- [Confer with customers to determine the nature of malfunctions.](Tasks/13730.mdx)
- [Remove seats, carpeting, and interiors of doors and add sound-absorbing material in empty spaces, reinstalling interior parts.](Tasks/13734.mdx)
- [Record results of diagnostic tests.](Tasks/13735.mdx)
- [Estimate costs of repairs, based on parts and labor charges.](Tasks/13729.mdx)
