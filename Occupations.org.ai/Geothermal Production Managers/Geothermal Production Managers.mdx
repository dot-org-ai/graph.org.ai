# Geothermal Production Managers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 11-3051.02

Manage operations at geothermal power generation facilities. Maintain and monitor geothermal plant equipment for efficient and safe plant operations.

## Tasks

- [Supervise employees in geothermal power plants or well fields.](Tasks/15446.mdx)
- [Oversee geothermal plant operations, maintenance, and repairs to ensure compliance with applicable standards or regulations.](Tasks/15447.mdx)
- [Communicate geothermal plant conditions to employees.](Tasks/15434.mdx)
- [Identify and evaluate equipment, procedural, or conditional inefficiencies involving geothermal plant systems.](Tasks/15443.mdx)
- [Perform or direct the performance of preventative maintenance on geothermal plant equipment.](Tasks/15439.mdx)
- [Inspect geothermal plant or injection well fields to verify proper equipment operations.](Tasks/15448.mdx)
- [Develop or manage budgets for geothermal operations.](Tasks/15445.mdx)
- [Select and implement corrosion control or mitigation systems for geothermal plants.](Tasks/15433.mdx)
- [Develop operating plans and schedules for geothermal operations.](Tasks/15444.mdx)
- [Record, review, or maintain daily logs, reports, maintenance, and other records associated with geothermal operations.](Tasks/15436.mdx)
