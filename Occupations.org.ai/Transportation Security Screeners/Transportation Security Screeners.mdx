# Transportation Security Screeners

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 33-9093.00

Conduct screening of passengers, baggage, or cargo to ensure compliance with Transportation Security Administration (TSA) regulations. May operate basic security equipment such as x-ray machines and hand wands at screening checkpoints.

## Tasks

- [Inspect carry-on items, using x-ray viewing equipment, to determine whether items contain objects that warrant further investigation.](Tasks/15341.mdx)
- [Search carry-on or checked baggage by hand when it is suspected to contain prohibited items such as weapons.](Tasks/15348.mdx)
- [Check passengers' tickets to ensure that they are valid, and to determine whether passengers have designations that require special handling, such as providing photo identification.](Tasks/15332.mdx)
- [Test baggage for any explosive materials, using equipment such as explosive detection machines or chemical swab systems.](Tasks/15350.mdx)
- [Perform pat-down or hand-held wand searches of passengers who have triggered machine alarms, who are unable to pass through metal detectors, or who have been randomly identified for such searches.](Tasks/15346.mdx)
- [Notify supervisors or other appropriate personnel when security breaches occur.](Tasks/15345.mdx)
- [Send checked baggage through automated screening machines, and set bags aside for searching or rescreening as indicated by equipment.](Tasks/15349.mdx)
- [Decide whether baggage that triggers alarms should be searched or should be allowed to pass through.](Tasks/15337.mdx)
- [Follow those who breach security until police or other security personnel arrive to apprehend them.](Tasks/15354.mdx)
- [Inform other screeners when baggage should not be opened because it might contain explosives.](Tasks/15339.mdx)
