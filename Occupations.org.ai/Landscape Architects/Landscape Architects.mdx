# Landscape Architects

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 17-1012.00

Plan and design land areas for projects such as parks and other recreational facilities, airports, highways, hospitals, schools, land subdivisions, and commercial, industrial, and residential sites.

## Tasks

- [Confer with clients, engineering personnel, or architects on landscape projects.](Tasks/20369.mdx)
- [Analyze data on conditions such as site location, drainage, or structure location for environmental reports or landscaping plans.](Tasks/20371.mdx)
- [Inspect landscape work to ensure compliance with specifications, evaluate quality of materials or work, or advise clients or construction personnel.](Tasks/20373.mdx)
- [Prepare site plans, specifications, or cost estimates for land development.](Tasks/20370.mdx)
- [Integrate existing land features or landscaping into designs.](Tasks/20375.mdx)
- [Collaborate with architects or related professionals on whole building design to maximize the aesthetic features of structures or surrounding land and to improve energy efficiency.](Tasks/19597.mdx)
- [Prepare graphic representations or drawings of proposed plans or designs.](Tasks/18470.mdx)
- [Inspect proposed sites to identify structural elements of land areas or other important site information, such as soil condition, existing landscaping, or the proximity of water management facilities.](Tasks/20378.mdx)
- [Collaborate with estimators to cost projects, create project plans, or coordinate bids from landscaping contractors.](Tasks/20380.mdx)
- [Create landscapes that minimize water consumption such as by incorporating drought-resistant grasses or indigenous plants.](Tasks/19598.mdx)
