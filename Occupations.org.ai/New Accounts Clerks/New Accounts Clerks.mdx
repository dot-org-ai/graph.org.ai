# New Accounts Clerks

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 43-4141.00

Interview persons desiring to open accounts in financial institutions. Explain account services available to prospective customers and assist them in preparing applications.

## Tasks

- [Perform teller duties as required.](Tasks/4730.mdx)
- [Compile information about new accounts, enter account information into computers, and file related forms or other documents.](Tasks/4723.mdx)
- [Collect and record customer deposits and fees and issue receipts, using computers.](Tasks/4728.mdx)
- [Inform customers of procedures for applying for services, such as ATM cards, direct deposit of checks, and certificates of deposit.](Tasks/4726.mdx)
- [Answer customers' questions and explain available services, such as deposit accounts, bonds, and securities.](Tasks/4722.mdx)
- [Interview customers to obtain information needed for opening accounts or renting safe-deposit boxes.](Tasks/4725.mdx)
- [Refer customers to appropriate bank personnel to meet their financial needs.](Tasks/4724.mdx)
- [Investigate and correct errors upon customers' request, according to customer and bank records.](Tasks/4729.mdx)
- [Execute wire transfers of funds.](Tasks/4731.mdx)
- [Issue initial and replacement safe-deposit keys to customers, and admit customers to vaults.](Tasks/4733.mdx)
