# Recycling and Reclamation Workers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 53-7062.04

Prepare and sort materials or products for recycling. Identify and remove hazardous substances. Dismantle components of products such as appliances.

## Tasks

- [Sort materials, such as metals, glass, wood, paper or plastics, into appropriate containers for recycling.](Tasks/17872.mdx)
- [Clean recycling yard by sweeping, raking, picking up broken glass and loose paper debris, or moving barrels and bins.](Tasks/17884.mdx)
- [Operate forklifts, pallet jacks, power lifts, or front-end loaders to load bales, bundles, or other heavy items onto trucks for shipping to smelters or other recycled materials processing facilities.](Tasks/17878.mdx)
- [Sort metals to separate high-grade metals, such as copper, brass, and aluminum, for recycling.](Tasks/17882.mdx)
- [Clean, inspect, or lubricate recyclable collection equipment or perform routine maintenance or minor repairs on recycling equipment, such as star gears, finger sorters, destoners, belts, and grinders.](Tasks/17886.mdx)
- [Collect and sort recyclable construction materials, such as concrete, drywall, plastics, or wood, into containers.](Tasks/17885.mdx)
- [Extract chemicals from discarded appliances, such as air conditioners or refrigerators, using specialized machinery, such as refrigerant recovery equipment.](Tasks/17871.mdx)
- [Deposit recoverable materials into chutes or place materials on conveyor belts.](Tasks/17874.mdx)
- [Operate balers to compress recyclable materials into bundles or bales.](Tasks/17877.mdx)
- [Clean materials, such as metals, according to recycling requirements.](Tasks/17883.mdx)
