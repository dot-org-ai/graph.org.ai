# Fish and Game Wardens

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 33-3031.00

Patrol assigned area to prevent fish and game law violations. Investigate reports of damage to crops or property by wildlife. Compile biological data.

## Tasks

- [Patrol assigned areas by car, boat, airplane, horse, or on foot to enforce game, fish, or boating laws or to manage wildlife programs, lakes, or land.](Tasks/7915.mdx)
- [Compile and present evidence for court actions.](Tasks/20582.mdx)
- [Investigate hunting accidents or reports of fish or game law violations.](Tasks/20583.mdx)
- [Protect and preserve native wildlife, plants, or ecosystems.](Tasks/7918.mdx)
- [Issue warnings or citations and file reports as necessary.](Tasks/20584.mdx)
- [Serve warrants and make arrests.](Tasks/20585.mdx)
- [Provide assistance to other local law enforcement agencies as required.](Tasks/7921.mdx)
- [Promote or provide hunter or trapper safety training.](Tasks/7919.mdx)
