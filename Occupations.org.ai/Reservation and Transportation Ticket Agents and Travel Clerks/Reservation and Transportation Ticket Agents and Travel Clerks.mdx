# Reservation and Transportation Ticket Agents and Travel Clerks

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 43-4181.00

Make and confirm reservations for transportation or lodging, or sell transportation tickets. May check baggage and direct passengers to designated concourse, pier, or track; deliver tickets and contact individuals and groups to inform them of package tours; or provide tourists with travel or transportation information.

## Tasks

- [Examine passenger documentation to determine destinations and to assign boarding passes.](Tasks/9758.mdx)
- [Trace lost, delayed, or misdirected baggage for customers.](Tasks/9762.mdx)
- [Check baggage and cargo and direct passengers to designated locations for loading.](Tasks/9760.mdx)
- [Provide boarding or disembarking assistance to passengers needing special assistance.](Tasks/9759.mdx)
- [Confer with customers to determine their service requirements and travel preferences.](Tasks/9757.mdx)
- [Announce arrival and departure information, using public address systems.](Tasks/9761.mdx)
- [Determine whether space is available on travel dates requested by customers, assigning requested spaces when available.](Tasks/9754.mdx)
- [Assemble and issue required documentation, such as tickets, travel insurance policies, or itineraries.](Tasks/9753.mdx)
- [Maintain computerized inventories of available passenger space and provide information on space reserved or available.](Tasks/9756.mdx)
- [Inform clients of essential travel information, such as travel times, transportation connections, or medical and visa requirements.](Tasks/9755.mdx)
