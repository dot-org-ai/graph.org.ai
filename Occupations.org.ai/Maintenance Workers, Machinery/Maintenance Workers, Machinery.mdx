# Maintenance Workers, Machinery

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 49-9043.00

Lubricate machinery, change parts, or perform other routine machinery maintenance.

## Tasks

- [Dismantle machines and remove parts for repair, using hand tools, chain falls, jacks, cranes, or hoists.](Tasks/11833.mdx)
- [Reassemble machines after the completion of repair or maintenance work.](Tasks/11828.mdx)
- [Record production, repair, and machine maintenance information.](Tasks/11834.mdx)
- [Lubricate or apply adhesives or other materials to machines, machine parts, or other equipment according to specified procedures.](Tasks/11831.mdx)
- [Install, replace, or change machine parts and attachments, according to production specifications.](Tasks/11832.mdx)
- [Set up and operate machines, and adjust controls to regulate operations.](Tasks/11836.mdx)
- [Collaborate with other workers to repair or move machines, machine parts, or equipment.](Tasks/11837.mdx)
- [Read work orders and specifications to determine machines and equipment requiring repair or maintenance.](Tasks/11835.mdx)
- [Inspect or test damaged machine parts, and mark defective areas or advise supervisors of repair needs.](Tasks/11830.mdx)
- [Start machines and observe mechanical operation to determine efficiency and to detect problems.](Tasks/11829.mdx)
