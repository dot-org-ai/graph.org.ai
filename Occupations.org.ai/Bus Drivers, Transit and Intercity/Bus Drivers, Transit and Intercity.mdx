# Bus Drivers, Transit and Intercity

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 53-3052.00

Drive bus or motor coach, including regular route operations, charters, and private carriage. May assist passengers with baggage. May collect fares or tickets.

## Tasks

- [Drive vehicles over specified routes or to specified destinations according to time schedules, complying with traffic regulations to ensure that passengers have a smooth and safe ride.](Tasks/18794.mdx)
- [Park vehicles at loading areas so that passengers can board.](Tasks/3146.mdx)
- [Inspect vehicles and check gas, oil, and water levels prior to departure.](Tasks/3144.mdx)
- [Announce stops to passengers.](Tasks/21085.mdx)
- [Assist passengers, such as elderly or individuals with disabilities, on and off bus, ensure they are seated properly, help carry baggage, and answer questions about bus schedules or routes.](Tasks/18795.mdx)
- [Collect tickets or cash fares from passengers.](Tasks/18798.mdx)
- [Handle passenger emergencies or disruptions.](Tasks/18796.mdx)
- [Report delays or accidents.](Tasks/3148.mdx)
- [Advise passengers to be seated and orderly while on vehicles.](Tasks/3149.mdx)
- [Regulate heating, lighting, and ventilating systems for passenger comfort.](Tasks/3150.mdx)
