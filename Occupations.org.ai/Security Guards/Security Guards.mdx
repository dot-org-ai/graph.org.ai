# Security Guards

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 33-9032.00

Guard, patrol, or monitor premises to prevent theft, violence, or infractions of rules. May operate x-ray and metal detector equipment.

## Tasks

- [Lock doors and gates of entrances and exits to secure buildings.](Tasks/20894.mdx)
- [Patrol industrial or commercial premises to prevent and detect signs of intrusion and ensure security of doors, windows, and gates.](Tasks/2131.mdx)
- [Respond to medical emergencies by administering basic first aid or by obtaining assistance from paramedics.](Tasks/20895.mdx)
- [Answer alarms and investigate disturbances.](Tasks/2132.mdx)
- [Circulate among visitors, patrons, or employees to preserve order and protect property.](Tasks/2136.mdx)
- [Monitor and authorize entrance and departure of employees, visitors, and other persons to guard against theft and maintain security of premises.](Tasks/2133.mdx)
- [Write reports of daily activities and irregularities, such as equipment or property damage, theft, presence of unauthorized persons, or unusual occurrences.](Tasks/2134.mdx)
- [Warn persons of rule infractions or violations, and apprehend or evict violators from premises, using force when necessary.](Tasks/2138.mdx)
- [Answer telephone calls to take messages, answer questions, and provide information during non-business hours or when switchboard is closed.](Tasks/2137.mdx)
- [Call police or fire departments in cases of emergency, such as fire or presence of unauthorized persons.](Tasks/2135.mdx)
