# First-Line Supervisors of Food Preparation and Serving Workers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 35-1012.00

Directly supervise and coordinate activities of workers engaged in preparing and serving food.

## Tasks

- [Resolve customer complaints regarding food service.](Tasks/2145.mdx)
- [Train workers in food preparation, and in service, sanitation, and safety procedures.](Tasks/2146.mdx)
- [Assign duties, responsibilities, and work stations to employees in accordance with work requirements.](Tasks/2150.mdx)
- [Present bills and accept payments.](Tasks/2157.mdx)
- [Perform various financial activities, such as cash handling, deposit preparation, and payroll.](Tasks/18714.mdx)
- [Supervise and participate in kitchen and dining area cleaning activities.](Tasks/18715.mdx)
- [Recommend measures for improving work procedures and worker performance to increase service quality and enhance job safety.](Tasks/2155.mdx)
- [Specify food portions and courses, production and time sequences, and workstation and equipment arrangements.](Tasks/2154.mdx)
- [Control inventories of food, equipment, smallware, and liquor, and report shortages to designated personnel.](Tasks/2148.mdx)
- [Observe and evaluate workers and work procedures to ensure quality standards and service, and complete disciplinary write-ups.](Tasks/18716.mdx)
