# Telephone Operators

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 43-2021.00

Provide information by accessing alphabetical, geographical, or other directories. Assist customers with special billing requests, such as charges to a third party and credits or refunds for incorrectly dialed numbers or bad connections. May handle emergency calls and assist children or people with physical disabilities to make telephone calls.

## Tasks

- [Observe signal lights on switchboards, and dial or press buttons to make connections.](Tasks/13246.mdx)
- [Operate telephone switchboards and systems to advance and complete connections, including those for local, long distance, pay telephone, mobile, person-to-person, and emergency calls.](Tasks/13247.mdx)
- [Listen to customer requests, referring to alphabetical or geographical directories to answer questions and provide telephone information.](Tasks/13243.mdx)
- [Update directory information.](Tasks/13257.mdx)
- [Suggest and check alternate spellings, locations, or listing formats to customers lacking details or complete information.](Tasks/13244.mdx)
- [Perform clerical duties such as typing, proofreading, and sorting mail.](Tasks/13251.mdx)
- [Offer special assistance to persons such as those who are unable to dial or who are in emergency situations.](Tasks/13245.mdx)
- [Operate paging systems or other systems of bells or buzzers to notify recipients of incoming calls.](Tasks/13256.mdx)
- [Monitor automated systems for placing collect calls and intervene for a callers needing assistance.](Tasks/13250.mdx)
- [Interrupt busy lines if an emergency warrants.](Tasks/13253.mdx)
