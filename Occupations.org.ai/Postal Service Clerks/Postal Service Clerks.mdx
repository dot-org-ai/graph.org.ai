# Postal Service Clerks

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 43-5051.00

Perform any combination of tasks in a United States Postal Service (USPS) post office, such as receive letters and parcels; sell postage and revenue stamps, postal cards, and stamped envelopes; fill out and sell money orders; place mail in pigeon holes of mail rack or in bags; and examine mail for correct postage. Includes postal service clerks employed by USPS contractors.

## Tasks

- [Weigh letters and parcels, compute mailing costs based on type, weight, and destination, and affix correct postage.](Tasks/11322.mdx)
- [Check mail to ensure correct postage and that packages and letters are in proper condition for mailing.](Tasks/11326.mdx)
- [Sort incoming and outgoing mail, according to type and destination, by hand or by operating electronic mail-sorting and scanning devices.](Tasks/11330.mdx)
- [Obtain signatures from recipients of registered or special delivery mail.](Tasks/11323.mdx)
- [Answer questions regarding mail regulations and procedures, postage rates, and post office boxes.](Tasks/11327.mdx)
- [Transport mail from one work station to another.](Tasks/11338.mdx)
- [Sell and collect payment for products such as stamps, prepaid mail envelopes, and money orders.](Tasks/11325.mdx)
- [Keep money drawers in order, and record and balance daily transactions.](Tasks/11321.mdx)
- [Register, certify, and insure letters and parcels.](Tasks/11324.mdx)
- [Complete forms regarding changes of address, or theft or loss of mail, or for special services such as registered or priority mail.](Tasks/11328.mdx)
