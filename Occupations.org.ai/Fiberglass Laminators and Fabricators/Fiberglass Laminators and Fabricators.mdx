# Fiberglass Laminators and Fabricators

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-2051.00

Laminate layers of fiberglass on molds to form boat decks and hulls, bodies for golf carts, automobiles, or other products.

## Tasks

- [Release air bubbles and smooth seams, using rollers.](Tasks/13970.mdx)
- [Spray chopped fiberglass, resins, and catalysts onto prepared molds or dies using pneumatic spray guns with chopper attachments.](Tasks/13971.mdx)
- [Mix catalysts into resins, and saturate cloth and mats with mixtures, using brushes.](Tasks/13974.mdx)
- [Check completed products for conformance to specifications and for defects by measuring with rulers or micrometers, by checking them visually, or by tapping them to detect bubbles or dead spots.](Tasks/13976.mdx)
- [Pat or press layers of saturated mat or cloth into place on molds, using brushes or hands, and smooth out wrinkles and air bubbles with hands or squeegees.](Tasks/13973.mdx)
- [Select precut fiberglass mats, cloth, and wood-bracing materials as required by projects being assembled.](Tasks/13972.mdx)
- [Bond wood reinforcing strips to decks and cabin structures of watercraft, using resin-saturated fiberglass.](Tasks/13975.mdx)
- [Trim excess materials from molds, using hand shears or trimming knives.](Tasks/13977.mdx)
- [Apply layers of plastic resin to mold surfaces prior to placement of fiberglass mats, repeating layers until products have the desired thicknesses and plastics have jelled.](Tasks/13980.mdx)
- [Inspect, clean, and assemble molds before beginning work.](Tasks/13984.mdx)
