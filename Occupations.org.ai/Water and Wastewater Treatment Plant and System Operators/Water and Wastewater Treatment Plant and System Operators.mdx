# Water and Wastewater Treatment Plant and System Operators

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-8031.00

Operate or control an entire process or system of machines, often through the use of control boards, to transfer or treat water or wastewater.

## Tasks

- [Collect and test water and sewage samples, using test equipment and color analysis standards.](Tasks/928.mdx)
- [Operate and adjust controls on equipment to purify and clarify water, process or dispose of sewage, and generate power.](Tasks/926.mdx)
- [Record operational data, personnel attendance, or meter and gauge readings on specified forms.](Tasks/929.mdx)
- [Add chemicals, such as ammonia, chlorine, or lime, to disinfect and deodorize water and other liquids.](Tasks/925.mdx)
- [Inspect equipment or monitor operating conditions, meters, and gauges to determine load requirements and detect malfunctions.](Tasks/927.mdx)
- [Direct and coordinate plant workers engaged in routine operations and maintenance activities.](Tasks/932.mdx)
- [Clean and maintain tanks, filter beds, and other work areas, using hand tools and power tools.](Tasks/18585.mdx)
- [Maintain, repair, and lubricate equipment, using hand tools and power tools.](Tasks/930.mdx)
