# Lighting Technicians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 27-4015.00

Set up, maintain, and dismantle light fixtures, lighting control devices, and the associated lighting electrical and rigging equipment used for photography, television, film, video, and live productions. May focus or operate light fixtures, or attach color filters or other lighting accessories.

## Tasks

- [Assess safety of wiring or equipment set-up to determine the risk of fire or electrical shock.](Tasks/22673.mdx)
- [Consult with lighting director or production staff to determine lighting requirements.](Tasks/22674.mdx)
- [Disassemble and store equipment after performances.](Tasks/22675.mdx)
- [Install color effects or image patterns, such as color filters, onto lighting fixtures.](Tasks/22676.mdx)
- [Install electrical cables or wire fixtures.](Tasks/22677.mdx)
- [Load, unload, or position lighting equipment.](Tasks/22678.mdx)
- [Match light fixture settings, such as brightness and color, to lighting design plans.](Tasks/22679.mdx)
- [Notify supervisors when major lighting equipment repairs are needed.](Tasks/22680.mdx)
- [Operate manual or automated systems to control lighting throughout productions.](Tasks/22681.mdx)
- [Patch or wire lights to dimmers or other electronic consoles.](Tasks/22682.mdx)
