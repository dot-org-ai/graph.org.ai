# Editors

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 27-3041.00

Plan, coordinate, revise, or edit written material. May review proposals and drafts for possible publication.

## Tasks

- [Read copy or proof to detect and correct errors in spelling, punctuation, and syntax.](Tasks/1777.mdx)
- [Verify facts, dates, and statistics, using standard reference sources.](Tasks/1780.mdx)
- [Read, evaluate and edit manuscripts or other materials submitted for publication, and confer with authors regarding changes in content, style or organization, or publication.](Tasks/1786.mdx)
- [Develop story or content ideas, considering reader or audience appeal.](Tasks/1782.mdx)
- [Prepare, rewrite and edit copy to improve readability, or supervise others who do this work.](Tasks/1776.mdx)
- [Oversee publication production, including artwork, layout, computer typesetting, and printing, ensuring adherence to deadlines and budget requirements.](Tasks/1783.mdx)
- [Write text, such as stories, articles, editorials, or newsletters.](Tasks/21000.mdx)
- [Supervise and coordinate work of reporters and other editors.](Tasks/1789.mdx)
- [Confer with management and editorial staff members regarding placement and emphasis of developing news stories.](Tasks/1784.mdx)
- [Plan the contents of publications according to the publication's style, editorial policy, and publishing requirements.](Tasks/1779.mdx)
