# Civil Engineering Technologists and Technicians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 17-3022.00

Apply theory and principles of civil engineering in planning, designing, and overseeing construction and maintenance of structures and facilities under the direction of engineering staff or physical scientists.

## Tasks

- [Calculate dimensions, square footage, profile and component specifications, and material quantities, using calculator or computer.](Tasks/1461.mdx)
- [Read and review project blueprints and structural specifications to determine dimensions of structure or system and material requirements.](Tasks/1464.mdx)
- [Draft detailed dimensional drawings and design layouts for projects to ensure conformance to specifications.](Tasks/1462.mdx)
- [Confer with supervisor to determine project details such as plan preparation, acceptance testing, and evaluation of field conditions.](Tasks/1466.mdx)
- [Analyze proposed site factors and design maps, graphs, tracings, and diagrams to illustrate findings.](Tasks/1463.mdx)
- [Prepare reports and document project activities and data.](Tasks/1465.mdx)
- [Report maintenance problems occurring at project site to supervisor and negotiate changes to resolve system conflicts.](Tasks/1470.mdx)
- [Inspect project site and evaluate contractor work to detect design malfunctions and ensure conformance to design specifications and applicable codes.](Tasks/1467.mdx)
- [Conduct materials test and analysis, using tools and equipment and applying engineering knowledge.](Tasks/1471.mdx)
- [Develop plans and estimate costs for installation of systems, utilization of facilities, or construction of structures.](Tasks/1469.mdx)
