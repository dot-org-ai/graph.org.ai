# Fundraising Managers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 11-2033.00

Plan, direct, or coordinate activities to solicit and maintain funds for special projects or nonprofit organizations.

## Tasks

- [Develop strategies to encourage new or increased contributions.](Tasks/21252.mdx)
- [Manage fundraising budgets.](Tasks/21258.mdx)
- [Develop fundraising activity plans that maximize participation or contributions and minimize costs.](Tasks/21251.mdx)
- [Plan and direct special events for fundraising, such as silent auctions, dances, golf events, or walks.](Tasks/21259.mdx)
- [Establish goals for soliciting funds, develop policies for collection and safeguarding of contributions, and coordinate disbursement of funds.](Tasks/21255.mdx)
- [Establish and maintain effective working relationships with clients, government officials, and media representatives and use these relationships to develop new fundraising opportunities.](Tasks/21254.mdx)
- [Compile or develop materials to submit to granting or other funding organizations.](Tasks/21247.mdx)
- [Contact corporate representatives, government officials, or community leaders to increase awareness of organizational causes, activities, or needs.](Tasks/21249.mdx)
- [Conduct research to identify the goals, net worth, charitable donation history, or other data related to potential donors, potential investors, or general donor markets.](Tasks/21248.mdx)
- [Write interesting and effective press releases, prepare information for media kits, and develop and maintain company internet or intranet Web pages.](Tasks/21261.mdx)
