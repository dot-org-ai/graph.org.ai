# Park Naturalists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 19-1031.03

Plan, develop, and conduct programs to inform public of historical, natural, and scientific features of national, state, or local park.

## Tasks

- [Provide visitor services, such as explaining regulations, answering visitor requests, needs and complaints, and providing information about the park and surrounding areas.](Tasks/3686.mdx)
- [Assist with operations of general facilities, such as visitor centers.](Tasks/3691.mdx)
- [Confer with park staff to determine subjects and schedules for park programs.](Tasks/3690.mdx)
- [Conduct field trips to point out scientific, historic, and natural features of parks, forests, historic sites, or other attractions.](Tasks/3687.mdx)
- [Plan and organize public events at the park.](Tasks/20866.mdx)
- [Prepare and present illustrated lectures and interpretive talks about park features.](Tasks/3688.mdx)
- [Plan, organize and direct activities of seasonal staff members.](Tasks/3692.mdx)
- [Perform emergency duties to protect human life, government property, and natural features of park.](Tasks/3689.mdx)
- [Train staff on park programs.](Tasks/23959.mdx)
- [Develop environmental educational programs and curricula for schools.](Tasks/20867.mdx)
