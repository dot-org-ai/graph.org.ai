# Athletic Trainers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 29-9091.00

Evaluate and treat musculoskeletal injuries or illnesses. Provide preventive, therapeutic, emergency, and rehabilitative care.

## Tasks

- [Conduct an initial assessment of an athlete's injury or illness to provide emergency or continued care and to determine whether they should be referred to physicians for definitive diagnosis and treatment.](Tasks/4221.mdx)
- [Assess and report the progress of recovering athletes to coaches or physicians.](Tasks/4225.mdx)
- [Care for athletic injuries, using physical therapy equipment, techniques, or medication.](Tasks/4222.mdx)
- [Evaluate athletes' readiness to play and provide participation clearances when necessary and warranted.](Tasks/4223.mdx)
- [Perform general administrative tasks, such as keeping records or writing reports.](Tasks/18691.mdx)
- [Clean and sanitize athletic training rooms.](Tasks/23925.mdx)
- [Instruct coaches, athletes, parents, medical personnel, or community members in the care and prevention of athletic injuries.](Tasks/4231.mdx)
- [Apply protective or injury preventive devices, such as tape, bandages, or braces, to body parts, such as ankles, fingers, or wrists.](Tasks/4224.mdx)
- [Collaborate with physicians to develop and implement comprehensive rehabilitation programs for athletic injuries.](Tasks/4226.mdx)
- [Travel with athletic teams to be available at sporting events.](Tasks/4230.mdx)
