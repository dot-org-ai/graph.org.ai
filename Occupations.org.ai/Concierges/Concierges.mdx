# Concierges

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 39-6012.00

Assist patrons at hotel, apartment, or office building with personal services. May take messages; arrange or give advice on transportation, business services, or entertainment; or monitor guest requests for housekeeping and maintenance.

## Tasks

- [Provide information about local features, such as shopping, dining, nightlife, or recreational destinations.](Tasks/4529.mdx)
- [Make reservations for patrons, such as for dinner, spa treatments, or golf tee times, and obtain tickets to special events.](Tasks/20259.mdx)
- [Provide directions to guests.](Tasks/20260.mdx)
- [Order flowers for guests.](Tasks/21203.mdx)
- [Make travel arrangements for sightseeing or other tours.](Tasks/4530.mdx)
- [Pick up and deliver items or run errands for guests.](Tasks/4533.mdx)
- [Plan special events, parties, or meetings, which may include booking musicians or celebrities.](Tasks/20262.mdx)
- [Book airline or train tickets, reserve rental cars, or arrange shuttle service for guests.](Tasks/24009.mdx)
- [Arrange childcare services for guests.](Tasks/20263.mdx)
- [Carry out unusual requests, such as searching for hard-to-find items or arranging for exotic services, such as hot-air balloon rides.](Tasks/4534.mdx)
