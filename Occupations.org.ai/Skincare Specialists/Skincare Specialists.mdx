# Skincare Specialists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 39-5094.00

Provide skincare treatments to face and body to enhance an individual's appearance. Includes electrologists and laser hair removal specialists.

## Tasks

- [Sterilize equipment and clean work areas.](Tasks/8069.mdx)
- [Cleanse clients' skin with water, creams, or lotions.](Tasks/8074.mdx)
- [Demonstrate how to clean and care for skin properly and recommend skin-care regimens.](Tasks/8071.mdx)
- [Determine which products or colors will improve clients' skin quality and appearance.](Tasks/8077.mdx)
- [Keep records of client needs and preferences and the services provided.](Tasks/8070.mdx)
- [Treat the facial skin to maintain and improve its appearance, using specialized techniques and products, such as peels and masks.](Tasks/8075.mdx)
- [Examine clients' skin, using magnifying lamps or visors when necessary, to evaluate skin condition and appearance.](Tasks/8072.mdx)
- [Perform simple extractions to remove blackheads.](Tasks/8078.mdx)
- [Select and apply cosmetic products, such as creams, lotions, and tonics.](Tasks/8073.mdx)
- [Stay abreast of latest industry trends, products, research, and treatments.](Tasks/20258.mdx)
