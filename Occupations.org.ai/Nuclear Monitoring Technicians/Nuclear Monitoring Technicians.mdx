# Nuclear Monitoring Technicians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 19-4051.02

Collect and test samples to monitor results of nuclear experiments and contamination of humans, facilities, and environment.

## Tasks

- [Brief workers on radiation levels in work areas.](Tasks/20539.mdx)
- [Calculate safe radiation exposure times for personnel using plant contamination readings and prescribed safe levels of radiation.](Tasks/7597.mdx)
- [Monitor personnel to determine the amounts and intensities of radiation exposure.](Tasks/7599.mdx)
- [Inform supervisors when individual exposures or area radiation levels approach maximum permissible limits.](Tasks/7600.mdx)
- [Provide initial response to abnormal events or to alarms from radiation monitoring equipment.](Tasks/7598.mdx)
- [Determine intensities and types of radiation in work areas, equipment, or materials, using radiation detectors or other instruments.](Tasks/7602.mdx)
- [Instruct personnel in radiation safety procedures and demonstrate use of protective clothing and equipment.](Tasks/7601.mdx)
- [Collect samples of air, water, gases, or solids to determine radioactivity levels of contamination.](Tasks/7603.mdx)
- [Analyze samples, such as air or water samples, for contaminants or other elements.](Tasks/20540.mdx)
- [Determine or recommend radioactive decontamination procedures, according to the size and nature of equipment and the degree of contamination.](Tasks/7605.mdx)
