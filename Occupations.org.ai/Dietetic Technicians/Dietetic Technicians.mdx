# Dietetic Technicians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 29-2051.00

Assist in the provision of food service and nutritional programs, under the supervision of a dietitian. May plan and produce meals based on established guidelines, teach principles of food and nutrition, or counsel individuals.

## Tasks

- [Observe and monitor patient food intake and body weight, and report changes, progress, and dietary problems to dietician.](Tasks/23922.mdx)
- [Conduct nutritional assessments of individuals, including obtaining and evaluating individuals' dietary histories, to plan nutritional programs.](Tasks/20421.mdx)
- [Prepare a major meal, following recipes and determining group food quantities.](Tasks/4165.mdx)
- [Supervise food production or service or assist dietitians or nutritionists in food service supervision or planning.](Tasks/4167.mdx)
- [Plan menus or diets or guide individuals or families in food selection, preparation, or menu planning, based upon nutritional needs and established guidelines.](Tasks/4169.mdx)
- [Develop job specifications, job descriptions, or work schedules.](Tasks/4171.mdx)
- [Attend interdisciplinary meetings with other health care professionals to discuss patient care.](Tasks/23923.mdx)
- [Provide dietitians with assistance researching food, nutrition, or food service systems.](Tasks/4173.mdx)
- [Select, schedule, or conduct orientation or in-service education programs.](Tasks/4172.mdx)
- [Analyze menus or recipes, standardize recipes, or test new products.](Tasks/4166.mdx)
