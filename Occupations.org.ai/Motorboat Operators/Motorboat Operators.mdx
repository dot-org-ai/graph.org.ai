# Motorboat Operators

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 53-5022.00

Operate small motor-driven boats. May assist in navigational activities.

## Tasks

- [Operate engine throttles and steering mechanisms to guide boats on desired courses.](Tasks/14537.mdx)
- [Direct safety operations in emergency situations.](Tasks/14538.mdx)
- [Secure boats to docks with mooring lines, and cast off lines to enable departure.](Tasks/14540.mdx)
- [Maintain desired courses, using compasses or electronic navigational aids.](Tasks/14535.mdx)
- [Organize and direct the activities of crew members.](Tasks/14545.mdx)
- [Follow safety procedures to ensure the protection of passengers, cargo, and vessels.](Tasks/14536.mdx)
- [Maintain equipment such as range markers, fire extinguishers, boat fenders, lines, pumps, and fittings.](Tasks/14541.mdx)
- [Report any observed navigational hazards to authorities.](Tasks/14542.mdx)
- [Oversee operation of vessels used for carrying passengers, motor vehicles, or goods across rivers, harbors, lakes, and coastal waters.](Tasks/14548.mdx)
- [Service motors by performing tasks such as changing oil and lubricating parts.](Tasks/14543.mdx)
