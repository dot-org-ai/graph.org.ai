# Private Detectives and Investigators

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 33-9021.00

Gather, analyze, compile, and report information regarding individuals or organizations to clients, or detect occurrences of unlawful acts or infractions of rules in private establishment.

## Tasks

- [Write reports or case summaries to document investigations.](Tasks/13095.mdx)
- [Conduct private investigations on a paid basis.](Tasks/13099.mdx)
- [Search computer databases, credit reports, public records, tax or legal filings, or other resources to locate persons or to compile information for investigations.](Tasks/13096.mdx)
- [Conduct personal background investigations, such as pre-employment checks, to obtain information about an individual's character, financial status, or personal history.](Tasks/20758.mdx)
- [Expose fraudulent insurance claims or stolen funds.](Tasks/13106.mdx)
- [Obtain and analyze information on suspects, crimes, or disturbances to solve cases, to identify criminal activity, or to gather information for court cases.](Tasks/13097.mdx)
- [Testify at hearings or court trials to present evidence.](Tasks/13100.mdx)
- [Question persons to obtain evidence for cases of divorce, child custody, or missing persons or information about individuals' character or financial status.](Tasks/13101.mdx)
- [Observe and document activities of individuals to detect unlawful acts or to obtain evidence for cases, using binoculars and still or video cameras.](Tasks/13102.mdx)
- [Confer with establishment officials, security departments, police, or postal officials to identify problems, provide information, or receive instructions.](Tasks/13107.mdx)
