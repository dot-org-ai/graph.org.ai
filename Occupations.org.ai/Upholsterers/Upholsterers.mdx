# Upholsterers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-6093.00

Make, repair, or replace upholstery for household furniture or transportation vehicles.

## Tasks

- [Fit, install, and secure material on frames, using hand tools, power tools, glue, cement, or staples.](Tasks/14228.mdx)
- [Measure and cut new covering materials, using patterns and measuring and cutting instruments, following sketches and design specifications.](Tasks/14236.mdx)
- [Build furniture up with loose fiber stuffing, cotton, felt, or foam padding to form smooth, rounded surfaces.](Tasks/14229.mdx)
- [Make, restore, or create custom upholstered furniture, using hand tools and knowledge of fabrics and upholstery methods.](Tasks/14235.mdx)
- [Read work orders, and apply knowledge and experience with materials to determine types and amounts of materials required to cover workpieces.](Tasks/14233.mdx)
- [Examine furniture frames, upholstery, springs, and webbing to locate defects.](Tasks/14230.mdx)
- [Adjust or replace webbing, padding, or springs, and secure them in place.](Tasks/14245.mdx)
- [Sew rips or tears in material, or create tufting, using needles and thread.](Tasks/14243.mdx)
- [Remove covering, webbing, padding, or defective springs from workpieces, using hand tools such as hammers and tack pullers.](Tasks/14232.mdx)
- [Attach fasteners, grommets, buttons, buckles, ornamental trim, and other accessories to covers or frames, using hand tools.](Tasks/14231.mdx)
