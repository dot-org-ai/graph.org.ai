# Project Management Specialists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 13-1082.00

Analyze and coordinate the schedule, timeline, procurement, staffing, and budget of a product or service on a per project basis. Lead and guide the work of technical staff. May serve as a point of contact for the client or customer.

## Tasks

- [Assign duties or responsibilities to project personnel.](Tasks/21462.mdx)
- [Communicate with key stakeholders to determine project requirements and objectives.](Tasks/21463.mdx)
- [Confer with project personnel to identify and resolve problems.](Tasks/21464.mdx)
- [Create project status presentations for delivery to customers or project personnel.](Tasks/21465.mdx)
- [Develop or update project plans including information such as objectives, technologies, schedules, funding, and staffing.](Tasks/21466.mdx)
- [Identify project needs such as resources, staff, or finances by reviewing project objectives and schedules.](Tasks/21467.mdx)
- [Identify, review, or select vendors or consultants to meet project needs.](Tasks/21468.mdx)
- [Monitor costs incurred by project staff to identify budget issues.](Tasks/21469.mdx)
- [Monitor project milestones and deliverables.](Tasks/21470.mdx)
- [Monitor the performance of project team members to provide performance feedback.](Tasks/21471.mdx)
