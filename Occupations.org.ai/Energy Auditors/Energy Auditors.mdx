# Energy Auditors

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-4011.01

Conduct energy audits of buildings, building systems, or process systems. May also conduct investment grade audits of buildings or systems.

## Tasks

- [Identify and prioritize energy-saving measures.](Tasks/18082.mdx)
- [Prepare audit reports containing energy analysis results or recommendations for energy cost savings.](Tasks/18074.mdx)
- [Identify any health or safety issues related to planned weatherization projects.](Tasks/19561.mdx)
- [Identify opportunities to improve the operation, maintenance, or energy efficiency of building or process systems.](Tasks/18083.mdx)
- [Calculate potential for energy savings.](Tasks/18077.mdx)
- [Inspect or evaluate building envelopes, mechanical systems, electrical systems, or process systems to determine the energy consumption of each system.](Tasks/18073.mdx)
- [Analyze technical feasibility of energy-saving measures, using knowledge of engineering, energy production, energy use, construction, maintenance, system operation, or process systems.](Tasks/18076.mdx)
- [Examine commercial sites to determine the feasibility of installing equipment that allows building management systems to reduce electricity consumption during peak demand periods.](Tasks/19560.mdx)
- [Recommend energy-efficient technologies or alternate energy sources.](Tasks/18087.mdx)
- [Collect and analyze field data related to energy usage.](Tasks/18078.mdx)
