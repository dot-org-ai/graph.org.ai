# Pediatricians, General

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 29-1221.00

Diagnose, treat, and help prevent diseases and injuries in children. May refer patients to specialists for further diagnosis or treatment, as needed.

## Tasks

- [Prescribe or administer treatment, therapy, medication, vaccination, and other specialized medical care to treat or prevent illness, disease, or injury in infants and children.](Tasks/7821.mdx)
- [Examine children regularly to assess their growth and development.](Tasks/7820.mdx)
- [Treat children who have minor illnesses, acute and chronic health problems, and growth and development concerns.](Tasks/7824.mdx)
- [Examine patients or order, perform, and interpret diagnostic tests to obtain information on medical condition and determine diagnosis.](Tasks/7819.mdx)
- [Advise patients, parents or guardians, and community members concerning diet, activity, hygiene, and disease prevention.](Tasks/7823.mdx)
- [Explain procedures and discuss test results or prescribed treatments with patients and parents or guardians.](Tasks/7825.mdx)
- [Collect, record, and maintain patient information, such as medical history, reports, or examination results.](Tasks/7822.mdx)
- [Monitor patients' conditions and progress and reevaluate treatments as necessary.](Tasks/7826.mdx)
- [Direct and coordinate activities of nurses, students, assistants, specialists, therapists, and other medical staff.](Tasks/7829.mdx)
- [Plan and execute medical care programs to aid in the mental and physical growth and development of children and adolescents.](Tasks/7827.mdx)
