# Helpers--Painters, Paperhangers, Plasterers, and Stucco Masons

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-3014.00

Help painters, paperhangers, plasterers, or stucco masons by performing duties requiring less skill. Duties include using, supplying, or holding materials or tools, and cleaning work area and equipment.

## Tasks

- [Clean work areas and equipment.](Tasks/13612.mdx)
- [Perform support duties to assist painters, paperhangers, plasterers, or masons.](Tasks/13613.mdx)
- [Apply protective coverings, such as masking tape, to articles or areas that could be damaged or stained by work processes.](Tasks/13614.mdx)
- [Erect scaffolding.](Tasks/13615.mdx)
- [Fill cracks or breaks in surfaces of plaster articles or areas with putty or epoxy compounds.](Tasks/13616.mdx)
- [Supply or hold tools and materials.](Tasks/13617.mdx)
- [Smooth surfaces of articles to be painted, using sanding and buffing tools and equipment.](Tasks/13618.mdx)
- [Mix plaster, and carry plaster to plasterers.](Tasks/13619.mdx)
- [Place articles to be stripped into stripping tanks.](Tasks/13620.mdx)
- [Remove articles such as cabinets, metal furniture, and paint containers from stripping tanks after prescribed periods of time.](Tasks/13621.mdx)
