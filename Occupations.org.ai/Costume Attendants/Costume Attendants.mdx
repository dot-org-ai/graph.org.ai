# Costume Attendants

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 39-3092.00

Select, fit, and take care of costumes for cast members, and aid entertainers. May assist with multiple costume changes during performances.

## Tasks

- [Create worksheets for dressing lists, show notes, or costume checks.](Tasks/9627.mdx)
- [Provide dressing assistance to cast members or assign cast dressers to assist specific cast members with costume changes.](Tasks/20898.mdx)
- [Arrange costumes in order of use to facilitate quick-change procedures for performances.](Tasks/9616.mdx)
- [Design or construct costumes or send them to tailors for construction, major repairs, or alterations.](Tasks/9621.mdx)
- [Examine costume fit on cast members and sketch or write notes for alterations.](Tasks/9629.mdx)
- [Distribute costumes or related equipment and keep records of item status.](Tasks/9615.mdx)
- [Check the appearance of costumes on stage or under lights to determine whether desired effects are being achieved.](Tasks/9623.mdx)
- [Clean and press costumes before and after performances and perform any minor repairs.](Tasks/9618.mdx)
- [Collaborate with production designers, costume designers, or other production staff to discuss and execute costume design details.](Tasks/9625.mdx)
- [Monitor, maintain, or secure inventories of costumes, wigs, or makeup, providing keys or access to assigned directors, costume designers, or wardrobe mistresses/masters.](Tasks/9626.mdx)
