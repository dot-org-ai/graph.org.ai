# Solar Photovoltaic Installers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-2231.00

Assemble, install, or maintain solar photovoltaic (PV) systems on roofs or other structures in compliance with site assessment and schematics. May include measuring, cutting, assembling, and bolting structural framing and solar modules. May perform minor electrical work such as current checks.

- [Identify electrical, environmental, and safety hazards associated with photovoltaic (PV) installations.](Tasks/17753.mdx)
- [Identify installation locations with proper orientation, area, solar access, or structural integrity for photovoltaic (PV) arrays.](Tasks/17754.mdx)
- [Assemble solar modules, panels, or support structures, as specified.](Tasks/17740.mdx)

- [Determine materials, equipment, and installation sequences necessary to maximize installation efficiency.](Tasks/17765.mdx)
