# Camera Operators, Television, Video, and Film

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 27-4031.00

Operate television, video, or film camera to record images or scenes for television, video, or film productions.

## Tasks

- [Compose and frame each shot, applying the technical aspects of light, lenses, film, filters, and camera settings to achieve the effects sought by directors.](Tasks/4031.mdx)
- [Operate television or motion picture cameras to record scenes for television broadcasts, advertising, or motion pictures.](Tasks/4030.mdx)
- [Adjust positions and controls of cameras, printers, and related equipment to change focus, exposure, and lighting.](Tasks/4035.mdx)
- [Confer with directors, sound and lighting technicians, electricians, and other crew members to discuss assignments and determine filming sequences, desired effects, camera movements, and lighting requirements.](Tasks/4037.mdx)
- [Operate zoom lenses, changing images according to specifications and rehearsal instructions.](Tasks/4032.mdx)
- [Observe sets or locations for potential problems and to determine filming and lighting requirements.](Tasks/4038.mdx)
- [Set up and perform live shots for broadcast.](Tasks/18664.mdx)
- [Use cameras in any of several different camera mounts, such as stationary, track-mounted, or crane-mounted.](Tasks/4033.mdx)
- [Test, clean, maintain, and repair broadcast equipment, including testing microphones, to ensure proper working condition.](Tasks/18666.mdx)
- [Edit video for broadcast productions, including non-linear editing.](Tasks/18663.mdx)
