# Home Appliance Repairers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 49-9031.00

Repair, adjust, or install all types of electric or gas household appliances, such as refrigerators, washers, dryers, and ovens.

## Tasks

- [Bill customers for repair work, and collect payment.](Tasks/13789.mdx)
- [Observe and examine appliances during operation to detect specific malfunctions such as loose parts or leaking fluid.](Tasks/13799.mdx)
- [Talk to customers or refer to work orders to establish the nature of appliance malfunctions.](Tasks/13793.mdx)
- [Refer to schematic drawings, product manuals, and troubleshooting guides to diagnose and repair problems.](Tasks/13801.mdx)
- [Trace electrical circuits, following diagrams, and conduct tests with circuit testers and other equipment to locate shorts and grounds.](Tasks/13790.mdx)
- [Replace worn and defective parts such as switches, bearings, transmissions, belts, gears, circuit boards, or defective wiring.](Tasks/13792.mdx)
- [Provide repair cost estimates, and recommend whether appliance repair or replacement is a better choice.](Tasks/13796.mdx)
- [Disassemble appliances so that problems can be diagnosed and repairs can be made.](Tasks/13788.mdx)
- [Respond to emergency calls for problems such as gas leaks.](Tasks/13810.mdx)
- [Service and repair domestic electrical or gas appliances, such as clothes washers, refrigerators, stoves, and dryers.](Tasks/13791.mdx)
