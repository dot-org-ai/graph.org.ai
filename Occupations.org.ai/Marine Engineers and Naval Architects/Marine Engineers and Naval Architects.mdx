# Marine Engineers and Naval Architects

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 17-2121.00

Design, develop, and evaluate the operation of marine vessels, ship machinery, and related equipment, such as power supply and propulsion systems.

## Tasks

- [Perform monitoring activities to ensure that ships comply with international regulations and standards for life-saving equipment and pollution preventatives.](Tasks/21890.mdx)
- [Design complete hull and superstructure according to specifications and test data, in conformity with standards of safety, efficiency, and economy.](Tasks/21887.mdx)
- [Conduct analyses of ships, such as stability, structural, weight, and vibration analyses.](Tasks/21915.mdx)
- [Study design proposals and specifications to establish basic characteristics of craft, such as size, weight, speed, propulsion, displacement, and draft.](Tasks/21889.mdx)
- [Maintain contact with, and formulate reports for, contractors and clients to ensure completion of work at minimum cost.](Tasks/21907.mdx)
- [Coordinate activities with regulatory bodies to ensure repairs and alterations are at minimum cost and consistent with safety.](Tasks/21903.mdx)
- [Check, test, and maintain automatic controls and alarm systems.](Tasks/21894.mdx)
- [Prepare technical reports for use by engineering, management, or sales personnel.](Tasks/21905.mdx)
- [Design layout of craft interior, including cargo space, passenger compartments, ladder wells, and elevators.](Tasks/21895.mdx)
- [Prepare, or direct the preparation of, product or system layouts and detailed drawings and schematics.](Tasks/21900.mdx)
