# Clinical Data Managers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 15-2051.02

Apply knowledge of health care and database management to analyze clinical data, and to identify and report trends.

## Tasks

- [Design and validate clinical databases, including designing or testing logic checks.](Tasks/16281.mdx)
- [Process clinical data, including receipt, entry, verification, or filing of information.](Tasks/16284.mdx)
- [Generate data queries, based on validation checks or errors and omissions identified during data entry, to resolve identified problems.](Tasks/16280.mdx)
- [Develop project-specific data management plans that address areas such as coding, reporting, or transfer of data, database locks, and work flow processes.](Tasks/16285.mdx)
- [Monitor work productivity or quality to ensure compliance with standard operating procedures.](Tasks/16279.mdx)
