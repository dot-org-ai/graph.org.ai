# Nuclear Technicians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 19-4051.00

Assist nuclear physicists, nuclear engineers, or other scientists in laboratory, power generation, or electricity production activities. May operate, maintain, or provide quality control for nuclear testing and research equipment. May monitor radiation.

## Tasks

- [Follow nuclear equipment operational policies and procedures that ensure environmental safety.](Tasks/22308.mdx)
- [Conduct surveillance testing to determine safety of nuclear equipment.](Tasks/22309.mdx)
- [Monitor nuclear reactor equipment performance to identify operational inefficiencies, hazards, or needs for maintenance or repair.](Tasks/22310.mdx)
- [Test plant equipment to ensure it is operating properly.](Tasks/22311.mdx)
- [Apply safety tags to equipment needing maintenance.](Tasks/22312.mdx)
- [Follow policies and procedures for radiation workers to ensure personnel safety.](Tasks/22313.mdx)
- [Modify, devise, or maintain nuclear equipment used in operations.](Tasks/22314.mdx)
- [Monitor instruments, gauges, or recording devices under direction of nuclear experimenters.](Tasks/22315.mdx)
- [Perform testing, maintenance, repair, or upgrading of accelerator systems.](Tasks/22316.mdx)
- [Warn maintenance workers of radiation hazards and direct workers to vacate hazardous areas.](Tasks/22318.mdx)
