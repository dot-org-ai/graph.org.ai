# Critical Care Nurses

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 29-1141.03

Provide specialized nursing care for patients in critical or coronary care units.

## Tasks

- [Evaluate patients' vital signs or laboratory data to determine emergency intervention needs.](Tasks/18327.mdx)
- [Monitor patients for changes in status and indications of conditions such as sepsis or shock and institute appropriate interventions.](Tasks/18334.mdx)
- [Administer medications intravenously, by injection, orally, through gastric tubes, or by other methods.](Tasks/18330.mdx)
- [Monitor patients' fluid intake and output to detect emerging problems, such as fluid and electrolyte imbalances.](Tasks/18333.mdx)
- [Prioritize nursing care for assigned critically ill patients, based on assessment data or identified needs.](Tasks/18344.mdx)
- [Compile and analyze data obtained from monitoring or diagnostic tests.](Tasks/18339.mdx)
- [Conduct pulmonary assessments to identify abnormal respiratory patterns or breathing sounds that indicate problems.](Tasks/18340.mdx)
- [Assess patients' pain levels or sedation requirements.](Tasks/18335.mdx)
- [Collaborate with other health care professionals to develop and revise treatment plans, based on identified needs and assessment data.](Tasks/18337.mdx)
- [Document patients' medical histories and assessment findings.](Tasks/18341.mdx)
