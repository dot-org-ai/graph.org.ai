# Mechanical Drafters

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 17-3013.00

Prepare detailed working diagrams of machinery and mechanical devices, including dimensions, fastening methods, and other engineering information.

## Tasks

- [Develop detailed design drawings and specifications for mechanical equipment, dies, tools, and controls, using computer-assisted drafting (CAD) equipment.](Tasks/1447.mdx)
- [Produce three-dimensional models, using computer-aided design (CAD) software.](Tasks/20964.mdx)
- [Lay out and draw schematic, orthographic, or angle views to depict functional relationships of components, assemblies, systems, and machines.](Tasks/1455.mdx)
- [Modify and revise designs to correct operating deficiencies or to reduce production problems.](Tasks/1452.mdx)
- [Review and analyze specifications, sketches, drawings, ideas, and related data to assess factors affecting component designs and the procedures and instructions to be followed.](Tasks/1449.mdx)
- [Check dimensions of materials to be used and assign numbers to the materials.](Tasks/1454.mdx)
- [Design scale or full-size blueprints of specialty items, such as furniture and automobile body or chassis components.](Tasks/1453.mdx)
- [Compute mathematical formulas to develop and design detailed specifications for components or machinery, using computer-assisted equipment.](Tasks/1450.mdx)
- [Coordinate with and consult other workers to design, lay out, or detail components and systems and to resolve design or other problems.](Tasks/1448.mdx)
- [Confer with customer representatives to review schematics and answer questions pertaining to installation of systems.](Tasks/1456.mdx)
