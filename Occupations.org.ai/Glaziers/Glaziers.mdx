# Glaziers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-2121.00

Install glass in windows, skylights, store fronts, and display cases, or on surfaces, such as building fronts, interior walls, ceilings, and tabletops.

## Tasks

- [Read and interpret blueprints or specifications to determine size, shape, color, type, or thickness of glass, location of framing, installation procedures, or staging or scaffolding materials required.](Tasks/11484.mdx)
- [Determine plumb of walls or ceilings, using plumb lines and levels.](Tasks/11485.mdx)
- [Install pre-assembled metal or wood frameworks for windows or doors to be fitted with glass panels, using hand tools.](Tasks/11496.mdx)
- [Fabricate or install metal sashes or moldings for glass installation, using aluminum or steel framing.](Tasks/11486.mdx)
- [Operate cranes or hoists with suction cups to lift large, heavy pieces of glass.](Tasks/11504.mdx)
- [Set glass doors into frames and bolt metal hinges, handles, locks, or other hardware to attach doors to frames and walls.](Tasks/11492.mdx)
- [Cut, fit, install, repair, or replace glass or glass substitutes, such as plastic or aluminum, in building interiors or exteriors or in furniture or other products.](Tasks/11490.mdx)
- [Drive trucks to installation sites and unload mirrors, glass equipment, or tools.](Tasks/11495.mdx)
- [Load and arrange glass or mirrors onto delivery trucks, using suction cups or cranes to lift glass.](Tasks/11499.mdx)
- [Measure mirrors and dimensions of areas to be covered to determine work procedures.](Tasks/11487.mdx)
