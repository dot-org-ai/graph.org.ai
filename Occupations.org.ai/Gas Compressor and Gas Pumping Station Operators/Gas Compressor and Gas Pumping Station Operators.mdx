# Gas Compressor and Gas Pumping Station Operators

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 53-7071.00

Operate steam-, gas-, electric motor-, or internal combustion-engine driven compressors. Transmit, compress, or recover gases, such as butane, nitrogen, hydrogen, and natural gas.

## Tasks

- [Monitor meters and pressure gauges to determine consumption rate variations, temperatures, and pressures.](Tasks/14596.mdx)
- [Respond to problems by adjusting control room equipment or instructing other personnel to adjust equipment at problem locations or in other control areas.](Tasks/14603.mdx)
- [Record instrument readings and operational changes in operating logs.](Tasks/14600.mdx)
- [Adjust valves and equipment to obtain specified performance.](Tasks/14597.mdx)
- [Move controls and turn valves to start compressor engines, pumps, and auxiliary equipment.](Tasks/14601.mdx)
- [Operate power-driven pumps that transfer liquids, semi-liquids, gases, or powdered materials.](Tasks/14599.mdx)
- [Submit daily reports on facility operations.](Tasks/14606.mdx)
- [Take samples of gases and conduct chemical tests to determine gas quality and sulfur or moisture content, or send samples to laboratories for analysis.](Tasks/14604.mdx)
- [Read gas meters, and maintain records of the amounts of gas received and dispensed from holders.](Tasks/14598.mdx)
- [Turn knobs or switches to regulate pressures.](Tasks/14602.mdx)
