# Airfield Operations Specialists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 53-2022.00

Ensure the safe takeoff and landing of commercial and military aircraft. Duties include coordination between air-traffic control and maintenance personnel, dispatching, using airfield landing and navigational aids, implementing airfield safety procedures, monitoring and maintaining flight records, and applying knowledge of weather information.

- [Inspect airfield conditions to ensure compliance with federal regulatory requirements.](Tasks/23939.mdx)
- [Implement airfield safety procedures to ensure a safe operating environment for personnel and aircraft operation.](Tasks/10613.mdx)

- [Assist in responding to aircraft and medical emergencies.](Tasks/20456.mdx)
- [Initiate or conduct airport-wide coordination of snow removal on runways and taxiways.](Tasks/23941.mdx)
- [Manage wildlife on and around airport grounds.](Tasks/20457.mdx)

- [Plan and coordinate airfield construction.](Tasks/10614.mdx)
- [Monitor the arrival, parking, refueling, loading, and departure of all aircraft.](Tasks/10616.mdx)
