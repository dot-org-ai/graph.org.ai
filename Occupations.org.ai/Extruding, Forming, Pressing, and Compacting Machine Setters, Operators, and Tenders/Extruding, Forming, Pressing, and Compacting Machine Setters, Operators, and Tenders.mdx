# Extruding, Forming, Pressing, and Compacting Machine Setters, Operators, and Tenders

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-9041.00

Set up, operate, or tend machines, such as glass-forming machines, plodder machines, and tuber machines, to shape and form products such as glassware, food, rubber, soap, brick, tile, clay, wax, tobacco, or cosmetics.

## Tasks

- [Adjust machine components to regulate speeds, pressures, and temperatures, and amounts, dimensions, and flow of materials or ingredients.](Tasks/12444.mdx)
- [Press control buttons to activate machinery and equipment.](Tasks/12447.mdx)
- [Examine, measure, and weigh materials or products to verify conformance to standards, using measuring devices such as templates, micrometers, or scales.](Tasks/12445.mdx)
- [Monitor machine operations and observe lights and gauges to detect malfunctions.](Tasks/12446.mdx)
- [Clear jams, and remove defective or substandard materials or products.](Tasks/12455.mdx)
- [Notify supervisors when extruded filaments fail to meet standards.](Tasks/12452.mdx)
- [Record and maintain production data, such as meter readings, and quantities, types, and dimensions of materials produced.](Tasks/12451.mdx)
- [Review work orders, specifications, or instructions to determine materials, ingredients, procedures, components, settings, and adjustments for extruding, forming, pressing, or compacting machines.](Tasks/12449.mdx)
- [Turn controls to adjust machine functions, such as regulating air pressure, creating vacuums, and adjusting coolant flow.](Tasks/12448.mdx)
- [Clean dies, arbors, compression chambers, and molds, using swabs, sponges, or air hoses.](Tasks/12466.mdx)
