# Technical Writers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 27-3042.00

Write technical materials, such as equipment manuals, appendices, or operating and maintenance instructions. May assist in layout work.

## Tasks

- [Organize material and complete writing assignment according to set standards regarding order, clarity, conciseness, style, and terminology.](Tasks/3966.mdx)
- [Maintain records and files of work and revisions.](Tasks/3967.mdx)
- [Edit, standardize, or make changes to material prepared by other writers or establishment personnel.](Tasks/3968.mdx)
- [Select photographs, drawings, sketches, diagrams, and charts to illustrate material.](Tasks/3971.mdx)
- [Interview production and engineering personnel and read journals and other material to become familiar with product technologies and production methods.](Tasks/3973.mdx)
- [Develop or maintain online help documentation.](Tasks/20243.mdx)
- [Assist in laying out material for publication.](Tasks/3976.mdx)
- [Study drawings, specifications, mockups, and product samples to integrate and delineate technology, operating procedure, and production sequence and detail.](Tasks/3972.mdx)
- [Arrange for typing, duplication, and distribution of material.](Tasks/3975.mdx)
- [Observe production, developmental, and experimental activities to determine operating procedure and detail.](Tasks/3974.mdx)
