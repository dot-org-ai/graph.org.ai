# Appraisers and Assessors of Real Estate

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 13-2023.00

Appraise real estate, exclusively, and estimate its fair value. May assess taxes in accordance with prescribed schedules.

## Tasks

- [Compute final estimation of property values, taking into account such factors as depreciation, replacement costs, value comparisons of similar properties, and income potential.](Tasks/21549.mdx)
- [Prepare written reports that estimate property values, outline methods by which the estimations were made, and meet appraisal standards.](Tasks/21550.mdx)
- [Photograph interiors and exteriors of properties to assist in estimating property value, substantiate findings, and complete appraisal reports.](Tasks/21569.mdx)
- [Search public records for transactions such as sales, leases, and assessments.](Tasks/21565.mdx)
- [Obtain county land values and sales information about nearby properties to aid in establishment of property values.](Tasks/21570.mdx)
- [Maintain familiarity with aspects of local real estate markets.](Tasks/21564.mdx)
- [Inspect properties, considering factors such as market value, location, and building or replacement costs to determine appraisal value.](Tasks/21556.mdx)
- [Collect and analyze relevant data to identify real estate market trends.](Tasks/21552.mdx)
- [Review information about transfers of property to ensure its accuracy, checking basic information on buyers, sellers, and sales prices and making corrections as necessary.](Tasks/21558.mdx)
- [Analyze trends in sales prices, construction costs, and rents, to assess property values or determine the accuracy of assessments.](Tasks/21577.mdx)
