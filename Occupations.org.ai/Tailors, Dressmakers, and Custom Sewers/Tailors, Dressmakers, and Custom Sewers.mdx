# Tailors, Dressmakers, and Custom Sewers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-6052.00

Design, make, alter, repair, or fit garments.

## Tasks

- [Measure parts, such as sleeves or pant legs, and mark or pin-fold alteration lines.](Tasks/12177.mdx)
- [Remove stitches from garments to be altered, using rippers or razor blades.](Tasks/12181.mdx)
- [Sew garments, using needles and thread or sewing machines.](Tasks/12176.mdx)
- [Let out or take in seams in suits and other garments to improve fit.](Tasks/12179.mdx)
- [Measure customers, using tape measures, and record measurements.](Tasks/12190.mdx)
- [Fit and study garments on customers to determine required alterations.](Tasks/12175.mdx)
- [Trim excess material, using scissors.](Tasks/12187.mdx)
- [Assemble garment parts and join parts with basting stitches, using needles and thread or sewing machines.](Tasks/12180.mdx)
- [Make garment style changes, such as tapering pant legs, narrowing lapels, and adding or removing padding.](Tasks/12189.mdx)
- [Maintain garment drape and proportions as alterations are performed.](Tasks/12185.mdx)
