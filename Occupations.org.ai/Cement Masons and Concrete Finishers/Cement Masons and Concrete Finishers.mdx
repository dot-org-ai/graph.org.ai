# Cement Masons and Concrete Finishers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-2051.00

Smooth and finish surfaces of poured concrete, such as floors, walks, sidewalks, roads, or curbs using a variety of hand and power tools. Align forms for sidewalks, curbs, or gutters; patch voids; and use saws to cut expansion joints.

## Tasks

- [Check the forms that hold the concrete to see that they are properly constructed.](Tasks/7047.mdx)
- [Set the forms that hold concrete to the desired pitch and depth, and align them.](Tasks/7048.mdx)
- [Spread, level, and smooth concrete, using rake, shovel, hand or power trowel, hand or power screed, and float.](Tasks/7049.mdx)
- [Monitor how the wind, heat, or cold affect the curing of the concrete throughout the entire process.](Tasks/7051.mdx)
- [Mold expansion joints and edges, using edging tools, jointers, and straightedge.](Tasks/7050.mdx)
- [Signal truck driver to position truck to facilitate pouring concrete, and move chute to direct concrete on forms.](Tasks/7052.mdx)
- [Direct the casting of the concrete and supervise laborers who use shovels or special tools to spread it.](Tasks/7055.mdx)
- [Produce rough concrete surface, using broom.](Tasks/7053.mdx)
- [Apply hardening and sealing compounds to cure surface of concrete, and waterproof or restore surface.](Tasks/7061.mdx)
- [Operate power vibrator to compact concrete.](Tasks/7054.mdx)
