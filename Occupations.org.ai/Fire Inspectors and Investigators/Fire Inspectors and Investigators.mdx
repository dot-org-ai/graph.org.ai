# Fire Inspectors and Investigators

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 33-2021.00

Inspect buildings to detect fire hazards and enforce local ordinances and state laws, or investigate and gather facts to determine cause of fires and explosions.

## Tasks

- [Prepare and maintain reports of investigation results, and records of convicted arsonists and arson suspects.](Tasks/22991.mdx)
- [Testify in court cases involving fires, suspected arson, and false alarms.](Tasks/22992.mdx)
- [Package collected pieces of evidence in securely closed containers, such as bags, crates, or boxes, to protect them.](Tasks/22993.mdx)
- [Conduct inspections and acceptance testing of newly installed fire protection systems.](Tasks/22994.mdx)
- [Analyze evidence and other information to determine probable cause of fire or explosion.](Tasks/22995.mdx)
- [Photograph damage and evidence related to causes of fires or explosions to document investigation findings.](Tasks/22996.mdx)
- [Inspect buildings to locate hazardous conditions and fire code violations, such as accumulations of combustible material, electrical wiring problems, and inadequate or non-functional fire exits.](Tasks/22998.mdx)
- [Examine fire sites and collect evidence such as glass, metal fragments, charred wood, and accelerant residue for use in determining the cause of a fire.](Tasks/22997.mdx)
- [Instruct children about the dangers of fire.](Tasks/22999.mdx)
- [Conduct fire code compliance follow-ups to ensure that corrective actions have been taken in cases where violations were found.](Tasks/23000.mdx)
