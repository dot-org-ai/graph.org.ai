# Dermatologists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 29-1213.00

Diagnose and treat diseases relating to the skin, hair, and nails. May perform both medical and dermatological surgery functions.

## Tasks

- [Conduct complete skin examinations.](Tasks/17094.mdx)
- [Diagnose and treat pigmented lesions such as common acquired nevi, congenital nevi, dysplastic nevi, Spitz nevi, blue nevi, or melanoma.](Tasks/17092.mdx)
- [Perform incisional biopsies to diagnose melanoma.](Tasks/17091.mdx)
- [Perform skin surgery to improve appearance, make early diagnoses, or control diseases such as skin cancer.](Tasks/17090.mdx)
- [Counsel patients on topics such as the need for annual dermatologic screenings, sun protection, skin cancer awareness, or skin and lymph node self-examinations.](Tasks/17093.mdx)
- [Diagnose and treat skin conditions such as acne, dandruff, athlete's foot, moles, psoriasis, or skin cancer.](Tasks/17095.mdx)
- [Record patients' health histories.](Tasks/17078.mdx)
- [Recommend diagnostic tests based on patients' histories and physical examination findings.](Tasks/17085.mdx)
- [Prescribe hormonal agents or topical treatments such as contraceptives, spironolactone, antiandrogens, oral corticosteroids, retinoids, benzoyl peroxide, or antibiotics.](Tasks/17089.mdx)
- [Conduct or order diagnostic tests such as chest radiographs (x-rays), microbiologic tests, or endocrinologic tests.](Tasks/17084.mdx)
