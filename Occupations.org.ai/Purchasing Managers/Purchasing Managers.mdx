# Purchasing Managers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 11-3061.00

Plan, direct, or coordinate the activities of buyers, purchasing officers, and related workers involved in purchasing materials, products, and services. Includes wholesale or retail trade merchandising managers and procurement managers.

## Tasks

- [Develop and implement purchasing and contract management instructions, policies, and procedures.](Tasks/1027.mdx)
- [Locate vendors of materials, equipment or supplies, and interview them to determine product availability and terms of sales.](Tasks/1021.mdx)
- [Prepare bid awards requiring board approval.](Tasks/1033.mdx)
- [Direct and coordinate activities of personnel engaged in buying, selling, and distributing materials, equipment, machinery, and supplies.](Tasks/1032.mdx)
- [Review purchase order claims and contracts for conformance to company policy.](Tasks/1025.mdx)
- [Review, evaluate, and approve specifications for issuing and awarding bids.](Tasks/1031.mdx)
- [Administer online purchasing systems.](Tasks/1035.mdx)
- [Prepare and process requisitions and purchase orders for supplies and equipment.](Tasks/1022.mdx)
