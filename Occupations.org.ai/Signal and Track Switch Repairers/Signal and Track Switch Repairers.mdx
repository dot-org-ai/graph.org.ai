# Signal and Track Switch Repairers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 49-9097.00

Install, inspect, test, maintain, or repair electric gate crossings, signals, signal equipment, track switches, section lines, or intercommunications systems within a railroad system.

## Tasks

- [Inspect and test operation, mechanical parts, and circuitry of gate crossings, signals, and signal equipment such as interlocks and hotbox detectors.](Tasks/13906.mdx)
- [Inspect electrical units of railroad grade crossing gates and repair loose bolts and defective electrical connections and parts.](Tasks/13910.mdx)
- [Test and repair track circuits.](Tasks/23936.mdx)
- [Drive motor vehicles to job sites.](Tasks/13908.mdx)
- [Install, inspect, maintain, and repair various railroad service equipment on the road or in the shop, including railroad signal systems.](Tasks/13905.mdx)
- [Tighten loose bolts, using wrenches, and test circuits and connections by opening and closing gates.](Tasks/13909.mdx)
- [Inspect switch-controlling mechanisms on trolley wires and in track beds, using hand tools and test equipment.](Tasks/13907.mdx)
- [Replace defective wiring, broken lenses, or burned-out light bulbs.](Tasks/13911.mdx)
- [Inspect, maintain, and replace batteries as needed.](Tasks/13913.mdx)
- [Record and report information about mileage or track inspected, repairs performed, and equipment requiring replacement.](Tasks/13912.mdx)
