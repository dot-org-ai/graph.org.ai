# Outdoor Power Equipment and Other Small Engine Mechanics

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 49-3053.00

Diagnose, adjust, repair, or overhaul small engines used to power lawn mowers, chain saws, recreational sporting equipment, and related equipment.

## Tasks

- [Record repairs made, time spent, and parts used.](Tasks/13777.mdx)
- [Test and inspect engines to determine malfunctions, to locate missing and broken parts, and to verify repairs, using diagnostic instruments.](Tasks/13780.mdx)
- [Dismantle engines, using hand tools, and examine parts for defects.](Tasks/13784.mdx)
- [Repair and maintain gasoline engines used to power equipment such as portable saws, lawn mowers, generators, and compressors.](Tasks/13774.mdx)
- [Adjust points, valves, carburetors, distributors, and spark plug gaps, using feeler gauges.](Tasks/13775.mdx)
- [Repair or replace defective parts such as magnetos, water pumps, gears, pistons, and carburetors, using hand tools.](Tasks/13781.mdx)
- [Perform routine maintenance such as cleaning and oiling parts, honing cylinders, and tuning ignition systems.](Tasks/13778.mdx)
- [Reassemble engines after repair or maintenance work is complete.](Tasks/13776.mdx)
- [Replace motors.](Tasks/13785.mdx)
- [Obtain problem descriptions from customers, and prepare cost estimates for repairs.](Tasks/13779.mdx)
