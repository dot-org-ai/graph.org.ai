# Electricians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-2111.00

Install, maintain, and repair electrical wiring, equipment, and fixtures. Ensure that work is in accordance with relevant codes. May install or service street lights, intercom systems, or electrical control systems.

## Tasks

- [Prepare sketches or follow blueprints to determine the location of wiring or equipment and to ensure conformance to building and safety codes.](Tasks/2882.mdx)
- [Place conduit, pipes, or tubing, inside designated partitions, walls, or other concealed areas, and pull insulated wires or cables through the conduit to complete circuits between boxes.](Tasks/2888.mdx)
- [Work from ladders, scaffolds, or roofs to install, maintain, or repair electrical wiring, equipment, or fixtures.](Tasks/2887.mdx)
- [Use a variety of tools or equipment, such as power construction equipment, measuring devices, power tools, and testing equipment, such as oscilloscopes, ammeters, or test lamps.](Tasks/2883.mdx)
- [Assemble, install, test, or maintain electrical or electronic wiring, equipment, appliances, apparatus, or fixtures, using hand tools or power tools.](Tasks/2873.mdx)
- [Connect wires to circuit breakers, transformers, or other components.](Tasks/2875.mdx)
- [Maintain current electrician's license or identification card to meet governmental regulations.](Tasks/2879.mdx)
- [Plan layout and installation of electrical wiring, equipment, or fixtures, based on job specifications and local codes.](Tasks/2880.mdx)
- [Direct or train workers to install, maintain, or repair electrical wiring, equipment, or fixtures.](Tasks/2881.mdx)
- [Test electrical systems or continuity of circuits in electrical wiring, equipment, or fixtures, using testing devices, such as ohmmeters, voltmeters, or oscilloscopes, to ensure compatibility and safety of system.](Tasks/2878.mdx)
