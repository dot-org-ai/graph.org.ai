# Insurance Underwriters

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 13-2053.00

Review individual applications for insurance to evaluate degree of risk involved and determine acceptance of applications.

## Tasks

- [Examine documents to determine degree of risk from factors such as applicant health, financial standing and value, and condition of property.](Tasks/21045.mdx)
- [Decline excessive risks.](Tasks/1261.mdx)
- [Write to field representatives, medical personnel, or others to obtain further information, quote rates, or explain company underwriting policies.](Tasks/1262.mdx)
- [Evaluate possibility of losses due to catastrophe or excessive insurance.](Tasks/1263.mdx)
- [Review company records to determine amount of insurance in force on single risk or group of closely related risks.](Tasks/1265.mdx)
- [Decrease value of policy when risk is substandard and specify applicable endorsements or apply rating to ensure safe, profitable distribution of risks, using reference materials.](Tasks/1264.mdx)
- [Authorize reinsurance of policy when risk is high.](Tasks/1266.mdx)
