# Forest Fire Inspectors and Prevention Specialists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 33-2022.00

Enforce fire regulations, inspect forest for fire hazards, and recommend forest fire prevention or control measures. May report forest fires and weather conditions.

## Tasks

- [Relay messages about emergencies, accidents, locations of crew and personnel, and fire hazard conditions.](Tasks/11102.mdx)
- [Conduct wildland firefighting training.](Tasks/21143.mdx)
- [Estimate sizes and characteristics of fires, and report findings to base camps by radio or telephone.](Tasks/11104.mdx)
- [Direct crews working on firelines during forest fires.](Tasks/11103.mdx)
- [Locate forest fires on area maps, using azimuth sighters and known landmarks.](Tasks/11107.mdx)
- [Extinguish smaller fires with portable extinguishers, shovels, and axes.](Tasks/11106.mdx)
- [Patrol assigned areas, looking for forest fires, hazardous conditions, and weather phenomena.](Tasks/11112.mdx)
- [Compile and report meteorological data, such as temperature, relative humidity, wind direction and velocity, and types of cloud formations.](Tasks/11113.mdx)
- [Examine and inventory firefighting equipment, such as axes, fire hoses, shovels, pumps, buckets, and fire extinguishers, to determine amount and condition.](Tasks/11109.mdx)
- [Educate the public about fire safety and prevention.](Tasks/21144.mdx)
