# Fashion Designers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 27-1022.00

Design clothing and accessories. Create original designs or adapt fashion trends.

## Tasks

- [Sketch rough and detailed drawings of apparel or accessories, and write specifications such as color schemes, construction, material types, and accessory requirements.](Tasks/13015.mdx)
- [Examine sample garments on and off models, modifying designs to achieve desired effects.](Tasks/13014.mdx)
- [Confer with sales and management executives or with clients to discuss design ideas.](Tasks/13016.mdx)
- [Select materials and production techniques to be used for products.](Tasks/13019.mdx)
- [Provide sample garments to agents and sales representatives, and arrange for showings of sample garments at sales meetings or fashion shows.](Tasks/13020.mdx)
- [Direct and coordinate workers involved in drawing and cutting patterns and constructing samples or finished garments.](Tasks/13013.mdx)
- [Identify target markets for designs, looking at factors such as age, gender, and socioeconomic status.](Tasks/13017.mdx)
- [Collaborate with other designers to coordinate special products and designs.](Tasks/13024.mdx)
- [Attend fashion shows and review garment magazines and manuals to gather information about fashion trends and consumer preferences.](Tasks/13018.mdx)
- [Purchase new or used clothing and accessory items as needed to complete designs.](Tasks/13022.mdx)
