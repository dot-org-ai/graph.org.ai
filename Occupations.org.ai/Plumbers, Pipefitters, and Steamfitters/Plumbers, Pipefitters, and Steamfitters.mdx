# Plumbers, Pipefitters, and Steamfitters

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-2152.00

Assemble, install, alter, and repair pipelines or pipe systems that carry water, steam, air, or other liquids or gases. May install heating and cooling equipment and mechanical control systems. Includes sprinkler fitters.

## Tasks

- [Shut off steam, water, or other gases or liquids from pipe sections, using valve keys or wrenches.](Tasks/23469.mdx)
- [Install underground storm, sanitary, or water piping systems, extending piping as needed to connect fixtures and plumbing.](Tasks/23478.mdx)
- [Assemble pipe sections, tubing, or fittings, using couplings, clamps, screws, bolts, cement, plastic solvent, caulking, or soldering, brazing, or welding equipment.](Tasks/23466.mdx)
- [Locate and mark the position of pipe installations, connections, passage holes, or fixtures in structures, using measuring instruments such as rulers or levels.](Tasks/23474.mdx)
- [Cut, thread, or hammer pipes to specifications, using tools such as saws, cutting torches, pipe threaders, or pipe benders.](Tasks/23459.mdx)
- [Lay out full scale drawings of pipe systems, supports, or related equipment, according to blueprints.](Tasks/23460.mdx)
- [Plan pipe system layout, installation, or repair, according to specifications.](Tasks/23462.mdx)
- [Review blueprints, building codes, or specifications to determine work details or procedures.](Tasks/23475.mdx)
- [Select pipe sizes, types, or related materials, such as supports, hangers, or hydraulic cylinders, according to specifications.](Tasks/23465.mdx)
- [Fill pipes or plumbing fixtures with water or air and observe pressure gauges to detect and locate leaks.](Tasks/23472.mdx)
