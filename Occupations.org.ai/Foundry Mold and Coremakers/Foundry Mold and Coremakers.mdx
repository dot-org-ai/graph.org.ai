# Foundry Mold and Coremakers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-4071.00

Make or form wax or sand cores or molds used in the production of metal castings in foundries.

## Tasks

- [Clean and smooth molds, cores, and core boxes, and repair surface imperfections.](Tasks/12050.mdx)
- [Sift and pack sand into mold sections, core boxes, and pattern contours, using hand or pneumatic ramming tools.](Tasks/12055.mdx)
- [Position patterns inside mold sections, and clamp sections together.](Tasks/12053.mdx)
- [Position cores into lower sections of molds, and reassemble molds for pouring.](Tasks/12054.mdx)
- [Sprinkle or spray parting agents onto patterns and mold sections to facilitate removal of patterns from molds.](Tasks/12052.mdx)
- [Form and assemble slab cores around patterns, and position wire in mold sections to reinforce molds, using hand tools and glue.](Tasks/12059.mdx)
- [Move and position workpieces, such as mold sections, patterns, and bottom boards, using cranes, or signal others to move workpieces.](Tasks/12051.mdx)
- [Lift upper mold sections from lower sections, and remove molded patterns.](Tasks/12058.mdx)
- [Cut spouts, runner holes, and sprue holes into molds.](Tasks/12057.mdx)
- [Tend machines that bond cope and drag together to form completed shell molds.](Tasks/12056.mdx)
