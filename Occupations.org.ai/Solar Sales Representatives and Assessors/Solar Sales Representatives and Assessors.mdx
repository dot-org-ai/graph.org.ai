# Solar Sales Representatives and Assessors

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 41-4011.07

Contact new or existing customers to determine their solar equipment needs, suggest systems or equipment, or estimate costs.

## Tasks

- [Prepare proposals, quotes, contracts, or presentations for potential solar customers.](Tasks/17655.mdx)
- [Select solar energy products, systems, or services for customers based on electrical energy requirements, site conditions, price, or other factors.](Tasks/17656.mdx)
- [Provide customers with information, such as quotes, orders, sales, shipping, warranties, credit, funding options, incentives, or tax rebates.](Tasks/17663.mdx)
- [Gather information from prospective customers to identify their solar energy needs.](Tasks/17661.mdx)
- [Calculate potential solar resources or solar array production for a particular site considering issues such as climate, shading, and roof orientation.](Tasks/17658.mdx)
- [Generate solar energy customer leads to develop new accounts.](Tasks/17654.mdx)
- [Provide technical information about solar power, solar systems, equipment, and services to potential customers or dealers.](Tasks/17664.mdx)
- [Assess sites to determine suitability for solar equipment, using equipment such as tape measures, compasses, and computer software.](Tasks/17657.mdx)
- [Take quote requests or orders from dealers or customers.](Tasks/17665.mdx)
- [Prepare or review detailed design drawings, specifications, or lists related to solar installations.](Tasks/17662.mdx)
