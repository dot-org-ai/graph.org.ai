# Financial Examiners

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 13-2061.00

Enforce or ensure compliance with laws and regulations governing financial and securities institutions and financial and real estate transactions. May examine, verify, or authenticate records.

## Tasks

- [Direct and participate in formal and informal meetings with bank directors, trustees, senior management, counsels, outside accountants, and consultants to gather information and discuss findings.](Tasks/7324.mdx)
- [Recommend actions to ensure compliance with laws and regulations, or to protect solvency of institutions.](Tasks/7317.mdx)
- [Prepare reports, exhibits, and other supporting schedules that detail an institution's safety and soundness, compliance with laws and regulations, and recommended solutions to questionable financial conditions.](Tasks/7319.mdx)
- [Resolve problems concerning the overall financial integrity of banking institutions including loan investment portfolios, capital, earnings, and specific or large troubled accounts.](Tasks/7327.mdx)
- [Investigate activities of institutions to enforce laws and regulations and to ensure legality of transactions and operations or financial solvency.](Tasks/7314.mdx)
- [Review balance sheets, operating income and expense accounts, and loan documentation to confirm institution assets and liabilities.](Tasks/7320.mdx)
- [Plan, supervise, and review work of assigned subordinates.](Tasks/7316.mdx)
- [Review audit reports of internal and external auditors to monitor adequacy of scope of reports or to discover specific weaknesses in internal routines.](Tasks/7321.mdx)
- [Examine the minutes of meetings of directors, stockholders, and committees to investigate the specific authority extended at various levels of management.](Tasks/7318.mdx)
- [Train other examiners in the financial examination process.](Tasks/7322.mdx)
