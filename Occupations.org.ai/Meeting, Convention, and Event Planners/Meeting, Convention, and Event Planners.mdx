# Meeting, Convention, and Event Planners

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 13-1121.00

Coordinate activities of staff, convention personnel, or clients to make arrangements for group meetings, events, or conventions.

## Tasks

- [Consult with customers to determine objectives and requirements for events, such as meetings, conferences, and conventions.](Tasks/1209.mdx)
- [Review event bills for accuracy and approve payment.](Tasks/1212.mdx)
- [Coordinate services for events, such as accommodation and transportation for participants, facilities, catering, signage, displays, special needs requirements, printing and event security.](Tasks/1214.mdx)
- [Arrange the availability of audio-visual equipment, transportation, displays, and other event needs.](Tasks/1215.mdx)
- [Confer with staff at a chosen event site to coordinate details.](Tasks/1211.mdx)
- [Inspect event facilities to ensure that they conform to customer requirements.](Tasks/1216.mdx)
- [Maintain records of event aspects, including financial details.](Tasks/1217.mdx)

- [Evaluate and select providers of services according to customer requirements.](Tasks/1222.mdx)
