# Metal-Refining Furnace Operators and Tenders

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-4051.00

Operate or tend furnaces, such as gas, oil, coal, electric-arc or electric induction, open-hearth, or oxygen furnaces, to melt and refine metal before casting or to produce specified types of steel.

## Tasks

- [Regulate supplies of fuel and air, or control flow of electric current and water coolant to heat furnaces and adjust temperatures.](Tasks/10163.mdx)
- [Draw smelted metal samples from furnaces or kettles for analysis, and calculate types and amounts of materials needed to ensure that materials meet specifications.](Tasks/10158.mdx)
- [Weigh materials to be charged into furnaces, using scales.](Tasks/10162.mdx)
- [Record production data, and maintain production logs.](Tasks/10160.mdx)
- [Observe air and temperature gauges or metal color and fluidity, and turn fuel valves or adjust controls to maintain required temperatures.](Tasks/10165.mdx)
- [Operate controls to move or discharge metal workpieces from furnaces.](Tasks/10161.mdx)
- [Inspect furnaces and equipment to locate defects and wear.](Tasks/10164.mdx)
- [Drain, transfer, or remove molten metal from furnaces, and place it into molds, using hoists, pumps, or ladles.](Tasks/10159.mdx)
- [Kindle fires, and shovel fuel and other materials into furnaces or onto conveyors by hand, with hoists, or by directing crane operators.](Tasks/10168.mdx)
- [Prepare material to load into furnaces, including cleaning, crushing, or applying chemicals, by using crushing machines, shovels, rakes, or sprayers.](Tasks/10171.mdx)
