# Railroad Brake, Signal, and Switch Operators and Locomotive Firers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 53-4022.00

Operate or monitor railroad track switches or locomotive instruments. May couple or uncouple rolling stock to make up or break up trains. Watch for and relay traffic signals. May inspect couplings, air hoses, journal boxes, and hand brakes. May watch for dragging equipment or obstacles on rights-of-way.

## Tasks

- [Observe train signals along routes and verify their meanings for engineers.](Tasks/23774.mdx)
- [Signal locomotive engineers to start or stop trains when coupling or uncoupling cars, using hand signals, lanterns, or radio communication.](Tasks/23775.mdx)
- [Pull or push track switches to reroute cars.](Tasks/23776.mdx)
- [Observe signals from other crew members so that work activities can be coordinated.](Tasks/23777.mdx)
- [Monitor trains as they go around curves to detect dragging equipment and smoking journal boxes.](Tasks/23778.mdx)
- [Inspect couplings, air hoses, journal boxes, and handbrakes to ensure that they are securely fastened and functioning properly.](Tasks/23779.mdx)
- [Observe tracks from left sides of locomotives to detect obstructions on tracks.](Tasks/23780.mdx)
- [Operate locomotives in emergency situations.](Tasks/23781.mdx)
- [Raise levers to couple and uncouple cars for makeup and breakup of trains.](Tasks/23782.mdx)
- [Climb ladders to tops of cars to set brakes.](Tasks/23783.mdx)
