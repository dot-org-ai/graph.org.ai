# Physician Assistants

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 29-1071.00

Provide healthcare services typically performed by a physician, under the supervision of a physician. Conduct complete physicals, provide treatment, and counsel patients. May, in some cases, prescribe medication. Must graduate from an accredited educational program for physician assistants.

## Tasks

- [Make tentative diagnoses and decisions about management and treatment of patients.](Tasks/4075.mdx)
- [Interpret diagnostic test results for deviations from normal.](Tasks/4074.mdx)
- [Prescribe therapy or medication with physician approval.](Tasks/4078.mdx)
- [Obtain, compile, and record patient medical data, including health history, progress notes, and results of physical examination.](Tasks/4076.mdx)
- [Examine patients to obtain information about their physical condition.](Tasks/4073.mdx)
- [Administer or order diagnostic tests, such as x-ray, electrocardiogram, and laboratory tests.](Tasks/4077.mdx)
- [Instruct and counsel patients about prescribed therapeutic regimens, normal growth and development, family planning, emotional problems of daily living, and health maintenance.](Tasks/4080.mdx)
- [Perform therapeutic procedures, such as injections, immunizations, suturing and wound care, and infection management.](Tasks/4079.mdx)
- [Visit and observe patients on hospital rounds or house calls, updating charts, ordering therapy, and reporting back to physician.](Tasks/4083.mdx)
- [Provide physicians with assistance during surgery or complicated medical procedures.](Tasks/4081.mdx)
