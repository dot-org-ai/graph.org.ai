# Merchandise Displayers and Window Trimmers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 27-1026.00

Plan and erect commercial displays, such as those in windows and interiors of retail stores and at trade exhibitions.

## Tasks

- [Plan commercial displays to entice and appeal to customers.](Tasks/9263.mdx)
- [Arrange properties, furniture, merchandise, backdrops, or other accessories, as shown in prepared sketches.](Tasks/9269.mdx)
- [Change or rotate window displays, interior display areas, or signage to reflect changes in inventory or promotion.](Tasks/9265.mdx)
- [Place prices or descriptive signs on backdrops, fixtures, merchandise, or floor.](Tasks/9264.mdx)
- [Consult with store managers, buyers, sales associates, housekeeping staff, or engineering staff to determine appropriate placement of displays or products.](Tasks/20752.mdx)
- [Maintain props, products, or mannequins, inspecting them for imperfections, doing touch-ups, cleaning up after customers, or applying preservative coatings as necessary.](Tasks/20753.mdx)
- [Develop ideas or plans for merchandise displays or window decorations.](Tasks/9267.mdx)
- [Assemble or set up displays, furniture, or products in store space, using colors, lights, pictures, or other accessories to display the product.](Tasks/20754.mdx)
- [Install booths, exhibits, displays, carpets, or drapes, as guided by floor plan of building or specifications.](Tasks/9281.mdx)
- [Select themes, lighting, colors, or props to be used.](Tasks/9275.mdx)
