# Gambling and Sports Book Writers and Runners

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 39-3012.00

Post information enabling patrons to wager on various races and sporting events. Assist in the operation of games such as keno and bingo. May operate random number-generating equipment and announce the numbers for patrons. Receive, verify, and record patrons' wagers. Scan and process winning tickets presented by patrons and pay out winnings for those wagers.

- [Collect bets in the form of cash or chips, verifying and recording amounts.](Tasks/8007.mdx)
- [Collect cards or tickets from players.](Tasks/8008.mdx)
- [Compute and verify amounts won or lost, paying out winnings or referring patrons to workers, such as gaming cashiers, so that winnings can be collected.](Tasks/20596.mdx)
- [Answer questions about game rules or casino policies.](Tasks/8009.mdx)
- [Conduct gambling tables or games, such as dice, roulette, cards, or keno, and ensure that game rules are followed.](Tasks/8012.mdx)

- [Compare the house hand with players' hands to determine the winner.](Tasks/8010.mdx)
- [Open or close cash floats or game tables.](Tasks/8017.mdx)
- [Pay off or move bets as established by game rules and procedures.](Tasks/8014.mdx)
