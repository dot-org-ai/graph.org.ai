# Parking Enforcement Workers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 33-3041.00

Patrol assigned area, such as public parking lot or city streets to issue tickets to overtime parking violators and illegally parked vehicles.

## Tasks

- [Enter and retrieve information pertaining to vehicle registration, identification, and status, using hand-held computers.](Tasks/7951.mdx)
- [Patrol an assigned area by vehicle or on foot to ensure public compliance with existing parking ordinance.](Tasks/7935.mdx)
- [Write warnings and citations for illegally parked vehicles.](Tasks/7937.mdx)
- [Appear in court at hearings regarding contested traffic citations.](Tasks/7947.mdx)
- [Maintain assigned equipment and supplies, such as hand-held citation computers, citation books, rain gear, tire-marking chalk, and street cones.](Tasks/7945.mdx)
- [Respond to and make radio dispatch calls regarding parking violations and complaints.](Tasks/7939.mdx)
- [Maintain close communications with dispatching personnel, using two-way radios or cell phones.](Tasks/7936.mdx)
- [Perform simple vehicle maintenance procedures, such as checking oil and gas, and report mechanical problems to supervisors.](Tasks/7942.mdx)
- [Observe and report hazardous conditions, such as missing traffic signals or signs, and street markings that need to be repainted.](Tasks/7943.mdx)
- [Identify vehicles in violation of parking codes, checking with dispatchers when necessary to confirm identities or to determine whether vehicles need to be booted or towed.](Tasks/7941.mdx)
