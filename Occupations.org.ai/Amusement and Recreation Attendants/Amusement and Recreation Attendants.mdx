# Amusement and Recreation Attendants

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 39-3091.00

Perform a variety of attending duties at amusement or recreation facility. May schedule use of recreation facilities, maintain and provide equipment to participants of sporting events or recreational pursuits, or operate amusement concessions and rides.

## Tasks

- [Sell tickets and collect fees from customers.](Tasks/5119.mdx)
- [Provide information about facilities, entertainment options, and rules and regulations.](Tasks/5116.mdx)
- [Keep informed of shut-down and emergency evacuation procedures.](Tasks/5120.mdx)
- [Direct patrons to rides, seats, or attractions.](Tasks/5130.mdx)
- [Monitor activities to ensure adherence to rules and safety procedures, or arrange for the removal of unruly patrons.](Tasks/5118.mdx)
- [Record details of attendance, sales, receipts, reservations, or repair activities.](Tasks/5117.mdx)
- [Maintain inventories of equipment, storing and retrieving items and assembling and disassembling equipment as necessary.](Tasks/5134.mdx)
- [Provide assistance to patrons entering or exiting amusement rides, boats, or ski lifts, or mounting or dismounting animals.](Tasks/5131.mdx)
- [Clean sporting equipment, vehicles, rides, booths, facilities, or grounds.](Tasks/5121.mdx)
- [Inspect equipment to detect wear and damage and perform minor repairs, adjustments, or maintenance tasks, such as oiling parts.](Tasks/5125.mdx)
