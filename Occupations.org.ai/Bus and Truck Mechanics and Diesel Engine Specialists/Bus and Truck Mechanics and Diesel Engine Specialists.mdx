# Bus and Truck Mechanics and Diesel Engine Specialists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 49-3031.00

Diagnose, adjust, repair, or overhaul buses and trucks, or maintain and repair any type of diesel engines. Includes mechanics working primarily with automobile or marine diesel engines.

## Tasks

- [Use handtools, such as screwdrivers, pliers, wrenches, pressure gauges, or precision instruments, as well as power tools, such as pneumatic wrenches, lathes, welding equipment, or jacks and hoists.](Tasks/2981.mdx)
- [Inspect brake systems, steering mechanisms, wheel bearings, and other important parts to ensure that they are in proper operating condition.](Tasks/2982.mdx)
- [Raise trucks, buses, and heavy parts or equipment using hydraulic jacks or hoists.](Tasks/2985.mdx)
- [Adjust and reline brakes, align wheels, tighten bolts and screws, and reassemble equipment.](Tasks/2984.mdx)
- [Attach test instruments to equipment, and read dials and gauges to diagnose malfunctions.](Tasks/2991.mdx)
- [Perform routine maintenance such as changing oil, checking batteries, and lubricating equipment and machinery.](Tasks/2983.mdx)
- [Examine and adjust protective guards, loose bolts, and specified safety devices.](Tasks/2988.mdx)
- [Inspect, test, and listen to defective equipment to diagnose malfunctions, using test instruments such as handheld computers, motor analyzers, chassis charts, or pressure gauges.](Tasks/2987.mdx)
- [Rewire ignition systems, lights, and instrument panels.](Tasks/2992.mdx)
- [Test drive trucks and buses to diagnose malfunctions or to ensure that they are working properly.](Tasks/2986.mdx)
