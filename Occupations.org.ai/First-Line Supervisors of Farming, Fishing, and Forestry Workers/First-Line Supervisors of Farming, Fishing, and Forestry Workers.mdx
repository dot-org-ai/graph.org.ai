# First-Line Supervisors of Farming, Fishing, and Forestry Workers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 45-1011.00

Directly supervise and coordinate the activities of agricultural, forestry, aquacultural, and related workers.

## Tasks

- [Assign tasks such as feeding and treatment of animals, and cleaning and maintenance of animal quarters.](Tasks/23340.mdx)
- [Record the numbers and types of fish or shellfish reared, harvested, released, sold, and shipped.](Tasks/23341.mdx)
- [Monitor workers to ensure that safety regulations are followed, warning or disciplining those who violate safety regulations.](Tasks/23342.mdx)
- [Observe animals for signs of illness, injury, or unusual behavior, notifying veterinarians or managers as warranted.](Tasks/23343.mdx)
- [Observe fish and beds or ponds to detect diseases, monitor fish growth, determine quality of fish, or determine completeness of harvesting.](Tasks/23344.mdx)
- [Train workers in tree felling or bucking, operation of tractors or loading machines, yarding or loading techniques, or safety regulations.](Tasks/23345.mdx)
- [Treat animal illnesses or injuries, following experience or instructions of veterinarians.](Tasks/23346.mdx)
- [Train workers in spawning, rearing, cultivating, and harvesting methods, and in the use of equipment.](Tasks/23347.mdx)
- [Train workers in techniques such as planting, harvesting, weeding, or insect identification and in the use of safety measures.](Tasks/23348.mdx)
- [Confer with managers to evaluate weather or soil conditions, to develop plans or procedures, or to discuss issues such as changes in fertilizers, herbicides, or cultivating techniques.](Tasks/23349.mdx)
