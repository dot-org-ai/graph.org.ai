# Chemical Equipment Operators and Tenders

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-9011.00

Operate or tend equipment to control chemical changes or reactions in the processing of industrial or consumer products. Equipment used includes devulcanizers, steam-jacketed kettles, and reactor vessels.

## Tasks

- [Observe safety precautions to prevent fires or explosions.](Tasks/12334.mdx)
- [Record operational data, such as temperatures, pressures, ingredients used, processing times, or test results.](Tasks/12342.mdx)
- [Control or operate equipment in which chemical changes or reactions take place during the processing of industrial or consumer products.](Tasks/12336.mdx)
- [Patrol work areas to detect leaks or equipment malfunctions or to monitor operating conditions.](Tasks/12339.mdx)
- [Draw samples of products at specified stages so that analyses can be performed.](Tasks/12341.mdx)
- [Adjust controls to regulate temperature, pressure, feed, or flow of liquids or gases and times of prescribed reactions, according to knowledge of equipment and processes.](Tasks/12333.mdx)
- [Monitor gauges, recording instruments, flowmeters, or products to ensure that specified conditions are maintained.](Tasks/12335.mdx)
- [Test product samples for specific gravity, chemical characteristics, pH levels, concentrations, or viscosities, or send them to laboratories for testing.](Tasks/12340.mdx)
- [Inspect equipment or units to detect leaks or malfunctions, shutting equipment down, if necessary.](Tasks/12338.mdx)
- [Open valves or start pumps, agitators, reactors, blowers, or automatic feed of materials.](Tasks/12345.mdx)
