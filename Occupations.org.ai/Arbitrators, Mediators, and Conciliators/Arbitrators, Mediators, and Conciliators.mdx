# Arbitrators, Mediators, and Conciliators

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 23-1022.00

Facilitate negotiation and conflict resolution through dialogue. Resolve conflicts outside of the court system by mutual consent of parties involved.

## Tasks

- [Prepare written opinions or decisions regarding cases.](Tasks/12999.mdx)
- [Apply relevant laws, regulations, policies, or precedents to reach conclusions.](Tasks/20238.mdx)
- [Conduct hearings to obtain information or evidence relative to disposition of claims.](Tasks/20239.mdx)
- [Determine extent of liability according to evidence, laws, or administrative or judicial precedents.](Tasks/20240.mdx)
- [Rule on exceptions, motions, or admissibility of evidence.](Tasks/13001.mdx)
- [Confer with disputants to clarify issues, identify underlying concerns, and develop an understanding of their respective needs and interests.](Tasks/12993.mdx)
- [Use mediation techniques to facilitate communication between disputants, to further parties' understanding of different perspectives, and to guide parties toward mutual agreement.](Tasks/12994.mdx)

- [Evaluate information from documents, such as claim applications, birth or death certificates, or physician or employer records.](Tasks/13003.mdx)
