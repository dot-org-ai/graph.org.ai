# Electrical and Electronic Equipment Assemblers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-2022.00

Assemble or modify electrical or electronic equipment, such as computers, test equipment telemetering systems, electric motors, and batteries.

## Tasks

- [Read and interpret schematic drawings, diagrams, blueprints, specifications, work orders, or reports to determine materials requirements or assembly instructions.](Tasks/10022.mdx)
- [Assemble electrical or electronic systems or support structures and install components, units, subassemblies, wiring, or assembly casings, using rivets, bolts, soldering or micro-welding equipment.](Tasks/10019.mdx)
- [Adjust, repair, or replace electrical or electronic components to correct defects and to ensure conformance to specifications.](Tasks/10020.mdx)
- [Position, align, or adjust workpieces or electrical parts to facilitate wiring or assembly.](Tasks/10024.mdx)
- [Explain assembly procedures or techniques to other workers.](Tasks/10027.mdx)
- [Clean parts, using cleaning solutions, air hoses, and cloths.](Tasks/10021.mdx)
- [Drill or tap holes in specified equipment locations to mount control units or to provide openings for elements, wiring, or instruments.](Tasks/10030.mdx)
- [Fabricate or form parts, coils, or structures according to specifications, using drills, calipers, cutters, or saws.](Tasks/10029.mdx)
- [Confer with supervisors or engineers to plan or review work activities or to resolve production problems.](Tasks/10026.mdx)
- [Inspect or test wiring installations, assemblies, or circuits for resistance factors or for operation, and record results.](Tasks/10018.mdx)
