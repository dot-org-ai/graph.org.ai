# Fabric and Apparel Patternmakers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-6092.00

Draw and construct sets of precision master fabric patterns or layouts. May also mark and cut fabrics and apparel.

## Tasks

- [Create a master pattern for each size within a range of garment sizes, using charts, drafting instruments, computers, or grading devices.](Tasks/12200.mdx)
- [Input specifications into computers to assist with pattern design and pattern cutting.](Tasks/20930.mdx)
- [Draw details on outlined parts to indicate where parts are to be joined, as well as the positions of pleats, pockets, buttonholes, and other features, using computers or drafting instruments.](Tasks/12198.mdx)
- [Make adjustments to patterns after fittings.](Tasks/20931.mdx)
- [Compute dimensions of patterns according to sizes, considering stretching of material.](Tasks/12206.mdx)
- [Mark samples and finished patterns with information, such as garment size, section, style, identification, and sewing instructions.](Tasks/12205.mdx)
- [Draw outlines of pattern parts by adapting or copying existing patterns, or by drafting new patterns.](Tasks/12201.mdx)
- [Test patterns by making and fitting sample garments.](Tasks/12197.mdx)
- [Position and cut out master or sample patterns, using scissors and knives, or print out copies of patterns, using computers.](Tasks/12203.mdx)
- [Create a paper pattern from which to mass-produce a design concept.](Tasks/12202.mdx)
