# Pipelayers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-2151.00

Lay pipe for storm or sanitation sewers, drains, and water mains. Perform any combination of the following tasks: grade trenches or culverts, position pipe, or seal joints.

## Tasks

- [Grade or level trench bases, using tamping machines or hand tools.](Tasks/11536.mdx)
- [Dig trenches to desired or required depths, by hand or using trenching tools.](Tasks/11539.mdx)
- [Cut pipes to required lengths.](Tasks/11533.mdx)
- [Install or use instruments such as lasers, grade rods, or transit levels.](Tasks/11535.mdx)
- [Cover pipes with earth or other materials.](Tasks/11531.mdx)
- [Connect pipe pieces and seal joints, using welding equipment, cement, or glue.](Tasks/11532.mdx)
- [Install or repair sanitary or stormwater sewer structures or pipe systems.](Tasks/11534.mdx)
- [Check slopes for conformance to requirements, using levels or lasers.](Tasks/11530.mdx)
- [Align and position pipes to prepare them for welding or sealing.](Tasks/11538.mdx)
- [Lay out pipe routes, following written instructions or blueprints and coordinating layouts with supervisors.](Tasks/11537.mdx)
