# Anesthesiologist Assistants

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 29-1071.01

Assist anesthesiologists in the administration of anesthesia for surgical and non-surgical procedures. Monitor patient status and provide patient care during surgical treatment.

## Tasks

- [Provide airway management interventions including tracheal intubation, fiber optics, or ventilary support.](Tasks/17301.mdx)
- [Respond to emergency situations by providing cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR), basic cardiac life support (BLS), advanced cardiac life support (ACLS), or pediatric advanced life support (PALS).](Tasks/17302.mdx)
- [Verify availability of operating room supplies, medications, and gases.](Tasks/17296.mdx)
- [Pretest and calibrate anesthesia delivery systems and monitors.](Tasks/17304.mdx)
- [Participate in seminars, workshops, or other professional activities to keep abreast of developments in anesthesiology.](Tasks/17299.mdx)
- [Control anesthesia levels during procedures.](Tasks/17310.mdx)
- [Assist anesthesiologists in monitoring of patients, including electrocardiogram (EKG), direct arterial pressure, central venous pressure, arterial blood gas, hematocrit, or routine measurement of temperature, respiration, blood pressure or heart rate.](Tasks/17305.mdx)
- [Administer blood, blood products, or supportive fluids.](Tasks/17309.mdx)
- [Collect and document patients' pre-anesthetic health histories.](Tasks/17300.mdx)
- [Assist in the provision of advanced life support techniques including those procedures using high frequency ventilation or intra-arterial cardiovascular assistance devices.](Tasks/17306.mdx)
