# Control and Valve Installers and Repairers, Except Mechanical Door

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 49-9012.00

Install, repair, and maintain mechanical regulating and controlling devices, such as electric meters, gas regulators, thermostats, safety and flow valves, and other mechanical governors.

## Tasks

- [Record maintenance information, including test results, material usage, and repairs made.](Tasks/11780.mdx)
- [Disassemble and repair mechanical control devices or valves, such as regulators, thermostats, or hydrants, using power tools, hand tools, and cutting torches.](Tasks/11779.mdx)
- [Lubricate wearing surfaces of mechanical parts, using oils or other lubricants.](Tasks/11782.mdx)
- [Calibrate instrumentation, such as meters, gauges, and regulators, for pressure, temperature, flow, and level.](Tasks/21024.mdx)
- [Install, inspect and test electric meters, relays, and power sources to detect causes of malfunctions and inaccuracies, using hand tools and testing equipment.](Tasks/11794.mdx)
- [Test valves and regulators for leaks and accurate temperature and pressure settings, using precision testing equipment.](Tasks/11783.mdx)
- [Record meter readings and installation data on meter cards, work orders, or field service orders, or enter data into hand-held computers.](Tasks/11777.mdx)
- [Turn meters on or off to establish or close service.](Tasks/11774.mdx)
- [Shut off service and notify repair crews when major repairs are required, such as the replacement of underground pipes or wiring.](Tasks/11785.mdx)
- [Install regulators and related equipment such as gas meters, odorization units, and gas pressure telemetering equipment.](Tasks/11784.mdx)
