# Education and Childcare Administrators, Preschool and Daycare

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 11-9031.00

Plan, direct, or coordinate academic or nonacademic activities of preschools or childcare centers and programs, including before- and after-school care.

- [Confer with parents and staff to discuss educational activities and policies and students' behavioral or learning problems.](Tasks/5193.mdx)
- [Monitor students' progress and provide students and teachers with assistance in resolving any problems.](Tasks/5196.mdx)
