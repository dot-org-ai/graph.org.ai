# Timing Device Assemblers and Adjusters

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-2061.00

Perform precision assembling or adjusting, within narrow tolerances, of timing devices such as digital clocks or timing devices with electrical or electronic components.

## Tasks

- [Assemble and install components of timepieces to complete mechanisms, using watchmakers' tools and loupes.](Tasks/15003.mdx)
- [Observe operation of timepiece parts and subassemblies to determine accuracy of movement, and to diagnose causes of defects.](Tasks/15004.mdx)
- [Test operation and fit of timepiece parts and subassemblies, using electronic testing equipment, tweezers, watchmakers' tools, and loupes.](Tasks/15005.mdx)
- [Replace specified parts to repair malfunctioning timepieces, using watchmakers' tools, loupes, and holding fixtures.](Tasks/15006.mdx)
- [Disassemble timepieces such as watches, clocks, and chronometers so that repairs can be made.](Tasks/15007.mdx)
- [Clean and lubricate timepiece parts and assemblies, using solvents, buff sticks, and oil.](Tasks/15008.mdx)
- [Examine components of timepieces such as watches, clocks, or chronometers for defects, using loupes or microscopes.](Tasks/15009.mdx)
- [Bend parts, such as hairsprings, pallets, barrel covers, and bridges, to correct deficiencies in truing or endshake, using tweezers.](Tasks/15010.mdx)
- [Change timing weights on balance wheels to correct deficient timing.](Tasks/15011.mdx)
- [Adjust sizes or positioning of timepiece parts to achieve specified fit or function, using calipers, fixtures, and loupes.](Tasks/15012.mdx)
