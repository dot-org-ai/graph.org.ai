# Legal Secretaries and Administrative Assistants

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 43-6012.00

Perform secretarial duties using legal terminology, procedures, and documents. Prepare legal papers and correspondence, such as summonses, complaints, motions, and subpoenas. May also assist with legal research.

## Tasks

- [Organize and maintain law libraries, documents, and case files.](Tasks/768.mdx)
- [Mail, fax, or arrange for delivery of legal correspondence to clients, witnesses, and court officials.](Tasks/764.mdx)
- [Prepare and distribute invoices to bill clients or pay account expenses.](Tasks/18780.mdx)
- [Prepare, proofread, or process legal documents, such as summonses, subpoenas, complaints, appeals, motions, or pretrial agreements.](Tasks/21078.mdx)
- [Make photocopies of correspondence, documents, and other printed matter.](Tasks/767.mdx)
- [Assist attorneys in collecting information such as employment, medical, and other records.](Tasks/769.mdx)
- [Complete various forms, such as accident reports, trial and courtroom requests, and applications for clients.](Tasks/774.mdx)
- [Receive and place telephone calls.](Tasks/765.mdx)
- [Schedule and make appointments.](Tasks/766.mdx)
- [Draft and type office memos.](Tasks/771.mdx)
