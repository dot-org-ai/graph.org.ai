# Subway and Streetcar Operators

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 53-4041.00

Operate subway or elevated suburban trains with no separate locomotive, or electric-powered streetcar, to transport passengers. May handle fares.

## Tasks

- [Monitor lights indicating obstructions or other trains ahead and watch for car and truck traffic at crossings to stay alert to potential hazards.](Tasks/12767.mdx)
- [Operate controls to open and close transit vehicle doors.](Tasks/12765.mdx)
- [Drive and control rail-guided public transportation, such as subways, elevated trains, and electric-powered streetcars, trams, or trolleys, to transport passengers.](Tasks/12766.mdx)
- [Report delays, mechanical problems, and emergencies to supervisors or dispatchers, using radios.](Tasks/12770.mdx)
- [Regulate vehicle speed and the time spent at each stop to maintain schedules.](Tasks/12769.mdx)
- [Make announcements to passengers, such as notifications of upcoming stops or schedule delays.](Tasks/12771.mdx)
- [Direct emergency evacuation procedures.](Tasks/12768.mdx)
- [Complete reports, including shift summaries and incident or accident reports.](Tasks/12772.mdx)
- [Greet passengers, provide information, and answer questions concerning fares, schedules, transfers, and routings.](Tasks/12773.mdx)
- [Attend meetings on driver and passenger safety to learn ways in which job performance might be affected.](Tasks/12774.mdx)
