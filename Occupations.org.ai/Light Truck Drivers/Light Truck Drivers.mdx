# Light Truck Drivers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 53-3033.00

Drive a light vehicle, such as a truck or van, with a capacity of less than 26,001 pounds Gross Vehicle Weight (GVW), primarily to pick up merchandise or packages from a distribution center and deliver. May load and unload vehicle.

## Tasks

- [Obey traffic laws and follow established traffic and transportation procedures.](Tasks/8615.mdx)
- [Report any mechanical problems encountered with vehicles.](Tasks/8617.mdx)
- [Verify the contents of inventory loads against shipping papers.](Tasks/8620.mdx)
- [Inspect and maintain vehicle supplies and equipment, such as gas, oil, water, tires, lights, or brakes, to ensure that vehicles are in proper working condition.](Tasks/8616.mdx)
- [Read maps and follow written or verbal geographic directions.](Tasks/8623.mdx)
- [Load and unload trucks, vans, or automobiles.](Tasks/8619.mdx)
- [Present bills and receipts and collect payments for goods delivered or loaded.](Tasks/8618.mdx)
- [Maintain records, such as vehicle logs, records of cargo, or billing statements, in accordance with regulations.](Tasks/8622.mdx)
- [Drive vehicles with capacities under three tons to transport materials to and from specified destinations, such as railroad stations, plants, residences, offices, or within industrial yards.](Tasks/8626.mdx)
- [Turn in receipts and money received from deliveries.](Tasks/8621.mdx)
