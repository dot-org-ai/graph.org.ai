# Compliance Managers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 11-9199.02

Plan, direct, or coordinate activities of an organization to ensure compliance with ethical or regulatory standards.

## Tasks

- [Report violations of compliance or regulatory standards to duly authorized enforcement agencies as appropriate or required.](Tasks/15645.mdx)
- [Identify compliance issues that require follow-up or investigation.](Tasks/15650.mdx)
- [Discuss emerging compliance issues to ensure that management and employees are informed about compliance reporting systems, policies, and practices.](Tasks/21088.mdx)
- [File appropriate compliance reports with regulatory agencies.](Tasks/15653.mdx)
- [Maintain documentation of compliance activities, such as complaints received or investigation outcomes.](Tasks/15638.mdx)
- [Consult with corporate attorneys as necessary to address difficult legal compliance issues.](Tasks/15639.mdx)
- [Conduct or direct the internal investigation of compliance issues.](Tasks/15656.mdx)
- [Provide employee training on compliance related topics, policies, or procedures.](Tasks/15644.mdx)
- [Serve as a confidential point of contact for employees to communicate with management, seek clarification on issues or dilemmas, or report irregularities.](Tasks/15637.mdx)
