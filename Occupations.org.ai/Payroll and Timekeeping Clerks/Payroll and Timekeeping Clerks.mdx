# Payroll and Timekeeping Clerks

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 43-3051.00

Compile and record employee time and payroll data. May compute employees' time worked, production, and commission. May compute and post wages and deductions, or prepare paychecks.

## Tasks

- [Verify attendance, hours worked, and pay adjustments, and post information onto designated records.](Tasks/2519.mdx)
- [Process and issue employee paychecks and statements of earnings and deductions.](Tasks/2515.mdx)
- [Compute wages and deductions, and enter data into computers.](Tasks/2516.mdx)
- [Process paperwork for new employees and enter employee information into the payroll system.](Tasks/18562.mdx)
- [Prepare and balance period-end reports, and reconcile issued payrolls to bank statements.](Tasks/2528.mdx)
- [Review time sheets, work charts, wage computation, and other information to detect and reconcile payroll discrepancies.](Tasks/2518.mdx)
- [Distribute and collect timecards each pay period.](Tasks/2526.mdx)
- [Record employee information, such as exemptions, transfers, and resignations, to maintain and update payroll records.](Tasks/2520.mdx)
- [Issue and record adjustments to pay related to previous errors or retroactive increases.](Tasks/2521.mdx)
- [Keep track of leave time, such as vacation, personal, and sick leave, for employees.](Tasks/18563.mdx)
