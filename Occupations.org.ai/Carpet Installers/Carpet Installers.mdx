# Carpet Installers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-2041.00

Lay and install carpet from rolls or blocks on floors. Install padding and trim flooring materials.

## Tasks

- [Inspect the surface to be covered to determine its condition, and correct any imperfections that might show through carpet or cause carpet to wear unevenly.](Tasks/2835.mdx)
- [Roll out, measure, mark, and cut carpeting to size with a carpet knife, following floor sketches and allowing extra carpet for final fitting.](Tasks/2836.mdx)
- [Join edges of carpet and seam edges where necessary, by sewing or by using tape with glue and heated carpet iron.](Tasks/2833.mdx)
- [Cut and trim carpet to fit along wall edges, openings, and projections, finishing the edges with a wall trimmer.](Tasks/2834.mdx)
- [Plan the layout of the carpet, allowing for expected traffic patterns and placing seams for best appearance and longest wear.](Tasks/2837.mdx)
- [Stretch carpet to align with walls and ensure a smooth surface, and press carpet in place over tack strips or use staples, tape, tacks or glue to hold carpet in place.](Tasks/2838.mdx)
- [Take measurements and study floor sketches to calculate the area to be carpeted and the amount of material needed.](Tasks/2839.mdx)
- [Install carpet on some floors using adhesive, following prescribed method.](Tasks/2841.mdx)
- [Clean up before and after installation, including vacuuming carpet and discarding remnant pieces.](Tasks/20142.mdx)
- [Measure, cut and install tackless strips along the baseboard or wall.](Tasks/2844.mdx)
