# Eligibility Interviewers, Government Programs

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 43-4061.00

Determine eligibility of persons applying to receive assistance from government programs and agency resources, such as welfare, unemployment benefits, social security, and public housing.

## Tasks

- [Compute and authorize amounts of assistance for programs, such as grants, monetary payments, and food stamps.](Tasks/9744.mdx)
- [Keep records of assigned cases, and prepare required reports.](Tasks/9739.mdx)
- [Compile, record, and evaluate personal and financial data to verify completeness and accuracy, and to determine eligibility status.](Tasks/9736.mdx)
- [Interview and investigate applicants for public assistance to gather information pertinent to their applications.](Tasks/9737.mdx)
- [Interview benefits recipients at specified intervals to certify their eligibility for continuing benefits.](Tasks/9733.mdx)
- [Interpret and explain information such as eligibility requirements, application details, payment methods, and applicants' legal rights.](Tasks/9734.mdx)
- [Initiate procedures to grant, modify, deny, or terminate assistance, or refer applicants to other agencies for assistance.](Tasks/9735.mdx)
- [Check with employers or other references to verify answers and obtain further information.](Tasks/9738.mdx)
- [Answer applicants' questions about benefits and claim procedures.](Tasks/9732.mdx)
- [Provide social workers with pertinent information gathered during applicant interviews.](Tasks/9743.mdx)
