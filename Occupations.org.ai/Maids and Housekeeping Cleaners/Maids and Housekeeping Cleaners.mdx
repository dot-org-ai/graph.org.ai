# Maids and Housekeeping Cleaners

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 37-2012.00

Perform any combination of light cleaning duties to maintain private households or commercial establishments, such as hotels and hospitals, in a clean and orderly manner. Duties may include making beds, replenishing linens, cleaning rooms and halls, and vacuuming.

## Tasks

- [Keep storage areas and carts well-stocked, clean, and tidy.](Tasks/9564.mdx)
- [Carry linens, towels, toilet items, and cleaning supplies, using wheeled carts.](Tasks/9560.mdx)
- [Clean rooms, hallways, lobbies, lounges, restrooms, corridors, elevators, stairways, locker rooms, and other work areas so that health standards are met.](Tasks/9561.mdx)
- [Empty wastebaskets, empty and clean ashtrays, and transport other trash and waste to disposal areas.](Tasks/9562.mdx)
- [Sweep, scrub, wax, or polish floors, using brooms, mops, or powered scrubbing and waxing machines.](Tasks/9566.mdx)
- [Replenish supplies, such as drinking glasses, linens, writing supplies, and bathroom items.](Tasks/9563.mdx)
- [Clean rugs, carpets, upholstered furniture, and draperies, using vacuum cleaners and shampooers.](Tasks/9567.mdx)
- [Wash windows, walls, ceilings, and woodwork, waxing and polishing as necessary.](Tasks/9568.mdx)
- [Dust and polish furniture and equipment.](Tasks/9565.mdx)
- [Disinfect equipment and supplies, using germicides or steam-operated sterilizers.](Tasks/9570.mdx)
