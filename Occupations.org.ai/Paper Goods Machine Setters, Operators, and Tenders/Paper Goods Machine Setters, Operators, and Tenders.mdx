# Paper Goods Machine Setters, Operators, and Tenders

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-9196.00

Set up, operate, or tend paper goods machines that perform a variety of functions, such as converting, sawing, corrugating, banding, wrapping, boxing, stitching, forming, or sealing paper or paperboard sheets into products.

## Tasks

- [Examine completed work to detect defects and verify conformance to work orders, and adjust machinery as necessary to correct production problems.](Tasks/12678.mdx)
- [Observe operation of various machines to detect and correct machine malfunctions such as improper forming, glue flow, or pasteboard tension.](Tasks/12683.mdx)
- [Start machines and move controls to regulate tension on pressure rolls, to synchronize speed of machine components, and to adjust temperatures of glue or paraffin.](Tasks/12679.mdx)
- [Disassemble machines to maintain, repair, or replace broken or worn parts, using hand or power tools.](Tasks/12689.mdx)
- [Install attachments to machines for gluing, folding, printing, or cutting.](Tasks/12681.mdx)
- [Cut products to specified dimensions, using hand or power cutters.](Tasks/12687.mdx)
- [Place rolls of paper or cardboard on machine feed tracks, and thread paper through gluing, coating, and slitting rollers.](Tasks/12685.mdx)
- [Monitor finished cartons as they drop from forming machines into rotating hoppers and into gravity feed chutes to prevent jamming.](Tasks/12688.mdx)
- [Adjust guide assemblies, forming bars, and folding mechanisms according to specifications, using hand tools.](Tasks/12680.mdx)
- [Measure, space, and set saw blades, cutters, and perforators, according to product specifications.](Tasks/12682.mdx)
