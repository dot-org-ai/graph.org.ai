# Power Plant Operators

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-8013.00

Control, operate, or maintain machinery to generate electric power. Includes auxiliary equipment operators.

## Tasks

- [Control generator output to match the phase, frequency, or voltage of electricity supplied to panels.](Tasks/12328.mdx)
- [Take regulatory action, based on readings from charts, meters and gauges, at established intervals.](Tasks/12318.mdx)
- [Control power generating equipment, including boilers, turbines, generators, or reactors, using control boards or semi-automatic equipment.](Tasks/12316.mdx)
- [Start or stop generators, auxiliary pumping equipment, turbines, or other power plant equipment as necessary.](Tasks/12319.mdx)
- [Monitor power plant equipment and indicators to detect evidence of operating problems.](Tasks/12314.mdx)
- [Operate or maintain distributed power generation equipment, including fuel cells or microturbines, to produce energy on-site for manufacturing or other commercial purposes.](Tasks/19914.mdx)
- [Open and close valves and switches in sequence to start or shut down auxiliary units.](Tasks/12325.mdx)
- [Control or maintain auxiliary equipment, such as pumps, fans, compressors, condensers, feedwater heaters, filters, or chlorinators, to supply water, fuel, lubricants, air, or auxiliary power.](Tasks/12321.mdx)
- [Regulate equipment operations and conditions, such as water levels, based on instrument data or from computers.](Tasks/12317.mdx)
- [Inspect records or log book entries or communicate with plant personnel to assess equipment operating status.](Tasks/12320.mdx)
