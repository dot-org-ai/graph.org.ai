# First-Line Supervisors of Mechanics, Installers, and Repairers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 49-1011.00

Directly supervise and coordinate the activities of mechanics, installers, and repairers. May also advise customers on recommended services. Excludes team or work leaders.

## Tasks

- [Inspect, test, and measure completed work, using devices such as hand tools or gauges to verify conformance to standards or repair requirements.](Tasks/2929.mdx)
- [Inspect and monitor work areas, examine tools and equipment, and provide employee safety training to prevent, detect, and correct unsafe conditions or violations of procedures and safety rules.](Tasks/15263.mdx)
- [Interpret specifications, blueprints, or job orders to construct templates and lay out reference points for workers.](Tasks/2920.mdx)
- [Monitor employees' work levels and review work performance.](Tasks/2914.mdx)
- [Perform skilled repair or maintenance operations, using equipment such as hand or power tools, hydraulic presses or shears, or welding equipment.](Tasks/2925.mdx)
- [Compute estimates and actual costs of factors such as materials, labor, or outside contractors.](Tasks/2919.mdx)
- [Monitor tool and part inventories and the condition and maintenance of shops to ensure adequate working conditions.](Tasks/15262.mdx)
- [Requisition materials and supplies, such as tools, equipment, or replacement parts.](Tasks/2918.mdx)
- [Confer with personnel, such as management, engineering, quality control, customer, or union workers' representatives, to coordinate work activities, resolve employee grievances, or identify and review resource needs.](Tasks/2923.mdx)
