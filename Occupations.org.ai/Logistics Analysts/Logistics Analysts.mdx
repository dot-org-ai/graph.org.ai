# Logistics Analysts

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 13-1081.02

Analyze product delivery or supply chain processes to identify or recommend changes. May manage route activity including invoicing, electronic bills, and shipment tracing.

## Tasks

- [Maintain databases of logistics information.](Tasks/15898.mdx)
- [Remotely monitor the flow of vehicles or inventory, using Web-based logistics information systems to track vehicles or containers.](Tasks/20009.mdx)
- [Communicate with or monitor service providers, such as ocean carriers, air freight forwarders, global consolidators, customs brokers, or trucking companies.](Tasks/15889.mdx)
- [Track product flow from origin to final delivery.](Tasks/15890.mdx)
- [Interpret data on logistics elements, such as availability, maintainability, reliability, supply chain management, strategic sourcing or distribution, supplier management, or transportation.](Tasks/15903.mdx)
- [Recommend improvements to existing or planned logistics processes.](Tasks/15893.mdx)
- [Apply analytic methods or tools to understand, predict, or control logistics operations or processes.](Tasks/15904.mdx)
- [Prepare reports on logistics performance measures.](Tasks/15895.mdx)
- [Enter logistics-related data into databases.](Tasks/15883.mdx)
- [Provide ongoing analyses in areas such as transportation costs, parts procurement, back orders, or delivery processes.](Tasks/15894.mdx)
