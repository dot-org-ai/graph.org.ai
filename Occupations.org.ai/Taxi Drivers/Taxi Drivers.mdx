# Taxi Drivers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 53-3054.00

Drive a motor vehicle to transport passengers on an unplanned basis and charge a fare, usually based on a meter.

## Tasks

- [Collect fares or vouchers from passengers, and make change or issue receipts as necessary.](Tasks/23758.mdx)
- [Communicate with dispatchers by radio, telephone, or computer to exchange information and receive requests for passenger service.](Tasks/23759.mdx)
- [Complete accident reports when necessary.](Tasks/23760.mdx)
- [Determine fares based on trip distances and times, using taximeters and fee schedules, and announce fares to passengers.](Tasks/23761.mdx)
- [Drive taxicabs or privately owned vehicles to transport passengers.](Tasks/23762.mdx)
- [Follow relevant safety regulations and state laws governing vehicle operation, and ensure that passengers follow safety regulations.](Tasks/23763.mdx)
- [Notify dispatchers or company mechanics of vehicle problems.](Tasks/23764.mdx)
- [Perform minor vehicle repairs, such as cleaning spark plugs, or take vehicles to mechanics for servicing.](Tasks/23765.mdx)
- [Perform routine vehicle maintenance, such as regulating tire pressure and adding gasoline, oil, and water.](Tasks/23766.mdx)
- [Pick up passengers at prearranged locations, at taxi stands, or by cruising streets in high-traffic areas.](Tasks/23767.mdx)
