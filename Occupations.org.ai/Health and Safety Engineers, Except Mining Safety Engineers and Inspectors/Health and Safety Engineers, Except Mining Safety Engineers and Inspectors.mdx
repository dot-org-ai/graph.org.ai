# Health and Safety Engineers, Except Mining Safety Engineers and Inspectors

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 17-2111.00

Promote worksite or product safety by applying knowledge of industrial processes, mechanics, chemistry, psychology, and industrial health and safety laws. Includes industrial product safety engineers.

## Tasks

- [Investigate industrial accidents, injuries, or occupational diseases to determine causes and preventive measures.](Tasks/21860.mdx)
- [Conduct research to evaluate safety levels for products.](Tasks/21861.mdx)
- [Evaluate product designs for safety.](Tasks/21862.mdx)
- [Conduct or coordinate worker training in areas such as safety laws and regulations, hazardous condition monitoring, and use of safety equipment.](Tasks/21863.mdx)
- [Maintain and apply knowledge of current policies, regulations, and industrial processes.](Tasks/21864.mdx)
- [Recommend procedures for detection, prevention, and elimination of physical, chemical, or other product hazards.](Tasks/21865.mdx)
- [Report or review findings from accident investigations, facilities inspections, or environmental testing.](Tasks/21866.mdx)
- [Evaluate potential health hazards or damage that could occur from product misuse.](Tasks/21867.mdx)
- [Evaluate adequacy of actions taken to correct health inspection violations.](Tasks/21868.mdx)
- [Interpret safety regulations for others interested in industrial safety, such as safety engineers, labor representatives, and safety inspectors.](Tasks/21869.mdx)
