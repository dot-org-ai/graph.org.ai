# Talent Directors

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 27-2012.04

Audition and interview performers to select most appropriate talent for parts in stage, television, radio, or motion picture productions.

## Tasks

- [Audition and interview performers to match their attributes to specific roles or to increase the pool of available acting talent.](Tasks/11018.mdx)
- [Prepare actors for auditions by providing scripts and information about roles and casting requirements.](Tasks/11020.mdx)
- [Select performers for roles or submit lists of suitable performers to producers or directors for final selection.](Tasks/11017.mdx)
- [Contact agents and actors to provide notification of audition and performance opportunities and to set up audition times.](Tasks/11024.mdx)
- [Serve as liaisons between directors, actors, and agents.](Tasks/11021.mdx)
- [Negotiate contract agreements with performers, with agents, or between performers and agents or production companies.](Tasks/11023.mdx)
- [Arrange for or design screen tests or auditions for prospective performers.](Tasks/11026.mdx)
- [Review performer information, such as photos, resumes, voice tapes, videos, and union membership, to decide whom to audition for parts.](Tasks/11015.mdx)
- [Maintain talent files that include information such as performers' specialties, past performances, and availability.](Tasks/11019.mdx)
- [Read scripts and confer with producers to determine the types and numbers of performers required for a given production.](Tasks/11016.mdx)
