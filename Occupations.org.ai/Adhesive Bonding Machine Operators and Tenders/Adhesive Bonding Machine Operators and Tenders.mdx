# Adhesive Bonding Machine Operators and Tenders

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-9191.00

Operate or tend bonding machines that use adhesives to join items for further processing or to form a completed product. Processes include joining veneer sheets into plywood; gluing paper; or joining rubber and rubberized fabric parts, plastic, simulated leather, or other materials.

## Tasks

- [Align and position materials being joined to ensure accurate application of adhesive or heat sealing.](Tasks/12607.mdx)
- [Adjust machine components according to specifications such as widths, lengths, and thickness of materials and amounts of glue, cement, or adhesive required.](Tasks/12598.mdx)
- [Monitor machine operations to detect malfunctions and report or resolve problems.](Tasks/12599.mdx)
- [Start machines, and turn valves or move controls to feed, admit, apply, or transfer materials and adhesives, and to adjust temperature, pressure, and time settings.](Tasks/12604.mdx)
- [Fill machines with glue, cement, or adhesives.](Tasks/12601.mdx)
- [Perform test production runs and make adjustments as necessary to ensure that completed products meet standards and specifications.](Tasks/12605.mdx)
- [Examine and measure completed materials or products to verify conformance to specifications, using measuring devices such as tape measures, gauges, or calipers.](Tasks/12597.mdx)
- [Read work orders and communicate with coworkers to determine machine and equipment settings and adjustments and supply and product specifications.](Tasks/12600.mdx)
- [Remove and stack completed materials or products, and restock materials to be joined.](Tasks/12609.mdx)
- [Observe gauges, meters, and control panels to obtain information about equipment temperatures and pressures, or the speed of feeders or conveyors.](Tasks/12608.mdx)
