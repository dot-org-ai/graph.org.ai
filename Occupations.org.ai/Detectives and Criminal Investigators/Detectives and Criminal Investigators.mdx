# Detectives and Criminal Investigators

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 33-3021.00

Conduct investigations related to suspected violations of federal, state, or local laws to prevent or solve crimes.

## Tasks

- [Check victims for signs of life, such as breathing and pulse.](Tasks/23021.mdx)
- [Obtain facts or statements from complainants, witnesses, and accused persons and record interviews, using recording device.](Tasks/23022.mdx)
- [Secure deceased body and obtain evidence from it, preventing bystanders from tampering with it prior to medical examiner's arrival.](Tasks/23023.mdx)
- [Record progress of investigation, maintain informational files on suspects, and submit reports to commanding officer or magistrate to authorize warrants.](Tasks/23024.mdx)
- [Prepare reports that detail investigation findings.](Tasks/23029.mdx)
- [Prepare charges or responses to charges, or information for court cases, according to formalized procedures.](Tasks/23025.mdx)
- [Preserve, process, and analyze items of evidence obtained from crime scenes and suspects, placing them in proper containers and destroying evidence no longer needed.](Tasks/23026.mdx)
- [Obtain summary of incident from officer in charge at crime scene, taking care to avoid disturbing evidence.](Tasks/23027.mdx)
- [Note, mark, and photograph location of objects found, such as footprints, tire tracks, bullets and bloodstains, and take measurements of the scene.](Tasks/23028.mdx)
- [Examine records and governmental agency files to find identifying data about suspects.](Tasks/23030.mdx)
