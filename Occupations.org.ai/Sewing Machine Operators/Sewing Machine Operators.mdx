# Sewing Machine Operators

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-6031.00

Operate or tend sewing machines to join, reinforce, decorate, or perform related sewing operations in the manufacture of garment or nongarment products.

## Tasks

- [Monitor machine operation to detect problems such as defective stitching, breaks in thread, or machine malfunctions.](Tasks/12149.mdx)
- [Place spools of thread, cord, or other materials on spindles, insert bobbins, and thread ends through machine guides and components.](Tasks/12151.mdx)
- [Position items under needles, using marks on machines, clamps, templates, or cloth as guides.](Tasks/12150.mdx)
- [Guide garments or garment parts under machine needles and presser feet to sew parts together.](Tasks/12153.mdx)
- [Remove holding devices and finished items from machines.](Tasks/12166.mdx)
- [Match cloth pieces in correct sequences prior to sewing them, and verify that dye lots and patterns match.](Tasks/12152.mdx)
- [Fold or stretch edges or lengths of items while sewing to facilitate forming specified sections.](Tasks/12160.mdx)
- [Cut excess material or thread from finished products.](Tasks/12159.mdx)
- [Select supplies such as fasteners and thread, according to job requirements.](Tasks/12157.mdx)
- [Examine and measure finished articles to verify conformance to standards, using rulers.](Tasks/12162.mdx)
