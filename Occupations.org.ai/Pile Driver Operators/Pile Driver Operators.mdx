# Pile Driver Operators

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-2072.00

Operate pile drivers mounted on skids, barges, crawler treads, or locomotive cranes to drive pilings for retaining walls, bulkheads, and foundations of structures such as buildings, bridges, and piers.

## Tasks

- [Move hand and foot levers of hoisting equipment to position piling leads, hoist piling into leads, and position hammers over pilings.](Tasks/14901.mdx)
- [Move levers and turn valves to activate power hammers, or to raise and lower drophammers that drive piles to required depths.](Tasks/14904.mdx)
- [Drive pilings to provide support for buildings or other structures, using heavy equipment with a pile driver head.](Tasks/14903.mdx)
- [Conduct pre-operational checks on equipment to ensure proper functioning.](Tasks/14902.mdx)
- [Clean, lubricate, and refill equipment.](Tasks/14905.mdx)
