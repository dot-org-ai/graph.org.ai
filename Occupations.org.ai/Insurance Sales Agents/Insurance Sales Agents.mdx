# Insurance Sales Agents

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 41-3021.00

Sell life, property, casualty, health, automotive, or other types of insurance. May refer clients to independent brokers, work as an independent broker, or be employed by an insurance company.

## Tasks

- [Customize insurance programs to suit individual customers, often covering a variety of risks.](Tasks/720.mdx)
- [Sell various types of insurance policies to businesses and individuals on behalf of insurance companies, including automobile, fire, life, property, medical and dental insurance, or specialized policies, such as marine, farm/crop, and medical malpractice.](Tasks/721.mdx)
- [Explain features, advantages, and disadvantages of various policies to promote sale of insurance plans.](Tasks/724.mdx)
- [Perform administrative tasks, such as maintaining records and handling policy renewals.](Tasks/728.mdx)
- [Seek out new clients and develop clientele by networking to find new customers and generate lists of prospective clients.](Tasks/723.mdx)
- [Call on policyholders to deliver and explain policy, to analyze insurance program and suggest additions or changes, or to change beneficiaries.](Tasks/718.mdx)
- [Confer with clients to obtain and provide information when claims are made on a policy.](Tasks/727.mdx)
- [Interview prospective clients to obtain data about their financial resources and needs, the physical condition of the person or property to be insured, and to discuss any existing coverage.](Tasks/722.mdx)
- [Contact underwriter and submit forms to obtain binder coverage.](Tasks/725.mdx)
- [Select company that offers type of coverage requested by client to underwrite policy.](Tasks/729.mdx)
