# Animal Control Workers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 33-9011.00

Handle animals for the purpose of investigations of mistreatment, or control of abandoned, dangerous, or unattended animals.

## Tasks

- [Write reports of activities, and maintain files of impoundments and dispositions of animals.](Tasks/7968.mdx)
- [Investigate reports of animal attacks or animal cruelty, interviewing witnesses, collecting evidence, and writing reports.](Tasks/7958.mdx)
- [Examine animals for injuries or malnutrition, and arrange for any necessary medical treatment.](Tasks/7960.mdx)
- [Contact animal owners to inform them that their pets are at animal holding facilities.](Tasks/7967.mdx)
- [Educate the public about animal welfare, and animal control laws and regulations.](Tasks/7966.mdx)
- [Remove captured animals from animal-control service vehicles and place animals in shelter cages or other enclosures.](Tasks/7961.mdx)
- [Answer inquiries from the public concerning animal control operations.](Tasks/7970.mdx)
- [Capture and remove stray, uncontrolled, or abused animals from undesirable conditions, using nets, nooses, or tranquilizer darts as necessary.](Tasks/7959.mdx)
- [Prepare for prosecutions related to animal treatment, and give evidence in court.](Tasks/7965.mdx)
- [Supply animals with food, water, and personal care.](Tasks/7963.mdx)
