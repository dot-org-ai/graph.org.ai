# Maintenance and Repair Workers, General

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 49-9071.00

Perform work involving the skills of two or more maintenance or craft occupations to keep machines, mechanical equipment, or the structure of a building in repair. Duties may involve pipe fitting; HVAC maintenance; insulating; welding; machining; carpentry; repairing electrical or mechanical equipment; installing, aligning, and balancing new equipment; and repairing buildings, floors, or stairs.

## Tasks

- [Perform routine maintenance, such as inspecting drives, motors, or belts, checking fluid levels, replacing filters, or doing other preventive maintenance actions.](Tasks/20671.mdx)
- [Inspect, operate, or test machinery or equipment to diagnose machine malfunctions.](Tasks/3024.mdx)
- [Adjust functional parts of devices or control instruments, using hand tools, levels, plumb bobs, or straightedges.](Tasks/3030.mdx)
- [Repair machines, equipment, or structures, using tools such as hammers, hoists, saws, drills, wrenches, or equipment such as precision measuring instruments or electrical or electronic testing devices.](Tasks/20672.mdx)
- [Order parts, supplies, or equipment from catalogs or suppliers.](Tasks/20674.mdx)
- [Diagnose mechanical problems and determine how to correct them, checking blueprints, repair manuals, or parts catalogs, as necessary.](Tasks/3023.mdx)
- [Design new equipment to aid in the repair or maintenance of machines, mechanical equipment, or building structures.](Tasks/20679.mdx)
- [Assemble, install, or repair wiring, electrical or electronic components, pipe systems, plumbing, machinery, or equipment.](Tasks/3022.mdx)
- [Clean or lubricate shafts, bearings, gears, or other parts of machinery.](Tasks/3026.mdx)
- [Estimate costs to repair machinery, equipment, or building structures.](Tasks/20678.mdx)
