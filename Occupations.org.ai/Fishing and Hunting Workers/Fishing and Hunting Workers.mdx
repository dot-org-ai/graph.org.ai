# Fishing and Hunting Workers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 45-3031.00

Hunt, trap, catch, or gather wild animals or aquatic animals and plants. May use nets, traps, or other equipment. May haul catch onto ship or other vessel.

## Tasks

- [Steer vessels and operate navigational instruments.](Tasks/23403.mdx)
- [Remove catches from fishing equipment and measure them to ensure compliance with legal size.](Tasks/23410.mdx)
- [Direct fishing or hunting operations, and supervise crew members.](Tasks/23428.mdx)
- [Interpret weather and vessel conditions to determine appropriate responses.](Tasks/23419.mdx)
- [Travel on foot, by vehicle, or by equipment such as boats, snowmobiles, helicopters, snowshoes, or skis to reach hunting areas.](Tasks/23402.mdx)
- [Select, bait, and set traps, and lay poison along trails, according to species, size, habits, and environs of birds or animals and reasons for trapping them.](Tasks/23415.mdx)
- [Maintain engines, fishing gear, and other on-board equipment and perform minor repairs.](Tasks/23408.mdx)
- [Connect accessories such as floats, weights, flags, lights, or markers to nets, lines, or traps.](Tasks/23424.mdx)
- [Wash decks, conveyors, knives, and other equipment, using brushes, detergents, and water.](Tasks/23423.mdx)
- [Harvest marine life for human or animal consumption, using diving or dredging equipment, traps, barges, rods, reels, or tackle.](Tasks/23427.mdx)
