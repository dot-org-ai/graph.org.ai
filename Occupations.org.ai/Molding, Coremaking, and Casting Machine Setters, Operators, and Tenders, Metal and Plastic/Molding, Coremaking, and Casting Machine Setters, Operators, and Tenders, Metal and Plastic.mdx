# Molding, Coremaking, and Casting Machine Setters, Operators, and Tenders, Metal and Plastic

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-4072.00

Set up, operate, or tend metal or plastic molding, casting, or coremaking machines to mold or cast metal or thermoplastic parts or products.

## Tasks

- [Measure and visually inspect products for surface and dimension defects to ensure conformance to specifications, using precision measuring instruments.](Tasks/10174.mdx)
- [Observe continuous operation of automatic machines to ensure that products meet specifications and to detect jams or malfunctions, making adjustments as necessary.](Tasks/10173.mdx)
- [Set up, operate, or tend metal or plastic molding, casting, or coremaking machines to mold or cast metal or thermoplastic parts or products.](Tasks/10175.mdx)
- [Turn valves and dials of machines to regulate pressure, temperature, and speed and feed rates, and to set cycle times.](Tasks/10177.mdx)
- [Read specifications, blueprints, and work orders to determine setups, temperatures, and time settings required to mold, form, or cast plastic materials, as well as to plan production sequences.](Tasks/10186.mdx)
- [Observe meters and gauges to verify and record temperatures, pressures, and press-cycle times.](Tasks/10184.mdx)
- [Connect water hoses to cooling systems of dies, using hand tools.](Tasks/10192.mdx)
- [Remove parts, such as dies, from machines after production runs are finished.](Tasks/10188.mdx)
- [Perform maintenance work such as cleaning and oiling machines.](Tasks/10197.mdx)
- [Smooth and clean inner surfaces of molds, using brushes, scrapers, air hoses, or grinding wheels, and fill imperfections with refractory material.](Tasks/10195.mdx)
