# Flight Attendants

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 53-2031.00

Monitor safety of the aircraft cabin. Provide services to airline passengers, explain safety information, serve food and beverages, and respond to emergency incidents.

## Tasks

- [Verify that first aid kits and other emergency equipment, including fire extinguishers and oxygen bottles, are in working order.](Tasks/9639.mdx)
- [Announce and demonstrate safety and emergency procedures, such as the use of oxygen masks, seat belts, and life jackets.](Tasks/9637.mdx)
- [Monitor passenger behavior to identify threats to the safety of the crew and other passengers.](Tasks/23942.mdx)
- [Walk aisles of planes to verify that passengers have complied with federal regulations prior to takeoffs and landings.](Tasks/9638.mdx)
- [Direct and assist passengers in emergency procedures, such as evacuating a plane following an emergency landing.](Tasks/9636.mdx)
- [Prepare passengers and aircraft for landing, following procedures.](Tasks/9642.mdx)
- [Administer first aid to passengers in distress.](Tasks/9640.mdx)
- [Determine special assistance needs of passengers, such as small children, the elderly, or persons with disabilities.](Tasks/9643.mdx)
- [Attend preflight briefings concerning weather, altitudes, routes, emergency procedures, crew coordination, lengths of flights, food and beverage services offered, and numbers of passengers.](Tasks/9641.mdx)
- [Reassure passengers when situations, such as turbulence, are encountered.](Tasks/9645.mdx)
