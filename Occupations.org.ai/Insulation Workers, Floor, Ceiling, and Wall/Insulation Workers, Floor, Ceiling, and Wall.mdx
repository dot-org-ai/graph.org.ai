# Insulation Workers, Floor, Ceiling, and Wall

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-2131.00

Line and cover structures with insulating materials. May work with batt, roll, or blown insulation materials.

## Tasks

- [Measure and cut insulation for covering surfaces, using tape measures, handsaws, power saws, knives, or scissors.](Tasks/13545.mdx)
- [Fit, wrap, staple, or glue insulating materials to structures or surfaces, using hand tools or wires.](Tasks/13547.mdx)
- [Cover and line structures with blown or rolled forms of materials to insulate against cold, heat, or moisture, using saws, knives, rasps, trowels, blowers, or other tools and implements.](Tasks/13546.mdx)
- [Distribute insulating materials evenly into small spaces within floors, ceilings, or walls, using blowers and hose attachments, or cement mortars.](Tasks/13549.mdx)
- [Move controls, buttons, or levers to start blowers and regulate flow of materials through nozzles.](Tasks/13551.mdx)
- [Fill blower hoppers with insulating materials.](Tasks/13550.mdx)
- [Cover, seal, or finish insulated surfaces or access holes with plastic covers, canvas strips, sealants, tape, cement or asphalt mastic.](Tasks/13548.mdx)
- [Read blueprints, and select appropriate insulation, based on space characteristics and the heat retaining or excluding characteristics of the material.](Tasks/13544.mdx)
- [Remove old insulation, such as asbestos, following safety procedures.](Tasks/13553.mdx)
- [Prepare surfaces for insulation application by brushing or spreading on adhesives, cement, or asphalt, or by attaching metal pins to surfaces.](Tasks/13552.mdx)
