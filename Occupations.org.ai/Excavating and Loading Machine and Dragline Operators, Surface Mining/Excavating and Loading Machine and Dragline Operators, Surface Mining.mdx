# Excavating and Loading Machine and Dragline Operators, Surface Mining

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-5022.00

Operate or tend machinery at surface mining site, equipped with scoops, shovels, or buckets to excavate and load loose materials.

## Tasks

- [Move levers, depress foot pedals, and turn dials to operate power machinery, such as power shovels, stripping shovels, scraper loaders, or backhoes.](Tasks/12777.mdx)
- [Set up or inspect equipment prior to operation.](Tasks/12778.mdx)
- [Become familiar with digging plans, machine capabilities and limitations, and efficient and safe digging procedures in a given application.](Tasks/12780.mdx)
- [Observe hand signals, grade stakes, or other markings when operating machines so that work can be performed to specifications.](Tasks/12779.mdx)
- [Operate machinery to perform activities such as backfilling excavations, vibrating or breaking rock or concrete, or making winter roads.](Tasks/12781.mdx)
- [Receive written or oral instructions regarding material movement or excavation.](Tasks/12786.mdx)
- [Move materials over short distances, such as around a construction site, factory, or warehouse.](Tasks/12784.mdx)
- [Create or maintain inclines or ramps.](Tasks/20458.mdx)
- [Lubricate, adjust, or repair machinery and replace parts, such as gears, bearings, or bucket teeth.](Tasks/12783.mdx)
- [Handle slides, mud, or pit cleanings or maintenance.](Tasks/20459.mdx)
