# Food Preparation Workers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 35-2021.00

Perform a variety of food preparation duties other than cooking, such as preparing cold foods and shellfish, slicing meat, and brewing coffee or tea.

## Tasks

- [Clean and sanitize work areas, equipment, utensils, dishes, or silverware.](Tasks/18728.mdx)
- [Assist cooks and kitchen staff with various tasks as needed, and provide cooks with needed items.](Tasks/2204.mdx)
- [Take and record temperature of food and food storage areas, such as refrigerators and freezers.](Tasks/18730.mdx)
- [Carry food supplies, equipment, and utensils to and from storage and work areas.](Tasks/2210.mdx)
- [Remove trash and clean kitchen garbage containers.](Tasks/2208.mdx)
- [Store food in designated containers and storage areas to prevent spoilage.](Tasks/2197.mdx)
- [Weigh or measure ingredients.](Tasks/2203.mdx)
- [Vacuum dining area and sweep and mop kitchen floor.](Tasks/18734.mdx)
- [Inform supervisors when equipment is not working properly and when food and supplies are getting low, and order needed items.](Tasks/18732.mdx)
- [Wash, peel, and cut various foods, such as fruits and vegetables, to prepare for cooking or serving.](Tasks/18731.mdx)
