# Helpers--Production Workers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-9198.00

Help production workers by performing duties requiring less skill. Duties include supplying or holding materials or tools, and cleaning work area and equipment.

## Tasks

- [Load and unload items from machines, conveyors, and conveyances.](Tasks/12699.mdx)
- [Operate machinery used in the production process, or assist machine operators.](Tasks/12693.mdx)
- [Place products in equipment or on work surfaces for further processing, inspecting, or wrapping.](Tasks/12706.mdx)
- [Examine products to verify conformance to quality standards.](Tasks/12694.mdx)
- [Start machines or equipment to begin production processes.](Tasks/12703.mdx)
- [Observe equipment operations so that malfunctions can be detected, and notify operators of any malfunctions.](Tasks/12695.mdx)
- [Remove products, machine attachments, or waste material from machines.](Tasks/12707.mdx)
- [Lift raw materials, finished products, and packed items, manually or using hoists.](Tasks/12696.mdx)
- [Transfer finished products, raw materials, tools, or equipment between storage and work areas of plants and warehouses, by hand or using hand trucks or powered lift trucks.](Tasks/12710.mdx)
- [Pack and store materials and products.](Tasks/12708.mdx)
