# Plasterers and Stucco Masons

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-2161.00

Apply interior or exterior plaster, cement, stucco, or similar materials. May also set ornamental plaster.

## Tasks

- [Cover surfaces such as windows, doors, or sidewalks to protect from splashing.](Tasks/20789.mdx)
- [Clean job sites.](Tasks/20790.mdx)
- [Mix mortar and plaster to desired consistency or direct workers who perform mixing.](Tasks/9879.mdx)
- [Apply coats of plaster or stucco to walls, ceilings, or partitions of buildings, using trowels, brushes, or spray guns.](Tasks/9878.mdx)
- [Set up scaffolds.](Tasks/20791.mdx)
- [Determine materials needed to complete the job and place orders accordingly.](Tasks/20792.mdx)
- [Apply weatherproof, decorative coverings to exterior surfaces of buildings, such as by troweling or spraying on coats of stucco.](Tasks/9885.mdx)
- [Clean and prepare surfaces for applications of plaster, cement, stucco, or similar materials, such as by drywall taping.](Tasks/9883.mdx)
- [Create decorative textures in finish coat, using brushes or trowels, sand, pebbles, or stones.](Tasks/9880.mdx)
- [Apply insulation to building exteriors by installing prefabricated insulation systems over existing walls or by covering the outer wall with insulation board, reinforcing mesh, and a base coat.](Tasks/9881.mdx)
