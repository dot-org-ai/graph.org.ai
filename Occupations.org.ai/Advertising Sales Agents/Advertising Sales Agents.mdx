# Advertising Sales Agents

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 41-3011.00

Sell or solicit advertising space, time, or media in publications, signage, TV, radio, or Internet establishments or public spaces.

## Tasks

- [Prepare and deliver sales presentations to new and existing customers to sell new advertising programs and to protect and increase existing advertising.](Tasks/4579.mdx)
- [Maintain assigned account bases while developing new accounts.](Tasks/4581.mdx)
- [Provide clients with estimates of the costs of advertising products or services.](Tasks/4586.mdx)
- [Locate and contact potential clients to offer advertising services.](Tasks/4585.mdx)
- [Explain to customers how specific types of advertising will help promote their products or services in the most effective way possible.](Tasks/4580.mdx)
- [Obtain and study information about clients' products, needs, problems, advertising history, and business practices to offer effective sales presentations and appropriate product assistance.](Tasks/4589.mdx)
- [Prepare promotional plans, sales literature, media kits, and sales contracts, using computer.](Tasks/4592.mdx)
- [Process all correspondence and paperwork related to accounts.](Tasks/4582.mdx)
- [Draw up contracts for advertising work, and collect payments due.](Tasks/4584.mdx)
- [Deliver advertising or illustration proofs to customers for approval.](Tasks/4583.mdx)
