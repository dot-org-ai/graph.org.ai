# Computer Systems Analysts

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 15-1211.00

Analyze science, engineering, business, and other data processing problems to develop and implement solutions to complex applications problems, system administration issues, or network concerns. Perform systems management and integration functions, improve existing computer systems, and review computer system capabilities, workflow, and schedule limitations. May analyze or recommend commercially available software.

- [Troubleshoot program and system malfunctions to restore normal functioning.](Tasks/20950.mdx)

- [Test, maintain, and monitor computer programs and systems, including coordinating the installation of computer programs and systems.](Tasks/3465.mdx)
- [Use the computer in the analysis and solution of business problems, such as development of integrated production and inventory control and cost analysis systems.](Tasks/3482.mdx)
- [Coordinate and link the computer systems within an organization to increase compatibility so that information can be shared.](Tasks/3468.mdx)

- [Consult with management to ensure agreement on system principles.](Tasks/3469.mdx)
- [Specify inputs accessed by the system and plan the distribution and use of the results.](Tasks/3484.mdx)
