# Medical and Clinical Laboratory Technicians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 29-2012.00

Perform routine medical laboratory tests for the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of disease. May work under the supervision of a medical technologist.

## Tasks

- [Conduct chemical analyses of body fluids, such as blood or urine, using microscope or automatic analyzer to detect abnormalities or diseases and enter findings into computer.](Tasks/1902.mdx)
- [Analyze the results of tests or experiments to ensure conformity to specifications, using special mechanical or electrical devices.](Tasks/1904.mdx)
- [Set up, maintain, calibrate, clean, and test sterility of medical laboratory equipment.](Tasks/18535.mdx)
- [Prepare standard volumetric solutions or reagents to be combined with samples, following standardized formulas or experimental procedures.](Tasks/1915.mdx)
- [Collect blood or tissue samples from patients, observing principles of asepsis to obtain blood sample.](Tasks/1910.mdx)
- [Supervise or instruct other technicians or laboratory assistants.](Tasks/1914.mdx)
- [Conduct blood tests for transfusion purposes and perform blood counts.](Tasks/1907.mdx)
- [Obtain specimens, cultivating, isolating, and identifying microorganisms for analysis.](Tasks/1908.mdx)
- [Examine cells stained with dye to locate abnormalities.](Tasks/1909.mdx)
- [Consult with a pathologist to determine a final diagnosis when abnormal cells are found.](Tasks/1911.mdx)
