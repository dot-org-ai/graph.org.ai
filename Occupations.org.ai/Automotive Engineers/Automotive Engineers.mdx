# Automotive Engineers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 17-2141.02

Develop new or improved designs for vehicle structural members, engines, transmissions, or other vehicle systems, using computer-assisted design technology. Direct building, modification, or testing of vehicle or components.

- [Conduct or direct system-level automotive testing.](Tasks/16438.mdx)

- [Perform failure, variation, or root cause analyses.](Tasks/16429.mdx)
- [Calibrate vehicle systems, including control algorithms or other software systems.](Tasks/16434.mdx)
- [Design or analyze automobile systems in areas such as aerodynamics, alternate fuels, ergonomics, hybrid power, brakes, transmissions, steering, calibration, safety, or diagnostics.](Tasks/16437.mdx)
- [Prepare or present technical or project status reports.](Tasks/16423.mdx)
