# Correctional Officers and Jailers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 33-3012.00

Guard inmates in penal or rehabilitative institutions in accordance with established regulations and procedures. May guard prisoners in transit between jail, courtroom, prison, or other point. Includes deputy sheriffs and police who spend the majority of their time guarding prisoners in correctional institutions.

## Tasks

- [Conduct head counts to ensure that each prisoner is present.](Tasks/15252.mdx)
- [Inspect conditions of locks, window bars, grills, doors, and gates at correctional facilities to ensure security and help prevent escapes.](Tasks/15254.mdx)
- [Monitor conduct of prisoners in housing unit, or during work or recreational activities, according to established policies, regulations, and procedures, to prevent escape or violence.](Tasks/15253.mdx)
- [Search prisoners and vehicles and conduct shakedowns of cells for valuables and contraband, such as weapons or drugs.](Tasks/15256.mdx)
- [Guard facility entrances to screen visitors.](Tasks/2102.mdx)
- [Record information, such as prisoner identification, charges, and incidents of inmate disturbance, keeping daily logs of prisoner activities.](Tasks/15255.mdx)
- [Inspect mail for the presence of contraband.](Tasks/2104.mdx)
- [Maintain records of prisoners' identification and charges.](Tasks/2117.mdx)
- [Use weapons, handcuffs, and physical force to maintain discipline and order among prisoners.](Tasks/2107.mdx)
- [Use nondisciplinary tools and equipment, such as a computer.](Tasks/15261.mdx)
