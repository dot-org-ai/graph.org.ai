# Mechanical Engineering Technologists and Technicians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 17-3027.00

Apply theory and principles of mechanical engineering to modify, develop, test, or adjust machinery and equipment under direction of engineering staff or physical scientists.

## Tasks

- [Assemble or disassemble complex mechanical systems.](Tasks/22072.mdx)
- [Interpret engineering sketches, specifications, or drawings.](Tasks/22082.mdx)
- [Calculate required capacities for equipment of proposed system to obtain specified performance and submit data to engineering personnel for approval.](Tasks/1485.mdx)
- [Review project instructions and blueprints to ascertain test specifications, procedures, and objectives, and test nature of technical problems such as redesign.](Tasks/1476.mdx)
- [Provide technical support to other employees regarding mechanical design, fabrication, testing, or documentation.](Tasks/22086.mdx)
- [Test machines, components, materials, or products to determine characteristics such as performance, strength, or response to stress.](Tasks/22087.mdx)
- [Draft detail drawing or sketch for drafting room completion or to request parts fabrication by machine, sheet or wood shops.](Tasks/1475.mdx)
- [Analyze test results in relation to design or rated specifications and test objectives, and modify or adjust equipment to meet specifications.](Tasks/1482.mdx)
- [Record test procedures and results, numerical and graphical data, and recommendations for changes in product or test methods.](Tasks/1486.mdx)
- [Prepare specifications, designs, or sketches for machines, components, or systems related to the generation, transmission, or use of mechanical or fluid energy.](Tasks/22085.mdx)
