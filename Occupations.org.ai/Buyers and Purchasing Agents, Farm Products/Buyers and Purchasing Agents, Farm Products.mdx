# Buyers and Purchasing Agents, Farm Products

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 13-1021.00

Purchase farm products either for further processing or resale. Includes tree farm contractors, grain brokers and market operators, grain buyers, and tobacco buyers. May negotiate contracts.

## Tasks

- [Purchase, for further processing or for resale, farm products, such as milk, grains, or Christmas trees.](Tasks/12878.mdx)
- [Arrange for processing or resale of purchased products.](Tasks/12880.mdx)
- [Negotiate contracts with farmers for the production or purchase of farm products.](Tasks/12879.mdx)
- [Arrange for transportation or storage of purchased products.](Tasks/12881.mdx)
- [Maintain records of business transactions and product inventories, reporting data to companies or government agencies as necessary.](Tasks/12883.mdx)
- [Review orders to determine product types and quantities required to meet demand.](Tasks/12882.mdx)
- [Examine or test crops or products to estimate their value, determine their grade, or locate any evidence of disease or insect damage.](Tasks/12884.mdx)
- [Coordinate or direct activities of workers engaged in cutting, transporting, storing, or milling products and maintaining records.](Tasks/12885.mdx)
- [Sell supplies, such as seed, feed, fertilizers, or insecticides, arranging for loans or financing as necessary.](Tasks/12887.mdx)
- [Advise farm groups or growers on land preparation or livestock care techniques that will maximize the quantity and quality of production.](Tasks/12889.mdx)
