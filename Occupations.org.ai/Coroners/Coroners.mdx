# Coroners

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 13-1041.06

Direct activities such as autopsies, pathological and toxicological analyses, and inquests relating to the investigation of deaths occurring within a legal jurisdiction to determine cause of death or to fix responsibility for accidental, violent, or unexplained deaths.

## Tasks

- [Complete death certificates, including the assignment of cause and manner of death.](Tasks/8915.mdx)
- [Perform medicolegal examinations and autopsies, conducting preliminary examinations of the body to identify victims, locate signs of trauma, and identify factors that would indicate time of death.](Tasks/8912.mdx)
- [Interview persons present at death scenes to obtain information useful in determining the manner of death.](Tasks/8921.mdx)
- [Observe and record the positions and conditions of bodies and related evidence.](Tasks/8916.mdx)
- [Provide information concerning the circumstances of death to relatives of the deceased.](Tasks/8923.mdx)
- [Remove or supervise removal of bodies from death scenes, using the proper equipment and supplies, and arrange for transportation to morgues.](Tasks/8920.mdx)
- [Inquire into the cause, manner, and circumstances of human deaths and establish the identities of deceased persons.](Tasks/8913.mdx)
- [Observe, record, and preserve any objects or personal property related to deaths, including objects such as medication containers and suicide notes.](Tasks/8918.mdx)
- [Complete reports and forms required to finalize cases.](Tasks/8919.mdx)
- [Arrange for the next of kin to be notified of deaths.](Tasks/8928.mdx)
