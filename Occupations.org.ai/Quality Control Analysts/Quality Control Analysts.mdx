# Quality Control Analysts

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 19-4099.01

Conduct tests to determine quality of raw materials, bulk intermediate and finished products. May conduct stability sample tests.

## Tasks

- [Conduct routine and non-routine analyses of in-process materials, raw materials, environmental samples, finished goods, or stability samples.](Tasks/16980.mdx)
- [Interpret test results, compare them to established specifications and control limits, and make recommendations on appropriateness of data for release.](Tasks/16974.mdx)
- [Calibrate, validate, or maintain laboratory equipment.](Tasks/16978.mdx)
- [Ensure that lab cleanliness and safety standards are maintained.](Tasks/16961.mdx)
- [Perform visual inspections of finished products.](Tasks/16956.mdx)
- [Complete documentation needed to support testing procedures, including data capture forms, equipment logbooks, or inventory forms.](Tasks/16977.mdx)
- [Compile laboratory test data and perform appropriate analyses.](Tasks/16979.mdx)
- [Identify and troubleshoot equipment problems.](Tasks/16959.mdx)
- [Write technical reports or documentation, such as deviation reports, testing protocols, and trend analyses.](Tasks/16964.mdx)
- [Investigate or report questionable test results.](Tasks/16973.mdx)
