# Molders, Shapers, and Casters, Except Metal and Plastic

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-9195.00

Mold, shape, form, cast, or carve products such as food products, figurines, tile, pipes, and candles consisting of clay, glass, plaster, concrete, stone, or combinations of materials.

## Tasks

- [Read work orders or examine parts to determine parts or sections of products to be produced.](Tasks/23666.mdx)
- [Trim or remove excess material, using scrapers, knives, or band saws.](Tasks/23667.mdx)
- [Brush or spray mold surfaces with parting agents or insert paper into molds to ensure smoothness and prevent sticking or seepage.](Tasks/23668.mdx)
- [Engrave or stamp identifying symbols, letters, or numbers on products.](Tasks/23669.mdx)
- [Assemble, insert, and adjust wires, tubes, cores, fittings, rods, or patterns into molds, using hand tools and depth gauges.](Tasks/23670.mdx)
- [Clean, finish, and lubricate molds and mold parts.](Tasks/23671.mdx)

- [Set the proper operating temperature for each casting.](Tasks/23673.mdx)
- [Load or stack filled molds in ovens, dryers, or curing boxes, or on storage racks or carts.](Tasks/23674.mdx)
- [Align and assemble parts to produce completed products, using gauges and hand tools.](Tasks/23675.mdx)
