# Emergency Medical Technicians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 29-2042.00

Assess injuries and illnesses and administer basic emergency medical care. May transport injured or sick persons to medical facilities.

## Tasks

- [Administer first aid treatment or life support care to sick or injured persons in prehospital settings.](Tasks/22852.mdx)
- [Assess nature and extent of illness or injury to establish and prioritize medical procedures.](Tasks/22853.mdx)
- [Attend training classes to maintain certification licensure, keep abreast of new developments in the field, or maintain existing knowledge.](Tasks/22854.mdx)
- [Comfort and reassure patients.](Tasks/22855.mdx)
- [Communicate with dispatchers or treatment center personnel to provide information about situation, to arrange reception of survivors, or to receive instructions for further treatment.](Tasks/22856.mdx)
- [Coordinate work with other emergency medical team members or police or fire department personnel.](Tasks/22857.mdx)
- [Decontaminate ambulance interior following treatment of patient with infectious disease, and report case to proper authorities.](Tasks/22858.mdx)
- [Drive mobile intensive care unit to specified location, following instructions from emergency medical dispatcher.](Tasks/22859.mdx)
- [Immobilize patient for placement on stretcher and ambulance transport, using backboard or other spinal immobilization device.](Tasks/22860.mdx)
- [Maintain vehicles and medical and communication equipment, and replenish first aid equipment and supplies.](Tasks/22861.mdx)
