# Tile and Stone Setters

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-2044.00

Apply hard tile, stone, and comparable materials to walls, floors, ceilings, countertops, and roof decks.

## Tasks

- [Align and straighten tile using levels, squares, and straightedges.](Tasks/2848.mdx)
- [Finish and dress the joints and wipe excess grout from between tiles, using damp sponge.](Tasks/2851.mdx)
- [Cut and shape tile to fit around obstacles and into odd spaces and corners, using hand and power cutting tools.](Tasks/2850.mdx)
- [Determine and implement the best layout to achieve a desired pattern.](Tasks/2849.mdx)
- [Mix, apply, and spread plaster, concrete, mortar, cement, mastic, glue or other adhesives to form a bed for the tiles, using brush, trowel and screed.](Tasks/2853.mdx)
- [Study blueprints and examine surface to be covered to determine amount of material needed.](Tasks/2859.mdx)
- [Measure and mark surfaces to be tiled, following blueprints.](Tasks/2855.mdx)
- [Lay and set mosaic tiles to create decorative wall, mural, and floor designs.](Tasks/2864.mdx)
- [Apply mortar to tile back, position the tile, and press or tap with trowel handle to affix tile to base.](Tasks/2852.mdx)
- [Mix and apply mortar or cement to edges and ends of drain tiles to seal halves and joints.](Tasks/2869.mdx)
