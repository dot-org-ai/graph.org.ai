# Electrical and Electronics Installers and Repairers, Transportation Equipment

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 49-2093.00

Install, adjust, or maintain mobile electronics communication equipment, including sound, sonar, security, navigation, and surveillance systems on trains, watercraft, or other mobile equipment.

## Tasks

- [Inspect and test electrical systems and equipment to locate and diagnose malfunctions, using visual inspections, testing devices, and computer software.](Tasks/11727.mdx)
- [Reassemble and test equipment after repairs.](Tasks/11728.mdx)
- [Adjust, repair, or replace defective wiring and relays in ignition, lighting, air-conditioning, and safety control systems, using electrician's tools.](Tasks/11732.mdx)
- [Splice wires with knives or cutting pliers, and solder connections to fixtures, outlets, and equipment.](Tasks/11729.mdx)
- [Locate and remove or repair circuit defects such as blown fuses or malfunctioning transistors.](Tasks/11731.mdx)
- [Maintain equipment service records.](Tasks/11734.mdx)
- [Refer to schematics and manufacturers' specifications that show connections and provide instructions on how to locate problems.](Tasks/11733.mdx)
- [Install fixtures, outlets, terminal boards, switches, and wall boxes, using hand tools.](Tasks/11738.mdx)
- [Install new fuses, electrical cables, or power sources as required.](Tasks/11730.mdx)
- [Cut openings and drill holes for fixtures, outlet boxes, and fuse holders, using electric drills and routers.](Tasks/11735.mdx)
