# Automotive Service Technicians and Mechanics

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 49-3023.00

Diagnose, adjust, repair, or overhaul automotive vehicles.

## Tasks

- [Inspect vehicles for damage and record findings so that necessary repairs can be made.](Tasks/23523.mdx)
- [Test drive vehicles and test components and systems, using equipment such as infrared engine analyzers, compression gauges, and computerized diagnostic devices.](Tasks/23522.mdx)
- [Test and adjust repaired systems to meet manufacturers' performance specifications.](Tasks/23524.mdx)
- [Repair, reline, replace, and adjust brakes.](Tasks/23525.mdx)
- [Review work orders and discuss work with supervisors.](Tasks/23527.mdx)
- [Estimate costs of vehicle repair.](Tasks/23526.mdx)
- [Confer with customers to obtain descriptions of vehicle problems and to discuss work to be performed and future repair requirements.](Tasks/23529.mdx)
- [Align vehicles' front ends.](Tasks/23530.mdx)
- [Align wheels, axles, frames, torsion bars, and steering mechanisms of automobiles, using special alignment equipment and wheel-balancing machines.](Tasks/23537.mdx)
- [Tear down, repair, and rebuild faulty assemblies, such as power systems, steering systems, and linkages.](Tasks/23532.mdx)
