# Counter and Rental Clerks

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 41-2021.00

Receive orders, generally in person, for repairs, rentals, and services. May describe available options, compute cost, and accept payment.

## Tasks

- [Compute charges for merchandise or services and receive payments.](Tasks/678.mdx)
- [Receive orders for services, such as rentals, repairs, dry cleaning, and storage.](Tasks/689.mdx)
- [Explain rental fees, policies, and procedures.](Tasks/687.mdx)
- [Provide information about rental items, such as availability, operation, or description.](Tasks/691.mdx)
- [Advise customers on use and care of merchandise.](Tasks/692.mdx)
- [Greet customers and discuss the type, quality, and quantity of merchandise sought for rental.](Tasks/682.mdx)
- [Answer telephones to provide information and receive orders.](Tasks/681.mdx)
- [Inspect and adjust rental items to meet needs of customer.](Tasks/686.mdx)
- [Prepare rental forms, obtaining customer signature and other information, such as required licenses.](Tasks/684.mdx)
- [Rent items, arrange for provision of services to customers, and accept returns.](Tasks/690.mdx)
