# Cutters and Trimmers, Hand

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-9031.00

Use hand tools or hand-held power tools to cut and trim a variety of manufactured items, such as carpet, fabric, stone, glass, or rubber.

## Tasks

- [Mark or discard items with defects such as spots, stains, scars, snags, chips, scratches, or unacceptable shapes or finishes.](Tasks/14349.mdx)
- [Trim excess material or cut threads off finished products, such as cutting loose ends of plastic off a manufactured toy for a smoother finish.](Tasks/14350.mdx)
- [Cut, shape, and trim materials, such as textiles, food, glass, stone, and metal, using knives, scissors, and other hand tools, portable power tools, or bench-mounted tools.](Tasks/14351.mdx)
- [Position templates or measure materials to locate specified points of cuts or to obtain maximum yields, using rules, scales, or patterns.](Tasks/14361.mdx)
- [Read work orders to determine dimensions, cutting locations, and quantities to cut.](Tasks/14354.mdx)
- [Mark cutting lines around patterns or templates, or follow layout points, using squares, rules, and straightedges, and chalk, pencils, or scribes.](Tasks/14356.mdx)
- [Mark identification numbers, trademarks, grades, marketing data, sizes, or model numbers on products.](Tasks/14353.mdx)
- [Unroll, lay out, attach, or mount materials or items on cutting tables or machines.](Tasks/14357.mdx)
- [Separate materials or products according to size, weight, type, condition, color, or shade.](Tasks/14352.mdx)
- [Fold or shape materials before or after cutting them.](Tasks/14359.mdx)
