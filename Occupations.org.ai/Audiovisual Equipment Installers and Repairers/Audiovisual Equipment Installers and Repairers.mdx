# Audiovisual Equipment Installers and Repairers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 49-2097.00

Install, repair, or adjust audio or television receivers, stereo systems, camcorders, video systems, or other electronic entertainment equipment in homes or other venues. May perform routine maintenance.

## Tasks

- [Install, service, and repair electronic equipment or instruments such as televisions, radios, and videocassette recorders.](Tasks/13738.mdx)
- [Calibrate and test equipment, and locate circuit and component faults, using hand and power tools and measuring and testing instruments such as resistance meters and oscilloscopes.](Tasks/13739.mdx)
- [Confer with customers to determine the nature of problems or to explain repairs.](Tasks/13740.mdx)
- [Position or mount speakers, and wire speakers to consoles.](Tasks/13747.mdx)
- [Instruct customers on the safe and proper use of equipment.](Tasks/13742.mdx)
- [Make service calls to repair units in customers' homes, or return units to shops for major repairs.](Tasks/13746.mdx)
- [Read and interpret electronic circuit diagrams, function block diagrams, specifications, engineering drawings, and service manuals.](Tasks/13744.mdx)
- [Tune or adjust equipment and instruments to obtain optimum visual or auditory reception, according to specifications, manuals, and drawings.](Tasks/13741.mdx)
- [Keep records of work orders and test and maintenance reports.](Tasks/13745.mdx)
- [Disassemble entertainment equipment and repair or replace loose, worn, or defective components and wiring, using hand tools and soldering irons.](Tasks/13737.mdx)
