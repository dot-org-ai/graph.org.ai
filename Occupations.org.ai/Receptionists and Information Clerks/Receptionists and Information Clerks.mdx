# Receptionists and Information Clerks

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 43-4171.00

Answer inquiries and provide information to the general public, customers, visitors, and other interested parties regarding activities conducted at establishment and location of departments, offices, and employees within the organization.

## Tasks

- [Operate telephone switchboard to answer, screen, or forward calls, providing information, taking messages, or scheduling appointments.](Tasks/744.mdx)
- [Greet persons entering establishment, determine nature and purpose of visit, and direct or escort them to specific destinations.](Tasks/747.mdx)
- [Receive payment and record receipts for services.](Tasks/745.mdx)
- [Schedule appointments and maintain and update appointment calendars.](Tasks/751.mdx)
- [Transmit information or documents to customers, using computer, mail, or facsimile machine.](Tasks/750.mdx)
- [Hear and resolve complaints from customers or the public.](Tasks/748.mdx)
- [File and maintain records.](Tasks/749.mdx)
- [Provide information about establishment, such as location of departments or offices, employees within the organization, or services provided.](Tasks/753.mdx)
- [Perform administrative support tasks, such as proofreading, transcribing handwritten information, or operating calculators or computers to work with pay records, invoices, balance sheets, or other documents.](Tasks/746.mdx)
- [Collect, sort, distribute, or prepare mail, messages, or courier deliveries.](Tasks/755.mdx)
