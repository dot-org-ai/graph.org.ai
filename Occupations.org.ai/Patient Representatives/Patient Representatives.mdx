# Patient Representatives

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 29-2099.08

Assist patients in obtaining services, understanding policies and making health care decisions.

## Tasks

- [Coordinate communication between patients, family members, medical staff, administrative staff, or regulatory agencies.](Tasks/17668.mdx)
- [Interview patients or their representatives to identify problems relating to care.](Tasks/17670.mdx)
- [Refer patients to appropriate health care services or resources.](Tasks/17671.mdx)
- [Maintain knowledge of community services and resources available to patients.](Tasks/17678.mdx)
- [Explain policies, procedures, or services to patients using medical or administrative knowledge.](Tasks/17667.mdx)
- [Investigate and direct patient inquiries or complaints to appropriate medical staff members and follow up to ensure satisfactory resolution.](Tasks/17669.mdx)
- [Read current literature, talk with colleagues, continue education, or participate in professional organizations or conferences to keep abreast of developments in the field.](Tasks/17677.mdx)
- [Develop and distribute newsletters, brochures, or other printed materials to share information with patients or medical staff.](Tasks/17674.mdx)
- [Provide consultation or training to volunteers or staff on topics, such as guest relations, patients' rights, or medical issues.](Tasks/17679.mdx)
- [Analyze patients' abilities to pay to determine charges on a sliding scale.](Tasks/17672.mdx)
