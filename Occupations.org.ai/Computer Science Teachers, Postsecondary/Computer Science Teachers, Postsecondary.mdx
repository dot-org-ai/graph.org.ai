# Computer Science Teachers, Postsecondary

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 25-1021.00

Teach courses in computer science. May specialize in a field of computer science, such as the design and function of computers or operations and research analysis. Includes both teachers primarily engaged in teaching and those who do a combination of teaching and research.

## Tasks

- [Prepare course materials, such as syllabi, homework assignments, and handouts.](Tasks/5688.mdx)
- [Compile, administer, and grade examinations or assign this work to others.](Tasks/5689.mdx)
- [Prepare and deliver lectures to undergraduate or graduate students on topics such as programming, data structures, and software design.](Tasks/5687.mdx)
- [Evaluate and grade students' class work, laboratory work, assignments, and papers.](Tasks/5685.mdx)
- [Maintain student attendance records, grades, and other required records.](Tasks/5686.mdx)
