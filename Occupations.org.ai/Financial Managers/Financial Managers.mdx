# Financial Managers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 11-3031.00

Plan, direct, or coordinate accounting, investing, banking, insurance, securities, and other financial activities of a branch, office, or department of an establishment.

## Tasks

- [Establish and maintain relationships with individual or business customers or provide assistance with problems these customers may encounter.](Tasks/21311.mdx)
- [Oversee the flow of cash or financial instruments.](Tasks/21319.mdx)
- [Plan, direct, or coordinate the activities of workers in branches, offices, or departments of establishments, such as branch banks, brokerage firms, risk and insurance departments, or credit departments.](Tasks/21312.mdx)
- [Recruit staff members.](Tasks/21313.mdx)
- [Evaluate data pertaining to costs to plan budgets.](Tasks/21315.mdx)
- [Oversee training programs.](Tasks/21316.mdx)
- [Establish procedures for custody or control of assets, records, loan collateral, or securities to ensure safekeeping.](Tasks/21327.mdx)
- [Communicate with stockholders or other investors to provide information or to raise capital.](Tasks/21322.mdx)
- [Develop or analyze information to assess the current or future financial status of firms.](Tasks/21321.mdx)
