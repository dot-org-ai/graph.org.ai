# Retail Loss Prevention Specialists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 33-9099.02

Implement procedures and systems to prevent merchandise loss. Conduct audits and investigations of employee activity. May assist in developing policies, procedures, and systems for safeguarding assets.

## Tasks

- [Investigate known or suspected internal theft, external theft, or vendor fraud.](Tasks/17561.mdx)
- [Implement or monitor processes to reduce property or financial losses.](Tasks/17560.mdx)
- [Identify and report merchandise or stock shortages.](Tasks/17565.mdx)
- [Maintain documentation or reports on security-related incidents or investigations.](Tasks/17567.mdx)
- [Apprehend shoplifters in accordance with guidelines.](Tasks/17575.mdx)
- [Verify proper functioning of physical security systems, such as closed-circuit televisions, alarms, sensor tag systems, or locks.](Tasks/17573.mdx)
- [Identify and report safety concerns to maintain a safe shopping and working environment.](Tasks/17578.mdx)
- [Conduct store audits to identify problem areas or procedural deficiencies.](Tasks/17563.mdx)
- [Monitor compliance with standard operating procedures for loss prevention, physical security, or risk management.](Tasks/17568.mdx)
- [Inspect buildings, equipment, or access points to determine security risks.](Tasks/17566.mdx)
