# Cashiers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 41-2011.00

Receive and disburse money in establishments other than financial institutions. May use electronic scanners, cash registers, or related equipment. May process credit or debit card transactions and validate checks.

## Tasks

- [Receive payment by cash, check, credit cards, vouchers, or automatic debits.](Tasks/2400.mdx)
- [Greet customers entering establishments.](Tasks/2403.mdx)
- [Issue receipts, refunds, credits, or change due to customers.](Tasks/2401.mdx)
- [Assist customers by providing information and resolving their complaints.](Tasks/18765.mdx)
- [Monitor checkout stations to ensure they have adequate cash available and are staffed appropriately.](Tasks/2422.mdx)
- [Establish or identify prices of goods, services, or admission, and tabulate bills, using calculators, cash registers, or optical price scanners.](Tasks/2405.mdx)
- [Answer incoming phone calls.](Tasks/21073.mdx)
- [Answer customers' questions, and provide information on procedures or policies.](Tasks/2408.mdx)
- [Request information or assistance, using paging systems.](Tasks/2419.mdx)
- [Help customers find the location of products.](Tasks/21072.mdx)
