# Nanosystems Engineers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 17-2199.09

Design, develop, or supervise the production of materials, devices, or systems of unique molecular or macromolecular composition, applying principles of nanoscale physics and electrical, chemical, or biological engineering.

## Tasks

- [Provide scientific or technical guidance or expertise to scientists, engineers, technologists, technicians, or others, using knowledge of chemical, analytical, or biological processes as applied to micro and nanoscale systems.](Tasks/16540.mdx)
- [Supervise technologists or technicians engaged in nanotechnology research or production.](Tasks/16532.mdx)
- [Conduct research related to a range of nanotechnology topics, such as packaging, heat transfer, fluorescence detection, nanoparticle dispersion, hybrid systems, liquid systems, nanocomposites, nanofabrication, optoelectronics, or nanolithography.](Tasks/16546.mdx)
- [Synthesize, process, or characterize nanomaterials, using advanced tools or techniques.](Tasks/16533.mdx)
- [Prepare reports, deliver presentations, or participate in program review activities to communicate engineering results or recommendations.](Tasks/16537.mdx)
- [Design or conduct tests of new nanotechnology products, processes, or systems.](Tasks/16542.mdx)
- [Create designs or prototypes for nanosystem applications, such as biomedical delivery systems or atomic force microscopes.](Tasks/16545.mdx)
- [Write proposals to secure external funding or to partner with other companies.](Tasks/16531.mdx)
- [Generate high-resolution images or measure force-distance curves, using techniques such as atomic force microscopy.](Tasks/16536.mdx)
- [Develop processes or identify equipment needed for pilot or commercial nanoscale scale production.](Tasks/16539.mdx)
