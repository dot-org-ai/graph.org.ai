# Baggage Porters and Bellhops

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 39-6011.00

Handle baggage for travelers at transportation terminals or for guests at hotels or similar establishments.

## Tasks

- [Receive and mark baggage by completing and attaching claim checks.](Tasks/2316.mdx)
- [Greet incoming guests and escort them to their rooms.](Tasks/2317.mdx)
- [Transport guests about premises and local areas, or arrange for transportation.](Tasks/2324.mdx)
- [Maintain clean lobbies or entrance areas for travelers or guests.](Tasks/2323.mdx)
- [Transfer luggage, trunks, and packages to and from rooms, loading areas, vehicles, or transportation terminals, by hand or using baggage carts.](Tasks/2314.mdx)
- [Supply guests or travelers with directions, travel information, and other information, such as available services and points of interest.](Tasks/2315.mdx)
- [Explain the operation of room features, such as locks, ventilation systems, and televisions.](Tasks/2321.mdx)
- [Assist travelers and guests with disabilities.](Tasks/2318.mdx)
- [Deliver messages and room service orders, and run errands for guests.](Tasks/2320.mdx)
- [Pick up and return items for laundry and valet service.](Tasks/2327.mdx)
