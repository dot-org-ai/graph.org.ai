# Compliance Officers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 13-1041.00

Examine, evaluate, and investigate eligibility for or conformity with laws and regulations governing contract compliance of licenses and permits, and perform other compliance and enforcement inspection and analysis activities not classified elsewhere.

## Tasks

- [Warn violators of infractions or penalties.](Tasks/21446.mdx)
- [Evaluate applications, records, or documents to gather information about eligibility or liability issues.](Tasks/21447.mdx)
- [Advise licensees or other individuals or groups concerning licensing, permit, or passport regulations.](Tasks/21448.mdx)
- [Prepare reports of activities, evaluations, recommendations, or decisions.](Tasks/21449.mdx)
- [Report law or regulation violations to appropriate boards or agencies.](Tasks/21450.mdx)
- [Confer with or interview officials, technical or professional specialists, or applicants to obtain information or to clarify facts relevant to licensing decisions.](Tasks/21451.mdx)
- [Issue licenses to individuals meeting standards.](Tasks/21452.mdx)
- [Collect fees for licenses.](Tasks/21453.mdx)
- [Administer oral, written, road, or flight tests to license applicants.](Tasks/21454.mdx)
- [Visit establishments to verify that valid licenses or permits are displayed and that licensing standards are being upheld.](Tasks/21455.mdx)
