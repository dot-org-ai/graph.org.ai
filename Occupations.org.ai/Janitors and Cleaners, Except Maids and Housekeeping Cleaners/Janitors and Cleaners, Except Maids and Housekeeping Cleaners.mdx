# Janitors and Cleaners, Except Maids and Housekeeping Cleaners

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 37-2011.00

Keep buildings in clean and orderly condition. Perform heavy cleaning duties, such as cleaning floors, shampooing rugs, washing walls and glass, and removing rubbish. Duties may include tending furnace and boiler, performing routine maintenance activities, notifying management of need for repairs, and cleaning snow or debris from sidewalk.

- [Monitor building security and safety by performing tasks such as locking doors after operating hours or checking electrical appliance use to ensure that hazards are not created.](Tasks/20590.mdx)
- [Notify managers concerning the need for major repairs or additions to building operating systems.](Tasks/9544.mdx)
