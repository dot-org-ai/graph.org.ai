# Print Binding and Finishing Workers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-5113.00

Bind books and other publications or finish printed products by hand or machine. May set up binding and finishing machines.

## Tasks

- [Examine stitched, collated, bound, or unbound product samples for defects, such as imperfect bindings, ink spots, torn pages, loose pages, or loose or uncut threads.](Tasks/19460.mdx)
- [Read work orders to determine instructions and specifications for machine set-up.](Tasks/19453.mdx)
- [Install or adjust bindery machine devices, such as knives, guides, rollers, rounding forms, creasing rams, or clamps, to accommodate sheets, signatures, or books of specified sizes.](Tasks/19461.mdx)
- [Trim edges of books to size, using cutting machines, book trimming machines, or hand cutters.](Tasks/19459.mdx)
- [Stitch or glue endpapers, bindings, backings, or signatures, using sewing machines, glue machines, or glue and brushes.](Tasks/19458.mdx)
- [Monitor machine operations to detect malfunctions or to determine whether adjustments are needed.](Tasks/19450.mdx)
- [Maintain records, such as daily production records, using specified forms.](Tasks/19448.mdx)
- [Lubricate, clean, or make minor repairs to machine parts to keep machines in working condition.](Tasks/19462.mdx)
- [Set up or operate bindery machines, such as coil binders, thermal or tape binders, plastic comb binders, or specialty binders.](Tasks/19455.mdx)
- [Set up or operate machines that perform binding operations, such as pressing, folding, or trimming.](Tasks/19457.mdx)
