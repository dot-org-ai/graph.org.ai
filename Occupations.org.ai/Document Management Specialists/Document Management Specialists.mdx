# Document Management Specialists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 15-1299.03

Implement and administer enterprise-wide document management systems and related procedures that allow organizations to capture, store, retrieve, share, and destroy electronic records and documents.

## Tasks

- [Assist in determining document management policies to facilitate efficient, legal, and secure access to electronic content.](Tasks/16234.mdx)
- [Assist in the development of document or content classification taxonomies to facilitate information capture, search, and retrieval.](Tasks/16232.mdx)
- [Implement electronic document processing, retrieval, and distribution systems in collaboration with other information technology specialists.](Tasks/16238.mdx)
- [Identify and classify documents or other electronic content according to characteristics such as security level, function, and metadata.](Tasks/16239.mdx)
- [Develop, document, or maintain standards, best practices, or system usage procedures.](Tasks/16229.mdx)
- [Assist in the assessment, acquisition, or deployment of new electronic document management systems.](Tasks/16233.mdx)
- [Administer document and system access rights and revision control to ensure security of system and integrity of master documents.](Tasks/16237.mdx)
- [Prepare and record changes to official documents and confirm changes with legal and compliance management staff, including enterprise-wide records management staff.](Tasks/20368.mdx)
- [Write, review, or execute plans for testing new or established document management systems.](Tasks/16220.mdx)
- [Monitor regulatory activity to maintain compliance with records and document management laws.](Tasks/16219.mdx)
