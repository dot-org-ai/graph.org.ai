# Educational, Guidance, and Career Counselors and Advisors

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 21-1012.00

Advise and assist students and provide educational and vocational guidance services.

## Tasks

- [Maintain accurate and complete student records as required by laws, district policies, and administrative regulations.](Tasks/5606.mdx)
- [Counsel students regarding educational issues, such as course and program selection, class scheduling and registration, school adjustment, truancy, study habits, and career planning.](Tasks/18627.mdx)
- [Provide crisis intervention to students when difficult situations occur at schools.](Tasks/5608.mdx)
- [Counsel individuals or groups to help them understand and overcome personal, social, or behavioral problems affecting their educational or vocational situations.](Tasks/23990.mdx)
- [Review transcripts to ensure that students meet graduation or college entrance requirements, and write letters of recommendation.](Tasks/5631.mdx)
- [Prepare students for later educational experiences by encouraging them to explore learning opportunities and to persevere with challenging tasks.](Tasks/5611.mdx)
- [Refer students to outside counseling services.](Tasks/23991.mdx)
- [Refer students to degree programs based on interests, aptitudes, or educational assessments.](Tasks/5632.mdx)
- [Evaluate students' or individuals' abilities, interests, and personality characteristics, using tests, records, interviews, or professional sources.](Tasks/18625.mdx)
