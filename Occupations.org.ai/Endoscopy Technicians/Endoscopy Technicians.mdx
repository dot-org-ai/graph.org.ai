# Endoscopy Technicians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 31-9099.02

Maintain a sterile field to provide support for physicians and nurses during endoscopy procedures. Prepare and maintain instruments and equipment. May obtain specimens.

## Tasks

- [Clean, disinfect, or calibrate scopes or other endoscopic instruments according to manufacturer recommendations and facility standards.](Tasks/17519.mdx)
- [Collect specimens from patients, using standard medical procedures.](Tasks/17527.mdx)
- [Perform safety checks to verify proper equipment functioning.](Tasks/17521.mdx)
- [Maintain or repair endoscopic equipment.](Tasks/17523.mdx)
- [Assist physicians or registered nurses in the conduct of endoscopic procedures.](Tasks/17525.mdx)
- [Place devices, such as blood pressure cuffs, pulse oximeter sensors, nasal cannulas, surgical cautery pads, and cardiac monitoring electrodes, on patients to monitor vital signs.](Tasks/17530.mdx)
- [Prepare suites or rooms according to endoscopic procedure requirements.](Tasks/17522.mdx)
- [Maintain inventories of endoscopic equipment and supplies.](Tasks/17520.mdx)
- [Attend in-service training to validate or refresh basic professional skills.](Tasks/17526.mdx)
- [Conduct in-service training sessions to disseminate information regarding equipment or instruments.](Tasks/17528.mdx)
