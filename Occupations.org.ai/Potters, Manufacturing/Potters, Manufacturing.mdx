# Potters, Manufacturing

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-9195.05

Operate production machines such as pug mill, jigger machine, or potter's wheel to process clay in manufacture of ceramic, pottery and stoneware products.

## Tasks

- [Operate gas or electric kilns to fire pottery pieces.](Tasks/21210.mdx)
- [Mix and apply glazes to pottery pieces, using tools, such as spray guns.](Tasks/21211.mdx)
- [Raise and shape clay into wares, such as vases and pitchers, on revolving wheels, using hands, fingers, and thumbs.](Tasks/15118.mdx)
- [Adjust wheel speeds according to the feel of the clay as pieces enlarge and walls become thinner.](Tasks/15115.mdx)
- [Position balls of clay in centers of potters' wheels, and start motors or pump treadles with feet to revolve wheels.](Tasks/15117.mdx)
- [Move pieces from wheels so that they can dry.](Tasks/15122.mdx)
- [Prepare work for sale or exhibition, and maintain relationships with retail, pottery, art, and resource networks that can facilitate sale or exhibition of work.](Tasks/15119.mdx)
- [Attach handles to pottery pieces.](Tasks/21212.mdx)
- [Press thumbs into centers of revolving clay to form hollows, and press on the inside and outside of emerging clay cylinders with hands and fingers, gradually raising and shaping clay to desired forms and sizes.](Tasks/15114.mdx)
- [Pack and ship pottery to stores or galleries for retail sale.](Tasks/21213.mdx)
