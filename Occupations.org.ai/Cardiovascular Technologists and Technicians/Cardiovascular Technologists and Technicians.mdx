# Cardiovascular Technologists and Technicians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 29-2031.00

Conduct tests on pulmonary or cardiovascular systems of patients for diagnostic, therapeutic, or research purposes. May conduct or assist in electrocardiograms, cardiac catheterizations, pulmonary functions, lung capacity, and similar tests.

## Tasks

- [Conduct electrocardiogram (EKG), phonocardiogram, echocardiogram, stress testing, or other cardiovascular tests to record patients' cardiac activity, using specialized electronic test equipment, recording devices, or laboratory instruments.](Tasks/4132.mdx)
- [Explain testing procedures to patients to obtain cooperation and reduce anxiety.](Tasks/4129.mdx)
- [Monitor patients' blood pressure and heart rate using electrocardiogram (EKG) equipment during diagnostic or therapeutic procedures to notify the physician if something appears wrong.](Tasks/4127.mdx)
- [Obtain and record patient identification, medical history, or test results.](Tasks/4134.mdx)
- [Monitor patients' comfort and safety during tests, alerting physicians to abnormalities or changes in patient responses.](Tasks/4128.mdx)
- [Prepare and position patients for testing.](Tasks/4133.mdx)
- [Attach electrodes to the patients' chests, arms, and legs, connect electrodes to leads from the electrocardiogram (EKG) machine, and operate the EKG machine to obtain a reading.](Tasks/4135.mdx)
- [Adjust equipment and controls according to physicians' orders or established protocol.](Tasks/4136.mdx)
- [Check, test, and maintain cardiology equipment, making minor repairs when necessary, to ensure proper operation.](Tasks/4137.mdx)
- [Supervise or train other cardiology technologists or students.](Tasks/4138.mdx)
