# Orthoptists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 29-1299.02

Diagnose and treat visual system disorders such as binocular vision and eye movement impairments.

## Tasks

- [Examine patients with problems related to ocular motility, binocular vision, amblyopia, or strabismus.](Tasks/18429.mdx)
- [Evaluate, diagnose, or treat disorders of the visual system with an emphasis on binocular vision or abnormal eye movements.](Tasks/18430.mdx)
- [Provide instructions to patients or family members concerning diagnoses or treatment plans.](Tasks/18432.mdx)
- [Perform diagnostic tests or measurements, such as motor testing, visual acuity testing, lensometry, retinoscopy, and color vision testing.](Tasks/18428.mdx)
- [Provide nonsurgical interventions, including corrective lenses, patches, drops, fusion exercises, or stereograms, to treat conditions such as strabismus, heterophoria, and convergence insufficiency.](Tasks/18426.mdx)
- [Develop nonsurgical treatment plans for patients with conditions such as strabismus, nystagmus, and other visual disorders.](Tasks/18427.mdx)
- [Interpret clinical or diagnostic test results.](Tasks/18431.mdx)
- [Develop or use special test and communication techniques to facilitate diagnosis and treatment of children or patients with disabilities.](Tasks/18434.mdx)
- [Provide training related to clinical methods or orthoptics to students, resident physicians, or other health professionals.](Tasks/18440.mdx)
- [Refer patients to ophthalmic surgeons or other physicians.](Tasks/18433.mdx)
