# Meter Readers, Utilities

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 43-5041.00

Read meter and record consumption of electricity, gas, water, or steam.

## Tasks

- [Read electric, gas, water, or steam consumption meters and enter data in route books or hand-held computers.](Tasks/11309.mdx)
- [Upload into office computers all information collected on hand-held computers during meter rounds, or return route books or hand-held computers to business offices so that data can be compiled.](Tasks/11311.mdx)
- [Walk or drive vehicles along established routes to take readings of meter dials.](Tasks/11310.mdx)
- [Inspect meters for unauthorized connections, defects, and damage, such as broken seals.](Tasks/11313.mdx)
- [Verify readings in cases where consumption appears to be abnormal, and record possible reasons for fluctuations.](Tasks/11312.mdx)
- [Report to service departments any problems, such as meter irregularities, damaged equipment, or impediments to meter access, including dogs.](Tasks/11314.mdx)
- [Leave messages to arrange different times to read meters in cases in which meters are not accessible.](Tasks/11317.mdx)
- [Connect and disconnect utility services at specific locations.](Tasks/11318.mdx)
- [Answer customers' questions about services and charges, or direct them to customer service centers.](Tasks/11315.mdx)
- [Update client address and meter location information.](Tasks/11316.mdx)
