# Motorboat Mechanics and Service Technicians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 49-3051.00

Repair and adjust electrical and mechanical equipment of inboard or inboard-outboard boat engines.

## Tasks

- [Start motors and monitor performance for signs of malfunctioning, such as smoke, excessive vibration, or misfiring.](Tasks/13764.mdx)
- [Document inspection and test results and work performed or to be performed.](Tasks/13765.mdx)
- [Mount motors to boats, and operate boats at various speeds on waterways to conduct operational tests.](Tasks/13763.mdx)
- [Repair engine mechanical equipment, such as power tilts, bilge pumps, or power take-offs.](Tasks/13770.mdx)
- [Perform routine engine maintenance on motorboats, such as changing oil and filters.](Tasks/20801.mdx)
- [Replace parts, such as gears, magneto points, piston rings, or spark plugs, and reassemble engines.](Tasks/13766.mdx)
- [Idle motors and observe thermometers to determine the effectiveness of cooling systems.](Tasks/13767.mdx)
- [Inspect and repair or adjust propellers or propeller shafts.](Tasks/13771.mdx)
- [Adjust carburetor mixtures, electrical point settings, or timing while motors are running in water-filled test tanks.](Tasks/13769.mdx)
- [Set starter locks and align and repair steering or throttle controls, using gauges, screwdrivers, or wrenches.](Tasks/13768.mdx)
