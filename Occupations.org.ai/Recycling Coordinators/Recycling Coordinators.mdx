# Recycling Coordinators

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 53-1042.01

Supervise curbside and drop-off recycling programs for municipal governments or private firms.

## Tasks

- [Oversee recycling pick-up or drop-off programs to ensure compliance with community ordinances.](Tasks/17888.mdx)
- [Maintain logs of recycling materials received or shipped to processing companies.](Tasks/17907.mdx)
- [Supervise recycling technicians, community service workers, or other recycling operations employees or volunteers.](Tasks/17889.mdx)
- [Review customer requests for service to determine service needs and deploy appropriate resources to provide service.](Tasks/17910.mdx)
- [Provide training to recycling technicians or community service workers on topics such as safety, solid waste processing, or general recycling operations.](Tasks/17909.mdx)
- [Identify or investigate new opportunities for materials to be collected and recycled.](Tasks/17905.mdx)
- [Assign truck drivers or recycling technicians to routes.](Tasks/17890.mdx)
- [Create or manage recycling operations budgets.](Tasks/17893.mdx)
- [Prepare bills of lading, statements of shipping records, or customer receipts related to recycling or hazardous material services.](Tasks/17908.mdx)
