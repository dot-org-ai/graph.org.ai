# Food Servers, Nonrestaurant

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 35-3041.00

Serve food to individuals outside of a restaurant environment, such as in hotel rooms, hospital rooms, residential care facilities, or cars.

## Tasks

- [Place food servings on plates or trays according to orders or instructions.](Tasks/11152.mdx)
- [Clean or sterilize dishes, kitchen utensils, equipment, or facilities.](Tasks/11150.mdx)
- [Monitor food distribution, ensuring that meals are delivered to the correct recipients and that guidelines, such as those for special diets, are followed.](Tasks/11149.mdx)
- [Examine trays to ensure that they contain required items.](Tasks/11151.mdx)
- [Load trays with accessories, such as eating utensils, napkins, or condiments.](Tasks/11153.mdx)
- [Take food orders and relay orders to kitchens or serving counters so they can be filled.](Tasks/11154.mdx)
- [Monitor food preparation or serving techniques to ensure that proper procedures are followed.](Tasks/11158.mdx)
- [Remove trays and stack dishes for return to kitchen after meals are finished.](Tasks/11156.mdx)
- [Carry food, silverware, or linen on trays or use carts to carry trays.](Tasks/11159.mdx)
- [Record amounts and types of special food items served to customers.](Tasks/11161.mdx)
