# Engine and Other Machine Assemblers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-2031.00

Construct, assemble, or rebuild machines, such as engines, turbines, and similar equipment used in such industries as construction, extraction, textiles, and paper manufacturing.

## Tasks

- [Read and interpret assembly blueprints or specifications manuals, and plan assembly or building operations.](Tasks/13962.mdx)
- [Inspect, operate, and test completed products to verify functioning, machine capabilities, or conformance to customer specifications.](Tasks/13958.mdx)
- [Position or align components for assembly, manually or using hoists.](Tasks/13959.mdx)
- [Set and verify parts clearances.](Tasks/13960.mdx)
- [Verify conformance of parts to stock lists or blueprints, using measuring instruments such as calipers, gauges, or micrometers.](Tasks/13965.mdx)
- [Fasten or install piping, fixtures, or wiring and electrical components to form assemblies or subassemblies, using hand tools, rivet guns, or welding equipment.](Tasks/13966.mdx)
- [Remove rough spots and smooth surfaces to fit, trim, or clean parts, using hand tools or power tools.](Tasks/13967.mdx)
- [Lay out and drill, ream, tap, or cut parts for assembly.](Tasks/13968.mdx)
- [Rework, repair, or replace damaged parts or assemblies.](Tasks/13961.mdx)
- [Assemble systems of gears by aligning and meshing gears in gearboxes.](Tasks/13964.mdx)
