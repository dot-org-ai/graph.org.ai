# Psychiatrists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 29-1223.00

Diagnose, treat, and help prevent mental disorders.

## Tasks

- [Prescribe, direct, or administer psychotherapeutic treatments or medications to treat mental, emotional, or behavioral disorders.](Tasks/1828.mdx)
- [Gather and maintain patient information and records, including social or medical history obtained from patients, relatives, or other professionals.](Tasks/1830.mdx)
- [Design individualized care plans, using a variety of treatments.](Tasks/1832.mdx)
- [Collaborate with physicians, psychologists, social workers, psychiatric nurses, or other professionals to discuss treatment plans and progress.](Tasks/1829.mdx)
- [Analyze and evaluate patient data or test findings to diagnose nature or extent of mental disorder.](Tasks/1827.mdx)
- [Examine or conduct laboratory or diagnostic tests on patients to provide information on general physical condition or mental disorder.](Tasks/1833.mdx)
- [Counsel outpatients or other patients during office visits.](Tasks/1831.mdx)
- [Advise or inform guardians, relatives, or significant others of patients' conditions or treatment.](Tasks/1834.mdx)
- [Teach, take continuing education classes, attend conferences or seminars, or conduct research and publish findings to increase understanding of mental, emotional, or behavioral states or disorders.](Tasks/15230.mdx)
- [Review and evaluate treatment procedures and outcomes of other psychiatrists or medical professionals.](Tasks/1835.mdx)
