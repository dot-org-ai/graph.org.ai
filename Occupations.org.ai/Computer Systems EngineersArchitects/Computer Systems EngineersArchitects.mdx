# Computer Systems Engineers/Architects

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 15-1299.08

Design and develop solutions to complex applications problems, system administration issues, or network concerns. Perform systems management and integration functions.

## Tasks

- [Communicate with staff or clients to understand specific system requirements.](Tasks/14666.mdx)
- [Investigate system component suitability for specified purposes, and make recommendations regarding component use.](Tasks/14677.mdx)
- [Provide customers or installation teams guidelines for implementing secure systems.](Tasks/14674.mdx)
- [Direct the analysis, development, and operation of complete computer systems.](Tasks/14676.mdx)
- [Direct the installation of operating systems, network or application software, or computer or network hardware.](Tasks/14689.mdx)
- [Monitor system operation to detect potential problems.](Tasks/14675.mdx)
- [Identify system data, hardware, or software components required to meet user needs.](Tasks/14673.mdx)
- [Perform ongoing hardware and software maintenance operations, including installing or upgrading hardware or software.](Tasks/14678.mdx)
- [Verify stability, interoperability, portability, security, or scalability of system architecture.](Tasks/14669.mdx)
- [Research, test, or verify proper functioning of software patches and fixes.](Tasks/14686.mdx)
