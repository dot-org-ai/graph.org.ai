# Interior Designers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 27-1025.00

Plan, design, and furnish the internal space of rooms or buildings. Design interior environments or create physical layouts that are practical, aesthetic, and conducive to the intended purposes. May specialize in a particular field, style, or phase of interior design.

## Tasks

- [Design plans to be safe and to be compliant with the American Disabilities Act (ADA).](Tasks/20996.mdx)
- [Use computer-aided drafting (CAD) and related software to produce construction documents.](Tasks/18521.mdx)
- [Research health and safety code requirements to inform design.](Tasks/23995.mdx)
- [Confer with client to determine factors affecting planning of interior environments, such as budget, architectural preferences, purpose, and function.](Tasks/1735.mdx)
- [Advise client on interior design factors, such as space planning, layout and use of furnishings or equipment, and color coordination.](Tasks/1736.mdx)
- [Coordinate with other professionals, such as contractors, architects, engineers, and plumbers, to ensure job success.](Tasks/18519.mdx)
- [Review and detail shop drawings for construction plans.](Tasks/18520.mdx)
- [Inspect construction work on site to ensure its adherence to the design plans.](Tasks/20997.mdx)
- [Render design ideas in form of paste-ups or drawings.](Tasks/1740.mdx)
- [Subcontract fabrication, installation, and arrangement of carpeting, fixtures, accessories, draperies, paint and wall coverings, art work, furniture, and related items.](Tasks/1739.mdx)
