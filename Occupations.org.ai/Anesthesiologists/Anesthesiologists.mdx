# Anesthesiologists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 29-1211.00

Administer anesthetics and analgesics for pain management prior to, during, or after surgery.

## Tasks

- [Monitor patient before, during, and after anesthesia and counteract adverse reactions or complications.](Tasks/7754.mdx)
- [Record type and amount of anesthesia and patient condition throughout procedure.](Tasks/7756.mdx)
- [Provide and maintain life support and airway management and help prepare patients for emergency surgery.](Tasks/7755.mdx)
- [Administer anesthetic or sedation during medical procedures, using local, intravenous, spinal, or caudal methods.](Tasks/7753.mdx)
- [Examine patient, obtain medical history, and use diagnostic tests to determine risk during surgical, obstetrical, and other medical procedures.](Tasks/7757.mdx)
- [Position patient on operating table to maximize patient comfort and surgical accessibility.](Tasks/7758.mdx)
- [Coordinate administration of anesthetics with surgeons during operation.](Tasks/7760.mdx)
- [Decide when patients have recovered or stabilized enough to be sent to another room or ward or to be sent home following outpatient surgery.](Tasks/7759.mdx)
- [Confer with other medical professionals to determine type and method of anesthetic or sedation to render patient insensible to pain.](Tasks/7761.mdx)
- [Order laboratory tests, x-rays, and other diagnostic procedures.](Tasks/7763.mdx)
