# Solar Energy Installation Managers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-1011.03

Direct work crews installing residential or commercial solar photovoltaic or thermal systems.

## Tasks

- [Plan and coordinate installations of photovoltaic (PV) solar and solar thermal systems to ensure conformance to codes.](Tasks/17725.mdx)
- [Supervise solar installers, technicians, and subcontractors for solar installation projects to ensure compliance with safety standards.](Tasks/17726.mdx)
- [Estimate materials, equipment, and personnel needed for residential or commercial solar installation projects.](Tasks/17735.mdx)
- [Prepare solar installation project proposals, quotes, budgets, or schedules.](Tasks/17738.mdx)
- [Provide technical assistance to installers, technicians, or other solar professionals in areas such as solar electric systems, solar thermal systems, electrical systems, or mechanical systems.](Tasks/17732.mdx)
- [Coordinate or schedule building inspections for solar installation projects.](Tasks/17729.mdx)
- [Perform start-up of systems for testing or customer implementation.](Tasks/17731.mdx)
- [Identify means to reduce costs, minimize risks, or increase efficiency of solar installation projects.](Tasks/17737.mdx)
- [Assess system performance or functionality at the system, subsystem, and component levels.](Tasks/17728.mdx)
- [Assess potential solar installation sites to determine feasibility and design requirements.](Tasks/17727.mdx)
