# Physical Therapist Aides

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 31-2022.00

Under close supervision of a physical therapist or physical therapy assistant, perform only delegated, selected, or routine tasks in specific situations. These duties include preparing the patient and the treatment area.

## Tasks

- [Clean and organize work area and disinfect equipment after treatment.](Tasks/4273.mdx)
- [Secure patients into or onto therapy equipment.](Tasks/4276.mdx)
- [Instruct, motivate, safeguard, or assist patients practicing exercises or functional activities, under direction of medical staff.](Tasks/4275.mdx)
- [Confer with physical therapy staff or others to discuss and evaluate patient information for planning, modifying, or coordinating treatment.](Tasks/4278.mdx)
- [Observe patients during treatment to compile and evaluate data on patients' responses and progress and report to physical therapist.](Tasks/4274.mdx)
- [Change linens, such as bed sheets and pillow cases.](Tasks/4283.mdx)
- [Administer active or passive manual therapeutic exercises, therapeutic massage, or heat, light, sound, water, or electrical modality treatments, such as ultrasound.](Tasks/4282.mdx)
- [Transport patients to and from treatment areas, using wheelchairs or providing standing support.](Tasks/4277.mdx)
- [Perform clerical duties, such as taking inventory, ordering supplies, answering telephone, taking messages, or filling out forms.](Tasks/4280.mdx)
- [Schedule patient appointments with physical therapists and coordinate therapists' schedules.](Tasks/15251.mdx)
