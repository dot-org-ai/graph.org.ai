# First-Line Supervisors of Correctional Officers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 33-1011.00

Directly supervise and coordinate activities of correctional officers and jailers.

## Tasks

- [Take, receive, or check periodic inmate counts.](Tasks/9428.mdx)
- [Maintain order, discipline, and security within assigned areas in accordance with relevant rules, regulations, policies, and laws.](Tasks/9429.mdx)
- [Maintain knowledge of, comply with, and enforce all institutional policies, rules, procedures, and regulations.](Tasks/9431.mdx)
- [Respond to emergencies, such as escapes.](Tasks/9430.mdx)
- [Supervise and direct the work of correctional officers to ensure the safe custody, discipline, and welfare of inmates.](Tasks/9432.mdx)
- [Supervise or perform searches of inmates or their quarters to locate contraband items.](Tasks/9434.mdx)
- [Monitor behavior of subordinates to ensure alert, courteous, and professional behavior toward inmates, parolees, fellow employees, visitors, and the public.](Tasks/9435.mdx)
- [Restrain, secure, or control offenders, using chemical agents, firearms, or other weapons of force as necessary.](Tasks/9433.mdx)
- [Carry injured offenders or employees to safety and provide emergency first aid when necessary.](Tasks/9440.mdx)
- [Complete administrative paperwork or supervise the preparation or maintenance of records, forms, or reports.](Tasks/9436.mdx)
