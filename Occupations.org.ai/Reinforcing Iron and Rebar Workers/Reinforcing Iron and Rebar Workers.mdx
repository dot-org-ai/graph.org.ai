# Reinforcing Iron and Rebar Workers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-2171.00

Position and secure steel bars or mesh in concrete forms in order to reinforce concrete. Use a variety of fasteners, rod-bending machines, blowtorches, and hand tools. Includes rod busters.

## Tasks

- [Determine quantities, sizes, shapes, and locations of reinforcing rods from blueprints, sketches, or oral instructions.](Tasks/13569.mdx)
- [Space and fasten together rods in forms according to blueprints, using wire and pliers.](Tasks/13568.mdx)
- [Position and secure steel bars, rods, cables, or mesh in concrete forms, using fasteners, rod-bending machines, blowtorches, or hand tools.](Tasks/13567.mdx)
- [Cut rods to required lengths, using metal shears, hacksaws, bar cutters, or acetylene torches.](Tasks/13571.mdx)
- [Place blocks under rebar to hold the bars off the deck when reinforcing floors.](Tasks/13570.mdx)
- [Cut and fit wire mesh or fabric, using hooked rods, and position fabric or mesh in concrete to reinforce concrete.](Tasks/13572.mdx)
- [Bend steel rods with hand tools or rod-bending machines and weld them with arc-welding equipment.](Tasks/13573.mdx)
