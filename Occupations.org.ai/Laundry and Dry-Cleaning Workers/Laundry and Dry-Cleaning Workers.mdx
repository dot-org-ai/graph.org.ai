# Laundry and Dry-Cleaning Workers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-6011.00

Operate or tend washing or dry-cleaning machines to wash or dry-clean industrial or household articles, such as cloth garments, suede, leather, furs, blankets, draperies, linens, rugs, and carpets. Includes spotters and dyers of these articles.

## Tasks

- [Load articles into washers or dry-cleaning machines, or direct other workers to perform loading.](Tasks/12121.mdx)
- [Start washers, dry cleaners, driers, or extractors, and turn valves or levers to regulate machine processes and the volume of soap, detergent, water, bleach, starch, and other additives.](Tasks/12118.mdx)
- [Operate extractors and driers, or direct their operation.](Tasks/12125.mdx)
- [Remove items from washers or dry-cleaning machines, or direct other workers to do so.](Tasks/12124.mdx)
- [Sort and count articles removed from dryers, and fold, wrap, or hang them.](Tasks/12119.mdx)
- [Clean machine filters, and lubricate equipment.](Tasks/12123.mdx)
- [Examine and sort into lots articles to be cleaned, according to color, fabric, dirt content, and cleaning technique required.](Tasks/12120.mdx)
- [Receive and mark articles for laundry or dry cleaning with identifying code numbers or names, using hand or machine markers.](Tasks/12117.mdx)
- [Apply bleaching powders to spots and spray them with steam to remove stains from fabrics that do not respond to other cleaning solvents.](Tasks/12136.mdx)
- [Determine spotting procedures and proper solvents, based on fabric and stain types.](Tasks/12127.mdx)
