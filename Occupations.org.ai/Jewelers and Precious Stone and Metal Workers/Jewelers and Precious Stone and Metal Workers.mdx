# Jewelers and Precious Stone and Metal Workers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-9071.00

Design, fabricate, adjust, repair, or appraise jewelry, gold, silver, other precious metals, or gems.

## Tasks

- [Clean and polish metal items and jewelry pieces, using jewelers' tools, polishing wheels, and chemical baths.](Tasks/23624.mdx)
- [Smooth soldered joints and rough spots, using hand files and emery paper, and polish smoothed areas with polishing wheels or buffing wire.](Tasks/23621.mdx)
- [Create jewelry from materials such as gold, silver, platinum, and precious or semiprecious stones.](Tasks/23622.mdx)
- [Cut and file pieces of jewelry such as rings, brooches, bracelets, and lockets.](Tasks/23625.mdx)
- [Examine assembled or finished products to ensure conformance to specifications, using magnifying glasses or precision measuring instruments.](Tasks/23628.mdx)
- [Make repairs, such as enlarging or reducing ring sizes, soldering pieces of jewelry together, and replacing broken clasps and mountings.](Tasks/23623.mdx)
- [Compute costs of labor and materials to determine production costs of products and articles.](Tasks/23627.mdx)
- [Position stones and metal pieces, and set, mount, and secure items in place, using setting and hand tools.](Tasks/23620.mdx)
- [Grade stones based on their color, perfection, and quality of cut.](Tasks/23635.mdx)
- [Select and acquire metals and gems for designs.](Tasks/23626.mdx)
