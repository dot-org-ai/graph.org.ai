# Food Science Technicians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 19-4013.00

Work with food scientists or technologists to perform standardized qualitative and quantitative tests to determine physical or chemical properties of food or beverage products. Includes technicians who assist in research and development of production technology, quality control, packaging, processing, and use of foods.

## Tasks

- [Taste or smell foods or beverages to ensure that flavors meet specifications or to select samples with specific characteristics.](Tasks/7582.mdx)
- [Measure, test, or weigh bottles, cans, or other containers to ensure that hardness, strength, or dimensions meet specifications.](Tasks/7585.mdx)
- [Maintain records of testing results or other documents as required by state or other governing agencies.](Tasks/20394.mdx)
- [Monitor and control temperature of products.](Tasks/20395.mdx)
- [Analyze test results to classify products or compare results with standard tables.](Tasks/7581.mdx)
- [Record or compile test results or prepare graphs, charts, or reports.](Tasks/7579.mdx)
- [Perform regular maintenance of laboratory equipment by inspecting, calibrating, cleaning, or sterilizing.](Tasks/20396.mdx)
- [Examine chemical or biological samples to identify cell structures or to locate bacteria or extraneous material, using a microscope.](Tasks/7583.mdx)
- [Conduct standardized tests on food, beverages, additives, or preservatives to ensure compliance with standards and regulations regarding factors such as color, texture, or nutrients.](Tasks/7576.mdx)
- [Train newly hired laboratory personnel.](Tasks/20397.mdx)
