# Hoist and Winch Operators

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 53-7041.00

Operate or tend hoists or winches to lift and pull loads using power-operated cable equipment.

## Tasks

- [Move levers, pedals, and throttles to stop, start, and regulate speeds of hoist or winch drums in response to hand, bell, buzzer, telephone, loud-speaker, or whistle signals, or by observing dial indicators or cable marks.](Tasks/4979.mdx)
- [Start engines of hoists or winches and use levers and pedals to wind or unwind cable on drums.](Tasks/4980.mdx)
- [Observe equipment gauges and indicators and hand signals of other workers to verify load positions or depths.](Tasks/4981.mdx)
- [Operate compressed air, diesel, electric, gasoline, or steam-driven hoists or winches to control movement of cableways, cages, derricks, draglines, loaders, railcars, or skips.](Tasks/4982.mdx)
- [Move or reposition hoists, winches, loads and materials, manually or using equipment and machines such as trucks, cars, and hand trucks.](Tasks/4983.mdx)
- [Select loads or materials according to weight and size specifications.](Tasks/4984.mdx)
- [Signal and assist other workers loading or unloading materials.](Tasks/4985.mdx)
- [Attach, fasten, and disconnect cables or lines to loads, materials, and equipment, using hand tools.](Tasks/4986.mdx)
- [Apply hand or foot brakes and move levers to lock hoists or winches.](Tasks/4987.mdx)
- [Oil winch drums so that cables will wind smoothly.](Tasks/4988.mdx)
