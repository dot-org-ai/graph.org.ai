# Telemarketers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 41-9041.00

Solicit donations or orders for goods or services over the telephone.

## Tasks

- [Contact businesses or private individuals by telephone to solicit sales for goods or services, or to request donations for charitable causes.](Tasks/4619.mdx)
- [Obtain customer information such as name, address, and payment method, and enter orders into computers.](Tasks/4621.mdx)
- [Explain products or services and prices, and answer questions from customers.](Tasks/4620.mdx)
- [Record names, addresses, purchases, and reactions of prospects contacted.](Tasks/4622.mdx)
- [Maintain records of contacts, accounts, and orders.](Tasks/4627.mdx)
- [Answer telephone calls from potential customers who have been solicited through advertisements.](Tasks/4625.mdx)
- [Deliver prepared sales talks, reading from scripts that describe products or services, to persuade potential customers to purchase a product or service or to make a donation.](Tasks/4618.mdx)
- [Telephone or write letters to respond to correspondence from customers or to follow up initial sales contacts.](Tasks/4626.mdx)
- [Adjust sales scripts to better target the needs and interests of specific individuals.](Tasks/4623.mdx)
- [Obtain names and telephone numbers of potential customers from sources such as telephone directories, magazine reply cards, and lists purchased from other organizations.](Tasks/4624.mdx)
