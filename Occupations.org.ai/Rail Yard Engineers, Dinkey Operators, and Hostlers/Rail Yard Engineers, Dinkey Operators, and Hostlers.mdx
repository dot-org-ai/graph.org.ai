# Rail Yard Engineers, Dinkey Operators, and Hostlers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 53-4013.00

Drive switching or other locomotive or dinkey engines within railroad yard, industrial plant, quarry, construction project, or similar location.

## Tasks

- [Observe and respond to wayside and cab signals, including color light signals, position signals, torpedoes, flags, and hot box detectors.](Tasks/10667.mdx)
- [Inspect engines before and after use to ensure proper operation.](Tasks/10669.mdx)
- [Apply and release hand brakes.](Tasks/10670.mdx)
- [Signal crew members for movement of engines or trains, using lanterns, hand signals, radios, or telephones.](Tasks/10666.mdx)
- [Confer with conductors and other workers via radiotelephones or computers to exchange switching information.](Tasks/10665.mdx)
- [Inspect track for defects such as broken rails and switch malfunctions.](Tasks/10675.mdx)
- [Observe water levels and oil, air, and steam pressure gauges to ensure proper operation of equipment.](Tasks/10673.mdx)
- [Couple and uncouple air hoses and electrical connections between cars.](Tasks/10679.mdx)
- [Drive engines within railroad yards or other establishments to couple, uncouple, or switch railroad cars.](Tasks/10668.mdx)
- [Inspect the condition of stationary trains, rolling stock, and equipment.](Tasks/10672.mdx)
