# Multiple Machine Tool Setters, Operators, and Tenders, Metal and Plastic

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-4081.00

Set up, operate, or tend more than one type of cutting or forming machine tool or robot.

## Tasks

- [Inspect workpieces for defects, and measure workpieces to determine accuracy of machine operation, using rules, templates, or other measuring instruments.](Tasks/10206.mdx)
- [Position, adjust, and secure stock material or workpieces against stops, on arbors, or in chucks, fixtures, or automatic feeding mechanisms, manually or using hoists.](Tasks/10210.mdx)
- [Read blueprints or job orders to determine product specifications and tooling instructions and to plan operational sequences.](Tasks/10208.mdx)
- [Select, install, and adjust alignment of drills, cutters, dies, guides, and holding devices, using templates, measuring instruments, and hand tools.](Tasks/10211.mdx)
- [Observe machine operation to detect workpiece defects or machine malfunctions, adjusting machines as necessary.](Tasks/10207.mdx)
- [Set up and operate machines, such as lathes, cutters, shears, borers, millers, grinders, presses, drills, or auxiliary machines, to make metallic and plastic workpieces.](Tasks/10209.mdx)
- [Change worn machine accessories, such as cutting tools or brushes, using hand tools.](Tasks/10212.mdx)
- [Set machine stops or guides to specified lengths as indicated by scales, rules, or templates.](Tasks/10221.mdx)
- [Select the proper coolants and lubricants and start their flow.](Tasks/10216.mdx)
- [Remove burrs, sharp edges, rust, or scale from workpieces, using files, hand grinders, wire brushes, or power tools.](Tasks/10217.mdx)
