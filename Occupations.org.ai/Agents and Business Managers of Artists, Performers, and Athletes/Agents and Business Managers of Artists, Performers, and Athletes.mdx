# Agents and Business Managers of Artists, Performers, and Athletes

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 13-1011.00

Represent and promote artists, performers, and athletes in dealings with current or prospective employers. May handle contract negotiation and other business matters for clients.

## Tasks

- [Collect fees, commissions, or other payments, according to contract terms.](Tasks/12865.mdx)
- [Send samples of clients' work and other promotional material to potential employers to obtain auditions, sponsorships, or endorsement deals.](Tasks/21163.mdx)
- [Keep informed of industry trends and deals.](Tasks/12870.mdx)
- [Conduct auditions or interviews to evaluate potential clients.](Tasks/12872.mdx)
- [Negotiate with managers, promoters, union officials, and other persons regarding clients' contractual rights and obligations.](Tasks/12869.mdx)
- [Confer with clients to develop strategies for their careers, and to explain actions taken on their behalf.](Tasks/12866.mdx)
- [Develop contacts with individuals and organizations, and apply effective strategies and techniques to ensure their clients' success.](Tasks/12867.mdx)
- [Schedule promotional or performance engagements for clients.](Tasks/12868.mdx)
- [Arrange meetings concerning issues involving their clients.](Tasks/12873.mdx)
- [Manage business and financial affairs for clients, such as arranging travel and lodging, selling tickets, and directing marketing and advertising activities.](Tasks/12871.mdx)
