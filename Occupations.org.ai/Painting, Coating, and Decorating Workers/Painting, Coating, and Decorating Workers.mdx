# Painting, Coating, and Decorating Workers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-9123.00

Paint, coat, or decorate articles, such as furniture, glass, plateware, pottery, jewelry, toys, books, or leather.

## Tasks

- [Apply coatings, such as paint, ink, or lacquer, to protect or decorate workpiece surfaces, using spray guns, pens, or brushes.](Tasks/12527.mdx)
- [Examine finished surfaces of workpieces to verify conformance to specifications and retouch any defective areas.](Tasks/12528.mdx)
- [Clean and maintain tools and equipment, using solvents, brushes, and rags.](Tasks/12529.mdx)
- [Read job orders and inspect workpieces to determine work procedures and materials required.](Tasks/12530.mdx)
- [Select and mix ingredients to prepare coating substances according to specifications, using paddles or mechanical mixers.](Tasks/12534.mdx)
- [Place coated workpieces in ovens or dryers for specified times to dry or harden finishes.](Tasks/12533.mdx)
- [Clean surfaces of workpieces in preparation for coating, using cleaning fluids, solvents, brushes, scrapers, steam, sandpaper, or cloth.](Tasks/12531.mdx)
- [Conceal blemishes in workpieces, such as nicks and dents, using fillers such as putty.](Tasks/12536.mdx)
- [Rinse, drain, or wipe coated workpieces to remove excess coating material or to facilitate setting of finish coats on workpieces.](Tasks/12532.mdx)
