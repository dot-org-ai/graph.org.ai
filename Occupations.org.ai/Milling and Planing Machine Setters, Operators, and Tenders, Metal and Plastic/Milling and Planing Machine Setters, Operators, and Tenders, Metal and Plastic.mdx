# Milling and Planing Machine Setters, Operators, and Tenders, Metal and Plastic

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-4035.00

Set up, operate, or tend milling or planing machines to mill, plane, shape, groove, or profile metal or plastic work pieces.

## Tasks

- [Remove workpieces from machines, and check to ensure that they conform to specifications, using measuring instruments such as microscopes, gauges, calipers, and micrometers.](Tasks/14001.mdx)
- [Verify alignment of workpieces on machines, using measuring instruments such as rules, gauges, or calipers.](Tasks/14007.mdx)
- [Move controls to set cutting specifications, to position cutting tools and workpieces in relation to each other, and to start machines.](Tasks/14005.mdx)
- [Observe milling or planing machine operation, and adjust controls to ensure conformance with specified tolerances.](Tasks/14002.mdx)
- [Select and install cutting tools and other accessories according to specifications, using hand tools or power tools.](Tasks/14011.mdx)
- [Position and secure workpieces on machines, using holding devices, measuring instruments, hand tools, and hoists.](Tasks/14003.mdx)
- [Replace worn tools, using hand tools, and sharpen dull tools, using bench grinders.](Tasks/14010.mdx)
- [Study blueprints, layouts, sketches, or work orders to assess workpiece specifications and to determine tooling instructions, tools and materials needed, and sequences of operations.](Tasks/14004.mdx)
- [Compute dimensions, tolerances, and angles of workpieces or machines according to specifications and knowledge of metal properties and shop mathematics.](Tasks/14006.mdx)
- [Move cutters or material manually or by turning handwheels, or engage automatic feeding mechanisms to mill workpieces to specifications.](Tasks/14009.mdx)
