# Pharmacy Aides

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 31-9095.00

Record drugs delivered to the pharmacy, store incoming merchandise, and inform the supervisor of stock needs. May operate cash register and accept prescriptions for filling.

## Tasks

- [Greet customers and help them locate merchandise.](Tasks/2048.mdx)
- [Accept prescriptions for filling, gathering and processing necessary information.](Tasks/2045.mdx)
- [Operate cash register to process cash or credit sales.](Tasks/2052.mdx)
- [Answer telephone inquiries, referring callers to pharmacist when necessary.](Tasks/2046.mdx)
- [Receive, store, and inventory pharmaceutical supplies or medications, check for out-of-date medications, and notify pharmacist when inventory levels are low.](Tasks/18704.mdx)
- [Unpack, sort, count, and label incoming merchandise, including items requiring special handling or refrigeration.](Tasks/2049.mdx)
- [Restock storage areas, replenishing items on shelves.](Tasks/2053.mdx)
- [Maintain and clean equipment, work areas, or shelves.](Tasks/2055.mdx)
- [Prepare prescription labels by typing or operating a computer and printer.](Tasks/2050.mdx)
- [Compound, package, and label pharmaceutical products, under direction of pharmacist.](Tasks/2059.mdx)
