# Office Machine Operators, Except Computer

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 43-9071.00

Operate one or more of a variety of office machines, such as photocopying, photographic, and duplicating machines, or other office machines.

## Tasks

- [Read job orders to determine the type of work to be done, the quantities to be produced, and the materials needed.](Tasks/13288.mdx)
- [Deliver completed work.](Tasks/13301.mdx)
- [Place original copies in feed trays, feed originals into feed rolls, or position originals on tables beneath camera lenses.](Tasks/13290.mdx)
- [Sort, assemble, and proof completed work.](Tasks/13292.mdx)
- [Operate office machines such as high speed business photocopiers, readers, scanners, addressing machines, stencil-cutting machines, microfilm readers or printers, folding and inserting machines, bursters, and binder machines.](Tasks/13289.mdx)
- [Complete records of production, including work volumes and outputs, materials used, and any backlogs.](Tasks/13300.mdx)
- [Compute prices for services and receive payment, or provide supervisors with billing information.](Tasks/13291.mdx)
- [Set up and adjust machines, regulating factors such as speed, ink flow, focus, and number of copies.](Tasks/13293.mdx)
- [Load machines with materials such as blank paper or film.](Tasks/13295.mdx)
- [Monitor machine operation, and make adjustments as necessary to ensure proper operation.](Tasks/13294.mdx)
