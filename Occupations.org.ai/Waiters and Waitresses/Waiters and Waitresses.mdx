# Waiters and Waitresses

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 35-3031.00

Take orders and serve food and beverages to patrons at tables in dining establishment.

## Tasks

- [Collect payments from customers.](Tasks/2276.mdx)
- [Check patrons' identification to ensure that they meet minimum age requirements for consumption of alcoholic beverages.](Tasks/2275.mdx)
- [Write patrons' food orders on order slips, memorize orders, or enter orders into computers for transmittal to kitchen staff.](Tasks/2277.mdx)
- [Check with customers to ensure that they are enjoying their meals, and take action to correct any problems.](Tasks/2279.mdx)
- [Take orders from patrons for food or beverages.](Tasks/2278.mdx)
- [Prepare checks that itemize and total meal costs and sales taxes.](Tasks/2281.mdx)
- [Remove dishes and glasses from tables or counters, and take them to kitchen for cleaning.](Tasks/18745.mdx)
- [Clean tables or counters after patrons have finished dining.](Tasks/2285.mdx)
- [Serve food or beverages to patrons, and prepare or serve specialty dishes at tables as required.](Tasks/2280.mdx)
