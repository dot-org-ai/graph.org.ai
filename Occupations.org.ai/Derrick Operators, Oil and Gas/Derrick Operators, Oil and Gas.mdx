# Derrick Operators, Oil and Gas

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-5011.00

Rig derrick equipment and operate pumps to circulate mud or fluid through drill hole.

## Tasks

- [Inspect derricks, or order their inspection, prior to being raised or lowered.](Tasks/9889.mdx)
- [Inspect derricks for flaws, and clean and oil derricks to maintain proper working conditions.](Tasks/9890.mdx)
- [Control the viscosity and weight of the drilling fluid.](Tasks/9891.mdx)
- [Repair pumps, mud tanks, and related equipment.](Tasks/9892.mdx)
- [Set and bolt crown blocks to posts at tops of derricks.](Tasks/9893.mdx)
- [Listen to mud pumps and check regularly for vibration and other problems to ensure that rig pumps and drilling mud systems are working properly.](Tasks/9894.mdx)
- [Start pumps that circulate mud through drill pipes and boreholes to cool drill bits and flush out drill cuttings.](Tasks/9895.mdx)
- [Position and align derrick elements, using harnesses and platform climbing devices.](Tasks/9896.mdx)
- [Supervise crew members, and provide assistance in training them.](Tasks/9897.mdx)
- [Guide lengths of pipe into and out of elevators.](Tasks/9898.mdx)
