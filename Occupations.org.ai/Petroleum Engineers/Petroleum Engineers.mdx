# Petroleum Engineers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 17-2171.00

Devise methods to improve oil and gas extraction and production and determine the need for new or modified tool designs. Oversee drilling and offer technical advice.

## Tasks

- [Specify and supervise well modification and stimulation programs to maximize oil and gas recovery.](Tasks/3582.mdx)
- [Monitor production rates, and plan rework processes to improve production.](Tasks/3580.mdx)
- [Maintain records of drilling and production operations.](Tasks/3586.mdx)
- [Analyze data to recommend placement of wells and supplementary processes to enhance production.](Tasks/3581.mdx)
- [Assist engineering and other personnel to solve operating problems.](Tasks/3584.mdx)
- [Direct and monitor the completion and evaluation of wells, well testing, or well surveys.](Tasks/3583.mdx)
- [Develop plans for oil and gas field drilling, and for product recovery and treatment.](Tasks/3585.mdx)
- [Assess costs and estimate the production capabilities and economic value of oil and gas wells, to evaluate the economic viability of potential drilling sites.](Tasks/3579.mdx)
- [Confer with scientific, engineering, and technical personnel to resolve design, research, and testing problems.](Tasks/3587.mdx)
- [Interpret drilling and testing information for personnel.](Tasks/3591.mdx)
