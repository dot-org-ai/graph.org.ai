# Cooks, Short Order

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 35-2015.00

Prepare and cook to order a variety of foods that require only a short preparation time. May take orders from customers and serve patrons at counters or tables.

## Tasks

- [Clean food preparation equipment, work areas, and counters or tables.](Tasks/2187.mdx)
- [Restock kitchen supplies, rotate food, and stamp the time and date on food in coolers.](Tasks/18725.mdx)
- [Complete orders from steam tables, placing food on plates and serving customers at tables or counters.](Tasks/2192.mdx)
- [Plan work on orders so that items served together are finished at the same time.](Tasks/2188.mdx)
- [Grill, cook, and fry foods such as french fries, eggs, and pancakes.](Tasks/2189.mdx)
- [Perform food preparation tasks, such as making sandwiches, carving meats, making soups or salads, baking breads or desserts, and brewing coffee or tea.](Tasks/18726.mdx)
- [Perform general cleaning activities in kitchen and dining areas.](Tasks/18727.mdx)
- [Take orders from customers and cook foods requiring short preparation times, according to customer requirements.](Tasks/2190.mdx)
- [Grill and garnish hamburgers or other meats, such as steaks and chops.](Tasks/2191.mdx)
- [Order supplies and stock them on shelves.](Tasks/2194.mdx)
