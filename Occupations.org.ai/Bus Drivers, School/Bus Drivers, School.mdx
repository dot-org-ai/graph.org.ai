# Bus Drivers, School

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 53-3051.00

Drive a school bus to transport students. Ensure adherence to safety rules. May assist students in boarding or exiting.

## Tasks

- [Comply with traffic regulations to operate vehicles in a safe and courteous manner.](Tasks/23716.mdx)
- [Follow safety rules as students board and exit buses or cross streets near bus stops.](Tasks/23719.mdx)
- [Check the condition of a vehicle's tires, brakes, windshield wipers, lights, oil, fuel, water, and safety equipment to ensure that everything is in working order.](Tasks/23715.mdx)
- [Report any bus malfunctions or needed repairs.](Tasks/23729.mdx)
- [Pick up and drop off students at regularly scheduled neighborhood locations, following strict time schedules.](Tasks/23724.mdx)
