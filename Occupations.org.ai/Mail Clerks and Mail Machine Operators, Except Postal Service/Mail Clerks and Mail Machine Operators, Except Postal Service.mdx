# Mail Clerks and Mail Machine Operators, Except Postal Service

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 43-9051.00

Prepare incoming and outgoing mail for distribution. Time-stamp, open, read, sort, and route incoming mail; and address, seal, stamp, fold, stuff, and affix postage to outgoing mail or packages. Duties may also include keeping necessary records and completed forms.

## Tasks

- [Wrap packages or bundles by hand, or by using tying machines.](Tasks/13268.mdx)
- [Verify that items are addressed correctly, marked with the proper postage, and in suitable condition for processing.](Tasks/13261.mdx)
- [Remove containers of sorted mail or parcels and transfer them to designated areas according to established procedures.](Tasks/13276.mdx)
- [Sort and route incoming mail, and collect outgoing mail, using carts as necessary.](Tasks/13264.mdx)
- [Affix postage to packages or letters by hand, or stamp materials, using postage meters.](Tasks/13260.mdx)
- [Determine manner in which mail is to be sent, and prepare it for delivery to mailing facilities.](Tasks/13266.mdx)
- [Accept and check containers of mail or parcels from large volume mailers, couriers, and contractors.](Tasks/13282.mdx)
- [Seal or open envelopes, by hand or by using machines.](Tasks/13259.mdx)
- [Weigh packages or letters to determine postage needed, using weighing scales and rate charts.](Tasks/13265.mdx)
- [Inspect mail machine output for defects and determine how to eliminate causes of any defects.](Tasks/13275.mdx)
