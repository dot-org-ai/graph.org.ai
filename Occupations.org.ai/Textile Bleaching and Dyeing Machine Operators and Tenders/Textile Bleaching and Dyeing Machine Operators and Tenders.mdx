# Textile Bleaching and Dyeing Machine Operators and Tenders

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-6061.00

Operate or tend machines to bleach, shrink, wash, dye, or finish textiles or synthetic or glass fibers.

## Tasks

- [Weigh ingredients, such as dye, to be mixed together for use in textile processing.](Tasks/23982.mdx)
- [Start and control machines and equipment to wash, bleach, dye, or otherwise process and finish fabric, yarn, thread, or other textile goods.](Tasks/14127.mdx)
- [Observe display screens, control panels, equipment, and cloth entering or exiting processes to determine if equipment is operating correctly.](Tasks/14124.mdx)
- [Notify supervisors or mechanics of equipment malfunctions.](Tasks/14122.mdx)
- [Monitor factors such as temperatures and dye flow rates to ensure that they are within specified ranges.](Tasks/14126.mdx)
- [Add dyes, water, detergents, or chemicals to tanks to dilute or strengthen solutions, according to established formulas and solution test results.](Tasks/14121.mdx)
- [Examine and feel products to identify defects and variations from coloring and other processing standards.](Tasks/14128.mdx)
- [Adjust equipment controls to maintain specified heat, tension, and speed.](Tasks/14123.mdx)
- [Soak specified textile products for designated times.](Tasks/14138.mdx)
- [Inspect machinery to determine necessary adjustments and repairs.](Tasks/14134.mdx)
