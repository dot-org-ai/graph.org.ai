# Aircraft Structure, Surfaces, Rigging, and Systems Assemblers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-2011.00

Assemble, fit, fasten, and install parts of airplanes, space vehicles, or missiles, such as tails, wings, fuselage, bulkheads, stabilizers, landing gear, rigging and control equipment, or heating and ventilating systems.

## Tasks

- [Assemble parts, fittings, or subassemblies on aircraft, using layout tools, hand tools, power tools, or fasteners, such as bolts, screws, rivets, or clamps.](Tasks/20810.mdx)
- [Read blueprints, illustrations, or specifications to determine layouts, sequences of operations, or identities or relationships of parts.](Tasks/20811.mdx)
- [Attach brackets, hinges, or clips to secure or support components or subassemblies, using bolts, screws, rivets, chemical bonding, or welding.](Tasks/13928.mdx)
- [Inspect or test installed units, parts, systems, or assemblies for fit, alignment, performance, defects, or compliance with standards, using measuring instruments or test equipment.](Tasks/13929.mdx)
- [Adjust, repair, rework, or replace parts or assemblies to ensure proper operation.](Tasks/20812.mdx)
- [Cut, trim, file, bend, or smooth parts to ensure proper fit and clearance.](Tasks/20813.mdx)
- [Fabricate parts needed for assembly or installation, using shop machinery or equipment.](Tasks/13934.mdx)
- [Layout and mark reference points and locations for installation of parts or components, using jigs, templates, or measuring and marking instruments.](Tasks/13926.mdx)
- [Clean, oil, or coat system components, as necessary, before assembly or attachment.](Tasks/13931.mdx)
- [Assemble prefabricated parts to form subassemblies.](Tasks/13919.mdx)
