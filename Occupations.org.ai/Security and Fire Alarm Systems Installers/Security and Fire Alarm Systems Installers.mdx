# Security and Fire Alarm Systems Installers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 49-2098.00

Install, program, maintain, and repair security and fire alarm wiring and equipment. Ensure that work is in accordance with relevant codes.

## Tasks

- [Install, maintain, or repair security systems, alarm devices, or related equipment, following blueprints of electrical layouts and building plans.](Tasks/11761.mdx)
- [Mount and fasten control panels, door and window contacts, sensors, or video cameras, and attach electrical and telephone wiring to connect components.](Tasks/11760.mdx)
- [Demonstrate systems for customers and explain details, such as the causes and consequences of false alarms.](Tasks/11767.mdx)
- [Test and repair circuits and sensors, following wiring and system specifications.](Tasks/11765.mdx)
- [Feed cables through access holes, roof spaces, or cavity walls to reach fixture outlets, positioning and terminating cables, wires, or strapping.](Tasks/20799.mdx)
- [Examine systems to locate problems, such as loose connections or broken insulation.](Tasks/11758.mdx)
- [Test backup batteries, keypad programming, sirens, or other security features to ensure proper functioning or to diagnose malfunctions.](Tasks/20800.mdx)
- [Drill holes for wiring in wall studs, joists, ceilings, or floors.](Tasks/11766.mdx)
- [Inspect installation sites and study work orders, building plans, and installation manuals to determine materials requirements and installation procedures.](Tasks/11763.mdx)
- [Consult with clients to assess risks and to determine security requirements.](Tasks/11768.mdx)
