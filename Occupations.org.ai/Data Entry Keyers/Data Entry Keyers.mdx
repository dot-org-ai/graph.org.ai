# Data Entry Keyers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 43-9021.00

Operate data entry device, such as keyboard or photo composing perforator. Duties may include verifying data and preparing materials for printing.

## Tasks

- [Locate and correct data entry errors, or report them to supervisors.](Tasks/11405.mdx)
- [Compile, sort, and verify the accuracy of data before it is entered.](Tasks/11402.mdx)
- [Compare data with source documents, or re-enter data in verification format to detect errors.](Tasks/11403.mdx)
- [Store completed documents in appropriate locations.](Tasks/11404.mdx)
- [Select materials needed to complete work assignments.](Tasks/11407.mdx)
- [Read source documents such as canceled checks, sales reports, or bills, and enter data in specific data fields or onto tapes or disks for subsequent entry, using keyboards or scanners.](Tasks/11401.mdx)
- [Maintain logs of activities and completed work.](Tasks/11406.mdx)
- [Load machines with required input or output media, such as paper, cards, disks, tape, or Braille media.](Tasks/11408.mdx)
- [Resolve garbled or indecipherable messages, using cryptographic procedures and equipment.](Tasks/11409.mdx)
