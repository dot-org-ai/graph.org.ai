# Production, Planning, and Expediting Clerks

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 43-5061.00

Coordinate and expedite the flow of work and materials within or between departments of an establishment according to production schedule. Duties include reviewing and distributing production, work, and shipment schedules; conferring with department supervisors to determine progress of work and completion dates; and compiling reports on progress of work, inventory levels, costs, and production problems.

## Tasks

- [Distribute production schedules or work orders to departments.](Tasks/11370.mdx)
- [Revise production schedules when required due to design changes, labor or material shortages, backlogs, or other interruptions, collaborating with management, marketing, sales, production, or engineering.](Tasks/11365.mdx)
- [Review documents, such as production schedules, work orders, or staffing tables, to determine personnel or materials requirements or material priorities.](Tasks/11363.mdx)
- [Arrange for delivery, assembly, or distribution of supplies or parts to expedite flow of materials and meet production schedules.](Tasks/11372.mdx)
- [Confer with establishment personnel, vendors, or customers to coordinate production or shipping activities and to resolve complaints or eliminate delays.](Tasks/11366.mdx)
- [Requisition and maintain inventories of materials or supplies necessary to meet production demands.](Tasks/11368.mdx)
- [Confer with department supervisors or other personnel to assess progress and discuss needed changes.](Tasks/11364.mdx)
- [Plan production commitments or timetables for business units, specific programs, or jobs, using sales forecasts.](Tasks/11375.mdx)
- [Compile information, such as production rates and progress, materials inventories, materials used, or customer information, so that status reports can be completed.](Tasks/11371.mdx)
- [Examine documents, materials, or products and monitor work processes to assess completeness, accuracy, and conformance to standards and specifications.](Tasks/11362.mdx)
