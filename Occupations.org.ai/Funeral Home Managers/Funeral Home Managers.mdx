# Funeral Home Managers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 11-9171.00

Plan, direct, or coordinate the services or resources of funeral homes. Includes activities such as determining prices for services or merchandise and managing the facilities of funeral homes.

## Tasks

- [Consult with families or friends of the deceased to arrange funeral details, such as obituary notice wording, casket selection, or plans for services.](Tasks/18832.mdx)
- [Schedule funerals, burials, or cremations.](Tasks/18842.mdx)
- [Deliver death certificates to medical facilities or offices to obtain signatures from legally authorized persons.](Tasks/23984.mdx)
- [Offer counsel and comfort to families and friends of the deceased.](Tasks/18837.mdx)
- [Monitor funeral service operations to ensure that they comply with applicable policies, regulations, and laws.](Tasks/18835.mdx)
- [Direct and supervise work of embalmers, funeral attendants, death certificate clerks, cosmetologists, or other staff.](Tasks/18833.mdx)
- [Complete and maintain records, such as state-required documents, tracking documents, or product inventories.](Tasks/18846.mdx)
- [Sell funeral services, products, or merchandise to clients.](Tasks/18843.mdx)
- [Plan and implement changes to service offerings to meet community needs or increase funeral home revenues.](Tasks/18838.mdx)
- [Respond to customer complaints, legal inquiries, payment negotiations, or other post-service matters.](Tasks/18840.mdx)
