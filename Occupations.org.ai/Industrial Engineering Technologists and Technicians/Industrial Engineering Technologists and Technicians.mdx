# Industrial Engineering Technologists and Technicians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 17-3026.00

Apply engineering theory and principles to problems of industrial layout or manufacturing production, usually under the direction of engineering staff. May perform time and motion studies on worker operations in a variety of industries for purposes such as establishing standard production rates or improving efficiency.

## Tasks

- [Test selected products at specified stages in the production process for performance characteristics or adherence to specifications.](Tasks/20516.mdx)
- [Compile and evaluate statistical data to determine and maintain quality and reliability of products.](Tasks/3672.mdx)
- [Study time, motion, methods, or speed involved in maintenance, production, or other operations to establish standard production rate or improve efficiency.](Tasks/3660.mdx)
- [Read worker logs, product processing sheets, or specification sheets to verify that records adhere to quality assurance specifications.](Tasks/3668.mdx)
- [Verify that equipment is being operated and maintained according to quality assurance standards by observing worker performance.](Tasks/20518.mdx)
- [Aid in planning work assignments in accordance with worker performance, machine capacity, production schedules, or anticipated delays.](Tasks/3663.mdx)
- [Evaluate industrial operations for compliance with permits or regulations related to the generation, storage, treatment, transportation, or disposal of hazardous materials or waste.](Tasks/19692.mdx)
- [Adhere to all applicable regulations, policies, and procedures for health, safety, and environmental compliance.](Tasks/22022.mdx)
- [Analyze, estimate, or report production costs.](Tasks/22023.mdx)
- [Assist engineers in developing, building, or testing prototypes or new products, processes, or procedures.](Tasks/22024.mdx)
