# Allergists and Immunologists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 29-1229.01

Diagnose, treat, and help prevent allergic diseases and disease processes affecting the immune system.

- [Diagnose or treat allergic or immunologic conditions.](Tasks/17076.mdx)
- [Educate patients about diagnoses, prognoses, or treatments.](Tasks/17069.mdx)
- [Order or perform diagnostic tests such as skin pricks and intradermal, patch, or delayed hypersensitivity tests.](Tasks/17068.mdx)
- [Prescribe medication such as antihistamines, antibiotics, and nasal, oral, topical, or inhaled glucocorticosteroids.](Tasks/17066.mdx)
- [Interpret diagnostic test results to make appropriate differential diagnoses.](Tasks/17070.mdx)
