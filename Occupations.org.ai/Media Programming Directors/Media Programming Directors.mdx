# Media Programming Directors

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 27-2012.03

Direct and coordinate activities of personnel engaged in preparation of radio or television station program schedules and programs, such as sports or news.

## Tasks

- [Operate and maintain on-air and production audio equipment.](Tasks/7680.mdx)
- [Check completed program logs for accuracy and conformance with Federal Communications Commission (FCC) rules and regulations and resolve program log inaccuracies.](Tasks/7665.mdx)
- [Read news, read or record public service and promotional announcements, or perform other on-air duties.](Tasks/7679.mdx)
- [Direct and coordinate activities of personnel engaged in broadcast news, sports, or programming.](Tasks/7664.mdx)
- [Monitor and review programming to ensure that schedules are met, guidelines are adhered to, and performances are of adequate quality.](Tasks/7663.mdx)
- [Prepare copy and edit tape so that material is ready for broadcasting.](Tasks/7675.mdx)
- [Coordinate activities between departments, such as news and programming.](Tasks/7667.mdx)
- [Perform personnel duties, such as hiring staff and evaluating work performance.](Tasks/7668.mdx)
- [Establish work schedules and assign work to staff members.](Tasks/7666.mdx)
- [Develop promotions for current programs and specials.](Tasks/7674.mdx)
