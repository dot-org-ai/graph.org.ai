# Preschool Teachers, Except Special Education

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 25-2011.00

Instruct preschool-aged students, following curricula or lesson plans, in activities designed to promote social, physical, and intellectual growth.

## Tasks

- [Teach basic skills, such as color, shape, number and letter recognition, personal hygiene, and social skills.](Tasks/6466.mdx)
- [Establish and enforce rules for behavior and procedures for maintaining order.](Tasks/6467.mdx)
- [Adapt teaching methods and instructional materials to meet students' varying needs and interests.](Tasks/6478.mdx)
- [Provide a variety of materials and resources for children to explore, manipulate, and use, both in learning activities and in imaginative play.](Tasks/6464.mdx)
- [Serve meals and snacks in accordance with nutritional guidelines.](Tasks/6476.mdx)
- [Attend to children's basic needs by feeding them, dressing them, and changing their diapers.](Tasks/6465.mdx)
- [Meet with parents and guardians to discuss their children's progress and needs, determine their priorities for their children, and suggest ways that they can promote learning and development.](Tasks/6472.mdx)
- [Organize and lead activities designed to promote physical, mental, and social development, such as games, arts and crafts, music, storytelling, and field trips.](Tasks/6469.mdx)
- [Identify children showing signs of emotional, developmental, or health-related problems and discuss them with supervisors, parents or guardians, and child development specialists.](Tasks/6471.mdx)
- [Maintain accurate and complete student records as required by laws, district policies, and administrative regulations.](Tasks/6482.mdx)
