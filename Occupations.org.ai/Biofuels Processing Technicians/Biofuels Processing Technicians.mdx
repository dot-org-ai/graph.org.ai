# Biofuels Processing Technicians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-8099.01

Calculate, measure, load, mix, and process refined feedstock with additives in fermentation or reaction process vessels and monitor production process. Perform, and keep records of, plant maintenance, repairs, and safety inspections.

## Tasks

- [Monitor batch, continuous flow, or hybrid biofuels production processes.](Tasks/17830.mdx)
- [Operate valves, pumps, engines, or generators to control and adjust biofuels production.](Tasks/17821.mdx)
- [Monitor and record biofuels processing data.](Tasks/17828.mdx)
- [Collect biofuels samples and perform routine laboratory tests or analyses to assess biofuels quality.](Tasks/17825.mdx)
- [Operate equipment, such as a centrifuge, to extract biofuels products and secondary by-products or reusable fractions.](Tasks/17820.mdx)
- [Process refined feedstock with additives in fermentation or reaction process vessels.](Tasks/17822.mdx)
- [Operate chemical processing equipment for the production of biofuels.](Tasks/17819.mdx)
- [Monitor and record flow meter performance.](Tasks/17829.mdx)
- [Inspect biofuels plant or processing equipment regularly, recording or reporting damage and mechanical problems.](Tasks/17826.mdx)
- [Measure and monitor raw biofuels feedstock.](Tasks/17827.mdx)
