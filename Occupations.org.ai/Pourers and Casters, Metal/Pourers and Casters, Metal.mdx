# Pourers and Casters, Metal

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-4052.00

Operate hand-controlled mechanisms to pour and regulate the flow of molten metal into molds to produce castings or ingots.

## Tasks

- [Pour and regulate the flow of molten metal into molds and forms to produce ingots or other castings, using ladles or hand-controlled mechanisms.](Tasks/12007.mdx)
- [Read temperature gauges and observe color changes, adjusting furnace flames, torches, or electrical heating units as necessary to melt metal to specifications.](Tasks/12008.mdx)
- [Remove solidified steel or slag from pouring nozzles, using long bars or oxygen burners.](Tasks/12016.mdx)
- [Examine molds to ensure they are clean, smooth, and properly coated.](Tasks/12009.mdx)
- [Collect samples, or signal workers to sample metal for analysis.](Tasks/12006.mdx)
- [Load specified amounts of metal and flux into furnaces or clay crucibles.](Tasks/12015.mdx)
- [Position equipment such as ladles, grinding wheels, pouring nozzles, or crucibles, or signal other workers to position equipment.](Tasks/12010.mdx)
- [Skim slag or remove excess metal from ingots or equipment, using hand tools, strainers, rakes, or burners, collecting scrap for recycling.](Tasks/12011.mdx)
- [Transport metal ingots to storage areas, using forklifts.](Tasks/12019.mdx)
- [Assemble and embed cores in casting frames, using hand tools and equipment.](Tasks/12017.mdx)
