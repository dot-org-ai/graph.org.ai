# Graders and Sorters, Agricultural Products

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 45-2041.00

Grade, sort, or classify unprocessed food and other agricultural products by size, weight, color, or condition.

## Tasks

- [Place products in containers according to grade and mark grades on containers.](Tasks/13352.mdx)
- [Weigh products or estimate their weight, visually or by feel.](Tasks/13351.mdx)
- [Discard inferior or defective products or foreign matter, and place acceptable products in containers for further processing.](Tasks/13350.mdx)
- [Grade and sort products according to factors such as color, species, length, width, appearance, feel, smell, and quality to ensure correct processing and usage.](Tasks/13349.mdx)
- [Record grade or identification numbers on tags or on shipping, receiving, or sales sheets.](Tasks/13353.mdx)
