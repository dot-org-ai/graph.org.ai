# Driver/Sales Workers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 53-3031.00

Drive truck or other vehicle over established routes or within an established territory and sell or deliver goods, such as food products, including restaurant take-out items, or pick up or deliver items such as commercial laundry. May also take orders, collect payment, or stock merchandise at point of delivery.

## Tasks

- [Drive trucks to deliver such items as food, medical supplies, or newspapers.](Tasks/3158.mdx)
- [Inform regular customers of new products or services and price changes.](Tasks/3156.mdx)
- [Record sales or delivery information on daily sales or delivery record.](Tasks/3161.mdx)
- [Listen to and resolve customers' complaints regarding products or services.](Tasks/3155.mdx)
- [Collect money from customers, make change, and record transactions on customer receipts.](Tasks/3154.mdx)
- [Maintain trucks and food-dispensing equipment and clean inside of machines that dispense food or beverages.](Tasks/3164.mdx)
- [Arrange merchandise and sales promotion displays or issue sales promotion materials to customers.](Tasks/3163.mdx)
- [Collect coins from vending machines, refill machines, and remove aged merchandise.](Tasks/3159.mdx)
- [Write customer orders and sales contracts according to company guidelines.](Tasks/3157.mdx)
- [Review lists of dealers, customers, or station drops and load trucks.](Tasks/3162.mdx)
