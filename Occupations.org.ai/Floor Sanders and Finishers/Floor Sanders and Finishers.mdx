# Floor Sanders and Finishers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-2043.00

Scrape and sand wooden floors to smooth surfaces using floor scraper and floor sanding machine, and apply coats of finish.

## Tasks

- [Buff and vacuum floors to ensure their cleanliness prior to the application of finish.](Tasks/23933.mdx)
- [Scrape and sand floor edges and areas inaccessible to floor sanders, using scrapers, disk-type sanders, and sandpaper.](Tasks/14870.mdx)
- [Inspect floors for smoothness.](Tasks/14869.mdx)
- [Attach sandpaper to rollers of sanding machines.](Tasks/14872.mdx)
- [Guide sanding machines over surfaces of floors until surfaces are smooth.](Tasks/14871.mdx)
- [Apply filler compound and coats of finish to floors to seal wood.](Tasks/14873.mdx)
- [Remove excess glue from joints, using knives, scrapers, or wood chisels.](Tasks/14874.mdx)
