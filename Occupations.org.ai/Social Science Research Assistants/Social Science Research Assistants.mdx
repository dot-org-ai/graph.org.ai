# Social Science Research Assistants

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 19-4061.00

Assist social scientists in laboratory, survey, and other social science research. May help prepare findings for publication and assist in laboratory analysis, quality control, or data management.

## Tasks

- [Design and create special programs for tasks such as statistical analysis and data entry and cleaning.](Tasks/12963.mdx)
- [Provide assistance with the preparation of project-related reports, manuscripts, and presentations.](Tasks/12953.mdx)
- [Prepare tables, graphs, fact sheets, and written reports summarizing research results.](Tasks/12957.mdx)
- [Perform descriptive and multivariate statistical analyses of data, using computer software.](Tasks/12955.mdx)
- [Verify the accuracy and validity of data entered in databases, correcting any errors.](Tasks/12956.mdx)
- [Develop and implement research quality control procedures.](Tasks/12959.mdx)
- [Prepare, manipulate, and manage extensive databases.](Tasks/12952.mdx)
- [Perform data entry and other clerical work as required for project completion.](Tasks/12962.mdx)
- [Conduct internet-based and library research.](Tasks/12960.mdx)
- [Present research findings to groups of people.](Tasks/12961.mdx)
