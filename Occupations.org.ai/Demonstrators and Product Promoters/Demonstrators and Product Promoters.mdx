# Demonstrators and Product Promoters

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 41-9011.00

Demonstrate merchandise and answer questions for the purpose of creating public interest in buying the product. May sell demonstrated merchandise.

## Tasks

- [Provide product samples, coupons, informational brochures, or other incentives to persuade people to buy products.](Tasks/13191.mdx)
- [Sell products being promoted and keep records of sales.](Tasks/13194.mdx)
- [Keep areas neat while working and return items to correct locations following demonstrations.](Tasks/13192.mdx)
- [Demonstrate or explain products, methods, or services to persuade customers to purchase products or use services.](Tasks/13190.mdx)
- [Record and report demonstration-related information, such as the number of questions asked by the audience or the number of coupons distributed.](Tasks/13193.mdx)
- [Suggest specific product purchases to meet customers' needs.](Tasks/13196.mdx)
- [Research or investigate products to be presented to prepare for demonstrations.](Tasks/13206.mdx)
- [Set up and arrange displays or demonstration areas to attract the attention of prospective customers.](Tasks/13195.mdx)
- [Identify interested and qualified customers to provide them with additional information.](Tasks/13198.mdx)
- [Visit trade shows, stores, community organizations, or other venues to demonstrate products or services or to answer questions from potential customers.](Tasks/13203.mdx)
