# Environmental Engineers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 17-2081.00

Research, design, plan, or perform engineering duties in the prevention, control, and remediation of environmental hazards using various engineering disciplines. Work may include waste treatment, site remediation, or pollution control technology.

## Tasks

- [Design, or supervise the design of, systems, processes, or equipment for control, management, or remediation of water, air, or soil quality.](Tasks/15210.mdx)
- [Assess the existing or potential environmental impact of land use projects on air, water, or land.](Tasks/1377.mdx)
- [Collaborate with environmental scientists, planners, hazardous waste technicians, engineers, experts in law or business, or other specialists to address environmental problems.](Tasks/20195.mdx)
- [Advise corporations or government agencies of procedures to follow in cleaning up contaminated sites to protect people and the environment.](Tasks/1374.mdx)
- [Develop proposed project objectives and targets and report to management on progress in attaining them.](Tasks/1373.mdx)
- [Monitor progress of environmental improvement programs.](Tasks/1370.mdx)
- [Prepare, review, or update environmental investigation or recommendation reports.](Tasks/1366.mdx)
- [Prepare, maintain, or revise quality assurance documentation or procedures.](Tasks/20197.mdx)
- [Develop site-specific health and safety protocols, such as spill contingency plans or methods for loading or transporting waste.](Tasks/1379.mdx)
- [Provide technical support for environmental remediation or litigation projects, including remediation system design or determination of regulatory applicability.](Tasks/1369.mdx)
