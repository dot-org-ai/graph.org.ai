# Computer, Automated Teller, and Office Machine Repairers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 49-2011.00

Repair, maintain, or install computers, word processing systems, automated teller machines, and electronic office machines, such as duplicating and fax machines.

## Tasks

- [Reassemble machines after making repairs or replacing parts.](Tasks/11624.mdx)
- [Converse with customers to determine details of equipment problems.](Tasks/11623.mdx)
- [Disassemble machines to examine parts, such as wires, gears, or bearings for wear or defects, using hand or power tools and measuring devices.](Tasks/11639.mdx)
- [Advise customers concerning equipment operation, maintenance, or programming.](Tasks/11627.mdx)
- [Align, adjust, or calibrate equipment according to specifications.](Tasks/11635.mdx)
- [Repair, adjust, or replace electrical or mechanical components or parts, using hand tools, power tools, or soldering or welding equipment.](Tasks/11637.mdx)
- [Travel to customers' stores or offices to service machines or to provide emergency repair service.](Tasks/11625.mdx)
- [Maintain parts inventories and order any additional parts needed for repairs.](Tasks/11633.mdx)
- [Operate machines to test functioning of parts or mechanisms.](Tasks/11630.mdx)
- [Reinstall software programs or adjust settings on existing software to fix machine malfunctions.](Tasks/11626.mdx)
