# Regulatory Affairs Managers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 11-9199.01

Plan, direct, or coordinate production activities of an organization to ensure compliance with regulations and standard operating procedures.

## Tasks

- [Develop regulatory strategies and implementation plans for the preparation and submission of new products.](Tasks/18026.mdx)
- [Review all regulatory agency submission materials to ensure timeliness, accuracy, comprehensiveness, or compliance with regulatory standards.](Tasks/18037.mdx)
- [Direct the preparation and submission of regulatory agency applications, reports, or correspondence.](Tasks/18022.mdx)
- [Investigate product complaints and prepare documentation and submissions to appropriate regulatory agencies as necessary.](Tasks/18029.mdx)
- [Provide responses to regulatory agencies regarding product information or issues.](Tasks/18035.mdx)
- [Represent organizations before domestic or international regulatory agencies on major policy matters or decisions regarding company products.](Tasks/18036.mdx)
- [Provide regulatory guidance to departments or development project teams regarding design, development, evaluation, or marketing of products.](Tasks/18024.mdx)
- [Manage activities such as audits, regulatory agency inspections, or product recalls.](Tasks/18031.mdx)
- [Communicate regulatory information to multiple departments and ensure that information is interpreted correctly.](Tasks/18025.mdx)
- [Maintain current knowledge of relevant regulations, including proposed and final rules.](Tasks/18030.mdx)
