# Nuclear Engineers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 17-2161.00

Conduct research on nuclear engineering projects or apply principles and theory of nuclear science to problems concerned with release, control, and use of nuclear energy and nuclear waste disposal.

## Tasks

- [Design or develop nuclear equipment, such as reactor cores, radiation shielding, or associated instrumentation or control mechanisms.](Tasks/5384.mdx)
- [Monitor nuclear facility operations to identify any design, construction, or operation practices that violate safety regulations and laws or could jeopardize safe operations.](Tasks/5380.mdx)
- [Initiate corrective actions or order plant shutdowns in emergency situations.](Tasks/5385.mdx)
- [Examine accidents to obtain data for use in design of preventive measures.](Tasks/5379.mdx)
- [Direct operating or maintenance activities of nuclear power plants to ensure efficiency and conformity to safety standards.](Tasks/5389.mdx)
- [Design or oversee construction or operation of nuclear reactors, power plants, or nuclear fuels reprocessing and reclamation systems.](Tasks/5383.mdx)
- [Direct environmental compliance activities associated with nuclear plant operations or maintenance.](Tasks/19642.mdx)
- [Write operational instructions to be used in nuclear plant operation or nuclear fuel or waste handling and disposal.](Tasks/5387.mdx)
- [Prepare technical reports of findings or recommendations, based on synthesized analyses of test results.](Tasks/20956.mdx)
- [Prepare environmental impact statements, reports, or presentations for regulatory or other agencies.](Tasks/19643.mdx)
