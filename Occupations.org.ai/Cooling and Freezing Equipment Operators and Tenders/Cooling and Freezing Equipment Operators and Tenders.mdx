# Cooling and Freezing Equipment Operators and Tenders

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-9193.00

Operate or tend equipment such as cooling and freezing units, refrigerators, batch freezers, and freezing tunnels, to cool or freeze products, food, blood plasma, and chemicals.

## Tasks

- [Record temperatures, amounts of materials processed, or test results on report forms.](Tasks/8544.mdx)
- [Monitor pressure gauges, ammeters, flowmeters, thermometers, or products, and adjust controls to maintain specified conditions, such as feed rate, product consistency, temperature, air pressure, and machine speed.](Tasks/8543.mdx)
- [Read dials and gauges on panel control boards to ascertain temperatures, alkalinities, and densities of mixtures, and turn valves to obtain specified mixtures.](Tasks/8545.mdx)
- [Start machinery, such as pumps, feeders, or conveyors, and turn valves to heat, admit, or transfer products, refrigerants, or mixes.](Tasks/8547.mdx)
- [Correct machinery malfunctions by performing actions such as removing jams, and inform supervisors of malfunctions as necessary.](Tasks/8546.mdx)
- [Assemble equipment, and attach pipes, fittings, or valves, using hand tools.](Tasks/8548.mdx)
- [Measure or weigh specified amounts of ingredients or materials, and load them into tanks, vats, hoppers, or other equipment.](Tasks/8553.mdx)
- [Adjust machine or freezer speed and air intake to obtain desired consistency and amount of product.](Tasks/8542.mdx)
- [Weigh packages and adjust freezer air valves or switches on filler heads to obtain specified amounts of product in each container.](Tasks/8550.mdx)
- [Inspect and flush lines with solutions or steam, and spray equipment with sterilizing solutions.](Tasks/8557.mdx)
