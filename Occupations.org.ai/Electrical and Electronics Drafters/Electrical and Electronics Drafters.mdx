# Electrical and Electronics Drafters

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 17-3012.00

Prepare wiring diagrams, circuit board assembly diagrams, and layout drawings used for the manufacture, installation, or repair of electrical equipment.

## Tasks

- [Draft detail and assembly drawings of design components, circuitry or printed circuit boards, using computer-assisted equipment or standard drafting techniques and devices.](Tasks/21951.mdx)
- [Draft working drawings, wiring diagrams, wiring connection specifications, or cross-sections of underground cables, as required for instructions to installation crew.](Tasks/21945.mdx)
- [Assemble documentation packages and produce drawing sets to be checked by an engineer or an architect.](Tasks/21946.mdx)
- [Review completed construction drawings and cost estimates for accuracy and conformity to standards and regulations.](Tasks/21948.mdx)
- [Consult with engineers to discuss or interpret design concepts, or determine requirements of detailed working drawings.](Tasks/21947.mdx)
- [Confer with engineering staff and other personnel to resolve problems.](Tasks/21950.mdx)
- [Measure factors that affect installation and arrangement of equipment, such as distances to be spanned by wire and cable.](Tasks/21952.mdx)
- [Design electrical systems, such as lighting systems.](Tasks/21954.mdx)
- [Draw master sketches to scale showing relation of proposed installations to existing facilities and exact specifications and dimensions.](Tasks/21955.mdx)
- [Review work orders or procedural manuals and confer with vendors or design staff to resolve problems or modify design.](Tasks/21958.mdx)
