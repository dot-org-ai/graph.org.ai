# Packaging and Filling Machine Operators and Tenders

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-9111.00

Operate or tend machines to prepare industrial or consumer products for storage or shipment. Includes cannery workers who pack food products.

## Tasks

- [Attach identification labels to finished packaged items, or cut stencils and stencil information on containers, such as lot numbers or shipping destinations.](Tasks/3142.mdx)
- [Sort, grade, weigh, and inspect products, verifying and adjusting product weight or measurement to meet specifications.](Tasks/3134.mdx)
- [Stop or reset machines when malfunctions occur, clear machine jams, and report malfunctions to a supervisor.](Tasks/3129.mdx)
- [Observe machine operations to ensure quality and conformity of filled or packaged products to standards.](Tasks/3124.mdx)
- [Remove finished packaged items from machine and separate rejected items.](Tasks/3127.mdx)
- [Monitor the production line, watching for problems such as pile-ups, jams, or glue that isn't sticking properly.](Tasks/3136.mdx)
- [Inspect and remove defective products and packaging material.](Tasks/3132.mdx)
- [Start machine by engaging controls.](Tasks/3138.mdx)
- [Tend or operate machine that packages product.](Tasks/3126.mdx)
- [Clean, oil, and make minor adjustments or repairs to machinery and equipment, such as opening valves or setting guides.](Tasks/3135.mdx)
