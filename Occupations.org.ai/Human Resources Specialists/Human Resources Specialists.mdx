# Human Resources Specialists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 13-1071.00

Recruit, screen, interview, or place individuals within an organization. May perform other activities in multiple human resources areas.

## Tasks

- [Interpret and explain human resources policies, procedures, laws, standards, or regulations.](Tasks/18861.mdx)
- [Hire employees and process hiring-related paperwork.](Tasks/18859.mdx)
- [Maintain current knowledge of Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO) and affirmative action guidelines and laws, such as the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA).](Tasks/18864.mdx)
- [Prepare or maintain employment records related to events, such as hiring, termination, leaves, transfers, or promotions, using human resources management system software.](Tasks/18866.mdx)
- [Address employee relations issues, such as harassment allegations, work complaints, or other employee concerns.](Tasks/18852.mdx)
- [Review employment applications and job orders to match applicants with job requirements.](Tasks/18868.mdx)
- [Inform job applicants of details such as duties and responsibilities, compensation, benefits, schedules, working conditions, or promotion opportunities.](Tasks/18860.mdx)
- [Select qualified job applicants or refer them to managers, making hiring recommendations when appropriate.](Tasks/18871.mdx)
- [Schedule or conduct new employee orientations.](Tasks/18870.mdx)
- [Maintain and update human resources documents, such as organizational charts, employee handbooks or directories, or performance evaluation forms.](Tasks/18863.mdx)
