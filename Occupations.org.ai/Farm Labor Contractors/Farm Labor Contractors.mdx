# Farm Labor Contractors

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 13-1074.00

Recruit and hire seasonal or temporary agricultural laborers. May transport, house, and provide meals for workers.

## Tasks

- [Pay wages of contracted farm laborers.](Tasks/14838.mdx)
- [Provide food, drinking water, and field sanitation facilities to contracted workers.](Tasks/14839.mdx)
- [Recruit and hire agricultural workers.](Tasks/14840.mdx)
- [Employ foremen to deal directly with workers when recruiting, hiring, instructing, assigning tasks, and enforcing work rules.](Tasks/14841.mdx)
- [Supervise the work of contracted employees.](Tasks/14842.mdx)
- [Furnish tools for employee use.](Tasks/14843.mdx)
- [Direct and transport workers to appropriate work sites.](Tasks/14844.mdx)
