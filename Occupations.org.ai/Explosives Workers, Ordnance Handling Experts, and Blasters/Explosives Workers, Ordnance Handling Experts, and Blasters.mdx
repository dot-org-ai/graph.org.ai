# Explosives Workers, Ordnance Handling Experts, and Blasters

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-5032.00

Place and detonate explosives to demolish structures or to loosen, remove, or displace earth, rock, or other materials. May perform specialized handling, storage, and accounting procedures.

## Tasks

- [Examine blast areas to determine amounts and kinds of explosive charges needed and to ensure that safety laws are observed.](Tasks/9904.mdx)
- [Tie specified lengths of delaying fuses into patterns in order to time sequences of explosions.](Tasks/9905.mdx)
- [Place safety cones around blast areas to alert other workers of danger zones, and signal workers as necessary to ensure that they clear blast sites prior to explosions.](Tasks/9906.mdx)
- [Place explosive charges in holes or other spots; then detonate explosives to demolish structures or to loosen, remove, or displace earth, rock, or other materials.](Tasks/9907.mdx)
- [Insert, pack, and pour explosives, such as dynamite, ammonium nitrate, black powder, or slurries into blast holes; then shovel drill cuttings, admit water into boreholes, and tamp material to compact charges.](Tasks/9908.mdx)
- [Mark patterns, locations, and depths of charge holes for drilling, and issue drilling instructions.](Tasks/9909.mdx)
- [Compile and keep gun and explosives records in compliance with local and federal laws.](Tasks/9910.mdx)
- [Measure depths of drilled blast holes, using weighted tape measures.](Tasks/9911.mdx)
- [Connect electrical wire to primers, and cover charges or fill blast holes with clay, drill chips, sand, or other material.](Tasks/9912.mdx)
- [Lay primacord between rows of charged blast holes, and tie cord into main lines to form blast patterns.](Tasks/9913.mdx)
