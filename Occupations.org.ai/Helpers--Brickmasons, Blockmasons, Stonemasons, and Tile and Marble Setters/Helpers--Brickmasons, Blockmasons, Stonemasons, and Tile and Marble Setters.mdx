# Helpers--Brickmasons, Blockmasons, Stonemasons, and Tile and Marble Setters

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-3011.00

Help brickmasons, blockmasons, stonemasons, or tile and marble setters by performing duties requiring less skill. Duties include using, supplying, or holding materials or tools, and cleaning work area and equipment.

## Tasks

- [Mix mortar, plaster, and grout, manually or using machines, according to standard formulas.](Tasks/13600.mdx)
- [Erect scaffolding or other installation structures.](Tasks/13598.mdx)
- [Cut materials to specified sizes for installation, using power saws or tile cutters.](Tasks/13597.mdx)
- [Modify material moving, mixing, grouting, grinding, polishing, or cleaning procedures, according to installation or material requirements.](Tasks/13603.mdx)
- [Transport materials, tools, or machines to installation sites, manually or using conveyance equipment.](Tasks/13606.mdx)
- [Provide assistance in the preparation, installation, repair, or rebuilding of tile, brick, or stone surfaces.](Tasks/13604.mdx)
- [Locate and supply materials to masons for installation, following drawings or numbered sequences.](Tasks/13599.mdx)
- [Arrange or store materials, machines, tools, or equipment.](Tasks/13610.mdx)
- [Clean installation surfaces, equipment, tools, work sites, or storage areas, using water, chemical solutions, oxygen lances, or polishing machines.](Tasks/13605.mdx)
- [Move or position materials such as marble slabs, using cranes, hoists, or dollies.](Tasks/13607.mdx)
