# First-Line Supervisors of Firefighting and Prevention Workers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 33-1021.00

Directly supervise and coordinate activities of workers engaged in firefighting and fire prevention and control.

## Tasks

- [Assign firefighters to jobs at strategic locations to facilitate rescue of persons and maximize application of extinguishing agents.](Tasks/22910.mdx)
- [Provide emergency medical services as required, and perform light to heavy rescue functions at emergencies.](Tasks/22911.mdx)
- [Assess nature and extent of fire, condition of building, danger to adjacent buildings, and water supply status to determine crew or company requirements.](Tasks/22912.mdx)
- [Communicate fire details to superiors, subordinates, or interagency dispatch centers, using two-way radios.](Tasks/22913.mdx)
- [Serve as a working leader of an engine, hand, helicopter, or prescribed fire crew of three or more firefighters.](Tasks/22914.mdx)
- [Instruct and drill fire department personnel in assigned duties, including firefighting, medical care, hazardous materials response, fire prevention, and related subjects.](Tasks/22915.mdx)
- [Maintain fire suppression equipment in good condition, checking equipment periodically to ensure that it is ready for use.](Tasks/22916.mdx)
- [Evaluate the performance of assigned firefighting personnel.](Tasks/22917.mdx)
- [Direct the training of firefighters, assigning of instructors to training classes, and providing of supervisors with reports on training progress and status.](Tasks/22918.mdx)
- [Perform maintenance and minor repairs on firefighting equipment, including vehicles, and write and submit proposals to modify, replace, and repair equipment.](Tasks/22919.mdx)
