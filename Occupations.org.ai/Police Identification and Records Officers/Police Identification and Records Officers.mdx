# Police Identification and Records Officers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 33-3021.02

Collect evidence at crime scene, classify and identify fingerprints, and photograph evidence for use in criminal and civil cases.

## Tasks

- [Photograph crime or accident scenes for evidence records.](Tasks/2120.mdx)
- [Maintain records of evidence and write and review reports.](Tasks/18708.mdx)
- [Submit evidence to supervisors, crime labs, or court officials for legal proceedings.](Tasks/18709.mdx)
- [Testify in court and present evidence.](Tasks/2121.mdx)
- [Look for trace evidence, such as fingerprints, hairs, fibers, or shoe impressions, using alternative light sources when necessary.](Tasks/2123.mdx)
- [Dust selected areas of crime scene and lift latent fingerprints, adhering to proper preservation procedures.](Tasks/2122.mdx)
- [Analyze and process evidence at crime scenes, during autopsies, or in the laboratory, wearing protective equipment and using powders and chemicals.](Tasks/21036.mdx)
- [Package, store and retrieve evidence.](Tasks/2125.mdx)
- [Process film and prints from crime or accident scenes.](Tasks/2129.mdx)
- [Take fingerprints.](Tasks/24007.mdx)
