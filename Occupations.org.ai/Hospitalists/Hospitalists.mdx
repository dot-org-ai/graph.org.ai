# Hospitalists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 29-1229.02

Provide inpatient care predominantly in settings such as medical wards, acute care units, intensive care units, rehabilitation centers, or emergency rooms. Manage and coordinate patient care throughout treatment.

## Tasks

- [Diagnose, treat, or provide continuous care to hospital inpatients.](Tasks/17106.mdx)
- [Prescribe medications or treatment regimens to hospital inpatients.](Tasks/17102.mdx)
- [Order or interpret the results of tests such as laboratory tests and radiographs (x-rays).](Tasks/17103.mdx)
- [Admit patients for hospital stays.](Tasks/17107.mdx)
- [Conduct discharge planning and discharge patients.](Tasks/17105.mdx)
- [Write patient discharge summaries and send them to primary care physicians.](Tasks/17099.mdx)
- [Refer patients to medical specialists, social services, or other professionals as appropriate.](Tasks/17096.mdx)
- [Direct, coordinate, or supervise the patient care activities of nursing or support staff.](Tasks/17098.mdx)
- [Attend inpatient consultations in areas of specialty.](Tasks/17104.mdx)
- [Communicate with patients' primary care physicians upon admission, when treatment plans change, or at discharge to maintain continuity and quality of care.](Tasks/17108.mdx)
