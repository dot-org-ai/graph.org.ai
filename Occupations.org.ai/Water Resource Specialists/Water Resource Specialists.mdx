# Water Resource Specialists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 11-9121.02

Design or implement programs and strategies related to water resource issues such as supply, quality, and regulatory compliance issues.

## Tasks

- [Perform hydrologic, hydraulic, or water quality modeling.](Tasks/15618.mdx)
- [Analyze storm water systems to identify opportunities for water resource improvements.](Tasks/15633.mdx)
- [Conduct, or oversee the conduct of, investigations on matters such as water storage, wastewater discharge, pollutants, permits, or other compliance and regulatory issues.](Tasks/15631.mdx)
- [Develop strategies for watershed operations to meet water supply and conservation goals or to ensure regulatory compliance with clean water laws or regulations.](Tasks/15634.mdx)
- [Conduct technical studies for water resources on topics such as pollutants and water treatment options.](Tasks/15630.mdx)
- [Review or evaluate designs for water detention facilities, storm drains, flood control facilities, or other hydraulic structures.](Tasks/15616.mdx)
- [Present water resource proposals to government, public interest groups, or community groups.](Tasks/15624.mdx)
- [Develop plans to protect watershed health or rehabilitate watersheds.](Tasks/15628.mdx)
- [Write proposals, project reports, informational brochures, or other documents on wastewater purification, water supply and demand, or other water resource subjects.](Tasks/15621.mdx)
- [Conduct cost-benefit studies for watershed improvement projects or water management alternatives.](Tasks/15632.mdx)
