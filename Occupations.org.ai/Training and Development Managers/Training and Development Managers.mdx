# Training and Development Managers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 11-3131.00

Plan, direct, or coordinate the training and development activities and staff of an organization.

## Tasks

- [Analyze training needs to develop new training programs or modify and improve existing programs.](Tasks/1015.mdx)
- [Evaluate instructor performance and the effectiveness of training programs, providing recommendations for improvement.](Tasks/1009.mdx)
- [Plan, develop, and provide training and staff development programs, using knowledge of the effectiveness of methods such as classroom training, demonstrations, on-the-job training, meetings, conferences, and workshops.](Tasks/1014.mdx)
- [Confer with management and conduct surveys to identify training needs based on projected production processes, changes, and other factors.](Tasks/1012.mdx)
- [Conduct orientation sessions and arrange on-the-job training for new hires.](Tasks/1008.mdx)
- [Train instructors and supervisors in techniques and skills for training and dealing with employees.](Tasks/1017.mdx)
- [Develop and organize training manuals, multimedia visual aids, and other educational materials.](Tasks/1013.mdx)
- [Prepare training budget for department or organization.](Tasks/1019.mdx)
