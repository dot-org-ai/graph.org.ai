# Motorcycle Mechanics

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 49-3052.00

Diagnose, adjust, repair, or overhaul motorcycles, scooters, mopeds, dirt bikes, or similar motorized vehicles.

## Tasks

- [Mount, balance, change, or check condition or pressure of tires.](Tasks/20651.mdx)
- [Replace defective parts, using hand tools, arbor presses, flexible power presses, or power tools.](Tasks/8353.mdx)
- [Dismantle engines and repair or replace defective parts, such as magnetos, carburetors, or generators.](Tasks/8357.mdx)
- [Connect test panels to engines and measure generator output, ignition timing, or other engine performance indicators.](Tasks/8354.mdx)
- [Listen to engines, examine vehicle frames, or confer with customers to determine nature and extent of malfunction or damage.](Tasks/8355.mdx)
- [Repair or replace other parts, such as headlights, horns, handlebar controls, gasoline or oil tanks, starters, or mufflers.](Tasks/8359.mdx)

- [Reassemble frames and reinstall engines after repairs.](Tasks/20652.mdx)
