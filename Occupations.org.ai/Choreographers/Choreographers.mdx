# Choreographers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 27-2032.00

Create new dance routines. Rehearse performance of routines. May direct and stage presentations.

## Tasks

- [Direct rehearsals to instruct dancers in dance steps and in techniques to achieve desired effects.](Tasks/11028.mdx)
- [Advise dancers on standing and moving properly, teaching correct dance techniques to help prevent injuries.](Tasks/11032.mdx)
- [Teach students, dancers, and other performers about rhythm and interpretive movement.](Tasks/11038.mdx)
- [Record dance movements and their technical aspects, using a technical understanding of the patterns and formations of choreography.](Tasks/11043.mdx)
- [Direct and stage dance presentations for various forms of entertainment.](Tasks/11035.mdx)
- [Choose the music, sound effects, or spoken narrative to accompany a dance.](Tasks/11031.mdx)
- [Experiment with different types of dancers, steps, dances, and placements, testing ideas informally to get feedback from dancers.](Tasks/11040.mdx)
- [Seek influences from other art forms, such as theatre, the visual arts, and architecture.](Tasks/11041.mdx)
- [Develop ideas for creating dances, keeping notes and sketches to record influences.](Tasks/11036.mdx)
- [Coordinate production music with music directors.](Tasks/11033.mdx)
