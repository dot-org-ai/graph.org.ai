# Hydroelectric Production Managers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 11-3051.06

Manage operations at hydroelectric power generation facilities. Maintain and monitor hydroelectric plant equipment for efficient and safe plant operations.

## Tasks

- [Direct operations, maintenance, or repair of hydroelectric power facilities.](Tasks/15520.mdx)
- [Identify and communicate power system emergencies.](Tasks/15515.mdx)
- [Maintain records of hydroelectric facility operations, maintenance, or repairs.](Tasks/15512.mdx)
- [Perform or direct preventive or corrective containment or cleanup to protect the environment.](Tasks/15509.mdx)
- [Monitor or inspect hydroelectric equipment, such as hydro-turbines, generators, or control systems.](Tasks/15513.mdx)
- [Inspect hydroelectric facilities, including switchyards, control houses, or relay houses, for normal operation or adherence to safety standards.](Tasks/15514.mdx)
- [Supervise or monitor hydroelectric facility operations to ensure that generation or mechanical equipment conform to applicable regulations or standards.](Tasks/15519.mdx)
- [Plan or coordinate hydroelectric production operations to meet customer requirements.](Tasks/15508.mdx)
- [Check hydroelectric operations for compliance with prescribed operating limits, such as loads, voltages, temperatures, lines, or equipment.](Tasks/15518.mdx)
- [Develop or implement projects to improve efficiency, economy, or effectiveness of hydroelectric plant operations.](Tasks/15504.mdx)
