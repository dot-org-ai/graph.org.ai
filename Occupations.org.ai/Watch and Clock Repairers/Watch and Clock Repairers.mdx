# Watch and Clock Repairers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 49-9064.00

Repair, clean, and adjust mechanisms of timing instruments, such as watches and clocks. Includes watchmakers, watch technicians, and mechanical timepiece repairers.

## Tasks

- [Clean, rinse, and dry timepiece parts, using solutions and ultrasonic or mechanical watch-cleaning machines.](Tasks/14977.mdx)
- [Adjust timing regulators, using truing calipers, watch-rate recorders, and tweezers.](Tasks/14984.mdx)
- [Reassemble timepieces, replacing glass faces and batteries, before returning them to customers.](Tasks/14979.mdx)
- [Disassemble timepieces and inspect them for defective, worn, misaligned, or rusty parts, using loupes.](Tasks/14978.mdx)
- [Oil moving parts of timepieces.](Tasks/14975.mdx)
- [Estimate repair costs and timepiece values.](Tasks/14982.mdx)
- [Repair or replace broken, damaged, or worn parts on timepieces, using lathes, drill presses, and hand tools.](Tasks/14976.mdx)
- [Test timepiece accuracy and performance, using meters and other electronic instruments.](Tasks/14980.mdx)
- [Perform regular adjustment and maintenance on timepieces, watch cases, and watch bands.](Tasks/14983.mdx)
- [Order supplies, including replacement parts, for timing instruments.](Tasks/21084.mdx)
