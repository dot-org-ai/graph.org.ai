# Industrial Engineers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 17-2112.00

Design, develop, test, and evaluate integrated systems for managing industrial production processes, including human work factors, quality control, inventory control, logistics and material flow, cost analysis, and production coordination.

## Tasks

- [Estimate production costs, cost saving methods, and the effects of product design changes on expenditures for management review, action, and control.](Tasks/18607.mdx)
- [Plan and establish sequence of operations to fabricate and assemble parts or products and to promote efficient utilization.](Tasks/1394.mdx)
- [Analyze statistical data and product specifications to determine standards and establish quality and reliability objectives of finished product.](Tasks/1391.mdx)
- [Confer with clients, vendors, staff, and management personnel regarding purchases, product and production specifications, manufacturing capabilities, or project status.](Tasks/18609.mdx)
- [Communicate with management and user personnel to develop production and design standards.](Tasks/1400.mdx)
- [Evaluate precision and accuracy of production and testing equipment and engineering drawings to formulate corrective action plan.](Tasks/1407.mdx)
- [Recommend methods for improving utilization of personnel, material, and utilities.](Tasks/1393.mdx)
- [Record or oversee recording of information to ensure currency of engineering drawings and documentation of production problems.](Tasks/1403.mdx)
- [Draft and design layout of equipment, materials, and workspace to illustrate maximum efficiency using drafting tools and computer.](Tasks/1398.mdx)
- [Direct workers engaged in product measurement, inspection, and testing activities to ensure quality control and reliability.](Tasks/1405.mdx)
