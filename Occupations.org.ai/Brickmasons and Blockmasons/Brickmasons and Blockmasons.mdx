# Brickmasons and Blockmasons

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-2021.00

Lay and bind building materials, such as brick, structural tile, concrete block, cinder block, glass block, and terra-cotta block, with mortar and other substances, to construct or repair walls, partitions, arches, sewers, and other structures.

## Tasks

- [Measure distance from reference points and mark guidelines to lay out work, using plumb bobs and levels.](Tasks/4769.mdx)
- [Construct corners by fastening in plumb position a corner pole or building a corner pyramid of bricks, and filling in between the corners using a line from corner to corner to guide each course, or layer, of brick.](Tasks/4768.mdx)
- [Apply and smooth mortar or other mixture over work surface.](Tasks/4775.mdx)
- [Calculate angles and courses and determine vertical and horizontal alignment of courses.](Tasks/4770.mdx)
- [Break or cut bricks, tiles, or blocks to size, using trowel edge, hammer, or power saw.](Tasks/4772.mdx)
- [Interpret blueprints and drawings to determine specifications and to calculate the materials required.](Tasks/4774.mdx)
- [Remove excess mortar with trowels and hand tools, and finish mortar joints with jointing tools, for a sealed, uniform appearance.](Tasks/4773.mdx)
- [Fasten or fuse brick or other building material to structure with wire clamps, anchor holes, torch, or cement.](Tasks/4771.mdx)
- [Clean working surface to remove scale, dust, soot, or chips of brick and mortar, using broom, wire brush, or scraper.](Tasks/4778.mdx)
- [Examine brickwork or structure to determine need for repair.](Tasks/4777.mdx)
