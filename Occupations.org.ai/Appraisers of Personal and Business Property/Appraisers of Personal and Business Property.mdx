# Appraisers of Personal and Business Property

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 13-2022.00

Appraise and estimate the fair value of tangible personal or business property, such as jewelry, art, antiques, collectibles, and equipment. May also appraise land.

## Tasks

- [Write descriptions of the property being appraised.](Tasks/21548.mdx)
- [Determine the appropriate type of valuation to make, such as fair market, replacement, or liquidation, based on the needs of the property owner.](Tasks/21537.mdx)
- [Document physical characteristics of property such as measurements, quality, and design.](Tasks/21538.mdx)
- [Calculate the value of property based on comparisons to recent sales, estimated cost to reproduce, and anticipated property income streams.](Tasks/21535.mdx)
- [Locate and record data on sales of comparable property using specialized software, internet searches, or personal records.](Tasks/21541.mdx)
- [Write and submit appraisal reports for property, such as jewelry, art, antiques, collectibles, and equipment.](Tasks/21547.mdx)
- [Inspect personal or business property.](Tasks/21540.mdx)
- [Create and maintain a database of completed appraisals.](Tasks/21536.mdx)
- [Take photographs of property.](Tasks/21543.mdx)
- [Verify that property matches legal descriptions or certifications.](Tasks/21546.mdx)
