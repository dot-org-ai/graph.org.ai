# Musical Instrument Repairers and Tuners

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 49-9063.00

Repair percussion, stringed, reed, or wind instruments. May specialize in one area, such as piano tuning.

## Tasks

- [Adjust string tensions to tune instruments, using hand tools and electronic tuning devices.](Tasks/11866.mdx)
- [Compare instrument pitches with tuning tool pitches to tune instruments.](Tasks/11871.mdx)
- [Play instruments to evaluate their sound quality and to locate any defects.](Tasks/11865.mdx)
- [Disassemble instruments and parts for repair and adjustment.](Tasks/11867.mdx)
- [Repair or replace musical instrument parts and components, such as strings, bridges, felts, and keys, using hand and power tools.](Tasks/11873.mdx)
- [Reassemble instruments following repair, using hand tools and power tools and glue, hair, yarn, resin, or clamps, and lubricate instruments as necessary.](Tasks/11870.mdx)
- [Inspect instruments to locate defects, and to determine their value or the level of restoration required.](Tasks/11868.mdx)
- [Shape old parts and replacement parts to improve tone or intonation, using hand tools, lathes, or soldering irons.](Tasks/11875.mdx)
- [String instruments, and adjust trusses and bridges of instruments to obtain specified string tensions and heights.](Tasks/11872.mdx)
- [Repair cracks in wood or metal instruments, using pinning wire, lathes, fillers, clamps, or soldering irons.](Tasks/11869.mdx)
