# Electro-Mechanical and Mechatronics Technologists and Technicians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 17-3024.00

Operate, test, maintain, or adjust unmanned, automated, servomechanical, or electromechanical equipment. May operate unmanned submarines, aircraft, or other equipment to observe or record visual information at sites such as oil rigs, crop fields, buildings, or for similar infrastructure, deep ocean exploration, or hazardous waste removal. May assist engineers in testing and designing robotics equipment.

## Tasks

- [Test performance of electromechanical assemblies, using test instruments such as oscilloscopes, electronic voltmeters, or bridges.](Tasks/10892.mdx)
- [Install or program computer hardware or machine or instrumentation software in microprocessor-based systems.](Tasks/22014.mdx)
- [Read blueprints, schematics, diagrams, or technical orders to determine methods and sequences of assembly.](Tasks/10893.mdx)
- [Modify, maintain, or repair electrical, electronic, or mechanical components, equipment, or systems to ensure proper functioning.](Tasks/22015.mdx)
- [Inspect parts for surface defects.](Tasks/10896.mdx)

- [Fabricate or assemble mechanical, electrical, or electronic components or assemblies.](Tasks/22012.mdx)
- [Align, fit, or assemble component parts, using hand or power tools, fixtures, templates, or microscopes.](Tasks/10895.mdx)
