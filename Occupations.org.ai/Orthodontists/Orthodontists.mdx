# Orthodontists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 29-1023.00

Examine, diagnose, and treat dental malocclusions and oral cavity anomalies. Design and fabricate appliances to realign teeth and jaws to produce and maintain normal function and to improve appearance.

## Tasks

- [Diagnose teeth and jaw or other dental-facial abnormalities.](Tasks/7734.mdx)
- [Examine patients to assess abnormalities of jaw development, tooth position, and other dental-facial structures.](Tasks/7735.mdx)
- [Study diagnostic records, such as medical or dental histories, plaster models of the teeth, photos of a patient's face and teeth, and X-rays, to develop patient treatment plans.](Tasks/7733.mdx)
- [Fit dental appliances in patients' mouths to alter the position and relationship of teeth and jaws or to realign teeth.](Tasks/7732.mdx)
- [Adjust dental appliances to produce and maintain normal function.](Tasks/7737.mdx)
- [Provide patients with proposed treatment plans and cost estimates.](Tasks/7738.mdx)
- [Advise patients to comply with treatment plans.](Tasks/23973.mdx)
- [Prepare diagnostic and treatment records.](Tasks/7736.mdx)
- [Instruct dental officers and technical assistants in orthodontic procedures and techniques.](Tasks/7739.mdx)
- [Coordinate orthodontic services with other dental and medical services.](Tasks/7740.mdx)
