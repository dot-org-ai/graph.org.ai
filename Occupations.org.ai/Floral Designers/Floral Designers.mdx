# Floral Designers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 27-1023.00

Design, cut, and arrange live, dried, or artificial flowers and foliage.

## Tasks

- [Confer with clients regarding price and type of arrangement desired and the date, time, and place of delivery.](Tasks/1719.mdx)
- [Select flora and foliage for arrangements, working with numerous combinations to synthesize and develop new creations.](Tasks/1722.mdx)
- [Order and purchase flowers and supplies from wholesalers and growers.](Tasks/1723.mdx)
- [Deliver arrangements to customers, or oversee employees responsible for deliveries.](Tasks/20751.mdx)
- [Plan arrangement according to client's requirements, using knowledge of design and properties of materials, or select appropriate standard design pattern.](Tasks/1720.mdx)
- [Water plants, and cut, condition, and clean flowers and foliage for storage.](Tasks/1721.mdx)
- [Trim material and arrange bouquets, wreaths, terrariums, and other items, using trimmers, shapers, wire, pins, floral tape, foam, and other materials.](Tasks/1725.mdx)
- [Wrap and price completed arrangements.](Tasks/1724.mdx)
- [Perform office and retail service duties, such as keeping financial records, serving customers, answering telephones, selling giftware items, and receiving payment.](Tasks/1726.mdx)
- [Unpack stock as it comes into the shop.](Tasks/1730.mdx)
