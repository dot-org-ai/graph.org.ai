# Chiropractors

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 29-1011.00

Assess, treat, and care for patients by manipulation of spine and musculoskeletal system. May provide spinal adjustment or address sacral or pelvic misalignment.

## Tasks

- [Evaluate the functioning of the neuromuscularskeletal system and the spine using systems of chiropractic diagnosis.](Tasks/6914.mdx)
- [Diagnose health problems by reviewing patients' health and medical histories, questioning, observing, and examining patients and interpreting x-rays.](Tasks/6915.mdx)
- [Perform a series of manual adjustments to the spine or other articulations of the body to correct the musculoskeletal system.](Tasks/6913.mdx)
- [Obtain and record patients' medical histories.](Tasks/6918.mdx)
- [Maintain accurate case histories of patients.](Tasks/6916.mdx)
- [Advise patients about recommended courses of treatment.](Tasks/6917.mdx)
- [Analyze x-rays to locate the sources of patients' difficulties and to rule out fractures or diseases as sources of problems.](Tasks/6919.mdx)
- [Counsel patients about nutrition, exercise, sleeping habits, stress management, or other matters.](Tasks/6920.mdx)
- [Consult with or refer patients to appropriate health practitioners when necessary.](Tasks/6922.mdx)
- [Recommend and arrange for diagnostic procedures, such as blood chemistry tests, saliva tests, x-rays, or other imaging procedures.](Tasks/20403.mdx)
