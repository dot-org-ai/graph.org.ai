# Fire-Prevention and Protection Engineers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 17-2111.02

Research causes of fires, determine fire protection methods, and design or recommend materials or equipment such as structural components or fire-detection equipment to assist organizations in safeguarding life and property against fire, explosion, and related hazards.

## Tasks

- [Advise architects, builders, and other construction personnel on fire prevention equipment and techniques and on fire code and standard interpretation and compliance.](Tasks/8984.mdx)
- [Design fire detection equipment, alarm systems, and fire extinguishing devices and systems.](Tasks/8982.mdx)
- [Inspect buildings or building designs to determine fire protection system requirements and potential problems in areas such as water supplies, exit locations, and construction materials.](Tasks/8983.mdx)
- [Review building plans to verify compliance with fire code.](Tasks/24004.mdx)
- [Prepare and write reports detailing specific fire prevention and protection issues, such as work performed, revised codes or standards, and proposed review schedules.](Tasks/20383.mdx)
- [Consult with authorities to discuss safety regulations and to recommend changes as necessary.](Tasks/8988.mdx)
- [Evaluate fire department performance and the laws and regulations affecting fire prevention or fire safety.](Tasks/8993.mdx)
- [Direct the purchase, modification, installation, testing, maintenance, and operation of fire prevention and protection systems.](Tasks/23902.mdx)
- [Attend workshops, seminars, or conferences to present or obtain information regarding fire prevention and protection.](Tasks/8991.mdx)
- [Determine causes of fires and ways in which they could have been prevented.](Tasks/8986.mdx)
