# Etchers and Engravers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-9194.00

Engrave or etch metal, wood, rubber, or other materials. Includes such workers as etcher-circuit processors, pantograph engravers, and silk screen etchers.

## Tasks

- [Inspect etched work for depth of etching, uniformity, and defects, using calibrated microscopes, gauges, fingers, or magnifying lenses.](Tasks/14395.mdx)
- [Examine sketches, diagrams, samples, blueprints, or photographs to decide how designs are to be etched, cut, or engraved onto workpieces.](Tasks/14386.mdx)
- [Clean and polish engraved areas.](Tasks/14385.mdx)
- [Prepare workpieces for etching or engraving by cutting, sanding, cleaning, polishing, or treating them with wax, acid resist, lime, etching powder, or light-sensitive enamel.](Tasks/14396.mdx)
- [Engrave and print patterns, designs, etchings, trademarks, or lettering onto flat or curved surfaces of a wide variety of metal, glass, plastic, or paper items, using hand tools or hand-held power tools.](Tasks/14379.mdx)
- [Prepare etching chemicals according to formulas, diluting acid with water to obtain solutions of specified concentration.](Tasks/14388.mdx)
- [Use computer software to design patterns for engraving.](Tasks/21027.mdx)
- [Expose workpieces to acid to develop etch patterns such as designs, lettering, or figures.](Tasks/14409.mdx)
- [Adjust depths and sizes of cuts by adjusting heights of worktables, or by adjusting machine-arm gauges.](Tasks/14378.mdx)
- [Measure and compute dimensions of lettering, designs, or patterns to be engraved.](Tasks/14383.mdx)
