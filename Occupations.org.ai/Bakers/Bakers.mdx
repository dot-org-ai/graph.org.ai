# Bakers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-3011.00

Mix and bake ingredients to produce breads, rolls, cookies, cakes, pies, pastries, or other baked goods.

## Tasks

- [Check products for quality, and identify damaged or expired goods.](Tasks/20819.mdx)
- [Set oven temperatures, and place items into hot ovens for baking.](Tasks/11916.mdx)
- [Combine measured ingredients in bowls of mixing, blending, or cooking machinery.](Tasks/11917.mdx)
- [Place dough in pans, molds, or on sheets, and bake in production ovens or on grills.](Tasks/11920.mdx)
- [Set time and speed controls for mixing machines, blending machines, or steam kettles so that ingredients will be mixed or cooked according to instructions.](Tasks/11926.mdx)
- [Measure or weigh flour or other ingredients to prepare batters, doughs, fillings, or icings, using scales or graduated containers.](Tasks/11918.mdx)
- [Observe color of products being baked, and adjust oven temperatures, humidity, or conveyor speeds accordingly.](Tasks/11915.mdx)
- [Check the quality of raw materials to ensure that standards and specifications are met.](Tasks/11921.mdx)
- [Check equipment to ensure that it meets health and safety regulations, and perform maintenance or cleaning, as necessary.](Tasks/11924.mdx)
- [Adapt the quantity of ingredients to match the amount of items to be baked.](Tasks/11922.mdx)
