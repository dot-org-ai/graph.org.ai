# Structural Metal Fabricators and Fitters

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-2041.00

Fabricate, position, align, and fit parts of structural metal products.

## Tasks

- [Verify conformance of workpieces to specifications, using squares, rulers, and measuring tapes.](Tasks/10036.mdx)
- [Study engineering drawings and blueprints to determine materials requirements and task sequences.](Tasks/10042.mdx)
- [Position, align, fit, and weld parts to form complete units or subunits, following blueprints and layout specifications, and using jigs, welding torches, and hand tools.](Tasks/10035.mdx)
- [Lay out and examine metal stock or workpieces to be processed to ensure that specifications are met.](Tasks/10038.mdx)
- [Tack-weld fitted parts together.](Tasks/10037.mdx)
- [Move parts into position, manually or with hoists or cranes.](Tasks/10043.mdx)
- [Set up and operate fabricating machines, such as brakes, rolls, shears, flame cutters, grinders, and drill presses, to bend, cut, form, punch, drill, or otherwise form and assemble metal components.](Tasks/10044.mdx)
- [Position or tighten braces, jacks, clamps, ropes, or bolt straps, or bolt parts in position for welding or riveting.](Tasks/10041.mdx)
- [Lift or move materials and finished products, using large cranes.](Tasks/10053.mdx)
- [Set up face blocks, jigs, and fixtures.](Tasks/10050.mdx)
