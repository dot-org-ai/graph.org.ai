# Transit and Railroad Police

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 33-3052.00

Protect and police railroad and transit property, employees, or passengers.

## Tasks

- [Prepare reports documenting investigation activities and results.](Tasks/9478.mdx)
- [Monitor transit areas and conduct security checks to protect railroad properties, patrons, and employees.](Tasks/21002.mdx)
- [Apprehend or remove trespassers or thieves from railroad property or coordinate with law enforcement agencies in apprehensions and removals.](Tasks/9477.mdx)
- [Direct security activities at derailments, fires, floods, or strikes involving railroad property.](Tasks/9480.mdx)
- [Patrol railroad yards, cars, stations, or other facilities to protect company property or shipments and to maintain order.](Tasks/9475.mdx)
- [Investigate or direct investigations of freight theft, suspicious damage or loss of passengers' valuables, or other crimes on railroad property.](Tasks/9479.mdx)
- [Examine credentials of unauthorized persons attempting to enter secured areas.](Tasks/9476.mdx)
- [Enforce traffic laws regarding the transit system and reprimand individuals who violate them.](Tasks/21003.mdx)
- [Provide training to the public or law enforcement personnel in railroad safety or security.](Tasks/21004.mdx)
- [Plan or implement special safety or preventive programs, such as fire or accident prevention.](Tasks/9484.mdx)
