# Food Cooking Machine Operators and Tenders

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-3093.00

Operate or tend cooking equipment, such as steam cooking vats, deep fry cookers, pressure cookers, kettles, and boilers, to prepare food products.

## Tasks

- [Clean, wash, and sterilize equipment and cooking area, using water hoses, cleaning or sterilizing solutions, or rinses.](Tasks/4949.mdx)
- [Read work orders, recipes, or formulas to determine cooking times and temperatures, and ingredient specifications.](Tasks/4948.mdx)
- [Observe gauges, dials, and product characteristics, and adjust controls to maintain appropriate temperature, pressure, and flow of ingredients.](Tasks/4947.mdx)
- [Measure or weigh ingredients, using scales or measuring containers.](Tasks/4952.mdx)
- [Tend or operate and control equipment, such as kettles, cookers, vats and tanks, and boilers, to cook ingredients or prepare products for further processing.](Tasks/4951.mdx)
- [Record production and test data, such as processing steps, temperature and steam readings, cooking time, batches processed, and test results.](Tasks/4944.mdx)
- [Set temperature, pressure, and time controls, and start conveyers, machines, or pumps.](Tasks/4950.mdx)
- [Remove cooked material or products from equipment.](Tasks/4954.mdx)
- [Collect and examine product samples during production to test them for quality, color, content, consistency, viscosity, acidity, or specific gravity.](Tasks/4946.mdx)
- [Pour, dump, or load prescribed quantities of ingredients or products into cooking equipment, manually or using a hoist.](Tasks/4958.mdx)
