# Training and Development Specialists

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 13-1151.00

Design or conduct work-related training and development programs to improve individual skills or organizational performance. May analyze organizational training needs or evaluate training effectiveness.

## Tasks

- [Present information with a variety of instructional techniques or formats, such as role playing, simulations, team exercises, group discussions, videos, or lectures.](Tasks/94.mdx)
- [Obtain, organize, or develop training procedure manuals, guides, or course materials, such as handouts or visual materials.](Tasks/20475.mdx)
- [Evaluate modes of training delivery, such as in-person or virtual, to optimize training effectiveness, training costs, or environmental impacts.](Tasks/19559.mdx)
- [Offer specific training programs to help workers maintain or improve job skills.](Tasks/97.mdx)
- [Assess training needs through surveys, interviews with employees, focus groups, or consultation with managers, instructors, or customer representatives.](Tasks/103.mdx)
- [Monitor, evaluate, or record training activities or program effectiveness.](Tasks/98.mdx)
- [Design, plan, organize, or direct orientation and training programs for employees or customers.](Tasks/20474.mdx)
- [Develop alternative training methods if expected improvements are not seen.](Tasks/102.mdx)
- [Evaluate training materials prepared by instructors, such as outlines, text, or handouts.](Tasks/101.mdx)
- [Monitor training costs and prepare budget reports to justify expenditures.](Tasks/20476.mdx)
