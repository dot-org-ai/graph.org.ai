# Postal Service Mail Carriers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 43-5052.00

Sort and deliver mail for the United States Postal Service (USPS). Deliver mail on established route by vehicle or on foot. Includes postal service mail carriers employed by USPS contractors.

## Tasks

- [Scan labels on letters or parcels to confirm receipt.](Tasks/20906.mdx)
- [Obtain signed receipts for registered, certified, and insured mail, collect associated charges, and complete any necessary paperwork.](Tasks/11341.mdx)
- [Return to the post office with mail collected from homes, businesses, and public mailboxes.](Tasks/11344.mdx)
- [Sort mail for delivery, arranging it in delivery sequence.](Tasks/11342.mdx)
- [Deliver mail to residences and business establishments along specified routes by walking or driving, using a combination of satchels, carts, cars, and small trucks.](Tasks/11343.mdx)
- [Meet schedules for the collection and return of mail.](Tasks/11351.mdx)
- [Sign for cash-on-delivery and registered mail before leaving the post office.](Tasks/11346.mdx)
- [Hold mail for customers who are away from delivery locations.](Tasks/11348.mdx)
- [Turn in money and receipts collected along mail routes.](Tasks/11345.mdx)
- [Leave notices telling patrons where to collect mail that could not be delivered.](Tasks/11350.mdx)
