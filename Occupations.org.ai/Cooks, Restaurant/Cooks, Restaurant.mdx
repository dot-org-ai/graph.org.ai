# Cooks, Restaurant

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 35-2014.00

Prepare, season, and cook dishes such as soups, meats, vegetables, or desserts in restaurants. May order supplies, keep records and accounts, price items on menu, or plan menu.

## Tasks

- [Ensure food is stored and cooked at correct temperature by regulating temperature of ovens, broilers, grills, and roasters.](Tasks/18723.mdx)
- [Inspect and clean food preparation areas, such as equipment, work surfaces, and serving areas, to ensure safe and sanitary food-handling practices.](Tasks/18722.mdx)
- [Portion, arrange, and garnish food, and serve food to waiters or patrons.](Tasks/2173.mdx)
- [Ensure freshness of food and ingredients by checking for quality, keeping track of old and new items, and rotating stock.](Tasks/18724.mdx)
- [Season and cook food according to recipes or personal judgment and experience.](Tasks/2170.mdx)
- [Coordinate and supervise work of kitchen staff.](Tasks/2180.mdx)
- [Bake, roast, broil, and steam meats, fish, vegetables, and other foods.](Tasks/2176.mdx)
- [Weigh, measure, and mix ingredients according to recipes or personal judgment, using various kitchen utensils and equipment.](Tasks/2172.mdx)
- [Turn or stir foods to ensure even cooking.](Tasks/2169.mdx)
- [Observe and test foods to determine if they have been cooked sufficiently, using methods such as tasting, smelling, or piercing them with utensils.](Tasks/2171.mdx)
