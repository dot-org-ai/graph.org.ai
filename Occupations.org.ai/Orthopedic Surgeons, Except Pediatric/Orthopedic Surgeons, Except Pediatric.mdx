# Orthopedic Surgeons, Except Pediatric

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 29-1242.00

Diagnose and perform surgery to treat and prevent rheumatic and other diseases in the musculoskeletal system.

## Tasks

- [Analyze patient's medical history, medication allergies, physical condition, and examination results to verify operation's necessity and to determine best procedure.](Tasks/22745.mdx)
- [Conduct research to develop and test surgical techniques that can improve operating procedures and outcomes related to musculoskeletal injuries and diseases.](Tasks/22746.mdx)
- [Diagnose bodily disorders and orthopedic conditions, and provide treatments, such as medicines and surgeries, in clinics, hospital wards, or operating rooms.](Tasks/22747.mdx)
- [Diagnose or treat disorders of the musculoskeletal system.](Tasks/22748.mdx)
- [Direct and coordinate activities of nurses, assistants, specialists, residents, and other medical staff.](Tasks/22749.mdx)
- [Examine instruments, equipment, and operating room to ensure sterility.](Tasks/22750.mdx)

- [Follow established surgical techniques during the operation.](Tasks/22752.mdx)
- [Manage surgery services, including planning, scheduling and coordination, determination of procedures, or procurement of supplies and equipment.](Tasks/22753.mdx)
