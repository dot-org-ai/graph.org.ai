# Special Effects Artists and Animators

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 27-1014.00

Create special effects or animations using film, video, computers, or other electronic tools and media for use in products, such as computer games, movies, music videos, and commercials.

## Tasks

- [Design complex graphics and animation, using independent judgment, creativity, and computer equipment.](Tasks/6882.mdx)
- [Create basic designs, drawings, and illustrations for product labels, cartons, direct mail, or television.](Tasks/6888.mdx)
- [Participate in design and production of multimedia campaigns, handling budgeting and scheduling, and assisting with such responsibilities as production coordination, background design, and progress tracking.](Tasks/6893.mdx)
- [Create two-dimensional and three-dimensional images depicting objects in motion or illustrating a process, using computer animation or modeling programs.](Tasks/6883.mdx)
- [Make objects or characters appear lifelike by manipulating light, color, texture, shadow, and transparency, or manipulating static images to give the illusion of motion.](Tasks/6884.mdx)
- [Apply story development, directing, cinematography, and editing to animation to create storyboards that show the flow of the animation and map out key scenes and characters.](Tasks/6886.mdx)
- [Implement and maintain configuration control systems.](Tasks/6895.mdx)
- [Script, plan, and create animated narrative sequences under tight deadlines, using computer software and hand drawing techniques.](Tasks/6887.mdx)
- [Develop briefings, brochures, multimedia presentations, web pages, promotional products, technical illustrations, and computer artwork for use in products, technical manuals, literature, newsletters, and slide shows.](Tasks/6890.mdx)
- [Assemble, typeset, scan, and produce digital camera-ready art or film negatives and printer's proofs.](Tasks/6885.mdx)
