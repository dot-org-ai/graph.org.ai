# Patternmakers, Wood

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-7032.00

Plan, lay out, and construct wooden unit or sectional patterns used in forming sand molds for castings.

## Tasks

- [Read blueprints, drawings, or written specifications to determine sizes and shapes of patterns and required machine setups.](Tasks/12230.mdx)
- [Fit, fasten, and assemble wood parts together to form patterns, models, or sections, using glue, nails, dowels, bolts, and screws.](Tasks/12235.mdx)
- [Lay out patterns on wood stock and draw outlines of units, sectional patterns, or full-scale mock-ups of products, based on blueprint specifications and sketches, and using marking and measuring devices.](Tasks/12231.mdx)
- [Trim, smooth, and shape surfaces, and plane, shave, file, scrape, and sand models to attain specified shapes, using hand tools.](Tasks/12234.mdx)
- [Divide patterns into sections according to shapes of castings to facilitate removal of patterns from molds.](Tasks/12247.mdx)
- [Verify dimensions of completed patterns, using templates, straightedges, calipers, or protractors.](Tasks/12232.mdx)
- [Correct patterns to compensate for defects in castings.](Tasks/12236.mdx)
- [Set up, operate, and adjust a variety of woodworking machines such as bandsaws and lathes to cut and shape sections, parts, and patterns, according to specifications.](Tasks/12233.mdx)
- [Finish completed products or models with shellac, lacquer, wax, or paint.](Tasks/12240.mdx)
- [Estimate costs for patternmaking jobs.](Tasks/12246.mdx)
