# Switchboard Operators, Including Answering Service

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 43-2011.00

Operate telephone business systems equipment or switchboards to relay incoming, outgoing, and interoffice calls. May supply information to callers and record messages.

## Tasks

- [Operate communication systems, such as telephone, switchboard, intercom, two-way radio, or public address.](Tasks/4630.mdx)
- [Answer incoming calls, greeting callers, providing information, transferring calls or taking messages as necessary.](Tasks/4631.mdx)
- [Greet visitors, log them in and out of the facility, assign them security badges, and contact employee escorts.](Tasks/20431.mdx)
- [Monitor emergency and code alarms, make emergency announcements, or route emergency calls to the appropriate location.](Tasks/20430.mdx)
- [Record messages, suggesting rewording for clarity or conciseness.](Tasks/4640.mdx)
- [Page individuals to inform them of telephone calls, using paging or interoffice communication equipment.](Tasks/4633.mdx)
- [Relay or route written or verbal messages.](Tasks/4634.mdx)
- [Perform various cash handling tasks, such as collecting payments, making bank deposits, or managing petty cash.](Tasks/20432.mdx)
- [Place telephone calls or arrange conference calls as instructed.](Tasks/4635.mdx)
- [Process incoming or outgoing mail, packages, or deliveries.](Tasks/20428.mdx)
