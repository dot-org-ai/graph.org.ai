# Shuttle Drivers and Chauffeurs

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 53-3053.00

Drive a motor vehicle to transport passengers on a planned or scheduled basis. May collect a fare. Includes nonemergency medical transporters and hearse drivers.

## Tasks

- [Test vehicle equipment, such as lights, brakes, horns, or windshield wipers, to ensure proper operation.](Tasks/23756.mdx)
- [Check the condition of a vehicle's tires, brakes, windshield wipers, lights, oil, fuel, water, and safety equipment to ensure that everything is in working order.](Tasks/23733.mdx)
- [Comply with traffic regulations to operate vehicles in a safe and courteous manner.](Tasks/23737.mdx)
- [Follow relevant safety regulations and state laws governing vehicle operation, and ensure that passengers follow safety regulations.](Tasks/23739.mdx)
- [Operate vehicles with specialized equipment, such as wheelchair lifts, to transport and secure passengers with special needs.](Tasks/23742.mdx)
- [Report any vehicle malfunctions or needed repairs.](Tasks/23754.mdx)

- [Drive shuttle busses, limousines, company cars, or privately owned vehicles to transport passengers.](Tasks/23738.mdx)
