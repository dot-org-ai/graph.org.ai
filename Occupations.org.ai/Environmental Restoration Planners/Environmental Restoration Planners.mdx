# Environmental Restoration Planners

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 19-2041.02

Collaborate with field and biology staff to oversee the implementation of restoration projects and to develop new products. Process and synthesize complex scientific data into practical strategies for restoration, monitoring or management.

## Tasks

- [Develop environmental restoration project schedules and budgets.](Tasks/16856.mdx)
- [Provide technical direction on environmental planning to energy engineers, biologists, geologists, or other professionals working to develop restoration plans or strategies.](Tasks/16862.mdx)
- [Create habitat management or restoration plans, such as native tree restoration and weed control.](Tasks/16875.mdx)
- [Conduct site assessments to certify a habitat or to ascertain environmental damage or restoration needs.](Tasks/16876.mdx)
- [Collect and analyze data to determine environmental conditions and restoration needs.](Tasks/16872.mdx)
- [Supervise and provide technical guidance, training, or assistance to employees working in the field to restore habitats.](Tasks/16861.mdx)
- [Plan environmental restoration projects, using biological databases, environmental strategies, and planning software.](Tasks/16865.mdx)
- [Communicate findings of environmental studies or proposals for environmental remediation to other restoration professionals.](Tasks/16871.mdx)
- [Apply for permits required for the implementation of environmental remediation projects.](Tasks/16859.mdx)
- [Inspect active remediation sites to ensure compliance with environmental or safety policies, standards, or regulations.](Tasks/16864.mdx)
