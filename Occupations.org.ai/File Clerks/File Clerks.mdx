# File Clerks

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 43-4071.00

File correspondence, cards, invoices, receipts, and other records in alphabetical or numerical order or according to the filing system used. Locate and remove material from file when requested.

## Tasks

- [Perform general office activities, such as typing, answering telephones, operating office machines, processing mail, or securing confidential materials.](Tasks/20628.mdx)
- [Keep records of materials filed or removed, using logbooks or computers and generate computerized reports.](Tasks/20629.mdx)
- [Gather materials to be filed from departments or employees.](Tasks/2596.mdx)
- [Find, retrieve, and make copies of information from files in response to requests and deliver information to authorized users.](Tasks/20630.mdx)
- [Add new material to file records or create new records as necessary.](Tasks/2593.mdx)
- [Sort or classify information according to guidelines, such as content, purpose, user criteria, or chronological, alphabetical, or numerical order.](Tasks/2597.mdx)
- [Scan or read incoming materials to determine how and where they should be classified or filed.](Tasks/2599.mdx)
- [Eliminate outdated or unnecessary materials, destroying them or transferring them to inactive storage, according to file maintenance guidelines or legal requirements.](Tasks/2605.mdx)
- [Answer questions about records or files.](Tasks/2602.mdx)
- [Assign and record or stamp identification numbers or codes to index materials for filing.](Tasks/2601.mdx)
