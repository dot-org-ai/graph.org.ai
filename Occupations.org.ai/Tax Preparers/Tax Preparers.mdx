# Tax Preparers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 13-2082.00

Prepare tax returns for individuals or small businesses.

## Tasks

- [Compute taxes owed or overpaid, using adding machines or personal computers, and complete entries on forms, following tax form instructions and tax tables.](Tasks/7358.mdx)
- [Use all appropriate adjustments, deductions, and credits to keep clients' taxes to a minimum.](Tasks/7360.mdx)
- [Furnish taxpayers with sufficient information and advice to ensure correct tax form completion.](Tasks/7363.mdx)
- [Interview clients to obtain additional information on taxable income and deductible expenses and allowances.](Tasks/7361.mdx)
- [Prepare or assist in preparing simple to complex tax returns for individuals or small businesses.](Tasks/7359.mdx)
- [Review financial records, such as income statements and documentation of expenditures to determine forms needed to prepare tax returns.](Tasks/7362.mdx)
- [Check data input or verify totals on forms prepared by others to detect errors in arithmetic, data entry, or procedures.](Tasks/7366.mdx)
- [Explain federal and state tax laws to individuals and companies.](Tasks/20364.mdx)
- [Answer questions and provide future tax planning to clients.](Tasks/20363.mdx)
