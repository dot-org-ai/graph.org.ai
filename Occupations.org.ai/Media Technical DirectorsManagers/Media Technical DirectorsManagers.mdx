# Media Technical Directors/Managers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 27-2012.05

Coordinate activities of technical departments, such as taping, editing, engineering, and maintenance, to produce radio or television programs.

## Tasks

- [Switch between video sources in a studio or on multi-camera remotes, using equipment such as switchers, video slide projectors, and video effects generators.](Tasks/3904.mdx)
- [Observe pictures through monitors and direct camera and video staff concerning shading and composition.](Tasks/3897.mdx)
- [Supervise and assign duties to workers engaged in technical control and production of radio and television programs.](Tasks/3899.mdx)
- [Monitor broadcasts to ensure that programs conform to station or network policies and regulations.](Tasks/3896.mdx)
- [Operate equipment to produce programs or broadcast live programs from remote locations.](Tasks/3902.mdx)
- [Test equipment to ensure proper operation.](Tasks/3895.mdx)
- [Train workers in use of equipment, such as switchers, cameras, monitors, microphones, and lights.](Tasks/3903.mdx)
- [Act as liaisons between engineering and production departments.](Tasks/3898.mdx)
- [Collaborate with promotions directors to produce on-air station promotions.](Tasks/3906.mdx)
- [Confer with operations directors to formulate and maintain fair and attainable technical policies for programs.](Tasks/3901.mdx)
