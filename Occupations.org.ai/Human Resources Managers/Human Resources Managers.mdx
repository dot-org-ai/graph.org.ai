# Human Resources Managers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 11-3121.00

Plan, direct, or coordinate human resources activities and staff of an organization.

- [Serve as a link between management and employees by handling questions, interpreting and administering contracts and helping resolve work-related problems.](Tasks/990.mdx)
