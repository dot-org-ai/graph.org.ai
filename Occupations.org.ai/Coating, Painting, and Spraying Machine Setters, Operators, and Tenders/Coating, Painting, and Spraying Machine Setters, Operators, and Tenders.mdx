# Coating, Painting, and Spraying Machine Setters, Operators, and Tenders

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-9124.00

Set up, operate, or tend spraying or rolling machines to coat or paint any of a wide variety of products, including glassware, cloth, ceramics, metal, plastic, paper, or wood, with lacquer, silver, copper, rubber, varnish, glaze, enamel, oil, or rust-proofing materials. Includes painters of transportation vehicles such as painters in auto body repair facilities.

- [Spray prepared surfaces with specified amounts of primers and decorative or finish coatings.](Tasks/23664.mdx)
- [Monitor painting operations to identify flaws, such as blisters or streaks, and correct their causes.](Tasks/23659.mdx)
- [Disassemble, clean, and reassemble sprayers or power equipment, using solvents, wire brushes, and cloths.](Tasks/23655.mdx)
- [Fill hoppers, reservoirs, troughs, or pans with material used to coat, paint, or spray, using conveyors or pails.](Tasks/12513.mdx)
- [Clean equipment and work areas.](Tasks/23654.mdx)
- [Apply rust-resistant undercoats and caulk and seal seams.](Tasks/23652.mdx)
- [Start and stop operation of machines, using levers or buttons.](Tasks/12509.mdx)
- [Determine paint flow, viscosity, and coating quality by performing visual inspections, or by using viscometers.](Tasks/12504.mdx)
