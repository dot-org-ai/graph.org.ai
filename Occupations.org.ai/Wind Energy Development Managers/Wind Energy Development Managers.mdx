# Wind Energy Development Managers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 11-9199.10

Lead or manage the development and evaluation of potential wind energy business opportunities, including environmental studies, permitting, and proposals. May also manage construction of projects.

## Tasks

- [Coordinate or direct development, energy assessment, engineering, or construction activities to ensure that wind project needs and objectives are met.](Tasks/15836.mdx)
- [Manage wind project costs to stay within budget limits.](Tasks/15834.mdx)
- [Lead or support negotiations involving tax agreements or abatements, power purchase agreements, land use, or interconnection agreements.](Tasks/15827.mdx)
- [Create wind energy project plans, including project scope, goals, tasks, resources, schedules, costs, contingencies, or other project information.](Tasks/15838.mdx)
- [Supervise the work of subcontractors or consultants to ensure quality and conformance to specifications or budgets.](Tasks/15824.mdx)
- [Develop scope of work for wind project functions, such as design, site assessment, environmental studies, surveying, or field support services.](Tasks/15835.mdx)
- [Provide verbal or written project status reports to project teams, management, subcontractors, customers, or owners.](Tasks/15830.mdx)
- [Update schedules, estimates, forecasts, or budgets for wind projects.](Tasks/15828.mdx)
- [Prepare or assist in the preparation of applications for environmental, building, or other required permits.](Tasks/15837.mdx)
- [Review or evaluate proposals or bids to make recommendations regarding awarding of contracts.](Tasks/15829.mdx)
