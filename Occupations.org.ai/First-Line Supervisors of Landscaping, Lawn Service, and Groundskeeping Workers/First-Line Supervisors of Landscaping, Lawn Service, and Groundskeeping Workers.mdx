# First-Line Supervisors of Landscaping, Lawn Service, and Groundskeeping Workers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 37-1012.00

Directly supervise and coordinate activities of workers engaged in landscaping or groundskeeping activities. Work may involve reviewing contracts to ascertain service, machine, and workforce requirements; answering inquiries from potential customers regarding methods, material, and price ranges; and preparing estimates according to labor, material, and machine costs.

## Tasks

- [Establish and enforce operating procedures and work standards that will ensure adequate performance and personnel safety.](Tasks/11199.mdx)
- [Schedule work for crews, depending on work priorities, crew or equipment availability, or weather conditions.](Tasks/11202.mdx)
- [Tour grounds, such as parks, botanical gardens, cemeteries, or golf courses, to inspect conditions of plants and soil.](Tasks/11225.mdx)
- [Monitor project activities to ensure that instructions are followed, deadlines are met, and schedules are maintained.](Tasks/11204.mdx)
- [Direct activities of workers who perform duties, such as landscaping, cultivating lawns, or pruning trees and shrubs.](Tasks/11201.mdx)
- [Inspect completed work to ensure conformance to specifications, standards, and contract requirements.](Tasks/11200.mdx)
- [Plant or maintain vegetation through activities such as mulching, fertilizing, watering, mowing, or pruning.](Tasks/11203.mdx)
- [Direct or perform mixing or application of fertilizers, insecticides, herbicides, or fungicides.](Tasks/11210.mdx)
- [Train workers in tasks such as transplanting or pruning trees or shrubs, finishing cement, using equipment, or caring for turf.](Tasks/11205.mdx)
- [Prepare service estimates based on labor, material, and machine costs and maintain budgets for individual projects.](Tasks/11224.mdx)
