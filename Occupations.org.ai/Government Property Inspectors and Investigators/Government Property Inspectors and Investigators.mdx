# Government Property Inspectors and Investigators

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 13-1041.04

Investigate or inspect government property to ensure compliance with contract agreements and government regulations.

## Tasks

- [Prepare correspondence, reports of inspections or investigations, or recommendations for action.](Tasks/12890.mdx)
- [Examine records, reports, or other documents to establish facts or detect discrepancies.](Tasks/12891.mdx)
- [Inspect government property, such as construction sites or public housing, to ensure compliance with contract specifications or legal requirements.](Tasks/20843.mdx)
- [Investigate alleged license or permit violations.](Tasks/20844.mdx)
- [Inspect manufactured or processed products to ensure compliance with contract specifications or legal requirements.](Tasks/12894.mdx)
- [Collect, identify, evaluate, or preserve case evidence.](Tasks/12893.mdx)
- [Submit samples of products to government laboratories for testing, as required.](Tasks/12900.mdx)
- [Inspect government-owned equipment or materials in the possession of private contractors to ensure compliance with contracts or regulations or to prevent misuse.](Tasks/12898.mdx)
