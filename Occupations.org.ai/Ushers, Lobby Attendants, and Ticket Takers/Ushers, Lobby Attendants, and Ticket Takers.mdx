# Ushers, Lobby Attendants, and Ticket Takers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 39-3031.00

Assist patrons at entertainment events by performing duties, such as collecting admission tickets and passes from patrons, assisting in finding seats, searching for lost articles, and helping patrons locate such facilities as restrooms and telephones.

## Tasks

- [Greet patrons attending entertainment events.](Tasks/5096.mdx)
- [Sell or collect admission tickets, passes, or facility memberships from patrons at entertainment events.](Tasks/20764.mdx)
- [Clean facilities.](Tasks/20765.mdx)
- [Settle seating disputes or help solve other customer concerns.](Tasks/5103.mdx)
- [Examine tickets or passes to verify authenticity, using criteria such as color or date issued.](Tasks/5097.mdx)
- [Provide assistance with patrons' special needs, such as helping those with wheelchairs.](Tasks/5100.mdx)
- [Guide patrons to exits or provide other instructions or assistance in case of emergency.](Tasks/5098.mdx)
- [Refuse admittance to undesirable persons or persons without tickets or passes.](Tasks/5102.mdx)
- [Assist patrons by giving directions to points in or outside of the facility or providing information about local attractions.](Tasks/20766.mdx)
- [Assist patrons in finding seats, lighting the way with flashlights, if necessary.](Tasks/5104.mdx)
