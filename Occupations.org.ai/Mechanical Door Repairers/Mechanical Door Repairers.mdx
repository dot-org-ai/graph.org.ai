# Mechanical Door Repairers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 49-9011.00

Install, service, or repair automatic door mechanisms and hydraulic doors. Includes garage door mechanics.

## Tasks

- [Wind large springs with upward motion of arm.](Tasks/8388.mdx)
- [Adjust doors to open or close with the correct amount of effort, or make simple adjustments to electric openers.](Tasks/8387.mdx)
- [Carry springs to tops of doors, using ladders or scaffolding, and attach springs to tracks to install spring systems.](Tasks/8394.mdx)
- [Repair or replace worn or broken door parts, using hand tools.](Tasks/8393.mdx)
- [Complete required paperwork, such as work orders, according to services performed or required.](Tasks/8391.mdx)
- [Fasten angle iron back-hangers to ceilings and tracks, using fasteners or welding equipment.](Tasks/8392.mdx)
- [Collect payment upon job completion.](Tasks/8390.mdx)
- [Install door frames, rails, steel rolling curtains, electronic-eye mechanisms, or electric door openers and closers, using power tools, hand tools, and electronic test equipment.](Tasks/8397.mdx)
- [Inspect job sites, assessing headroom, side room, or other conditions to determine appropriateness of door for a given location.](Tasks/8389.mdx)
- [Assemble and fasten tracks to structures or bucks, using impact wrenches or welding equipment.](Tasks/8399.mdx)
