# Electromechanical Equipment Assemblers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 51-2023.00

Assemble or modify electromechanical equipment or devices, such as servomechanisms, gyros, dynamometers, magnetic drums, tape drives, brakes, control linkage, actuators, and appliances.

## Tasks

- [Inspect, test, and adjust completed units to ensure that units meet specifications, tolerances, and customer order requirements.](Tasks/13944.mdx)
- [Position, align, and adjust parts for proper fit and assembly.](Tasks/13946.mdx)
- [Assemble parts or units, and position, align, and fasten units to assemblies, subassemblies, or frames, using hand tools and power tools.](Tasks/13945.mdx)
- [Connect cables, tubes, and wiring, according to specifications.](Tasks/13947.mdx)
- [Measure parts to determine tolerances, using precision measuring instruments such as micrometers, calipers, and verniers.](Tasks/13951.mdx)
- [Read blueprints and specifications to determine component parts and assembly sequences of electromechanical units.](Tasks/13949.mdx)
- [Attach name plates and mark identifying information on parts.](Tasks/13948.mdx)
- [Disassemble units to replace parts or to crate them for shipping.](Tasks/13950.mdx)
- [File, lap, and buff parts to fit, using hand and power tools.](Tasks/13954.mdx)
- [Clean and lubricate parts and subassemblies, using grease paddles or oilcans.](Tasks/13952.mdx)
