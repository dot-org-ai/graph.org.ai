# Naturopathic Physicians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 29-1299.01

Diagnose, treat, and help prevent diseases using a system of practice that is based on the natural healing capacity of individuals. May use physiological, psychological or mechanical methods. May also use natural medicines, prescription or legend drugs, foods, herbs, or other natural remedies.

## Tasks

- [Document patients' histories, including identifying data, chief complaints, illnesses, previous medical or family histories, or psychosocial characteristics.](Tasks/18418.mdx)
- [Educate patients about health care management.](Tasks/18412.mdx)
- [Advise patients about therapeutic exercise and nutritional medicine regimens.](Tasks/18413.mdx)
- [Conduct physical examinations and physiological function tests for diagnostic purposes.](Tasks/18410.mdx)
- [Administer, dispense, or prescribe natural medicines, such as food or botanical extracts, herbs, dietary supplements, vitamins, nutraceuticals, and amino acids.](Tasks/18409.mdx)
- [Interview patients to document symptoms and health histories.](Tasks/18411.mdx)
- [Diagnose health conditions, based on patients' symptoms and health histories, laboratory and diagnostic radiology test results, or other physiological measurements, such as electrocardiograms and electroencephalographs.](Tasks/18414.mdx)
- [Administer treatments or therapies, such as homeopathy, hydrotherapy, Oriental or Ayurvedic medicine, electrotherapy, and diathermy, using physical agents including air, heat, cold, water, sound, or ultraviolet light to catalyze the body to heal itself.](Tasks/18408.mdx)
- [Consult with other health professionals to provide optimal patient care, referring patients to traditional health care professionals as necessary.](Tasks/18420.mdx)
- [Order diagnostic imaging procedures such as radiographs (x-rays), ultrasounds, mammograms, and bone densitometry tests, or refer patients to other health professionals for these procedures.](Tasks/18407.mdx)
