# Electrical and Electronic Engineering Technologists and Technicians

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 17-3023.00

Apply electrical and electronic theory and related knowledge, usually under the direction of engineering staff, to design, build, repair, adjust, and modify electrical components, circuitry, controls, and machinery for subsequent evaluation and use by engineering staff in making engineering design decisions.

## Tasks

- [Modify, maintain, or repair electronics equipment or systems to ensure proper functioning.](Tasks/21975.mdx)
- [Replace defective components or parts, using hand tools and precision instruments.](Tasks/21976.mdx)
- [Set up and operate specialized or standard test equipment to diagnose, test, or analyze the performance of electronic components, assemblies, or systems.](Tasks/21977.mdx)
- [Read blueprints, wiring diagrams, schematic drawings, or engineering instructions for assembling electronics units, applying knowledge of electronic theory and components.](Tasks/21978.mdx)
- [Identify and resolve equipment malfunctions, working with manufacturers or field representatives as necessary to procure replacement parts.](Tasks/21979.mdx)
- [Assemble electrical systems or prototypes, using hand tools or measuring instruments.](Tasks/21980.mdx)
- [Review electrical engineering plans to ensure adherence to design specifications and compliance with applicable electrical codes and standards.](Tasks/21981.mdx)
- [Assemble, test, or maintain circuitry or electronic components, according to engineering instructions, technical manuals, or knowledge of electronics, using hand or power tools.](Tasks/21982.mdx)
- [Review existing electrical engineering criteria to identify necessary revisions, deletions, or amendments to outdated material.](Tasks/21983.mdx)
- [Maintain system logs or manuals to document testing or operation of equipment.](Tasks/21984.mdx)
