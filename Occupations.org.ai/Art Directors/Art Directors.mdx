# Art Directors

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 27-1011.00

Formulate design concepts and presentation approaches for visual productions and media, such as print, broadcasting, video, and film. Direct workers engaged in artwork or layout design.

## Tasks

- [Work with creative directors to develop design solutions.](Tasks/291.mdx)
- [Present final layouts to clients for approval.](Tasks/288.mdx)
- [Manage own accounts and projects, working within budget and scheduling requirements.](Tasks/286.mdx)
- [Confer with creative, art, copywriting, or production department heads to discuss client requirements and presentation concepts and to coordinate creative activities.](Tasks/287.mdx)
- [Confer with clients to determine objectives, budget, background information, and presentation approaches, styles, and techniques.](Tasks/289.mdx)
- [Formulate basic layout design or presentation approach and specify material details, such as style and size of type, photographs, graphics, animation, video, and sound.](Tasks/284.mdx)
- [Review and approve art materials, copy materials, and proofs of printed copy developed by staff members.](Tasks/285.mdx)
- [Create custom illustrations or other graphic elements.](Tasks/294.mdx)
- [Attend photo shoots and printing sessions to ensure that the products needed are obtained.](Tasks/293.mdx)
- [Review illustrative material to determine if it conforms to standards and specifications.](Tasks/292.mdx)
