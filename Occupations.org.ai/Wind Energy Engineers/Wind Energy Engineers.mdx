# Wind Energy Engineers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 17-2199.10

Design underground or overhead wind farm collector systems and prepare and develop site specifications.

## Tasks

- [Create or maintain wind farm layouts, schematics, or other visual documentation for wind farms.](Tasks/16559.mdx)
- [Recommend process or infrastructure changes to improve wind turbine performance, reduce operational costs, or comply with regulations.](Tasks/16549.mdx)
- [Create models to optimize the layout of wind farm access roads, crane pads, crane paths, collection systems, substations, switchyards, or transmission lines.](Tasks/16560.mdx)
- [Provide engineering technical support to designers of prototype wind turbines.](Tasks/16553.mdx)
- [Investigate experimental wind turbines or wind turbine technologies for properties such as aerodynamics, production, noise, and load.](Tasks/16550.mdx)
- [Develop active control algorithms, electronics, software, electromechanical, or electrohydraulic systems for wind turbines.](Tasks/16558.mdx)
- [Develop specifications for wind technology components, such as gearboxes, blades, generators, frequency converters, or pad transformers.](Tasks/16557.mdx)
- [Test wind turbine components, using mechanical or electronic testing equipment.](Tasks/16552.mdx)
- [Oversee the work activities of wind farm consultants or subcontractors.](Tasks/16548.mdx)
- [Test wind turbine equipment to determine effects of stress or fatigue.](Tasks/16551.mdx)
