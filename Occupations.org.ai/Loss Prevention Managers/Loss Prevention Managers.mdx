# Loss Prevention Managers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 11-9199.08

Plan and direct policies, procedures, or systems to prevent the loss of assets. Determine risk exposure or potential liability, and develop risk control measures.

## Tasks

- [Coordinate or conduct internal investigations of problems such as employee theft and violations of corporate loss prevention policies.](Tasks/15800.mdx)
- [Administer systems and programs to reduce loss, maintain inventory control, or increase safety.](Tasks/15807.mdx)
- [Review loss prevention exception reports and cash discrepancies to ensure adherence to guidelines.](Tasks/15781.mdx)
- [Train loss prevention staff, retail managers, or store employees on loss control and prevention measures.](Tasks/15791.mdx)
- [Investigate or interview individuals suspected of shoplifting or internal theft.](Tasks/15786.mdx)
- [Provide recommendations and solutions in crisis situations such as workplace violence, protests, and demonstrations.](Tasks/15783.mdx)
- [Identify potential for loss and develop strategies to eliminate it.](Tasks/15806.mdx)
- [Hire or supervise loss prevention staff.](Tasks/15795.mdx)
- [Advise retail managers on compliance with applicable codes, laws, regulations, or standards.](Tasks/15804.mdx)
- [Develop and maintain partnerships with federal, state, or local law enforcement agencies or members of the retail loss prevention community.](Tasks/15799.mdx)
