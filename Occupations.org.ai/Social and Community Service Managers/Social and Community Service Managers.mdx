# Social and Community Service Managers

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 11-9151.00

Plan, direct, or coordinate the activities of a social service program or community outreach organization. Oversee the program or organization's budget and policies regarding participant involvement, program requirements, and benefits. Work may involve directing social workers, counselors, or probation officers.

- [Direct activities of professional and technical staff members and volunteers.](Tasks/1129.mdx)

- [Participate in the determination of organizational policies regarding such issues as participant eligibility, program requirements, and program benefits.](Tasks/1132.mdx)
- [Prepare and maintain records and reports, such as budgets, personnel records, or training manuals.](Tasks/1128.mdx)
