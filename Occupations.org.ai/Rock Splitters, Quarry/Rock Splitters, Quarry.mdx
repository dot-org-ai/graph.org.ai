# Rock Splitters, Quarry

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 47-5051.00

Separate blocks of rough dimension stone from quarry mass using jackhammers, wedges, or chop saws.

## Tasks

- [Locate grain line patterns to determine how rocks will split when cut.](Tasks/14966.mdx)
- [Remove pieces of stone from larger masses, using jackhammers, wedges, and other tools.](Tasks/14967.mdx)
- [Insert wedges and feathers into holes, and drive wedges with sledgehammers to split stone sections from masses.](Tasks/14968.mdx)
- [Mark dimensions or outlines on stone prior to cutting, using rules and chalk lines.](Tasks/14969.mdx)
- [Cut slabs of stone into sheets that will be used for floors or counters.](Tasks/14970.mdx)
- [Set charges of explosives to split rock.](Tasks/14971.mdx)
- [Drill holes along outlines, using jackhammers.](Tasks/14972.mdx)
- [Drill holes into sides of stones broken from masses, insert dogs or attach slings, and direct removal of stones.](Tasks/14973.mdx)
- [Cut grooves along outlines, using chisels.](Tasks/14974.mdx)
