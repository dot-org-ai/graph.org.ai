# Electrical and Electronics Repairers, Commercial and Industrial Equipment

**O*NET-SOC Code**: 49-2094.00

Repair, test, adjust, or install electronic equipment, such as industrial controls, transmitters, and antennas.

## Tasks

- [Test faulty equipment to diagnose malfunctions, using test equipment or software, and applying knowledge of the functional operation of electronic units and systems.](Tasks/886.mdx)
- [Maintain equipment logs that record performance problems, repairs, calibrations, or tests.](Tasks/892.mdx)
- [Set up and test industrial equipment to ensure that it functions properly.](Tasks/884.mdx)
- [Inspect components of industrial equipment for accurate assembly and installation or for defects, such as loose connections or frayed wires.](Tasks/890.mdx)
- [Install repaired equipment in various settings, such as industrial or military establishments.](Tasks/897.mdx)
- [Operate equipment to demonstrate proper use or to analyze malfunctions.](Tasks/885.mdx)
- [Enter information into computer to copy program or to draw, modify, or store schematics, applying knowledge of software package used.](Tasks/899.mdx)
- [Perform scheduled preventive maintenance tasks, such as checking, cleaning, or repairing equipment, to detect and prevent problems.](Tasks/882.mdx)
- [Calibrate testing instruments and installed or repaired equipment to prescribed specifications.](Tasks/888.mdx)
- [Repair or adjust equipment, machines, or defective components, replacing worn parts, such as gaskets or seals in watertight electrical equipment.](Tasks/887.mdx)
