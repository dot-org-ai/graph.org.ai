# Cardboard band drain or wick drain

**UNSPSC Code**: 22101908
**Class**: [Building construction machinery and accessories](Building construction machinery and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Heavy construction machinery and equipment](../Heavy construction machinery and equipment.mdx)
**Segment**: [Building and Construction Machinery and Accessories](../../Building and Construction Machinery and Accessories.mdx)

A type of equipment used to improve weak foundation comprised of vertically inserted columns sunk into porous soil to promote drainage through capillary tube action of card-board which is paper inserted into weak foundation by means of pressure.
