# Floating production storage and offloading

**UNSPSC Code**: 22110200
**Family**: [Marine construction and installation equipment](Marine construction and installation equipment.mdx)
**Segment**: [Building and Construction Machinery and Accessories](../Building and Construction Machinery and Accessories.mdx)

Offloading systems, FPSO and FSO, often consist of a hose reel, offloading hose, and hawser winch. The offloading system is the transmission of crude oil from the FPSO to the shuttle tanker. (FPSO - Floating Production Storage and Offloading) (FSO - Floating Storage and Offloading).
