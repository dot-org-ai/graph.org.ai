# Tandem mooring equipment

**UNSPSC Code**: 22110204
**Class**: [Floating production storage and offloading](Floating production storage and offloading.mdx)
**Family**: [Marine construction and installation equipment](../Marine construction and installation equipment.mdx)
**Segment**: [Building and Construction Machinery and Accessories](../../Building and Construction Machinery and Accessories.mdx)

Tandem Mooring is used for the transfer of oil and other products typically from FSO, FPSO, and other extracting facilities to shuttle tankers. It can be floating or submarine, and it can be left hanging off the vessel's side or wrapped on a reel.  (FPSO - Floating Production Storage and Offloading) (FSO - Floating Storage and Offloading).
