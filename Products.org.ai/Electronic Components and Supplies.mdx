# Electronic Components and Supplies

**UNSPSC Code**: 32000000

This segment includes electronic components used (or consumed) in the manufacturing and maintenance of electronic or electro-mechanical devices and systems. Examples of commodities found within this segment include semiconductors, integrated circuits, circuit card assemblies and light emitting diodes.
