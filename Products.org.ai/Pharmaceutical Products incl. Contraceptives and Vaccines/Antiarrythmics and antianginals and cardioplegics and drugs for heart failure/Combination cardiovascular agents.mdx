# Combination cardiovascular agents

**UNSPSC Code**: 51122700
**Family**: [Antiarrythmics and antianginals and cardioplegics and drugs for heart failure](Antiarrythmics and antianginals and cardioplegics and drugs for heart failure.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes combination drugs that affect the rate or intensity of cardiac contraction, blood vessel diameter, or blood volume, and thereby improve cardiovascular function, hence the term CARDIOVASCULAR AGENTS. Typical combination cardiovascular agent constituents are calcium chloride and potassium chloride.
