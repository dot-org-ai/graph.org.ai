# Antiarrythmic benzofurans

**UNSPSC Code**: 51123600
**Family**: [Antiarrythmics and antianginals and cardioplegics and drugs for heart failure](Antiarrythmics and antianginals and cardioplegics and drugs for heart failure.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes compounds that contain a benzene ring fused to a furan ring, substances which are therefore called benzo-furans, that prevent or treat irregular heartbeat, disturbances of normal rhythmic heart beating, or normal operation of myocardial contraction, thus the term ANTIARRYTHMIC BENZOFURANS.
