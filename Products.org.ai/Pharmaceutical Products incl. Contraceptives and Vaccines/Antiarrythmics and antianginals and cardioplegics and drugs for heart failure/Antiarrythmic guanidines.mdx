# Antiarrythmic guanidines

**UNSPSC Code**: 51123800
**Family**: [Antiarrythmics and antianginals and cardioplegics and drugs for heart failure](Antiarrythmics and antianginals and cardioplegics and drugs for heart failure.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes iminourea derivatives based in whole or part on guanidine or CH5N3, substances called guanidines, that prevent or treat irregular heartbeat, disturbances of normal rhythmic heart beating, or normal operation of myocardial contraction, thus the term ANTIARRYTHMIC GUANIDINES.
