# Antiarrythmic alkaline earth metals

**UNSPSC Code**: 51122900
**Family**: [Antiarrythmics and antianginals and cardioplegics and drugs for heart failure](Antiarrythmics and antianginals and cardioplegics and drugs for heart failure.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes based in part or whole on metals that constitute the group 2 of the periodic table (barium, beryllium, calcium, magnesium, radium, and strontium), substances called alkaline earth metals, that prevent or treat irregular heartbeat, disturbances of normal rhythmic heart beating, or normal operation of myocardial contraction, thus the term ANTIARRYTHMIC ALKALINE EARTH METALS.
