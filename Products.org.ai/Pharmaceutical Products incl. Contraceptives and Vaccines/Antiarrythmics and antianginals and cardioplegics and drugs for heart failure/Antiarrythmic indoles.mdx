# Antiarrythmic indoles

**UNSPSC Code**: 51123900

**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes substances derived from benzopyrroles with the nitrogen at the number one carbon adjacent to the benzyl portion, substances called indoles, that prevent or treat irregular heartbeat, disturbances of normal rhythmic heart beating, or normal operation of myocardial contraction, thus the term ANTIARRYTHMIC INDOLES.
