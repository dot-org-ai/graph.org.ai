# Combination sun protectants or screens

**UNSPSC Code**: 51242300
**Family**: [Drugs affecting the ears, eye, nose and skin](Drugs affecting the ears, eye, nose and skin.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes combination drugs and preparations that protect the skin from sunburn and erythema by absorbing or blocking ultraviolet radiation, chiefly from solar rays, hence the term SUN PROTECTANTS OR SCREENS. Typical sun protectant or sun-screen constituents are ethyl dihydroxypropyl paba, methyl anthranilate, octyl salicylate, oxybenzone, padimate o, padimate-o, para-aminobenzoic acid, petrolatum, titanium dioxide, and zinc oxide.
