# Combination contact lens solutions

**UNSPSC Code**: 51241700

**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes combination drugs and preparations that consist of sterile solutions to clean and/or disinfect contact lenses. Typical combination contact lens solution constituents are benzalkonium chloride, boric acid, chlorhexidine, edta, hydroxyethyl cellulose, polyvinyl alcohol, and sodium chloride.
