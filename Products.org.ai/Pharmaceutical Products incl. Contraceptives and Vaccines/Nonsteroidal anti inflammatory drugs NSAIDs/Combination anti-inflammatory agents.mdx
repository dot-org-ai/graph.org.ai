# Combination anti-inflammatory agents

**UNSPSC Code**: 51386600
**Family**: [Nonsteroidal anti inflammatory drugs NSAIDs](Nonsteroidal anti inflammatory drugs NSAIDs.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes combination drugs that reduce or suppress a pathological process characterized by injury or destruction of tissues caused by a variety of cytologic and chemical reactions, and which manifests as pain, heat, redness, swelling, and loss of function, a condition known as inflammation, hence the term ANTI-INFLAMMATORY AGENTS. Typical combination anti-inflammatory agent constituents are alcohol, aluminum hydroxide, dibucaine, esomeprazole, fluocinolone, hydrocortisone, and lansoprazole.
