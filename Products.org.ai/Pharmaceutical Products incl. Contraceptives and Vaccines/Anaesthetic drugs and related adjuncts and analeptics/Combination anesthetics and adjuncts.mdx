# Combination anesthetics and adjuncts

**UNSPSC Code**: 51273900
**Family**: [Anaesthetic drugs and related adjuncts and analeptics](Anaesthetic drugs and related adjuncts and analeptics.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes combination drugs that consist of various combined compounds that are a) agents that are capable of inducing a total or partial loss of sensation, especially tactile sensation and pain, known as ANESTHETICS; and/or b) agents that enhance, modify, or modulate said anesthetics, agents generally known as ADJUNCTS. Typical combination anesthetic and adjunct constituents are articaine, benzocaine, bupivacaine, dibucaine, and lidocaine.
