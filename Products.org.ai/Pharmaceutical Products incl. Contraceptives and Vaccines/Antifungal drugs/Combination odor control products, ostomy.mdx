# Combination odor control products, ostomy

**UNSPSC Code**: 51303700
**Family**: [Antifungal drugs](Antifungal drugs.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes combination drugs and preparations that diminish smells that emanate from any artificial opening (stoma) for external fistulization of a duct or vessel by insertion of a tube with or without a supportive stent, such as an artificial anus, and which, on the aforesaid, bear the name ODOR CONTROL PRODUCTS, OSTOMY. Typical combination ostomy odor control product constituents are chlorophyll and thymol.
