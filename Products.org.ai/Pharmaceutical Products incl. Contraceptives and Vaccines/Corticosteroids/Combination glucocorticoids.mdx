# Combination glucocorticoids

**UNSPSC Code**: 51422600
**Family**: [Corticosteroids](Corticosteroids.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes combination drugs and preparations that consist of corticosteroids that affect carbohydrate metabolism or GLUCONEO-GENESIS, liver glycogen deposition, and elevation of BLOOD SUGAR, inhibit adrenocorticotropic hormone secretion, and possess anti-inflammatory activity, and which thefore bear the name GLUCO-CORTICOIDS or GLUCOCORTICOIDS. Typical combination glucocorticoid constituents are dexamethasone and lidocaine.
