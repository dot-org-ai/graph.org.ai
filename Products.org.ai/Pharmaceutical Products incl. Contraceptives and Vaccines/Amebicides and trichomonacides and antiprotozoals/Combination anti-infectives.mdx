# Combination anti-infectives

**UNSPSC Code**: 51102800
**Family**: [Amebicides and trichomonacides and antiprotozoals](Amebicides and trichomonacides and antiprotozoals.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes combination drugs that prevent infectious agents or organisms from spreading or kill infectious agents in order to prevent the spread of infection, hence the term ANTI-INFECTIVES. The US National Library of Medicine includes, under this general heading, a wide variety of agents, including anti-bacterials, antifungals, antiparasitics, antivirals, and disinfectants. Typical combination anti-infective constituents are acetic acid, benzalkonium, cetylpyridinium, hexachlorophene, and isopropyl alcohol.
