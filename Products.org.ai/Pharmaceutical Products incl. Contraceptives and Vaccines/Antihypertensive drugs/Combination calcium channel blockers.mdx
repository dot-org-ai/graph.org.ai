# Combination calcium channel blockers

**UNSPSC Code**: 51435000
**Family**: [Antihypertensive drugs](Antihypertensive drugs.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes combination drugs that act by selective inhibition of calcium influx through cell membranes or on the release and binding of calcium in intracellular pools, hence the term calcium channel blockers. Typical combination calcium channel blocker constituents are amlodipine and sodium chloride.
