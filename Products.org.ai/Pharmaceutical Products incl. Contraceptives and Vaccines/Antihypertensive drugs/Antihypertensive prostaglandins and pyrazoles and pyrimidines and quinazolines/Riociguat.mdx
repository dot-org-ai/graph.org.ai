# Riociguat

**UNSPSC Code**: 51433708
**Class**: [Antihypertensive prostaglandins and pyrazoles and pyrimidines and quinazolines](Antihypertensive prostaglandins and pyrazoles and pyrimidines and quinazolines.mdx)
**Family**: [Antihypertensive drugs](../Antihypertensive drugs.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

Riociguat is a stimulator of soluble guanylate cyclase. It is used to treat two forms of pulmonary hypertension: chronic thromboembolic pulmonary hypertension and pulmonary arterial hypertension.
