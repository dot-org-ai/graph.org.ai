# Combination antiparkinson agents

**UNSPSC Code**: 51143500
**Family**: [Central nervous system drugs](Central nervous system drugs.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes combination drugs used in the treatment of a progressive, degenerative neurologic disease characterized by a tremor that is maximal at rest, retropulsion, rigidity, stooped posture, slowness of voluntary movements, and a masklike facial expression, a disorder called Parkinson Disease, hence the term ANTIPARKINSON AGENTS. Typical combination Antiparkinson agent constituents are carbidopa and levodopa.
