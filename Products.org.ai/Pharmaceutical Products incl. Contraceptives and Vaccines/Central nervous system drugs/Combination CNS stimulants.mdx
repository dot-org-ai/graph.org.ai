**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes combination drugs and preparations that stimulate the main information-processing organs of the nervous system, consisting of the brain, spinal cord, and meninges, or the Central Nervous System or CNS, hence the term CNS STIMULANTS. Typical combination CNS stimulant constituents are amphetamine and caffeine.
