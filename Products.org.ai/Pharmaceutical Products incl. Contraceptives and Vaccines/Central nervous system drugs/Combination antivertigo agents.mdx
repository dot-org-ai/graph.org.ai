# Combination antivertigo agents

**UNSPSC Code**: 51143600
**Family**: [Central nervous system drugs](Central nervous system drugs.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes combination drugs used in the treatment of an illusion of movement, either of the external world revolving around the individual or of the individual revolving in space, a condition called Vertigo, hence the term ANTIVERTIGO AGENTS. Typical combination antivertigo agent constituents are dimenhydrinate and niacin.
