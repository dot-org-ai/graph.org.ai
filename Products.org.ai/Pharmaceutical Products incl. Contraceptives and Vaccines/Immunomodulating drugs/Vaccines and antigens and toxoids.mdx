# Vaccines and antigens and toxoids

**UNSPSC Code**: 51201600
**Family**: [Immunomodulating drugs](Immunomodulating drugs.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes a) suspensions of killed or attenuated microorganisms (bacteria, viruses, fungi, protozoa, or rickettsiae), antigenic proteins derived from them, or synthetic constructs, administered for the prevention, amelioration, or treatment of infectious and other diseases; b) substances that are recognized by the immune system and induce an immune reaction; or c) preparations of pathogenic organisms or their derivatives made nontoxic and intended for active immunologic prophylaxis.
