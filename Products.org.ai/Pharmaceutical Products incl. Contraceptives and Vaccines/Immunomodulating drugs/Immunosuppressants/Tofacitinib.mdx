# Tofacitinib

**UNSPSC Code**: 51201576
**Class**: [Immunosuppressants](Immunosuppressants.mdx)
**Family**: [Immunomodulating drugs](../Immunomodulating drugs.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

Tofacitinib is an immunosuppressant (a medicine that reduces the activity of the immune system) that works by blocking the action of enzymes known as Janus kinases and used to treat rheumatoid arthritis, psoriatic arthritis, and ulcerative colitis.
