# Immunostimulating agents

**UNSPSC Code**: 51201800
**Family**: [Immunomodulating drugs](Immunomodulating drugs.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes drugs or other preparations that engender a natural but heightened imune response, or formulations for presenting an antigen to induce specific immunologic responses. it consists of an assembly of antigens in multimeric form. the assembly is attached to a matrix with a built-in adjuvant, saponin. iscoms induce strong serum antibody responses, and are used as highly immunogenic forms of subunit vaccines.
