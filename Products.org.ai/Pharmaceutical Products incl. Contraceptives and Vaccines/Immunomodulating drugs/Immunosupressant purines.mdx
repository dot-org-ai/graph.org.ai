This classification denotes heterocyclic pyrimidine-imidazole ring compounds present in many alkaloids and nucleic acids, substances called purines, that reduce a human host's humoral and/or cell-mediated defense mechanisms against deviant native cells, foreign organisms, or foreign substances (the human immune response and system thereof), thus the term IMMUNOSUPRESSANT PURINES.
