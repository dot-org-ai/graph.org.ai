# Immunosupressant aziridines

**UNSPSC Code**: 51203500
**Family**: [Immunomodulating drugs](Immunomodulating drugs.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes saturated azacyclopropane compounds, called aziridines, that reduce a human host's humoral and/or cell-mediated defense mechanisms against deviant native cells, foreign organisms, or foreign substances (the human immune response and system thereof), thus the term IMMUNOSUPPRESSANT AZIRIDINES.
