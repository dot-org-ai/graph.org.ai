# Combination toxoids and vaccines

**UNSPSC Code**: 51202100
**Family**: [Immunomodulating drugs](Immunomodulating drugs.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes combination drugs and preparations which constitute a) pathogenic organisms (toxoids) or their derivatives made nontoxic and intended for active immunologic prophylaxis; or b) killed or attenuated microorganisms, their antigenic proteins, or synthetic constructs thereof, most often for prevention or treatment of infectious diseases. These are commonly known as toxoids and vaccines. Typical combination toxoid and vaccine constituents are mumps, pertussis, rubella, and tetanus.
