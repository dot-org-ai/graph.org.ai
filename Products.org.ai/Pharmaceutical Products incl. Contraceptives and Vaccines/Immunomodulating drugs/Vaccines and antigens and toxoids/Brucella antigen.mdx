# Brucella antigen

**UNSPSC Code**: 51201602
**Class**: [Vaccines and antigens and toxoids](Vaccines and antigens and toxoids.mdx)
**Family**: [Immunomodulating drugs](../Immunomodulating drugs.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes a vaccine against a genus of gram-negative, aerobic bacteria that causes brucellosis. Its cells are nonmotile coccobacilli and are animal parasites and pathogens. The bacterium is transmissible to humans through contact with infected dairy products or tissue.
