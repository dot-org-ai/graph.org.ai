# Clostridium botulinum vaccine

**UNSPSC Code**: 51204208
**Class**: [Veterinary vaccines and virology products](Veterinary vaccines and virology products.mdx)
**Family**: [Immunomodulating drugs](../Immunomodulating drugs.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

The first vaccine used to protect against botulinum neurotoxin was a chemically detoxified extract from Clostridium botulinum. A Pentavalent botulinum toxoid (PBT) vaccine in service today is administered under an Investigational New Drug (IND) application held by the CDC.
