# Cough and cold and antiallergy products

**UNSPSC Code**: 51161800
**Family**: [Drugs affecting the respiratory tract](Drugs affecting the respiratory tract.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes agents that are used to treat the common cold and similar such maladies. these might treat allergic reactions, decongest, suppress cough, or render coughs more productive. of the allergy-oriented medications, most of these drugs act by preventing the release of inflammatory mediators or inhibiting the actions of released mediators on their target cells.
