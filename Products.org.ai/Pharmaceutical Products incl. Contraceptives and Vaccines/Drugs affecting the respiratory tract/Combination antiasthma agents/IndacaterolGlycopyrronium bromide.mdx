# Indacaterol/Glycopyrronium bromide

**UNSPSC Code**: 51162038
**Class**: [Combination antiasthma agents](Combination antiasthma agents.mdx)
**Family**: [Drugs affecting the respiratory tract](../Drugs affecting the respiratory tract.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

Indacaterol/glycopyrronium bromide is a fixed-dose combination medication for inhalation consisting of the following two active ingredients: Indacaterol maleate, an ultra-long-acting beta-adrenoceptor agonist (ultra-LABA); Glycopyrronium bromide (glycopyrrolate), a muscarinic anticholinergic.
