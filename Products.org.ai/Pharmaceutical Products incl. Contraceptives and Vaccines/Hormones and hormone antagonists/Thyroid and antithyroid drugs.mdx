# Thyroid and antithyroid drugs

**UNSPSC Code**: 51181600
**Family**: [Hormones and hormone antagonists](Hormones and hormone antagonists.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes preparations that treat diseases and disorders of a highly vascularized endocrine gland consisting of two lobes joined by a thin band of tissue with one lobe on each side of the trachea. it secretes thyroid hormones from the follicular cells and calcitonin from the parafollicular cells thereby regulating metabolism and calcium level in blood, respectively.
