# Antidiabetic and hyperglycemic amines

**UNSPSC Code**: 51183200
**Family**: [Hormones and hormone antagonists](Hormones and hormone antagonists.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes compounds derived from ammonia by substituting organic radicals for the hydrogens, substances called amines, that lower blood glucose levels, substances generally classed as antidiabetics or hyperglycemics, thus the term ANTIDIABETIC AND HYPERGLYCEMIC AMINES.
