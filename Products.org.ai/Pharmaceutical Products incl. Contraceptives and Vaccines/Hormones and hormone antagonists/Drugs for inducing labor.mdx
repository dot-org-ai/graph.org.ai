# Drugs for inducing labor

**UNSPSC Code**: 51182200
**Family**: [Hormones and hormone antagonists](Hormones and hormone antagonists.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes drugs that stimulate contraction of the myometrium. they are used to induce labor, obstetric at term, to prevent or control postpartum or postabortion hemorrhage, and to assess fetal status in high risk pregnancies. they may also be used alone or with other drugs to induce abortions (abortifacients). oxytocics used clinically include the neurohypophyseal hormone oxytocin and certain prostaglandins and ergot alkaloids.
