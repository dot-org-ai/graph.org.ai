# Antidiabetic and hyperglycemic alpha-glucosidase inhibitors

**UNSPSC Code**: 51183800
**Family**: [Hormones and hormone antagonists](Hormones and hormone antagonists.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes substances that consist of saccharides that inhibit human digestive enzymes and which thereby disrupt and markedly reduce carbohydrate absorption, substances called alpha-glucosidase inhibitors, that lower blood glucose levels, thus the term ANTIDIABETIC AND HYPERGLYCEMIC ALPHA-GLUCOSIDASE INHIBITORS.
