# Combination antithyroid agents and supplements

**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes combination drugs used to treat hyperthyroidism by reducing the excessive production of thyroid hormones, hence the term ANTITHYROID AGENTS. Typical combination antithyroid agent and supplement constituents are iodine and levothyroxine.
