# Idursulfase

**UNSPSC Code**: 51172486
**Class**: [Combination antacid, antidiarrheal, anti-flatulence, digestive, and gastric preparations](Combination antacid, antidiarrheal, anti-flatulence, digestive, and gastric preparations.mdx)

Idursulfase is a purified form of human iduronate-2-sulfatase, a lysosomal enzyme is used to treat some of the symptoms of a genetic condition called Hunter's syndrome, also called mucopolysaccharidosis.
