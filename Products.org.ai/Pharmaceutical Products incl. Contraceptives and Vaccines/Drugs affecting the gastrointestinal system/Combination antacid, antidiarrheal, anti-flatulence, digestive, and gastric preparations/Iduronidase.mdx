# Iduronidase

**UNSPSC Code**: 51172484
**Class**: [Combination antacid, antidiarrheal, anti-flatulence, digestive, and gastric preparations](Combination antacid, antidiarrheal, anti-flatulence, digestive, and gastric preparations.mdx)
**Family**: [Drugs affecting the gastrointestinal system](../Drugs affecting the gastrointestinal system.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

Iduronidase is an enzyme with the systematic name glycosaminoglycan alpha-L-iduronohydrolase.This enzyme catalyses the hydrolysis of unsulfated alpha-L-iduronosidic linkages in dermatan sulfate and is indicated for people with Mucopolysaccharidosis type 1 (MPS 1).
