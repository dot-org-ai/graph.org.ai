# Combination laxatives

**UNSPSC Code**: 51173200
**Family**: [Drugs affecting the gastrointestinal system](Drugs affecting the gastrointestinal system.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes combination drugs and preparations that produce a soft formed stool, and relax and loosen the bowels, typically used over a protracted period, to relieve constipation, sometimes also called purgatives, but more generally known as LAXATIVES. Typical combination laxative constituents are bisacodyl, casanthranol, docusate, electrolytes, hydrastis, lactose, and magnesium citrate.
