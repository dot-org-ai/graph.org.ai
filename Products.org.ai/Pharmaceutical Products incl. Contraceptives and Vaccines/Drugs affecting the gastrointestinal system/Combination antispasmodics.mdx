# Combination antispasmodics

**UNSPSC Code**: 51172700
**Family**: [Drugs affecting the gastrointestinal system](Drugs affecting the gastrointestinal system.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes combination drugs that inhibit the actions of the parasympathetic nervous system, sometimes also called spasmolytics, or drugs which inhibit spasms, hence the term ANTISPASMODICS. Typical combination antispasmodic constituents are atropine, belladonna, butabarbital, hyoscyamine, hyoscyamus, and methenamine.
