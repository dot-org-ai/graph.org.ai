# Combination hemorrhoidal preparations

**UNSPSC Code**: 51173000
**Family**: [Drugs affecting the gastrointestinal system](Drugs affecting the gastrointestinal system.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes combination drugs and preparations that treat or alleviate symptoms of swollen veins in the lower part of the rectum or anus. Typical combination hemorrhoidal preparation constituents are ismuth, bismuth subgallate, boric acid, camphor, ephedrine, hydrocortisone, lidocaine, pramoxine, and zinc oxide.
