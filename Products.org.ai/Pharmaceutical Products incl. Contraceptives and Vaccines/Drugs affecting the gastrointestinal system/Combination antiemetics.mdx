# Combination antiemetics

**UNSPSC Code**: 51172600
**Family**: [Drugs affecting the gastrointestinal system](Drugs affecting the gastrointestinal system.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes combination drugs that prevent nausea or vomiting, ANTIEMETICS act by a wide range of mechanisms: some act on the medullary control centers (the vomiting center and the chemoreceptive trigger zone) while others affect the peripheral receptors. Typical antiemetic constituents are dextrose and fructose.
