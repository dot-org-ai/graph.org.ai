# Combination neuromuscular blocking agents

**UNSPSC Code**: 51152200
**Family**: [Autonomic nervous system drugs](Autonomic nervous system drugs.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes combination drugs and preparations that interrupt transmission of nerve impulses at the skeletal neuromuscular junction and which therefore bear the name NEUROMUSCULAR BLOCKING AGENTS. Typical combination neuromuscular blocking agent constituents are mivacurium chloride and dextrose.
