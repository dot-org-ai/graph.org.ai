# Antihistamines or H1 blockers

**UNSPSC Code**: 51310000
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes drugs that bind to but do not activate histamine receptors, thereby blocking the actions of histamine or histamine agonists, and which thereby act in the bronchi, capillaries, and other smooth muscles to prevent or allay motion sickness, seasonal rhinitis, and allergic dermatitis and to induce somnolence.
