# Potassium chloride/Sodium chloride/Dextrose/Sodium citrate

**UNSPSC Code**: 51192318
**Class**: [Combination electrolytes/minerals](Combination electrolytesminerals.mdx)
**Family**: [Agents affecting water and electrolytes](../Agents affecting water and electrolytes.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This product is used to replace fluids and minerals (such as sodium, potassium) lost due to diarrhea and vomiting. It helps prevent or treat the loss of too much body water (dehydration). Having the right amount of fluids and minerals is important for the normal functioning of the body.
