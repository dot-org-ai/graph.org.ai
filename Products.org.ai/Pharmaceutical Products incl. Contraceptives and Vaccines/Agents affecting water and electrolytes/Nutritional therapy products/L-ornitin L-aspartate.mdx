# L-ornitin/ L-aspartate

**UNSPSC Code**: 51191967
**Class**: [Nutritional therapy products](Nutritional therapy products.mdx)
**Family**: [Agents affecting water and electrolytes](../Agents affecting water and electrolytes.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

L-ornithine-L-aspartate (LOLA) is a salt of two nonessential L-amino acids: ornithine and aspartic acid. It is used as a medicinal product to lower blood ammonia concentration and to eliminate symptoms of hepatic encephalopathy associated with liver cirrhosis.
