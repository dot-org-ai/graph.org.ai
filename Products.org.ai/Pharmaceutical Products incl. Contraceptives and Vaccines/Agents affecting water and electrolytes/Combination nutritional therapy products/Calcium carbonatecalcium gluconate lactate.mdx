# Calcium carbonate/calcium gluconate lactate

**UNSPSC Code**: 51192460
**Class**: [Combination nutritional therapy products](Combination nutritional therapy products.mdx)
**Family**: [Agents affecting water and electrolytes](../Agents affecting water and electrolytes.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

Calcium is a mineral that is found naturally and is necessary for many normal functions especially bone formation and maintenance. Calcium carbonate/calcium gluconate lactate is a combination of inorganic salts used for the prevention and treatment of calcium deficiency, osteoporosis and for the treatment of rickets and osteomalacia.
