# Combination antigout agents

**UNSPSC Code**: 51192100
**Family**: [Agents affecting water and electrolytes](Agents affecting water and electrolytes.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes combination drugs that increase uric acid excretion by the kidney, decrease uric acid production, or alleviate the pain and inflammation of acute attacks of a hereditary metabolic disorder characterized by recurrent acute arthritis, hyperuricemia, and deposition of sodium urate, called GOUT, hence the term ANTIGOUT AGENTS. Typical combination antigout agent constituents are colchicine and probenecid.
