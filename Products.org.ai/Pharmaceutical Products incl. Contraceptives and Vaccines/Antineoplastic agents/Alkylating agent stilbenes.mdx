# Alkylating agent stilbenes

**UNSPSC Code**: 51112800
**Family**: [Antineoplastic agents](Antineoplastic agents.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes organic compounds that contain the functional group 1,2-diphenylethylene, substances called stilbenes, that constitute reactive chemicals that introduce into biologically active molecules alkyl radicals and thereby disrupt functioning of the same, substances called alkyl-ating agents, thus the term ALKYLATING AGENT STILBENES.
