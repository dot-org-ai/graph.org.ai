# Alkylating agent nitrosourea compounds

**UNSPSC Code**: 51112600
**Family**: [Antineoplastic agents](Antineoplastic agents.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes compounds that bear a nitroso (R-NO) group and a urea, substances called nitrosoureas, that constitute reactive chemicals that introduce into biologically active molecules alkyl radicals and thereby disrupt functioning of the same, substances called alkyl-ating agents, thus the term ALKYLATING AGENT NITROSOUREA COMPOUNDS.
