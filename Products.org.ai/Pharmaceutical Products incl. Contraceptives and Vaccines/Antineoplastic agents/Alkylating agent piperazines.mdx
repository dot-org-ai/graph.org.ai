# Alkylating agent piperazines

**UNSPSC Code**: 51113500
**Family**: [Antineoplastic agents](Antineoplastic agents.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes six-membered ring with two nitrogen atoms positioned opposite to one another, substances called piperazines, that constitute reactive chemicals that introduce into biologically active molecules alkyl radicals and thereby disrupt functioning of the same, substances called alkyl-ating agents, thus the term ALKYLATING AGENT PIPERAZINES.
