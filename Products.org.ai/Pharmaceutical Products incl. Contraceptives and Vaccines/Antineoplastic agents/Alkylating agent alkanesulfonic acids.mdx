# Alkylating agent alkanesulfonic acids

**UNSPSC Code**: 51112300
**Family**: [Antineoplastic agents](Antineoplastic agents.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes sulfonic acid derivatives that are substituted with an aliphatic hydrocarbon group, substances called alkanesulfonic acids, that constitute reactive chemicals that introduce into biologically active molecules alkyl radicals and thereby disrupt functioning of the same, substances called alkyl-ating agents, thus the term ALKYLATING AGENT ALKANESULFONIC ACIDS.
