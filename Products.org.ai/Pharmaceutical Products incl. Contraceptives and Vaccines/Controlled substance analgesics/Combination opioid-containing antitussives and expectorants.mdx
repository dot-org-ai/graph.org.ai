# Combination opioid-containing antitussives and expectorants

**UNSPSC Code**: 51373400
**Family**: [Controlled substance analgesics](Controlled substance analgesics.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes combination drugs and preparations that do bind to opioid receptors, which do derive from opium or morphine, which are addictive, and which are narcotics, that a) suppress cough, hence the term ANTITUSSIVES; and/or b) increase mucous excretion, hence the term EXPECTORANTS. These drugs therefore bear the names OPIOID-CONTAINING ANTITUSSIVES AND EXPECTORANTS. Typical combination opioid-containing antitussive and expectorant constituents are codeine, dihydrocodeine, hydrocodone, morphine, etc.
