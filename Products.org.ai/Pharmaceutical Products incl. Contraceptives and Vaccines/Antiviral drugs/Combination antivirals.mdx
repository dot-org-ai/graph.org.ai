# Combination antivirals

**UNSPSC Code**: 51343800
**Family**: [Antiviral drugs](Antiviral drugs.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes combination drugs used in the prophylaxis or therapy of viral diseases (or diseases that viruses cause), which operate by preventing viral replication by inhibiting viral DNA polymerase; binding to specific cell-surface receptors and inhibiting viral penetration or uncoating; inhibiting viral protein synthesis; or blocking late stages of virus assembly. Typical combination antiviral constituents are abacavir, efavirenz, emtricitabine, interferon alfa-2b, lamivudine, lopinavir, and peginterferon.
