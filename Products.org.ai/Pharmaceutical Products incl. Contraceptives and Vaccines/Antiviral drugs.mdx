# Antiviral drugs

**UNSPSC Code**: 51340000
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes agents used in the prophylaxis or therapy of viral diseases. Some of the ways they may act include preventing viral replication by inhibiting viral DNA polymerase; binding to specific cell-surface receptors and inhibiting viral penetration or uncoating; inhibiting viral protein synthesis; or blocking late stages of virus assembly.
