# Combination antimicrobials

**Family**: [Antibacterials](Antibacterials.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes combination drugs that prevent infectious agents or organisms from spreading or which kill infectious agents and thereby prevent spread of infection. Typical combination antimicrobial constituents aztreonam, cilastatin, methenamine, methinamine, phenazopyridine, sulfadiazine, sulfamethizole, and sulfamethoxazole.
