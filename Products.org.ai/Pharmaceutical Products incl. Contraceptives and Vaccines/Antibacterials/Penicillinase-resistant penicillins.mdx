# Penicillinase-resistant penicillins

**UNSPSC Code**: 51283500
**Family**: [Antibacterials](Antibacterials.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes highly-target-specific, second-generation penicillins chiefly employed against staphylococcal infections, especially against Staphylococcus aureus, and which are not rendered inactive by penicillinas, a beta-lactamase or bacteria-produced enzyme preferentially cleaving penicillins.
