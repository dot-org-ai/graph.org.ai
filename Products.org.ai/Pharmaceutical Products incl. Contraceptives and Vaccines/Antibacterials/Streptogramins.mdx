# Streptogramins

**UNSPSC Code**: 51283800
**Family**: [Antibacterials](Antibacterials.mdx)
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](../Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes a class of natural cyclic peptide antibiotics produced by certain subspecies of streptomyces, hence the name STREPTO-GRAMIN, STREPTO-GRAMINS, and STREPTOGRAMINS. They include two structurally unrelated components, streptogramin group a and streptogramin group b, which generally act synergistically to inhibit bacterial growth.
