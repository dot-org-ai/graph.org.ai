# Sympathomimetic or adrenergic drugs

**UNSPSC Code**: 51390000
**Segment**: [Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines](Pharmaceutical Products incl. Contraceptives and Vaccines.mdx)

This classification denotes drugs that mimic the effects of stimulating postganglionic adrenergic sympathetic nerves. Included here are drugs that directly stimulate adrenergic receptors and drugs that act indirectly by provoking the release of adrenergic transmitters.
