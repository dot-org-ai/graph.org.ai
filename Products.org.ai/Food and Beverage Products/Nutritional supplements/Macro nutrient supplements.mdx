# Macro nutrient supplements

**UNSPSC Code**: 50501700
**Family**: [Nutritional supplements](Nutritional supplements.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

Nutritional supplements are used to ensure that adequate amounts of nutrients are obtained on a daily basis, if optimal amounts cannot be obtained through a varied diet. Whereas vitamins and minerals are required in only trace amounts, this Class contains dietary fiber, fatty acids, amino acids, and other substances consumed in macro levels. This category is intended for nutritional supplements designed and marketed in dosage form (typically tablets, capsules, prepared drinks and drink mixes (powders)) for the ultimate consumer.
