# Nutritional sodium supplement

**UNSPSC Code**: 50501612
**Class**: [Nutritional mineral supplements](Nutritional mineral supplements.mdx)
**Family**: [Nutritional supplements](../Nutritional supplements.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

Mineral supplements are used to ensure that adequate amounts of nutrients are obtained on a daily basis, if optimal amounts cannot be obtained through a varied diet. Sodium is a systemic electrolyte and is essential in coregulating ATP with potassium.
