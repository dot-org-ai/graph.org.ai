# Nutritional mineral supplements

**UNSPSC Code**: 50501600
**Family**: [Nutritional supplements](Nutritional supplements.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

Mineral supplements are used to ensure that adequate amounts of nutrients are obtained on a daily basis, if optimal amounts cannot be obtained through a varied diet. Dietary minerals are the chemical elements required by humans, other than the four elements carbon, hydrogen, nitrogen, and oxygen present in common organic molecules.
