# Sodium monofluorophosphate

**UNSPSC Code**: 50501805
**Class**: [Combination nutritional supplements](Combination nutritional supplements.mdx)
**Family**: [Nutritional supplements](../Nutritional supplements.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

This classification denotes a nutritional supplement with the molecular formula FO3P.2Na, a preparation that US FDA recognizes as an active ingredient or moiety under Unique Ingredient Identifier C810JCZ56Q, chemically known as phosphorofluoridic acid, sodium salt but more generally known as sodium monofluorophosphate, which bears U.S. NIH Compound Identifier 24266. European Medicines Agency schedules Sodium monofluorophosphate in its eXtended EudraVigilance Medicinal Product Dictionary or XEVMPD under Index SUB33441. World Health Organization schedules sodium monofluorophosphate in its Anatomical Therapeutic Chemical (ATC) Classification and its WHO-DD or Drug Dictionary.
