# Choline

**UNSPSC Code**: 50501514
**Class**: [Vitamin supplements](Vitamin supplements.mdx)
**Family**: [Nutritional supplements](../Nutritional supplements.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

This classification denotes a water-soluble vitamin with the molecular formula C5H14NO, a preparation that US FDA recognizes as an active ingredient or moiety under Unique Ingredient Identifier N91BDP6H0X, chemically known as 2-hydroxy-n,n,n-trimethylethanaminium but more generally known as choline, which bears U.S. National Institutes of Health Compound Identifier 305. The European Medicines Agency schedules Choline in its eXtended EudraVigilance Medicinal Product Dictionary or XEVMPD under Index SUB13349MIG. CHOLINE is scheduled in the U.S. International Trade Commission's Harmonized Tariff System (HTS) Pharmaceutical Appendix. SMILES: OCC[N](C)(C)C
