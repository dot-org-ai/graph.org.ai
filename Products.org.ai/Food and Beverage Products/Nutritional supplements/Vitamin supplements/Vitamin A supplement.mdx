# Vitamin A supplement

**UNSPSC Code**: 50501501
**Class**: [Vitamin supplements](Vitamin supplements.mdx)
**Family**: [Nutritional supplements](../Nutritional supplements.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

Vitamin supplements are used to ensure that adequate amounts of nutrients are obtained on a daily basis, if optimal amounts cannot be obtained through a varied diet. Vitamin A is a group of nutritionally unsaturated hydrocarbons that includes retinol, retinal, retinoic acid, and several provitamin A carotenoids including beta-carotene.
