# Vitamin B2 supplement

**UNSPSC Code**: 50501503
**Class**: [Vitamin supplements](Vitamin supplements.mdx)
**Family**: [Nutritional supplements](../Nutritional supplements.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

Vitamin supplements are used to ensure that adequate amounts of nutrients are obtained on a daily basis, if optimal amounts cannot be obtained through a varied diet. Vitamin B2 (or riboflavin) plays a key role in energy metabolism, and for the metabolism of fats, ketone bodies, carbohydrates, and proteins.
