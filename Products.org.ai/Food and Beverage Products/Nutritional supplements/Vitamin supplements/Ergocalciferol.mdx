# Ergocalciferol

**UNSPSC Code**: 50501516
**Class**: [Vitamin supplements](Vitamin supplements.mdx)
**Family**: [Nutritional supplements](../Nutritional supplements.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

This classification denotes a vitamin d with the molecular formula C28H44O, a preparation that US FDA recognizes as an active ingredient or moiety under Unique Ingredient Identifier VS041H42XC, chemically known as 9,10-secoergosta-5,7,10(19),22-tetraen-3-ol, (3beta,5z,7e,22e)- but more generally known as ergocalciferol, which bears U.S. National Institutes of Health Compound Identifier 5280793. European Medicines Agency schedules Ergocalciferol in its eXtended EudraVigilance Medicinal Product Dictionary or XEVMPD under Index SUB06596MIG. The term ERGOCALCIFEROL is an International Non-Proprietary Name. World Health Organization schedules ergocalciferol in its Anatomical Therapeutic Chemical (ATC) Classification and its WHO-DD or Drug Dictionary. ERGOCALCIFEROL is scheduled in the U.S. International Trade Commission's Harmonized Tariff System (HTS) Pharmaceutical Appendix.
