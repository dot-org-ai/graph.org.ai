# Corn starch or corn flour

**UNSPSC Code**: 50221303
**Class**: [Flour and milled products](Flour and milled products.mdx)
**Family**: [Cereal and pulse products](../Cereal and pulse products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

A starch derivative of the corn or maize grain. It is also grown from the endosperm of the corn kernel. It is usually included as an anti-caking agent in powdered sugar 10X or confectioner's sugar.
