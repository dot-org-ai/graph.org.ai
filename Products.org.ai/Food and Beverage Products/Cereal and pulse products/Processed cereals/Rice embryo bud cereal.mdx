# Rice embryo bud cereal

**UNSPSC Code**: 50221203
**Class**: [Processed cereals](Processed cereals.mdx)
**Family**: [Cereal and pulse products](../Cereal and pulse products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

Cereal comprised of seeds processed from rice.  Composed of the outer peel and inner seed coat of brown rice, it acts to protect the embryo and the endosperm. The cortex contains high levels of crude fiber, ash, vegetable fats, protein, vitamin B1 and many assorted minerals.
