# Frozen vegetables

**UNSPSC Code**: 50440000
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

Plants cultivated for an edible part, as roots, stems, leaves or flower that have been frozen for preservation. Frozen vegetables are those that have been cooled to a temperature at which the vegetable generally becomes hard or rigid in nature. To qualify as being frozen the vegetable should have been subjected to a commercial process for freezing that reached a temperature in excess of -20 degrees Centigrade. Also includes freeze-drying of of these vegetables.
