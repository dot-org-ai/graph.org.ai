# Canned or jarred organic fruit

**UNSPSC Code**: 50370000
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

The ripened seed-bearing part of a plant when fleshy and edible that has been canned or jarred for preservation. To qualify as being canned or jarred the fruit should have been subjected to one or more commercial processes for sterilizing, pressure sealing (vacuum), etc. in order to prevent contamination by microbial agents.
