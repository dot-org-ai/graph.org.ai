# Canned or jarred Non GMO new potatoes

**UNSPSC Code**: 50615607
**Class**: [Canned or jarred Non GMO potatoes](Canned or jarred Non GMO potatoes.mdx)
**Family**: [Canned or jarred Non GMO vegetables](../Canned or jarred Non GMO vegetables.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

The variety of Non GMO potatoes known as Non GMO new potatoes that have been canned or jarred for preservation. This means that they are  produced without the use of methods that genetically modify organisms by means that are not possible under natural conditions.  Such methods include cell fusion, microencapsulation and macro-encapsulation, and recombinant DNA technology (including gene deletion, gene doubling, introducing a foreign gene, and changing the positions of genes when achieved by recombinant DNA technology).  Traditional methods of breeding, conjugation, fermentation, hybridization, in vitro fertilization, or tissue culture are considered to be non-GMO.
