# Canned or jarred Non GMO san marzano tomatoes

**UNSPSC Code**: 50616418

**Family**: [Canned or jarred Non GMO vegetables](../Canned or jarred Non GMO vegetables.mdx)

The variety of Non GMO tomatoes known as Non GMO san marzano tomatoes that have been canned or jarred for preservation. This means that they are  produced without the use of methods that genetically modify organisms by means that are not possible under natural conditions.  Such methods include cell fusion, microencapsulation and macro-encapsulation, and recombinant DNA technology (including gene deletion, gene doubling, introducing a foreign gene, and changing the positions of genes when achieved by recombinant DNA technology).  Traditional methods of breeding, conjugation, fermentation, hybridization, in vitro fertilization, or tissue culture are considered to be non-GMO.
