# Key lime purees

**UNSPSC Code**: 50384602
**Class**: [Lime purees](Lime purees.mdx)
**Family**: [Fresh fruit purees](../Fresh fruit purees.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

the variety of lime grown especially in the florida keys and adjacent area. Smaller than regular "persian" limes, the peel is thin, smooth and greenish-yellow when ripe. The flesh is also greenish-yellow and full of seeds. The interior is divided by 10 to 12 segments, quite juicy and have a higher acidity than regular persian limes. which have been pureed.
