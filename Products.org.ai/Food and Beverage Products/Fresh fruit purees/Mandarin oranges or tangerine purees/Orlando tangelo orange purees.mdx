# Orlando tangelo orange purees

**UNSPSC Code**: 50384829
**Class**: [Mandarin oranges or tangerine purees](Mandarin oranges or tangerine purees.mdx)
**Family**: [Fresh fruit purees](../Fresh fruit purees.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

This variety of early maturing tangelo is noted for its juicy, mild, sweet flavor. Orlandos are flat-round in shape and larger in size. The Orlando tangelo originated as a cross between a Duncan grapefruit and a Dancy tangerine. which have been pureed.
