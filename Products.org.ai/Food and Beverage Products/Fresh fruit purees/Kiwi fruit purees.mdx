# Kiwi fruit purees

**UNSPSC Code**: 50384300
**Family**: [Fresh fruit purees](Fresh fruit purees.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

The type of egg-shaped kiwi fruit have a furry brownish green skin and firm, translucent green flesh with edible purple-black seeds at the centre. Also called chinese gooseberry, edible fruit of the vine actinidia chinensis (family actinidiaceae). The plant is native to china and taiwan and is now grown commercially in new zealand and california. which have been pureed.
