# Canned or jarred organic vegetables

**UNSPSC Code**: 50470000
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

Plants cultivated organically for an edible part, as roots, stems, leaves or flower that have been canned or jarred for preservation. To qualify as being canned or jarred the vegetable should have been subjected to one or more commercial processes for sterilizing, pressure sealing (vacuum), etc. in order to prevent contamination by microbial agents.
