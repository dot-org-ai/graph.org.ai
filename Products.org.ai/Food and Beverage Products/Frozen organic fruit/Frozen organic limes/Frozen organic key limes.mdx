# Frozen organic key limes

**UNSPSC Code**: 50354202
**Class**: [Frozen organic limes](Frozen organic limes.mdx)
**Family**: [Frozen organic fruit](../Frozen organic fruit.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

A lime grown especially in the florida keys and adjacent area. Smaller than regular "persian" limes, the peel is thin, smooth and greenish-yellow when ripe. The flesh is also greenish-yellow and full of seeds. The interior is divided by 10 to 12 segments, It has been frozen for preservation.
