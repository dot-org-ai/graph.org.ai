# Dried organic kiwi fruit

**UNSPSC Code**: 50333900
**Family**: [Dried organic fruit](Dried organic fruit.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

The egg-shaped kiwi fruit has a furry brownish green skin and firm, translucent green flesh with edible purple-black seeds at the centre. Also called chinese gooseberry, edible fruit of the vine actinidia chinensis (family actinidiaceae). The plant is native to china and taiwan and is now grown commercially in new zealand and california. It has been dried for preservation.
