# Green onion kimchi

**UNSPSC Code**: 50467008
**Class**: [Canned or jarred kimchis](Canned or jarred kimchis.mdx)
**Family**: [Canned or jarred vegetables](../Canned or jarred vegetables.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

A type of Kimchi that uses medium thick green onions known as jjokpa, which are fermented to maturity in powdered red pepper gochutgaru, garlic, ginger and seasoned with lots of myeolchi jeot (salted anchovies).
