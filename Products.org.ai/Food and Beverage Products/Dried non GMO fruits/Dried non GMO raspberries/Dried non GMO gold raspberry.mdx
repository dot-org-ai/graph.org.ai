# Dried non GMO gold raspberry

**UNSPSC Code**: 50536210
**Class**: [Dried non GMO raspberries](Dried non GMO raspberries.mdx)
**Family**: [Dried non GMO fruits](../Dried non GMO fruits.mdx)

The variety of non GMO raspberries known as non GMO gold raspberry that has been dried for preservation. This means that they are  produced without the use of methods that genetically modify organisms by means that are not possible under natural conditions.   Such methods include cell fusion, microencapsulation and macro-encapsulation, and recombinant DNA technology (including gene deletion, gene doubling, introducing a foreign gene, and changing the positions of genes when achieved by recombinant DNA technology).   Traditional methods of breeding, conjugation, fermentation, hybridization, in vitro fertilization, or tissue culture are considered to be non-GMO.
