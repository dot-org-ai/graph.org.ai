# Frozen kumquats

**UNSPSC Code**: 50344000
**Family**: [Frozen fruit](Frozen fruit.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

Any of several small yellow to orange citrus fruits with sweet spongy rind and somewhat acid pulp that are used chiefly for preserves produced by a tree or shrub (genus fortunella) of the rue family that have been frozen for preservation.
