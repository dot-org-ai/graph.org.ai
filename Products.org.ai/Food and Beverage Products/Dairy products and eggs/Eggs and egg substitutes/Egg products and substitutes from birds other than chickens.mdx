# Egg products and substitutes from birds other than chickens

**UNSPSC Code**: 50131631
**Class**: [Eggs and egg substitutes](Eggs and egg substitutes.mdx)
**Family**: [Dairy products and eggs](../Dairy products and eggs.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

Includes any homogenous dried, frozen, liquid, or further processed content of an egg after removal from the shell from birds other than chicken and to which other food ingredients (meat, cheese, vegetables) have not been added. Excludes recipe-based egg-based products such as quiche mixes or mayonnaise.
