# In shell table egg from squabs or pigeons

**UNSPSC Code**: 50131620
**Class**: [Eggs and egg substitutes](Eggs and egg substitutes.mdx)
**Family**: [Dairy products and eggs](../Dairy products and eggs.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

Includes eggs in the shell that have been sorted to remove eggs of lower quality such as cracked and dirty eggs and are often sorted by size that have been laid by birds of the species Columba livia. Eggs may be unwashed, washed, washed and oiled, aged, and/or pasteurized in the shell. Excludes eggs that have been cooked.
