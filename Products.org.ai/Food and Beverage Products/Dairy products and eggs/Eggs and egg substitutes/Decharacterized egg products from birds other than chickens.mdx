# Decharacterized egg products from birds other than chickens

**UNSPSC Code**: 50131632
**Class**: [Eggs and egg substitutes](Eggs and egg substitutes.mdx)
**Family**: [Dairy products and eggs](../Dairy products and eggs.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

Includes any dried, frozen, or liquid content of an egg after removal from the shell from birds other than chicken to which a substance has been added to distinguish the product as not fit for human consumption.
