# In shell checked and dirty egg industrial from birds other than chickens

**UNSPSC Code**: 50131626
**Class**: [Eggs and egg substitutes](Eggs and egg substitutes.mdx)
**Family**: [Dairy products and eggs](../Dairy products and eggs.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

Includes eggs in the shell from birds other than chikcen that have cracked shells and/or have dirt on the surface that makes the eggs unsuitable for use as a table egg. These eggs are typically those that were rejected during a quality sorting process. Eggs may be unwashed, washed, or washed and oiled. Excludes eggs that have been cooked.
