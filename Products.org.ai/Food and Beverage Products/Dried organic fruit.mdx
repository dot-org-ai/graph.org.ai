# Dried organic fruit

**UNSPSC Code**: 50330000
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

The ripened seed-bearing part of a plant when fleshy and edible that has been dried for preservation. To qualify as being dried, the fruit should have been subjected to a commercial process for moisture reduction.
