# Dried vegetables

**UNSPSC Code**: 50420000
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

Plants cultivated for an edible part, as roots, stems, leaves or flower that have been dried for preservation. To qualify as being dried, the vegetables should have been subjected to a commercial process for moisture reduction.
