# Non-GMO

**UNSPSC Code**: 50630300
**Family**: [Fresh vegetables processed](Fresh vegetables processed.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

Non-GMO  fresh vegetables which have been processed. Fresh vegetables that have been grown and harvested from plants certified as being non GMO. This means that they are produced without the use of methods that genetically modify organisms by means that are not possible under natural conditions.  Such methods include cell fusion, microencapsulation and macro-encapsulation, and recombinant DNA technology (including gene deletion, gene doubling, introducing a foreign gene, and changing the positions of genes when achieved by recombinant DNA technology). Traditional methods of breeding, conjugation, fermentation, hybridization, in vitro fertilization, or tissue culture are considered to be non-GMO.
