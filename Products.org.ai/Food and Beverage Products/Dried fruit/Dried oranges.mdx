# Dried oranges

**UNSPSC Code**: 50325000
**Family**: [Dried fruit](Dried fruit.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

Any of several species of small trees or shrubs in the genus citrus of the rue or citrus family and their fruits. Grown in tropical and subtropical regions, the nearly round fruits have leathery, oily rinds and edible, juicy inner flesh rich in vitamin c. They have been dried for preservation.
