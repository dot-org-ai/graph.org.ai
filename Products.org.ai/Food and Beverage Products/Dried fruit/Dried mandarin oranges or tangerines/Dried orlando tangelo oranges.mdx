# Dried orlando tangelo oranges

**UNSPSC Code**: 50324429
**Class**: [Dried mandarin oranges or tangerines](Dried mandarin oranges or tangerines.mdx)
**Family**: [Dried fruit](../Dried fruit.mdx)

This early maturing tangelo is noted for its juicy, mild, sweet flavor. Orlandos are flat-round in shape and larger in size. The Orlando tangelo originated as a cross between a Duncan grapefruit and a Dancy tangerine. This tangelo has been dried for preservation.
