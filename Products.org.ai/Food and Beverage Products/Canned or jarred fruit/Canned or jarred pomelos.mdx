# Canned or jarred pomelos

**UNSPSC Code**: 50366000
**Family**: [Canned or jarred fruit](Canned or jarred fruit.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

An exotic large citrus fruit that is an ancient ancestor of the common grapefruit. It is the largest of the citrus fruits with a shape that can be fairly round or slightly pointed at one end (the fruit ranges from nearly round to oblate or pear-shaped). T It has been canned or jarred for preservation.
