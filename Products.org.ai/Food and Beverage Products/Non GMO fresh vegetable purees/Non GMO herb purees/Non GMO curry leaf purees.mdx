# Non GMO curry leaf purees

**Family**: [Non GMO fresh vegetable purees](../Non GMO fresh vegetable purees.mdx)

The variety of Non GMO herbs known as Non GMO curry leaf. This means that they are produced without the use of methods that genetically modify organisms by means that are not possible under natural conditions.  Such methods include cell fusion, microencapsulation and macro-encapsulation, and recombinant DNA technology (including gene deletion, gene doubling, introducing a foreign gene, and changing the positions of genes when achieved by recombinant DNA technology).  Traditional methods of breeding, conjugation, fermentation, hybridization, in vitro fertilization, or tissue culture are considered to be non-GMO.
