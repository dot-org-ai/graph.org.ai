# Orlando tangelo oranges

**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

This early maturing tangelo is noted for its juicy, mild, sweet flavor. Orlandos are flat-round in shape and larger in size. The Orlando tangelo originated as a cross between a Duncan grapefruit and a Dancy tangerine.
