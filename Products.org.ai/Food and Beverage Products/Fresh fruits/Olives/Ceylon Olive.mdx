# Ceylon Olive

**UNSPSC Code**: 50307238
**Class**: [Olives](Olives.mdx)
**Family**: [Fresh fruits](../Fresh fruits.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

The Ceylon Olive (Elaeocarpus serratus L.; family Elaeocarpaceae) also known as the Veralu or veralakkai is a tropical fruit found in the Indian Subcontinent, Indo-China and South East Asia. It is an ornamental medium sized tree indigenous to Sri Lanka, producing smooth, ovoid green fruits. The fruit has nutritive and medicinal values.
