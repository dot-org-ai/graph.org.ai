# Kiwi fruit actinidia chinensis

**UNSPSC Code**: 50303907
**Class**: [Kiwi fruit](Kiwi fruit.mdx)
**Family**: [Fresh fruits](../Fresh fruits.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

A kiwi fruit, also known as actinidia chinensis, has an oval shape, a fuzzy brown exterior, and a bright green flesh. A fruiting vine native to China, the golden kiwifruit is widely cultivated throughout the world, including New Zealand, Italy, and California. There are some 40 species of Actinidia, and the most commonly cultivated commercial kiwifruit is Actinidia deliciosa.
