# Water extracted soluble orange solids wesos

**UNSPSC Code**: 50307502
**Class**: [Fresh fruit byproducts](Fresh fruit byproducts.mdx)
**Family**: [Fresh fruits](../Fresh fruits.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

In the final stages of separating orange pulp, the pulp is washed with water to recover extra solids. When the pulp and wash are separated, the wash water is concentrated to make water-extracted soluble orange solids (wesos).
