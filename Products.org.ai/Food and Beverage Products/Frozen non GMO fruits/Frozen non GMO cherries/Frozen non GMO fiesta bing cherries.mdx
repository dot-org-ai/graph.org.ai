# Frozen non GMO fiesta bing cherries

**UNSPSC Code**: 50542508
**Class**: [Frozen non GMO cherries](Frozen non GMO cherries.mdx)
**Family**: [Frozen non GMO fruits](../Frozen non GMO fruits.mdx)
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](../../Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

The variety of non GMO cherries known as non GMO fiesta bing cherries that have been frozen for preservation. This means that they are  produced without the use of methods that genetically modify organisms by means that are not possible under natural conditions.   Such methods include cell fusion, microencapsulation and macro-encapsulation, and recombinant DNA technology (including gene deletion, gene doubling, introducing a foreign gene, and changing the positions of genes when achieved by recombinant DNA technology).   Traditional methods of breeding, conjugation, fermentation, hybridization, in vitro fertilization, or tissue culture are considered to be non-GMO.
