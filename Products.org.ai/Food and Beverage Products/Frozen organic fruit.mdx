# Frozen organic fruit

**UNSPSC Code**: 50350000
**Segment**: [Food and Beverage Products](Food and Beverage Products.mdx)

The ripened seed-bearing part of a plant when fleshy and edible that has been frozen for preservation. Frozen fruits are those that have been cooled to a temperature at which the fruit generally becomes hard or rigid in nature. To qualify as being frozen the fruit should have been subjected to a commercial process for freezing that reached a temperature in excess of -20 degrees Centigrade. Also includes freeze-drying of of these fruits.
