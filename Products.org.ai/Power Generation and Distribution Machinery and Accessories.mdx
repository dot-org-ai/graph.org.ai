# Power Generation and Distribution Machinery and Accessories

**UNSPSC Code**: 26000000

This segment includes machinery for converting any of various forms of energy into mechanical force or converting mechanical force into electricity. This segment also includes mechanisms used to transmit power from an engine to an actuator and systems and components used in the distribution of electrical and mechanical energy such as motors, generators, transformers and transmissions.
