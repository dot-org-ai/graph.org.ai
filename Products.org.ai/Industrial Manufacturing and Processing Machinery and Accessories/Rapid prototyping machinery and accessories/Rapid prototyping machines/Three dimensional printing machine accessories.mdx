# Three dimensional printing machine accessories

**UNSPSC Code**: 23261508

Three-dimensional (3D) printing machine accessories are additional components or tools that enhance the functionality, versatility, and performance of a 3D printer. These accessories are designed to work in conjunction with the 3D printer to improve the printing process, allow for greater customization, and expand the range of materials and objects that can be printed.
