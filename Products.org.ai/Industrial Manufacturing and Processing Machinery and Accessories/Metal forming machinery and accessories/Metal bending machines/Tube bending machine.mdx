# Tube bending machine

**UNSPSC Code**: 23251505

**Segment**: [Industrial Manufacturing and Processing Machinery and Accessories](../../Industrial Manufacturing and Processing Machinery and Accessories.mdx)

Bending machines that use internal mandrels, or filling tubes with particulate materials such as sand, which are often necessary to prevent collapsing of the tubes during the bending. Solid rods and structural shapes are also bent by these techniques.
