# Trailing shield

**UNSPSC Code**: 23271720
**Class**: [Welding and soldering and brazing accessories](Welding and soldering and brazing accessories.mdx)

Trailing shield is a welding enhancement tool designed for high quality gas coverage during welding of Titanium and other high alloy metals. It provides better gas coverage in the heat effected zone (HAZ) and prevents oxidation, weld defects, re-work and allows faster welding.
