# Braze welding machine

**UNSPSC Code**: 23271417
**Class**: [Welding machinery](Welding machinery.mdx)

A type of welding machine that uses a bronze or brass filler rod coated with flux to join steel workpieces. It is virtually identical to the equipment used in brazing, except there is no capillary action.
