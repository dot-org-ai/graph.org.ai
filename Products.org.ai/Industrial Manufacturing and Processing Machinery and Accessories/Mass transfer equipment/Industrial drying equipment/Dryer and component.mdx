# Dryer and component

**UNSPSC Code**: 23201205
**Class**: [Industrial drying equipment](Industrial drying equipment.mdx)
**Family**: [Mass transfer equipment](../Mass transfer equipment.mdx)
**Segment**: [Industrial Manufacturing and Processing Machinery and Accessories](../../Industrial Manufacturing and Processing Machinery and Accessories.mdx)

A dryer (or drier) is a machine or apparatus used to remove moisture. It may be a small laboratory oven taking a few grams of moist material or a large industrial unit handling tons of wet feed per hour. An industrial dryer is never a stand-alone unit; it is part of a drying system which includes the feeder, the heaters and product collectors besides the actual drying section itself
