# Schoepentoeter inlet device

**UNSPSC Code**: 23201011
**Class**: [Gas liquid contacting systems](Gas liquid contacting systems.mdx)
**Family**: [Mass transfer equipment](../Mass transfer equipment.mdx)
**Segment**: [Industrial Manufacturing and Processing Machinery and Accessories](../../Industrial Manufacturing and Processing Machinery and Accessories.mdx)

Schoepentoeter is a feed inlet vane device commonly used for introducing gas-liquid mixtures into distillation columns or gas-liquid separators . Schoepentoeter has two main functions 1) To separate the liquid from the gas and 2) To distribute the vapor in the gas compartment of the column.
