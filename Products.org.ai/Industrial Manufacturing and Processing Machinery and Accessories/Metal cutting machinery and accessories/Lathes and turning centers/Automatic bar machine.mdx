# Automatic bar machine

**UNSPSC Code**: 23242301
**Class**: [Lathes and turning centers](Lathes and turning centers.mdx)
**Family**: [Metal cutting machinery and accessories](../Metal cutting machinery and accessories.mdx)
**Segment**: [Industrial Manufacturing and Processing Machinery and Accessories](../../Industrial Manufacturing and Processing Machinery and Accessories.mdx)

All operations on these machines are performed automatically, with tools attached to a turret. The bar stock is fed forward automatically after each part is machined to finished dimensions and cut off. These machines may be equipped with single or multiple spindles.
