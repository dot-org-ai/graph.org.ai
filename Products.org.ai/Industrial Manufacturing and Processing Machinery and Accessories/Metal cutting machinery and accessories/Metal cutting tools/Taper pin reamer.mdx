# Taper pin reamer

**UNSPSC Code**: 23241617
**Class**: [Metal cutting tools](Metal cutting tools.mdx)
**Family**: [Metal cutting machinery and accessories](../Metal cutting machinery and accessories.mdx)
**Segment**: [Industrial Manufacturing and Processing Machinery and Accessories](../../Industrial Manufacturing and Processing Machinery and Accessories.mdx)

A type of reamer manufactured with a taper of 1/4 per foot. They are used to ream holes into which standard taper pins will fit. The point of each reamer will enter the hole reamed by the next smaller size.
