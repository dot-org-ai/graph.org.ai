# Tool presetter

**UNSPSC Code**: 23242608
**Class**: [Specialized or miscellaneous metal cutting machines](Specialized or miscellaneous metal cutting machines.mdx)
**Family**: [Metal cutting machinery and accessories](../Metal cutting machinery and accessories.mdx)
**Segment**: [Industrial Manufacturing and Processing Machinery and Accessories](../../Industrial Manufacturing and Processing Machinery and Accessories.mdx)

Tool measuring and inspection systems that provide tool presetting functions prior to the tool being used in a specific machine. A tool presetter may use video cameras, manage tool assemblies for jobs running on machining centers and cells, allow CAD files to be viewed on presetter, or run complex measuring routines.
