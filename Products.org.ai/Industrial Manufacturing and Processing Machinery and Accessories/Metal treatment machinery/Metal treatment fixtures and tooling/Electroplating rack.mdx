# Electroplating rack

**UNSPSC Code**: 23281801
**Class**: [Metal treatment fixtures and tooling](Metal treatment fixtures and tooling.mdx)
**Family**: [Metal treatment machinery](../Metal treatment machinery.mdx)
**Segment**: [Industrial Manufacturing and Processing Machinery and Accessories](../../Industrial Manufacturing and Processing Machinery and Accessories.mdx)

Electroplating racks are used in electrochemical plating operations. They hold the workpieces to be plate and are electrically conductive at certain contact points, to enable the plating process. Noncontact surfaces are covered with insulating material.
