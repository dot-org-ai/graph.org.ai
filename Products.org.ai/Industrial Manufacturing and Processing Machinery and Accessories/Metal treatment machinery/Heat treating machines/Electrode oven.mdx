# Electrode oven

**UNSPSC Code**: 23281604
**Class**: [Heat treating machines](Heat treating machines.mdx)
**Family**: [Metal treatment machinery](../Metal treatment machinery.mdx)
**Segment**: [Industrial Manufacturing and Processing Machinery and Accessories](../../Industrial Manufacturing and Processing Machinery and Accessories.mdx)

Electrode ovens are designed to store welding electrodes properly in order to prevent moisture absorption, which produces porosity in the weld deposit. Electrode ovens also serve as a redrying equipment in order restore the welding electrodes' quality.
