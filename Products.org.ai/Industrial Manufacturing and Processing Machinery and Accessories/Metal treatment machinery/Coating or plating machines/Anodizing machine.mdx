# Anodizing machine

**UNSPSC Code**: 23281501
**Class**: [Coating or plating machines](Coating or plating machines.mdx)
**Family**: [Metal treatment machinery](../Metal treatment machinery.mdx)
**Segment**: [Industrial Manufacturing and Processing Machinery and Accessories](../../Industrial Manufacturing and Processing Machinery and Accessories.mdx)

Machine wherein the workpiece is the anode in an electrolytic cell immersed in an acid bath, resulting in chemical adsorption of oxygen from the bath. Anodizing is an oxidation process in which the workpiece surfaces are converted to a hard and porous oxide layer that provides corrosion resistance and a decorative finish.
