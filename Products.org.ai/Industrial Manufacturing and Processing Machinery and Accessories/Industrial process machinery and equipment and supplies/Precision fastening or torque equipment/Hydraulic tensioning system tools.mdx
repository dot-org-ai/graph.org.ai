# Hydraulic tensioning system tools

**UNSPSC Code**: 23153703
**Class**: [Precision fastening or torque equipment](Precision fastening or torque equipment.mdx)
**Family**: [Industrial process machinery and equipment and supplies](../Industrial process machinery and equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Industrial Manufacturing and Processing Machinery and Accessories](../../Industrial Manufacturing and Processing Machinery and Accessories.mdx)

Hydraulic tensioning systems are hydraulic tools that use stretching to tighten studs. Once a stud is stretched, a nut is then put on the joint. Then the load on the stud is removed resulting in a clamping force on the now bolted joint.
