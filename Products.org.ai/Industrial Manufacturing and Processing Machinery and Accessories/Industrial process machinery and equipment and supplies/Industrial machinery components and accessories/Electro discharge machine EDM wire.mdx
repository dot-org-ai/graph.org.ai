# Electro discharge machine EDM wire

**UNSPSC Code**: 23153141
**Class**: [Industrial machinery components and accessories](Industrial machinery components and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Industrial process machinery and equipment and supplies](../Industrial process machinery and equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Industrial Manufacturing and Processing Machinery and Accessories](../../Industrial Manufacturing and Processing Machinery and Accessories.mdx)

Attachment used in a wire electro discharge machine EDM. Removes metal by burning the work piece with a very thin wire instead of cutting or grinding. One of three basic types of EDM. The other two are electrode EDM and small-hole EDM.
