# Steel A-frame

**UNSPSC Code**: 30241513

**Family**: [Portable Structure Building Components](../Portable Structure Building Components.mdx)

Triangular shaped structure composed of square steel tubing with a stirrup welded at the top in which the stringer or support beam sits. Acts as a structural support. In some cases, the stirrup can be mounted on a threaded rod or screw-jack which makes it adjustable.
