# Tower gangway

**UNSPSC Code**: 30103626
**Class**: [Structural products](Structural products.mdx)
**Family**: [Structural components and basic shapes](../Structural components and basic shapes.mdx)
**Segment**: [Structures and Building and Construction and Manufacturing Components and Supplies](../../Structures and Building and Construction and Manufacturing Components and Supplies.mdx)

Gangway access towers type with a telescopic gangway that moves vertically along the tower by means of a lifting platform. The tower gangway can cover all levels of the ship deck, from small barges to ultra-large crude carriers, in combination with ship movements resulting from tidal movements, loading and unloading and wave movements.
