# Commercial use combination oven

A combination oven combines the elements of heat and steam to provide a controlled cooking environment with reduced food shrinkage. It allows cooking at low temperatures with steam, through mid range temperatures with a combination of steam and dry heat through to high temperature dry heat cooking that permits grilling of foods
