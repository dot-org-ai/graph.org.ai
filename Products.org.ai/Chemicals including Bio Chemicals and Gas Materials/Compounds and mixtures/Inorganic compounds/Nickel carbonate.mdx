# Nickel carbonate

**UNSPSC Code**: 12352326
**Class**: [Inorganic compounds](Inorganic compounds.mdx)
**Family**: [Compounds and mixtures](../Compounds and mixtures.mdx)
**Segment**: [Chemicals including Bio Chemicals and Gas Materials](../../Chemicals including Bio Chemicals and Gas Materials.mdx)

Nickel Carbonate is a light green, crystalline inorganic compound that produces toxic gases upon heating. Nickel carbonate is used in electroplating, to prepare nickel monoxide, to make colored glass and as a catalyst in the treatment of wastewater.
