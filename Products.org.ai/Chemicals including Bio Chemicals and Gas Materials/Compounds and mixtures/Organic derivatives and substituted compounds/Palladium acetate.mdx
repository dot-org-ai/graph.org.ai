# Palladium acetate

**UNSPSC Code**: 12352141
**Class**: [Organic derivatives and substituted compounds](Organic derivatives and substituted compounds.mdx)
**Family**: [Compounds and mixtures](../Compounds and mixtures.mdx)
**Segment**: [Chemicals including Bio Chemicals and Gas Materials](../../Chemicals including Bio Chemicals and Gas Materials.mdx)

Palladium acetate is a chemical compound of palladium described by the formula [Pd(O2CCH3)2]n, abbreviated [Pd(OAc)2]n. It is more reactive than the analogous platinum compound. Depending on the value of n, the compound is soluble in many organic solvents and is commonly used as a catalyst for organic reactions.
