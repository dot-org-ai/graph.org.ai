# Ethyl mercaptan

**UNSPSC Code**: 12352152
**Class**: [Organic derivatives and substituted compounds](Organic derivatives and substituted compounds.mdx)
**Family**: [Compounds and mixtures](../Compounds and mixtures.mdx)
**Segment**: [Chemicals including Bio Chemicals and Gas Materials](../../Chemicals including Bio Chemicals and Gas Materials.mdx)

Ethyl mercaptan is a colorless or yellowish liquid or a gas with a pungnet , garlic or skunk-like odor. It is used as an additive to odorless gases likebutane, porpane, and petroleum to give them a warning odor. Also used in the manufacture of fungicides, pharmaceuticals, insecticides, resins and other chemicals.
