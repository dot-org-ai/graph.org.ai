# N-acetylanthranilic acid

**UNSPSC Code**: 12361506
**Class**: [Narcotic drug precursors](Narcotic drug precursors.mdx)
**Family**: [Pharmaceutical drug precursors](../Pharmaceutical drug precursors.mdx)
**Segment**: [Chemicals including Bio Chemicals and Gas Materials](../../Chemicals including Bio Chemicals and Gas Materials.mdx)

An organic compound with the molecular formula C9H9NO3. It is an intermediate product in catabolism of quinaldine in Arthrobacter sp., and is further metabolized to anthranilic acid.  It is classified as an illicit drug precursor by the United Nations.
