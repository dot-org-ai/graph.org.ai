# Optical brightener

**UNSPSC Code**: 12164603
**Class**: [Paper plastic and fabric modifiers](Paper plastic and fabric modifiers.mdx)
**Family**: [Additives](../Additives.mdx)
**Segment**: [Chemicals including Bio Chemicals and Gas Materials](../../Chemicals including Bio Chemicals and Gas Materials.mdx)
