# Carbon steel instrumentation tube

**UNSPSC Code**: 40183113
**Class**: [Tube fittings](Tube fittings.mdx)
**Family**: [Tubes tubing and tube fittings](../Tubes tubing and tube fittings.mdx)
**Segment**: [Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components](../../Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components.mdx)

Instrumentation tubing is made from carbon steel, and connects to the tubing or tubes utilized to attach instruments to central piping, other machines, appliances, or plumbing equipment. Instrument tubing is usually used adjacent to these piping systems, pressure switches, valves, flow monitors, and permitting the combination of pressure gauges.
