# Crankcase ventilation (CCV) filter

**UNSPSC Code**: 40161535
**Class**: [Filters](Filters.mdx)
**Family**: [Industrial filtering and purification](../Industrial filtering and purification.mdx)
**Segment**: [Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components](../../Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components.mdx)

Crankcase ventilation (CCV) filter is part of a vehicle's crankcase ventilation system in internal combustion engines. This filter removes oil particles and other debris in the gases while releasing excess pressure on the environment.
