# Pump soleplate

**UNSPSC Code**: 40151733
**Class**: [Pump parts and accessories](Pump parts and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Industrial pumps and compressors](../Industrial pumps and compressors.mdx)
**Segment**: [Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components](../../Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components.mdx)

A mounting plate onto which a vertical turbine pump VTP is attached to provide a foundation for installation and maintenance. It allows the removal of the pump without disturbing the foundation. Together with the pump discharge head base flange, it is machined to allow precision mating.
