# Macerator pump

**UNSPSC Code**: 40151565
**Class**: [Pumps](Pumps.mdx)
**Family**: [Industrial pumps and compressors](../Industrial pumps and compressors.mdx)
**Segment**: [Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components](../../Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components.mdx)

A type of pump designed to empty holding tanks. Includes a bronze cutter to grind waste down to small particle size. By grinding down the particles, it makes it possible to eject them (pump out) through the discharge nozzle of the pump.
