# Lubricator pump

**UNSPSC Code**: 40151580
**Class**: [Pumps](Pumps.mdx)
**Family**: [Industrial pumps and compressors](../Industrial pumps and compressors.mdx)
**Segment**: [Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components](../../Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components.mdx)

A type of precision metering pump operating within rugged cast iron reservoirs which contain the lubricant to be pumped. Each pumping unit supplies a single point of lubrication. Lubricators may be furnished in single or multi feed units for any number of points of lubrication. They are available with various drive arrangements, for a broad range of lubricants, viscosities, and required line pressures.
