# Rotary airlock valve

**UNSPSC Code**: 40141644
**Class**: [Valves](Valves.mdx)
**Family**: [Fluid and gas distribution](../Fluid and gas distribution.mdx)
**Segment**: [Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components](../../Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components.mdx)

This valve is made up of a rotor with 6 rigid vanes that moves inside a round casing. The gap between the rotor vanes and the casing is further reduced by means of adjustable vane blades made of teflon or spring steel.
