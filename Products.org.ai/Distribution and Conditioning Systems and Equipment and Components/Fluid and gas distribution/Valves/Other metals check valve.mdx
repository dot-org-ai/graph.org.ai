# Other metals check valve

**UNSPSC Code**: 40141682
**Class**: [Valves](Valves.mdx)
**Family**: [Fluid and gas distribution](../Fluid and gas distribution.mdx)
**Segment**: [Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components](../../Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components.mdx)

A check valve is a type of valve that allows fluid or gas to flow in one direction only, preventing backflow and protecting the equipment downstream. Other metals check valves are check valves made of materials other than the commonly used metals such as brass or stainless steel.
