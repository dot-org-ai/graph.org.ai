# Engineered special valves

**UNSPSC Code**: 40141683
**Class**: [Valves](Valves.mdx)
**Family**: [Fluid and gas distribution](../Fluid and gas distribution.mdx)
**Segment**: [Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components](../../Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components.mdx)

Engineered special valves are custom-made valves designed to meet specific requirements of an application, which cannot be fulfilled by standard valves available in the market. These valves are designed and manufactured based on the unique requirements of a particular application and may involve complex designs, materials, and operating conditions. Special valves Include's (Plug, choke, slide, gate, diaphragm, double block/bleed, block, pressure balanced piston, choke, gate/globe/ball, mono flange etc.)
