# Poppet valve

**UNSPSC Code**: 40141641
**Class**: [Valves](Valves.mdx)
**Family**: [Fluid and gas distribution](../Fluid and gas distribution.mdx)
**Segment**: [Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components](../../Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components.mdx)

A type of valve consisting of a hole and a tapered plug on the end of a shaft also called a valve stem. The shaft guides the plug portion by sliding through a valve guide. The differential pressure seals the valve.
