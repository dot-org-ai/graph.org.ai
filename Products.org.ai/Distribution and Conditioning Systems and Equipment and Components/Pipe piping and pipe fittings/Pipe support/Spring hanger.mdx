# Spring hanger

**UNSPSC Code**: 40175405
**Class**: [Pipe support](Pipe support.mdx)
**Family**: [Pipe piping and pipe fittings](../Pipe piping and pipe fittings.mdx)
**Segment**: [Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components](../../Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components.mdx)

To permit the operational movement of the pipe as it goes from cold to hot or vice versa. It does not cause excessive expansion stresses in the pipe as the spring load changes from cold to hot or vice versa. Sometimes, Spring hangers can be used to dampen vibration as well.
