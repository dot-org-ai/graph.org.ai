# Other Metal Pipe Bursting Disc

A metal pipe bursting disc is a safety device that ruptures at a predetermined pressure level to protect equipment and piping systems from overpressure. It is made of a thin metal membrane and comes in different types such as reverse and forward acting bursting discs, as well as composite bursting discs.Rupture discs are also known as a pressure safety disc, burst disc, bursting disc, or burst diaphragm, Major types of Rupture Discs are Reverse Acting Rupture Discs & Forward Acting Rupture Discs, construction of disc followed by Carbon Steel, Stainless Steel,Graphite,Hastelloy,Aluminum,Nickel,Monel,Tantalum,Inconel etc.
