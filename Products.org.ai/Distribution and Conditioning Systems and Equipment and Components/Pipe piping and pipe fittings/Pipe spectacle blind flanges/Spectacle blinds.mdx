# Spectacle blinds

**UNSPSC Code**: 40174404
**Class**: [Pipe spectacle blind flanges](Pipe spectacle blind flanges.mdx)
**Family**: [Pipe piping and pipe fittings](../Pipe piping and pipe fittings.mdx)
**Segment**: [Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components](../../Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components.mdx)

A spectacle blind consists of a spade and a spacer which are manufactured for the purpose of installation in one piece. It is used as a safety device to isolate a particular section of pipe or equipment when this section or this equipment is to be inspected or decommissioned.
