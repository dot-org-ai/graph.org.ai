# Expansion joint

**UNSPSC Code**: 40102200
**Family**: [Heating and ventilation and air circulation](Heating and ventilation and air circulation.mdx)
**Segment**: [Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components](../Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components.mdx)

An assembly designed to hold parts of an industrial system together while safely accommodating temperature-induced expansion and contraction movement or system  vibration. Includes flexible, bellows, rigid, and reinforced types of expansion joints. They are used for a variety of applications, commonly including heat exchanger systems.
