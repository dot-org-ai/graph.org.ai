# Centrifugal liquid chiller

**UNSPSC Code**: 40101711
**Class**: [Cooling equipment and parts and accessories](Cooling equipment and parts and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Heating and ventilation and air circulation](../Heating and ventilation and air circulation.mdx)
**Segment**: [Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components](../../Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components.mdx)

A type of refrigeration unit which changes the kinetic energy of refrigerant gas to pressure using the centrifugal force of an impeller rotating at high speed.It consists of a compressor, condenser and evaporator, and its refrigerating capacity is large.It isalso called a turbo refrigerator.
