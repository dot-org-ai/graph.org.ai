# Heat exchanger tube

**UNSPSC Code**: 40101860
**Class**: [Heating equipment and parts and accessories](Heating equipment and parts and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Heating and ventilation and air circulation](../Heating and ventilation and air circulation.mdx)
**Segment**: [Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components](../../Distribution and Conditioning Systems and Equipment and Components.mdx)

A shell-and-tube heat exchanger is the most common type of heat exchanger. It consists of a number of tubes (often finned) placed inside a volume (shell). One of the fluids runs through the tubes while the second fluid runs across and along the tubes to be heated.
