# Offshore jacket foundation

**UNSPSC Code**: 26131904
**Class**: [Offshore wind farm machinery and equipment](Offshore wind farm machinery and equipment.mdx)
**Family**: [Power generation](../Power generation.mdx)
**Segment**: [Power Generation and Distribution Machinery and Accessories](../../Power Generation and Distribution Machinery and Accessories.mdx)

The primary function of a jacket is to support the static and dynamic loads of the wind turbine by anchoring it firmly to the sea bed using a set of pin piles. Secondary functions include supporting the wave loads on the jacket itself and enabling cable entry.
