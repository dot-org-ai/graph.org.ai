# Offshore monopile foundation

**UNSPSC Code**: 26131903
**Class**: [Offshore wind farm machinery and equipment](Offshore wind farm machinery and equipment.mdx)
**Family**: [Power generation](../Power generation.mdx)
**Segment**: [Power Generation and Distribution Machinery and Accessories](../../Power Generation and Distribution Machinery and Accessories.mdx)

The primary function of a monopile is to support the static and dynamic loads of the wind turbine through anchoring it firmly to the sea bed using the embedded part of the monopile. Secondary functions include supporting the wave loads on the monopile itself and enabling cable entry.
