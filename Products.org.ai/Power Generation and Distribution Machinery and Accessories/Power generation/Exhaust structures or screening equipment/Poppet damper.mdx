# Poppet damper

**UNSPSC Code**: 26131618
**Class**: [Exhaust structures or screening equipment](Exhaust structures or screening equipment.mdx)
**Family**: [Power generation](../Power generation.mdx)
**Segment**: [Power Generation and Distribution Machinery and Accessories](../../Power Generation and Distribution Machinery and Accessories.mdx)

A device used to control pressure, flow, or flow direction in an air or gas system. A poppet damper contains a weight or spring-loaded poppet device that opens when the pressure differential across it exceeds a predetermined value.
