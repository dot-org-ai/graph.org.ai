# Motor stator

**UNSPSC Code**: 26101410
**Class**: [Motor or generator components](Motor or generator components.mdx)
**Family**: [Power sources](../Power sources.mdx)
**Segment**: [Power Generation and Distribution Machinery and Accessories](../../Power Generation and Distribution Machinery and Accessories.mdx)

A stator is made up of windings that are inserted into a lamination stack. The stator is stationary and is mounted in the motor housing; when electric current is applied the stator provides a magnetic field that enables a rotor to revolve.
