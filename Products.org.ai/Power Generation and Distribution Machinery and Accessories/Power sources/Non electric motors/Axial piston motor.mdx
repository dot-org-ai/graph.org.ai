# Axial piston motor

**UNSPSC Code**: 26101312
**Class**: [Non electric motors](Non electric motors.mdx)
**Family**: [Power sources](../Power sources.mdx)
**Segment**: [Power Generation and Distribution Machinery and Accessories](../../Power Generation and Distribution Machinery and Accessories.mdx)

The axial piston motor is a type of hydraulic motor which has six pistons mounted axially ie., along the shaft contained in a swash plate which is fixed to the pump shaft When hydraulic oil flows into it, the oil operates the piston, which in turn will rotate the output shaft
