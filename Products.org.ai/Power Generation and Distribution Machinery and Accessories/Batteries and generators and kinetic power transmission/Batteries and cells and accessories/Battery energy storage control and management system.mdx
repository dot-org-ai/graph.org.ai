# Battery energy storage control and management system

**UNSPSC Code**: 26111733
**Class**: [Batteries and cells and accessories](Batteries and cells and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Batteries and generators and kinetic power transmission](../Batteries and generators and kinetic power transmission.mdx)
**Segment**: [Power Generation and Distribution Machinery and Accessories](../../Power Generation and Distribution Machinery and Accessories.mdx)

An energy storage system / power management system provides firm frequency response (FFR) services, performs Triad management, wholesale trading, and other related services (includes lithium-ion batteries), and evaluates user consumption data taking weather statistics into account, to determine the ideal charging and discharging intervals. It is fully integrated into the ESS system, including the battery modules, an integrated power inverter, a power management unit, along with electronic meters for power consumption in the building, photovoltaic power generation, and power sharing.
