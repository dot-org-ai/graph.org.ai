# Medium density fiberboard

**UNSPSC Code**: 11122003
**Class**: [Engineered wood products](Engineered wood products.mdx)
**Family**: [Non edible plant and forestry products](../Non edible plant and forestry products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Mineral and Textile and Inedible Plant and Animal Materials](../../Mineral and Textile and Inedible Plant and Animal Materials.mdx)

Building material which combines the technologies of the hardboard and particleboard industries and is manufactured from wood fibers combined with a synthetic resin, usually urea fomaldehyde. It is intended for interior use.
