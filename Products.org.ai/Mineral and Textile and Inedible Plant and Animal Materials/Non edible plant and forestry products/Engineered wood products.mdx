# Engineered wood products

**UNSPSC Code**: 11122000
**Family**: [Non edible plant and forestry products](Non edible plant and forestry products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Mineral and Textile and Inedible Plant and Animal Materials](../Mineral and Textile and Inedible Plant and Animal Materials.mdx)

A range of derivative wood products which are manufactured by binding together the strands, particles, fibers, or veneers of wood, together with adhesives, to form composite materials. These products are engineered to precise design specifications which are tested to meet national or international standards.
