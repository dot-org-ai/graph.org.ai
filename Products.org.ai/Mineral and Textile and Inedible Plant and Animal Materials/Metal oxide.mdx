# Metal oxide

**UNSPSC Code**: 11180000
**Segment**: [Mineral and Textile and Inedible Plant and Animal Materials](Mineral and Textile and Inedible Plant and Animal Materials.mdx)

Metal oxides are used as raw material for the manufacturing of alloys. They are a binary compound of oxygen with an atom or radical, or a compound which is regarded as binary; as, iron oxide, ethyl oxide, nitrogen oxide, etc.
