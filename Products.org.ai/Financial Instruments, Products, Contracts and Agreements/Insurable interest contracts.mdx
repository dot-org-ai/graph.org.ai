# Insurable interest contracts

**UNSPSC Code**: 64120000
**Segment**: [Financial Instruments, Products, Contracts and Agreements](Financial Instruments, Products, Contracts and Agreements.mdx)

Contracts, in the form of policies, that involve the equitable transfer of the risk of a loss from one entity to another in exchange for payment.  They are a form of risk management primarily used to hedge against the risk of a contingent, uncertain loss.
