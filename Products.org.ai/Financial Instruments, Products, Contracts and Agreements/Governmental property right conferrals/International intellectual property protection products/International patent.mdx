# International patent

**UNSPSC Code**: 64141501
**Class**: [International intellectual property protection products](International intellectual property protection products.mdx)
**Family**: [Governmental property right conferrals](../Governmental property right conferrals.mdx)
**Segment**: [Financial Instruments, Products, Contracts and Agreements](../../Financial Instruments, Products, Contracts and Agreements.mdx)

A set of exclusive rights, recognized internationally, granted by a sovereign state as a signatory to the Patent Cooperation Treaty PCT of 1970 to an inventor or assignee for a limited period of time in exchange for detailed public disclosure of an invention. An invention is a solution to a specific technological problem and is a product or a process.
