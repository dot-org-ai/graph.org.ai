# Local currency

**UNSPSC Code**: 64151504
**Class**: [Representative money or currency](Representative money or currency.mdx)
**Family**: [Monetary instruments or currency](../Monetary instruments or currency.mdx)
**Segment**: [Financial Instruments, Products, Contracts and Agreements](../../Financial Instruments, Products, Contracts and Agreements.mdx)

Money in the form of a currency that can be spent in a particular area at participating organizations.  Usually it will act as a complementary currency that is to be used in addition to a national currency, rather than replace it.  It may not be backed by a national government or be legal tender.
