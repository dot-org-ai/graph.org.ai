# Rainbow option contract

**UNSPSC Code**: 64111712
**Class**: [Derivative securities](Derivative securities.mdx)
**Family**: [Securities](../Securities.mdx)
**Segment**: [Financial Instruments, Products, Contracts and Agreements](../../Financial Instruments, Products, Contracts and Agreements.mdx)

A single option contract between a buyer and a seller, usually on the floor of an exchange, linked to two or more underlying assets. In order for the option to pay off, all the underlying assets must move in the intended direction.
