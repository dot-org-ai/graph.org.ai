# Derivative securities

**UNSPSC Code**: 64111700
**Family**: [Securities](Securities.mdx)
**Segment**: [Financial Instruments, Products, Contracts and Agreements](../Financial Instruments, Products, Contracts and Agreements.mdx)

Financial contracts whose value is derived from the performance of assets, interest rates, currency exchange rates, indexes, environmental phenomena, etc. Derivative transactions include a wide assortment of financial contracts including structured debt obligations and deposits, swaps, futures, options, caps, floors, collars, forwards and various combinations thereof.  Derivatives can be used for a number of purposes - including insuring against price movements --hedging--, increasing exposure to price movements for speculation or getting access to otherwise hard to trade assets or markets.
