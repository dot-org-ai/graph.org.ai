# Procurement card

**UNSPSC Code**: 64101804
**Class**: [Card based revolving credit products](Card based revolving credit products.mdx)
**Family**: [Bank offered products](../Bank offered products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Financial Instruments, Products, Contracts and Agreements](../../Financial Instruments, Products, Contracts and Agreements.mdx)

A procurement card is a type of company charge card used for smaller purchases to achieve greater cost efficiency, control and convenience. Procurement cards are also known as purchasing cards, P-Cards or PCards. Procurement cards can be tied to either a credit card or a bank account.
