# Over draft protection

**UNSPSC Code**: 64101704
**Class**: [Electronic fund transfer and payment products](Electronic fund transfer and payment products.mdx)
**Family**: [Bank offered products](../Bank offered products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Financial Instruments, Products, Contracts and Agreements](../../Financial Instruments, Products, Contracts and Agreements.mdx)

A line of credit that banks offer to their customers to cover overdrafts. Overdraft protection is initiated when a customer writes a check for more than the amount in their account.
