# Receivable loan

**UNSPSC Code**: 64101908
**Class**: [Account based credit products](Account based credit products.mdx)
**Family**: [Bank offered products](../Bank offered products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Financial Instruments, Products, Contracts and Agreements](../../Financial Instruments, Products, Contracts and Agreements.mdx)

A short-term loan made to a company so it can fund the collection period for the accounts receivable arising from credit sales. Those receivables serve as collateral for the loan if the business does not collect its receivables and cannot repay the loan.
