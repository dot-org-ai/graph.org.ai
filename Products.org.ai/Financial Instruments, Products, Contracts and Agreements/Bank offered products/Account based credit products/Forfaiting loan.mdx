# Forfaiting loan

**UNSPSC Code**: 64101903
**Class**: [Account based credit products](Account based credit products.mdx)
**Family**: [Bank offered products](../Bank offered products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Financial Instruments, Products, Contracts and Agreements](../../Financial Instruments, Products, Contracts and Agreements.mdx)

The purchasing of an exporter's receivables --the amount importers owe the exporter-- at a discount by paying cash. The forfaiter, the purchaser of the receivables, becomes the entity to whom the importer is obliged to pay its debt.
