# Governmental property right conferrals

**UNSPSC Code**: 64140000
**Segment**: [Financial Instruments, Products, Contracts and Agreements](Financial Instruments, Products, Contracts and Agreements.mdx)

Governmental or public property is property that is owned by all, but its access and use is controlled by government.  In these cases, government, acting on behalf of the public, confers rights on individuals or groups in pursuit of the larger public interest.
