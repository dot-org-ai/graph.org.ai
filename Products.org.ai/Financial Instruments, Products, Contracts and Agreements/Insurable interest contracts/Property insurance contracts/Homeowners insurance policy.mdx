# Homeowners insurance policy

**UNSPSC Code**: 64121508
**Class**: [Property insurance contracts](Property insurance contracts.mdx)
**Family**: [Insurable interest contracts](../Insurable interest contracts.mdx)
**Segment**: [Financial Instruments, Products, Contracts and Agreements](../../Financial Instruments, Products, Contracts and Agreements.mdx)

A type of risk management contract that covers an individual residence against damages to the house itself, or to possessions in the home. Homeowners insurance also provides liability coverage against accidents in the home or on the property.
