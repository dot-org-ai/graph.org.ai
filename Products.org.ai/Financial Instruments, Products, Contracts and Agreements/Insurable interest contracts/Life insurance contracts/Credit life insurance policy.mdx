# Credit life insurance policy

**UNSPSC Code**: 64122001
**Class**: [Life insurance contracts](Life insurance contracts.mdx)
**Family**: [Insurable interest contracts](../Insurable interest contracts.mdx)
**Segment**: [Financial Instruments, Products, Contracts and Agreements](../../Financial Instruments, Products, Contracts and Agreements.mdx)

A type of life insurance policy designed to pay off a borrower's debt if that borrower dies. The face value of a credit life insurance policy decreases proportionately with an outstanding loan amount as the loan is paid off over time until both reach zero value.
