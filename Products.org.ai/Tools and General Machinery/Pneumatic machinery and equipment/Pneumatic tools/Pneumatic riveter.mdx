# Pneumatic riveter

**UNSPSC Code**: 27131521
**Class**: [Pneumatic tools](Pneumatic tools.mdx)
**Family**: [Pneumatic machinery and equipment](../Pneumatic machinery and equipment.mdx)
**Segment**: [Tools and General Machinery](../../Tools and General Machinery.mdx)

A gun-shaped or straight tool used to drive rivets. Such pneumatic or oleo pneumatic (with oil) tools are able to invert the spin and set free the rivet to make the job easier. Can be used with riveted screws and nuts.
