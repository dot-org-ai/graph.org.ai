# Teff seed

**UNSPSC Code**: 10151616
**Class**: [Cereal seeds](Cereal seeds.mdx)
**Family**: [Seeds and bulbs and seedlings and cuttings](../Seeds and bulbs and seedlings and cuttings.mdx)
**Segment**: [Live Plant and Animal Material and Accessories and Supplies](../../Live Plant and Animal Material and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

An annual grass, a species of lovegrass native to the northern Ethiopian Highlands of East (Horn of) Africa.  It is high in dietary fibre and iron and provides protein and calcium.  It is similar to millet and quinoa in cooking, but the seed is much smaller and cooks faster, thus using less fuel.
