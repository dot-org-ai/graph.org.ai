# Enana bougainvillea spp.

**UNSPSC Code**: 10152065

It is a species of climbing plant in the Nyctaginaceae family; and is the most common of the bonsai species. It is native to Brazil. The woody trunk tends to curle, and the thin branches have sharp spines and dark green leaves. It is very good for hedges, arches, or shrubs in the ground, and in pots. It is ideal for bonsai.
