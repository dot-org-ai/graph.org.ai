# Triple superphosphate fertilizer

**UNSPSC Code**: 10171618

Triple superphosphate is a component of fertilizer that primarily consists of monocalcium phosphate, Ca(H2PO4)2. Triple superphosphate is obtained by treating phosphate rock with phosphoric acid. ... Many fertilizers are derived from triple superphosphate, e.g. by blending with ammonium sulfate and potassium chloride.
