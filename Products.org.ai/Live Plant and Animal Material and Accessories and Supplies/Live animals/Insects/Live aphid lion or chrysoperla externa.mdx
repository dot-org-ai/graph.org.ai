The aphid lion or chrysoperla externa is an insect species used as a biological control agent for agriculture in tropical and warm regions of Central and South America. It is a highly adaptable predator that can be found in cold, warm and tropical climates. It can also tolerate carbamate and phosporous organic pesticides.
