# Carissa grandiflora

**UNSPSC Code**: 10161590
**Class**: [Trees and shrubs](Trees and shrubs.mdx)
**Family**: [Floriculture and silviculture products](../Floriculture and silviculture products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Live Plant and Animal Material and Accessories and Supplies](../../Live Plant and Animal Material and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

It is a spectacular shrub for its persistent leaves and large fruits. It has a dark green foliage and its flowers are white and highly perfumed that appear from April to September, producing large red fruits. It reaches a height of 0.5 / 1 meter and 2-3 meters wide. It does not support frost. It needs moderate sun, moderate watering, resistant to saline winds.
