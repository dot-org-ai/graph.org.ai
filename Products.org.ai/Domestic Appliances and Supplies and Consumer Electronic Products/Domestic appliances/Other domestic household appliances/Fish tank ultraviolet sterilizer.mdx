# Fish tank ultraviolet sterilizer

**UNSPSC Code**: 52141808
**Class**: [Other domestic household appliances](Other domestic household appliances.mdx)
**Family**: [Domestic appliances](../Domestic appliances.mdx)
**Segment**: [Domestic Appliances and Supplies and Consumer Electronic Products](../../Domestic Appliances and Supplies and Consumer Electronic Products.mdx)

A device that is used to control infections by stopping the spread of microorganisms from one fish/coral/invertebrate to another through the water. It is also used in pond applications to control free-floating algae. When operated correctly, free-floating microorganisms will be killed by the UV light.
