# Emergency telecommunications equipment

**UNSPSC Code**: 57080101
**Class**: [Emergency IT equipment kits](Emergency IT equipment kits.mdx)
**Family**: [Humanitarian IT equipment](../Humanitarian IT equipment.mdx)
**Segment**: [Humanitarian Relief Items, Kits, or Accessories](../../Humanitarian Relief Items, Kits, or Accessories.mdx)

A stock of telecommunication equipment, adequate for establishing communication links for two emergency operations has been established. This equipment is intended to provide emergency staff with immediate communication links even from the most remote locations. The stock, which will be replenished as it is deployed, consists of: - 8 pactors - 80 codans base/MOB - 100 VHF base/MOB - 300 VHF handhelds - 20 VHF repeaters - 14 VHF packet - 9 SATCOM M - 4 SATCOM C - 26 Natel D - 10 fax (UNHCR).
