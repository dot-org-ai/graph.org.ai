# Early identification kit for unaccompanied children

**UNSPSC Code**: 57070101
**Class**: [Logistics emergency kits](Logistics emergency kits.mdx)
**Family**: [Humanitarian logistics supplies](../Humanitarian logistics supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Humanitarian Relief Items, Kits, or Accessories](../../Humanitarian Relief Items, Kits, or Accessories.mdx)

This kit was developed jointly by UNHCR and UNICEF and is intended to promote early identification, documentation, and reunification of unaccompanied children. The kit includes the priority action handbook, emergency registration books, basic supplies (such as cameras and film) and a kit container.
