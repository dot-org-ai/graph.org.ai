# Primary healthcare kit

**UNSPSC Code**: 57030107
**Class**: [Health emergency kits](Health emergency kits.mdx)
**Family**: [Humanitarian health supplies](../Humanitarian health supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Humanitarian Relief Items, Kits, or Accessories](../../Humanitarian Relief Items, Kits, or Accessories.mdx)

Primary health kits embed the inter-agency emergency health kits (IEHK) that have different modules containing essential medicines and medical devices for different amounts of individuals (ranging from 10 to 10,000). Depending  on the modules, IEHKs can be used from primary health care workers with limited training to professional health workers or physicians. the modules can meet specific needs of population in need affected by (but not limited to) emergencies, malaria, acute conditions related to non-communicable diseases etc...
