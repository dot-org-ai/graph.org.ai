# Oral-rehydration-points consumable kit

**UNSPSC Code**: 57030104
**Class**: [Health emergency kits](Health emergency kits.mdx)
**Family**: [Humanitarian health supplies](../Humanitarian health supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Humanitarian Relief Items, Kits, or Accessories](../../Humanitarian Relief Items, Kits, or Accessories.mdx)

Each packet of oral-rehydration-points (ORS) contains the equivalent of: Sodium chloride 3.5 g potassium chloride 1.5 g trisodium citrate, dihydrate 2.9 g glucose anhydrous 20 g The 27.9 gram dose is intended for dissolution into one liter of drinking water. Packets are packed as follows: Carton 125 packets, case 625 packets -5 cartons. Further information available on https://ifrcgo.org/ecv- toolkit/action/community-oral-rehydration-points/ .
