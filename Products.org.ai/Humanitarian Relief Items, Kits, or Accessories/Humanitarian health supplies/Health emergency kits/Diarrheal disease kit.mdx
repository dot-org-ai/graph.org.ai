# Diarrheal disease kit

**UNSPSC Code**: 57030112
**Class**: [Health emergency kits](Health emergency kits.mdx)
**Family**: [Humanitarian health supplies](../Humanitarian health supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Humanitarian Relief Items, Kits, or Accessories](../../Humanitarian Relief Items, Kits, or Accessories.mdx)

This module is part of the Interagency diarrheal disease kits designed for 100 severe cholera cases (cholera treatment unit), plus 400 moderate cholera cases (oral rehydration unit), and 100 adults plus 100 children affected by Shigella dysentery. The ORS module contains items for 400 cholera patients with no - or with moderate - dehydration. This material covers the needs for two ORUs. Other Interagency diarrheal disease kits modules are 'Basic Module' (B35667102), 'Infusion Module' (B35667108) and 'ORS module'(B35667101). Packaging details: items are packed on 1 pallet ea of 0.8x1.6x1.2mt - 1.63cub.m. / 76kg.
