# Baduk or go game and accessories

**UNSPSC Code**: 60141116
**Class**: [Games](Games.mdx)
**Family**: [Toys and games](../Toys and games.mdx)
**Segment**: [Musical Instruments and Games and Toys and Arts and Crafts and Educational Equipment and Materials and Accessories and Supplies](../../Musical Instruments and Games and Toys and Arts and Crafts and Educational Equipment and Materials and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

A game and parts consisting of a board measuring 450mm by 450mm, baduk stones, and containers for the stones. The game involves 2 persons holding either white or black stones. Each player in turn places his or her stones on the board which has 19 columns and rows. The player managing to win the largest number of points of crisscrossing of the columns and rows wins.
