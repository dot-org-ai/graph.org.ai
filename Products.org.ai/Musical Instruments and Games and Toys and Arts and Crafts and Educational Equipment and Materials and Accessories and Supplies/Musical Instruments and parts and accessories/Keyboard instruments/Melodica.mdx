# Melodica

**UNSPSC Code**: 60131007
**Class**: [Keyboard instruments](Keyboard instruments.mdx)
**Family**: [Musical Instruments and parts and accessories](../Musical Instruments and parts and accessories.mdx)
**Segment**: [Musical Instruments and Games and Toys and Arts and Crafts and Educational Equipment and Materials and Accessories and Supplies](../../Musical Instruments and Games and Toys and Arts and Crafts and Educational Equipment and Materials and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

A small keyboard instrument that creates sound by blowing wind and pressing keyboard. It has about 20~40 keys on the keyboard. One can hold the instrument in both hands to play by blowing air into the instrument or place it on the floor and blow air into the instrument by using long pipe connected to the instrument.
