# Process control or packaged automation systems

**UNSPSC Code**: 32152000
**Family**: [Automation control devices and components and accessories](Automation control devices and components and accessories.mdx)
**Segment**: [Electronic Components and Supplies](../Electronic Components and Supplies.mdx)

Complete packaged systems that automate control of a process and typically deal with analog signals from sensors and meters that are transmitted to specialized computers which cause the temperature, pressure and flow to be continually adjusted. Process control makes extensive use of analog to digital and digital to analog conversion.
