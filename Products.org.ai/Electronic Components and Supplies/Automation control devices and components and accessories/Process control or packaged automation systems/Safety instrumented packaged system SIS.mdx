# Safety instrumented packaged system SIS

**UNSPSC Code**: 32152003
**Class**: [Process control or packaged automation systems](Process control or packaged automation systems.mdx)
**Family**: [Automation control devices and components and accessories](../Automation control devices and components and accessories.mdx)
**Segment**: [Electronic Components and Supplies](../../Electronic Components and Supplies.mdx)

A process control system that performs specified functions to achieve or maintain a safe state of the process when unacceptable or dangerous process conditions are detected. It is separate and independent from regular control systems but is composed of similar elements, including sensors, logic solvers, actuators and support systems.
