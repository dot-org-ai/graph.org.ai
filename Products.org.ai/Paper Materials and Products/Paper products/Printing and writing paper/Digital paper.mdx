# Digital paper

**UNSPSC Code**: 14111538
**Class**: [Printing and writing paper](Printing and writing paper.mdx)
**Family**: [Paper products](../Paper products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Paper Materials and Products](../../Paper Materials and Products.mdx)

Digital paper is patterned paper used in conjunction with a digital pen to create handwritten digital documents. The printed dot pattern uniquely identifies the position coordinates on the paper. The digital pen uses this pattern to store the handwriting and upload it to a computer.
