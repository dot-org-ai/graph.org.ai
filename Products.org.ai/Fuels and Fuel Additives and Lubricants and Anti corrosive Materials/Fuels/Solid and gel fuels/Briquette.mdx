# Briquette

**UNSPSC Code**: 15101609
**Class**: [Solid and gel fuels](Solid and gel fuels.mdx)
**Family**: [Fuels](../Fuels.mdx)
**Segment**: [Fuels and Fuel Additives and Lubricants and Anti corrosive Materials](../../Fuels and Fuel Additives and Lubricants and Anti corrosive Materials.mdx)

A dried solid fuel for heating made from a mixture of anthracite and another pulverized carbide or combined with bonding materials and then molded into cylinder shape. It is called a nine-holed briquette or just a holed briquette as it has airholes.
