# Compressed natural gas CNG

**UNSPSC Code**: 15111512
**Class**: [Gaseous fuels](Gaseous fuels.mdx)
**Family**: [Gaseous fuels and additives](../Gaseous fuels and additives.mdx)
**Segment**: [Fuels and Fuel Additives and Lubricants and Anti corrosive Materials](../../Fuels and Fuel Additives and Lubricants and Anti corrosive Materials.mdx)

A gaseous by-product of oil used as fuel. CNG is composed of methane gas in a compressed form generally at 3,000 PSI to store as many cubic meters in the cylinders in order to provide greater autonomy to the vehicle.
