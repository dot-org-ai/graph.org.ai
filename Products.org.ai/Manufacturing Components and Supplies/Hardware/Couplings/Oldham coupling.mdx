# Oldham coupling

**UNSPSC Code**: 31163015
**Class**: [Couplings](Couplings.mdx)
**Family**: [Hardware](../Hardware.mdx)
**Segment**: [Manufacturing Components and Supplies](../../Manufacturing Components and Supplies.mdx)

A type of coupling consisting of a pair of disks, one flat and the other hollow, both pinned to the ends of the shafts.A third, center disk with a pair of lugs projecting from each face of the disk fits into the slots between the two end disks and enables one shaft to drive the other shaft.
