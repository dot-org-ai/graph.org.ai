# Muff coupling

**UNSPSC Code**: 31163023
**Class**: [Couplings](Couplings.mdx)
**Family**: [Hardware](../Hardware.mdx)
**Segment**: [Manufacturing Components and Supplies](../../Manufacturing Components and Supplies.mdx)

A type of device that has two shafts to face and fit together within a cast iron or cast steel cylinder and is bound with keys. This device is the simplest coupling to use if the axis diameter and the load for conveyance are very small.
