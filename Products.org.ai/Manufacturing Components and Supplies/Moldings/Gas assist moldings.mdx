# Gas assist moldings

**UNSPSC Code**: 31143000
**Family**: [Moldings](Moldings.mdx)
**Segment**: [Manufacturing Components and Supplies](../Manufacturing Components and Supplies.mdx)

Parts produced by an injection molding process involving injection of high pressure nitrogen gas into the resin melt stream immediately after injection of the resin allowing the gas to displace a portion of the resin and force the resin to the walls of the mold.
