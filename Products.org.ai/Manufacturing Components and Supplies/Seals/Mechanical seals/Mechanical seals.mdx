# Mechanical seals

**UNSPSC Code**: 31411706
**Class**: [Mechanical seals](Mechanical seals.mdx)
**Family**: [Seals](../Seals.mdx)
**Segment**: [Manufacturing Components and Supplies](../../Manufacturing Components and Supplies.mdx)

Mechanical seals are devices that are used to provide a seal at the point of entry or exit of a rotating shaft. Simply a method of containing fluid within a vessel (typically pumps, mixers, etc.) where a rotating shaft passes through a stationary housing or occasionally, where the housing rotates around the shaft.
