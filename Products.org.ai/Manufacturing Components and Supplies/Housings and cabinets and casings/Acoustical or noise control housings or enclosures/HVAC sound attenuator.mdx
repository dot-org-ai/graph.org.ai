# HVAC sound attenuator

**UNSPSC Code**: 31261707
**Class**: [Acoustical or noise control housings or enclosures](Acoustical or noise control housings or enclosures.mdx)
**Family**: [Housings and cabinets and casings](../Housings and cabinets and casings.mdx)
**Segment**: [Manufacturing Components and Supplies](../../Manufacturing Components and Supplies.mdx)

A sound attenuator, or duct silencer, sound trap, or muffler, is a noise control acoustical treatment of Heating Ventilating and Air-Conditioning (HVAC) ductwork designed to reduce transmission of noise through the ductwork, either from equipment into occupied spaces in a building, or between occupied spaces.
