# Epoxy primer paint

It is an airtight sealer that protects the primer before the walls are ready for paint. It also acts as a binding ingredient between the concrete, and the topcoat of the paint. Epoxy primer paint protects the walls from extreme weather conditions. It is a two component, Epoxy Zinc Phosphate high performance primer for new and maintenance application. Advanced 2-pack epoxy primer Grey is equipped with surface tolerant property that is designed to offer excellent corrosion protection to mild steel substrates.
