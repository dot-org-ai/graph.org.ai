# Aluminum paint

**UNSPSC Code**: 31211519
**Class**: [Paints and primers](Paints and primers.mdx)
**Family**: [Paints and primers and finishes](../Paints and primers and finishes.mdx)
**Segment**: [Manufacturing Components and Supplies](../../Manufacturing Components and Supplies.mdx)

A type of paint that has the main ingredients of petroleum type synthetic resin and aluminum pigment. The product has strong adhesion, durability and anti-corrosion, outstanding weatherability and moisture resistance, and it is the paint appropriate to the steel-material facilities for the purpose of reflection of hot wire, prevention of moisture penetration and others.
