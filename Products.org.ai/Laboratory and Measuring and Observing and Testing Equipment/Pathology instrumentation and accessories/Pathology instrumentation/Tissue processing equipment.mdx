# Tissue processing equipment

**UNSPSC Code**: 41191501
**Class**: [Pathology instrumentation](Pathology instrumentation.mdx)
**Family**: [Pathology instrumentation and accessories](../Pathology instrumentation and accessories.mdx)
**Segment**: [Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment](../../Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment.mdx)

In this classification, Tissue processing equipment connotes a device used to process human or animal tissue so it can be utilized to visualize normal and abnormal structures. US FDA regulates Tissue processing equipment under 21 CFR 864.3010 and under FDA product code KET.
