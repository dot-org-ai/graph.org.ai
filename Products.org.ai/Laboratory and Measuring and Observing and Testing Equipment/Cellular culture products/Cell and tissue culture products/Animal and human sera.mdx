# Animal and human sera

**UNSPSC Code**: 41161506
**Class**: [Cell and tissue culture products](Cell and tissue culture products.mdx)
**Family**: [Cellular culture products](../Cellular culture products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment](../../Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment.mdx)

In this classification, animal and human sera are biological products, obtained from the blood of humans or other animals, that provide the necessary growth-promoting nutrients in a cell culture system. US FDA regulates Animal and human sera under 21 CFR 864.2800 and under FDA product code KIS.
