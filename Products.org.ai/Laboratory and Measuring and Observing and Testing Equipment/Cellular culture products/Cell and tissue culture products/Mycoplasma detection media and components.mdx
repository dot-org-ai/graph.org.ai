# Mycoplasma detection media and components

**UNSPSC Code**: 41161505
**Class**: [Cell and tissue culture products](Cell and tissue culture products.mdx)
**Family**: [Cellular culture products](../Cellular culture products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment](../../Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment.mdx)

In this classification, mycoplasma detection media and components are used to detect and isolate mycoplasma pleuropneumonia-like organisms (PPLO), a common microbial contaminant in cell cultures. US FDA regulates Mycoplasma detection media and components under 21 CFR 864.2360 and under FDA product code KIX.
