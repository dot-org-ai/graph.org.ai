# Rna preanalytical systems

**UNSPSC Code**: 41181601
**Class**: [Immunology laboratory equipment and reagents](Immunology laboratory equipment and reagents.mdx)
**Family**: [Immunological testing systems, components, and supplies](../Immunological testing systems, components, and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment](../../Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment.mdx)

In this classification, Rna preanalytical systems connotes a device used to collect, store, and transport patient specimens, and stabilize intracellular RNA from the specimens, for subsequent isolation and purification of the intracellular RNA for RT-PCR used in in vitro molecular diagnostic testing. US FDA regulates Rna preanalytical systems under 21 CFR 866.4070 and under FDA product code NTW.
