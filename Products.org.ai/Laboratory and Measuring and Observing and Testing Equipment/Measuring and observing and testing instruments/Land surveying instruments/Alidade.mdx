# Alidade

**UNSPSC Code**: 41114216
**Class**: [Land surveying instruments](Land surveying instruments.mdx)
**Family**: [Measuring and observing and testing instruments](../Measuring and observing and testing instruments.mdx)
**Segment**: [Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment](../../Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment.mdx)

A type of device that is designed to test the direction of the sideline during the flat-table surveying. It is the device to fill in on the drawing paper by setting forth the line of collimation direction that is sometimes used to measure the height difference or distance of two points.
