# Twist vibration measuring instrument

**UNSPSC Code**: 41114514
**Class**: [Mechanical instruments](Mechanical instruments.mdx)
**Family**: [Measuring and observing and testing instruments](../Measuring and observing and testing instruments.mdx)
**Segment**: [Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment](../../Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment.mdx)

Twist vibration is the phenomenon that changes very finitely for rotation by the load change, resonance of shafting and others of the body of revolution (engine, motor, prime mover, pump, compressor, coupling, shaft, damper, ship propelling system and others). This is an instrument that measures twist vibration.
