# Rail joint gap measuring instrument

**UNSPSC Code**: 41112806
**Class**: [Transportation related equipment and instruments](Transportation related equipment and instruments.mdx)
**Family**: [Measuring and observing and testing instruments](../Measuring and observing and testing instruments.mdx)
**Segment**: [Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment](../../Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment.mdx)

A type of device that is designed to measure the rail temperature and the gap of joint parts at the time of having the interval to the joint of rail. It occurs when the running rail is expanded and contracted in length direction in accordance with the temperature changes. It may deform 2-way direction or 1-way direction depending on each case.
