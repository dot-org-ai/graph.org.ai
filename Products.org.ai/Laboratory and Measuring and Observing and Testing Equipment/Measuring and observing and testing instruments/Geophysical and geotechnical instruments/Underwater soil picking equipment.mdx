# Underwater soil picking equipment

**UNSPSC Code**: 41113829
**Class**: [Geophysical and geotechnical instruments](Geophysical and geotechnical instruments.mdx)
**Family**: [Measuring and observing and testing instruments](../Measuring and observing and testing instruments.mdx)
**Segment**: [Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment](../../Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment.mdx)

Equipment that is designed to collect the sample to analyze the soil of seabed. The lower part of the collector is made in sharp tube to catch the sample as the lower part of the collector speared deep into the seabed from its weight, and independent heavyweight is sometimes attached to enlarge the weight.
