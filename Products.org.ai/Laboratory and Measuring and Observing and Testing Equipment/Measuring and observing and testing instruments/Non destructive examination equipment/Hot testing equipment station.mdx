# Hot testing equipment station

**UNSPSC Code**: 41111810
**Class**: [Non destructive examination equipment](Non destructive examination equipment.mdx)
**Family**: [Measuring and observing and testing instruments](../Measuring and observing and testing instruments.mdx)
**Segment**: [Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment](../../Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment.mdx)

Test equipment station that contains equipment which monitors the performance of devices including engines and transmissions.  The device is hooked up to a test stand contained within the test station and all required fluids are supplied to start and run the device for an extended period of time.  The performance is then measured and recorded
