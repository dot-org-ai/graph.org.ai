# Fresh frozen plasma

**UNSPSC Code**: 41132004
**Class**: [Processed blood products for transfusion](Processed blood products for transfusion.mdx)
**Family**: [Blood manufacturing and hematology systems, components and supplies](../Blood manufacturing and hematology systems, components and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment](../../Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment.mdx)

Plasma is the yellowish liquid portion of whole blood that contains all clotting factors.. It is administered to treat bleeding disorders. Plasma extracted within 18 hours of donation is "fresh", but is then frozen to allow a 12 month shelf life.
