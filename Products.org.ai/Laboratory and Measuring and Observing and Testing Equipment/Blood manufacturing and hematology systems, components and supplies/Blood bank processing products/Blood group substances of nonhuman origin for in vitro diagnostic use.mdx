# Blood group substances of nonhuman origin for in vitro diagnostic use

**UNSPSC Code**: 41131605
**Class**: [Blood bank processing products](Blood bank processing products.mdx)
**Family**: [Blood manufacturing and hematology systems, components and supplies](../Blood manufacturing and hematology systems, components and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment](../../Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment.mdx)

In this classification, blood group substances of nonhuman origin for in vitro diagnostic use are materials, such as blood group specific substances prepared from nonhuman sources (e.g., pigs, cows, and horses) used to detect, identify, or neutralize antibodies to various human blood group antigens. US FDA regulates Blood group substances of nonhuman origin for in vitro diagnostic use under 21 CFR 864.9160 and under FDA product code KSX.
