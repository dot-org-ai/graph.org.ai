# Potentiating media for in vitro diagnostic use

**UNSPSC Code**: 41131619
**Class**: [Blood bank processing products](Blood bank processing products.mdx)
**Family**: [Blood manufacturing and hematology systems, components and supplies](../Blood manufacturing and hematology systems, components and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment](../../Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment.mdx)

In this classification, potentiating media for in vitro diagnostic use are media, such as bovine albumin, that are used to suspend red cells and to enhance cell reactions for antigen-antibody testing. US FDA regulates Potentiating media for in vitro diagnostic use under 21 CFR 864.96 and under FDA product code KSG.
