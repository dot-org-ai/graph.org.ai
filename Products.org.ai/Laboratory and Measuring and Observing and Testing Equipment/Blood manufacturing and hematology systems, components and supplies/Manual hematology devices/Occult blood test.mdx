# Occult blood test

**UNSPSC Code**: 41131905
**Class**: [Manual hematology devices](Manual hematology devices.mdx)
**Family**: [Blood manufacturing and hematology systems, components and supplies](../Blood manufacturing and hematology systems, components and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment](../../Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment.mdx)

In this classification, Occult blood test connotes a device used to detect occult blood in urine or feces. Occult blood is blood present in such small quantities that it can be detected only by chemical tests of suspected material, or by microscopic or spectroscopic examination. US FDA regulates Occult blood test under 21 CFR 864.6550 and under FDA product code OOX.
