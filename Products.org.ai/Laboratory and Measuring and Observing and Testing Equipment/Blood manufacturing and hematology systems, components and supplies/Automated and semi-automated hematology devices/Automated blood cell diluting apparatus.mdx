# Automated blood cell diluting apparatus

**UNSPSC Code**: 41131503
**Class**: [Automated and semi-automated hematology devices](Automated and semi-automated hematology devices.mdx)
**Family**: [Blood manufacturing and hematology systems, components and supplies](../Blood manufacturing and hematology systems, components and supplies.mdx)

In this classification, an automated blood cell diluting apparatus is a fully automated or semi-automated device used to make appropriate dilutions of a blood sample for further testing. US FDA regulates Automated blood cell diluting apparatus under 21 CFR 864.5240 and under FDA product code GKH.
