# Automated hemoglobin system

**UNSPSC Code**: 41131510
**Class**: [Automated and semi-automated hematology devices](Automated and semi-automated hematology devices.mdx)
**Family**: [Blood manufacturing and hematology systems, components and supplies](../Blood manufacturing and hematology systems, components and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment](../../Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment.mdx)

In this classification, an automated hemoglobin system is a fully automated or semi-automated device which may or may not be part of a larger system. The generic type of device consists of the reagents, calibrators, controls, and instrumentation used to determine the hemoglobin content of human blood. US FDA regulates Automated hemoglobin system under 21 CFR 864.5620 and under FDA product code GKR.
