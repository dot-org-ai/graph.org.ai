# Red cell indices device

**UNSPSC Code**: 41131505
**Class**: [Automated and semi-automated hematology devices](Automated and semi-automated hematology devices.mdx)
**Family**: [Blood manufacturing and hematology systems, components and supplies](../Blood manufacturing and hematology systems, components and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment](../../Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment.mdx)

In this classification, a red cell indices device, usually part of a larger system, calculates or directly measures the erythrocyte mean corpuscular volume (MCV), the mean corpuscular hemoglobin (MCH), and the mean corpuscular hemoglobin concentration (MCHC). US FDA regulates Red cell indices device under 21 CFR 864.5300 and under FDA product code KQH.
