# Electrophoretic hemoglobin analysis system

**UNSPSC Code**: 41131717
**Class**: [Hematology kits and packages](Hematology kits and packages.mdx)
**Family**: [Blood manufacturing and hematology systems, components and supplies](../Blood manufacturing and hematology systems, components and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment](../../Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment.mdx)

In this classification an electrophoretic hemoglobin analysis system is a device that electrophoretically separates and identifies normal and abnormal hemoglobin types as an aid in the diagnosis of anemia or erythrocytosis (increased total red cell mass) due to a hemoglobin abnormality US FDA regulates Electrophoretic hemoglobin analysis system under 21 CFR 864.7440 and under FDA product code OOX.
