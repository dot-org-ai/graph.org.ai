# Euglobulin lysis time tests

**UNSPSC Code**: 41131706
**Class**: [Hematology kits and packages](Hematology kits and packages.mdx)
**Family**: [Blood manufacturing and hematology systems, components and supplies](../Blood manufacturing and hematology systems, components and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment](../../Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment.mdx)

In this classification a euglobulin lysis time test is a device that measures the length of time required for the lysis (dissolution) of a clot formed from fibrinogen in the euglobulin fraction (that fraction of the plasma responsible for the formation of plasmin, a clot lysing enzyme) US FDA regulates Euglobulin lysis time tests under 21 CFR 864.7275 and under FDA product code JBO.
