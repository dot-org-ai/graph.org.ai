# Photosynthesis measurement apparatus

**UNSPSC Code**: 41106702
**Class**: [Laboratory botanical equipment and accessories](Laboratory botanical equipment and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Laboratory and scientific equipment](../Laboratory and scientific equipment.mdx)
**Segment**: [Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment](../../Laboratory and Measuring and Observing and Testing Equipment.mdx)

Photosynthesis reaction is the reaction that synthesizes the carbohydrate with the energy source of carbon gas, water and inorganic chlorine from the green plant and the solar ray and with the catalyst of chlorophyll. And the device measuring it is referred to as the photosynthesis measurement apparatus.
