# Computer displays

**UNSPSC Code**: 43211900
**Family**: [Computer Equipment and Accessories](Computer Equipment and Accessories.mdx)
**Segment**: [Information Technology Broadcasting and Telecommunications](../Information Technology Broadcasting and Telecommunications.mdx)

A display is a computer output surface or a screen and a projecting mechanism that shows text and graphic images. In some computers, the display is packaged in a separate unit called a monitor. In notebook computers, the display is integrated into a unit. Also called video display terminals VDTs, a display uses a cathode ray tube (CRT), liquid crystal display (LCD), light-emitting diode, gas plasma, or other image projection technology.
