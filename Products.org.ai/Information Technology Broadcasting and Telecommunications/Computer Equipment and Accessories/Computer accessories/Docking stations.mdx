A port replicator is similar to a docking station but does not include expansion slots. A port replicator is an attachment for a notebook computer that allows a printer, monitor, and keyboard to be simultaneously connected. The port replicator duplicates all of the notebook's ports,and may provide specific ports for joysticks and MIDI devices.
