# Dot matrix printers

**UNSPSC Code**: 43212102
**Class**: [Computer printers](Computer printers.mdx)
**Family**: [Computer Equipment and Accessories](../Computer Equipment and Accessories.mdx)
**Segment**: [Information Technology Broadcasting and Telecommunications](../../Information Technology Broadcasting and Telecommunications.mdx)

A kind of printer with a vertical column of up to 48 small closely packed needles or pins each of which can be individually forced forwards to press an ink ribbon against the paper. The print head is repeatedly scanned across the page and different combinations of needles activated at each point.
