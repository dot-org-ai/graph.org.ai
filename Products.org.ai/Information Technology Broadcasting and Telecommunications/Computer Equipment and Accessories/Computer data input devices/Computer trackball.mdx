# Computer trackball

**UNSPSC Code**: 43211734
**Class**: [Computer data input devices](Computer data input devices.mdx)
**Family**: [Computer Equipment and Accessories](../Computer Equipment and Accessories.mdx)
**Segment**: [Information Technology Broadcasting and Telecommunications](../../Information Technology Broadcasting and Telecommunications.mdx)

A trackball consists of a metal or plastic housing or casing, a ball that sticks out of the top of the casing and ,when rolled, a sensor sends impulses to the computer that causes a mouse-responsive program to reposition a visible indicator or cursor on the display screen. One or more buttons is used to select actions. This tracking device can be cabled, send infrared signals or connects via other wireless solutions to the computer.
