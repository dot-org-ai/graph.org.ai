# Computer mouse

A mouse consists of a metal or plastic housing or casing, a sensor on the bottom of the casing and ,when rolled, when moved this sends impulses to the computer that causes a mouse-responsive program to reposition a visible indicator or cursor on the display screen. One or more buttons is used to select actions. This tracking device can be cabled, send infared signals or connects via other wirelless solutions to the computer.
