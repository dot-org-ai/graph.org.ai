# Scanners

**UNSPSC Code**: 43211711
**Class**: [Computer data input devices](Computer data input devices.mdx)
**Family**: [Computer Equipment and Accessories](../Computer Equipment and Accessories.mdx)
**Segment**: [Information Technology Broadcasting and Telecommunications](../../Information Technology Broadcasting and Telecommunications.mdx)

A scanner captures images of photographic prints, bar codes, posters, magazine pages, and similar sources for computer editing and display. Scanners come in hand-held, feed-in, and flatbed types and for scanning black-and-white only or color. Optical character recognition software allows scanned printed documents to be converted to electronic text without having to type them in at a keyboard.
