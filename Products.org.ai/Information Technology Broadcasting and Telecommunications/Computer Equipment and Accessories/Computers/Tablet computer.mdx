# Tablet computer

**UNSPSC Code**: 43211509
**Class**: [Computers](Computers.mdx)
**Family**: [Computer Equipment and Accessories](../Computer Equipment and Accessories.mdx)
**Segment**: [Information Technology Broadcasting and Telecommunications](../../Information Technology Broadcasting and Telecommunications.mdx)

A computer in the form of a tablet designed to mimic the use of a traditional clip board. Larger and more powerful than a PDA, input is predominantly by the use of a stylus, finger touch or voice recognition. Tablet Computers usually run a specialised Tablet Computer Operating System and frequently have integrated wireless connectivity features, which enable them to communicate with corporate networks as the user moves through the building. They may or may not have an integral keyboard.
