# Reflector for antenna

A conductor bar or conductor plate that has shape of bar-type, net-type, flat-type, or paraboloid surface. It is used in the event of reaching farther distance or increasing the sensitivity of reception input by intensifying the energy that is to be radiated from the antenna to the relative direction.
