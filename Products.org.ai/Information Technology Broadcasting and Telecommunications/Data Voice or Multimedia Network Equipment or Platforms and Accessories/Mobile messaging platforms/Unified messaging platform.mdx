# Unified messaging platform

**UNSPSC Code**: 43223204
**Class**: [Mobile messaging platforms](Mobile messaging platforms.mdx)
**Family**: [Data Voice or Multimedia Network Equipment or Platforms and Accessories](../Data Voice or Multimedia Network Equipment or Platforms and Accessories.mdx)
**Segment**: [Information Technology Broadcasting and Telecommunications](../../Information Technology Broadcasting and Telecommunications.mdx)

This platform is a combination of a Telecommunications Network Component and an IT Network Component that is capable of receiving, storing and forwarding messages (voice, text and/or graphics) to various devices (PC, fax, voicemail) that are part of the Networks involved. A unified message can be created and forwarded by the user of the Network involved.
