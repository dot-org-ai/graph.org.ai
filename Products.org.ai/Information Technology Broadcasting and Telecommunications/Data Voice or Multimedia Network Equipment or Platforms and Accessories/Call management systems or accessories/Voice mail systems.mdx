# Voice mail systems

An automatic answering service with the ability to record and replay messages. Unlike simple answering machines, voice mail uses a programmable computer system with options such as temporary call routing, monitoring, and reporting. Refers to e-mail or mobile network systems that support audio. Users can leave spoken messages or listen to messages by executing commands in the e-mail system.
