# Frequency division multiplexer

**UNSPSC Code**: 43222828
**Class**: [Telephony equipment](Telephony equipment.mdx)

An electronic device or system that a device that selects one of several analog or digital input signals and forwards the selected input into a single line. The transmission medium is divided into channels divided into different frequency bands, and each information signal is converted into a transmission wave of the corresponding frequency band and transmitted.
