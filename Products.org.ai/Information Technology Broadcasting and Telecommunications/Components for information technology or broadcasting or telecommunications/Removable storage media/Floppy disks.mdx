# Floppy disks

**UNSPSC Code**: 43202004
**Class**: [Removable storage media](Removable storage media.mdx)
**Family**: [Components for information technology or broadcasting or telecommunications](../Components for information technology or broadcasting or telecommunications.mdx)
**Segment**: [Information Technology Broadcasting and Telecommunications](../../Information Technology Broadcasting and Telecommunications.mdx)

Removable magnetic diskettes that are read-writeable. Called "floppy disks" or "floppies" because their housings or outer sleeves were originally flexible, current standard for floppy format is a hard plastic 3.5 inch outer shell with 1.44 MB or 2.88 MB capacity.
