# Digital versatile disks DVDs

**UNSPSC Code**: 43202003
**Class**: [Removable storage media](Removable storage media.mdx)
**Family**: [Components for information technology or broadcasting or telecommunications](../Components for information technology or broadcasting or telecommunications.mdx)
**Segment**: [Information Technology Broadcasting and Telecommunications](../../Information Technology Broadcasting and Telecommunications.mdx)

A removable disk media that can be read-only or recordable. DVD or digital versatile/video disc - a type of CD-ROM that holds a minimum of 4.7GB (gigabytes), enough for a full-length movie. The DVD specification supports disks with capacities of from 4.7GB to 17GB and access rates of 600KBps to 1.3 MBps. DVD uses MPEG-2 to compress video data.
