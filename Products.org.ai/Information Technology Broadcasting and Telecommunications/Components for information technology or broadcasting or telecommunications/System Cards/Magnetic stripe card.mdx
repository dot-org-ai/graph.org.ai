# Magnetic stripe card

**UNSPSC Code**: 43201413
**Class**: [System Cards](System Cards.mdx)
**Family**: [Components for information technology or broadcasting or telecommunications](../Components for information technology or broadcasting or telecommunications.mdx)
**Segment**: [Information Technology Broadcasting and Telecommunications](../../Information Technology Broadcasting and Telecommunications.mdx)

A type of card capable of storing data by modifying the magnetism of tiny iron-based magnetic particles on a band of magnetic material on the card. The magnetic stripe, sometimes called a magstripe, is read by physical contact and swiping past a reading head.
