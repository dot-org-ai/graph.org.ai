# Graphics or video accelerator cards

**UNSPSC Code**: 43201401
**Class**: [System Cards](System Cards.mdx)
**Family**: [Components for information technology or broadcasting or telecommunications](../Components for information technology or broadcasting or telecommunications.mdx)
**Segment**: [Information Technology Broadcasting and Telecommunications](../../Information Technology Broadcasting and Telecommunications.mdx)

A graphics accelerator is a computer microelectronics component to which a computer program can offload the sending and refreshing of images to the display monitor and the computation of visual effects. Hardware (often an extra circuit board)to perform tasks such as plotting lines and surfaces in two or three dimensions, filling, shading and hidden line removal.
