# Motherboards

**UNSPSC Code**: 43201513

**Family**: [Components for information technology or broadcasting or telecommunications](../Components for information technology or broadcasting or telecommunications.mdx)

A motherboard is the physical component in a computer that contains the computer's basic circuitry and other components. On the typical motherboard, the circuitry is imprinted or affixed to the surface of a firm planar surface and usually manufactured in a single step.
