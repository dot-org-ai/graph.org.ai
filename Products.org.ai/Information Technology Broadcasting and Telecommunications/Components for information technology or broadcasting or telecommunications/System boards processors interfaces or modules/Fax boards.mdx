# Fax boards

**UNSPSC Code**: 43201545
**Class**: [System boards processors interfaces or modules](System boards processors interfaces or modules.mdx)
**Family**: [Components for information technology or broadcasting or telecommunications](../Components for information technology or broadcasting or telecommunications.mdx)
**Segment**: [Information Technology Broadcasting and Telecommunications](../../Information Technology Broadcasting and Telecommunications.mdx)

It is a loop-start interface, four-port fax board for medium to heavy fax traffic on a LAN. It supports modems, ECM, advanced compression (MR and MMR), and BFT. When combined with fax server software it can be used for LAN fax broadcasting.
