# Fire extinguisher cartridges 

**UNSPSC Code**: 46191623
**Class**: [Fire fighting equipment](Fire fighting equipment.mdx)
**Family**: [Fire protection](../Fire protection.mdx)
**Segment**: [Law Enforcement and National Security and Security and Safety Equipment and Supplies](../../Law Enforcement and National Security and Security and Safety Equipment and Supplies.mdx)

Replacement cartridges for extinguishers that contain the propellant (typically CO2 or nitrogen) in a separate container, or cartridge, located on the outside of the extinguisher. Within the cylinder is the extinguishing agent. When operated, the cartridge is punctured and the agent is discharged.
