# Intravenous or arterial arm boards

**UNSPSC Code**: 42221802
**Class**: [Intravenous and arterial catheter and needle positioning aids](Intravenous and arterial catheter and needle positioning aids.mdx)
**Family**: [Intravenous and arterial administration products](../Intravenous and arterial administration products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that consists of tubing needleless injection ports or stopcocks or manifolds used in an intravenous or arterial medical procedure.  US FDA regulates Intravenous or arterial arm boards under 21 CFR 878.3910 and under FDA product code BTX.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901890.
