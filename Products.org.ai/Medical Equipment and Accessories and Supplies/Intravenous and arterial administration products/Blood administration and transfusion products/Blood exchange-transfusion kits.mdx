# Blood exchange-transfusion kits

**UNSPSC Code**: 42222310
**Class**: [Blood administration and transfusion products](Blood administration and transfusion products.mdx)
**Family**: [Intravenous and arterial administration products](../Intravenous and arterial administration products.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a prepackaged collection (either custom or standard) of the equipment and supplies used to perform a blood exchange-transfusion procedure in a medical setting.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901890.
