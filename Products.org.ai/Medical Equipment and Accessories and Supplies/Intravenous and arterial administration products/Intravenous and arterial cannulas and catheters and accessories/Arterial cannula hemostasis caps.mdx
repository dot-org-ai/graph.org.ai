# Arterial cannula hemostasis caps

**UNSPSC Code**: 42221525
**Class**: [Intravenous and arterial cannulas and catheters and accessories](Intravenous and arterial cannulas and catheters and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Intravenous and arterial administration products](../Intravenous and arterial administration products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a hemostasis cap for thin tube inserted into a vein or body cavity to administer medicine, drain off fluid, or insert a surgical instrument associated with an intravenous or arterial procedure.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901831.
