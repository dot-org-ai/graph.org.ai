# Central venous catheters

**UNSPSC Code**: 42221503
**Class**: [Intravenous and arterial cannulas and catheters and accessories](Intravenous and arterial cannulas and catheters and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Intravenous and arterial administration products](../Intravenous and arterial administration products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a flexible tube inserted through a narrow opening into a central vein in an arterial lining procedure.  US FDA regulates Central venous catheters under 21 CFR 880.5970 and under FDA product code LJS.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901839.
