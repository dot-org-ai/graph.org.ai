# Intravenous catheters

**UNSPSC Code**: 42221512
**Class**: [Intravenous and arterial cannulas and catheters and accessories](Intravenous and arterial cannulas and catheters and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Intravenous and arterial administration products](../Intravenous and arterial administration products.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a flexible tube inserted through a narrow opening into a vein in an intravenous procedure.  US FDA regulates Intravenous catheters under 21 CFR 880.52 and under FDA product code FOZ.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901839.
