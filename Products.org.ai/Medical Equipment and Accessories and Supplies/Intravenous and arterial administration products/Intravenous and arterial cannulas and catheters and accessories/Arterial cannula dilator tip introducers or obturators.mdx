# Arterial cannula dilator tip introducers or obturators

**UNSPSC Code**: 42221520
**Class**: [Intravenous and arterial cannulas and catheters and accessories](Intravenous and arterial cannulas and catheters and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Intravenous and arterial administration products](../Intravenous and arterial administration products.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is used as a dilator tip introducers or obtruators for a thin tube inserted into a vein or body cavity to administer medicine, drain off fluid, or insert a surgical instrument associated with an arterial procedure.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901831.
