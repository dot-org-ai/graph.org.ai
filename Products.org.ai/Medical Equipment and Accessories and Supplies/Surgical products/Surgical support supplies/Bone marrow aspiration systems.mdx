# Bone marrow aspiration systems

In this classification, this commodity indicates a system consisting of products, consumables and accessories that are used to execute a bone marrow aspiration procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Bone marrow aspiration systems under 21 CFR 876.1075 and under FDA product code KNG.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901890.
