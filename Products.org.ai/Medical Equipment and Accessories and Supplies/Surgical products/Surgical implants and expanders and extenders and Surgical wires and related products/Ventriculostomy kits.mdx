# Ventriculostomy kits

**UNSPSC Code**: 42295520
**Class**: [Surgical implants and expanders and extenders and Surgical wires and related products](Surgical implants and expanders and extenders and Surgical wires and related products.mdx)
**Family**: [Surgical products](../Surgical products.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a prepackaged collection (either custom or standard) of the equipment and supplies used to perform ventriculostomy procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Ventriculostomy kits under 21 CFR 882.43 and under FDA product code HBG.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901890.
