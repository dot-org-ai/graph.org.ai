# Implantable infusion ports

**UNSPSC Code**: 42295503
**Class**: [Surgical implants and expanders and extenders and Surgical wires and related products](Surgical implants and expanders and extenders and Surgical wires and related products.mdx)
**Family**: [Surgical products](../Surgical products.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as an implantable infusion port for dispensing medication in the body of a patient comprises a septum which is preferably integrally molded from a nontoxic, rubberized material and has an interior cavity formed therein for receiving medication in the septum, and a catheter element which extends from the cavity to the exterior of the septum for dispensing medication in the body of a patient in a medical setting Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901890.
