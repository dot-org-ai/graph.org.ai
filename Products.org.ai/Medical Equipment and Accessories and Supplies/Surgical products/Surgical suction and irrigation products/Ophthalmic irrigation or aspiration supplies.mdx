# Ophthalmic irrigation or aspiration supplies

**UNSPSC Code**: 42293504

**Family**: [Surgical products](../Surgical products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is either an accessory or consumable associated with ophthalmic irrigation or aspiration supplies.  US FDA regulates Ophthalmic irrigation or aspiration supplies or accessories under 21 CFR 886.4150 and under FDA product code HQE.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901920.
