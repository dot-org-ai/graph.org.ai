# Cystoscopes or cystourethroscopes

**UNSPSC Code**: 42296311
**Class**: [Endoscopes](Endoscopes.mdx)
**Family**: [Surgical products](../Surgical products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a slender tubular medical instrument for examining the interior of the urethra and urinary bladder in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Cystoscopes or cystourethroscopes under 21 CFR 884.1690 and under FDA product code HIH.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 900630.
