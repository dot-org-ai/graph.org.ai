# Duodenoscopes

**UNSPSC Code**: 42296313
**Class**: [Endoscopes](Endoscopes.mdx)
**Family**: [Surgical products](../Surgical products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a flexible, lighted tubes that are threaded through the mouth, throat, and stomach into the top of the small intestine (duodenum) in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Duodenoscopes under 21 CFR 876.15 and under FDA product code FDT.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 900630.
