# Amnioscopes or fetoscopes

**UNSPSC Code**: 42296301
**Class**: [Endoscopes](Endoscopes.mdx)
**Family**: [Surgical products](../Surgical products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is used as an  endoscope that, by introduction into the cervical canal, permits direct visualization of the fetus and amniotic fluid in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Amnioscopes or fetoscopes under 21 CFR 876.1330 and under FDA product code PGD.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 900630.
