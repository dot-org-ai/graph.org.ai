# Tracheal video intubation fiberscope or endoscope

**UNSPSC Code**: 42296322
**Class**: [Endoscopes](Endoscopes.mdx)
**Family**: [Surgical products](../Surgical products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as an endoscope that transmits images of the interior of a trachea by fiber optics in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Intubation scopes or tracheal fibrescopes under 21 CFR 874.4680 and under FDA product code EOQ.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 900630.
