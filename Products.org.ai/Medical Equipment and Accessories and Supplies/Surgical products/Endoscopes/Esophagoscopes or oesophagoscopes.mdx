# Esophagoscopes or oesophagoscopes

**UNSPSC Code**: 42296318
**Class**: [Endoscopes](Endoscopes.mdx)
**Family**: [Surgical products](../Surgical products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as an endoscope that transmits images of the interior of the esophagus by fiber optics in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Esophagoscopes or oesophagoscopes under 21 CFR 874.4710 and under FDA product code EOX.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 900630.
