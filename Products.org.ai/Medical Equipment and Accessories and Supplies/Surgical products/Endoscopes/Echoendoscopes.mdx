# Echoendoscopes

**UNSPSC Code**: 42296314
**Class**: [Endoscopes](Endoscopes.mdx)
**Family**: [Surgical products](../Surgical products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as an endoscope fitted with an ultrasound probe. It is used to visualize both the lumen of the gastrointestinal tract and abnormalities that are present in or near the wall of the intubated organ in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Echoendoscopes under 21 CFR 877.1330 and under FDA product code PGD.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 900630.
