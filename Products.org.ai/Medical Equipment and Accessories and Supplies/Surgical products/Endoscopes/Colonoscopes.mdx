# Colonoscopes

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is used as a flexible fiber-optic instrument inserted through the anus in order to examine the colon in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Colonoscopes under 21 CFR 876.15 and under FDA product code FDF.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 900630.
