# Thoracoscopes

**UNSPSC Code**: 42296338
**Class**: [Endoscopes](Endoscopes.mdx)
**Family**: [Surgical products](../Surgical products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as an endoscope involving internal examination, biopsy, and/or resection of disease or masses within the pleural cavity and thoracic cavity in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Thoracoscopes under 21 CFR 876.15 and under FDA product code GCJ.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 900630.
