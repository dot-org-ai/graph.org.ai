# Proctoscopes or protoscopes or rectoscopes

**UNSPSC Code**: 42296334
**Class**: [Endoscopes](Endoscopes.mdx)
**Family**: [Surgical products](../Surgical products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a medical instrument with an integral lamp for examining the anus and lower part of the rectum or carrying out minor medical procedures in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Proctoscopes or protoscopes or rectoscopes under 21 CFR 876.15 and under FDA product code FER.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 900630.
