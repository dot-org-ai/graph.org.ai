# Surgical dilator or probe or groove accessories

**UNSPSC Code**: 42292604
**Class**: [Surgical dilators and probes and grooves and related products](Surgical dilators and probes and grooves and related products.mdx)
**Family**: [Surgical products](../Surgical products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a component or attachment for an instrument cleaning device used during a surgical procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates surgical probes or directors under 21 CFR 878.44 and under FDA product code GEI.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule surgical dilator or probe or groove accessories under HS 901920 and 901890.
