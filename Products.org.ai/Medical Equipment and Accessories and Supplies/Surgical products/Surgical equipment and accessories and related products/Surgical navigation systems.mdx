# Surgical navigation systems

**UNSPSC Code**: 42295148
**Class**: [Surgical equipment and accessories and related products](Surgical equipment and accessories and related products.mdx)
**Family**: [Surgical products](../Surgical products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a system consisting of products, consumables and accessories that are used to execute a surgical navigation procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates surgical navigation systems under 21 CFR 882.4560 and under FDA product code HAW.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901890.
