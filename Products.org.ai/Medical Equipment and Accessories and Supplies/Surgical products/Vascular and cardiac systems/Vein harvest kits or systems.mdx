# Vein harvest kits or systems

**UNSPSC Code**: 42294401
**Class**: [Vascular and cardiac systems](Vascular and cardiac systems.mdx)
**Family**: [Surgical products](../Surgical products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a prepackaged collection (either custom or standard) of the equipment and supplies used to perform a vein harvesting procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Vein harvest kits or systems under 21 CFR 878.48 and under FDA product code GAD.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901890.
