# Breast localization needles

**UNSPSC Code**: 42294306
**Class**: [Breast biopsy equipment and instruments and supplies](Breast biopsy equipment and instruments and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Surgical products](../Surgical products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a hollow needle commonly used with a syringe to inject or extract substances or fluids associated with a breast localization procedure.  US FDA regulates Breast localization needles under 21 CFR 880.5570 and under FDA product code FMI.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901832.
