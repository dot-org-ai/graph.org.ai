# Ventricular cannulas or needles

**UNSPSC Code**: 42294741
**Class**: [Open heart perfusion equipment and monitors and accessories and related products](Open heart perfusion equipment and monitors and accessories and related products.mdx)
**Family**: [Surgical products](../Surgical products.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a metal tube or hollow needle commonly used with a syringe to inject or extract substances or fluids associated with a ventricular procedure.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901831.
