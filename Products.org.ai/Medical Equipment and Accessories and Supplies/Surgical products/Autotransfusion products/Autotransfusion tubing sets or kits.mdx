# Autotransfusion tubing sets or kits

**UNSPSC Code**: 42294606
**Class**: [Autotransfusion products](Autotransfusion products.mdx)
**Family**: [Surgical products](../Surgical products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a prepackaged collection (either custom or standard) of the equipment and supplies used to perform an autotrasfusion intubation procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Autotransfusion tubing sets or kits under 21 CFR 868.5830 and under FDA product code CAC.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901839.
