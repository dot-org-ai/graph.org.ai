# Peritoneal dialysis catheters or indwelling access devices

**UNSPSC Code**: 42161506
**Class**: [Peritoneal and equilibrium dialysis equipment and supplies](Peritoneal and equilibrium dialysis equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Dialysis equipment and supplies](../Dialysis equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as the indwelling access devices for a flexible tube inserted through a narrow opening into the peritoneal cavity in a peritoneal procedure.  US FDA regulates Peritoneal dialysis catheters or indwelling access devices under 21 CFR 878.42 and under FDA product code GBW.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901819.
