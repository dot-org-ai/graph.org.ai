# Peritoneal dialysis catheter adapters or clamps or connectors

**UNSPSC Code**: 42161504
**Class**: [Peritoneal and equilibrium dialysis equipment and supplies](Peritoneal and equilibrium dialysis equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Dialysis equipment and supplies](../Dialysis equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a flexible tube inserted through a narrow opening into the peritoneal cavity (along with the associated adapters and clamps) in a peritoneal procedure.  US FDA regulates Peritoneal dialysis catheter adapters or clamps or connectors under 21 CFR 876.5630 and under FDA product code FKO.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901832.
