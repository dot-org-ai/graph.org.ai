# Hemodialysis administration kits or sets

**UNSPSC Code**: 42161601

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a prepackaged collection (either custom or standard) of the equipment and supplies used to perform a hemodialysis administration procedure in a medical setting.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901890.
