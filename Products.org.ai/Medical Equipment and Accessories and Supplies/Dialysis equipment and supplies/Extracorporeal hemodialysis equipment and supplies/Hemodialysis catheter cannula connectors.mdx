# Hemodialysis catheter cannula connectors

**UNSPSC Code**: 42161645
**Class**: [Extracorporeal hemodialysis equipment and supplies](Extracorporeal hemodialysis equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Dialysis equipment and supplies](../Dialysis equipment and supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as the cannula connecter for a flexible tube inserted through a narrow opening into the kidney for a hemodialysis procedure.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901831.
