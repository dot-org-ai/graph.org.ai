# Continuous passive motion CPM devices

**UNSPSC Code**: 42251603
**Class**: [Rehabilitation exercise devices and equipment](Rehabilitation exercise devices and equipment.mdx)
**Family**: [Physical and occupational therapy and rehabilitation products](../Physical and occupational therapy and rehabilitation products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a device which constantly moves the joint through a controlled range of motion; the exact range is dependent upon the joint, but in most cases the range of motion is increased over time in a medical setting Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901910.
