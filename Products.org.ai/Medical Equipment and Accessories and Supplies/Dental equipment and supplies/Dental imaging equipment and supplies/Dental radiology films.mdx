# Dental radiology films

**UNSPSC Code**: 42152010

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is the film used in a dental radiology machine.  US FDA regulates Dental radiology films under 21 CFR 892. 184 and under FDA product code IWZ.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 370200.
