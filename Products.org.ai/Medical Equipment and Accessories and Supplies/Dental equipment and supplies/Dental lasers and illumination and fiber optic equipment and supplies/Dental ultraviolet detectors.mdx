# Dental ultraviolet detectors

**UNSPSC Code**: 42152308

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that provides a source of ultraviolet light which is used to identify otherwise invisible material, such as dental plaque, present in or on teeth.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901819.
