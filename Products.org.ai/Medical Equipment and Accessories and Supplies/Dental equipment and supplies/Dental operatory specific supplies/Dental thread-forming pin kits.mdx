# Dental thread-forming pin kits

**UNSPSC Code**: 42152610
**Class**: [Dental operatory specific supplies](Dental operatory specific supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Dental equipment and supplies](../Dental equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a kit that allows for the creation of a thread-forming item designed for insertion into a drilled and untapped hole to reinforce and anchor the restorative material to a patient's tooth.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901819.
