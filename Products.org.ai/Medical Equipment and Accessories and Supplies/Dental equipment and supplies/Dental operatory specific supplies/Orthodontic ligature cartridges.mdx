# Orthodontic ligature cartridges

**UNSPSC Code**: 42152608
**Class**: [Dental operatory specific supplies](Dental operatory specific supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Dental equipment and supplies](../Dental equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that dispenses ligature which are a small elastic band that is used to affix the archwire to the bracket in a dental procedure.  US FDA regulates Orthodontic ligature cartridges under 21 CFR 872.4565 and under FDA product code ECP.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901819.
