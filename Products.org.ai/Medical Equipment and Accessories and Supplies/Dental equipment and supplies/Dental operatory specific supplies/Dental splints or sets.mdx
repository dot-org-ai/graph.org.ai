# Dental splints or sets

**UNSPSC Code**: 42152606
**Class**: [Dental operatory specific supplies](Dental operatory specific supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Dental equipment and supplies](../Dental equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that creates a plastic splint or setting that reinforces and limits the location of teeth.  US FDA regulates Dental splints or sets under 21 CFR 872.3740 and under FDA product code EBL.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901819.
