# Dental matrices or sets

**UNSPSC Code**: 42153002
**Class**: [Dental and subspecialty instrument and device sets](Dental and subspecialty instrument and device sets.mdx)
**Family**: [Dental equipment and supplies](../Dental equipment and supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a matrice to form the interproximal surfaces when fabricating a direct restoration in a dental procedure.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901811.
