# Crowns or crown forms

**UNSPSC Code**: 42151503
**Class**: [Cosmetic dentistry equipment and supplies](Cosmetic dentistry equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Dental equipment and supplies](../Dental equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is custom configured and intended to provide damage abatement and functional restoration to single teeth that are damaged or malformed.  US FDA regulates Crowns or crown forms under 21 CFR 872.3330 and under FDA product code ELZ.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 611420.
