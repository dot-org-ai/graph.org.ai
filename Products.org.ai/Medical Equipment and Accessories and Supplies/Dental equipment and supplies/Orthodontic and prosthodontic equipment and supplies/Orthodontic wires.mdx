# Orthodontic wires

**UNSPSC Code**: 42152717
**Class**: [Orthodontic and prosthodontic equipment and supplies](Orthodontic and prosthodontic equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Dental equipment and supplies](../Dental equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a band driver, pusher or setter used during an orthodontic dental procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Orthodontic wires under 21 CFR 872.5410 and under FDA product code DZC.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 902110.
