# Dental pulp or vitality testers

**UNSPSC Code**: 42151655
**Class**: [Dental and subspecialty instruments and devices](Dental and subspecialty instruments and devices.mdx)
**Family**: [Dental equipment and supplies](../Dental equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a device that electronically ascertains the vitality of a tooth.  US FDA regulates Dental pulp or vitality testers under 21 CFR 872.1730 and under FDA product code EAS.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901849.
