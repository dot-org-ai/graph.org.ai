# Dental retractors

**UNSPSC Code**: 42151612
**Class**: [Dental and subspecialty instruments and devices](Dental and subspecialty instruments and devices.mdx)
**Family**: [Dental equipment and supplies](../Dental equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is an instrument that's used to move the cheek, lips, and tongue out of the way during a dental procedure.  US FDA regulates Dental retractors under 21 CFR 872.4565 and under FDA product code EIF.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901890.
