# Dental implant overdenture kits

**UNSPSC Code**: 42152472
**Class**: [Dental materials](Dental materials.mdx)
**Family**: [Dental equipment and supplies](../Dental equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that consists of a prearranged grouping of implant of denture instruments and or products that normally would be used in a specific over denture implant procedure.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901819.
