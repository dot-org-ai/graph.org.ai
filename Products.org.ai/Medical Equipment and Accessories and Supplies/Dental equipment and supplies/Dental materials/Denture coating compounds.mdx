# Denture coating compounds

**UNSPSC Code**: 42152460
**Class**: [Dental materials](Dental materials.mdx)
**Family**: [Dental equipment and supplies](../Dental equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that provides a coating for the proper installation and maintenance of dentures.  US FDA regulates Denture coating compounds under 21 CFR 872.33 and under FDA product code EBE.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 300640.
