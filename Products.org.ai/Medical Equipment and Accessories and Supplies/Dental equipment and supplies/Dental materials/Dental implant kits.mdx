# Dental implant kits

**UNSPSC Code**: 42152464
**Class**: [Dental materials](Dental materials.mdx)
**Family**: [Dental equipment and supplies](../Dental equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that consists of a prearranged grouping of implants, components and accessories that normally would be used in a specific over dental implant procedure.  US FDA regulates Dental implant kits under 21 CFR 872.3980 and under FDA product code OFY.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901819.
