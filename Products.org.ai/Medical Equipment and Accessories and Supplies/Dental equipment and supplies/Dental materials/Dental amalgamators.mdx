# Dental amalgamators

**UNSPSC Code**: 42152434

**Family**: [Dental equipment and supplies](../Dental equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that are the instruments used to create amalgams used in dental impression molding and installation procedures.  US FDA regulates Dental amalgamators under 21 CFR 872.31 and under FDA product code EFD.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901839.
