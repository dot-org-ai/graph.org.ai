# Dental amalgam capsules

**UNSPSC Code**: 42151803
**Class**: [Dental fillers and finishing and polishing supplies](Dental fillers and finishing and polishing supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Dental equipment and supplies](../Dental equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that contains in a capsule containing amalgam used to fill caries in a dental setting.  US FDA regulates Dental amalgam capsules under 21 CFR 872.3110 and under FDA product code DZS.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901839.
