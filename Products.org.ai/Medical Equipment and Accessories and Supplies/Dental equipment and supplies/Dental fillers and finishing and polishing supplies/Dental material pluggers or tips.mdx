# Dental material pluggers or tips

**UNSPSC Code**: 42151802
**Class**: [Dental fillers and finishing and polishing supplies](Dental fillers and finishing and polishing supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Dental equipment and supplies](../Dental equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that acts as a plugger or forming tip for the instruments that compact and shape material used in dental carie procedures.  US FDA regulates Dental material pluggers or tips under 21 CFR 872.4565 and under FDA product code EKR.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901839.
