# Dental impression material syringes

**UNSPSC Code**: 42152108
**Class**: [Dental impression and forming equipment and supplies](Dental impression and forming equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Dental equipment and supplies](../Dental equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that delivers through a syringe the materials used to create dental impressions, castings, models and investments.  US FDA regulates Dental impression material syringes under 21 CFR 872.4565 and under FDA product code EID.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901831.
