# Dental impression trays

**UNSPSC Code**: 42152105
**Class**: [Dental impression and forming equipment and supplies](Dental impression and forming equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Dental equipment and supplies](../Dental equipment and supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that consists of the components and materials used to create a dental impression.  US FDA regulates Dental impression trays under 21 CFR 872.6880 and under FDA product code EHY.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 392410.
