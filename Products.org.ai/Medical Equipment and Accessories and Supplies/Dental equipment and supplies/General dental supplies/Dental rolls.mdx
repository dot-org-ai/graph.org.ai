# Dental rolls

**UNSPSC Code**: 42152507
**Class**: [General dental supplies](General dental supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Dental equipment and supplies](../Dental equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a roll cotton or other fiber based that absorbs liquids or provides spacing in the mouths of people undergoing dental procedures.  US FDA regulates Dental rolls under 21 CFR 872.42 and under FDA product code EBW.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 300510.
