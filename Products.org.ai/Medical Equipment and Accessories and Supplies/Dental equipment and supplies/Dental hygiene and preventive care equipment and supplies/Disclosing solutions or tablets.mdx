# Disclosing solutions or tablets

**UNSPSC Code**: 42151904
**Class**: [Dental hygiene and preventive care equipment and supplies](Dental hygiene and preventive care equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Dental equipment and supplies](../Dental equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that in either a solution or tablet form discloses the parts of plaque or food residue missed by flossing.  US FDA regulates Disclosing solutions or tablets under 21 CFR 872.1740 and under FDA product code NYH.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 300339.
