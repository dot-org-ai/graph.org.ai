# Dental examination chair related parts or accessories

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is used as component parts or related accessories to a dental examination chair.  US FDA regulates related parts to dental examination chairs under 21 CFR 872.6250 and under FDA product code EIA. Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule dental examination chair related parts or accessories under HS 940290.
