# Veterinary external fixation kits

**UNSPSC Code**: 42121514
**Class**: [Veterinary equipment](Veterinary equipment.mdx)
**Family**: [Veterinary equipment and supplies](../Veterinary equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a prepackaged collection (either custom or standard) of the equipment and supplies used to perform the surgical treatment that stabilizes bone and soft tissues at a distance from the operative or injury focus in animals.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 902110.
