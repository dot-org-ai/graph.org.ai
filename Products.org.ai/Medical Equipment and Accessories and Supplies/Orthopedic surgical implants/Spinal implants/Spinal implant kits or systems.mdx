# Spinal implant kits or systems

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a prepackaged collection (either custom or standard) of the equipment and supplies used to perform a spinal implant procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Spinal implant kits or systems under 21 CFR 888.3080 and under FDA product code MAX.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 902129.
