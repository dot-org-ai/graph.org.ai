# Ostomy appliances

**UNSPSC Code**: 42312102
**Class**: [Ostomy supplies and non surgical wound drainage products](Ostomy supplies and non surgical wound drainage products.mdx)
**Family**: [Wound care products](../Wound care products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates an appliance used to create the alteration of tissue required in an ostomy procedure done in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Ostomy appliances under 21 CFR 876.59 and under FDA product code EXB.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 300691.
