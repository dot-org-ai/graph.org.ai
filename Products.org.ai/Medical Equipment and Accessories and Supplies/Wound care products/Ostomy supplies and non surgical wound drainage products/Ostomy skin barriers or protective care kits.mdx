# Ostomy skin barriers or protective care kits

**UNSPSC Code**: 42312105
**Class**: [Ostomy supplies and non surgical wound drainage products](Ostomy supplies and non surgical wound drainage products.mdx)
**Family**: [Wound care products](../Wound care products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a prepackaged collection (either custom or standard) of the equipment and supplies that enable ostomy skin barriers use in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Ostomy skin barriers or protective care kits under 21 CFR 876.59 and under FDA product code EXB.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901819.
