# Wet dressing systems

**UNSPSC Code**: 42311528
**Class**: [Bandages and dressings and related products](Bandages and dressings and related products.mdx)
**Family**: [Wound care products](../Wound care products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a system consisting of products, consumables and accessories that are used to execute a Wet dressing procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Wet dressing systems under 21 CFR 888.5090 and under FDA product code KMF.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901890.
