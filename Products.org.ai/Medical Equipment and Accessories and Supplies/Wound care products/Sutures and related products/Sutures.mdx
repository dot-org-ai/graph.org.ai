# Sutures

**UNSPSC Code**: 42312201
**Class**: [Sutures and related products](Sutures and related products.mdx)
**Family**: [Wound care products](../Wound care products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as to create a stitch or row of stitches holding together the edges of a wound or surgical incision  in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Sutures under 21 CFR 878.4830 and under FDA product code GAL.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 300610.
