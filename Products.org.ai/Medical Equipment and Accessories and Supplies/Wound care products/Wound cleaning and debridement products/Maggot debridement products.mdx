# Maggot debridement products

**UNSPSC Code**: 42312314
**Class**: [Wound cleaning and debridement products](Wound cleaning and debridement products.mdx)
**Family**: [Wound care products](../Wound care products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a type of biotherapy involving the introduction of live, disinfected maggots (fly larvae) into the non-healing skin and soft tissue wound of a human or animal for the purpose of cleaning out the necrotic tissue within a wound and disinfection in a medical setting Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 902110.
