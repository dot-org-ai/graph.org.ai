# Abdominal binders

In this classification, this commodity indicates a fitted elastic material that goes around your abdomen. It may be used to support muscles. It also keeps bandages in place, or helps incisions heal after abdominal or pelvic surgery product in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Abdominal binders under 21 CFR 890.3490 and under FDA product code KTD.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 902110.
