# Autopsy dissection kits

**UNSPSC Code**: 42261509
**Class**: [Pathology dissection instruments and supplies](Pathology dissection instruments and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Postmortem and mortuary equipment and supplies](../Postmortem and mortuary equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a prepackaged collection (either custom or standard) of the equipment and supplies used to perform an autopsy dissection procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Autopsy dissection kits under 21 CFR 872.4120 and under FDA product code DWI.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901890.
