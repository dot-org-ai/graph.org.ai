**UNSPSC Code**: 42261708

In this classification, this commodity is a product used as an accessory to grossing workstations and associated furniture in association with autopsy procedures in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates autopsy grossing workstations under 21 CFR 878.4960 and under FDA product code LGX. Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule grossing workstations under HS 940290.
