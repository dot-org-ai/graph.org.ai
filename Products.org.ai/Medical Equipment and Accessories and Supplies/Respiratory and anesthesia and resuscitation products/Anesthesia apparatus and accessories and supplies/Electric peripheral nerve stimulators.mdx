# Electric peripheral nerve stimulators

**UNSPSC Code**: 42272514
**Class**: [Anesthesia apparatus and accessories and supplies](Anesthesia apparatus and accessories and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products](../Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a small electrical device (a wire-like electrode) next to one of the peripheral nerves located beyond the brain or spinal cord. The electrode delivers rapid electrical pulses that are felt like mild tingles (so-called paresthesias) in a medical setting Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901920.
