# Chest cuirass products

**UNSPSC Code**: 42272102
**Class**: [Negative mechanical pressure ventilators](Negative mechanical pressure ventilators.mdx)
**Family**: [Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products](../Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as  part of a non-invasive method of negative pressure ventilation which requires the patient to wear an upper body shell or cuirass in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Chest cuirass products under 21 CFR 868.5935 and under FDA product code BYT.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901890.
