# Non invasive continuous positive air pressure machines

**UNSPSC Code**: 42272202
**Class**: [Positive mechanical pressure ventilators and accessories](Positive mechanical pressure ventilators and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products](../Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a machine that is a form of bilevel positive airway pressure , which applies mild air pressure on a continuous basis to keep the airways continuously open in people who are able to breathe spontaneously on their own in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Non invasive continuous positive air pressure machines under 21 CFR 872.5410 and under FDA product code DZD.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 480220.
