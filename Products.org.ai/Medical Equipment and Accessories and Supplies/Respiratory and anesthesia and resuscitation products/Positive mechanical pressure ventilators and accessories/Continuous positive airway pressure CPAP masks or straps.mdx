# Continuous positive airway pressure CPAP masks or straps

**UNSPSC Code**: 42272213
**Class**: [Positive mechanical pressure ventilators and accessories](Positive mechanical pressure ventilators and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products](../Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a mask or strap that maintains continuous positive airway pressure in an artificial airway management procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Continuous positive airway pressure CPAP masks or straps under 21 CFR 868.5905 and under FDA product code BZD.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901890.
