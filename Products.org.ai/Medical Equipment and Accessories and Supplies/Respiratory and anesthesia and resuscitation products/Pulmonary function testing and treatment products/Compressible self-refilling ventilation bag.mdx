# Compressible self-refilling ventilation bag

**UNSPSC Code**: 42271651
**Class**: [Pulmonary function testing and treatment products](Pulmonary function testing and treatment products.mdx)
**Family**: [Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products](../Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

Hand operated, compressible self refilling ventilation bag with non-rebreathing patient valve with pressure limitation valve to minimize the risk of overinflating, intake valve with nipple for oxygen tubing. Supplied with oxygen reservoir bag (suitable capacity) and 2-meter oxygen tubing. The resuscitator can be disassembled for cleaning, disinfecting, or autoclaving process. Size: Adult, Paediatric & Neonatal.
