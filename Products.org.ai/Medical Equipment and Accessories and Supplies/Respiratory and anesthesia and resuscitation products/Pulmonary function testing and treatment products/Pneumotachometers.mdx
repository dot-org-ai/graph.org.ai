# Pneumotachometers

**UNSPSC Code**: 42271615
**Class**: [Pulmonary function testing and treatment products](Pulmonary function testing and treatment products.mdx)
**Family**: [Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products](../Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a transducer for measuring exhaled air flow of a patient  in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Pneumotachometers under 21 CFR 868.2550 and under FDA product code JAX.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901819.
