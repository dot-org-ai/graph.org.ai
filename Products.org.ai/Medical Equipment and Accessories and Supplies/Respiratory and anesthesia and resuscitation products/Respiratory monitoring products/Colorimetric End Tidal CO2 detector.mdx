# Colorimetric End Tidal CO2 detector

**UNSPSC Code**: 42271517
**Class**: [Respiratory monitoring products](Respiratory monitoring products.mdx)
**Family**: [Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products](../Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

For adult or pediatric. Colorimetric CO2 detectors visually indicate the presence of exhaled carbon dioxide. They are designed to connect between an endotracheal tube and a resuscitation bag to aid in the verification of proper ETT placement upon intubation.
