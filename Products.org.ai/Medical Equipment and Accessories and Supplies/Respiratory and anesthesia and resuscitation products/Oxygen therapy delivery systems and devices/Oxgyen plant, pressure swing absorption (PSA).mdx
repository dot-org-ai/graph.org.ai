# Oxgyen plant, pressure swing absorption (PSA)

**UNSPSC Code**: 42271731
**Class**: [Oxygen therapy delivery systems and devices](Oxygen therapy delivery systems and devices.mdx)
**Family**: [Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products](../Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

PSA oxygen plants are based on the well-known PSA technology (PSA means pressure swing adsorption). Two pressurized vessels with zeolite ensure continuous production for the separation of oxygen from other gases. An OXYMAT O2 generator consists of two pressure vessels storing zeolite.
