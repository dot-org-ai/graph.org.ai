# Reprocessed/ sustainable laryngoscope blades

**UNSPSC Code**: 42272021
**Class**: [Intubation supplies](Intubation supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products](../Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a Reprocessed (Reprocessing medical devices is a process that involves cleaning, disinfection, sterilization, and other procedures to make used devices safe for reuse) or Sustainable (made with recycled materials) Laryngoscope blade which is used in conjunction with a laryngoscope handle to examine the larynx and place an endotracheal tube. They are attached to a handle that can be reusable or disposable.
