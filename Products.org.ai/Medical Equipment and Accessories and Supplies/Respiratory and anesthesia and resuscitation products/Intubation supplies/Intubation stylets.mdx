# Intubation stylets

**UNSPSC Code**: 42272004

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a stylet in an intubation procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Intubation stylets under 21 CFR 868.5790 and under FDA product code BSR.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901839.
