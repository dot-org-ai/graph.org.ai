# Intubation laryngoscopes/ laryngoscope blades

**UNSPSC Code**: 42272001
**Class**: [Intubation supplies](Intubation supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products](../Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a laryngoscope in an intubation procedure in a medical setting (the blade could be straight or curved on the laryngoscope).  US FDA regulates Intubation laryngoscopes under 21 CFR 874.4760 and under FDA product code EQN.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 392690.
