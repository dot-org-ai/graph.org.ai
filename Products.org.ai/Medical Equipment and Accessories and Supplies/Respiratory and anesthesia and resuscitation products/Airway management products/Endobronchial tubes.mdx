# Endobronchial tubes

**UNSPSC Code**: 42271905
**Class**: [Airway management products](Airway management products.mdx)
**Family**: [Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products](../Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is used as a hollow tube to support ventilation or an inflatable balloon to block a bronchus in an endobronchial procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Endobronchial tubes under 21 CFR 868.5720 and under FDA product code BTS.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901839.
