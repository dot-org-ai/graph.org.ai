# Endotracheal or tracheotomy sets

In this classification, this commodity indicates a set of instruments and consumables used in a endotracheal or tracheotomy procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Endotracheal or tracheotomy sets under 21 CFR 868.58 and under FDA product code OGW.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901920.
