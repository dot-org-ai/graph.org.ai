# Pharyngeal airways or airways kits

**UNSPSC Code**: 42271913
**Class**: [Airway management products](Airway management products.mdx)
**Family**: [Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products](../Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a prepackaged collection (either custom or standard) of the equipment and supplies that enable pharyngeal airway use in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Pharyngeal airways or airways kits under 21 CFR 868.6810 and under FDA product code OFO.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901811.
