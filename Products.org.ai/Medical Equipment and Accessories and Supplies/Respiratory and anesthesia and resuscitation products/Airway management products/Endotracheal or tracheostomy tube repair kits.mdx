# Endotracheal or tracheostomy tube repair kits

**UNSPSC Code**: 42271906
**Class**: [Airway management products](Airway management products.mdx)
**Family**: [Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products](../Respiratory and anesthesia and resuscitation products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a prepackaged collection (either custom or standard) of the equipment and supplies used to perform an endotracheal or tracheostomy tube repair procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Endotracheal or tracheostomy tube repair kits under 21 CFR 868.5730 and under FDA product code BSK.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901839.
