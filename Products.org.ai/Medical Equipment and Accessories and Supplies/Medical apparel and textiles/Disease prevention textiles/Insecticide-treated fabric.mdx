# Insecticide-treated fabric

**UNSPSC Code**: 42132303
**Class**: [Disease prevention textiles](Disease prevention textiles.mdx)
**Family**: [Medical apparel and textiles](../Medical apparel and textiles.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

Insecticide-treated fabric is a type of textile that has been treated with insecticides to repel or kill insects, particularly mosquitoes. The insecticide is typically a pyrethroid insecticide, which is applied to the fabric using a variety of methods, such as impregnation, spraying, or dipping.
