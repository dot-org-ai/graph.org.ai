# Insecticide-treated bed net

**UNSPSC Code**: 42132301
**Class**: [Disease prevention textiles](Disease prevention textiles.mdx)
**Family**: [Medical apparel and textiles](../Medical apparel and textiles.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

An insecticide-treated bed net is a type of mosquito net that has been treated with insecticides, typically a pyrethroid insecticide. The insecticide is usually applied to the netting material through a process called impregnation, which involves soaking the net in a solution of the insecticide.
