# Latex Surgical gloves

**UNSPSC Code**: 42132205
**Class**: [Medical gloves and accessories](Medical gloves and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Medical apparel and textiles](../Medical apparel and textiles.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a Surgical Glove that is made with Latex (powdered or powder-free) that provides prevention of contamination of the patient during invasive procedures and to protect the hand from exposure to potentially infectious materials during surgical procedures.  US FDA regulates surgical gloves under 21 CFR 878.4460 and under FDA product code KGO.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 920999.
