# Nitrile medical exam or non surgical procedure gloves

**UNSPSC Code**: 42132203
**Class**: [Medical gloves and accessories](Medical gloves and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Medical apparel and textiles](../Medical apparel and textiles.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity product that is made out of Nitrile material which provides prevention of contamination of the patient during invasive procedures and to protect the hand from exposure to potentially infectious materials during medical examinations.  US FDA regulates Medical exam or non surgical procedure gloves under 21 CFR 880.6250 and under FDA product code FM.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 392620.
