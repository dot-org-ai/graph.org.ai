# Medical staff isolation or cover gowns

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that has been rated by ssociation for the Advancement of Medical Instrumentation for its protection against fluids and used as an isolation or cover gown by people engaged as medical staff.  US FDA regulates Medical staff isolation or cover gowns under 21 CFR 878.4040 and under FDA product code OEA.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 621010.
