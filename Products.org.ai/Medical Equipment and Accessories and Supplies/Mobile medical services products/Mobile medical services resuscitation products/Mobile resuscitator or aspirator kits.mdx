# Mobile resuscitator or aspirator kits

**UNSPSC Code**: 42172103
**Class**: [Mobile medical services resuscitation products](Mobile medical services resuscitation products.mdx)
**Family**: [Mobile medical services products](../Mobile medical services products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a prepackaged collection (either custom or standard) of the equipment and supplies used to perform resuscitation or aspiration procedures by mobile medical personnel in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Mobile resuscitator or aspirator kits under 21 CFR 868.5915 and under FDA product code BT.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901890.
