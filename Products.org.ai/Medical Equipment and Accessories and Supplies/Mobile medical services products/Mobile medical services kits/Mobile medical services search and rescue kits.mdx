# Mobile medical services search and rescue kits

**UNSPSC Code**: 42172009
**Class**: [Mobile medical services kits](Mobile medical services kits.mdx)
**Family**: [Mobile medical services products](../Mobile medical services products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a prepackaged collection (either custom or standard) of the equipment and supplies used to perform search and rescue procedures by mobile medical personnel in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Mobile medical services search and rescue kits under 21 CFR 878.4104 and under FDA product code OEV.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 300650.
