# Medical imaging dry laser printers or imagers

**UNSPSC Code**: 42203705
**Class**: [Medical imaging processing equipment and supplies](Medical imaging processing equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Medical diagnostic imaging and nuclear medicine products](../Medical diagnostic imaging and nuclear medicine products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a laser printer or imager for images created during a medical imaging procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Medical imaging dry laser printers or imagers under 21 CFR 892.2040 and under FDA product code LMC.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 844331.
