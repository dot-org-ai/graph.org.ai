# Medical neutron radiation therapy systems

**UNSPSC Code**: 42203903
**Class**: [Medical radiation detection or monitoring products](Medical radiation detection or monitoring products.mdx)
**Family**: [Medical diagnostic imaging and nuclear medicine products](../Medical diagnostic imaging and nuclear medicine products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a system consisting of products, consumables and accessories that are used to execute a medical neutron radiation therapy procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Medical neutron radiation therapy systems under 21 CFR 892.53 and under FDA product code IWL.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901819.
