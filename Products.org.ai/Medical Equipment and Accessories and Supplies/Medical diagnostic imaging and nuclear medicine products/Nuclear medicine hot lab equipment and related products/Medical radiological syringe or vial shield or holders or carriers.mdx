# Medical radiological syringe or vial shield or holders or carriers

**UNSPSC Code**: 42204101

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as shield for either a vial holding the radioisotopes or the syringe injecting those isotopes during medical radiological procedures in a medical setting.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901819.
