# Cardiac pacing lead introducers or sets

**UNSPSC Code**: 42203503
**Class**: [Cardiac pacemakers and related products](Cardiac pacemakers and related products.mdx)
**Family**: [Medical diagnostic imaging and nuclear medicine products](../Medical diagnostic imaging and nuclear medicine products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a set including several pacing leads or electrodes placed into the chamber of the heart to provide the channel for cardiac pacing from an electronic device in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Cardiac pacing lead introducers or sets under 21 CFR 870.3620 and under FDA product code DTD.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901819.
