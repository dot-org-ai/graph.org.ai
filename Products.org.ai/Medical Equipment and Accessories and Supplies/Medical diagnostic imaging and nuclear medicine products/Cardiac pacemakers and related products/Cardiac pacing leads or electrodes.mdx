# Cardiac pacing leads or electrodes

**UNSPSC Code**: 42203502
**Class**: [Cardiac pacemakers and related products](Cardiac pacemakers and related products.mdx)
**Family**: [Medical diagnostic imaging and nuclear medicine products](../Medical diagnostic imaging and nuclear medicine products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a pacing lead or electrode placed into the chamber of the heart to provide the channel for cardiac pacing from an electronic device in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Cardiac pacing leads or electrodes under 21 CFR 870.3680 and under FDA product code DTB.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901890.
