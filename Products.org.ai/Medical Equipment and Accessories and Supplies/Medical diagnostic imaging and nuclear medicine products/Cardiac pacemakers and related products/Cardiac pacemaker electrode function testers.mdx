# Cardiac pacemaker electrode function testers

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a device which is connected to an implanted pacemaker lead that supplies an accurately calibrated, variable pacing pulse for measuring the patient's pacing threshold and intracardiac R-wave potential in a medical setting.
