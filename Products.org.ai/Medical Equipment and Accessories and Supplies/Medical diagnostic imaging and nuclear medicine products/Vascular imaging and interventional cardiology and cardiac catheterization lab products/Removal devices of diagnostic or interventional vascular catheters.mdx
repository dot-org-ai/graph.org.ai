# Removal devices of diagnostic or interventional vascular catheters

**UNSPSC Code**: 42203406

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used to remove a flexible tube inserted through a vein or artery in a diagnostic or interventional procedure.  US FDA regulates Removal devices of diagnostic or interventional vascular catheters under 21 CFR 880.5970 and under FDA product code ODY.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901839.
