# Vascular imaging snares

**UNSPSC Code**: 42203420
**Class**: [Vascular imaging and interventional cardiology and cardiac catheterization lab products](Vascular imaging and interventional cardiology and cardiac catheterization lab products.mdx)
**Family**: [Medical diagnostic imaging and nuclear medicine products](../Medical diagnostic imaging and nuclear medicine products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as an endovascular device that is used to remove foreign bodies from inside arteries and veins. The snare consists of several radiopaque loops of wire inside a catheter, which when extended flower out, and which collapse when withdrawn into the catheter in a medical setting.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901819.
