# Medical x ray films or cassettes

**UNSPSC Code**: 42201810
**Class**: [Medical diagnostic x ray products](Medical diagnostic x ray products.mdx)
**Family**: [Medical diagnostic imaging and nuclear medicine products](../Medical diagnostic imaging and nuclear medicine products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is either the film used in medical x ray procedures or the protective cases for such film in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Medical x ray films or cassettes under 21 CFR 892.1640 and under FDA product code JAC.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 902214.
