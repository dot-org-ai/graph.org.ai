# Medical enteroclysis catheters or catheters kits

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a prepackaged collection (either custom or standard) of the equipment and supplies that enable medical enterclysis catheter use in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Medical enteroclysis catheters or catheters kits under 21 CFR 876.5980 and under FDA product code FGD.
