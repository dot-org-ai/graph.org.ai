# Medical arthrography sets

**UNSPSC Code**: 42201824
**Class**: [Medical diagnostic x ray products](Medical diagnostic x ray products.mdx)
**Family**: [Medical diagnostic imaging and nuclear medicine products](../Medical diagnostic imaging and nuclear medicine products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a set of materials used in a test using X-rays to obtain a series of pictures of a joint after a contrast material in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Medical arthrography sets under 21 CFR 892.1650 and under FDA product code OLL.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 902214.
