# Nuclear tomography systems

**UNSPSC Code**: 42201850
**Class**: [Medical diagnostic x ray products](Medical diagnostic x ray products.mdx)
**Family**: [Medical diagnostic imaging and nuclear medicine products](../Medical diagnostic imaging and nuclear medicine products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a system consisting of products, consumables and accessories that are used to execute a nuclear tomography procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Nuclear tomography systems under 21 CFR 892.12 and under FDA product code KPS.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901890.
