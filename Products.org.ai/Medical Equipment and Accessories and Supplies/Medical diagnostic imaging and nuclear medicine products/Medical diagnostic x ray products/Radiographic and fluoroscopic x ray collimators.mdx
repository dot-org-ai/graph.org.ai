# Radiographic and fluoroscopic x ray collimators

**UNSPSC Code**: 42201846
**Class**: [Medical diagnostic x ray products](Medical diagnostic x ray products.mdx)
**Family**: [Medical diagnostic imaging and nuclear medicine products](../Medical diagnostic imaging and nuclear medicine products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a device that narrows a beam of particles or waves in a radiographic and fluoroscopic x ray procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Radiographic and fluoroscopic x ray collimators under 21 CFR 892.1610 and under FDA product code KPW.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 902214.
