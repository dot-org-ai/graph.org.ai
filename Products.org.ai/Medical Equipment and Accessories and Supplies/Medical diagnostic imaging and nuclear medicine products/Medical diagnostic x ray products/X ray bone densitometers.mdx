# X ray bone densitometers

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a bone densitometer for medical x ray quality assurance or calibration in medical x ray procedures in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates X ray bone densitometers under 21 CFR 892.1170 and under FDA product code KGI.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 902214.
