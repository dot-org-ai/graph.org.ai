# Conductive gel, container

**UNSPSC Code**: 42201728
**Class**: [Medical ultrasound and doppler and echo imaging products](Medical ultrasound and doppler and echo imaging products.mdx)
**Family**: [Medical diagnostic imaging and nuclear medicine products](../Medical diagnostic imaging and nuclear medicine products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

Container of conductive gels that are applied to the body of the patient to reduce its impedance to help facilitate the delivery of an electric shock. This gel provides a high conductive effect for clear and accurate recording and lower electrical artifacts (errors).
