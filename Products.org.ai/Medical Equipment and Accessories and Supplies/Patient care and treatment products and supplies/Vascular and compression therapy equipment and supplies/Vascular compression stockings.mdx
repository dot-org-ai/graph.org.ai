# Vascular compression stockings

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142809
**Class**: [Vascular and compression therapy equipment and supplies](Vascular and compression therapy equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a stocking (up to the calf or thigh) that apply compression to a patient's body part. These are also known as T.E.D stockings ( thromboembolism-deterrent ,T.E.D Compression stockings) they are manufactured and worn to provide support to the lymphatic drainage and veins of the lower extremities in the body. This product can be used by itself or in conjunction with vascular compression sleeves and pumps.
