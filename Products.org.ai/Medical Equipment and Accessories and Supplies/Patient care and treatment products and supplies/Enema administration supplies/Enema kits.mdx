# Enema kits

**UNSPSC Code**: 42141903
**Class**: [Enema administration supplies](Enema administration supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that allows for fluid to be injected into the lower bowel by way of the rectum for medical purposes.  US FDA regulates Enema kits under 21 CFR 876.5210 and under FDA product code FCD.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 392690.
