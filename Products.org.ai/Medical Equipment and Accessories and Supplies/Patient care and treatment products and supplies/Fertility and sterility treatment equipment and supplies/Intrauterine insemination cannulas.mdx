# Intrauterine insemination cannulas

**UNSPSC Code**: 42143208
**Class**: [Fertility and sterility treatment equipment and supplies](Fertility and sterility treatment equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a thin tube inserted into a vein or body cavity to administer medicine, drain off fluid, or insert a surgical instrument associated with a procedure.  in this case, it is used as part of a procedure in which a fine catheter (tube) is inserted through the cervix (the natural opening of the uterus) into the uterus (the womb) to deposit a sperm sample directly into the uterus.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901831.
