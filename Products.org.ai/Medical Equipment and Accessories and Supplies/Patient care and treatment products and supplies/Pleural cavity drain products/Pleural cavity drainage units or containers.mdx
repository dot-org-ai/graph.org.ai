# Pleural cavity drainage units or containers

**UNSPSC Code**: 42144101
**Class**: [Pleural cavity drain products](Pleural cavity drain products.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a device used for pleural cavity drainage or the containment of the drained substances in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates pleural cavity drainage units or containers or accessories under 21 CFR 880.6740 and under FDA product code PAD.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule pleural cavity drainage units or containers under HS 901890.
