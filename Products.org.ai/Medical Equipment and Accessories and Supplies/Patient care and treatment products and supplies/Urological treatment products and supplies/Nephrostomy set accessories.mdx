# Nephrostomy set accessories

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142729
**Class**: [Urological treatment products and supplies](Urological treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that contains the instruments and accessories to perform a nephrostomy (an artificial opening created between the kidney and the skin which allows for the urinary diversion directly from the upper part of the urinary system).  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901811.
