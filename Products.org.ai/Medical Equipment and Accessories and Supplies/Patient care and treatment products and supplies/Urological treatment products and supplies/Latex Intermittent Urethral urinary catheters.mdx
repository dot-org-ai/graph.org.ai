# Latex Intermittent Urethral urinary catheters

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142702
**Class**: [Urological treatment products and supplies](Urological treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a Latex product consisting of a flexible tube inserted through a narrow opening into the uterus this is used for a short period of time to empty the bladder.  US FDA regulates Urethral urinary catheters under 21 CFR 876.5130 and under FDA product code GBL.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901839.
