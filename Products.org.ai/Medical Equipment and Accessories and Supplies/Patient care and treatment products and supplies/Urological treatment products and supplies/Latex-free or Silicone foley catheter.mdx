# Latex-free or Silicone foley catheter

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142744
**Class**: [Urological treatment products and supplies](Urological treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a Latex-free or silicone product consisting of a flexible tube inserted through a narrow opening into the uterus.  The device drains your urine into a drainage bag. A small balloon filled with sterile water is placed inside your bladder to hold the catheter in place. This catheter is designed to stay in place for days or weeks.
