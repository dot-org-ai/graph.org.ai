# Urinary drainage bag straps or fasteners

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142705

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that consists of the straps or fasteners associated with a bag or meter utilized to aid drainage of the urinary tract in a person.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901890.
