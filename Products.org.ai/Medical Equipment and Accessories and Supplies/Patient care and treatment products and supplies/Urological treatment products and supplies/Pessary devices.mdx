# Pessary devices

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142716
**Class**: [Urological treatment products and supplies](Urological treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that consists of a medical device similar to the outer ring of a diaphragm used to support the uterus, vagina, bladder, or rectum.  US FDA regulates Pessary devices under 21 CFR 884.3570 and under FDA product code HHW.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901811.
