# Latex intermittent urinary catheterization kits

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142715
**Class**: [Urological treatment products and supplies](Urological treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that consists of an intermittent Latex catheter inserted into the urinary tract. US FDA regulates Urinary catheterization kits under 21 CFR 876.5130 and under FDA product code EYC.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901839.
