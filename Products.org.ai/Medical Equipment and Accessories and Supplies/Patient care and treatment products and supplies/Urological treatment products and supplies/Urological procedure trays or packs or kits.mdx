# Urological procedure trays or packs or kits

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142706
**Class**: [Urological treatment products and supplies](Urological treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that includes tray, packs or kits that are configured with instruments and accessories used in urological procedures.  US FDA regulates Urological procedure trays or packs or kits under 21 CFR 864.3250 and under FDA product code EXG.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 392410.
