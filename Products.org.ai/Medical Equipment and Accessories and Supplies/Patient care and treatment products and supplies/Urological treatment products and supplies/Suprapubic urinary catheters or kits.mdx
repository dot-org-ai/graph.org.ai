# Suprapubic urinary catheters or kits

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142701
**Class**: [Urological treatment products and supplies](Urological treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product consisting of a flexible tube inserted through a narrow opening into the pubic bone.  US FDA regulates suprapubic urinary catheters or kits under 21 CFR 876.5090 and under FDA product code KOB.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901839.
