# Nephrostomy catheters or drains

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142717
**Class**: [Urological treatment products and supplies](Urological treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product consisting of a flexible tube inserted through a narrow opening between the skin and kidney.  US FDA regulates Nephrostomy catheters or drains under 21 CFR 878.42 and under FDA product code GBO.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901839.
