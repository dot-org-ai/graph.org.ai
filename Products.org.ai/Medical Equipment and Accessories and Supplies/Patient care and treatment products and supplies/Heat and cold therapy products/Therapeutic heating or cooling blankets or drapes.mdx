# Therapeutic heating or cooling blankets or drapes

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142106
**Class**: [Heat and cold therapy products](Heat and cold therapy products.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates the blankets that are used to provide either heat or cold through the covering of a human body part in a medical setting.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 630130.
