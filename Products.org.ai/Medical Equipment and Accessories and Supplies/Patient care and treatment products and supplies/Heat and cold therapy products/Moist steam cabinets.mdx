# Moist steam cabinets

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142121
**Class**: [Heat and cold therapy products](Heat and cold therapy products.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product intended for medical purposes that delivers a flow of heated, moisturized air to a patient in an enclosed  unit.  US FDA  regulates moist steam cabinets under 21 CFR 890.5250 and under FDA product code IMB.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 940320.
