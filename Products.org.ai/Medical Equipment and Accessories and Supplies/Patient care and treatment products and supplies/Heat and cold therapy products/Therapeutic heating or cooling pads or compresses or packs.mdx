# Therapeutic heating or cooling pads or compresses or packs

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142108
**Class**: [Heat and cold therapy products](Heat and cold therapy products.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates the pads, compresses or packs that are used to provide either heat or cold through the covering of a human body part in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Therapeutic heating or cooling pads or compresses or packs under 21 CFR 890.5740 and under FDA product code IRT.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 630493.
