# Therapeutic hot or cold water bottles

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142110
**Class**: [Heat and cold therapy products](Heat and cold therapy products.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that consists of a bottle used to deliver either heat or cold in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Therapeutic hot or cold water bottles under 21 CFR 880.6085 and under FDA product code FPF.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 701090.
