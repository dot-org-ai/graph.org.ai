# Medical hydrocollators

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142104
**Class**: [Heat and cold therapy products](Heat and cold therapy products.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a device that consists of thermostatically controlled water bath for placing bentonite-filled cloth heating pads. When the pads are removed from the bath, they are placed in covers and placed on the patient.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 902119.
