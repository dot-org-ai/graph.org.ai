# Syringe accessories

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142615
**Class**: [Syringes and accessories](Syringes and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that provides accessories for a device consisting of a tube with a nozzle and piston or bulb for sucking in and ejecting liquid in a thin stream in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Syringe accessories under 21 CFR 872.6770 and under FDA product code EJI.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901831.
