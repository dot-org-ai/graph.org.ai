# Prefilled flush syringes

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142619
**Class**: [Syringes and accessories](Syringes and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that provides accessories for a device consisting of a tube with a nozzle and piston or bulb for pre filled with a saline solution used for ejecting liquid in a thin stream in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Prefilled flush syringes under 21 CFR 880.52 and under FDA product code FOZ.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901831.
