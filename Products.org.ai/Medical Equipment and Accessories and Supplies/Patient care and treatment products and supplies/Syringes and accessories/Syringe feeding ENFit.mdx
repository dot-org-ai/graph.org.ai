# Syringe feeding ENFit

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142628
**Class**: [Syringes and accessories](Syringes and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

ENFit is a global change to make all enteral (tube feeding) devices specific to tube feeding. Every extension set, syringe, long tube/PEG, and NG-tube will be designed with a specific ENFit end so that you can only use products designed for enteral/tube feeding access.
