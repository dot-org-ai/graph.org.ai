# Medical syringes without needle

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142608
**Class**: [Syringes and accessories](Syringes and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that consists of a tube with a nozzle and a bulb for sucking in and ejecting liquid in a thin stream lacking a needle for use in a general medical procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Medical syringes without needle under 21 CFR 870.1650 and under FDA product code DQF.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901831.
