# Medical bulb syringes

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142602
**Class**: [Syringes and accessories](Syringes and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that consists of a tube with a nozzle and a bulb for sucking in and ejecting liquid in a thin stream used in a procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Medical bulb syringes under 21 CFR 880.6960 and under FDA product code KYZ.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901831.
