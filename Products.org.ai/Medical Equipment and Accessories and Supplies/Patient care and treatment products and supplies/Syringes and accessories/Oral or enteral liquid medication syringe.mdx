# Oral or enteral liquid medication syringe

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142610
**Class**: [Syringes and accessories](Syringes and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that consists of a tube with a nozzle and a bulb for sucking in and ejecting liquid in a thin stream used in an oral medication induction based procedure in a medical setting.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901831.
