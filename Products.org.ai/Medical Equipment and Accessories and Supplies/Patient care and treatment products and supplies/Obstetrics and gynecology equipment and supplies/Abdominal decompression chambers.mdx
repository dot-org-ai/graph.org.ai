# Abdominal decompression chambers

**UNSPSC Code**: 42143108
**Class**: [Obstetrics and gynecology equipment and supplies](Obstetrics and gynecology equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a hoodlike device used to reduce pressure on the pregnant patient's abdomen for the relief of abdominal pain during pregnancy or labor.  US FDA regulates Abdominal decompression chambers under 21 CFR 884.5225 and under FDA product code HEH.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901890.
