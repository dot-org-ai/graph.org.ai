# Perinometers

**UNSPSC Code**: 42143107
**Class**: [Obstetrics and gynecology equipment and supplies](Obstetrics and gynecology equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is used for measuring the strength of voluntary contractions of the pelvic floor muscles of a woman.  US FDA regulates Perinometers under 21 CFR 884.1425 and under FDA product code HIR.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901890.
