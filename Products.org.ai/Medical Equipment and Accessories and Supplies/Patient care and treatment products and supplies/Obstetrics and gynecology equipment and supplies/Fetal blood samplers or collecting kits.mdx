# Fetal blood samplers or collecting kits

**UNSPSC Code**: 42143111
**Class**: [Obstetrics and gynecology equipment and supplies](Obstetrics and gynecology equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is prepackaged collections (either custom or standard) of the equipment and supplies used to perform a collection and sampling fetal blood directly from the umbilical cord or fetus.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901890.
