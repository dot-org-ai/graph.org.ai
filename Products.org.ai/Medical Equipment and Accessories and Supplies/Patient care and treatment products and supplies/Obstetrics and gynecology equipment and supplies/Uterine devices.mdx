# Uterine devices

**UNSPSC Code**: 42143102

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a device inserted into the uterus to prevent pregnancy.  US FDA regulates Uterine devices under 21 CFR 884.4530 and under FDA product code HCY.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901811.
