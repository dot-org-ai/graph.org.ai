# Skin preparation wipes

**UNSPSC Code**: 42141503
**Class**: [Applicator swabs and cotton balls](Applicator swabs and cotton balls.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that provides a pre formed wipe that can contain various treatments that are used to clean and prepare skin in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Skin preparation wipes under 21 CFR 880.5090 and under FDA product code NEC.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 560369.
