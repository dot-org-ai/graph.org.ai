# Fiber tipped sticks

**UNSPSC Code**: 42141502
**Class**: [Applicator swabs and cotton balls](Applicator swabs and cotton balls.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a stick covered with soft, absorbent cotton or fiber, often used to remove cosmetics or to cushion fragile objects.  US FDA regulates Fiber tipped sticks under 21 CFR 880.6025 and under FDA product code KXF.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901890.
