# Chemotherapy administration sets or kits

**UNSPSC Code**: 42143301
**Class**: [Chemotherapy equipment and supplies](Chemotherapy equipment and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a prepackaged collection (either custom or standard) of the equipment and supplies used to perform chemotherapy administration procedures in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Chemotherapy administration sets or kits under 21 CFR 880.5540 and under FDA product code FPA.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901890.
