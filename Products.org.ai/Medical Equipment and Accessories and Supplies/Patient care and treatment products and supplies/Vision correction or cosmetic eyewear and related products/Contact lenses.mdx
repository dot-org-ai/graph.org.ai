# Contact lenses

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142913
**Class**: [Vision correction or cosmetic eyewear and related products](Vision correction or cosmetic eyewear and related products.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that uses a thin plastic lens placed directly on the surface of the eye to correct visual defects in a human.  US FDA regulates Contact lenses under 21 CFR 886.5916 and under FDA product code MUW.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 900130.
