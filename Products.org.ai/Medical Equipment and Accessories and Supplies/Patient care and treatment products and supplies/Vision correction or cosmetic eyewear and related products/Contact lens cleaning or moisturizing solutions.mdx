# Contact lens cleaning or moisturizing solutions

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142914
**Class**: [Vision correction or cosmetic eyewear and related products](Vision correction or cosmetic eyewear and related products.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates the cleaning a moisturizing solutions used for a product that uses a thin plastic lens placed directly on the surface of the eye to correct visual defects in a human.  US FDA regulates Contact lens cleaning or moisterizing solutions under 21 CFR 886.5918 and under FDA product code MRC.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 300339.
