# Anesthesia nerve block kit or tray

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142535
**Class**: [Injection and aspiration needles and accessories](Injection and aspiration needles and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that consists of a tray with components and accessories or a hollow needle used in a nerve block anesthesia procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Anesthesia nerve block trays or needles under 21 CFR 868.5120 and under FDA product code BSP.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901832.
