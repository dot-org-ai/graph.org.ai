# Intraosseous needles

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142534
**Class**: [Injection and aspiration needles and accessories](Injection and aspiration needles and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a hollow needle commonly used for rapid delivery or evacuation of liquids in intraosseous procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Intraosseous needles under 21 CFR 880. 557 and under FDA product code MHC.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901832.
