# Diagnostic procedure needles

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142530
**Class**: [Injection and aspiration needles and accessories](Injection and aspiration needles and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a hollow needle commonly used for rapid delivery or evacuation of liquids in diagnostic procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Diagnostic procedure needles under 21 CFR 880.5440 and under FDA product code FPA.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901832.
