# Needle caps or protection devices

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142511
**Class**: [Injection and aspiration needles and accessories](Injection and aspiration needles and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a cap or protective device or accessories focused on needles commonly used for rapid delivery or evacuation of liquids in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Needle caps or protection devices or accessories under 21 CFR 880.5440 and under FDA product code FMI.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901839.
