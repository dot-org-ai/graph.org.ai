# Amniocentesis needles

**UNSPSC Code**: 42142501
**Class**: [Injection and aspiration needles and accessories](Injection and aspiration needles and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a hollow needle commonly used for rapid delivery or evacuation of liquids in an amniocentesis procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Amniocentesis needles under 21 CFR 884.1550 and under FDA product code HIO.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901832.
