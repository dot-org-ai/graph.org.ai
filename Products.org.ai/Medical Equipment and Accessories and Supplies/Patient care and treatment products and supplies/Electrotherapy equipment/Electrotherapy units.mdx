# Electrotherapy units

**UNSPSC Code**: 42141825
**Class**: [Electrotherapy equipment](Electrotherapy equipment.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

A medical device that helps generate and deliver small pulses of electrical current to the user's skin through adhesive electrode pads.  The device can be be used for temporary relief of pain associated with sore and aching muscles in the shoulders, waist, neck, back, arms, legs, hands and soles of feet due to strain from exercise or normal household and work activities.
