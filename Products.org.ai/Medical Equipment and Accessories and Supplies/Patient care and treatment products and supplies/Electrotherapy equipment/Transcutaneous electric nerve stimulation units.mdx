# Transcutaneous electric nerve stimulation units

**UNSPSC Code**: 42141807
**Class**: [Electrotherapy equipment](Electrotherapy equipment.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that contains either a unit or a unit and accessories that provides low level electricity to the nerves in order to relieve pain.  US FDA regulates Transcutaneous electric nerve stimulation units under 21 CFR 882.5890 and under FDA product code GZJ.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901839.
