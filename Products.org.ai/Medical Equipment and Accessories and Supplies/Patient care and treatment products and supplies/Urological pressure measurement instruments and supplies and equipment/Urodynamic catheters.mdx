# Urodynamic catheters

**UNSPSC Code**: 42143803
**Class**: [Urological pressure measurement instruments and supplies and equipment](Urological pressure measurement instruments and supplies and equipment.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient care and treatment products and supplies](../Patient care and treatment products and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a flexible tube inserted through a narrow opening into a body cavity in an artificial insemination procedure.  US FDA regulates Urodynamic catheters under 21 CFR 876.1620 and under FDA product code FEN.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901839.
