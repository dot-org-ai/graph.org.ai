# Nearpoint rulers

**UNSPSC Code**: 42183052
**Class**: [Ophthalmic diagnostic exam products](Ophthalmic diagnostic exam products.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient exam and monitoring products](../Patient exam and monitoring products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a device calibrated in centimeters intended to measure the nearpoint of convergence (the point to which the visual lines are directed when convergence is at its maximum) in a medical setting.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901811.
