# Ophthalmic tonometers

**UNSPSC Code**: 42183018
**Class**: [Ophthalmic diagnostic exam products](Ophthalmic diagnostic exam products.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient exam and monitoring products](../Patient exam and monitoring products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as an instrument that performs an intraocular pressure test inside the eye in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Ophthalmic tonometers under 21 CFR 886.1930 and under FDA product code NJJ.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901811.
