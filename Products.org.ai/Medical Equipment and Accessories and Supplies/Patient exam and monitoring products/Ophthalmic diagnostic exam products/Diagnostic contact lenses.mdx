# Diagnostic contact lenses

**UNSPSC Code**: 42183065
**Class**: [Ophthalmic diagnostic exam products](Ophthalmic diagnostic exam products.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient exam and monitoring products](../Patient exam and monitoring products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a device that is a self-adaptable diagnostic lens for the purpose of viewing the interior of a patient's eye in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Diagnostic contact lenses under 21 CFR 886.1385 and under FDA product code NYK.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 900130.
