# Pseudoisochromatic plate sets

**UNSPSC Code**: 42183031
**Class**: [Ophthalmic diagnostic exam products](Ophthalmic diagnostic exam products.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient exam and monitoring products](../Patient exam and monitoring products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as one of a set of colored plates that include some which appear isochromatic to individuals with color-vision abnormality and that are widely used as a test for color blindness in a medical setting.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901811.
