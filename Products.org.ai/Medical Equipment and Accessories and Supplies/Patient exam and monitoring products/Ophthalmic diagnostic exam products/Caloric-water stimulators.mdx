# Caloric-water stimulators

**UNSPSC Code**: 42183070
**Class**: [Ophthalmic diagnostic exam products](Ophthalmic diagnostic exam products.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient exam and monitoring products](../Patient exam and monitoring products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a caloric testing apparatus for introducing water, under direct visual control, into the outer ear canal of a human subject for purposes of testing inner ear balance mechanisms in a medical setting.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901811.
