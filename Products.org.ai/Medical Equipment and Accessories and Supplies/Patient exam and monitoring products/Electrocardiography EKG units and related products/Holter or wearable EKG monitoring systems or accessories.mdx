# Holter or wearable EKG monitoring systems or accessories

**UNSPSC Code**: 42181713
**Class**: [Electrocardiography EKG units and related products](Electrocardiography EKG units and related products.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient exam and monitoring products](../Patient exam and monitoring products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates an external or wearable product that can record and measure electronic waves generated from the heart of a human for protracted periods of time.  US FDA regulates continuous electrocardiography EKG or holter monitoring systems under 21 CFR 870.28 and under FDA product code MXC. Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 852849.
