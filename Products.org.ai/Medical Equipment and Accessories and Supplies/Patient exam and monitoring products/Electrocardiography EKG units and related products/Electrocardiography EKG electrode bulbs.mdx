# Electrocardiography EKG electrode bulbs

**UNSPSC Code**: 42181717
**Class**: [Electrocardiography EKG units and related products](Electrocardiography EKG units and related products.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient exam and monitoring products](../Patient exam and monitoring products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates the peripheral calipers or rulers for a product that can record and measure electronic waves generated from the heart of a human.  US FDA regulates Electrocardiography EKG electrode bulbs under 21 CFR 870.2360 and under FDA product code DRX.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901890.
