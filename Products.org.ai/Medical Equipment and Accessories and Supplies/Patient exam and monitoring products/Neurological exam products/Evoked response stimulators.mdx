# Evoked response stimulators

**UNSPSC Code**: 42182321

**Family**: [Patient exam and monitoring products](../Patient exam and monitoring products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a device used to deliver an electrical, visual, or auditory stimulus and record the evoked response in the patient via electrodes attached to the patient's skin in a medical setting.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901811.
