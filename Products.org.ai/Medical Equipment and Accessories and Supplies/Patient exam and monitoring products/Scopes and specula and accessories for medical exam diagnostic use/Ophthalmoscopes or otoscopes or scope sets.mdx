# Ophthalmoscopes or otoscopes or scope sets

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a is used to inspect the fundus of the eye or its associated consumables in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Ophthalmoscopes or otoscopes or scope sets under 21 CFR 876.15 8 and under FDA product code KYH.
