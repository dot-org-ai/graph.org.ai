# Otoscope speculums

**UNSPSC Code**: 42182015
**Class**: [Scopes and specula and accessories for medical exam diagnostic use](Scopes and specula and accessories for medical exam diagnostic use.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient exam and monitoring products](../Patient exam and monitoring products.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is a tool designed be used in association with an Otoscope to inspect the orifices of humans.  US FDA regulates Otoscope speculums under 21 CFR 886.4350 and under FDA product code HNC.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 300640.
