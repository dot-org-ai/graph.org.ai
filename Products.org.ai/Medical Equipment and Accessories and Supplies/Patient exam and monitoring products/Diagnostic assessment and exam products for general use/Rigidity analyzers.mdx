# Rigidity analyzers

**UNSPSC Code**: 42181524
**Class**: [Diagnostic assessment and exam products for general use](Diagnostic assessment and exam products for general use.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient exam and monitoring products](../Patient exam and monitoring products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a device for quantifying the extent of the rigidity of a patient's limb to determine the effectiveness of drugs or other treatments in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Rigidity analyzers under 21 CFR 882.1020 and under FDA product code GZM.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 903020.
