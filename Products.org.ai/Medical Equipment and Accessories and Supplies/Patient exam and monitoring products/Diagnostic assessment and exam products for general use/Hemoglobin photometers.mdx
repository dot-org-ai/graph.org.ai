# Hemoglobin photometers

**UNSPSC Code**: 42181514
**Class**: [Diagnostic assessment and exam products for general use](Diagnostic assessment and exam products for general use.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient exam and monitoring products](../Patient exam and monitoring products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a photometer to measure the amount of hemoglobin in a specimen in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Hemoglobin photometers under 21 CFR 864.5620 and under FDA product code PHP.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901890.
