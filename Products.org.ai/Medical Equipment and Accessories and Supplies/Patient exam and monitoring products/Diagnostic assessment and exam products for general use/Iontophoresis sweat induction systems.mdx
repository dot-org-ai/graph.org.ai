# Iontophoresis sweat induction systems

**UNSPSC Code**: 42181530
**Class**: [Diagnostic assessment and exam products for general use](Diagnostic assessment and exam products for general use.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient exam and monitoring products](../Patient exam and monitoring products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a system consisting of products, consumables and accessories that are used to execute an iontophoresis sweat induction procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Iontophoresis sweat induction systems under 21 CFR 890.5525 and under FDA product code EGJ.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901849.
