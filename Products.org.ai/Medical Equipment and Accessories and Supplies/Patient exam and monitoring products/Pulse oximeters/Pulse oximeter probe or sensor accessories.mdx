# Pulse oximeter probe or sensor accessories

**UNSPSC Code**: 42181804
**Class**: [Pulse oximeters](Pulse oximeters.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient exam and monitoring products](../Patient exam and monitoring products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates the probe or sensor accessories used in association with a product that records the level of oxygen saturation in a human.  US FDA regulates Pulse oximeter probe or sensor accessories under 21 CFR 864.75 and under FDA product code DPZ.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901819.
