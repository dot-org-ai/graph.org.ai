# Pulse oximeter probes or sensors

**UNSPSC Code**: 42181803
**Class**: [Pulse oximeters](Pulse oximeters.mdx)
**Family**: [Patient exam and monitoring products](../Patient exam and monitoring products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates the probes or sensors used in association with a product that records the level of oxygen saturation in a human.  US FDA regulates Pulse oximeter probes or sensors under 21 CFR 864.75 and under FDA product code DPZ.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901819.
