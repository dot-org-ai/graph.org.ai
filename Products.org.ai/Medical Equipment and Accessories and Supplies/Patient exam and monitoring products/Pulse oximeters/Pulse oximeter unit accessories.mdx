# Pulse oximeter unit accessories

**UNSPSC Code**: 42181805

In this classification, this commodity indicates the accessories for a product that records the level of oxygen saturation in a human.  US FDA regulates Pulse oximeter unit accessories under 21 CFR 864.75 and under FDA product code DPZ.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901819.
