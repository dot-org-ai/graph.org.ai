# Infrared/non-contact thermometer

This product takes a patient's temperature in both a home or check-in in a medical setting.  They are used to check to see if a patient has a fever without touching them. These thermometers are not a precise at other medical thermometers so are not used in diagnostic procedures. This product infers the temperature of a person without touching them.
