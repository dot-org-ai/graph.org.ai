# Lower extremity prosthetic devices

**UNSPSC Code**: 42242001
**Class**: [Prosthetic devices or accessories and supplies](Prosthetic devices or accessories and supplies.mdx)
**Family**: [Orthopedic and prosthetic and sports medicine products](../Orthopedic and prosthetic and sports medicine products.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a lower extremity device in a prosthetic procedure in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Lower extremity prosthetic devices under 21 CFR 888.3350 and under FDA product code JDI.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 901890.
