# Isolation and treatment unit

**UNSPSC Code**: 42192434
**Class**: [Medical equipment transport and transfer and storage products](Medical equipment transport and transfer and storage products.mdx)
**Family**: [Medical facility products](../Medical facility products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

Isolation treatment Unit is Stretcher/size, a small Chamber movable device which is equipped with its own negative pressure filtration system for isolating and transporting of infectious patients to the emergency center. It can prevent the proliferation of pathogens during the transport of the patient and reduce the infection rate of medical staff.
