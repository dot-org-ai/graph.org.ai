# Wheelchair lifts

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a device to assist people in placing wheelchairs into and out of their cars or other areas.  US FDA regulates Wheelchair lifts under 21 CFR 890.3920 and under FDA product code IMN.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 842860.
