# Wheelchairs

**UNSPSC Code**: 42192210
**Class**: [Patient transport products](Patient transport products.mdx)
**Family**: [Medical facility products](../Medical facility products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product used as a chair fitted with wheels for use as a means of transport by a person who is unable to walk as a result of illness, injury, or disability in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Wheelchairs under 21 CFR 890.3850 and under FDA product code IMK.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 871310.
