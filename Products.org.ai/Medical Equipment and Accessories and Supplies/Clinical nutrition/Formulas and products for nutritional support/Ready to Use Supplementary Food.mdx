# Ready to Use Supplementary Food

**UNSPSC Code**: 42231816
**Class**: [Formulas and products for nutritional support](Formulas and products for nutritional support.mdx)
**Family**: [Clinical nutrition](../Clinical nutrition.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

Ready-to-Use Supplementary Foods (RUSF) are highly fortified, nutrient dense pastes specifically designed for prevention and treatment of moderate acute malnutrition among children over 6 months. Generally made with heat treated oil seeds/pulses/cereals, sugar, milk powder, vegetable oils, vitamins and minerals
