# Dry heat or hot air sterilizers

**UNSPSC Code**: 42281502
**Class**: [Autoclave and sterilizer equipment and accessories](Autoclave and sterilizer equipment and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Medical cleaning and sterilization products](../Medical cleaning and sterilization products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is machine, used to destroy microorganisms in or on, usually by bringing to a high temperature by the use, dry heat, or hot air, in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Dry heat or hot air sterilizers under 21 CFR 880.6870 and under FDA product code KCT.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 841920.
