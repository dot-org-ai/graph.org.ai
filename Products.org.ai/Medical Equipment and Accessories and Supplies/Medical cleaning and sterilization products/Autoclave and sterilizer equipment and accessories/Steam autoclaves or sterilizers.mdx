# Steam autoclaves or sterilizers

**UNSPSC Code**: 42281508
**Class**: [Autoclave and sterilizer equipment and accessories](Autoclave and sterilizer equipment and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Medical cleaning and sterilization products](../Medical cleaning and sterilization products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is steam based, used to destroy microorganisms in or on, usually by bringing to a high temperature with steam, dry heat, or boiling liquid, in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Steam autoclaves or sterilizers under 21 CFR 880.6880 and under FDA product code FLE.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 841920.
