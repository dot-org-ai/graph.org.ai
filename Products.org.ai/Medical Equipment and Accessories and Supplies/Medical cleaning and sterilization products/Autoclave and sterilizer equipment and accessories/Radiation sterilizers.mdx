# Radiation sterilizers

**UNSPSC Code**: 42281507

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is radiation based, used to destroy microorganisms in or on, usually by bringing to a high temperature with steam, dry heat, or boiling liquid, in a medical setting.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 841920.
