# Chemical or gas sterilizers

**UNSPSC Code**: 42281501
**Class**: [Autoclave and sterilizer equipment and accessories](Autoclave and sterilizer equipment and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Medical cleaning and sterilization products](../Medical cleaning and sterilization products.mdx)
**Segment**: [Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies](../../Medical Equipment and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

In this classification, this commodity indicates a product that is machine, used to destroy microorganisms in or on, usually by the use of chemicals or gas, in a medical setting.  US FDA regulates Chemical or gas sterilizers under 21 CFR 88.686 and under FDA product code ECC.  Most nations, for tariff and trade purposes, schedule these products under HS 841920.
