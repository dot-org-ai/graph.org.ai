# Parabolic fixture

**UNSPSC Code**: 39111535
**Class**: [Interior lighting fixtures and accessories](Interior lighting fixtures and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Lighting Fixtures and Accessories](../Lighting Fixtures and Accessories.mdx)
**Segment**: [Electrical Systems and Lighting and Components and Accessories and Supplies](../../Electrical Systems and Lighting and Components and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

Parabolic light fixtures are open cell lights recessed into the ceiling. They offer a sharp cut-off level, meaning that light can be focused directly downward more effectively than with older prismatic fixtures.
