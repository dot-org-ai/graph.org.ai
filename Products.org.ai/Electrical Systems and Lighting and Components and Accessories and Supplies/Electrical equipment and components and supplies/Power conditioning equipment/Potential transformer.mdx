# Potential transformer

**UNSPSC Code**: 39121033
**Class**: [Power conditioning equipment](Power conditioning equipment.mdx)
**Family**: [Electrical equipment and components and supplies](../Electrical equipment and components and supplies.mdx)
**Segment**: [Electrical Systems and Lighting and Components and Accessories and Supplies](../../Electrical Systems and Lighting and Components and Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

A potential transformer (PT) is a type of instrument transformer designed to provide a voltage in its secondary winding proportional to the voltage in its primary. They are commonly used in metering and protective relaying in the electrical power industry where they facilitate the safe measurement of voltages
