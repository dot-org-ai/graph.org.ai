# Automatic guided vehicles AGV

**UNSPSC Code**: 24102300
**Family**: [Material handling machinery and equipment](Material handling machinery and equipment.mdx)
**Segment**: [Material Handling and Conditioning and Storage Machinery and their Accessories and Supplies](../Material Handling and Conditioning and Storage Machinery and their Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

An Automatic Guided Vehicle, or AGV consists of a computer controlled wheel based load carrier (normally battery powered) that runs on the plant floor (or if outdoors on a paved area) without the need for an onboard operator or driver. AGVs have defined paths or areas within which or over which they can navigate. Navigation is achieved by any one of several means, including following a path defined by buried inductive wires, surface mounted magnetic or optical strips.
