# Windlass

**UNSPSC Code**: 24101639
**Class**: [Lifting equipment and accessories](Lifting equipment and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Material handling machinery and equipment](../Material handling machinery and equipment.mdx)
**Segment**: [Material Handling and Conditioning and Storage Machinery and their Accessories and Supplies](../../Material Handling and Conditioning and Storage Machinery and their Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

An apparatus for moving a heavy weight. It consists of a horizontal cylinder (barrel), which is rotated by the turn of a crank or belt. A winch is affixed to one or both ends, and a cable or rope is wound around the winch, pulling a weight attached to the opposite end.
