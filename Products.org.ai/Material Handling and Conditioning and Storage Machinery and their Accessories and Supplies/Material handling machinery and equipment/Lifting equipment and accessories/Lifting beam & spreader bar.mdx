# Lifting beam & spreader bar

**UNSPSC Code**: 24101668
**Class**: [Lifting equipment and accessories](Lifting equipment and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Material handling machinery and equipment](../Material handling machinery and equipment.mdx)
**Segment**: [Material Handling and Conditioning and Storage Machinery and their Accessories and Supplies](../../Material Handling and Conditioning and Storage Machinery and their Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

A Lifting Beams or Spreader Beams and Frames are intended to balance and support a load by providing multiple lift points. Top and bottom flange stiffeners are added to strengthen the Beam laterally in case of sideward movement caused by shifting of the load. A spreader is a device used for lifting containers and unitized cargo. The spreader is placed between the container and the lifting machine.
