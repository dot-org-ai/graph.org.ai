# Overhead track conveyor

**UNSPSC Code**: 24101750
**Class**: [Conveyors and accessories](Conveyors and accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Material handling machinery and equipment](../Material handling machinery and equipment.mdx)
**Segment**: [Material Handling and Conditioning and Storage Machinery and their Accessories and Supplies](../../Material Handling and Conditioning and Storage Machinery and their Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

A type of conveyor that consists of a continuous loop of chain or cable that is suspended from a track. Carriers or trolleys are attached periodically to hang or contain products. There are several different types of overhead trolleys. Manual trolleys or push systems are used mainly in low-volume applications. Motorized overhead trolleys have wheels that run on a rail and are driven by a motor.
