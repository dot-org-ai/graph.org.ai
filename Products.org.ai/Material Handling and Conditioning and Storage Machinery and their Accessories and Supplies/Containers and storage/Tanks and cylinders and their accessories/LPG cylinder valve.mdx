# LPG cylinder valve

**UNSPSC Code**: 24111835
**Class**: [Tanks and cylinders and their accessories](Tanks and cylinders and their accessories.mdx)
**Family**: [Containers and storage](../Containers and storage.mdx)
**Segment**: [Material Handling and Conditioning and Storage Machinery and their Accessories and Supplies](../../Material Handling and Conditioning and Storage Machinery and their Accessories and Supplies.mdx)

A LPG cylinder valve is fitted the open ends of the cylinder. The valves close the open part of the cylinder. A way to allow the cylinder to be refilled also a port for the gas outlet. Valves can incorporate an integrated pressure regulator. They are designed in different shapes due to the different properties of the various gases.
