# Downhole shock absorber parts and accessories

**UNSPSC Code**: 20121521
**Class**: [Conventional drilling tools](Conventional drilling tools.mdx)
**Family**: [Oil and gas drilling and exploration equipment](../Oil and gas drilling and exploration equipment.mdx)
**Segment**: [Mining and Well Drilling Machinery and Accessories](../../Mining and Well Drilling Machinery and Accessories.mdx)

Parts and accessories for an apparatus that absorbs shock downhole in a wellbore. The kinetic energy from a moving tool is absorbed to dissipate such energy, to decelerate the moving tool, or to prevent impact damage from contact between the tool and other downhole components.
