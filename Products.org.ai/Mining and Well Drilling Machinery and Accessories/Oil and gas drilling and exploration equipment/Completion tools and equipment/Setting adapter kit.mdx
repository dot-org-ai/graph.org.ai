# Setting adapter kit

Assortment of components used in constructing the setting adapter which is part of the setting tool also known as a running tool. This is a tool or device that is used in the placement or setting of downhole equipment such as permanent packers or plugs. The running tool can be retrieved after the operation or setting process.
