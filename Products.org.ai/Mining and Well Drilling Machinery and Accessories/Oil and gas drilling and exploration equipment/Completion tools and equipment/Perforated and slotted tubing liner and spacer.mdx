# Perforated and slotted tubing liner and spacer

**UNSPSC Code**: 20121454

Liner is a casing string that does not extend back to the wellhead, but is hung from another casing string. Liners are used instead of full casing strings to: Reduce cost. Improve hydraulic performance when drilling deeper. Allow the use of larger tubing above the liner top.
