# Beam pumping unit

**UNSPSC Code**: 20141019
**Class**: [Wellhead equipment](Wellhead equipment.mdx)
**Family**: [Oil and gas operating and production equipment](../Oil and gas operating and production equipment.mdx)
**Segment**: [Mining and Well Drilling Machinery and Accessories](../../Mining and Well Drilling Machinery and Accessories.mdx)

A beam pumping unit is a machine for translating rotary motion from a crankshaft to a linear reciprocating motion for the purpose of transferring mechanical power to a down-hole pump. The purpose, simply stated, of the basic system is to transmit energy from the surface to the downhole pump.
