# Conventional wellhead

**UNSPSC Code**: 20141025
**Class**: [Wellhead equipment](Wellhead equipment.mdx)
**Family**: [Oil and gas operating and production equipment](../Oil and gas operating and production equipment.mdx)
**Segment**: [Mining and Well Drilling Machinery and Accessories](../../Mining and Well Drilling Machinery and Accessories.mdx)

The primary purpose of a wellhead is to provide the suspension point and pressure seals for the casing strings that run from the bottom of the hole sections to the surface pressure control equipment. While drilling the oil well, surface pressure control is provided by a blowout preventer (BOP).
