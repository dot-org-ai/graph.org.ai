# Muscular strength measurement instrument

**UNSPSC Code**: 49211817
**Class**: [Physical education classroom equipment](Physical education classroom equipment.mdx)
**Family**: [Other sports](../Other sports.mdx)
**Segment**: [Sports and Recreational Equipment and Supplies and Accessories](../../Sports and Recreational Equipment and Supplies and Accessories.mdx)

A type of device that is designed to find out the muscular strength by measuring the leg force and brachial muscular strength. It measures the estimated maximum muscular strength with the average power by the speed training and it is possible to measure the muscular endurance force to fit to the age of each gender for tested person.
