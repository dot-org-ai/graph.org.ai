# Insecticide-treated bed net

**UNSPSC Code**: 49121515

Bed nets form a protective barrier around people sleeping under them. However, bed nets treated with an insecticide are much more protective than untreated nets.  The insecticides that are used for treating bed nets kill mosquitoes, as well as other insects.
