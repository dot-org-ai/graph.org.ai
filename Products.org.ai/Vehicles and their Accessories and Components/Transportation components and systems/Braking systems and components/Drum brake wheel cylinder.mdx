# Drum brake wheel cylinder

**UNSPSC Code**: 25171727
**Class**: [Braking systems and components](Braking systems and components.mdx)
**Family**: [Transportation components and systems](../Transportation components and systems.mdx)
**Segment**: [Vehicles and their Accessories and Components](../../Vehicles and their Accessories and Components.mdx)

In a drum brake system, the Wheel Cylinder puts pressure on the brake shoes, which when pressed against the drum, slows down and stops the vehicle. The cylinder is connected to the shoes by small rods.
