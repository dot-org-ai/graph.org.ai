# Linkage clevis assembly

**UNSPSC Code**: 25174816
**Class**: [Specialized vehicle systems and components](Specialized vehicle systems and components.mdx)
**Family**: [Transportation components and systems](../Transportation components and systems.mdx)
**Segment**: [Vehicles and their Accessories and Components](../../Vehicles and their Accessories and Components.mdx)

A U-shaped or horseshoe shaped assembly consisting of a connector and a pin. The clevis is used in many linkage applications that provides a secure yet easily removable link where both transfer of force and rotation in the link is necessary.
