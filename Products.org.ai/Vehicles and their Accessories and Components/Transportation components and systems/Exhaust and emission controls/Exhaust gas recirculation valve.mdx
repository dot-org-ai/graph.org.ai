# Exhaust gas recirculation valve

**UNSPSC Code**: 25173712
**Class**: [Exhaust and emission controls](Exhaust and emission controls.mdx)
**Family**: [Transportation components and systems](../Transportation components and systems.mdx)
**Segment**: [Vehicles and their Accessories and Components](../../Vehicles and their Accessories and Components.mdx)

Exhaust gas recirculation valve (EGR valve), recirculates small amounts of hot exhaust gases back into the combustion chambers of the engine in order to decrease the combustion temperature, thereby reducing the formation of smog-producing nitrogen oxides.
