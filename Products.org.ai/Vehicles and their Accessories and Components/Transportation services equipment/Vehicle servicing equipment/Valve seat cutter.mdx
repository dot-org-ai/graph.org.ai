# Valve seat cutter

**UNSPSC Code**: 25191712
**Class**: [Vehicle servicing equipment](Vehicle servicing equipment.mdx)
**Family**: [Transportation services equipment](../Transportation services equipment.mdx)
**Segment**: [Vehicles and their Accessories and Components](../../Vehicles and their Accessories and Components.mdx)

Equipment that grinds the valve sheet of the cylinder head of reciprocating type internal combustion engine with certain angle and shape. The pilot stem is inserted to the valve guide hole to insert the T shaped handle axis and process the valve sheet by turning the handle manually.
