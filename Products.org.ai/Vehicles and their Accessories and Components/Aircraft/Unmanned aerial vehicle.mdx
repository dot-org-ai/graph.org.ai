# Unmanned aerial vehicle

**UNSPSC Code**: 25132100
**Family**: [Aircraft](Aircraft.mdx)
**Segment**: [Vehicles and their Accessories and Components](../Vehicles and their Accessories and Components.mdx)

An unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV), commonly known as a drone, is an aircraft without a human pilot aboard. UAVs are a component of an unmanned aircraft system (UAS); which includes a UAV, a ground-based controller, and a system of communications between the two. The flight of UAVs may operate with various degrees of autonomy: either under remote control by a human operator or autonomously by onboard computers
